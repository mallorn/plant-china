## Description
Shrubs or small trees, 1.5-13 m tall. Leaf blade subreniform to transversely oblong, 6-14 × 6.5-20 cm, apex rounded to subtruncate, abruptly shortly acuminate. Male pedicels 6-7 mm; females 2.5-4 mm. Fl. May-Jun, fr. Jun-Aug.

> ● Mountain valleys, roadsides, forests; 600-2000 m. W Sichuan, NE Yunnan.
