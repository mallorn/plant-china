基隆毛茛(原变种)",
25a.**Ranunculus hirtellus var. hirtellus**",

## Description
Stems 15--27 cm. Basal leaves with petiole 6--9.5 cm; blade 3-sect, 1.3--3.5 × 1.6--4.2 cm. Flowers 2 or 3 in a terminal monochasium, 1.2--1.5 in diam. Receptacle and achenes puberulent. Fl. May--Jun.

> Scrub, weedy places; 3000--3400 m. S Xizang [Afghanistan, N India, Kashmir, Nepal, N Pakistan].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Ranunculus jilongensis&lt;/I&gt; L. Liou.
