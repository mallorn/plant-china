## Description
Plants &amp;#177; woody. Stems many branched apically or unbranched. Leaves alternate, ovate to linear-oblong, shallowly to deeply crenate. Inflorescences spicate, racemose, or axillary, usually short and compact; bracts sessile. Calyx 2-lobed or sometimes 4- or 5-lobed. Corolla tube less than 2 X as long as calyx; galea recurved apically; beak short, indistinct.
