> *Polystichum* sect. *Haplopolystichum* in its current circumscription is possibly not monophyletic.

## Description
Plants medium-sized, evergreen. Lamina 1-pinnate, papery or rarely thickly papery. Rachis without bulbils; scales lanceolate, subulate, or linear. Pinnae oblong, lanceolate, or falcate-lanceolate. Microscales linear or subulate.
  "Statistics": "More than 200 (ca. 54 described) species: mainly in limestone areas in S China, a dozen of species in neighboring Asian regions, especially in N Vietnam; 48 species (43 endemic) in China.
