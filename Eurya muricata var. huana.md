## Description
Current year branchlets, terminal buds, petioles, and abaxial surface of leaf blades pubescent or at least terminal buds pubescent. Fl. Nov-Dec, fr. Jul-Sep.

> ●  Forest or thickets; 300-1300 m. Guizhou, SW and W Hunan, S Jiangxi, SE Sichuan, S and SE Yunnan, S Zhejiang.
  "Synonym": "*Eurya huana* Kobuski, J. Arnold Arbor. 20: 366. 1939 [*\"huiana\"*].
