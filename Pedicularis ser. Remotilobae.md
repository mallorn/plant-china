## Description
Herbs low. Stems often several, ascending or procumbent, unbranched. Leaves mostly basal, long petiolate, small, rounded. Stem leaves opposite. Corolla tube ca. 2 X as long as calyx; galea marginally 2-toothed; beak slender.
