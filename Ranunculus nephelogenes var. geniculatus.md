## Description
Stems 10--26 cm, glabrous throughout or sparsely puberulent only above. Basal leaves with blade undivided, or 3-lobed to 3-sect, lanceolate-linear, oblong, or narrowly oblong, Stem leaves linear or lanceolate-linear, undivided or 3-sect. Sepals 4.5--6 mm. Petals 6--9 mm. Fl. Apr--Aug, fr. Jun--Aug.

> * Meadows, by streams, swamps; 2500--3200 m. NW Yunnan.

**1c．曲升毛茛（变种）**

Ranunculus longicaulis var. geniculatus (Hand.-Mazz.) L. Liou. comb. nov. ——R. pulchellus var. geniculatus Hand.-Mazz. Symb. Sin. 7: 305, 1931.

与长茎毛茛及云生毛茛的区别：茎基部偃卧地面，下部节上生根，膝曲上升; 叶片椭圆形至披针形，顶端有3齿裂或全缘。

产云南西北部。生于海拔约3000米的潮湿草甸。模式标本采自云南。
