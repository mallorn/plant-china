金雀花黄堇(原亚种)",
249a.**Corydalis cytisiflora subsp. cytisiflora**",

## Description
Corolla yellow; upper petal 15-18 mm; dorsal crest of upper petal extended to spur end; lower petal usually without crest. Capsule 12-15 × 1.5-2 mm. Seeds ca. 1.5 mm.
  "Statistics": "● Grasslands; 3200-3300 m. S Gansu (Wenchuan), N Sichuan (Songpan).
  "Synonym": "*Corydalis curviflora* Maximowicz var. *cytisiflora* Fedde, Repert. Spec. Nov. Regni Veg. 20: 290. 1924; *C. curviflora* var. *smithii* Fedde.
