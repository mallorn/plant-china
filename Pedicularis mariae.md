## Description
Herbs perennial, ca. 20 cm tall. Stems erect, unbranched, sparsely pubescent. Leaves alternate, glabrous; basal and proximal stem leaves petiolate; leaf blade oblong, pinnatisect; segments ovate or oblong, pinnatilobate or pectinate-dentate. Inflorescences racemose, dense initially, becoming lax; bracts ovate basally, 3--5-lobed, long pubescent, serrate or entire, distal ones obovate-cuneate to oblong-cuneate; lobes 3, entire. Calyx tube 5-angular; lobes 5, unequal, lanceolate, less than 1/2 as long as tube, 2 pairs of lateral lobes connivent; entire or serrate. Corolla yellow, ca. 2 X as long as calyx; galea &amp;#177; erect; beakless, apex rounded; lower lip ca. as long as galea, ciliate. Filaments glabrous. Capsule obliquely oblong, 1.3--1.5 cm. Fl. May--Aug, fr. Jun--Sep.

> Xinjiang [Kazakhstan].
**195.玛丽马先蒿**

Pedicularis mariae Regel in Acta Hort. Petrop. Vl (1880) 351; Vved. in Fl. URSS XXII (1955) 772. ——P. altaica auct. non Steph. : Maxim. in Bull. Acad. St. Petersb. XXXII (1888) , f. 147, et in Mel. iol. XII (1888) , f. 147; Limpr. in Repert. Sp. Nov. XX (1924) 218, pro parte: Li, in Proc. Acad. Nat. Sci. Philad. CI (1949) 18, pro parte.

茎不分枝，直立，高约20厘米，有疏毛或近顶处毛较长。叶互生，长圆形，无毛，基生者与下部茎生者有柄，羽状全裂达有翅的中肋，裂片卵形或长圆形，有胼胝质之齿或微微羽状浅裂，上部茎叶无柄，有篦状之齿或篦状浅裂，齿或小裂片有时全缘，有时有胼胝状小齿。花序总状，始稠密，后伸长而基部有间断；苞片有长毛，下部者卵形，基部楔形，3-5浅裂，前方渐狭为长圆状有齿或全缘，上部者倒卵状楔形或长圆状楔形，3裂，裂片全缘，有时裂片亚相等或中裂较狭长；萼有长毛，管五角形，齿5枚，不等，披针形，较管短2倍有余，长过于芝，渐渐锐尖而全缘或端稍膨大而有细齿；花冠黄色，较萼几长2倍，盔亚直立，无喙，端圆形，下方有2齿；下唇约与盔等长，裂片大；花丝无毛。

产我国新疆伊犂（库车）。亦产苏联中亚伊犂河流域及天山。
