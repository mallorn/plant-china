筐条菝葜 (原变种)",
32a.**Smilax corbularia var. corbularia**",

## Description
Leaf blade ovate, ovate-oblong, or narrowly elliptic, adaxially slightly shiny, reticulate veins conspicuous adaxially, base subrounded, margin slightly recurved.

> Forests, thickets; near sea level to 1600 m. Guangdong, Guangxi, Hainan, Yunnan [Myanmar, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt; Smilax banglaoensis&lt;/I&gt; R. H. Miao.
