## Description
Stem erect, 30-160 cm tall, 4-15 mm in diam. at base, proximally glabrous, distally white arachnoid-puberulent. Basal leaves petiolate; petiole 9-30 cm, glabrous, narrowly winged, gradually enlarged toward base into a sheath; sheath 7-11 cm, to 2 cm wide, oppositely amplexicaul, mouth of sheath spreading toward outside, forming an oblique petiole; leaf blade ovate-oblong or ovate-triangular, 4-25 × 3-20 cm, glabrous, pinnately veined, base shallowly cordate or truncate, margin regularly dentate, apex acute. Stem leaves sessile, erect, adnate, ovate-lanceolate, to 9 cm, smaller distally, tubular-amplexicaul. Synflorescence racemose, 15-40 cm; leaflike bracts narrowly lanceolate or rhombic, 2-3.3 cm × 3-4 mm, subentire or dentate, narrowed at base; peduncles 5-20 mm, smaller distally. Capitula numerous; supplementary bracts linear-lanceolate, 8-12 mm. Involucre campanulate, 8-10 × 5-8 mm, outside glabrous; phyllaries 8, in 2 rows, oblong, 2-3 mm wide, apex acute or obtuse; inner phyllaries broadly membranous at margin. Ray florets 5-7, yellow; lamina oblong or obovate-oblong, 1.4-1.8 cm × 3-4.5 mm, apex obtuse; tube ca. 4 mm. Tubular florets numerous, 7-8 mm; tube ca. 3 mm. Achenes brown, cylindric, ca. 5 mm. Pappus yellow, as long as tubular corolla. Fl. and fr. Jul-Oct. 2*n* = 58*.

**85a. 千崖子橐吾（原变种）**

Ligularia kanaitzensis (Franch.) Hand.-Mazz. Symb. var. kanaitzensis

多年生草本。根肉质，直径4-5毫米。茎直立，高30-160厘米，上部被白色蛛丝状柔毛，下部光滑，基部直径0.4-1.5厘米。丛生叶与茎基部叶具柄，柄长9-33厘米，光滑，具狭翅，向基部渐宽，膨大成鞘，鞘长7-13厘米，宽达2厘米，茎基部叶的鞘对生抱茎，鞘口外展，致使叶柄斜生，叶片卵状长圆形或卵状三角形，长4-25厘米，宽3-14厘米，先端急尖，边缘具整齐的小齿，基部平截或浅心形，两面光滑，叶脉羽状；茎生叶无柄，直立，贴生，卵状披．针形，长达9厘米，向上渐小，筒状半抱茎。总状花序长15-40厘米；苞片狭披针形，长2-3.3厘米，宽3-4毫米，近全缘，基部狭，有短柄；花序梗长5-20毫米，向上渐短；头状花序多数，辐射状；小苞片线状披针形，长8-12毫米；总苞钟状，长8-10毫米，宽5-8毫米，总苞片8，2层，长圆形，宽2-3毫米，先端急尖或钝，背部光滑，内层具宽膜质边缘。舌状花5-7，黄色，舌片倒卵状长圆形或长圆形，长14-18毫米，宽3-4毫少长，先端钝，管部长约4毫米；管状花多数，长7-8毫米，管部长约3毫米，冠毛黄色与花冠等长。瘦果圆柱形，长约4毫米，光滑。花果期7-10月。

产云南西北部。生于海拔2400-3500米的水边、山坡、沼泽地及灌丛。模式标本采自洱源（干崖子）。

Ligularia mosoyinensis (Franch.) Hand.-Mazz.与本种的模式标本均采自云南洱源，从前者的模式标本（Delavay 1109）照片看，形态上与本种一致，两者叶子都是长圆状卵形或心形，基部截形至心形。前人以叶基部心形或截形作为它们的主要区别点显然是不妥当的，它们应是同一种。
**85b. 菱苞豪吾（变种）**

Ligularia kanaitzensis (Franch.) Hand.-Mazz. Symb. var. subnudicaulis (Hand.-Mazz.) S. W. Liu, comb. nov.——Ligulaia subnudicaulis Hand.-Mazz. in Bot. Jahrb. 69: 73. 1938; S. Y. Hu in Quart. Journ Taiwan Mus. 20: 73. 1967; H. Koyama in Mern. Fac. Sci. Kyoto Univ. Ser. Biol. 2: 48. 1968. in obs.

苞片菱形，具短柄，边缘有齿，干时常紫红色。

产云南西北部。生于海拔2400-3650米的竹林中和山间草地。模式标本采自丽江（玉龙山）。
