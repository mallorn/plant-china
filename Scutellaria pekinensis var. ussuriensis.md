黑龙江",
38b.**Scutellaria pekinensis var. ussuriensis** (Regel) Handel-Mazzetti",

## Description
Stems green, base purplish, subglabrous or sparsely pubescent. Leaves membranous, adaxially glabrous or strigose, abaxially sparsely villous on veins. Calyx veins sparsely villous. Corolla 1.7-2.2 cm.

> Forested slopes, wet forested meadows; 500-1500 m. Heilongjiang, Jilin, Nei Mongol [Japan, Korea, Russia].

这一变种与原变种不同在于茎几无毛或被极疏的小柔毛；叶膜质，上面无毛或着生于基部者被糙伏毛，下面仅沿脉上被极疏的柔毛；花萼仅脉上被疏柔毛。

产吉林，黑龙江，辽宁及内蒙古；生于海拔450-1500米的山地林下或林间草甸湿地。苏联，朝鲜，日本也有。模式标本采自苏联。
