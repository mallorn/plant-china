## Description
Herbs low or some stemless. Leaves basal. Flowers few, often capitate. Pedicel long. Calyx (3--)5-lobed. Corolla &amp;#177; red-purple; tube long, often more than 2 X as long as calyx; beak slender; lower lip less than 2 cm wide.
