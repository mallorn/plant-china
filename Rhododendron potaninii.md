## Description
Small shrubs, erect, branchlets glabrous. Petiole 20–25 mm, densely tomentose, hairs branched; leaf blade leathery, oblong to obovate, 7–9 × 2.5–4 cm; base rounded; apex obtuse, mucronate; abaxial surface pale, only lower half of midrib densely fine branched-hairy; adaxial surface glabrous. Inflorescence racemose or subumbellate, 7–8-flowered. Pedicel ca. 2 cm, densely rufous-pubescent; calyx lobes 5, ca. 1 mm, irregular, ± hairy; corolla broadly campanulate, white, unspotted, ca. 3 cm, glabrous; lobes 5, ca. 1 cm, rounded; stamens 10, included in the tube, reaching only to base of lobes, filaments sparsely puberulent in lower half; ovary conical, ca. 4 mm, densely rufous-tomentose; style glabrous.

**152.甘肃杜鹃（中国高等植物图鉴）**

Rhododendron potaninii Batalin in Act. Hort. Petrop. 11: 489. 1891; Tagg in Stevenson, Spec. Rhodod. 840. 1930; Chamb. in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 39: 428. 1982.

常绿小乔木，高约0.5米；小枝无毛。叶革质，长圆形或倒卵形，长7-8厘米，宽3.5-4厘米，先端钝，具凸尖头，基部圆形，边缘近于平坦，上面无毛，中脉凹入，侧脉15-16对，微凹，下面仅中脉下半部密被分枝毛；叶柄长2-2.5厘米，密被分枝柔毛。顶生短总状或近于伞形花序，有花7-8朵，花梗长约2厘米，密被红褐色柔毛；花萼小，长约1毫米，不规则地5齿裂，多少被毛；花冠宽钟形，长3厘米，白色，不具斑点，裂片5，扁圆形，长约1厘米，宽1.5厘米；雄蕊10，内藏。其长达裂片基部，花丝下半部被微柔毛，花药长2毫米；雌蕊与花冠近相等；子房圆锥状，长4毫米，密被红褐色绒毛，花柱无毛。

产甘肃东部。模式标本采自甘肃东部。
