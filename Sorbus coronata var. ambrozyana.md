少脉冠萼花楸",
47b.**Sorbus coronata var. ambrozyana** (C. K. Schneider) L. T. Lu",

## Description
Petiole 1–2 cm; leaf blade ovate-lanceolate, abaxial surface and pedicels usually persistently pubescent; lateral veins 9 or 10 pairs.

> Mountain regions; 3000--3200 m. NW Yunnan (Lijiang Naxizu Zizhixian).
  "Synonym": "*Sorbus ambrozyana* C. K. Schneider, Bot. Gaz. 63. 401. 1917.
