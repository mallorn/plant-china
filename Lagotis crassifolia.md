厚叶兔耳草",
6.**Lagotis crassifolia** Prain",

## Description
Herbs, 2-12 cm tall. Lateral roots few, fibrous. Rhizomes elongated, coarse, fleshy. Stems to 8, ascending. Basal leaves numerous, thick, fleshy; petiole stout, 2-7 cm, flattened, base expanded, sheathlike; leaf blade ovate to ovate-oblong, 2-6 cm, thick, base cuneate, margin coarsely toothed, apex obtuse, acuminate tipped. Stem leaves 3 or 4, subsessile, smaller than basal leaves. Spikes elongated, more than 1/2 as long as stem; bracts ovate, shorter than flower, margin subentire, apex acute. Calyx apex of upper side 2-lobed. Corolla blue-purple or white, 1-1.3 cm; tube straight, ca. 2 X as long as limb; lower lip lobes 2 or 3, lanceolate; upper lip unlobed, ovate-oblong. Filaments short; anthers reniform. Style shorter than corolla tube; stigma capitate or 2-lobed. Fruit oblong. Fl. and fr. Jul-Sep.

> Alpine grassland; 4200-5300 m. Xizang (Yadong Xian) [Bhutan, Sikkim].
**6.厚叶兔耳草（新拟）图版40：10-14**

Lagotis crassifolia Prain, Journ. Asiat. Soc. Bengal. 65: 63. pl. 2. 1896; Hand.-Mazz. , Symb. Sin. 7: 841. 1936.

多年生草本，高约6-12厘米。根状茎斜走，伸长，粗大，肉质；根多数，条形，有少数须根。茎多条可达7-8条，稍细长，下部多平卧。基生叶多数，厚肉质，略皱，叶柄粗壮，长2-5(7)厘米，扁平，两侧具膜质翅，基部扩大成鞘状；叶片卵形至卵状矩圆形，长2-6厘米，顶端钝或具凸尖，基部楔形，边缘具尖头的粗齿；茎生叶3-4片，与基生叶同形而小，近无柄。穗状花序伸长，约占茎长的一半以上，花稠密；苞片卵形，锐头，近全缘，膜质，较花短；花萼佛焰苞状，薄膜质，透明，较花冠筒及苞片稍长，后方顶端2浅裂，裂片有缘毛；花冠自蓝紫色一直退至纯白色，长约10-13毫米，花冠筒伸直，较唇约长一倍，上唇卵状矩圆形，全缘，下唇2裂少3裂，裂片披针形；雄蕊花丝极短，花药肾形；雌蕊内藏，短于花冠筒，柱头头状或2浅裂。果实矩圆形。花果期7-9月。

分布于西藏的亚东、帕里及雪古拉山，海拔4200-5300米的高山草地。
