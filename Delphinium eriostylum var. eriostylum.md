毛梗翠雀花(原变种)",
93a.**Delphinium eriostylum var. eriostylum**",

## Description
Proximal leaves absent; leaf blade shortly strigose. Ovary sparsely pilose. Fl. Mar--Jun.

> * Slopes, grassy places, streamsides in valleys; 500--2000 m. Guizhou, SE Sichuan (Nanchuan Xian).
