## Description
Shrubs or small trees; bark rough; young shoots tomentose, also with bristles or glandular-setae, bud scales often persistent. Leaf blade oblong-elliptic, oblong-lanceolate or oblanceolate; apex acute apiculate; abaxial surface with a persistent or evanescent stellate tomentum and bristles, more so on the midrib. Inflorescence lax, 5–20-flowered; rachis 2–20 mm. Calyx minute, 2 mm, (8–13 mm in R. longesquamatum); corolla widely or narrowly campanulate, funnel- or tubular-campanulate, 5-lobed, white, pink or deep red; stamens 10(12–14); ovary tomentose glandular-hairy, rarely glabrous.
