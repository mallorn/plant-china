刺毛白珠(原变种)",
27a.**Gaultheria trichophylla var. trichophylla**",

## Description
Leaf blade elliptic or elliptic-oblong, long ciliate along margin. Thecae 1-awned, awn ca. 0.5 mm.

> Mountain slopes, montane grasslands, rocky places; 3000–4200 m. C Sichuan, S Xizang, NW Yunnan [Bhutan, NE India (?Assam, Darjiling), Kashmir, Nepal, Sikkim].
