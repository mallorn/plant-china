光茎短距翠雀花",
1b.**Delphinium forrestii var. viride** (W. T. Wang) W. T. Wang",

## Description
Stem distally glabrous, proximally sparsely puberulent, often glabrescent. Sepals green, abaxial hairs 0.1--0.2 mm.

> * Forest margins, grassy slopes; 3100--4100 m. SE Xizang (Chawalong), NW Yunnan (Dêqên Xian).
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Delphinium mairei&lt;/I&gt; Ulbrich var. &lt;I&gt;viride&lt;/I&gt; W. T. Wang, Acta Bot. Sin. 10: 73. 1962, based on &lt;I&gt;D. viride&lt;/I&gt; Chen, Bull. Fan Mem. Inst. Biol., n.s., 1: 165. 1948, not Watson (1888).
**lb．光茎短距翠雀花（变种）**

Delphinium forrestii var. viride (W. T. Wang) W. T. Wang, st. nov. ——D. viride Chen in Bull. Fan Mem. Inst. Biol. , n. s. 1: 165. 1948, non Watson, 1888.——D. mairei var. viride W. T. Wang, 植物学报10: 73. 1962.

与短距翠雀花的区别：茎下部疏被短柔毛，常变无毛；萼片外面被短糙毛（毛长0.1-0.2毫米）。萼片绿色。

产云南西北部（德钦）和西藏东南部（察隅，察瓦龙）。生海拔3100-4100米间山地草坡或林边。模式标本来自云南德钦。
