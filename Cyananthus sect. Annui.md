## Description
Plants annual, lacking a caudex. Calyx sometimes 4-lobed. Corolla sometimes 3- or 4-lobed. Ovary sometimes 3- or 4-locular.
  "Synonym": "*Cyananthus* subsect. *Annui* Y. S. Lian in D. Y. Hong, Fl. Reipubl. Popularis Sin. 73(2): 183. 1983.
