## Description
Plants medium-sized. Lamina papery, bipinnate at base, 1-pinnate upward. Basal pinnae extraordinarily prolonged.
  "Statistics": "About four species: Asia; three species (one endemic) in China.
  "keylist": "
1 Pinnae coarsely serrate, falcate-lanceolate, acuminate.[*P. tripteron* 戟叶耳蕨](Polystichum tripteron.md)
1 Pinnae toothed, oblong or almost rectangular, acute——(2)
2(1) Basal pinnae 1-pinnate (at least some laminae of same plant with 1-pinnate basal pinnae); sori slightly closer to midrib; indusia much smaller than below; perispore with reticulate sculpture.[*P. hancockii* 小戟叶耳蕨](Polystichum hancockii.md)
2 Basal pinnae rarely 1-pinnate; sori closer to pinna margin; indusia 0.6-0.8 mm in diam.; perispore with irregularly perforate sculpture.[*P. normale* 渝黔耳蕨",](Polystichum normale.md)
