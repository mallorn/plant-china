长叶菝葜
52d.**Smilax lanceifolia var. lanceolata** (J. B. Norton) T. Koyama

## Description
Leaf blade lanceolate to narrowly oblong-lanceolate, usually 5--7 × as long as wide, main veins raised adaxially (sometimes midvein slightly concave). Peduncle shorter than or subequaling petiole. Tepals purple.


> * Forests, forest margins; 1500--2000 m. S Yunnan.

  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt; Smilax cocculoides&lt;/I&gt; Warburg var. &lt;I&gt; lanceolata&lt;/I&gt; J. B. Norton in Sargent, Pl. Wilson. 3: 11. 1916.

**长叶菝葜（变种）**

Smilax lanceifolia Roxb. var. lanceolata (Norton) T. Koyama in Quart. Journ. Taiwan Mus. 13: 26. 1960. ——S. cocculoides Warb. var. lanceolata Norton in Pl. Wils. 3: 11. 1916.

叶革质，长披针形或矩圆状披针形；小枝伸直匐不为折状；总花梗短于叶柄或近等长；花紫色；花药近圆形。花期2月。

产云南南部（临沧、思茅、金平、屏边、文山一带）。生于海拔1500-2000米的林下或路旁。
