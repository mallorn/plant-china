6b.**Pimpinella diversifolia var. angustipetala** R. H. Shan & F. T. Pu",

## Description
Plants without stolons. Petals ovate-lanceolate, apex mucronate, slightly incurved, but not notched and without incurved lobule.

> Forests, grassy slopes. SC Sichuan (Emei Shan).
**2c. 尖瓣异叶茴芹 图版25: 1-5**

Pimpinella diversifolia DC. var. angustipetala Shan et Pu, 植物分类学报21 (1): 81. 1983.

与原变种的主要区别在于：本变种的花瓣呈披针形，顶端全缘微弯曲。

产四川。模式标本采自峨眉山。
