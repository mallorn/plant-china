## Description
Stem appressed puberulent, mixed with some retrorse, longer hairs. Inflorescence racemose; raceme appressed puberulent, rarely mixed with yellow, glandular hairs. Staminode limb entire, emarginate, or 2-lobed. Carpels puberulent. Fl. Jul--Nov.

> * Scrub, grassy slopes; 1900--3500 m. C and NW Yunnan.
