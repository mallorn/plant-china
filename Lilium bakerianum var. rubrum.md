紫红花滇百合",
14d.**Lilium bakerianum var. rubrum** Stearn",

## Description
Leaves glabrous. Tepals purple-red or pink, with purple or red spots adaxially.

> * Forest margins, hillsides along streams, grassy slopes; 1500--2000 m. Guizhou, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt; Lilium linceorum&lt;/I&gt; H. Léveillé &amp; Vaniot.
**紫红花滇百合（变种）**

var. rubrum Stearn in Gard. Chron. ser. 3, 124: 4. 1948.

本变种与滇百合的区别在于花红色或粉红色，有紫红色或红色斑点。

产云南。生杂木林缘、溪边或山坡草地，海拔1500-2000米。
