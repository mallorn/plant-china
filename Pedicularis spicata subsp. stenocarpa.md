## Description
Bracts leaflike or rhomboid-ovate apically. 2 filaments densely villous. Capsule lanceolate-ovoid, 8--10 mm, long apiculate. Fr. Sep.

> * W Hebei.
**穗花马先蒿狭果亚种**

Pedicularis spicata Pall. subsp. stenocarpa Tsoong, subsp. nov., in Addenda, 405.

本亚种的不同在于蒴果较细瘦而长，长约8-40毫米，披针状卵形，上半部强烈向下弯曲而有长尖。

仅见我国河北省西灵山与东灵山。
