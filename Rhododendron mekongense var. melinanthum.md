密花弯月杜鹃
186b.**Rhododendron mekongense var. melinanthum** (I. B. Balfour & Kingdon Ward) Cullen

## Description
Shrubs deciduous; branchlets and pedicels setose. Leaf blade adaxially glabrous. Calyx glabrous; corolla yellow. Fl. May–Jun, fr. Jul–Aug.


> Forest margins, thickets, scrub, grassy slopes, valleys; 3300–4300 m. SE Xizang, NW Yunnan [NE Myanmar].

产云南西北部、西藏东南部。生于林缘、砾石灌丛、高山灌丛草地或冰川地带，海拔3 300-4 300米。缅甸北部也有。模式标本采自云南德钦。
