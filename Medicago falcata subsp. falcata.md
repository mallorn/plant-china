野苜蓿(原亚种)",
6a.**Medicago falcata subsp. falcata**",

## Description
Stems ascending, appressed puberulent. Leaflets obovate to linear-oblanceolate, appressed puberulent abaxially, glabrous adaxially, lateral veins 12-15 pairs, margin serrulate in apical 1/4, apex obtuse, mucronulate. Legume falcate.
  "Statistics": "Grassy places, slopes, ravines, dry sandy fields. Gansu, Hebei, Jilin, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shanxi, Xinjiang, Xizang [Asia, Europe].
  "Synonym": "*Medicago sativa* Linnaeus subsp. *falcata* (Linnaeus) Arcangeli.
