狭叶委陵菜(原变种)",
18a.**Potentilla stenophylla var. stenophylla**",

## Description
Leaflets with 3–5 teeth at apex. Fl. and fr. Jun–Sep.

> Forest margins, alpine meadows; 3200--5300 m. Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Potentilla peduncularis* D. Don var. *stenophylla* Franchet, Pl. Delavay. 3: 214. 1890; *P. millefolia* H. Léveillé (1914), not Rydberg (1896); *P. stenophylla* var. *millefolia* Soják.
