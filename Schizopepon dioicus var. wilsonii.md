## Description
Leaf blade narrowly ovate-cordate, 3-5 lobed, apex caudate-acuminate. Ovary and fruit glabrous; fruit black-brown, smooth.
  "Statistics": "● Roadsides and thickets on mountain slopes; 1500-2400 m. NW Guizhou, E and W Sichuan.
  "Synonym": "*Schizopepon wilsonii* Gagnepain, Bull. Mus. Natl. Hist. Nat. 24: 378. 1918.
**3c. 四川裂瓜（变种）**

var. wilsonii (Gagnep.) A. M. Lu et Z. Y. Zhang, 植物分类学报23 (2): 113. 1985. ——S. wilsonii Gagnep. in Bull. Mus. Hist. Nat. Par. 24: 378. 1918.

不同于原变种在于叶长卵状心形，先端尾状渐尖，边缘3-5裂，裂片先端渐尖；果实黑褐色，表面平滑。

产四川东部和西部、贵州西北部。常生于海拔1500-2400米的山坡、路旁及灌丛中。模式标本采自四川城口。
