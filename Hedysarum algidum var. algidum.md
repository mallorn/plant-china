块茎岩黄耆(原变种)",
21a.**Hedysarum algidum var. algidum**",

## Description
Leaflet blades obovate to elliptic. Corolla standard 1.3-1.6 cm. Calyx teeth ± as long as tube.
  "Statistics": "● Alpine meadows, alpine gravelly areas; 3000-4200 m. SW Gan-su, Qinghai, Sichuan.
  "Synonym": "*Hedysarum algidum* var. *thyrsum* Y. H. Wu; *H. tuberosum* B. Fedtschenko (1900), not Roxburgh ex Willdenow (1825).
