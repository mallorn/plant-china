## Description
Leaves 5-9-foliolate; leaflet blades alternate or opposite, both surfaces with sparse appressed medifixed trichomes. Pedi-cel 4-5 mm but to 1 cm in fruit.
  "Statistics": "● Scrub. W Hubei (Xingshan).

**14c.兴山木蓝（变种）华中木蓝（中国主要植物图说·豆科）**

var. chalara (Craib) Y. Y. Fang et C. Z. Zheng in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 27: 164. 1989.——I. chalara Craib in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 8: 49. 1913; 中国主要植物图说·豆科241. 图229. 1955.

本变种小叶2-4对，互生或对生；花梗长4-5毫米；果梗长可达1厘米，与原变种不同。

产湖北（兴山）。模式标本采自兴山。
