21b.**Rubus idaeus var. glabratus** T. T. Yu & L. T. Lu",

## Description
Inflorescence rachis, pedicels, and abaxial surface of calyx without glandular hairs. Branchlets, petioles, and pedicels glabrous, with sparse prickles.

> Mixed forests, roadsides.  S Heilongjiang.
**15c.无毛复盆子（变种）**

Rubus idaeus L. var. glabratus Yu et Lu,植物分类学报20(3): 297. 1982.

本变种枝、叶柄和花梗具极稀疏小刺，均无毛，也无腺毛。

产黑龙江南部。生路边杂木林下，在齐齐哈尔试验场有栽培。
