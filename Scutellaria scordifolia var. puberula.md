## Description
Stems with dense, upwardly curved puberulent hairs on angles. Leaves triangular-ovate to lanceolate, 4-14 mm wide, sparsely appressed puberulent except on veins of abaxial surface.

> * Grassy slopes, marshy grasslands; 0-1400 m. Hebei, Heilongjiang, Nei Mongol, Shanxi.
**85b. 微柔毛变种**

var. puberula Regel ex Komarov in Act. Hort, Petrop. 25: 344. 1907; Kitagawa, Lineam. Fl. Mansh. 386. 1939.

这一变种与原变种不同在于茎沿棱上被有较密的上曲微柔毛；叶上面疏被但在下面除脉外亦被有紧贴微柔毛。

产黑龙江，内蒙古，河北及山西；生于山地草坡上或湿草甸上，海拔1400米以下。模式标本采自黑龙江。
