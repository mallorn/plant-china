羽裂密枝委陵菜",
57b.**Potentilla virgata var. pinnatifida** (Lehmann) T. T. Yü & C. L. Li",

## Description
Stems low and spreading, almost on ground, or ascending. Radical leaves 3–5-foliolate; segments 2–4(or 5) on each side. Inflorescences few flowered. Fl. Jun, fr. Sep.

> Sunny mountain slopes, valleys, grasslands, flood lands; 1000--3700 m. Gansu, Qinghai, Xinjiang.
  "Synonym": "*Potentilla nivea* Linnaeus var. *pinnatifida* Lehmann, Nov. Stirp. Pug. 9: 67. 1851; *P. altaica* Bunge.
**54b.羽裂密枝委陵菜（变种）**

Potentilla virgata Lehm. var. pinnatifida (Lehm.) Yu et Li, comb. nov. ——P. nivea L. var. pinnatifida Lehm. Nov. Stirp. Pugill. 9: 67. 1851. ——P. altaica Bge in Ldb. Fl. Alt. 2:252. 1830.

植株矮小，近铺地或上升，通常少花，基生叶有小叶3-5，小叶裂片较少，通常每边2-4（-5)。花期6月，果期9月。

产新疆、青海、甘肃。生阳坡、山谷水边、河漫滩、草原，海拔1000-3700米。
