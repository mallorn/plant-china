## Description
Shrubs, sometimes epiphytic, 1–1.5 m tall; old branches glabrous; shoots spreading; young shoots scaly, sparsely brown-hispid. Petiole 3–7 mm, densely brown-scaly; leaf blade leathery, oblong-elliptic or ovate-elliptic, 2–4(–6) × 1.2–2.3(–4) cm; base obtuse, apex obtuse, apiculate; abaxial surface gray-white, scales contiguous to 1 × their own diameter apart, small, unequal, brown; adaxial surface pubescent along midrib only near the base; midrib raised abaxially, concave adaxially; lateral veins inconspicuous on both surfaces. Inflorescence terminal, cymose, 1- or 2-flowered. Pedicel 0.5–0.7 cm, scaly, pubescent or hispid, sometimes glabrous; calyx lobes 5, 2–4 mm, oblong or ovate, persistent in fruit, scales present; corolla broadly funnelform, white or tinged with pale red, with red markings inside, ca. 4 cm, outer surface glabrous; stamens 10, unequal, 2–3 cm, shorter than corolla; filaments with lower part white pubescent; ovary 5-locular, densely scaly; style slightly longer than corolla, glabrous. Capsule ovate, 15–23 mm. Fl. Apr–May, fr. Jul–Oct.

> Epiphytic on trees in forests, rocks; 1900–4000 m. Guizhou, Sichuan, NE Yunnan.

灌木，有时附生，高1-1.5米。枝条开展，幼枝有鳞片，密被褐色刚毛，老枝无毛。叶芽鳞倒卵形，早落。叶聚生枝条上部，近假轮生，叶片革质，长圆状椭圆形或卵状椭圆形，长2-4（-6）厘米，宽1.2-2.3（-4 ）厘米，两端钝，顶端有钝的小短尖头，边缘通常反卷，具缘毛，以后无毛，上面除中脉近叶基部被褐色短硬毛外，其余无毛，下面略灰白色，密被褐色鳞片，鳞片小，略不等大，相距为其直径或相互邻接，中脉在上面明显下陷，下面凸起，侧脉在两面不显；叶柄长3-7毫米，密被褐色刚毛。花序顶生，1-2花伞形着生；花梗长5-7毫米，被鳞片，被短柔毛或刚毛，有时近无毛；花萼5裂，裂片长圆形或卵圆形，长2-4毫米，下部连合，外面被鳞片，具缘毛；花冠宽漏斗状，长约4厘米，白色或带淡红色，内有红色斑点，外面洁净；雄蕊10，不等长，长2-3厘米，短于花冠，花丝下部有开展的白色柔毛；子房5室，密被鳞片，花柱伸出，略长于花冠，洁净。蒴果卵形，长1.5-2.3厘米，被宿存萼。花期4-5月，果期7-10月。

产四川东南至中西部、贵州（梵净山）、云南东北部。通常附生于林中树上，或生于岩石上，海拔1 900-2 000米。模式标本采自四川宝兴。
