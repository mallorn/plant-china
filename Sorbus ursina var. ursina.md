美叶花楸(原变种)",
18a.**Sorbus ursina var. ursina**",

## Description
Leaflet blades linear or narrowly lanceolate, margin sharply serrate from base or above middle. Hypanthium widely obconical or somewhat shortly tubular.

> Coniferous forests, high mountain thickets; 2700--4600 m. NW Sichuan, S Xizang, NW Yunnan [Bhutan, India, NE Myanmar, Nepal, Sikkim].
  "Synonym": "*Sorbus foliolosa* (Wallich) Spach var. *ursina* Wenzig, Linnaea 38: 75. 1874.
