渐尖楼梯草(原变种)",
13a.**Elatostema acuminatum var. acuminatum**",

## Description
Petiole to 2 mm or absent; leaf blade 1-3.5 cm wide, cystoliths absent or obscure, margin of broader side 3-7-dentate. Fl. Dec-May.

> Dense forests in valleys; 500-1500 m. W Guangdong, Hainan, Yunnan [Bhutan, Indonesia, Malaysia, Myanmar, Nepal, Sikkim, Thailand, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Procris* *acuminata* Poiret, Encycl. 5: 629. 1804.
