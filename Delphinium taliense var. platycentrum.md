## Description
Leaf blade less divided; ultimate lobules triangular or narrowly so. Spur cylindric-subulate, ca. 2.5 cm, base 4--5 mm in diam.; other sepals ca. 2.5 cm, appressed puberulent abaxially. Fl. Aug.

> * Rocks; ca. 3500 m. SW Sichuan (Butuo Xian).
**77d. 粗距大理翠雀花（变种）**

Delphinium taliense var. platycentrum W. T. Wang in Addenda.

与大理翠雀花的区别：花较大，萼距圆筒状钻形，与萼片近等长，长约2.5厘米，基部粗4-5毫米。茎下部有稀疏开展的糙毛。花序轴和花梗无毛。退化雄蕊的瓣片顶端微凹。子房密被柔毛。

产四川布拖。生海拔3500米一带山地林中石上。
