圆丛红景天(原亚种)",
12a.**Rhodiola coccinea subsp. coccinea**",

## Description
Flowering stems 1.5-2 mm in diam. Stem leaf apex subobtuse to acute. Sepals oblong, apex obtuse. Petals oblong-ovate to subobovate, ca. 4 mm. Fl. Jun-Jul, fr. Aug.

> Alpine regions, stony soils, rocks; 2600-4900 m. Gansu, Qinghai, Sichuan, Xinjiang, Xizang [Afghanistan, Bhutan, NW India, Kashmir, Nepal].
