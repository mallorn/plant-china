1.**Eurya sect. Meristotheca** Vesque",

## Description
Current year branchlets terete or 2-4-ribbed. Anthers locellate (not locellate in *E. acuminatissima*, *E. megatrichocarpa*, *E. pittosporifolia*, and *E. subcordata* but then ovary pubescent). Ovary pubescent or glabrous, with more than 10 ovules per locule, rarely with only 10 ovules per locule; style 0.5-6 mm.
