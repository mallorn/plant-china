## Description
Plants tall. Lamina pinnatifid-tripinnate. Basiscopic pinnule at base of basal pinnae not elongated, equal to or slightly shorter than next pinnule.
  "Statistics": "About 26 species: E and S Asia, Africa; seven species in China.

1 Lamina oblong-lanceolate, often slightly tapering to base——(4)
