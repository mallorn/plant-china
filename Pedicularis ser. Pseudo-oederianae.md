## Description
Herbs less than 15 cm tall. Roots fusiform, fleshy. Leaves alternate. Basal leaves numerous; petiole long; leaf blade pinnatisect. Inflorescences more than 1/2 height of plant, centrifugal. Calyx lobes 5. Corolla tube decurved; galea with 1 marginal tooth near apex, beakless.
