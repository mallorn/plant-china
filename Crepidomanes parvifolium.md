纤小单叶假脉蕨",
4.**Crepidomanes parvifolium** (Baker) K. Iwatsuki J. Fac. Sci. Univ. Tokyo, Sect. 3, Bot. 13: 535. 1985.",

## Description
Rhizome ca. 0.2 mm in diam., densely covered with brownish hairs. Stipe less than 3 mm, with caducous hairs like those on rhizome, winged only at apex. Lamina cuneate or oval, small, 0.5-2.5 cm, less than 0.5 cm wide, with a simple distinct vein, margin wavy, apex rounded or obtuse. Sori solitary, terminal on ultimate segment; involucre obconic or tubular, included, mouth dilated, entire at margin.

> On moss-covered rocks in forests. Guangxi, Yunnan [NE India, Myanmar, Nepal, Thailand].
  "Synonym": "*Hymenophyllum parvifolium* Baker, J. Linn. Soc., Bot. 9: 340. 1866; *Microgonium parvifolium* (Baker) Tagawa &amp; K. Iwatsuki; *Trichomanes parvifolium* (Baker) Copeland.
