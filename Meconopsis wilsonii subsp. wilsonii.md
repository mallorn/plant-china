尼泊尔绿绒蒿(原亚种)",
5a.**Meconopsis wilsonii subsp. wilsonii**",

## Description
Basal and lower leaves with 6-8 pairs of primary segments, middle and upper cauline leaves and bracts deeply divided with 6-8 pairs of segments. Peduncle and pedicel hairs ascending to subappressed.
  "Statistics": "● Grassy places, open scrub; 3300-4000 m. W Sichuan (Baoxing, Mianning).
