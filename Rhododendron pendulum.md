## Description
Straggling shrubs, evergreen, epiphytic, 0.3–1.3 m tall; indumentum whitish to beige. Petiole 4–8 mm, densely woolly; leaf blade oblong or oblong-elliptic, 2–5 × 1–2.5 cm; margin slightly revolute; apex obtuse or rounded; abaxial surface densely brown or fawn-woolly, scales ca. 0.5 × their own diameter apart, small, brown; adaxial surface smooth, shiny, scales and hairs absent. Inflorescence (1–)2- or 3-flowered. Flowers not fragrant. Pedicel 0.6–1.6 cm, densely woolly; calyx red or tinged red, lobes 5–9 mm, oblong or oblong-obovate, unequal, persisting to enclose mature capsule, sparsely reddish scaly, woolly, margin long ciliate; corolla funnelform-campanulate, white, white tinged pink, or rarely pale yellow, 1.5–2.2 cm; tube 7–10 mm; outer surface scaly; stamens 10, filaments pilose below; ovary densely woolly, scaly; style sharply deflexed, shorter than stamens, usually with a few hairs and scales at base. Capsule ovate or cylindric-ovate, 7–10 mm, densely woolly. Fl. Apr–Jun, fr. Jul–Oct.

> Dense forests, cliffs, rocks, often epiphytic; 2200–3700 m. S Xizang [Bhutan, Nepal, Sikkim].
**2．凸叶杜鹃（西藏植物志）图版4：6-7**

Rhododendron pendulum Hook. f. Rhodod. Sikkim Himal. t. 13. 1849; Hutch. in Stevenson, Spec. Rhodod. 232. 1930; Hara, Fl. East. Himal. 240. 1966; Cullen in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 39: 27. 1980; Davidian, Rhodod. Spec. 1: 126. t. 13, 1982; 西藏植物志3: 632, 图版252, 4-7. 1986.

常绿蜿蜒状附生灌木，高0.3-1.3米，小枝密被灰白至淡褐色绵毛，有散生鳞片或无。叶长圆形或长圆状椭圆形，长2-5厘米，宽1-2厘米，顶端钝或圆，边缘稍反卷，上面拱起，仅中脉明显下陷，不呈泡泡状，下面被黄棕色松软的绵毛，鳞片为毛被覆盖，相距为其直径或直径的1/2；叶柄长4-8毫米，密被绵毛。花序顶生，短总状或伞形，具1-3朵花；花梗长0.6-1.6厘米，密被褐色绵毛，有疏鳞片或无；花萼红色或带红色，5裂，裂片长圆形或长圆状倒卵形，不等大，长5-9毫米，外面被密或疏绵毛和少数鳞片，被长缘毛；花冠漏斗状钟形，短而宽，无香气，白色，淡红色，稀淡黄色，长1.5-2.2厘米，花管长0.7-1厘米，外面被鳞片；雄蕊10，不超出花冠，花丝下部被毛；子房5室，密被毛和鳞片，花柱短，明显弓弯，通常仅基部被疏毛和鳞片。蒴果卵形或长圆状卵形，长0.7-1厘米，密被毛，为宿萼所覆被。花期4-6月，果期7-10月。

产西藏南部（亚东、错那）。生于密林、灌丛或附生于沟谷边大树上，海拔2200-3 700米。尼泊尔、锡金、不丹、印度大吉岭也有。
