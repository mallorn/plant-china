白毛小叶栒子
41d.**Cotoneaster microphyllus var. cochleatus** (Franchet) Rehder & E. H. Wilson

## Description
Petiole 2–4 mm; leaf blade elliptic or ovate-elliptic, 5–11 × 3–8 mm, abaxially grayish white pubescent, adaxially glabrous, margin revolute. Hypanthium and sepals abaxially densely persistently long gray pubescent. Petals white. 2n = 68.


> Rocks, slopes; 2000--3000 m. Sichuan, Yunnan [Bhutan, Nepal].

  "Synonym": "*Cotoneaster buxifolius* Lindley f. *cochleatus* Franchet, Pl. Delavay. 224. 1890; *C. cochleatus* (Franchet) G. Klotz; *C. thymifolius* var. *cochleatus* (Franchet) Franchet.

**小叶栒子白毛变种**

C. microphylIus Wall. ex Lindl. var. cochleatus (Franch.) Rehd. & Wils. in Sarg. Pl. Wils. 1: 176. 1912; Yu in Bull. Brit. Mus. Bot. 1: 135. 1954. ——C. buxifolia f. cochleata Franch. Pl. Delav. 224. 1890.——C. cochleatus (Franch.) Klotz in Wiss. Zeits. Univ. Halle 6: 952. 1957.

叶片和萼筒密被白色柔毛，叶边反卷。

产云南、四川。模式标本采自云南鹤庆。不丹、尼泊尔也有分布。
