> About five species: Bhutan, China, Japan, C and E Nepal, Russia, Sikkim; one species in China.

## Description
Stems numerous, more than 40 cm tall, usually flexuous and ascending, branched basally or unbranched; branches and leaves opposite. Leaves ovate-lanceolate or oblong, pinnatipartite. Flowers axillary. Calyx 1/3 cleft anteriorly. Corolla tube more than 2 X as long as calyx; beak of galea robust.
