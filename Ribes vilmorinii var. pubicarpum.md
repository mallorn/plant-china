Ovary and fruit pubescent.

> * &lt;I&gt;Abies&lt;/I&gt; forests; ca. 4000 m. W Sichuan (Kangding Xian).

var. pubicarpum L. T. Lu in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 31 (5): 458. fig. 2. 1-4. 1933.
