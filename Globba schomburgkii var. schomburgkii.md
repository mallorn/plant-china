双翅舞花姜 (原变种)",
3a.**Globba schomburgkii var. schomburgkii**",

## Description
Inflorescence branched and thyrsoid distally, 5--11 cm; bulblets ovoid. Flowers not reduced. Fl. Aug--Sep. 2&lt;I&gt; n&lt;/I&gt; = 48.

> Forests; ca. 1300 m. S Yunnan [Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt; Globba chinensis&lt;/I&gt; K. Schumann.
