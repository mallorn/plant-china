## Description
Stems slender. Leaves petiolate. Calyx obconical-campanulate. Corolla slightly longer than calyx, tube included.

> * Dry sandy areas, waste fields; 0-500 m. Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Stachys leptopoda&lt;/I&gt; Hayata, Icon. Pl. Formos. 8: 93. 1919; &lt;I&gt;S&lt;/I&gt;. &lt;I&gt;oblongifolia&lt;/I&gt; Bentham f. &lt;I&gt;leptopoda&lt;/I&gt; (Hayata) Kudo.
**9b.细柄变种 臭草（广西）**

var. leptopoda (Hayata) C. Y. Wu in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 10 (3) : 222. 1965——Stachys leptopoda Hayata Icon. Pl. Formos. 8: 93. 1919——Stachys oblongifolia Benth. f. leptopoda (Hayata) Kudo in Mem. Fac. Sci. Agr. Taihoku Unirv. 2: 188. 1929.

这一变种与原变种不同在于茎较纤细；叶较小，几全具柄；花萼倒圆锥状钟形；花冠较小，略超出萼，冠筒内藏。

产台湾，福建，广东，广西，四川及云南；生于干燥沙地及荒田中，海拔500米以下。越南北部有分布。模式标本采自台湾。
