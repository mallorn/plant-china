## Description
Branchlets yellow-brown villous. Leaflets yellow tomentose on both surfaces, densely so abaxially, apex shortly acuminate, acumen 3-10 mm.
  "Statistics": "Mountain slopes, valley forests, thickets; 100-1000 m. Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Yunnan [Cambodia, Laos, Thailand, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Illigera dunniana* H. Léveillé, Repert. Spec. Nov. Regni Veg. 2: 326. 1911; *I. fordii* Gagnepain; *I. glandulosa* Gagnepain.

与原变种不同在于枝被黄褐色长柔毛；小叶长达7-16厘米，宽4-9厘米，先端短渐尖，长0.3-1厘米，纸质，两面被黄色绒毛，背面较密，叶柄及小叶柄密被金黄褐绒毛，背面较密，叶柄及小叶柄密被金黄褐色绒毛。

产云南、贵州、广西、广东等省区。生于海拔120-1000 米的山坡、谷地密林或灌丛中。越南、老挝、泰国、柬埔寨有分布。
