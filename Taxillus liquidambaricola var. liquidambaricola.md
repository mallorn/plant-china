阆阚果寄生(原变种)",
7a.**Taxillus liquidambaricola var. liquidambaricola**",

> Recorded hosts include *Camellia* *oleifera*, *Diospyros* *kaki*, and *Liquidambar* *formosana*.

## Description
Leaf blade ovate-oblong to oblong, 5-10 × 2.5-5.5 cm, lateral veins slightly prominent. Umbels 2-4-flowered. Corolla red, 2.4-2.8 cm. Berry 6-9 × 3-5 mm. Fl. Jul-Nov, fr. Aug-Dec.

> * Forests, tea plantations; 300-700 m. Taiwan.
  "Synonym": "*Loranthus* *liquidambaricola* Hayata, Icon. Pl. Formosan. 6: 38. 1916; *Scurrula* *liquidambaricola* (Hayata) Danser; *Taxillus* *limprichtii* (Grüning) H. S. Kiu var. *liquidambaricola* (Hayata) H. S. Kiu.
