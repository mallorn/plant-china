岗柃(原变种)",
33a.**Eurya groffii var. groffii**",

## Description
Leaf blade secondary veins adaxially obscure to slightly impressed. Ovary glabrous. Fl. Sep-Nov, fr. Apr.

> Forests or thickets; 300-2700 m. S Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, S Guizhou, Hainan, E and W Sichuan, SE Xizang, NW and SE Yunnan [Myanmar, N Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Eurya acuminata* Candolle var. *groffii* (Merrill) Kobuski; *Myrsine cavaleriei* H. Léveillé (1912), not *E. cavaleriei* H. Léveillé (1911).
