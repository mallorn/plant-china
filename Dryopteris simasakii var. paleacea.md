## Description
Scales on upper part of stipe persistent, rachis and pinna rachis with both broadly lanceolate and linear-lanceolate scales. Lamina bipinnate, pinnules pinnately lobed.

> Subtropical broad-leaved evergreen forests. Guangxi, Guizhou, Sichuan, Yunnan [Japan].
