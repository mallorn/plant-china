## Description
Rhizome very short. Culms 19-22 cm tall, obtusely trigonous, slightly thick, smooth, clothed at base with brown sheaths disintegrating into fibers. Leaves longer than culm, blades linear, 3-5 mm wide, slightly curved or plicate, apex acuminate. Involucral bracts shortly bladed, shorter than subtending spike, long sheathing. Spikes 4 or 5, approximate, lowest one often distant; terminal spike male, brown, narrowly cylindric, 2.5-3 cm; lateral spikes female, erect, cylindric, 2.5-5 cm, densely many flowered, sessile or with short peduncles. Female glumes pale green, oblong-lanceolate, green 3-veined costa excurrent into a mucro. Utricles green-brown, longer and broader than glume, obliquely patent, lanceolate-ovate, subtrigonous, 4.5-5 mm, membranous, sparsely hispid, many veined, base cuneate, apex gradually tapering into a conic long beak, orifice 2-toothed. Nutlets tightly enveloped, elliptic, trigonous, faces concave at base, apex abruptly contracted into a short beak, beak annulate at orifice; style base thickened; stigmas 3. Fr. Apr.

**261. 秦岭薹草（秦岭植物志）图版71：9-12**

Carex diplodon Nelmes in Kew Bull. 1939; 184, 1939; 秦岭植物志1 (1): 271, 图240, 1976.

根状茎极短。秆高19-22厘米，钝三棱形，稍粗壮，坚硬，平滑，基部具褐色分裂成纤维状的老叶鞘。叶长于秆，宽3-5毫米，微弯曲，平张或基部对折，边缘粗糙，淡绿色，稍坚硬，向顶端渐尖。苞片短叶状，短于小穗，具长鞘。小穗4-5个，彼此靠近，最下部1个常远离，顶生1个雄性，窄圆柱形，棕色，长2.5-3厘米；侧生的雌性，直立，圆柱状，长2.5-5厘米，花密生，下部的小穗具柄。雌花鳞片长圆状披针形，白绿色，背面3脉绿色并延伸为短尖。果囊长于和宽于鳞片，斜展，披针状卵形，近三棱状，长4.5-5毫米，膜质，被稀疏糙硬毛，具多条脉，绿褐色，中部以下狭窄，上部渐狭成稍向外弯的长喙，喙圆锥状圆筒形，近于平喙，喙口具2齿。小坚果紧包于果囊中，椭圆形，三棱形，棱面下部凹陷，顶部骤缩成短喙，喙顶端稍膨大；花柱基部增粗，柱头3个。果期4月。

产陕西、甘肃；生于山谷和山坡岩石上，海拔1100-1600米。模式标本采自甘肃东南部。
