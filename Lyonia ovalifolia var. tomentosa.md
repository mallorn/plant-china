绒毛珍珠花",
1f.**Lyonia ovalifolia var. tomentosa** (W. P. Fang) C. Y. Wu",

## Description
Twigs, petioles, leaf blades abaxially, and inflorescences densely tomentose. Leaf blade elliptic, 4–8 × 2.5–5 cm, adaxially pubescent, base obtuse to rounded, apex abruptly acuminate. Inflorescences with leaflike bracts. Filaments with 2 ?distinct spurs. Capsule unknown.

> Ravines; ca. 1700 m. NC Yunnan (Yongren).
