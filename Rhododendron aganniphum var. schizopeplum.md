裂毛雪山杜鹃",
365c.**Rhododendron aganniphum var. schizopeplum** (I. B. Balfour & Forrest) T. L. Ming",

## Description
Leaf blade 6–8 × 3–3.5 cm; abaxial surface indumentum 2-layered, upper layer pale brown, splitting into an irregular netlike pattern to reveal white compact under layer, without pellicle. Fl. Jun.

> Forest margins, thickets, mountain slopes; 3500–4500 m. SE Xizang, NW Yunnan.
**158c. 裂毛雪山杜鹃（变种）（新拟）裂毛海绵杜鹃（西藏植物志、云南植物志）**

Edinb. 39: 356. 1982, pro parte, quoad syn. R. schizopeplum Balf. f. et Forrest, R. fissotectum Balf. f. et Forrest et R. doshongense Tagg.

本变种与原变种的区别在于叶片下面毛被薄，多少粘结，两层，上层毛被淡棕色，老时呈网隙分裂，显出白色紧密的下层毛被。花期6月。

产云南西北部和西藏东南部。生于海拔3 500-4 560米的高山针叶林缘或灌丛中。模式标本采自云南德钦。
