无腺里白悬钩子",
18c.**Rubus sachalinensis var. eglandulatus** (Y. B. Chang) L. T. Lu",

## Description
Pedicel and abaxial surface of calyx without glandular hairs. Flowers and fruit less than 1 cm in diam. Indumentum of aggregate fruit unknown.

> Forests, slopes; 400--500 m. Heilongjiang (Yichuan), S Jilin (Fusong Xian).
  "Synonym": "*Rubus matsumuranus* H. Léveillé &amp; Vaniot var. *eglandulatus* Y. B. Chang, Bull. Bot. Res., Harbin 1(3): 97. 1981.
