小鸢尾 (原变种)
44a.**Iris proantha var. proantha**

## Description
Leaves 5--20 cm × 1--2.5 mm at anthesis, 40--50 cm × ca. 7 mm in fruit. Flowering stems 5--7 cm. Flowers 3.5--4 in diam. 2&lt;I&gt; n&lt;/I&gt; = 32*, 44*.


> * Forest margins, open groves, hillsides, grasslands. Anhui, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Zhejiang.

  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt; Iris pseudorossii&lt;/I&gt; S. S. Chien.
