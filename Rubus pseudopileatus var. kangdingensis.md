## Description
Pedicel glabrous, with dense needle-like prickles and sparse, stalked glands. Calyx abaxially glabrous.

> C Sichuan (Kangding Xian).
**22c.康定假帽莓（变种）**

Rubus pseudopileatus Card. var. kangdingensis Yu et Lu,植物分类学报20(3): 298. 1982.

叶柄和花梗均无毛，但有较密针刺和稀疏腺毛；花萼外面无毛，仅内萼片边缘具绒毛。

产四川（康定）。
