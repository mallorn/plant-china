毛枝翼核果",
5b.**Ventilago calyculata var. trichoclada** Y. L. Chen & P. K. Chou",

## Description
Young branches, petioles, and veins on abaxial leaf surface densely yellow-brown pubescent. Leaf blade ovate-elliptic, margin sparsely serrulate.

> ●  Forests; ca. 600 m. NW Guangxi (Tianlin).
**5b.毛枝翼核果（变种）（东北林学院植物研究室汇刊）**

Ventilago calyculata Tulasne var. trichoclada Y. L. Chen et P. K. Chou in Bull. Bot. Lab. North-East. Forest. Inst. 5: 90. 1979.

与原变种区别在于：叶卵状椭圆形，边缘具疏细锯齿，当年生枝和叶下面被黄褐色细柔毛，叶柄被黄褐色密柔毛。

产广西田林，海拔600米。
