## Description
Stem and raceme appressed puberulent, without glandular hairs. Inflorescence racemose. Staminode limb entire or emarginate. Carpels densely puberulent. Fl. May--Oct.

> Sparse forests, grassy slopes; 500--2800 m. Hebei, Heilongjiang, W Henan, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, E Qinghai, N Shaanxi, Shanxi, NW Sichuan [Mongolia, Russia (Siberia)].
