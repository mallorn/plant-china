矮蝇子草",
45.**Silene nana** Karelin & Kirilov",

## Description
Herbs annual, 3--15 cm, glabrous. Stems erect, simple or much branched at base. Leaves sessile, lanceolate-linear or lanceolate, 2--3.5 cm × 2--6 mm, margin ciliate with curved hairs, base cuneate, apex acute or obtuse. Dichasial cymes (1-to) few flowered. Pedicel subequaling to 3 × as long as calyx, usually curved at late anthesis; bracts lanceolate. Calyx greenish, ovoid-campanulate, ca. 10 × 3.5 mm, umbilicate at base; calyx teeth triangular-lanceolate, ca. 1/4 as long as calyx, margin narrowly membranous, shortly ciliate, apex acute. Androgynophore 1--2 mm, glabrous. Petals slightly exserted beyond calyx, white, ca. 1.1 cm; claws narrowly oblanceolate, glabrous, auricles triangular; limbs subovate, ca. 2.5 mm, margin entire; coronal scales narrowly ovoid, ca. 1/2 as long as limbs, margin entire, rarely laciniate. Stamens and styles slightly exserted; filaments glabrous. Capsule ovoid, ca. 8 mm, shorter than calyx. Seeds orbicular-reniform, flat, ca. 2 mm in diam., abaxially grooved with undulate wings. Fl. Apr--May, fr. Jun.

> Sand dunes, sandy steppes. N Xinjiang [Kazakhstan, Pakistan; SW Asia].

一年生小草本，高3-15厘米，全株无毛。茎直立，不分枝或基部具开展的分枝。叶片披针状线形或披针形，长2-3.5厘米，宽2-6毫米，基部楔形，无柄，顶端急尖或钝，边缘具卷曲柔毛。二歧聚伞花序具数花，稀减少为单花；花梗与花萼几等长或长2-3倍，斜展，后期常下弯；苞片披针形；花萼卵状钟形，长约10毫米，直径约3.5毫米，淡绿色，基部脐状，萼齿三角状披针形，长为花萼的1/4，顶端急尖，边缘狭膜质，具短缘毛；雌雄蕊柄长1-2毫米，无毛；花瓣白色，长约11毫米，微露出花萼，爪狭倒披针形，无毛，耳三角形，瓣片轮廓近卵形，长约2.5毫米，全缘；副花冠片狭卵形，长为瓣片1/2，全缘，稀具缺刻；雄蕊微外露，花丝无毛。蒴果卵形，长约8毫米，比宿存萼短；种子圆肾形，压扁，脊具皱波状翅，连翅直径约2毫米。花期4-5月，果期6月。

产新疆（准噶尔盆地和伊犁河谷）。生于沙丘或沙质草原。伊朗、巴基斯坦西部和哈萨克斯坦也有。模式标本采自准噶尔。
