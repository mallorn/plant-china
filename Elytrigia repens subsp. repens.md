偃麦草(原亚种)",
1a.**Elytrigia repens subsp. repens**",

## Description
Glumes 10–15 mm, apex usually with point 1–2 mm. Lemma ca. 12 mm, apex acuminate or with awn to 2 mm. Fl. and fr. Jul–Aug. 2*n* = 42*.

> Pastures, meadows, waste places. Gansu, Hebei, Heilongjiang, Nei Mongol, Qinghai, Shandong, Sichuan, Xinjiang, Xizang, Yunnan [India, Japan, Korea, Mongolia, Russia; C and SW Asia, Europe; introduced in North America].
  "Synonym": "*Triticum repens* Linnaeus, Sp. Pl. 1: 86. 1753; *Agropyron junceum* (Linnaeus) P. Beauvois var. *repens* (Linnaeus) M. Marsson; *A. repens* (Linnaeus) P. Beauvois; *Braconotia offici-narum* Godron, nom. illeg. superfl.; *Elymus repens* (Linnaeus) Gould; *T. infestum* Salisbury, nom. illeg. superfl.; *Zeia repens* (Linnaeus) Lunell.
