竹节参(原变种)",
1a.**Panax japonicus var. japonicus**",

> *\"Panax quinquefolius* var. *japonicus\"* (Siebold, Verh. Batav. Genootsch. Kunsten 12: 45. 1830) belongs here but is a nomen nudum.

## Description
Rootstock flagellate, like rhizome of bamboo. Leaflets not 2-pinnatifid, obovate-elliptic to oblong, 2.5-3 × as long as wide, apex long acuminate. Fl. May-Jun, fr. Jul-Sep.

> Forests; 1200-3600 m. S Anhui, N Fujian, Gansu, Guangxi, Guizhou, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan, Zhejiang [Japan, Korea, Vietnam].

[under *Panax schin-seng*]. 1833; *Aralia quinquefolia* (Linnaeus) Decaisne &amp; Planchon var. *repens* (Maximo wicz) Burkill; *P. pseudoginseng* Wallich subsp. *japonicus* (T. Nees) H. Hara; *P. pseudoginseng* var. *japonicus* (T. Nees) G. Hoo &amp; C. J. Tseng; *P. repens* Maximowicz.
