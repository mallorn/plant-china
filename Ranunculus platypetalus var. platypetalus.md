大瓣毛茛(原变种)",
9a.**Ranunculus platypetalus var. platypetalus**",

## Description
Basal leaves 0.7--1.2 cm wide; Stem leaves distinctly petiolate, 3-partite or 3-sect. Flowers 1--1.4 cm in diam. Receptacle sparsely puberulent.

> * Grassy slopes; ca. 4100 m. NW Yunnan (Zhongdian Xian).
