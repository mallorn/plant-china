小叶野山楂",
5b.**Crataegus cuneata var. tangchungchangii** (F. P. Metcalf) T. C. Ku & Spongberg",

## Description
Leaf blade obovate-elliptic, 2–3(–4) × 1–1.5(–2) cm, both surfaces glabrous, base attenuate, margin serrate in apical part, not lobed.

> Thickets; 200--1500 m. Fujian (Changluo, Fuqing Xian, Fuzhou Shi).
