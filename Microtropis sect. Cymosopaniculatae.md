## Description
Inflorescences generally with prolonged rachises, thyrses arranged in dichasia, branching peduncles long and obvious, inflorescence loose. Flowers 5-merous.
  "Statistics": "About ten species: mainly in SE Asia; three species (all endemic) in China.
  "Synonym": "*Microtropis* ser. *Cymosopaniculatae* Merrill &amp; F. L. Freeman, Proc. Amer. Acad. Arts 73: 304. 1940.
