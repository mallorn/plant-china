直叶香柏",
6c.**Juniperus pingii var. carinata** Y. F. Yu & L. K. Fu",

## Description
Shrubs procumbent or erect to 4 m, rarely small trees; branchlets not pendulous, stout, usually not 6-angled. Leaves ascending, straight or slightly arched, 4-7 mm.

> *  Montane forests and thickets; 2700-4500 m. S Gansu, S Shaanxi, Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan
