西南乌头(原变种)",
121a.**Aconitum episcopale var. episcopale**",

## Description
Pedicels appressed white pubescent or subglabrous. Sepals abaxially white pubescent.

> * Mountains, forests; 2400--3200 m. W Guizhou, SW Sichuan, N Yunnan.
