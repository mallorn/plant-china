芒(艹恰)草(原亚种)",
1a.**Koeleria litvinowii subsp. litvinowii**",

> This subspecies tends to have a more northwestern distribution than subsp. *argentea*.

## Description
Perennial forming small dense tussocks. Culms tinged purple, tomentose below panicle. Leaf sheaths densely pubescent. Panicle grayish violet. Lemma apex acute.

> Grassy or stony mountain slopes, alpine meadows; 3000–5200 m. Gansu, Qinghai, Sichuan, Xinjiang, Xizang, Yunnan [E Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan (Pamirs)].
  "Synonym": "*Koeleria enodis* Keng; *K. hosseana* Domin; *K. hosseana* var. *tafelii* Domin; *K. litvinowii* var. *tafelii* (Domin) P. C. Kuo &amp; Z. L. Wu;* Trisetum litvinowii* (Domin) Nevski.
