大叶柳叶栒子",
1c.**Cotoneaster salicifolius var. henryanus** (C. K. Schneider) T. T. Yü",

## Description
Leaf blade elliptic-oblong, adaxially pilose, veins slightly impressed. Fruit ovoid, 5–7 mm in diam. 2n = 34*.

> Open places; 700--1900 m. W Hubei, Sichuan.
  "Synonym": "*Cotoneaster rugosus* E. Pritzel var. *henryanus* C. K. Schneider, Ill. Handb. Laubholzk. 1: 758. 1906; *C. henryanus* (C. K. Schneider) Rehder &amp; E. H. Wilson.
**柳叶栒子大叶变种**

C. salicifolius Franch. var. henryanus (Schneid.) Yu, stat. nov.——C. rugosa var. henryana Schneid. Ill. Handb. Laubh. 1: 758. 1906.——C. henryana (Schneid.) Rehd. & Wils. in Sarg. Pl. Wils. 1: 174. 1912; Klotz in Wiss. Zeits. Univ. Halle 6: 958. 1957.

A. Rehder & E. H. Wilson 将湖北西部和四川东部所采的柳叶栒子划分为一独立种，命名为 C. henryanus Rehd. & Wils., 其区别点在于叶片稍宽大，上面具柔毛，枝条密被柔毛，果实卵形，这些特征在本种中变异颇大，列入本种的变种较为合理，故更名如上。

产湖北、四川。模式标本采自湖北西部。
