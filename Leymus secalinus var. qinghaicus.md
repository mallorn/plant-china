## Description
Culms 18–45 cm tall, 2- or 3-noded. Leaf blade abaxial surface scabrous or smooth, adaxial surface scabrous or pubescent. Spike brown or brownish, 6–10 cm. Glumes narrowly lanceolate, usually 1-veined. Palea shorter than lemma.

> * Mountain slopes; 2900–3100 m. Qinghai.
