## Description
Mostly trees; branches succulent, often striate. Leaves alternate, usually short-lived; stipules glandular. Cyathia clustered at stem apex, small, unisexual; cyathophylls very inconspicuous.
  "Statistics": "About ten species as here defined: most species in S Africa and Madagascar, with an outlying species on Socotra and one species (*E. tirucalli*) now widespread throughout the tropics; one species (introduced) in China.
  "Synonym": "*Euphorbia* sect. *Tirucalli* Boissier in A. Candolle, Prodr. 15(2): 94. 1862.
