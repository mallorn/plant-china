## Description
Plants to 60 cm tall, &amp;#177; pubescent. Bracts 2--3 X as long as flowers. Corolla 1.3--1.6 cm; galea rose to purple; lower lip slightly longer than galea. Fl. Jun--Aug.

> * Alpine meadows; 2700--4300 m. W Sichuan, S and SE Xizang, NW Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Pedicularis densispica&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;schneideri&lt;/I&gt; Bonati, Notes Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinburgh 13: 133. 1921.
**密穗马先蒿许氏亚种**

Pedicularis densispica Franch. subsp. schneideri (Bonati) Tsoong, comb. nov. ——P. densispica Franch. var. schneideri Bonati, in Notes Bot. Gard. Edinb. XIII (1921) 133, et XV (1926) 150; Limpr. in Repert. Sp. Nov. XX (1924) 210: Hand.-Mazz. Symb. Sin. VII (1936) 850; Li, in Proc. Acad. Nat. Sci. Philad. C (1948) 268.

这一亚种与前一亚种之别，在于其植株较高，达60厘米，苞片伸展或反曲，长于花2-3倍；花冠下唇略长于盔。

产我国四川西部与云南西北部，经西藏昌都专区而达拉隆附近。生于海拔2,750-4,270米的高山草原中。
