大明凤尾蕨",
47b.**Pteris decrescens var. parviloba** (Christ) C. Christensen & Tardieu Notul. Syst. (Paris). 6: 137. 1937.",

## Description
Lateral pinnae 12-14 pairs, ca. 7 × 1.3 cm, narrowly linear caudate apex 3-4 cm; basal pair almost not shortened.

> Guangxi (Daming Shan) [N Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Pteris parviloba* Christ, Bull. Acad. Int. Géogr. Bot. 1907: 149. 1907.
**38b. 大明凤尾蕨（变种）（新拟）**

Pteris decrescens Christ in Bull. var. parviloba (Christ) C. Chr. et Tard.-Blot in Lecomte, Not. Syst. 6:137. 1937; Tard.-Blot et C. Chr. in Fl. Indo-Chine 7 (2):153. 1940.

侧生羽片长7厘米，基部宽1.3厘米，狭披针形，先端尾头长3-4厘米，基部一对羽片几不缩短。

产广西（大明山)。模式标本采自越南北部。
