杉木(原变种)",
1a.**Cunninghamia lanceolata var. lanceolata**",

## Description
Abaxial stomatal bands of leaves with (14-)16-28 rows of stomata. Seed cones 2.5-4.5 ×  2.5-4 cm.

> Forests, rocky hillsides, roadsides; 200-2800 m. Exact native distribution uncertain owing to widespread planting: Anhui, Fujian, Gansu, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hainan, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan, Zhejiang [?Cambodia, ?Laos, N Vietnam]
