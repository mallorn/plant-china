黑紫荛花",
49b.**Wikstroemia leptophylla var. atroviolacea** Handel-Mazzetti",

## Description
Leaves usually alternate; leaf blade firm, adaxially black-purple when dried, lateral veins conspicuous. Inflorescences much branched. Calyx exterior deep brown-purple to almost black, interior yellowish green; tube ca. 10 mm.

> ● Shrubby slopes, on limestone; below 2400 m. Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Daphne leptophylla* (W. W. Smith) Halda var. *atrovio* *lacea* (Handel-Mazzetti) Halda.
