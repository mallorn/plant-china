> Zhou &amp; Wu (Acta Bot. Yunnan. 23: 29–36. 2001) treated this variety as a synonym of *Cotoneaster buxifolius*.

## Description
Leaf blade 8–10 × 6–8 mm, apex obtuse or slightly mucronate. Fruit usually with 3 pyrenes.

> Rocky places.  W Sichuan.
