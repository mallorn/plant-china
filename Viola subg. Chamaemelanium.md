## Description
Plant with aerial stem. Stipules free, small. Flowers yellow, rarely white or with violet or brown upper petals. Anterior petal spurred. Style gradually globose, incrassate and not beaked at apex; stigmas on front side of style incrassation, on hemispheric projection.
  "Statistics": "About 26 species: NE Asia, North America; three species in China.
  "Synonym": "*Viola* sect. *Chamaemelanium* Gingins, Mem. Fam. Viol. 1. 1823.
