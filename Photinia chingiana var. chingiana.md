宜山石楠(原变种)",
12a.**Photinia chingiana var. chingiana**",

## Description
Leaf blade oblong-elliptic to obovate-oblong, abaxially tomentose-villous, margin sparsely minutely incurved-serrate apically, subentire or with few inconspicuous teeth basally.

> Evergreen broad-leaved forests, open forests, valley thickets, river banks; below 1200 m. Guangxi, Guizhou.
  "Synonym": "*Photinia austroguizhouensis* Y. K. Li; *P. simplex* Y. K. Li &amp; X. M. Wang.
