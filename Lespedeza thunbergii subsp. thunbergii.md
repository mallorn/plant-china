日本胡枝子(原亚种)",
4a.**Lespedeza thunbergii subsp. thunbergii**",

## Description
Shrubs or perennial herbs. Lateral calyx lobes usually 1-1.5 × as long as calyx tube.
  "Statistics": "Mountain slopes, forest margins, roadsides, thickets; below 2800 m. Anhui, Fujian, Gansu, Guangdong, Hebei, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Shaanxi, Shandong, Zhejiang [India, Japan, Korea].
  "Synonym": "*Desmodium thunbergii* Candolle, Prodr. 2: 337. 1825; *D. penduliflorum* Oudemans; *Lespedeza bicolor* Turczaninow var. *velutina* Nakai; *L. formosa* (Vogel) Koehne subsp. *velutina* (Nakai) S. Akiyama &amp; H. Ohba; *L. formosa* var. *velutina* (Nakai) S. Akiyama &amp; H. Ohba; *L. intermedia* Nakai var. *angustifolia* Nakai; *L. japonica* L. H. Bailey f. *angustifolia* (Nakai) Murata; *L. liukiuensis* Hatusima; *L. penduliflora* (Oudemans) Nakai; *L. sieboldii* Miquel.
