南洋厚壁蕨",
18.**Hymenophyllum holochilum** (Bosch) C. Christensen Index Filic. 362. 1905.",

## Description
Plants 5-10 cm tall. Rhizome long creeping, filiform, slender, sparsely hairy. Stipe brown, ca. 3 cm, slender, sparsely hairy, very narrowly winged. Rachis hairy on lower surface, usually winged throughout, wings entire. Lamina bipinnate to tripinnatifid, lanceolate to oblong-lanceolate, 4-6 × 1.5-2 cm; pinnae 5-7 pairs, closely spaced, alternate, subsessile, broadly ovate, oblique, unequally cuneate at base, with 3-6 segments; ultimate segments simple, rarely forked, closely spaced, linear, with a single veinlet, margin sparsely toothed, apex obtuse. Veins dichotomous, brown, obvious on each surface. Sori large, apical on short acroscopic segments; involucres ovate to elliptic, narrowly winged, cleft to 1/3-1/2, lips entire and slightly revolute; receptacles projecting.

> On trees in tropical evergreen forests; 700-1400 m. Taiwan [Malesia, Thailand; Pacific islands (Melanesia, Micronesia, Polynesia)].

植株高5-10厘米。根状茎纤细，丝状，长而横走，褐色，被疏毛。叶远生，相距约1-2厘米；叶柄长约3厘米，纤细，褐色，被疏毛，两侧有很狭的翅；叶片披针形至长圆状披针形，长4-6厘米，宽1.5-2厘米，二回羽裂；羽片5-7对，互生，几无柄，斜向上，阔卵形，长约1厘米，宽4-6毫米，基部斜楔形，彼此接近；裂片3-6个，互生，极斜向上，线形，单一或很少分叉，密接，钝头，边缘有疏的尖齿，稍呈浅波浪状。叶脉叉状分枝，褐色，两面均明显，末回裂片有小脉1条。叶为薄膜质，半透明，干后为绿褐色，叶面平坦。叶轴褐色，下部圆柱形而有极狭的边，上部两侧有全缘的翅。孢子囊羣大，生于羽片腋间向轴的短裂片顶端；囊苞卵形至椭圆形，管部光滑，两侧有翅，口部开裂1/3-1/2成为两唇瓣，唇瓣全缘并稍为外捲；囊羣托突出，刺毛状。

产于台湾。马来亚及印度尼西亚（爪哇）也有分布。
