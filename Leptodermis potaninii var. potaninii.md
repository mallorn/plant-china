野丁香(原变种)",
20c.**Leptodermis potaninii var. potaninii**",

## Description
Leaves white pubescent on both surfaces, though usually subglabrous and pale abaxially.
  "Statistics": "● Thickets on hill slopes; 800-2400 m. W Guizhou, W Hubei (Shennongjia, Xingshan), S Shaanxi (Pingli), W Sichuan (Kangding), Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Leptodermis nigricans* H. J. P. Winkler.
