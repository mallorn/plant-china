保山翠雀花",
79d.**Delphinium delavayi var. baoshanense** (W. T. Wang) W. T. Wang",

## Description
Stem and petioles retrorsely hispidulous; hairs 0.5--2 mm. Leaf blade central lobe rhombic. Raceme strigose only, without yellow, glandular hairs. Filaments glabrous.

> * Grassy slopes; 2000--2400 m. W Yunnan (Baoshan Xian).
