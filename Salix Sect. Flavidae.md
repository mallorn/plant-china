黄柳组",
37.**Salix Sect. Flavidae** Y. L. Chang & Skvortzov",

## Description
Shrubs. Branchlets yellow, shiny. Leaf blade linear-lanceolate, glabrous, margin glandular serrate. Flowering precocious. Bracts 2-colored. Male flower: glands adaxial; stamens 2, distinct; filaments glabrous. Female flower: ovary glabrous or subglabrous, sessile; style short. Capsule glabrous.
