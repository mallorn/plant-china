## Description
Leaf blade obovate-oblong, base attenuate, apex acute or obtuse. Catkin bracts ovate, apex truncate. Male flower: gland 1, adaxial, square.

> *  Woodlands on mountain slopes; 2600-3000 m. N Sichuan
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Salix apatela&lt;/I&gt; C. K. Schneider in Sargent, Pl. Wilson. 3: 46. 1916.
