喜马红景天(原亚种)",
25a.**Rhodiola himalensis subsp. himalensis**",

## Description
Flowering stems 25-50 cm. Stem leaves lanceolate, oblanceolate, oblong-oblanceolate, or obovate, 1.7-2.7 × 0.4-1 cm. Carpels erect, ca. 9 mm.

> Forests, scrub, slopes; 3700-4200 m. NW Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, Nepal, Sikkim].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Sedum himalense&lt;/I&gt; D. Don, Prodr. Fl. Nepal. 212. 1825; &lt;I&gt;Chamaerhodiola himalensis&lt;/I&gt; (D. Don) Nakai; &lt;I&gt;S. quadrifidum&lt;/I&gt; Pallas var. &lt;I&gt;himalense&lt;/I&gt; (D. Don) Fröderström.
