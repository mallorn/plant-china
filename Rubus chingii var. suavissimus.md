甜茶",
88b.**Rubus chingii var. suavissimus** (S. Lee) L. T. Lu",

> The leaves are rich in sugar and are used to make a sweet tea in S China.

## Description
Leaves palmately 5–7-parted, rarely 6- or 8-parted. Flowers 3–5 cm in diam. Petals 1.4–2.5 cm.

> Broad-leaved evergreen forests on hills, coniferous forests, thickets; 500--1000 m. EC Guangxi (Guiping Xian, Jinxiu Yaozu Zizhixian).
  "Synonym": "*Rubus suavissimus* S. Lee, Guihaia 1(4): 17. 1981.
