台北堇菜(原变种)",
31a.**Viola nagasawae var. nagasawae**",

## Description
Leaf blade ovate or elliptic, apex obtuse, abaxially glabrous; sepals sparsely fimbriate-ciliate along margin; lateral petals beardless. Fl. Mar-Nov, fr. May-Nov. 2*n* = 48*.

> ● Moist places on grassy slopes; 200-1100 m. Taiwan.
  "Synonym": "*Viola acutilabella* Hayata; *V. nagasawae* var. *acutilabella* (Hayata) Nakai.
