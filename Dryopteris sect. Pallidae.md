## Description
Lamina 2-pinnate to 3-pinnatifid. Lamina fertile on upper portion, sterile on lower portion. Indusia thick, entire on margin.
  "Statistics": "About 17 species: NW Africa, Asia, Australia, N Europe, North America; 11 species (three endemic) in China.
