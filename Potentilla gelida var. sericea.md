绢毛耐寒委陵菜",
79b.**Potentilla gelida var. sericea** T. T. Yu & C. L. Li",

## Description
Flowering stems spreading, together with petioles, both surfaces of leaflets, and sepals sericeous. Fl. Jun.

> Alpine meadows; ca. 3200 m. Xinjiang.
**76b.绢毛耐寒委陵菜（变种）**

P. gelida C. A. Mey. var. sericea Yu et Li,植物分类学报18(l):13.图版4. 图4. 1980.

植株铺散，花茎、叶柄和小叶上下两面及萼片均被绢状柔毛，花数朵集生于茎顶端。花期6月。

产我国新疆。生高山草甸，海拔3150米。
