乌苏里瓦韦(原变种)",
6a.**Lepisorus ussuriensis var. ussuriensis**",

## Description
Rhizome scales with apex extended into long awn. Lamina 15-25 × 1-1.5 cm, abaxially glabrous, apex shortly acuminate or obtuse.

> In rock crevices in forests or shaded slopes; 700-1700 m. Anhui, Hebei, Heilongjiang, Henan, Jilin, Liaoning, Shandong [Japan, Korea, Russia].
  "Synonym": "*Pleopeltis ussuriensis* Regel &amp; Maack, Mém. Acad. Imp. Sci. Saint Pétersbourg, Sér. 7, 4: 175. 1861; *Polypodium lineare* N. L. Burman var. *ussuriense* (Regel &amp; Maack) C. Christensen; *P. ussuriense* (Regel &amp; Maack) Regel.
