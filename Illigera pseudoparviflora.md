## Description
Lianas. Stem angulate, glabrous. Leaves 3-foliolate; peti- ole 4-5 cm; leaflets: petiolules 0.5-1.2 cm, adaxially yellow-brown pubescent on midvein; blade blackish on both surfaces when dry, lanceolate to broadly lanceolate, 6-8 × 2-2.5 cm, thickly papery, abaxially yellow-brown villous at leaf axils, adaxially slightly nitid and glabrous, lateral veins 3- or 4-paired, very conspicuous abaxially, slightly conspicuous adaxially, base cuneate to broadly cuneate, apex caudate-acuminate, acumen 1-1.2 cm. Cymose panicles axillary or terminal, robust, shorter than leaves, densely yellow-brown pubescent; rachis 3-4 cm. Flowers green. Outer tepals oblong, 6-7 mm, 5-veined. Inner tepals similar to outer ones but shorter, 3-veined. Stamens 4.5-5 mm; filaments straight in bud; appendages small, solid. Ovary densely yellow-brown pubescent, tetragonous; style 3-4 mm, densely yellow-brown villous. Interstaminal glands 5, small. Fruit unknown. Fl. May.
**13.尾叶青藤 图版128: 2-4**

Illigera pseudoparviflora Y. R. Li in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 17 (2): 77, f. 2: 4-6. 1979.

藤本。茎具棱，光滑无毛。叶指状有小叶3枚；叶柄长4-5厘米，有条纹，无毛。小叶披针形至阔披针形，先端尾状渐尖，长达1-1.2厘米，基部楔形至阔楔形，厚纸质，干后变黑色，长6-8厘米，宽2-2.5厘米，上面稍有光泽，光滑无毛，下面脉腋被黄褐色长柔毛，侧脉3-4对，在上面稍明显，下面十分显著；小叶柄长0.5-1.2厘米，沿沟槽被黄褐色短柔毛。聚伞花序组成的圆锥花序腋生和顶生，粗壮，较叶为短，密被黄褐色短柔毛，花序轴长3-4厘米。花绿色；萼片长圆形，有5条脉，长6-7毫米；花瓣与萼片同形，稍短，有3条脉；雄蕊5，长4.5-5毫米，在花芽内直立，附属物小而坚实；花柱长3-4毫米，密被黄褐色柔毛，柱头波状扩大成鸡冠状；子房下位，密被黄褐色短柔毛，四棱形。果未见。花期5月。

产贵州南部（罗甸）。生于山坡路边疏林下。

本种近似小花青藤I. parviflora Dunn；但花序较粗壮，密被黄褐色短柔毛，雄蕊较花瓣为短，长4.5-5毫米，在花芽中直立而有所不同。
