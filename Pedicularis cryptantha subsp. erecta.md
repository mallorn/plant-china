312b.**Pedicularis cryptantha subsp. erecta** P. C. Tsoong",

## Description
Stems erect, ca. 7 cm tall. Leaves barely 4.5 cm. Calyx usually densely pubescent.

> * &lt;I&gt;Pinus&lt;/I&gt; forests. SE Xizang.
**隐花马先蒿直立亚种**

Pedicularis cryptantha Marq. et Shaw subsp. erecta Tsoong, in Acta Phytotax. Sinic. III (1954) 275, et in Bull. Brit. Mus. (Nat. Hist.) II, i (1955) 4.

与前一亚种之别在于其体态直立而不铺散，高约7厘米，叶较小，连柄长仅4.5厘米；萼被毛较厚。

产西藏昌都专区南部定坝南面的第阳拉山，生于松林中。
