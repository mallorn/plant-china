甘南灰栒子",
34c.**Cotoneaster acutifolius var. lucidus** (Schlechtendal) L. T. Lu",

## Description
Leaf blade villous abaxially, glabrous adaxially. Hypanthium glabrous or only slightly villous abaxially. Fruit obovoid or subglobose, 8–10 mm in diam. 2n = 68.

> Rocky slopes, thickets; below 2900 m. S Gansu [Russia].
  "Synonym": "*Cotoneaster lucidus* Schlechtendal, Linnaea 27: 541. 1854.
