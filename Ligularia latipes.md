## Description
Stem erect, to 70 cm tall, 5-8 mm in diam. at base, proximally glabrous, distally and synflorescence purplish red, shortly blackish purple pilose. Basal leaves petiolate; petiole 4-8 cm, winged; wings to 2 cm wide, margin entire or dentate; base enlarged sheathed; sheath to 11 × 4 cm, glabrous, margin entire or dentate; leaf blade cordate or ovate-cordate, 3.5-15 × 5.5-11 cm, pinnately veined, base cordate, margin sinuate-dentate, apex rounded; basal lobes orbicular. Middle stem leaves smaller, shortly petiolate, enlarged sheathed; leaf blade ovate. Synflorescence paniculate-racemose, to 10 cm, lax, proximal branches with 3 or 4 capitula; leaflike bracts linear, shorter; peduncles to 1.5 cm. Capitula numerous; supplementary bracts subulate, shorter than involucre. Involucre narrowly campanulate or cylindric, 6-8 × 4-5 mm, outside purplish red, glabrous; phyllaries 5-8, in 2 rows, oblong, white ciliate, margin membranous and pale colored, apex obtuse, triangular. Ray florets ?yellow, not seen. Tubular florets 10-15, 8-9 mm, tube slightly longer than broadly campanulate limb. Achenes dark brown, narrowly oblanceolate, 5-6 mm, attenuate at apex. Pappus rufous, 6-7 mm, slightly shorter than tubular corolla. Fl. and fr. Aug-Sep.

**91. 阔柄橐吾**

Ligularia latipes S. W. Liu, 植物研究 5: 71. 图4. 1985.

多年生草本。根多数，肉质，细瘦。茎直立，高达70厘米，上部紫红色以及花序被密的黑紫色有节短柔毛，下部光滑，基部直径5-8毫米。丛生叶及茎基部叶具柄，柄长4-8厘米，具宽达2厘米的翅，翅全缘或有齿，基部膨大成鞘，鞘长达11厘米，宽至4厘米，全缘或有锯齿，光滑，叶片心形或卵状心形，长3.5-15厘米，宽5.5-11.5厘米，先端圆形，边缘具缺刻状齿，齿端具软骨质小尖头，基部心形，两侧裂片圆形，叶脉羽状；茎中部叶较小，具短柄，有膨大的鞘，叶片卵形。圆锥状总状花序长达10厘米，疏离，下部分枝常具3-4个头状花序；苞片着生于花序梗中部，线形，较短；花序梗长至15毫米；头状花序辐射状（据记录有黄色舌状花）；小苞片钻形，比总苞短；总苞狭钟形或筒形，长6-8毫米，宽4-5毫米，总苞片5-8，2层，长圆形，先端钝，三角形，具白色睫毛，背部紫红色，光滑，边缘膜质，色浅。舌状花黄色（未见）；管状花5-10，长8-9毫米，管部稍长于檐部，檐部钟形，冠毛火红色，长6-7毫米，稍短于花冠。瘦果狭倒披针形，黑褐色，长5-6毫米，光滑，先端略狭缩。花果期8-9月。

产四川北部。生于海拔2600米的山坡。模式标本采自茂汶。

根据本种的采集记录，头状花序有黄色舌状花，但检查标本后未见舌状花，仅有管状花。
