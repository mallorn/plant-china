马兰(原变种)",

## Description
Rhizomes 0.5-18 cm. Cauline leaves oblong, oblanceolate, elliptic, or obovate, glabrous or sparsely puberulent, eglandular. Phyllaries obovate-oblong. Fl. Jun-Nov.
