美翼杯冠藤
19.**Cynanchum callialatum** Buchanan-Hamilton ex Wight

> Records of &lt;I&gt;Cynanchum alatum&lt;/I&gt; Wight &amp;amp; Arnott in China are based on a misidentification of &lt;I&gt;C. callialatum&lt;/I&gt;. &lt;I&gt;Cynanchum alatum&lt;/I&gt; appears to be restricted to India and Sri Lanka.


## Description
Herbs twining, to 3 m. Stems pubescent along 1 side. Leaves opposite; petiole 1-4 cm; leaf blade abaxially glaucous, oblong or ovate-oblong, 4.5-8 &amp;#215; 2-4 cm, slightly fleshy, base cordate, apex acuminate, basal lobes incurved, sometimes overlapping; lateral veins 6-12 pairs. Inflorescences simple, umbel-like, dense, 6-12-flowered; peduncle 1-7 mm. Pedicel 3-6 mm. Sepals lanceolate, 2-2.5 &amp;#215; 1 mm; basal glands ca. 10. Corolla whitish, drying darker than corona, rotate; tube ca. 1 mm; lobes oblong, ca. 4 &amp;#215; 1.5-2 mm. Corona white, cupular, ca. 3.5 mm, or reduced to membranous ring, apex 2-cleft, with a longitudinal ridge below each sinus. Gynostegium distinctly stipitate. Anthers &amp;#177; rhomboid, ca. 1 mm, appendages oval; pollinia oblong. Stigma head ovoid, slightly exserted. Follicles oblong-lanceolate, 5-8 &amp;#215; 1-1.5 cm, with 2 wings especially prominent at tip. Seeds oval-oblong, ca. 5 &amp;#215; 3 mm; coma ca. 2 cm. Fl. Aug-Oct, fr. Oct-Dec.


> Open woodland, thickets along mountain roadsides; 1000-1500 m. W Guangxi, Yunnan [India, Myanmar, Pakistan]

  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Cyathella callialata&lt;/I&gt; (Buchanan-Hamilton ex Wight) C. Y. Wu &amp; D. Z. Li; &lt;I&gt;Cynoctonum callialatum&lt;/I&gt; (Buchanan-Hamilton ex Wight) Decaisne; &lt;I&gt;Vincetoxicum callialatum&lt;/I&gt; (Buchanan-Hamilton ex Wight) Kuntze.
