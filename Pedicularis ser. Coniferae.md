## Description
Herbs tall. Stems branched apically; branches short, ascending; branches and leaves often in whorls of 3--5. Leaves small, pinnatisect. Inflorescences compact racemes. Flowers scarcely 6 mm. Corolla small; tube ca. as long as calyx; beak slender; middle lobe of lower lip hoodlike at apex.
