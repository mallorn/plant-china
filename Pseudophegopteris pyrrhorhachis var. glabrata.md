## Description
Lamina abaxially glabrous or with extremely short capitate hairs along rachises, costae, and costules, lacking hairs between veins.

> Forests beside streams; below 3000 m. N Guizhou, W Hubei, Sichuan, Yunnan [N India, Myanmar, SW regions of Himalaya].
  "Synonym": "*Polypodium distans* var. *glabrata* C. B. Clarke, Trans. Linn. Soc. London, Bot. 1: 544. 1880; *Phegopteris distans* var. *glabrata* (C. B. Clarke) Beddome; *P. pyrrhorhachis* var. *glabrata* (C. B. Clarke) Tagawa; *Thelypteris brunnea* var. *glabrata* (C. B. Clarke) Ching.
