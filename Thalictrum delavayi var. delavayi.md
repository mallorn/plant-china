偏翅唐松草(原变种)
11a.**Thalictrum delavayi var. delavayi**

> This variety is used medicinally.


## Description
Sepals oblong or narrowly elliptic to ovate, 6--11 × 2.2--5 mm. Anther mucro (0.01--)0.1--0.15(--0.4) mm. Fl. Jun--Sep.


> * Forests, scrub, damp rocky ledges; 1900--3400 m. W Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan.
