毛叶长蕊绣线菊",
8c.**Spiraea miyabei var. pilosula** Rehder",

## Description
Leaf blade ± pubescent on both surfaces, more densely so abaxially, base cuneate. Inflorescences and hypanthium abaxially sparsely pubescent.

> Mountain forests; 1000--1600 m. Hubei, Sichuan, Yunnan.
**长蕊绣线菊毛叶变种**

S. miyabei Koidz. var. pilosula Rehd. in Sarg. Pl. Wils. 1: 45. 1913; Hand. -Mazz. Symb. Sin. 7: 454. 1933.

叶片基部楔形，上面有稀疏柔毛，下面密被柔毛，边缘锯齿深，花萼外被稀疏柔毛。

产湖北、四川、云南。生山地林边，海拔1000-1600米。模式标本采自湖北兴山。
