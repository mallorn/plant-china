## Description
Upper culm internode sometimes elongated, especially after flowering. Ligule of upper leaf 0.8–1.5 mm. Panicle effuse, 10–12 cm. Spikelets 3–5 mm; rachilla smooth or scabrid; callus glabrous or very sparsely villous.

> * Meadows along forest margins; 1200–1600 m. Xinjiang.
