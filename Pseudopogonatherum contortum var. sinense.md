## Description
Spikelet pair with equal pedicels, rachis internode equaling pedicels; callus 0.2–0.3 mm, obtuse; upper glume with 0.5–1 mm mucro. Fl. and fr. autumn.

> * Hill slopes; ca. 700 m. Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Hainan, Jiangxi.
**1c．中华笔草（禾本科图说）图版21：8-12**
