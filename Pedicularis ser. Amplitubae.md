## Description
Herbs erect, more than 10 cm tall, villous. Stems single, rigid or flexuous. Basal leaves numerous, long petiolate. Stem leaves few, alternate or &amp;#177; opposite, smaller than basal leaves, linear-oblong, pinnatifid. Inflorescences short racemose, centripetal. Calyx deeply cleft anteriorly, 5-lobed. Corolla purple; tube ca. 2.5 X as long as calyx; galea falcate, marginally 2-toothed; beak straight.
