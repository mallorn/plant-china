## Description
Plants without glandular hairs. Inflorescences long subracemes or narrow cymose panicles. Sepals ovate, 5–8 mm.

> Thickets, slopes, roadsides; 800--2000 m. Anhui, Fujian, Gansu, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan, Zhejiang.
  "Synonym": "*Rubus kuntzeanus* Hemsley, J. Linn. Soc., Bot 23: 232. 1887.
