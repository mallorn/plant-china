## Description
Herbs, acaulescent, exceptionally caulescent, caespitose. Leaves imparipinnate; leaflets not verticillate. Racemes 1-8-flowered or more; peduncle 1-9 cm, shorter to slightly longer than leaves. Legume sessile or with a stipe to 5 mm; body ovoid or cylindric, 5-27 mm, membranous.
