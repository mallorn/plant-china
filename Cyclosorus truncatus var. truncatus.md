截裂毛蕨(原变种)",
2a.**Cyclosorus truncatus var. truncatus**",

> In FRPS (4(1): 257. 1999), K. H. Shing mentioned that many specimens from China with broadened pinna bases differed from the type. Although both broadened and narrowed pinna bases exist, all of the Chinese specimens are ± different in general outline from the type. More detailed studies are needed.

## Description
Plants 0.6-2 m tall. Middle pinnae 18-30 × 1.5-2.5 cm, lobed 1/3-1/2 toward costae; veinlets 6-10 pairs per segment, proximal 1 or 2 pairs anastomosing, next pair running to sinus membrane. 2*n* = 72, 144.

> Streamsides, wet places in forests; 100-1300 m. S Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, S Guizhou, Hainan, S Hunan, Taiwan, SE Xizang, S Yunnan [India, Indonesia, Japan, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, Philippines, Sri Lanka, Thailand, Vietnam; N Australia, Pacific islands (Polynesia)].
