石生阴山荠(原亚种)",
11a.**Yinshania rupicola subsp. rupicola**",

## Description
Petals white. Fruit narrowly elliptic, 5-6 × 2-2.5 mm. Fl. May, fr. Jun.

> * Forests, rock crevices in wet shady places; 1000-1200 m. Anhui.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Cochlearia rupicola&lt;/I&gt; D. C. Zhang &amp; J. Z. Shao, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 24: 404. 1986; &lt;I&gt;Hilliella rupicola&lt;/I&gt; (D. C. Zhang &amp; J. Z. Shao) Y. H. Zhang.
