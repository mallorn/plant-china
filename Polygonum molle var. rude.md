倒毛神血宁",
79b.**Polygonum molle var. rude** (Meisner) A. J. Li",

## Description
Stems long hirsute with appressed, deflexed hairs. Leaves sericeous.

> Slopes, forests, thickets in valleys; 1400-3500 m. Guangxi, Guizhou, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, India (Assam), N Myanmar, Nepal, Thailand].
  "Synonym": "*Polygonum* *rude* Meisner in Candolle, Prodr. 14(1): 137. 1856; *Aconogonon* *molle* (D. Don) H. Hara var. *rude* (Meisner) H. Hara; *Persicaria* *rudis* (Meisner) H. Gross; *Polygonum* *deflexipilosum* Kitamura; *P*. *esquirolii* H. Léveillé; *P*. paniculatum Blume var. *rude* (Meisner) Steward; *P*. *tsangschangicum* Lingelsheim &amp; Borza.
