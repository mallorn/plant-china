> Some 156 species: N Africa (Morocco), America, Asia, Australia, Europe; 117 species in China.

## Description
Annuals, rarely perennials. Flowers terminal, solitary, small. Plicae usually symmetrical. Capsules narrowly obovoid to obovoid, conspicuously winged; wing strong and broad at apex but gradually narrowed toward base. Seed coat minutely reticulate.
