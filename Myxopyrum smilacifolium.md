## Description
Shrubs scandent. Branchlets glabrous. Petiole 5-10 mm, canaliculate above; leaf blades elliptic or ovate-elliptic, 10-16 &amp;#215; 4-10 cm, leathery, glabrous, dotted with peltate scales, base broadly cuneate to obtuse, margin entire or finely serrate, apex acute to short acuminate. Panicles axillary, 7-19 cm, finely papillate hairy to glabrous. Flowers subsessile. Calyx ca. 0.5 mm; lobes elliptic, ca. 0.5 mm. Corolla campanulate; tube ca. 1 mm; lobes linear-spatulate, ca. 1.5 mm. Berry globose, 5-10 mm in diam. Fr. Dec.

> Forest; ca. 700 m. Hainan [Bangladesh, Cambodia, India, Laos, Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam]
