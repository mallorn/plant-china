镰叶水珍珠菜",
16.**Pogostemon falcatus** (C. Y. Wu) C. Y. Wu & H. W. Li",

## Description
Herbs erect. Rhizomes woody. Stems 30-40 cm tall, base &amp;#177; woody, densely appressed hirsute, apex densely gray hairy, subterete, slightly swollen at nodes. Petiole densely silky; leaf blade falcate, 4.5-6.5 cm &amp;#215; 4-7 mm, appressed silky, densely so along veins, base attenuate, margin remotely serrate except for &amp;#177; entire lower margin, apex acuminate; lateral veins 3-5-paired, conspicuous abaxially. Spikes 4.5-5.5 cm &amp;#215; ca. 6.5 mm, dense, subtended by floral leaves at base; bracts linear, slightly longer than calyx, densely silky. Calyx ca. 1.7 &amp;#215; 1 mm, membranous, pilose outside, teeth short. Corolla red, black when dry, ca. 1.5 &amp;#215; as long as calyx. Filaments black when dry, sparsely hairy. Nutlets unknown. Fl. Oct.

> * Rocky areas near water; ca. 800 m. Yunnan (Jinghong Xian).

**16.镰叶水珍珠菜（植物分类学报10卷3期）**

Pogostemon falcatus (C. Y. Wu) C. Y. Wu et H. W. Li in Act. Phytotax. Sin 13(1): 76. 1975——Dysophylla falcata C. Y. Wu in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 10(3): 237. 1965.

直立草本；根茎木质。茎高30-40厘米，分枝，基部几木质化，全茎密被平伏长硬毛，上部因被毛极密而呈灰白色，近圆柱形，节稍膨大。叶长镰形，长4.5-6.5厘米，宽4-7毫米，先端渐尖，基部渐狭，边缘下部近全缘，其上具疏锯齿，草质，上面榄绿色，下面略淡，两面均被平伏绢毛，脉上尤密，中肋两面隆起，侧脉3-5对，在下面极明显；叶柄极短，腹平背凸，密被平伏绢毛。穗状花序长4.5-5.5厘米，直径约6.5毫米，密集，基部衬有苞叶；苞片线形，略超出花萼，密被绢毛。花萼长约1.7毫米，宽约1毫米，膜质，外面被疏柔毛，萼齿5，短小。花冠红色，干后变黑色，为花萼长之1.5倍。雄蕊4，伸出，花丝干后呈黑色，其上毛茸较少。小坚果未见。花期10月。

产云南南部；生于水边石上，海拔800米。模式标本采自云南景洪。
