芒颖披碱草(原变种)",
33a.**Elymus aristiglumis var. aristiglumis**",

## Description
Leaf blade flat, 0.4–0.5 cm wide, glabrous or scabrous. Lemma ± hirsutulous throughout except callus glabrous; awn 20–30 mm. Fl. and fr. late summer.

> * Mountain slopes; 1500–3000 m. Gansu, Qinghai, Sichuan, Xinjiang, Xizang.
  "Synonym": "*Roegneria aristiglumis* Keng &amp; S. L. Chen, Acta Univ. Nankin. Sci. Nat. 1963(1): 55. 1963.
