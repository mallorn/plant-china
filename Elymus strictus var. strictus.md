肃草(原变种)",
22a.**Elymus strictus var. strictus**",

## Description
Lemma glabrous at middle abaxially, otherwise *±* puberulent. Palea between keels puberulent distally.

> * Mountain slopes, valleys, meadows, roadsides; 1300–2200 m. Gansu, Guizhou, Henan, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shanxi, Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Elymus gmelinii* (Ledebour) Tzvelev subsp. *strictus* (Keng) K. B. Jensen; *Roegneria stricta* Keng; *R. stricta* f.* major* Keng; *R. varia* Keng.
