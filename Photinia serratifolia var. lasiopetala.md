毛瓣石楠",
5b.**Photinia serratifolia var. lasiopetala** (Hayata) H. Ohashi",

## Description
Leaf blade obovate-lanceolate, 8–15 cm, margin distinctly serrate, entire only near base. Rachis, pedicels, hypanthium, and petals basally villous.

> Low mountain areas; ca. 900 m. C Taiwan.
  "Synonym": "*Photinia lasiopetala* Hayata, Icon. Pl. Formosan. 6: 17. 1916; *P. serrulata* Lindley f. *lasiopetala* (Hayata) T. Shimizu; *P. serrulata* var. *lasiopetala* (Hayata) K. C. Kuan.
**石楠毛瓣变种**

P. serrulata Lindl. var. lasiopetala (Hayata) Kuan, stat. nov.——P. laslopetala Hayata, Ic. Pl. Formos. 6: 117. f. 1. 1916; Kanehira, Formos. Trees 265. f. 210. 1936; Li in Lloydia 14: 233. 1951; Liu, Ill. Nat. Intr. Lign. Pl. Taiwan 1: 436. f. 370. 1960; Li, Woody Fl. Taiwan 233. 1963. ——P. serrulata Lindl. forma lasiopetala (Hayata) Shimizu in Acta Phytotax. Geobot. 21: 18. 1964.

叶片革质，倒卵状椭圆形，长8-15厘米，宽2.5-4.5厘米；总花梗和花梗无毛或有柔毛；总花梗和花梗无毛或有柔毛；萼筒杯状，外面有柔毛，萼片三角形，先端急尖，内面无毛；花瓣白色，倒卵形，内面有白色长柔毛，基部有短爪。花期4月。

产台湾。生于海拔850米。模式标本采自南投雾社。
