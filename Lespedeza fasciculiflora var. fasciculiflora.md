束花铁马鞭(原变种)",
10a.**Lespedeza fasciculiflora var. fasciculiflora**",

## Description
Stems and branchlets densely white hirsute. Leaflets ob-cordate, terminal one 4-9 × 4-7 mm, abaxially densely villous, adaxially sparsely hairy or subglabrous. Corolla pink or pale purplish red. Fl. Jul-Aug, fr. Sep-Feb.
  "Statistics": "● Sandy grasslands on high mountains; 1600-3000 m. W Si-chuan, Xizang, NW Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Lespedeza floribunda* Bunge var. *fasciculiflora* (Franchet) Schindler; *L. monnoyeri* H. Léveillé.
