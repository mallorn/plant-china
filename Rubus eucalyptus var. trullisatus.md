无腺桉叶悬钩子",
20c.**Rubus eucalyptus var. trullisatus** (Focke) T. T. Yu & L. T. Lu",

## Description
Leaves 3-foliolate, rarely 5-foliolate. Branchlets, petioles, pedicel, and abaxial surface of calyx with soft hairs, without glandular hairs. Calyx abaxially with sparse, needle-like prickles.

> Thickets, ravines, valleys; 1000--2500 m. W Hubei, SE Shaanxi, W Sichuan.
  "Synonym": "*Rubus trullisatus* Focke, Biblioth. Bot. 17(Heft 72): 169. 1911; *R. lasiostylus* Focke var. *villosus* Cardot.
**14c. 无腺按叶悬钩子（变种）**

Rubus eucalyptus Focke var. trullisatus (Focke) Yu et. Lu, stat. nov.——R. trullisatus Focke, Bibl. not. 72(2): 169. 1911.——R. lasiostylus Focke var. villosus Card. in Lecomte, Not. Syst. 3: 309. 1914. syn. nov.

本变种常为3小叶，稀5小叶；叶柄、花枝和花萼仅具柔毛，无腺毛；花萼上具稀疏针刺。

产陕西东南部、湖北西部、四川（城口、峨眉、宝兴）。生沟谷旁灌丛中，海拔1000-2500米。
