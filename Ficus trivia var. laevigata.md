## Description
Petiole 1-2(-9) cm, thick; leaf blade elliptic, 6.5-11(-16) × 2.5-4(-6) cm, glabrous or sparsely covered with white coarse hairs, base narrowly cuneate; secondary veins 3 or 4(-7) on each side of midvein. Figs many, dense, ca. 1 cm in diam.; peduncle 5-10 mm.

> * Guangxi (Pingguo), Guizhou (Pingtang).
**39b.光叶楔叶榕（变种）（广西植物）**

Ficus trivia Corner var. laevigata S. S. Chang in Guihaia 4 (2): 118. 1984.

叶椭圆形，长6. 5-11(-16)厘米，宽2.5-4(-6)厘米，两面无毛或疏被稀疏白色糙毛，基部狭楔形，侧脉3-4对（-7）对；叶柄粗壮，长1-2 (-5)厘米。榕果多数密集，直径约1厘米，果柄长5-10毫米。花果期同上。

产广西平果（模式标本产地）、贵州平塘。
