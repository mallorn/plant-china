黄绿花滇百合",
14c.**Lilium bakerianum var. delavayi** (Franchet) E. H. Wilson",

## Description
Leaves glabrous. Tepals yellowish green, pale yellow-green, greenish, or pale green, with purple or bright red spots adaxially.

> Forested or grassy slopes; 2500--3800 m. Guizhou, Sichuan, Yunnan [Myanmar].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt; Lilium delavayi&lt;/I&gt; Franchet, J. Bot. (Morot) 6: 314. 1892.
**黄绿花滇百合（变种）**

var. delavayi (Franch.) Wilson, Lil. East. As. 43. 1925. ——Lilium delavayi Franch. in Journ. de Bot. 6: 314. 1892.

本变种与滇百合的区别在于花黄绿色或橄榄绿至淡绿色，内具红紫色或鲜红色斑点。

产云南、四川和贵州。生山坡林中或草坡，海拔2500-3800米。
