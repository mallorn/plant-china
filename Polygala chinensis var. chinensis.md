华南远志(原变种)",
37a.**Polygala chinensis var. chinensis**",

## Description
Herbs erect, 10-25 cm tall. Stems, branches, and leaves crisped pubescent. Leaf blade obovate, elliptic, or lanceolate, 2.6-10 × 1-1.5 cm. Fl. Apr-Oct, fr. May-Nov.

> Grasslands, shrub forests on slopes of hills; 500-1000 m. Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Hainan, Yunnan [India, Philippines, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Polygala densiflora* Blume; *P. glomerata* Loureiro; *P. telephioides* Willdenow.
