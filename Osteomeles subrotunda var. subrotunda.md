圆叶小石积(原变种)",
3a.**Osteomeles subrotunda var. subrotunda**",

## Description
Leaf blade and petiole hairy; leaflets 4–6 × 2–3 mm, apex obtuse or mucronulate.

> Mixed forests, roadsides; 200--500 m. N Guangdong (Renhua Xian) [Japan (Ryukyu Islands)].
  "Synonym": "*Osteomeles anthyllidifolia* Lindley f. *subrotunda* (K. Koch) Koidzumi; *O. anthyllidifolia* var. *subrotunda* (K. Koch) Masamune.
