## Description
Plants up to 5 cm. Rhizome dark brown or black, filiform, 0.2-0.3 mm in diam., densely covered with reddish brown or dark brown short hairs, irregularly branching. Stipes remote, 3-12 mm apart, dark brown to dark green, filiform, 1-10 mm, densely hairy at base, subglabrous upward. Lamina flabellate or ovate, 1-2 × 0.5-1.5 cm, base cordate or cuneate; ultimate segments linear, 1.5-8 × 0.3-2 mm, margin entire, apex obtuse. Sori terminal on lobes; involucres funnelform or bottle-shaped, winged, dilated at mouth. 2*n* = 72, 108, 144.

> On muddy rocks in forests and valleys, or wet rocks near streams, on moss-covered tree trunks, in tropical evergreen forests; near sea level to 1600 m (in Taiwan). Anhui, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hainan, Hunan, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan, Zhejiang [Bhutan, Cambodia, NE India, Indonesia, Japan, Korea, Malaysia, Nepal, Philippines, Russia (Siberia), Sri Lanka, Thailand, Vietnam; Africa, Australia, Pacific islands (Melanesia, Micronesia, Polynesia)].

植株高仅约2厘米。根状茎纤细，丝状，横走，黑色，疏被暗褐色的短毛。叶远生，相距8-12毫米；叶柄长约1-3厘米，纤细如丝，黑褐色，光滑或稍被毛，无翼，节节分枝，为多回多育性；叶片近于扇形或卵形，长1-2厘米，宽8-10毫米，一回深羽裂或几为掌状深裂达2/3，基部楔形；裂片线形，长2-4毫米，宽约1毫米，分叉，先端钝圆，全缘。叶脉多回叉状分枝，两面均可见，不隆起，暗褐色，末回裂片有小脉1条。叶为薄膜质，半透明，干后呈淡褐色，两面光滑无毛。孢子囊群生于裂片顶部；囊苞花瓶状，突出，中部稍膨大，两侧有翅，口部膨大，囊群托稍突出。

我国西南部的邻近越南地区可能也有。生长在潮湿岩石上，常与苔藓混生。分布于越南及热带亚洲地区。
