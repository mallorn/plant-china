## Description
Stems branched or not. Leaves in whorls of 4; leaf blade ovate-lanceolate to linear-oblong, pinnatipartite to pinnatisect. Inflorescences racemose. Calyx lobes 5. Corolla tube as long as or slightly exceeding calyx; galea bent apically; beak semicircular or S-shaped, long; lower lip with hoodlike middle lobe.
