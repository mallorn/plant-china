翠绿凤尾蕨(原变种)",
48a.**Pteris longipinnula var. longipinnula**",

## Description
Plants 1-1.2 m tall. Stipe, rachis, costae, and costules glabrous. Stipe 60-70 cm, upper part of stipe and rachis, costae, and costules blue-green; basal segments abruptly shortened, narrowly triangular, ca. 6 mm.

> ● Hainan (Diaoluo Shan), S Yunnan (Hekou, Xishuangbanna).
  "Synonym": "*Pteris umbraculifera* Mettenius ex Miquel.
