假斗大王马先蒿",
88e.**Pedicularis rex subsp. pseudocyathus** (Vaniot ex Bonati) P. C. Tsoong",

## Description
Plants often more than 20 cm tall. Basal leaves short petiolate. Calyx 2-toothed. Corolla yellow, 2--2.5 cm; galea teeth subulate. Fl. May.

> * SC Guizhou.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Pedicularis rex&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;pseudocyathus&lt;/I&gt; Vaniot ex Bonati, Bull. Acad. Int. Geogr. Bot. 13: 240. 1904.
**大王马先蒿假斗亚种**

Pedicularis rex C. B. Clarke subsp. pseudocyathus (Vaniot ex Bonati) Tsoong, comb. nov. ——P. rex C. B. Clarke var. pseudocyathus Vaniot ex Bonati in Bull. Acad. Ceogr. Bot. XIII (1904) 240; Limpr. in Repert. Sp. Nov. XX (1924) 224; Li, in Proc. Acad. Nat. Sci. Philad. C (1948) 338.

这一亚种的区别在于其基叶之柄较短，宿存；其花仅长2-2.5厘米。花期5月中旬。

产我国贵州省中南部。
