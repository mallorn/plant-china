异果鹤虱属",
34.**Heterocaryum** A. de Candolle",

> About seven species: C to SW Asia; one species in China.

## Description
Herbs annual, hirsute, hairs tuberclelike at base. Stems erect or decumbent, branched. Cymes bracteate. Calyx 5-parted to base; lobes enlarged in fruit. Corolla funnelform to campanulate; throat appendages 5, trapeziform. Stamens included. Ovary 4-parted. Style short; stigma capitate. Gynobase columnar, low. Nutlets homomorphic or heteromorphic, dorsiventrally compressed, firmly coherent to gynobase along entire adaxial surface; abaxially discoid, with prickles or other appendages.
**35. 异果鹤虱属——Heterocaryum DC. 
<p style='text-indent:28px'>DC. Prodr. 10: 144. 1846.**

一年生草本，被有具疣状基盘的长糙毛。茎直立或外倾，有分枝。聚伞花序有苞片。花萼5裂至基部，裂片果时增大；花冠漏斗状至钟状，喉部具5个梯形附属物；雄蕊5，内藏；子房4裂，花柱很短，柱头头状；雌蕊基细柱状。小坚果同型或异型，背腹扁，以腹面全长与雌蕊基贴生、不易分离，背面盘状，边缘具刺状或其它形状的附属物。

约7种，分布中亚至土耳其。我国产1种。
