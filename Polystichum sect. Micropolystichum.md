## Description
Plants small-sized. Lamina lanceolate, 1-pinnate. Pinnae deeply or shallowly pinnatifid. Pinnae ovate or oblong, indistinctly toothed, mucronate. Sori abaxial or terminal on veinlets. Indusia large, membranous, erose.
  "Statistics": "About six species: montane to alpine regions in Asia; six species (five endemic) in China.
