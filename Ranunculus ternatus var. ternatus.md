猫爪草(原变种)",
89a.**Ranunculus ternatus var. ternatus**",

## Description
Basal leaves with blade ternate, sometimes simple, leaflets 2- or 3-lobed or 2- or 3-partite, ultimate lobes ovate or narrowly ovate.

> Fields, grassy slopes, forests; below 500 m. Anhui, Fujian, N Guangxi, S Henan, Hubei, Hunan, S Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Taiwan, Zhejiang [Japan].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Ranunculus extorris&lt;/I&gt; Hance; &lt;I&gt;R. formosanus&lt;/I&gt; Masamune; &lt;I&gt;R. leiocladus&lt;/I&gt; Hayata; &lt;I&gt;R. zuccarinii&lt;/I&gt; Miquel.
