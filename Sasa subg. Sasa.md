赤竹亚属",
1.**Sasa subg. Sasa**",

## Description
Culms and axis of inflorescence not white powdery; nodal ridge elevated (in Chinese species); culm sheaths shorter than internodes; auricles and oral setae present or absent; branch at 20–30° angle with culm; leaf blade dull.
  "Statistics": "More than 40 species: China, Japan, Korea, E Russia; five species (all endemic) in China.
