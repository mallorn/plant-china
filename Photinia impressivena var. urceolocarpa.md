## Description
Petiole 5–8 mm. Compound corymbs 5–10 cm in diam., many flowered; rachis, pedicels, and hypanthium abaxially white tomentose. Pedicel 2–3 mm.

> Forests, thickets; ca. 500 m. Guangxi [Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Photinia lancilimbum* J. E. Vidal var. *urceolocarpa* J. E. Vidal, Notul. Syst. 13: 299. 1948.
**陷脉石楠毛序变种**

P. impressivena Hayata var. urceolocarpa (Vidal) Vidal in Fl. Camb. Laos et Vietnam 6: 51. pl. 6: 4-5. 1968. ——P. lancilimbum Vidal var. urceolocarpa Vidal in Not. Syst. 13: 299. 1948.

叶柄长5-8毫米，无毛或稍有绒毛；伞房花序有多数花，直径5-10厘米；总花梗和花梗具白色绒毛，有疣点，花梗长2-3毫米；萼筒外面具绒毛。

产广西。生于林下。分布越南。
