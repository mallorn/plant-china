毛药翠雀花",
118.**Delphinium lasiantherum** W. T. Wang",

> The type citation gives a February collection date. No other specimens have been seen. August or September is a more likely collection date at this location and elevation.

## Description
Stem 60--70 cm tall, glabrous or subglabrous, 1-branched distally. Leaf blade 9--13 cm wide, base cordate; primary lobes separate for at least 60% of blade radius; central lobe rhombic, 3-lobulate, distally 1- or 2-dentate, apex acute; ultimate lobules triangular or lanceolate; proximal leaves withered. Raceme 11--15 cm, 5- or 6-flowered, yellow glandular puberulent; bracts narrowly linear. Pedicels 1.8--4.5 cm; bracteoles distal, lanceolate-linear or subulate, 5--6 mm. Sepals deep purple, abaxially yellow pilose; spur subulate, 2.2--2.5 cm, recurved, base ca. 2.5 mm in diam.; other sepals 1--1.6 cm. Petals 2-lobulate, sparsely ciliate. Staminode limb suboblong, 2-parted, yellow barbate. Filaments pilose distally; anthers abaxially puberulent. Carpels 3; ovaries densely pubescent. Fl. Feb.

> * Scrub; ca. 3200 m. SW Sichuan (Daocheng Xian).

**77c. 毛梗大理翠雀花（变种）**

Delphinium taliense var. pubipes W. T. Wang，植物学报10：159.1962.

与大理翠雀花的区别：花序轴上部、花梗和小苞片都有开展的黄色短腺毛。茎无 毛。萼片长约1.4厘米，外面有短毛,距钻形，长约2.2厘米，基部粗约3毫米。退化 雄蕊的瓣片二裂稍超过中部，裂片狭披针形。子房密被柔毛。

产四川西南部稻城。生海拔3000米一带山地疏林中。
