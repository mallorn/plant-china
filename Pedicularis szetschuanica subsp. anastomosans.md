## Description
Plants to 10 cm tall, &amp;#177; woody. Calyx with reticulate veins almost throughout. Margin of lower corolla lip erose-serrulate. Fl. Aug.

> * 3800--3900 m. E Xizang.
**四川马先蒿网脉亚种**

Pedicularis szetschuanica Maxim. subsp. anastomosans Tsoong, subsp. nov., in Addend 405. ——P. szetschuanica Maxim. subsp. angustifolia (Bonati) Tsoong in Acta Phytotax. Sin. III (1954) 311, et in Bull. Brit. Mus. (Nat. Hist.) Bot. II, i (1955) 30.

这一亚种的不同，在于其体多少木质化，高仅to厘米左右，其萼几全部有网脉，其下唇边缘有不整齐的啮痕状齿。

仅见于我国西藏昌都专区中西部。
