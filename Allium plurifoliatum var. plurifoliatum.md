多叶韭 (原变种)",
30a.**Allium plurifoliatum var. plurifoliatum**",

## Description
Inner filaments broadened at base, 1-toothed on each side, teeth irregularly denticulate at apex. Fl. and fr. Aug--Oct. 2&lt;I&gt; n&lt;/I&gt; = 16*, 32*.

> * Forests, slopes, pastures; 1600--3300 m. SE Anhui (Huang Shan), Gansu, NW Hubei, Shaanxi, Sichuan.
