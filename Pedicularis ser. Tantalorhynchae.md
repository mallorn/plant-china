> One species.

## Description
Herbs low. Stems single or numerous, unbranched. Basal leaves persistent, long petiolate, &amp;#177; lanceolate or narrowly oblong, large, pinnatifid, with many segments. Stem leaves in whorls of 3. Inflorescences spicate. Corolla tube erect, ca. as long as calyx; beak of galea long, horizontal or slightly decurved.
