## Description
Annuals. Stems slender, ca. 20 cm tall, glabrous. Leaves linear-subulate, to 10 X 1 mm, margin entire. Pedicel to 4 mm. Calyx 3-5 mm in fruit; lobes subulate. Capsule ovoid-ellipsoid, ca. 2 mm. Seeds yellow-brown, ca. 0.5 mm.

> Swamps. Heilongjiang, Jiangsu (Jurong Xian), Jilin, Liaoning [Japan, Korea, Russia (Far East)].
