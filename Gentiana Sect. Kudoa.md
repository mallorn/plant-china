> Thirty-nine species: Asia; 30 species in China.

## Description
Perennials. Roots linear-cylindric, lax, fleshy, arising from a collar. Stems branching monopodially. Stamens unequal (equal in &lt;I&gt;Gentiana altorum&lt;/I&gt;). Seed coat alveolate, with simple shallow hexagonal pits.
