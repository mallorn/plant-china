## Description
Fronds with basal acroscopic segments of lower pinnae slightly enlarged, not pinnatipartite or pinnatilobate, toothed or long toothed at margin.

> ● Coniferous forests on mountain slopes; ca. 2800 m. NW Yunnan (Zhongdian).
  "Synonym": "Basionym: *Lunathyrium auriculatum* var. *zhongdianense* Z. R. Wang, Fl. Reipubl. Popularis Sin. 3(2): 511. 1999.
**15b. 中甸蛾眉蕨（横断山区维管植物）（变种）**

var. zhongdianense Z. R. Wang, var. nov. in Addenda; in W. T. Wang, Vasc. Pl. Hengduan Mount. 1: 74. 1993, nom. seminud.

与原变种不同在于叶下部羽片的基部上侧裂片仅稍增大，其边缘仅有锯齿或长齿。

特产于云南西北部（中甸）。生于山坡针叶林下，海拔2800米。
