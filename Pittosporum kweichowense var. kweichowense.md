贵州海桐(原变种)",
12a.**Pittosporum kweichowense var. kweichowense**",

## Description
Leaf blade narrowly oblong, 3–8 × 1–2.2 cm. Sepals 1–1.5 mm. Capsule cylindric.

> Forests, thickets, river sides; 500--2000 m. SW Guizhou (Anlong Bouyeizu Miaozu Zizhixian), W Hunan (Zhijiang Xian), SE Yunnan (Wenshan Xian).
  "Synonym": "*Pittosporum densinervatum* H. T. Chang &amp; S. Z. Yan; *P. longicarpum* S. K. Wu ex W. C. Yin.
