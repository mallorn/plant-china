江西绣线菊",
36c.**Spiraea cantoniensis var. jiangxiensis** (Z. X. Yu) L. T. Lu",

## Description
Branchlets pubescent. Leaf blades 2–8 × 0.7–1.5 cm, abaxially pubescent.

> Slopes; 200--300 m. N Jiangxi (Jing’an Xian).
  "Synonym": "*Spiraea jiangxiensis* Z. X. Yu, Bull. Bot. Res., Harbin 3(1): 151. 1983.
