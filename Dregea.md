## Description
Lianas. Inflorescences umbel-like clusters of cymules; peduncle and pedicels long and slender. Sepals overlapping, with 5 basal glands. Corolla rotate to shallowly bowl-shaped, deeply 5-divided; lobes overlapping to right. Corona lobes 5, &amp;#177; spreading, thick fleshy, adnate to stamens, outer angle obtuse or rectangular, interior angle produced into an acute tooth incumbent to anthers. Anthers with membranous apical appendages; pollinia 2 per pollinarium, oblong, erect. Stigma head umbonate or thick conical. Follicles widely divergent, thick, finely longitudinally ribbed or corrugate, not winged [or winged]. Seeds comose.

**37. 南山藤属——Dregea E. Mey., nom. cons.**

E. Mey. Comm. Pl. Afr. Austr. 199. 14-20 Jan. 1838.——Wattakaka Hassk. in Flora 60: 99. 1857.

攀援木质藤本。叶对生，基部通常心形或截形，稀楔形，具柄，全缘，羽状脉。伞形状聚伞花序腋生，着花多朵；花萼5裂，裂片卵圆形，内面有腺体；花冠辐状，顶端5裂，裂片向右覆盖；副花冠5裂，肉质，贴生在雄蕊的背面，呈放射状展开，内角延长成一尖齿紧靠花药；雄蕊着生于花冠的近基部，花药顶端具内弯的膜片；花粉块每室1个，长圆形，直立；子房由2枚离生心皮组成，每心皮有胚珠多颗，柱头圆锥状凸起。蓇葖双生，外果皮具纵棱条或横皱褶片状，或平滑；种子顶端具白色绢质种毛。

约12种，分布于亚洲南部和非洲南部。我国产4种，4变种，主要分布于南部。

本属模式种为：Dregea floribunda E. Mey.

产于我国台湾的Dregea formosana Yamazaki in Journ. Jap. Bot. 43: 219, fig.1．1968．，我们未见标本，故本志未有收入。
