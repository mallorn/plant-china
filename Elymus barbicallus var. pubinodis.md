## Description
Culms 40–50 cm tall, puberulent below nodes; nodes white pilose. Leaf sheath outer margin ciliate; leaf blade 16–30 × 0.4–0.7 cm, both surfaces glabrous or adaxial surface scabrous. Proximal glume 10–14 mm; distal glume 12–14.5 mm. Lemma 12–13 mm. Fl. and fr. May–Jul.

> * Mountain slopes, valleys. Hebei, Nei Mongol.
  "Synonym": "*Roegneria barbicalla* var. *pubinodis* Keng, Acta Univ. Nankin. Sci. Nat. 1963(1):24. 1963.
**21b.毛节毛盘草（变种）**

Roegneria barbicalla Ohwi var. pubinodis Keng,耿以礼及陈守良，南京大学学报（生物学）1: 24. 1963;禾本科图说359. 1959. nom. nud.

植株高40-50厘米，秆节密生白色柔毛，紧接节下亦多被柔毛；叶鞘外侧边缘具纤毛；第一颖长10-14毫米，第二颖长12-14.5毫米；第一外稃长12-13毫米。

产河北、内蒙古。生于山坡、山沟。模式标本采自河北内邱县。
