## Description
Plants medium-sized, evergreen. Lamina papery to leathery, 1-pinnate, monomorphic or nearly dimorphic. Rachis of sterile lamina often prolonged and viviparous. Stipe and rachis scales often ciliate on margin. Sori abaxial on veinlets. Indusia small and caducous.
