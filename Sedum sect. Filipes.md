## Description
Leaf base often narrowed into a petiole, spurless. Inflorescence cymose or paniculate-corymbose, lax. Flowers long pedicellate. Sepals spurless. Petals white or reddish purple, rarely yellow. Carpels and follicles adaxially not gibbous. Carpels erect to suberect, base connate to subconnate. Follicles usually many seeded.
