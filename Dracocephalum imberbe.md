## Description
Rhizomes 3.5-9 mm in diam. Stems erect or ascending, unbranched, ca. 25 cm, sparsely minute hairy and villous, rarely glabrescent basally, densely retrorse hairy and villous at apex. Basal leaves numerous; petioles 3-12 mm, sparsely retrorse pubescent; blade circular-ovate to reni-form, 1.7-3.7 &amp;#215; 1.5-4 cm; blade of mid stem leaves ovate to reniform, sparsely pubescent along veins, base cor-date, margin undulate-crenate. Verticillasters few, crowded in capitula; bracts spatulate-obovate, ca. 6-7.5 mm, margin 1- or 2-serrate, sparsely pubescent, ciliate, teeth spine-scent-triangular to spinescent-lanceolate, with spines 1.5-2.5 mm. Calyx purplish, 1.2-1.5 cm, pubescent to silky-villous, margin white ciliate, 2-lipped to 1/4 its length; upper lip teeth subequal, ovate-triangular, ca. 3 mm, apex acute; lower lip teeth narrower than those of upper lip, ca. 3 mm. Corolla blue-purple, 2.5-2.7 cm, villous. Fl. Jul-Aug.

> Grassy slopes; 2400-2500 m. Xinjiang [Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Russia, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan].
**18.无髭毛建草 图版71:12-13; 72:1-8**

Dracocephalum imberbe Bunge Suppl. Fl. Alt. 50. 1836; Ledeb. Fl. Ross. 3: 385. 1849; Lipsky in Act. Hort. Petrop. 25: 579. 1910; Schischk. in Fl. URSS 20: 453. 1954, cum syn.

根茎粗3.5-9毫米，顶部生数茎。茎直立或渐升，不分枝，长约25厘米，四棱形，被倒向小毛，夹以长柔毛，上部密，中部以下稍稀疏，稀变无毛，中部2-3节最长，上部生花序处之2-3节很短。基出叶多数，具长柄，多被与茎相同的毛，叶片圆卵形或肾形，先端圆或钝，基部深心形，长1.7-3.7厘米，宽1.5-4厘米，两面沿脉上疏被短柔毛，边缘具圆形波状牙齿，叶柄长4-10.5厘米，通常为叶片的3-4倍，疏被倒向短柔毛；茎中部叶具鞘状短柄，柄长3-12毫米，叶片卵圆形或肾形，基部心形。轮伞花序密集，头状；苞片约为萼之1/2，匙状倒卵形，疏被短毛及睫毛，每侧具1-2齿，齿三角形至披针形，先端具长1.5-2.5毫米的刺。花萼长1.2-1.5厘米，常带紫色，外被短毛至绢状长柔毛，缘被白色睫毛，2裂至1/4处，上唇3深裂近本身基部，齿近等大，卵状三角形，长约3毫米，先端锐尖；下唇2裂至墓部，齿似上唇之齿，但较狭，长约3毫米。花冠蓝紫色，长2.5-3.7厘米，外被柔毛。雄蕊不伸出，花丝疏被毛。花期7-8月。

产新疆（阿尔泰及天山东段）；生于海拔2400-2500米间山地草坡。苏联也有。模式标本采自苏联西伯利亚。
