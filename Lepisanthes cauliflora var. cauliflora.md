茎花赤才(原变种)",
7a.**Lepisanthes cauliflora var. cauliflora**",

## Description
Leaflet blades adaxially deep green, 13-30 × 5-11 cm, abaxially sparsely tomentose along midveins and lateral veins, adaxially sparsely tomentose along midveins, or glabrous on both surfaces.

> ●  Forests. Guangxi (Longzhou).
