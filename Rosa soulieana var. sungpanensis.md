大叶川滇蔷薇",
85c.**Rosa soulieana var. sungpanensis** Rehder",

## Description
Leaflets large, ca. 3.5 cm. Flowers in corymbose panicle; pedicel glabrous, not glandular.

> N Sichuan (Songpan Xian).
**72c. 大叶川滇蔷薇**

R. soulieana Crep. var. sungpanensis Rehd. in Journ. Arn. Arb. 11: 161. 1930.

小叶片较大，长约3.5厘米，花序呈伞房状圆锥花序；花梗无毛，无腺。

产四川（松潘、茂县）。
