## Description
Stem ca. 9 cm tall, retrorsely appressed puberulent, branched. Leaf blade 2--3.5 cm wide, base cordate; primary lobes separate for at least 60% of blade radius; central lobe broadly rhombic or obovate-rhombic, 3-lobulate, distally 1- or 2-dentate or entire, apex acute; ultimate lobules narrowly oblong or lanceolate; proximal leaves usually withered. Corymb 3--6 cm, 1--3-flowered; bracts leaflike. Pedicels 3.2--7 cm, appressed puberulent; bracteoles distal, linear, 6--10 mm. Sepals blue, abaxially densely puberulent; spur narrowly cylindric, 1.9--2.1 cm, nearly straight, base 3--4.5 mm in diam.; other sepals 2.1--2.6 cm. Petals undivided, glabrous. Staminode limb narrowly obovate, emarginate, white barbate. Filaments glabrous. Carpels 3; ovaries densely puberulent. Fl. Jul--Aug.

> * Alpine meadows; 4200--4500 m. NW Yunnan (Zhongdian Xian).
