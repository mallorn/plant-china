縳颖假蛇尾草",
2b.**Mnesithea laevis var. chenii** (Hsu) de Koning & Sosef",

> This is a local variant with pitted sessile spikelets. Traces of pits also occur on some specimens of the species from Hong Kong.

## Description
Culms 15–30 cm tall. Sessile spikelet 3.5–5 mm; lower glume sunken and pitted between ribs. Fl. and fr. autumn.

> * By the sea. S Taiwan.
  "Synonym": "*Thaumastochloa chenii* Hsu, Taiwania 16: 216. 1971; *Het-eropholis cochinchinensis* var. *chenii* (Hsu) de Koning &amp; Sosef; *Ophiuros shimadanus* Ohwi &amp; Odashima; *Thaumastochloa shimadana* (Ohwi &amp; Odashima) Ohwi &amp; Odashima.

本变种无柄小穗第一颖脉间有窝点，可与假蛇尾草区别。花果期夏秋季。

产于我国台湾。模式标本采自台湾屏东。
