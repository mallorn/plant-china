## Description
Lower stems and abaxial leaf blade subglabrous; stem in upper part, petioles, and peduncles sparsely covered with multicellular hairs. Male panicles to 10 cm. Fl. Sep.

> Shaded moist places in mixed forests; ca. 2600 m. SE Xizang, NW Yunnan [Nepal].

本变种同原变种区别在于植物被稀疏的毛，茎下部和叶下面近于无毛，花序较短。花期9月。

在我国仅发现于云南西北部和西藏东南部。生于海拔2 600米的混交林下阴湿处。分布于尼泊尔。
