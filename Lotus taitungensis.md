## Description
Herbs, perennial, 50-80 cm, puberulent. Rootstock woody. Stem terete, strong, fleshy, decumbent with end ascending. Leaves sessile; stipules absent or present as inconspicuous dark glands; rachis 4-12 mm; leaflets 5(-7), all similar in shape, ob-lanceolate to narrowly obovate, 1-2(-4) cm, base cuneate, apex acute to almost rounded with a cusp. Heads (1 or)2-6(-8)-flow-ered; peduncle (0.6-)2-3 cm; sterile bract with 1-3 leaflets; subtending floral bracts present, wholly glandular or rarely with a minute blade and a pair of glands; pedicels short, pubescent. Flowers (8.5-)10-14.5(-20) mm. Calyx 7-11 mm; teeth subu-late, as long as or longer than tube. Corolla white, occasionally pale pink or tinged with violet, keel dark spotted with reddish or pink tip; keel incurved by acute angle, shortly rostrate. Ovary linear, glabrous; style (3-)4.5-6.5 mm. Legume cylindric, 3-5 cm × 2.7-4.4 mm, straight, valves twisted. Seeds numerous, globose, smooth. Fl. Sep-Mar.

**1. 兰屿百脉根（台湾植物志）**

Lotus australis Andr. in Bot. Repos. Pl. t. 264. 1799; Black in Fl. S. Austr. 2, 2: 461. 1963; Larsen ＆ Zertova in Fedde, Repert. Sp. Nov. 72 (1): 1. 1965; 台湾植物志3: 325. 1977.

多年生草本，高50-80厘米，被细柔毛。根茎木质，茎圆柱形，粗壮，近肉质，平卧或斜升，劲直。羽状复叶小叶5枚；叶轴长约5毫米；顶端3小叶，倒卵状披针形至阔线形，长1-2 (-4) 厘米，先端钝圆具细尖，基部楔形，叶柄基部2小叶较小，贴茎生；小叶柄甚短，密被长柔毛。伞形花序腋生；总花梗长2-3厘米，顶端有3枚叶状苞片或仅着生1枚叶片；花 (3) 4 (-8) 朵，长14-20毫米；花梗甚短，被毛；萼钟形被柔毛，萼齿5，线形，锥尖，长于萼筒；花冠白色，偶为红色或带紫色，旗瓣长12-14毫米，瓣片近圆形，先端钝圆或微凹，花开后反折，基部狭，翼瓣长圆形，与旗瓣近等长，龙骨瓣阔镰形，先端弯曲，具黑色晕斑；花丝分离部短于雄蕊筒；花柱与子房呈直角上指，子房线形，无毛。荚果线状圆柱形，长3-5厘米，径2-3毫米，直，熟后两瓣裂，果瓣扭曲；有种子多数。种子球形，光滑。

产台湾，仅见于兰屿。澳大利亚、东南亚、日本琉球群岛有分布，原产地不详。

野生牧草。
