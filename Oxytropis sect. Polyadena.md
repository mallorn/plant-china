## Description
Herbs, acaulescent, glandular. Leaves imparipinnate; leaflets verticillate. Racemes 3- to many flowered; peduncle 2-25 cm, shorter to longer than leaves. Corolla bluish purple, purple, or yellow. Ovary sessile or shortly stipitate, with 15-35 ovules. Legume usually inflated, 1- or ± 2-locular.
