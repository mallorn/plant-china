珠峰垂花报春",
290.**Primula buryana** I. B. Balfour",

## Description
Herbs perennial. Leaves forming a rosette; petiole winged, ca. 1/2 as long as to nearly as long as leaf blade; leaf blade ovate to oblong-ovate, 10--25 X 4--10 mm, with multicellular hairs, base attenuate, margin irregularly deep crenate-dentate, apex rounded to obtuse. Scapes 3--9(--18) cm, with multicellular white hairs; inflorescence subcapitate, 2--5(--7)-flowered; bracts often tinged with purple, narrowly lanceolate to linear, 2--3 mm, densely pubescent. Flowers heterostylous. Calyx cup-shaped, ca. 6 mm, pubescent outside, parted to middle; lobes slightly unequal, narrowly ovate to lanceolate, apex obtuse to acute. Corolla white or purple, infundibuliform; tube 6--8 mm, pubescent; limb 1--1.5 cm wide; lobes obovate, 6--8 mm, deeply emarginate. Pin flowers: stamens ca. 2 mm above base of corolla tube; style ca. as long as tube. Thrum flowers: stamens ca. 4.5 mm above base of corolla tube; style ca. 2 mm. Capsule subglobose, nearly as long as calyx. Fl. Jun-Jul. 2&lt;I&gt;n&lt;/I&gt; = 20.

> Damp meadows near ditches, among rocks on N-facing slopes; 4100--5000 m. S Xizang [Nepal].
**284.珠峰垂花报春（西藏植物志）**

Primula buryana Balf. f. in Kew Bull. 1322: 151. 922; W. W. Smith in Not. Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 16: 227. 1931, et in Bot. Mag. t. 9401. 1935, sub P. wollastonii Balf. f.; W. W. smith et Fletcher in Journ. Linn. Soc. Bot. 52: 323. 1942; S. Could in Hara et Williams Enum. Pl. Pl. Nepal 3: 70. 1982;西藏植物志3: 838. 1986.

多年生草本。根状茎短，具多数须根。叶丛高1-3（5）厘米，叶片卵形至矩圆状卵形，长1-2.5厘米，宽4-10毫米，先端圆形或钝，基部渐狭窄，边缘具不整齐的圆齿或钝牙齿，两面均被多细胞柔毛；叶柄甚短或与叶片近等长。花葶高3-9(18）厘米，被白色多细胞柔毛；花序近头状，具2-5(7）花；苞片狭披针形或线形，长2-3毫米，密被毛，常染紫色；花萼杯状，长约6毫米，外面被多细胞柔毛，分裂达中部，裂片大小稍不相等，窄卵形至披针形，先端钝或锐尖；花冠白色或紫色，冠筒长6-8毫米，内外两面均被柔毛，冠檐直径1-1.5厘米，裂片倒卵形，长6-8毫米，稍开展，先端具深凹缺；长花柱花：雄蕊距冠筒基部约2毫米着生，花柱约与冠筒等长；短花柱花：雄蕊着生于冠筒上部，距基部约4.5毫米，花柱长约2毫米。花期6-7月。

产于我国西藏南部（吉隆和珠穆朗玛峰西坡）。生长于沟边草地和阴坡石堆中，海拔4100-5000米。模式标本采自尼泊尔境内的珠穆朗玛峰西坡。
