## Description
Herbs low, usually more than 10 cm tall. Stems unbranched, robust, often several. Leaves mostly basal, long petiolate. Stem leaves few, opposite, &amp;#177; elliptic, pinnatipartite to pinnatisect. Inflorescences short racemose. Calyx unequally 5-lobed. Corolla tube slightly longer than calyx; galea falcate, with or without marginal protuberances.
