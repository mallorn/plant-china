白蓝翠雀花(原变种)",
39a.**Delphinium albocoeruleum var. albocoeruleum**",

## Description
Stem and pedicels retrorsely puberulent. Spur cylindric-subulate or subulate, 1.7--3.3 cm.

> * Juniperus forests, grassy slopes; 3600--4900 m. Gansu, E Qinghai, NW Sichuan, NE Xizang.
