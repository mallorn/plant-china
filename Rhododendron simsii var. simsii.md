杜鹃(原变种)",
510a.**Rhododendron simsii var. simsii**",

## Description
Corolla bright to dark red, 3.5–6 cm.

> *Pinus massoniana* forests, open upland thickets; 500–1200 (–2600) m. Anhui, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan, Zhejiang [Japan, Laos, Myanmar, Thailand].
