笔罗子(原变种)",
18a.**Meliosma rigida var. rigida** (C. B. Clarke) Ridley",

## Description
Leaf blade abaxially and inflorescences sparsely pilose.

> Broad-leaved forests; below 1500 m. Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Henan, SW Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Taiwan, S Yunnan, Zhejiang [Japan, Laos, Philippines, S Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Meliosma glomerulata* Rehder &amp; E. H. Wilson; *M. patens* Hemsley; *M. rigida* var. *patens* (Hemsley) Cufodontis; *M. simplicifolia* (Roxburgh) Walpers subsp. *rigida* (Siebold &amp; Zuccarini) Beusekom.
