云南杜英",
35c.**Elaeocarpus japonicus var. yunnanensis** C. Chen & Y. Tang",

## Description
Branchlets glabrous. Leaf blade ovate, elliptic, or obovate, glands present in lateral and tertiary vein axils. Petals pubescent on both surfaces, margin entire or cleft. Disk 10-lobed.

> ●  Evergreen forests; 1200-1700 m. S and SE Yunnan.
