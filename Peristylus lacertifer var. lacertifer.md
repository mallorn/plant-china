撕唇阔蕊兰(原变种)",
14a.**Peristylus lacertifer var. lacertifer**",

## Description
Flowers green to pale yellowish green. Lateral lobes of lip shorter than to subequal in length to mid-lobe.
  "Statistics": "Forests, thickets, grassy slopes; 600-1300 m. S Fujian, S and SW Guangdong, C and SE Guangxi, S Hainan, SW Sichuan, C Taiwan, S Yunnan [India, Indonesia, Japan (Ryukyu Islands), Malaysia, Myanmar, Philippines, Thailand, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Coeloglossum lacertiferum* Lindley, Gen. Sp. Orchid. Pl. 302. 1835; *Choeradoplectron spiranthes* Schauer; *Coeloglossum acuminatum* Lindley; *Habenaria lacertifera* (Lindley) Bentham; *H. maingayi* King &amp; Pantling; *H. meyenii* Merrill; *Peristylus chloranthus* Lindley ex Bentham; *P. spiranthes* (Schauer) S. Y. Hu.
