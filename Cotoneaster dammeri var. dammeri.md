矮生栒子(原变种)",
39a.**Cotoneaster dammeri var. dammeri**",

## Description
Petiole 2–3 mm; leaf blade elliptic or oblong-elliptic. Pedicel 4–6 mm.

> Rocky mountain areas, sparse mixed forests; 1300--2600 m. Gansu, Guizhou, Hubei, Sichuan, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Cotoneaster dammeri* subsp. *songmingensis* C. Y. Wu &amp; Lihua Zhou; *C. humifusus* Duthie ex Veitch; *C. kweitschoviensis* G. Klotz.
