浙贝母 (原变种)",
12a.**Fritillaria thunbergii var. thunbergii**",

> Widely cultivated in China for its bulbs, which are used medicinally.

## Description
Bulb of 2(or 3) scales, 1.5--3 cm in diam. Stem 50--80 cm. Leaves mostly alternate. 2&lt;I&gt; n&lt;/I&gt; = 24*.

> * Bamboo forests, shady and moist places; near sea level to 600 m. Anhui, Jiangsu, Zhejiang.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt; Uvularia cirrhosa&lt;/I&gt; Thunberg, Fl. Jap. 136. 1784; &lt;I&gt; Fritillaria austroanhuiensis&lt;/I&gt; Y. K. Yang &amp; J. K. Wu; &lt;I&gt; F. collicola&lt;/I&gt; Hance; &lt;I&gt; F. verticillata&lt;/I&gt; Willdenow var. &lt;I&gt; thunbergii&lt;/I&gt; (Miquel) Baker.
