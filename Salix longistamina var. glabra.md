无毛长蕊柳",
47b.**Salix longistamina var. glabra** Y. L. Chou",

> Very similar to both &lt;I&gt;Salix pycnostachya&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;oxycarpa&lt;/I&gt;  and &lt;I&gt;S. sericocarpa&lt;/I&gt; but filaments completely connate.

## Description
Branchlets dark purple. Male flower: filaments short, ca. 4 ×  as long as bracts, glabrous.

> *  Cultivated; circa 3800 m. Xizang
**44b.无毛长蕊柳（变种）**

var. glabra Y. L. Chou,植物研究, 1 (1-2): 162. 1981. ——S. longistamina C. Wang et P. Y. Fu, 植物分类学报, 12 (2): 199. 1974, quoad sp. G. S. Fu et. J. W. Chang (傅国勋及张经炜) no. 16 (masc.).

与原变种的主要区别：小枝暗紫色，花丝无毛，也较短，约为苞片长的3倍。

产我国西藏。栽培在海拔3755米。模式标本采自拉萨。

王战与傅沛云发表S. longistamina C. Wang et P. Y. Fu所列的副模式标本中（傅国勋及张经炜6号）混有此变种。本新变种与S. sericocarpa Andexss.和S. pycnostachya Anderss. var. oxycarpa (Anderss.) Y. L. Chou et C. F. Fang相近, 但花丝完全合生, 为主要区别。
