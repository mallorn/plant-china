起绒飘拂草(原变种)",
1a.**Fimbristylis dipsacea var. dipsacea**",

## Description
Leaves often as long as or shorter than culm. Glumes white, elliptic, apex with a long excurved awn. Stamens 1 or 2. Fl. and fr. Mar-Nov. 2*n* = 10.
  "Statistics": "Field margins, wet grassy places by rivers, swampy places, pond margins; below 100 m. Guangdong, Guangxi, Hainan, Yunnan [India, Indonesia, Laos, Myanmar, Papua New Guinea, Philippines, Sri Lanka, Thailand, Vietnam; Africa, N Australia, South America].
  "Synonym": "*Scirpus dipsaceus* Rottb?ll, Descr. Icon. Rar. Pl. 56. 1773.
