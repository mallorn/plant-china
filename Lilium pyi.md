## Description
Stem 40--60 cm, slender. Leaves linear, 4--7 cm × 3--5 mm. Flower solitary, nodding. Tepals with dark reddish brown spots only on margin, narrowly oblong-lanceolate; nectaries blackish, glabrous.

> * W Yunnan (Binchuan Xian).
