## Description
Plants usually tall. Lamina bipinnate to tripinnate. Stipe usually reddish or reddish brown. Base of pinnules asymmetrical, shortly serrate.
  "Statistics": "About six species: China, E Himalaya, New Guinea; five species (three endemic) in China.
  "keylist": "
1 Plants 60-85 cm tall; lamina bipinnate to tripinnatifid, pinnules incised; stipe stramineous——(2)
1 Plants 1-2 m tall; lamina bipinnate-tripinnate; stipe dark brown or ebeneous——(3)
2(1) Lamina grass-green; pinnules incised ca. 1/2 of distance to midrib and dentate at apex; sori not limited to apical half of pinnules.[*D. reflexosquamata* 倒鳞鳞毛蕨](Dryopteris reflexosquamata.md)
2 Lamina dark green; pinnules deeply cut, incised at least 2/3 of distance to midrib and sharply serrate-dentate at apex; sori typically confined to apical half of pinnules.[*D. kwanzanensis* 拟倒鳞鳞毛蕨](Dryopteris kwanzanensis.md)
3(1) Stipe and lower rachis with ovate-lanceolate to broadly ovate, glossy black scales.[*D. sikkimensis* 锡金鳞毛蕨](Dryopteris sikkimensis.md)
3 Stipe and lower rachis with thin, lanceolate, appressed, brown scales——(4)
