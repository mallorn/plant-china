缘毛橐吾(原变种)
68a.**Ligularia liatroides var. liatroides**

## Description
Leaves and involucre white pubescent.

  "Statistics": "● Stream banks, swamps, scrub-covered meadows, alpine meadows; 2900-4500 m. SW Qinghai, N, SW, and W Sichuan, NE Xizang.

  "Synonym": "*Senecio liatroides* C. Winkler, Trudy Imp. S.-Peterburgsk. Bot. Sada 13: 8. 1893; *Cremanthodium plantaginifolium* (Franchet) R. D. Good f. *winkleri* R. D. Good; *Senecillis liatroides* (C. Winkler) Kitamura.
