## Description
Branches, abaxial surface of leaf blade, petioles, and inflorescences densely pilose or tomentose. Fl. May-Jun, fr. Aug-Sep.

> ●  Mountain forests; below 800 m. S Fujian, N Guangdong, NE Guangxi, SE Guizhou, Hubei, S Hunan, S Jiangxi, Zhejiang.
  "Synonym": "*Meliosma pannosa* Handel-Mazzetti, Anz. Akad. Wiss. Wien, Math.-Naturwiss. Kl. 58: 179. 1921.
