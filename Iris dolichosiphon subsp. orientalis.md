## Description
Leaves 3--11 cm × 2--4 mm at anthesis, to 40 cm × 4 mm in fruit. Flowers 3--6 cm in diam.; perianth tube 4--7 cm.

> Among shrubs, alpine meadows, limestone cliffs; 3000--4300 m. Sichuan, Yunnan [NE India, Myanmar].
