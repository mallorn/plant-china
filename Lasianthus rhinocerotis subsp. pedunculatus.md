## Description
Young branches and leaf abaxially densely hairy. Leaf blade papery to leathery, base broadly cuneate to truncate or subrounded; lateral veins 8-12 pairs; nervules densely anastomosing, parallel; stipules ± inconspicuous. Bracts relatively thick, linear to narrowly triangular.
  "Statistics": "Forests, shaded and wet places; 300-1000 m. Guangxi, Hainan, Yunnan [N Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Lasianthus rhinocerotis* var. *pedunculatus* Pitard in Lecomte, Fl. Indo-Chine 3: 395. 1924; *L. koi* Merrill &amp; Chun.
