## Description
Leaves large, few, usually 2 or 3; blade orbicular, palmately medially sect; petioles amplexicaul, shortly sheathed at base. Involucres narrowly campanulate; phyllaries 3-5. Florets 5-10. Pappus sordid white.
  "Synonym": "*Cacalia* sect. *Taimingasa* Kitamura, Acta Phytotax. Geobot. 20: 172. 1962.
