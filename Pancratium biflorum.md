## Description
Leaves several, ensiform, 30--45 × 2.5--5 cm. Flowering stem shorter than leaves. Inflorescences 2- or 3-flowered; involucres lanceolate. Perianth tube green, slender, 10--12 cm; lobes white, linear, subequaling tube. Staminal cup ca. 1/3 as long as perianth tube and equal to free part of filament. Fl. Jul--Aug.

> Hong Kong [India].
**1. 全能花（中国种子植物科属辞典）**

Pancratium biflorum Roxb. Fl. Ind. 2: 125. 1824; Benth. Fl. Hongk. 367. 1861; Maxim. in Bot. Jahrb. 6: 81. 1885; C. H. Wright in Journ. Linn. Soc. Bot. 36: 90. 1903.

叶数枚，剑形，长30-45厘米，宽2.5-5厘米。花茎比叶短；伞形花序有花2-3朵；佛焰苞状总苞片披针形；花被管纤细，长10-12厘米，绿色，花被裂片狭线形，白色，与花被管近等长；杯状体（雄蕊杯）长约为花被管的1/3，花丝离生部分约与杯状体等长。花期夏季。

香港曾有记载；也分布于印度。模式标本采自印度。
