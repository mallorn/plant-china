## Description
Shrubs, 2-3 m tall; branches terete, densely tomentulose to hirtellous becoming glabrescent. Leaves opposite; petiole 5-15 mm, densely tomentulose; blade drying papery, elliptic or elliptic-ovate, 9-15.5 × 2-6 cm, adaxially sparsely hispidulous to glabrous on lamina and sparsely to densely puberulent on principal veins, abaxially sparsely strigillose to hispidulous, base cuneate to acute, apex acute to acuminate; secondary veins 7-9 pairs; stipules generally persistent, pandurate, 5-7 × 3-4 mm, densely hirtellous, pilosulous, or strigillose, apically spreading, obtuse to rounded. Inflorescences paniculate, pyramidal in outline, 11-12 × 11-14 cm, branched to 2 or 3 orders, densely pilosulous to strigillose, pedunculate; peduncle 0.8-1.5 cm; bracts linear to narrowly lanceolate, 1-1.5 mm. Flowers sessile or subsessile. Calyx densely pilosulous to puberulent; hypanthium portion ellipsoid to turbinate, 0.8-1.2 mm; limb deeply lobed; lobes triangular, ca. 0.3 mm. Corolla white, funnelform, outside glabrous; tube 4-5 mm, inside glabrous or puberulent in throat; lobes ovate, 0.6-1 mm. Anthers elliptic, subsessile, ca. 0.8 mm, partially exserted. Stigma bilobed, ca. 0.4 mm. Capsules not seen.

**22. 思茅水锦树（植物分类学报）**

Wendlandia augustinii Cowan in Not. Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 16: 298, pl. 235. fig. 6. 1932; How in Sunyatsenia 7 (1-2): 56. 1948; W. C. Chen in Acta Phytotax. Sinica 21 (4): 397. 1983; 云南种子植物名录, 下册: 1276. 1984.

灌木，高2-3米；小枝纤细，有褐色柔毛。叶纸质，椭圆形或椭圆状卵形，长达13厘米，宽4厘米，顶端稍渐尖，基部楔形，上面有微弯的白色刚毛和短硬毛，后变无毛，下面有较弯曲的柔毛；侧脉8-9对；叶柄长5-10毫米；托叶革质，宿存，中部收缩，顶端圆形，宽3-4毫米，有疏硬毛。花序顶生，密被柔毛，长不过12厘米；苞片线形，有柔毛；花无梗，或有时具短花梗；花萼长约1毫米，有疏柔毛，裂片三角形，长约0.3毫米；花冠淡白色，冠管管状，在上部稍扩大，无毛，长5毫米，裂片卵形，长0.6-0.8毫米；花药椭圆形，花丝很短；柱头2裂，稍伸出。果未见。

产于云南思茅；生于海拔1270米的山地林中。模式标本采自云南思茅。
