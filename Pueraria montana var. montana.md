葛(原变种)",
5a.**Pueraria montana var. montana**",

## Description
Bracts shorter than bracteoles. Calyx 7-8 mm. Standard ca. 8 mm in diam. Legumes 4-9 cm × 6-8 mm. Fl. Jul-Sep, fr. Oct-Dec.
  "Statistics": "Open places, thickets, forests. Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Gui-zhou, Hainan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan, Zhe-jiang [Japan, Laos, Myanmar, Philippines, Thailand, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Dolichos montanus* Loureiro, Fl. Cochinch. 2: 440. 1790; *Glycine javanica* Linnaeus (1753), not *Pueraria javanica* (Ben-tham) Bentham (1865); *P. lobata* (Willdenow) Ohwi var. *mon-tana* (Loureiro) Maesen; *P. thunbergiana* (Siebert &amp; Zuccarini) Bentham var. *formosana* Hosokawa; *P. tonkinensis* Gagnepain.
