## Description
Herbs, rhizomatous, with branched leafy stems. Inflorescences racemelike, with pendent flowers. Sepals petaloid. Corolla with 2 planes of symmetry, flattened, broadly cordate in profile. Stamens mostly free, only shortly cohering below anthers. Seeds black, carunculate.
  "Synonym": "*Capnorchis* Borkhausen (1797), not Miller (1754); *Eucapnos* Siebold &amp; Zuccarini (1843), not Bernhardi (1833).
