藤五加(原变种)",
7a.**Eleutherococcus leucorrhizus var. leucorrhizus**",

## Description
Leaflets (3-)5, abaxially pubescent when young, both surfaces soon glabrous.

> ● Scrub fields, forest margins in valleys; 100-3200 m. Anhui, Gansu, Guangdong, Guizhou, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan, Zhejiang.
  "Synonym": "*Acanthopanax cuspidatus* G. Hoo; *A. leucorrhizus* (Oliver) Harms; *A. leucorrhizus* f. *angustifoliatus* G. Hoo; *A. leucorrhizus* var. *axillaritomentosus* G. Hoo; *Eleutherococcus cuspidatus* (G. Hoo) H. Ohashi; *E. leucorrhizus* var. *axillaritomentosus* (G. Hoo) H. Ohashi; *E. leucorrhizus* var. *brevipedunculatus* Y. R. Ling.
