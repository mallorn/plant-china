## Description
Leaflets densely pubescent especially abaxially, densely and shortly velutinous-pubescent adaxially. Fruit small, 2.5-4 cm in diam., large wings 1.3-2 cm wide, very sparsely pubescent.
  "Statistics": "● Dense valley forests, thickets; ca. 1100 m. NW Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Illigera mollissima* W. W. Smith, Notes Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinburgh 10: 42. 1917.
**2b.多毛青藤（变种）葛根（云南元谋）**

Illigera cordata Dunn var. mollissima (W. W. Sm.) Kubitzki in Bot. Jahrb. 89: 176. 1969.——I. rnollisshna W. W. Sm. in Notes Bot. Gard. Edinb. 10: 42. 1917; Hand.-Mazz. Symb. Sin. 7: 260. 1931; Merr. in Journ. Arn. Arb. 23: 166. 1942. ——I. cordata Dunn in Journ. Linn. Soc. Bot. 38: 296. 1908, p. p. min.

与原变种不同在于小叶密被柔毛，尤其在下面密被毡状短柔毛；果较小而被极疏的短柔毛。

产云南北部。生于海拔1100米山谷密林或灌丛中。

云南元谋民间药用根，作牲畜滋补药。
