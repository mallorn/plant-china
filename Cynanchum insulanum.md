海南杯冠藤",
18.**Cynanchum insulanum** (Hance) Hemsley",

## Description
Herbs, glabrous except for pubescent nodes, petioles, and leaf margin. Rhizome nodes sometimes forming globose tubers. Stems twining, to 60 cm. Leaves opposite; petiole 1-15 mm; leaf blade oblong-hastate, ovate-triangular, to linear-lanceolate, 1-4 &amp;#215; 0.1-1.5 cm, base rounded to subcordate-hastate, apex acute or short apiculate; lateral veins 5 or 6 pairs. Inflorescences umbel-like, much shorter than leaves, 4- or 5-flowered. Sepals oblong, ca. 1 &amp;#215; 0.5 mm, acute; basal glands 5. Corolla greenish white, subrotate; tube ca. 0.5 mm; lobes ovate-oblong, ca. 2 &amp;#215; 1 mm, acute. Corona tube cupular, thin membranous, ca. 2 mm, longer than gynostegium, longitudinally plicate; anthers nearly square, appendages ovate; pollinia oblong. Stigma head capitate, included within anthers. Follicles lanceolate, 4.5-5 cm &amp;#215; 7-8 mm, with 2 inconspicuous longitudinal ridges, glabrous. Seeds oblong, ca. 3 mm; coma ca. 2 cm.

**53a．海南杯冠藤（原变种）**

Cynanchum insulanum (Hance) Hemsl. var. insulanum

柔弱草质藤本，全株无毛。叶对生，长圆状戟形至三角状披针形，长2-3.5厘米，宽0.5-1.5厘米；侧脉5对；叶柄长约1厘米。伞形聚伞花序腋生，着花4-5朵；花萼5深裂，内面基部有腺体5个，裂片长圆形；花冠绿白色，裂片长圆形，长约3毫米；副花冠杯状，薄膜质，顶端浅10裂，裂片顶端钝形；花粉块每室1个，下垂，长圆形；花药近四方形；柱头全缘。蓇葖单生，长披针形，长4.5-5厘米，直径8毫米；种子长圆形，长3毫米；种毛白色绢质，长2厘米。花期5-10月，果期10月至翌年春季。

产于广东和广西等省区。生长于海边砂地或海拔50米的平原疏林中。模式标本采自广东海南。
**53b. 线叶杯冠藤（植物分类学报）**

Cynanchum insulanum (Hance) Hemsl. var. lineare (Tsiang et Zhang) Tsiang et Zhang in Acta Phytotax. Sinica 12: 109. 1974. ——Cyathella insulana (Hance) Tsiang et Zhang var. linearis Tsiang ex Zhang in Acta Phytotax. Sinica 10: 39, Pl. 10. 1965.

与原变种不同之处：副花冠10深裂，裂片渐尖；叶线状披针形，长1-4厘米，宽1-3毫米；叶柄长1-4毫米。花期6-11月。

产于广东（南部和海南）。生长于海边湿地。模式标本采自海南。
