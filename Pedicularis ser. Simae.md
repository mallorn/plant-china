## Description
Herbs not drying black, pubescent. Stems branched basally or unbranched. Leaves opposite or in whorls of 3, petiolate, oblong, pinnatipartite. Inflorescences spicate. Calyx lobes 5, unequal. Corolla tube &amp;#177; erect; galea longer than lower lip, terminating in a very short obtuse beak, marginal teeth lacking.
  "Synonym": "Ser. &lt;I&gt;Verticillatae&lt;/I&gt; Maximowicz.
