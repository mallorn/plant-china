狭叶金丝桃",
22b.**Hypericum lagarocladum subsp. angustifolium** N. Robson",

## Description
Plants to 1.2 m tall; branches arching to pendulous. Stem internodes 1-2.5 cm. Leaves narrowly elliptic to narrowly lanceolate, 2-4.5 × 0.8-1.1 cm; abaxial glands dense to absent, apex acute to obtuse. Inflorescence 1(or 2)-flowered. Sepals lanceolate, ca. 3 mm wide, apex acuminate. Capsule narrowly ovoid-conic (3-4.5 mm wide).

> ● 400-1400 m. Guizhou, W Hunan, E Yunnan.
