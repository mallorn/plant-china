## Description
Shrubs deciduous, erect, unarmed. Stem and branches terete, indistinctly striate-ridged. Petiole 5--10 mm, widened toward base, abaxially striate-ridged, adaxially channeled, papillose-puberulent in distal 1/2, narrowly winged for ca. 2/3 its length; wings apically confluent to petiole; abscission zone apical; tendrils absent. Leaf blade ovate or broadly so, 2--4 × 1--3 cm, papery, abaxially papillose-puberulent on basal veins. Inflorescence of 1 umbel, basally not prophyllate. Flowers very similar to those of &lt;I&gt; Smilax stans&lt;/I&gt;. Berries black, globose, 5--7 mm in diam. Fl. May--Jun, fr. Oct.

> * Forests, thickets, shaded places on slopes; 1300--3100 m. Gansu, Henan, Hubei, Shaanxi, Sichuan.

和上种极相似，但在叶下面的主脉和支脉的下半部或近基部，以及叶柄的上半部都具有乳突状毛。花期5-6月，果期10月。

产陕西（秦岭以南）、甘肃（东南部）、四川、湖北（西部）和河南（西部）o生于海拔1300-3100米的林下、灌丛中或山坡阴处。
