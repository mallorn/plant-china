柔毛针刺悬钩子",
54b.**Rubus pungens var. villosus** Cardot",

## Description
Branchlets and abaxial surface of calyx with dense, needle-like prickles. Flower bearing branchlets, petioles, pedicel, and abaxial surface of calyx with conspicuous stalked glands. Leaflets 5–7, 1–3 cm.

> Grassy slopes; below 2600 m. Hubei, Shaanxi, Sichuan.
**49b.柔毛针刺悬钩子（变种）**

R. pungens Camb. var. villosus Card. in Lecomte, Not. Syst. 3: 307. 1914

枝和花萼外密被针刺；花枝、叶柄和花梗上均有稀疏柔毛和明显腺毛；小叶5-7枚，较小，长1-3厘米，顶端钝或急尖，两面有稀疏柔毛。

产陕西、湖北、四川。生草坡，海拔达2600米。
