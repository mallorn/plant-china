## Description
Stem leaves many; blade palmately medially to deeply 5-7-lobed, palmately veined. Phyllaries 3-5, rarely 7 or 8. Florets 3-5, rarely 10-14. Pappus white or red-brown.
  "Synonym": "*Cacalia* sect. *Delphiniifoliae* H. Koyama, Mem. Fac. Sci. Kyoto Univ., Ser. Biol. 2(2): 168. 1969.
