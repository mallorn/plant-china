## Description
Lamina lanceolate or ovate-lanceolate, usually pinnate to bipinnate; upper stipe with many small scales; scales on base of stipe brown or light brown, lanceolate; scales bullate or rarely flat.
  "Statistics": "About 20 species: confined to Asia; 11 species (three endemic) in China.
