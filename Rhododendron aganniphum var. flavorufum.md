黄毛雪山杜鹃",
365b.**Rhododendron aganniphum var. flavorufum** (I. B. Balfour & Forrest) D. F. Chamberlain",

## Description
Petiole 10–15 mm, eglandular; leaf blade 4.5–12 × 2.5–6 cm; abaxial surface indumentum 1-layered, at first yellow, becoming deep red-brown, splitting to irregularly patchy, with pellicle. Fl. Jun–Jul, fr. Oct.

> Coniferous forests, thickets, rocky slopes; 3200–4400 m. SW Sichuan, SE Xizang, NW Yunnan.
**158b.黄毛雪山杜鹃（变种）（新拟）黄色海绵杜鹃（西藏植物志、云南植物志）图版68: 6-7**

Rhododendron aganniphum Balf. f. et K. Ward. var. flavorufum (Balf. f. et Forrest) Chamb. ex Cullen et Chamb. in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 36: 119. 1978; Chamb. in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 39:357. 1982; 西藏植物志3: 618, 图247: 5-9. 1986; 云南植物志4: 422. 1986. ——R. flavorufum Balf. f. et Forrest in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 11: 65. 1919; Tagg in Stevenson, Spec. Rhodod. 677. 1930.

本变种与原变种的区别是叶片椭圆形，下面毛被厚，初为黄色，后变为深红棕色，呈不规则的块状分裂。花期6-7月，果期10月。

产四川西南部、云南西北部和西藏东南部。生于海拔3 250-4 400米的针叶林下或多岩石的山坡杜鹃灌丛中。模式标本采自云南德钦。
