## Description
Leaves semideciduous; leaf blade more than 2.2 × as long as broad. Corolla narrowly campanulate, plum purple; lobes with scales abaxially.

> Forest margins, *Rhododendron* thickets; 3300–3500 m. NW Yunnan (Dulongjiang) [NE Myanmar].
