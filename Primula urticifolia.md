荨麻叶报春",
238.**Primula urticifolia** Maximowicz",

## Description
Herbs perennial. Leaves forming a lax rosette; petiole 2--5 X as long as leaf blade, narrowly winged; leaf blade suborbicular to broadly ovate, 3--7 mm wide, efarinose but sparsely minutely glandular, base cuneate, pinnately 5--11-lobed, apex rounded; lobules linear-oblong, ca. 1 mm, apex obtuse. Scapes 2--7 cm, 1--3-flowered; bracts subulate, 3--5 mm. Pedicel 0.5--1.5 cm, sparingly glandular. Flowers heterostylous. Calyx narrowly campanulate, 4--5 mm, parted to middle, 5-ribbed; lobes ovate to oblong, apex acute. Corolla rose-pink; tube ca. 2 X as long as calyx; limb 1--1.5 cm wide; lobes broadly obovate, deeply emarginate. Pin flowers: stamens near base of corolla tube; style ca. as long as to slightly longer than calyx. Thrum flowers: stamens at middle of corolla tube; style ca. 2 mm. Capsule oblong, slightly shorter than calyx.

> * In limestone rock crevices; ca. 4000 m. E Qinghai (Datong Xian, Xining Shi)
**234.荨麻叶报春（拉汉植物名称）图版46: 5**

Primula urticifolia Maxim. in Bull. Acad. St. Petersb. 27: 497. 1881; Forbes et Hemsl. in Journ. Linn. Soc. Bot. 26: 43. 1889; Bax in Engl. Pflanzenr. 22 (IV-237): lll. 1905; W. W. Smith et Fletcher in Trans. Roy. Soc. Edinb. (1): 56. 1943.

多年生柔小草本，高3-8厘米，具极短的根状茎和多数须根。叶丛疏松，叶片近圆形或阔卵形，直径3-7毫米，基部楔形，边缘近掌状5-11裂，裂深约1毫米，小裂片线状矩圆形或近三角形，先端钝，无毛，两面散布有少数小腺体，中肋在下面明显，侧脉隐蔽；叶柄具狭翅，长于叶片2-5倍。花葶纤细，高2-7厘米；花1-3朵生于花葶端；苞片钻形，长3-5毫米，基部下延；花梗长5-15毫米，疏被小腺体，顶端稍增粗；花萼狭钟状，长4-6 毫米，具5棱，分裂深达中部，裂片卵形至矩圆形，先端稍锐尖；花冠玫瑰红色，冠筒约长于花萼1倍，喉部无环状附属物，冠檐直径1-1.5厘米，裂片阔倒卵形，先端具深凹缺；长花柱花：雄蕊靠近冠筒基部着生，花柱长与花萼相等或微高出花萼；短花柱花：雄蕊着生于冠筒中部，花柱长约2毫米。蒴果稍短于宿存花萼。

产于青海东部（大通、西宁）。生长于石灰岩石缝中，海拔约4000米。模式标本采自大通。
