美容杜鹃(原变种)
207a.**Rhododendron calophytum var. calophytum** (C. B. Clarke) Ridley

## Description
Leaf blade oblong-oblanceolate or oblong-lanceolate, 11–30 × 4–7.5 cm; apex acuminate. Inflorescence 15–30-flowered, rachis 15–20 mm. Pedicel 3–6.5 cm; corolla widely campanulate, lobes 5–7. Fl. Apr–May, fr. Sep–Oct.


> Forests; 1800–4000 m. SE Gansu, C and N Guizhou, W Hubei, S Shaanxi, Sichuan, NE Yunnan.
