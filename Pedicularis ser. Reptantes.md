## Description
Herbs low, repent. Stem leaves opposite, petiolate, rounded, small; segments 3 or 4 pairs. Flowers axillary. Calyx lobes 5. Corolla tube &amp;#177; elongated, ca. 2 X as long as calyx; beak straight, short.
