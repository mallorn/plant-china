毛瓣无患子(原变种)
2a.**Sapindus rarak var. rarak** (C. B. Clarke) Ridley

## Description
Leaf axis and abaxial surface of leaflets glabrous.


> Sparse forests; 500-1700 m. Taiwan, Yunnan [Bhutan, Cambodia, India, Indonesia, Laos, W Malaysia, Myanmar, Sri Lanka, Thailand, Vietnam].

  "Synonym": "*Dittelasma rarak* (Candolle) Hiern.
