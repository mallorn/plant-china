## Description
Bulb ovoid, ca. 2 cm in diam.; scales pale brown, lanceolate, ca. 2.5 cm × 7 mm. Stem 20--30 cm. Leaves scattered, occasionally several crowded and subwhorled, ovate or elliptic-lanceolate, 2--4 × 0.8--1.2 cm. Flower solitary, nodding, campanulate. Tepals purple-red, with dark spots adaxially, narrowly elliptic, 2.3--2.5 cm × 8--9 mm, basally saccate; nectaries not papillose. Stamens converging; filaments 1--1.2 cm, glabrous; anthers ca. 6 mm. Ovary purple-red, cylindric, 8--10 × 2--3 mm. Style 8--9 mm; stigma swollen, shallowly 3-lobed. Fl. Jul.

> * Bushy and grassy slopes; ca. 3900 m. SE Xizang (Mainling Xian).
