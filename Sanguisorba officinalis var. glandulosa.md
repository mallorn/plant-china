腺地榆",
1b.**Sanguisorba officinalis var. glandulosa** (Komarov) Voroschilov",

## Description
Base of petiole or stem sparsely glandular hairy; leaflets of radical leaves ovate or oblong-ovate, abaxially sparsely pilose, base cordate to subcordate. Sepals purple, red, or tinged purple. Filaments nearly equaling sepals. Fl. and fr. Jul–Sep.

> Forest margins, damp places in ravines; 600--1900 m. Gansu, Heilongjiang, Shaanxi [Mongolia, E Russia].
  "Synonym": "*Sanguisorba glandulosa* Komarov, Bot. Mater. Gerb. Glavn. Bot. Sada SSSR 6: 10. 1926.
**1b. 腺地榆（变种）（东北植物检索表）**

P. officinalis L. var. glandulosa (Kom.) Worosch. ФЛ. Советск. Дальн. Вост. 265. 1966; 俞德浚、李朝銮, 植物分类学报 17(1): 9. 1979. ——Sanguisorba glandzdsa Kom. in Not. Syst. Herb. Inst. Bot. URSS 6: 10. 1926; 东北植物检索表 156. 1959; 东北草本植物志 5: 54. 图版24: 5-9. 1976.

本变种茎、叶柄及花序梗或多或少有柔毛和腺毛，叶下面散生短柔毛。花果期7-9月。

产黑龙江、陕西、甘肃。生山谷阴湿处林缘，海拔630-1820米。苏联远东地区也有分布。
