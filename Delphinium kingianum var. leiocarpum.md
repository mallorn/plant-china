## Description
Stem densely retrorsely puberulent and yellow glandular puberulent. Leaf segments apically acute. Raceme densely yellow glandular puberulent, mixed with a few retrorse, white, short hairs. Ovary mostly glabrous, with only a few hairs on ventral suture.
