18a.**Manglietia fordiana var. fordiana**",

## Description
Leaf blade leathery, reticulate veins inconspicuous on both surfaces when dry, margin not wavy. Peduncle with reddish brown trichomes. Outer tepals oblong-elliptic. Carpel with a ca. 1 mm beak. Fl. May, fr. Oct. 2*n* = 38.
