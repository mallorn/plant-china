狭叶总梗委陵菜",
22c.**Potentilla peduncularis var. vittata** (Soják) H. Ikeda & H. Ohba",

## Description
Leaflets narrowly ovate, 1.5–3 cm, margin bluntly 5–9-serrate or apex dentate. Fl. and fr. Jun–Oct.

> Alpine meadows, gravelly slopes; 3000--4200 m. Xizang, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Potentilla vittata* Soják, Candollea 43: 164. 1988; *P. vittata* var. *abbreviata* Soják; *P. vittata* var. *assidens* Soják; *P. vittata* var. *pluriflora* Soják; *P. oxyodonta* Soják.
