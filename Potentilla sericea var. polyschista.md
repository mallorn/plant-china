变叶绢毛委陵菜
38b.**Potentilla sericea var. polyschista** (Boissier) Lehmann

## Description
Radical leaves with 2 pairs of leaflets; leaflets adjacent, abaxially densely white tomentose, densely sericeous on veins, margin not revolute.


> Alpine meadows, rock crevices on mountain slopes; 4400--5200 m. Xizang [NW Himalayas to Kashmir].

  "Synonym": "*Potentilla polyschista* Boissier, Diagn. Pl. Orient., ser. 1, 10: 6. 1849.

**35b. 变叶绢毛委陵菜（变种）**

P. sericea L. var. polyschista Lehm. Revis. Potent. 34. 1856. ——P. polyschista Boiss. Oiagn. Pl. Or. Nov. ser, 1. 10:6. 1849.

基生叶只有小叶2对，叶片接近，叶边不反卷，下面密被白色绒毛，沿脉密生绢毛；茎生叶1-2，具3小叶或单叶有深裂片；托叶有齿或全缘，密被柔毛。

产我国西藏。生高山草甸及山坡石缝中，海拔4400-5200米。分布喜马拉雅西北部至克什米尔地区。
