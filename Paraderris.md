## Description
Lianas. Stipules small. Leaves imparipinnate; stipels much reduced or absent. Flowers in axillary pseudoracemes, occasionally aggregated into pseudopanicles. Brachyblasts rather thin, with (1 or)2 or 3(-5) flowers. Bracts shorter than corresponding flower buds. Bracteoles present. Calyx with 5 short lobes. Corolla white to rose-red; standard blade elliptic to suborbicular, reflexed at base; basal calluses distinct, large; wings and keel petals ± equally long. Stamens monadelphous; upper staminal filament adnate to other 9; anthers usually with trichomes. Disk distinct, flat to slightly hollow. Ovary with trichomes; ovules 1-7. Legume indehiscent, woody to thickly woody; either both sutures with a longitudinal wing or winged only on adaxial suture. Seeds 1 to several per legume, lens-shaped; radicle folded.
