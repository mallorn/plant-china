## Description
Stem erect, 28-32 cm tall, 4-6 mm in diam. at base, proximally glabrous, distally and synflorescence shortly yellowish brown pilose. Rosette of leaves not seen. Stem leaves oblong or ovate-oblong, 7-15 × 4.5-6 cm, smaller distally, glabrous or abaxially shortly pilose along veins, pinnately veined, base cuneate or rounded, margin denticulate, apex rounded; proximal stem leaves shortly petiolate, sheath amplexicaul; distal stem leaves sessile, base semiamplexicaul. Synflorescence racemose, 7-22 cm; leaflike bracts linear (except lower one ovate); peduncles 1-5 cm. Capitula 4-13; supplementary bracts linear. Involucre green or blackish purple, campanulate, 8-10 × 6-7 mm, outside shortly pilose; phyllaries 8-12, in 2 rows, lanceolate, apex acuminate; inner phyllaries membranous at margin. Ray florets 8-10, yellow; lamina linear-oblong, ca. 10 × 2 mm, apex obtuse. Tubular florets numerous, 6.5-7 mm; tube 2.5-3 mm. Achenes cylindric, ca. 5 mm. Pappus white, shorter than tubular corolla. Fl. and fr. Jul-Oct.

**89．木里橐吾**

Ligularia muliensis Hand. -Mazz. in Bot. Jahrb. 69: 117. 1938; S. Y. Hn in Quart. Journ. Taiwan Mus. 26: 66. 1967; H. Koyama in Mem. Fac. Sci. Kyoto, Univ. Ser. Biol. 2: 50. 1968, in add.

多年生草本。根肉质，被短毛。茎直立，高28-32厘米，上部及花序被黄褐色有节短柔毛，下部光滑，基部直径．4-6毫米，被枯叶柄纤维包围。丛生叶未见；茎生叶长圆形或卵状长圆形，长7-15厘米，宽4.5-6厘米，向上渐小，先端圆形，边缘具软骨质小齿，基部楔形或近圆形，两面光滑或下面脉上有短毛，叶脉羽状，下部叶有短柄，鞘状抱茎，上部叶无柄，半抱茎。总状花序长7-9厘米，苞片除最下部1枚为卵形外，余者均为线形；花序梗长3-5毫米；头状花序4-13，辐射状；小苞片线形；总苞钟状，长8-10毫米，宽6-7毫米，绿色或紫黑色，总苞片8-10，2层，披针形，先端渐尖，背部被有节柔毛，内层边缘膜质。舌状花8-10，黄色，舌片线状长圆形，长约10毫米，宽约2毫米，先端钝；管状花长6.5-7毫米，管部长2.5-3毫米，冠毛白色比花冠稍短。瘦果圆柱形，长约5毫米，具肋，光滑。花果期7-10月。

产云南西北部、四川西南部。生于海拔3800-3900米的草坡、林下及灌丛中。模式标本采自四川木里。
