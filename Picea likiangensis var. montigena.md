康定云杉",
11d.**Picea likiangensis var. montigena** (Masters) W. C. Cheng",

## Description
First-year branchlets usually stout, with nodes of normal length, densely pubescent. Leaves usually with 1-4 stomatal lines along each surface abaxially. Seed scales green, tinged red-brown or reddish purple on distal margin before maturity, 4-9 cm.

> *  Mountains; above 3300 m. W Sichuan

本变种小枝有密毛，叶下面每边有1一4条气孔线，而近似川西云杉，与丽江云杉（原变种）及川西云杉的区别在球果成熟前种鳞露出部分背部绿色，上部边缘红色或紫红色。

产于四川西部康定折多山（模式标本产地）海拔3300米以上地带。

木材性质和用途同丽江云杉。
