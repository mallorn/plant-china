## Description
Shrubs erect. Stems robust. Leaflets abaxially sparsely spreading pilose, adaxially densely silvery tomentose or sericeous, margin flat. Fl. and fr. Jun–Sep.

> Forest margins, thickets, alpine meadows, dry mountain slopes; 400--4600 m. Sichuan, Xinjiang, Xizang, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Potentilla arbuscula* var. *albicans* (Rehder &amp; E. H. Wilson) Handel-Mazzetti; *P. arbuscula* var. *bulleyana* I. B. Balfour ex H. R. Fletcher; *P. fruticosa* var. *vilmoriniana* Komarov.

本变种小叶下面密被银白色绒毛或绢毛，易与原变种相区别。花果期6-9月。

产新疆、四川、云南、西藏。生高山草地、干旱山坡、林缘及灌丛中，海拔400-4600米。
