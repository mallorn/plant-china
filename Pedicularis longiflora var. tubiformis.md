## Description
Leaves alternate. Calyx lobes 2. Corolla small; lower lip with a narrow maroon stripe on each antero-lateral ridge of palate. Fl. and fr. May--Oct.

> Alpine meadows, springs, seeps, along streams; 2700--5300 m. W Sichuan, SE Xizang, NW Yunnan [Nepal, Pakistan, Sikkim].

自我国云南西北部与四川西部经东喜马拉雅至西喜马拉雅，我国西藏昌都专区以西地区亦均有之，生于海拔2,700-5,300米的高山草甸及溪流两旁等处。
