秦氏耳蕨组
6.**Polystichum sect. Chingiarum** Li Bing Zhang

> Plants medium-sized, evergreen. Lamina thickly papery, 1-pinnate. Rachis without proliferous bulbils. Pinnae without open hard spinules at apex or on margin, without cartilaginous margins; auricles well developed. Sori in 2 or 3 rows on each side of midrib, abaxial on veinlets.Type: *Polystichum chingiae* Ching.

  "Statistics": "About one species: S China and N Vietnam.

  "Synonym": "*Sectio nova *Polysticho *sect. *Achrolomati* affinis, sed ab eo pinnis non cartilagineis atque soris 2- vel 3-seriatis utroque costae latere differt*.
