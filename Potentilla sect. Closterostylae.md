## Description
Herbs perennial. Basal leaves pinnate, sometimes 3-foliolate. Ovary glabrous; style sub-basal, fusiform, thickened at middle, gradually tapering at both ends.
  "Statistics": "About 20 species: mainly temperate regions of N hemisphere; one species in China.
  "Synonym": "*Potentilla* subsect. *Closterostylae* Th. Wolf, Biblioth. Bot. 16(Heft 71): 48. 1908.
