首阳变豆菜(原变种)",
15a.**Sanicula giraldii var. giraldii** (C. B. Clarke) Ridley",

## Description
Fertile flowers usually 3 per umbellule. Calyx teeth ovate, ca. 0.5 × 0.3 mm. Fruit broadly ovoid, 2–2.5 × 2.5–3 mm.

> Forests on mountain slopes, stream banks, roadsides; 1500–3400 m. Gansu, Hebei, Henan, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shanxi, N and W Sichuan, S Xizang.
