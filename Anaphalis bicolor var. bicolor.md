二色香青(原变种)",
13a.**Anaphalis bicolor var. bicolor**",

## Description
Upper parts of plants yellow or whitish yellow cottony tomentose, lower parts ash-gray cottony tomentose, capitate glandular hairs with long stalk under lanate hairs. Leaves dense; internodes usually 2-5 mm. Upper leaves somewhat erect or adnate to stems; leaves linear or oblong-linear, 1.5-4 cm × 2-4 mm, apex acuminate. Involucre 6-6.5 mm.
  "Statistics": "● 2000-3500 m. SW and W Sichuan, N and W Yunnan.
