带叶瓦韦(原变种)
39a.**Lepisorus loriformis var. loriformis**

> The name was first used by Wallich (Numer. List, no. 271. 1829, nom. nud.).


## Description
Rhizome 1.5-2 mm in diam.; scales dark or light brown, 2.2-5 × (0.5-)0.8-1.5 mm. Lamina linear-lanceolate, 20-40 × 0.5-2.5 cm, widest at middle. Sori 2-3 mm in diam.; paraphyses 0.2-0.3 mm in diam.


> ● On tree trunks or in rock crevices in forests; 2000-2700 m. Gansu, Hubei, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan.
