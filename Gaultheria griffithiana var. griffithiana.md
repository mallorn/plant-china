尾叶白珠(原变种)",
18a.**Gaultheria griffithiana var. griffithiana**",

## Description
Leaf base cuneate to obtuse-rounded. Rachis and pedicels pubescent; bracts ovate, 2–4 mm, both surfaces subglabrous. Calyx subglabrous adaxially.

> *Abies*, *Pinus*, *Quercus*, *Rhododendron*, and *Tsuga* forests, thickets on slopes; 2000–2800(–3600) m. SW Sichuan, SE Xizang, SE and W Yunnan [Bhutan, NE India (Darjiling), Myanmar, Nepal, Sikkim, Vietnam].
