角萼翠雀花(原变种)",
128a.**Delphinium ceratophorum var. ceratophorum**",

## Description
Leaf blade to 6.5 × 10 cm, less divided; central lobe 3-lobulate; ultimate lobules ovate or triangular. Bracteoles contiguous to flower or nearly so. Sepals abaxially shortly strigose; hairs to 0.5 mm; cornicle 1.5--2 mm. Staminode limb 2-lobed, sometimes to middle.

> * Forest margins, grassy slopes; 2800--4200 m. NW Yunnan.
