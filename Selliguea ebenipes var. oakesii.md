## Description
Rachis and costae pubescent.

> Epiphytic on rocks; 2300-3500 m. Xizang, Yunnan [NE India].
  "Synonym": "Basionym: *Polypodium ebenipes* var. *oakesii* C. B. Clarke, Trans. Linn. Soc. London, Bot. 1: 564. 1880; *Phymatopteris ebenipes* var. *oakesii* (C. B. Clarke) Satija &amp; Bir.
**30b.毛轴黑鳞假瘤蕨（变种）**

Phymatopteris ebenipes (Hook.) Pic. Serm. var. oakesii (C. B. Clarke) Satija et Bir in Aspects Pl. Sci. 8: 61. 1985. ——Polypodium ebenipes Hook. var. oakesii C. B. Clarke in Trans. Linn. Soc. 2: Bot. 1: 564. 1880.

与原变种的区别在于叶轴及裂片中脉基部密被短柔毛。

产云南西北部、西藏东南部。附生石上，海拔2300-3500米。印度东北部。模式标本采自印度。
