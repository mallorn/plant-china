多叶香茶菜
49a.**Isodon pleiophyllus var. pleiophyllus**

## Description
Calyx ca. 4 mm, teeth ovate-triangular, less than 2 mm. Corolla ca. 8 mm.


> * Thickets on slopes, pine forests; 2800-3500 m. Yunnan.

  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Plectranthus pleiophyllus&lt;/I&gt; Diels, Notes Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinburgh 5: 228. 1912; &lt;I&gt;Rabdosia pleiophylla&lt;/I&gt; (Diels) C. Y. Wu &amp; H. W. Li.
