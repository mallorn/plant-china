腹水草",
4c.**Veronicastrum stenostachyum subsp. plukenetii** (T. Yamazaki) D. Y. Hong",

## Description
Stems arching and rooting apically, sparsely with yellow short curly hairs. Leaf blade narrowly ovate to ovate-lanceolate, 9-16 X 3-6 cm, membranous to papery. Inflorescences 1.5-5 cm. Bracts and calyx lobes subulate, ciliate or glabrous. Fl. Jul-Aug.

> * NW Fujian, NE Guizhou, Hubei (Xingshan Xian), Hunan, Jiangxi.

茎弓曲，顶端着地生根，多少被黄色倒生卷毛。叶长卵形至卵状披针形，膜质至纸质，长9-16厘米，宽3-6厘米。花序长1.5-5厘米；苞片及花萼裂片钻形，具睫毛或否。

产福建西北部、江西、湖南、贵州东北部、湖北（兴山）。生林下及林缘草地。
