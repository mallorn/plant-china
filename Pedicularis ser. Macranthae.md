> Four species: China, Himalayas; one species in China.

## Description
Leaves mostly pseudo-opposite, lanceolate; petiole long. Calyx deeply cleft anteriorly, 5-lobed. Corolla tube short, slightly exceeding calyx; beak &amp;#177; coiled, long, usually deeply 2-cleft; lower lip usually more than 1.5 cm wide.
