钟花胡颓子(原变种)",
4a.**Elaeagnus griffithii var. griffithii** (C. B. Clarke) Ridley",

## Description
Leaf blade 8-12.5 × 3-6 cm, membranous or thinly papery. Inflorescence 1-3-flowered. Calyx lobes 5-6 mm, inside densely stellate-hairy. Filaments ca. 2 mm. Fl. Nov-Feb, fr. Apr-May.

> Dense forests; 1600-2800 m. Yunnan [Bangladesh].
