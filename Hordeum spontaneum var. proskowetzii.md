## Description
Lateral spikelets: lemma apex cuspidate or with very short awn. Fl. and fr. Apr–Jun.

> Roadsides, waste places, as a weed in cultivated fields of *Hor-deum*. Sichuan, Xizang [C and SW Asia].
**8c.芒稃野大麦（变种）图版8：7**

Hordeum spontaneum C. Koch. var. proskowetzii Nabel. Publ. Fac. Sci. Univ. (Brno) 111: 32. 1929. ——Hordeum proskowetzii Nabel. 1. c. 32. nom. altern.

与钝稃野大麦的形态极为近似，仅两侧生不育小穗的外稃顶部延伸为6-10毫米长的芒。
