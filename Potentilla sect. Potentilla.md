## Description
Herbs perennial. Basal leaves palmate or 3-foliolate, rarely pinnate. Ovary glabrous; style subterminal, clavate, tapered toward base, thickened at apex.
  "Statistics": "About 20 species: mainly temperate and subarctic regions of N hemisphere; eight species (one endemic) in China.
