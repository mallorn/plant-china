类华丽龙胆 (原变种)",
58a.**Gentiana sinoornata var. sinoornata**",

## Description
Stem leaf blades linear-lanceolate to lanceolate. Corolla narrowly obconic.

> * Grassland slopes, wet meadows on river banks, alpine meadows, scrub, forests; 2800-4400 m. W Sichuan, E Xizang, NW Yunnan.
