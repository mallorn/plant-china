叉蕊薯蓣 (原变种)",
10a.**Dioscorea collettii var. collettii**",

## Description
Leaf blade triangular-cordate or ovate-lanceolate, margin not transparent. Anther connective often 1--2 × as wide as anther at anthesis. Capsule broadly obovoid, apex truncate or rounded. Fl. May--Aug, fr. Jun--Oct.

> Secondary &lt;I&gt; Quercus&lt;/I&gt; forests, scrub forests; 1500--3200 m. S Anhui, Fujian, N Guangdong, NE Guangxi, Guizhou, S Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, W Sichuan, N Taiwan, Zhejiang [India, Myanmar].
