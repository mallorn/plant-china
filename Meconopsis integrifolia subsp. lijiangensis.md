## Description
Plants mostly 45-75 cm tall, with a well-defined stem but few cauline leaves except for whorl beneath flowers. Flowers cup-shaped. Petals not markedly incurved, pale to mid-lemon yellow. Ovary narrowly obconical, densely hairy; styles short but distinct, 2-4 mm, glabrous; stigmas 5-7 mm in diam., with 4-7 rays.
