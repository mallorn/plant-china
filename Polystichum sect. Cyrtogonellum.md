> *Polystichum* sect. *Cyrtogonellum* is defined based on morphology but is possibly not monophyletic (Zhang &amp; He, Syst. Bot. 35: 13-19. 2010).

## Description
Plants medium-sized. Lamina leathery, 1-pinnate. Rachis without proliferous bulbils. Pinnae falcate-lanceolate or obliquely oblong, length more than 3 × width or only ca. 1.5 × width, symmetrical or nearly symmetrical, rarely asymmetrical at base. Venation free, rarely anastomosing to form 1 or 2 rows of areoles. Sori in 1 row on each side of midrib.
