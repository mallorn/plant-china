钝叶栒子(原变种)",
14a.**Cotoneaster hebephyllus var. hebephyllus**",

## Description
Leaf blade abaxially sparsely tomentose-villous. Fruit ovoid, sometimes oblong, 7–8 mm in diam., pyrenes 2 usually united into 1.

> Rocky mountain regions, clearings at forest margins, thickets; 1300--3400 m. Gansu, Hebei, Sichuan, SE Xizang, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Cotoneaster giraldii* Flinck &amp; Hylmö ex G. Klotz; *C. hebephyllus* var. *monopyrenus* W. W. Smith; *C. monopyrenus* (W. W. Smith) Flinck &amp; Hylmö.
