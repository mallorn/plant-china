## Description
Bud scales deciduous. Leaf blade linear or linear-lanceolate; adaxial surface usually scaly. Corolla pale rose; outer surface usually not scaly; style nearly as long as ovary. Fl. May–Jun.

> Forests, thickets; (3500–)3900–4600 m. SW Sichuan, NW Yunnan.
**166b. 筒花杜鹃（变种）（云南植物志）**

Rhododendron trichostomum Franch. var. ledoides (Balf. f. et W. W. Smith) Cowan et Davidian in R. H. S. Rhodod. Year Book, 2: 84. 1947; 云南植物志4: 533. 1986. ——R. ledoides Balf. f. et W. W. Smith in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 9: 243. 1916.

本变种与原变种的区别在于叶较狭，线状披针形；花淡蔷薇色。

产云南西北部、四川西南部。生于高山栎林及杜鹃灌丛中，海拔（3 500-）3 900-4 500米。模式标本采自云南中甸。
