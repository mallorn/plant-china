> Losina-Losinskaja (in Komarov, Fl. URSS 9: 217. 1939) divided &lt;I&gt;Parnassia palustris&lt;/I&gt; into four forms. However, after examining a large number of Chinese specimens of this species, the present authors have found that &lt;I&gt;P. palustris&lt;/I&gt; is highly variable in many morphological characters, e.g., height of stem, number, shape, and size of basal leaves, position, shape, and size of cauline leaf, punctate marking, shape, and venation of sepals and petals, and number and length of staminode branches. It is not possible to classify Losina-Losinskaja's forms according to these characters. On the other hand, the the present authors accept the two varieties of P. palustris described by Ledebour (Fl. Ross. 1: 262. 1842) based on the number of staminode branches. In the present account, according to Ledebour's delimitation, the population in Xinjiang is identified as var. palustris and the populations in N and NE China as var. &lt;I&gt;multiseta&lt;/I&gt;.

## Description
Staminodes 13-21-branched.

> Shaded places in valleys, streamsides, grassy fields; 1200-2200 m. Hebei, Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Shanxi [Japan, Korea, Russia].

本变种和原变种主要区别为退化雄蕊分枝多，(11-) 13-23条，比雄蕊长，比花瓣稍短；花期植株较高大，基生叶缺如或少而小。花期8月。

产黑龙江、吉林、辽宁、内蒙古、河北、山西以及陕甘盆地等处。生于山坡和山沟阴处、河边以及草原和路边等处，海拔1250-2220米。朝鲜、日本、阿穆尔地区、乌苏里和达乌里也有分布。模式标本采自俄罗斯（西伯利亚）。
