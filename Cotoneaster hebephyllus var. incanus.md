灰毛钝叶栒子",
14c.**Cotoneaster hebephyllus var. incanus** W. W. Smith",

## Description
Leaf blade abaxially densely gray or yellowish gray tomentose. Fruit pyriform, 5–7 mm in diam., pyrenes 2.

> Mountain regions; 2000--3300 m. NW Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Cotoneaster incanus* (W. W. Smith) G. Klotz.
**钝叶栒子灰毛变种**

C. hebephyllus var. incanus W. W. Smith in Not. Bat. Gard. Edinb. 10: 22. 1917. ——C. incanus (W. W. Smith) Klotz in Wiss. Zeits. Univ. Halle 12 (10): 766. 1963.

叶片上面无毛，下面密被灰色或黄色绒毛；果梨形，长7-9毫米，红色，具2小核。

产云南丽江。
