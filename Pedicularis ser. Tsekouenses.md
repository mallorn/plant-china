茨口系",
3.**Pedicularis ser. Tsekouenses** H. L. Li",

> One species: China and N Myanmar.

## Description
Herbs not drying black. Stems many branched basally. Leaves alternate; basal leaves numerous, long petiolate, pinnatifid to pinnatipartite. Inflorescences often capitate, few flowered. Corolla often purple spotted, large, throat expanded; galea falcate, rounded or obtuse apically, toothless; lower lip long stipitate basally.
