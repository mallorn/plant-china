头状蓼组",
4.**Polygonum sect. Cephalophilon** Meisner",

## Description
Plants ± prostrate, without prickles. Leaves ovate, oblong, or lyrate. Inflorescence capitate. Tepals fused for about 1/3 their length. Nectaries free, globular. Styles 1, bifid or trifid.
  "Statistics": "Between 20 and 30 species: mainly in E Asia; 17 species (six endemic) in China.
