## Description
Herbs, acaulescent (but shortly caulescent in *Oxytropis parasericeopetala*). Leaves imparipinnate; petioles and rachises densely white sericeous-villous; leaflets not verticillate; leaflet blades whitish gray villous. Racemes subcapitate, many flowered. Corolla purple, blue, or white; standard and keel sericeous on outside surfaces. Legume narrowly ellipsoid, ellipsoid-ovoid, or ovoid, 1-locu-lar, densely sericeous-villous.
