## Description
Leaves 9. Inner tepals absent or 3.5--7 cm, equaling or slightly longer than outer ones. Filaments 4--6 mm; anthers ca. 3 × as long as filaments; free portion of connective variable in length, 3--10(--15) mm.

> * Forests, thickets; 1800--1900 m. NE Yunnan (Yiliang Xian).
