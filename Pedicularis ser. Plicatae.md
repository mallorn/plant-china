## Description
Herbs low. Stems unbranched. Basal leaves long petiolate, persistent. Stem leaves in whorls of 4 or lowermost opposite, 1- or 2-pinnatisect. Inflorescences densely spicate. Calyx 1/2 cleft anteriorly, 4- or 5-lobed. Corolla yellow, large; tube decurved near base; galea rounded in front, plicate, toothless, beakless. Anthers apiculate at apex.
