## Description
Stems soft, long, &amp;#177; diffuse, branches many, long, curved, whorled. Stem leaves in whorls of 3 or 4, pinnatisect or pinnatipartite. Flowers axillary or racemose. Calyx lobes 5, dentate. Corolla small; tube decurved in throat of calyx tube; galea beak distinct, shorter than anther-containing part of galea; middle lobe of lower lip smaller than lateral lobes, apex hoodlike, acute or acuminate.
