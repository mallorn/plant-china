四川对囊蕨(原变种)",
19a.**Deparia sichuanensis var. sichuanensis**",

## Description
Hairs on abaxial surface of rachis and costae thick and long; pinna segments obtuse-rounded or obtuse at apex.

> ● Mountain slopes, damp areas at streamsides; 1400-3400 m. Chongqing, S Gansu, Guizhou, Sichuan, SE Xizang, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "Basionym: *Lunathyrium sichuanense* Z. R. Wang, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 32: 87. 1994.
