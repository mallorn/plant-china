毛叶返顾马先蒿",
98d.**Pedicularis resupinata subsp. lasiophylla** P. C. Tsoong",

## Description
Plants densely pubescent. Leaves long, 4--5.5 cm. Corolla pink to purple; beak conical, long. Capsule obliquely oblong-lanceolate, 1.1--1.6 cm. Fl. Jul--Aug.

> * Shaanxi.
**返顾马先蒿毛叶亚种（新亚种）**

Pedicularis resupinata Linn. subsp. lasiophylla Tsoong, subsp. nov., in Addenda 402.

这一亚种与原种的区别在于体被密短毛，叶面粗涩。其叶长4-5.5厘米，边缘反卷，锯齿上有明显的胼胝，其喙先端圆锥形而长，也和前一亚种不同。

产我国陕西太白山。
