宽叶石楠",
5c.**Photinia serratifolia var. daphniphylloides** (Hayata) L. T. Lu",

## Description
Leaves elliptic or obovate-elliptic, 8–16 cm, margin entire or very inconspicuously minutely dentate. Rachis and pedicels tomentose; hypanthium abaxially and petals glabrous.

> Sea shores; sea level. E Taiwan.
  "Synonym": "*Photinia daphniphylloides* Hayata, Icon. Pl. Formosan. 7: 30. 1918; *P. serrulata* Lindley f. *daphniphylloides* (Hayata) H. L. Li; *P. serrulata* var. *daphniphylloides* (Hayata) K. C. Kuan.
**石楠宽叶变种**

Geobot. 21: 19. 1964.

叶片椭圆形或长圆倒卵形，长8-16厘米，宽3-7厘米，先端急尖至圆钝，基部圆钝或截形，近全缘至有锯齿。花期4月。

产台湾东海岸。模式标本采自花莲港。
