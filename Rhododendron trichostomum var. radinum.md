## Description
Bud scales persistent. Leaf blade with adaxial surface usually densely scaly. Corolla outer surface densely scaly; style usually shorter than ovary. Fl. May–Jun.

> Thickets, scrub, open slopes, rocks; 2700–3400(–3800) m. SW Sichuan, NW Yunnan.
**166c. 鳞斑毛嘴杜鹃（变种）（云南植物志）**

Rhododendron trichostomum Franch. var. radinum (Balf. f. et W. W. Smith) Cowan et Davidian in R. H. S. Rhodod. Year Book, 2: 84. 1974; 云南植物志4: 533. 1986. ——R. radinum Balf. f. et W. W. Smith in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 9: 268. 1916.

本变种与原变种的区别在于叶芽鳞宿存；花冠外面密被鳞片；花柱通常短于子房。

产云南西北部、四川西南部。生于松林下、高山灌丛中或岩坡，海拔2 700-3 400（-3 800）米。模式标本采自云南丽江。
