> Three species: N Asia, Europe; one species in China.

## Description
Herbs low, &amp;#177; stemless. Leaves alternate; petiole long; lanceolate to oblong-lanceolate, pinnatisect. Flowers axillary; pedicel long. Corolla large; galea falcate, lacking marginal teeth, apex usually acute, beakless.
