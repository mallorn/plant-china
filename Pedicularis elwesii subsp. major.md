## Description
Plants to 32 cm tall. Stems erect. Petiole of basal leaves to 12 cm. Racemes laxly flowered basally, long, to 15 cm. Calyx lobes 3.

> * SE Xizang, NW Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Pedicularis elwesii&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;major&lt;/I&gt; H. L. Li, Proc. Acad. Nat. Sci. Philadelphia 101: 145. 1949.
**哀氏马先蒿高大亚种**

Pedicularis elwesii Hk. f. subsp. major Tsoong. stat. nov. ——P. elwesii Hk. f. var. major Li, in Proc. Acad. Nat. Sci. Philad. CI (1949) 145.

与第一亚种之别在于植株较大，茎直立，高达32厘米，叶基生者较长，达25厘米，叶柄长达12厘米，叶片长达13厘米，宽4.3厘米；花序长达15厘米，多花，下部之花疏距。

产我国云南西北部与西藏昌都专区东南部。
