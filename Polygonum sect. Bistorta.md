## Description
Plants without prickles. Leaves lanceolate to ovate or elliptical, margin revolute. Inflorescence a solitary terminal spike. Tepals free or only slightly fused at base. Inner nectaries confluent with inner filament bases. Styles 3, free, entire.
  "Statistics": "About 50 species: centered in the Arctic and E Asia; 22 species (six endemic) in China.
