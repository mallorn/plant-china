12b.**Photinia chingiana var. lipingensis** (Y. K. Li & M. Z. Yang) L. T. Lu & C. L. Li",

## Description
Leaf blade elliptic-lanceolate, abaxially densely floccosely tomentose, margin prominently sharply serrate.

> Forests; ca. 400 m. SE Guizhou (Liping Xian).
  "Synonym": "*Photinia lipingensis* Y. K. Li &amp; M. Z. Yang, Bull. Bot. Res., Harbin 8(3): 134. 1988.
