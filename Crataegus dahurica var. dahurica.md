光叶山楂(原变种)",
12a.**Crataegus dahurica var. dahurica**",

## Description
Petiole and leaf blade abaxially glabrous.

> Grassy places on river banks in forests, sandy slopes; 500--1000 m. Heilongjiang, Nei Mongol [N Mongolia, Russia (E Siberia)].
  "Synonym": "*Crataegus chitaensis* Sargent; *C. purpurea* J. A. Bosc ex Candolle; *C. sanguinea* Pallas var. *glabra* Maximowicz.
