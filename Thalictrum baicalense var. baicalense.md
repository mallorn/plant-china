贝加尔唐松草(原变种)",
5a.**Thalictrum baicalense var. baicalense**",

## Description
Style straight, ca. 0.5 mm; stigma elliptic. Fl. May--Jun.

> Montane forests, wet meadows; 900--2800 m. Gansu, Hebei, Heilongjiang, Henan, Jilin, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Xizang [Korea, Mongolia, Russia (Siberia)].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Thalictrum giraldii&lt;/I&gt; Ulbrich.
