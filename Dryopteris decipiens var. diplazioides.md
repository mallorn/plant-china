深裂迷人鳞毛蕨",
98b.**Dryopteris decipiens var. diplazioides** (Christ) Ching Bull. Fan Mem. Inst. Biol., Bot. 8: 476. 1938.",

## Description
Lamina pinnate, pinna pinnately lobed or pinnately divided, pinnules sessile, or rarely pinna pinnatisect, so that lamina becomes pinnatifid to bipinnate.

> Subtropical broad-leaved evergreen forests. Anhui, Fujian, Guizhou, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Zhejiang [Japan].
