碧江乌头(原变种)",
110a.**Aconitum tsaii var. tsaii**",

## Description
Stem glabrous. Rachis and pedicels glabrous. Sepals green or purple.

> * Grassy slopes; 3200--4000 m. NW Yunnan (Bijiang Xian).
