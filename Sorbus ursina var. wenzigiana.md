## Description
Leaflet blades narrowly lanceolate, margin sharply serrate apically. Hypanthium somewhat turbinate.

> Coniferous forests, high mountain thickets; 3000--5400 m. SE Xizang [NW India, Nepal, Sikkim].
  "Synonym": "*Sorbus wenzigiana* (C. K. Schneider) Koehne.
