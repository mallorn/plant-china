## Description
Annuals 10-40 cm tall. Roots yellow, stout. Stems erect, slender, subquadrangular, narrowly winged on angles, branched. Basal leaves withered at anthesis, short petiolate; leaf blade spatulate to oblong-spatulate, 1.5-3 cm &amp;#215; 6-7 mm including petiole, base narrowed, apex obtuse, midvein distinct. Stem leaves subsessile, linear to narrowly elliptic, 1.3-3.5 cm &amp;#215; 2-7 mm, base narrowed, apex acute, veins 1-3. Inflorescences usually reduced to a single flower. Flower 5merous. Pedicel erect, 1.5-4 cm, stout. Calyx tube 2-3 mm; lobes bractlike, unequal, 3 ovate and 0.9-2.5 cm &amp;#215; 6-12 mm, 2 ovate-lanceolate and 0.7-1.6 cm &amp;#215; 3-4 mm, base cordate, apex obtuse, reticulate veins purple. Corolla blue-purple, 2.5-3 cm in diam., tube 2-3 mm; lobes ovate-oblong to ovate, 0.9-1.6 cm &amp;#215; 3-5 mm, apex obtuse. Nectaries 2 per corolla lobe, radially elongated, with a narrow scale, and raised margin with many compound fimbriae. Filaments ca. 6 mm; anthers ellipsoid, ca. 2 mm. Ovary ellipsoid, 5-7 mm. Style ca. 2 mm; stigma lobes capitate. Fl. Aug-Oct.

> * Gravel slopes, forests; 1900-4000 m. S Sichuan, NW Yunnan.
**51．丽江獐牙菜（中国高等植物图鉴）图版63：3-5**

Swertia delavayi Franch. in Bull. Soc. Bot. France 46: 323. 1899; Forrest in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 4: 80. 1907; H. Smith in Hand. -Mazz. Symb. Sin. 7: 987. 1936;中国高等植物图鉴3:407. 图4767. 1974.

一年生草本，高10-40厘米。茎直立，具狭翅，下部常带紫色，从基部起作帚状分枝。基生叶花期枯萎，具短柄，叶片匙形或矩圆状匙形，连柄长15-30毫米，宽6-7毫米，先端钝；茎生叶狭椭圆形至线形，长13-35毫米，宽1.5-6.5毫米，愈向茎上部叶愈小，先端急尖，基部渐狭成短柄，具1-3脉。花5数，直径达3厘米，单生枝顶；花梗粗，直立，长1.5-4厘米，果时略伸长；花萼绿色，脉纹紫色，包被花冠，裂片不等大，外面3枚大，卵形，长9-25毫米，宽6-12毫米，基部宽心形，内面2枚小，卵状披针形，长7-16毫米，宽3-4毫米，全部裂片先端急尖，背面具突起的网脉；花冠蓝紫色，裂片卵形或卵状矩圆形，长9-16毫米，宽3-5毫米，先端钝，基部具2个腺窝，腺窝矩圆形，沟状，基部有浅囊，边缘有密的绒毛状流苏；花丝线形，长约6毫米，花药矩圆形，长约2毫米；子房椭圆形，长5-7毫米，花柱短而明显，柱头2裂，裂片半圆形。花期8-10月。

产云南西北部及四川南部。生于山坡、多石山坡、林下沙地，海拔1930-4000米。模式标本采自云南鹤庆。
