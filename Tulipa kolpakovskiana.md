3.**Tulipa kolpakovskiana** Regel",

## Description
Bulb ovoid, 1.5--2 cm in diam.; tunic black, leathery, appressed hairy inside distally. Stem usually glabrous. Leaves 3, spaced, linear-lanceolate, varying in size, usually overtopping flower, 0.5--1.5 cm wide, glabrous, margin crisped. Bracts absent. Flowers solitary or paired, nodding in bud. Tepals yellow, rarely orange-red, ± tinged with violet, oblong to rhomboidal-oblong, 3--6 × 0.5--2 cm. Stamens equal, 1--2 cm; filaments glabrous, gradually attenuate from base. Style very short. Fl. May. 2&lt;I&gt; n&lt;/I&gt; = 24.

> Semideserts. NW Xinjiang [Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan].
