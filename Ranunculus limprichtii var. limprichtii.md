纺锤毛茛(原变种)",
72a.**Ranunculus limprichtii var. limprichtii**",

## Description
Stems glabrous or sparsely puberulent. Flowers 0.8--1.1 cm in diam. Sepals abaxially puberulent. Petals obovate, 4.8--5.5 × 2.8--3.8 mm, nectary pit without a scale, apex rounded.

> * Grassy slopes, forest margins; 2600--5100 m. W Sichuan.
