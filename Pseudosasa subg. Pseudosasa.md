## Description
Culm internodes moderately deeply grooved above branches. Branch initially solitary, with basal nodes compressed but without buds, laterals only arising from nodes further from culm. Culm sheaths persistent. Pedicels glabrous.
  "Statistics": "Two species: native to Japan and Korea; one species (introduced) in China.
