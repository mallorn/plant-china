黄亮橐吾
104.**Ligularia caloxantha** (Diels) Handel-Mazzetti Anz. Akad. Wiss. Wien, Math.-Naturwiss. Kl. 60: 101. 1923.

## Description
Stem erect, 40-115 cm tall, 4-6 mm in diam. at base, proximally glabrous, distally and synflorescence shortly yellow pilose. Basal leaves petiolate; petiole to 45 cm, glabrous, base sheathed, distally narrowly winged; leaf blade triangular- or ovate-cordate, (4.5-)6-14 × (3.5-)8-18 cm, glabrous, palmately veined, base cordate, margin regularly dentate, apex obtuse or acute; sinus ca. 1/3 as long as leaf blade; basal lobes slightly divergent, oblong, outer margin coarsely dentate. Middle stem leaves petiolate or sessile, small; petiole winged; sheath enlarged, tubular or leaflike, suborbicular, base sometimes auriculate, margin dentate; leaf blade ovate-triangular or triangular, to 9 × 11 cm. Distal stem leaves sessile, ovate or orbicular. Synflorescence racemose, 9-30 cm, lax; leaflike bracts ovate-lanceolate, proximal ones to 4 × 3 cm, smaller distally, margin dentate, apex acuminate; peduncles erect, 5-10 mm, slender, sometimes proximal ones to 8 cm. Capitula 10-25; supplementary bracts narrowly lanceolate, margin dentate. Involucre broadly campanulate, 9-11 × 5-9 mm, outside glabrous; phyllaries 8-10, in 2 rows, oblong, 2-4 mm wide, apex triangular-acute; inner phyllaries broadly brown membranous at margin. Ray florets yellow; lamina oblanceolate, 1.2-2 cm × 3-6 mm, apex obtuse; tube 5-6 mm. Tubular florets numerous, 1-1.1 cm; tube ca. 5 mm. Achenes brown, oblong, 5-6 mm. Pappus pale yellow, as long as tube of tubular corolla. Fl. Jul-Oct.

多年生草本。根肉质，簇生。茎直立，高40-115厘米，上部及花序被黄色有节短柔毛，下部光滑，基部直径4-6毫米。丛生叶和茎下部叶具柄，柄长达45厘米，光滑，上部具狭翅，基部具鞘，叶片三角状或卵状心形，长6-14厘米，宽8-18厘米，先端钝或急尖，边缘有整齐的细齿，基部心形，弯缺长为叶片的1/3，两侧裂片略叉开，长圆形，边缘具大齿，两面光滑，叶脉掌状；茎中部叶小，具短柄或无柄，柄有狭翅，基部膨大成鞘，鞘筒形或平展成叶片状，近圆形，边缘有齿，基部有时耳形，叶片卵状三角形或三角形，长达9厘米，宽至11厘米；茎上部叶无柄，卵形或圆形。总状花序疏离，长9-30厘米；苞片卵状披针形，下部者长达4 厘米，宽至3厘米，向上渐小，先端渐尖，边缘具齿；花序梗细而直立，长5-10毫米，有时下部者长达8厘米；头状花序10-25，辐射状；小苞片狭披针形，边缘具齿；总苞钟形，长9-11毫米，宽5-7毫米，总苞片3-10，长圆形，宽2-4毫米，先端三角状急尖，背部光滑，内层具宽膜质边缘。舌状花黄色，舌片倒披针形，长12-20毫米，宽3-6毫米，先端钝，管部长5-6毫米；管状花多数，长10-11毫米，管部长约5毫米，冠毛淡黄色与管部等长。瘦果（未熟）光滑。花期7-10月。

产云南西北部至东北部、四川南部。生于海拔1600-3800米的草坡、水边及山顶草地。模式标本采自云南丽江。
