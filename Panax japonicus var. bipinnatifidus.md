疙瘩七",
1d.**Panax japonicus var. bipinnatifidus** (Seemann) C. Y. Wu & K. M. Feng",

## Description
Rootstock moniliform-mounded, rarely like knot of bamboo. Leaflets 2-pinnatifid.

> Forests in valleys; 1800-3400 m. Gansu, Hubei, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, N India, Myanmar, Nepal].
  "Synonym": "*Panax bipinnatifidus* Seemann, J. Bot. 6: 54. 1868; *Aralia bipinnatifida* (Seemann) C. B. Clarke; *A. quinquefolia* var. *elegantior* Burkill; *P. pseudoginseng* var. *bipinnatifidus* (Seemann) H. L. Li; *P. pseudoginseng* var. *elegantior* (Burkill) G. Hoo &amp; C. J. Tseng.
