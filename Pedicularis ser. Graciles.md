纤细系
24.**Pedicularis ser. Graciles** Maximowicz

> Five species: Afghanistan, Bhutan, China, India (Himachal Pradesh), Kashmir, Nepal, Pakistan, Sikkim; two species in China.


## Description
Stems often rigid, woody; branches often in whorls of 4--6 and rigid. Leaves whorled or sometimes opposite. Calyx lobes 5, ovate. Corolla small; beak slightly falcate, slender. Capsule ca. 1/2 exceeding calyx or enclosed in accrescent calyx.
