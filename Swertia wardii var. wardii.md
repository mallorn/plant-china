苇叶獐牙菜 (原变种)",
23a.**Swertia wardii var. wardii**",

## Description
Inflorescences paniculate, many flowered, usually 20-40 cm. Pedicel spreading to somewhat arcuate, not rigid, 1.2-3 cm, elongated to 7.5 cm in fruit.

> Swamps, scrub, forests, alpine meadows; 3800-5200 m. SE Xizang [Bhutan, Sikkim].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Swertia phragmitiphylla&lt;/I&gt; T. N. Ho &amp; S. W. Liu.
