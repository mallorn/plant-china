萸叶五加(原变种)",
1a.**Gamblea ciliata var. ciliata**",

## Description
Largest leaflets (8-)10-20 cm, with (6-)8-14 pairs of secondary veins. Flowers and fruit with (2 or)3 or 4(or 5) style branches. Fruit with a disk (1-)1.5-3 mm in diam. 2*n* = 48.

> Forests in humid valleys or on slopes; 1400-3500 m. Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, India, Myanmar, Nepal].

Smith; *Evodiopanax evodiifolius* (Franchet) Nakai var. *ferrugineus* (W. W. Smith) Nakai; *E. evodiifolius* var. *glaucus* (K. M. Feng) H. Ohashi; *E. evodiifolius* var. *gracilis* (W. W. Smith) S. Y. Hu; *E. ferrugineus* (W. W. Smith) Grushvitzky &amp; Skvortsova; *E.* *gracilis* (W. W. Smith) Grushvitzky &amp; Skvortsova.
