滇川翠雀花(原变种)",
79a.**Delphinium delavayi var. delavayi**",

## Description
Stem and petioles densely retrorsely hispidulous; hairs 0.5--2 mm. Leaf blade central lobe rhombic. Raceme strigose and yellow glandular puberulent. Filaments glabrous.

> * Sparse forests, grassy slopes; 2600--3600 m. W Guizhou, SW Sichuan, NC and SE Yunnan.
