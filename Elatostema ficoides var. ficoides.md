梨序楼梯草(原变种)",
145a.**Elatostema ficoides var. ficoides**",

## Description
Stems glabrous. Leaf blade abaxial surface glabrous. Fl. Aug-Sep.

> Forests, thickets, streamsides, dark damp places or rocks in valleys; 900-2000 m. N Guangxi, Guizhou, SW Hunan, Sichuan, NW Yunnan [NE India, Nepal, Sikkim].
