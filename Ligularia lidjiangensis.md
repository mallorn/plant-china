丽江橐吾",
86.**Ligularia lidjiangensis** Handel-Mazzetti Bot. Jahrb. Syst. 69: 134. 1938.",

## Description
Stem erect, 36-45 cm tall, 5-10 mm in diam. at base, proximally glabrous, distally white arachnoid-puberulent and shortly pilose. Basal leaves petiolate; petiole to 18 cm, glabrous; wings 2-4 mm wide; sheath ca. 5.5 cm; leaf blade ovate-cordate, 13-15 × 10-11 cm, glabrous, pinnately veined, lateral veins 8-10 pairs, base shallowly cordate or rarely subtruncate, margin regularly denticulate, apex acute or obtuse. Proximal stem leaves 2 or 3, similar to basal leaves but smaller; petiole winged; sheath ovate, subopposite, 4-8 × to 2 cm, mouth of sheath spreading toward outside, forming an oblique petiole. Middle to distal stem leaves erect, ovate-lanceolate, to 7 × 1 cm, smaller distally, base semiamplexicaul or not, margin denticulate, apex acute. Synflorescence racemose, 10-12 cm, clustered initially; leaflike bracts ovate-lanceolate or narrowly lanceolate, to 3 cm × 3-6 mm, denticulate, narrowed at base into a short petiole; peduncles ca. 5 mm, slender. Capitula numerous; supplementary bracts linear-lanceolate, 8-10 mm. Involucre cupular, 5-8 mm, mouth ca. 1 cm in diam., outside glabrous; phyllaries in 2 rows, broadly obovate or elliptic, 3-4 mm wide, apex brown, broadly triangular, acute and recurved mucronate; inner phyllaries broadly membranous at margin. Ray florets 5-10, yellow; lamina oblong, 1.3-1.5 cm × 2-4.5 mm, apex obtuse; tube ca. 3 mm. Tubular florets numerous, 6-7 mm; tube ca. 4 mm. Achenes cylindric, ca. 4 mm. Pappus white, as long as tubular corolla. Fl. and fr. Aug-Sep.

Ligularia lidjiangensis Hand.-Mazz. in Bot. Jahrb. 69: 134. 1938; S. Y. Hu in Quart. Journ. Taiwan Mus. 20: 65. 1967; H. Koyama in Mem. Fac. Sci. Kyoto Univ. Ser. Biol. 2: 48. 1968.

多年生草本。根肉质，细长。茎直立，高36-45厘米，上部及花序被白色蛛丝状柔毛和有节短柔毛，下部光滑，基部直径5-10毫米。丛生叶近直立，具柄，柄长达18厘米，具2-4毫米宽的翅，光滑，基部鞘状，鞘长约5.5厘米，叶片卵状心形，长13-15厘米，宽10-11厘米，先端急尖或钝，边缘具整齐的细齿，基部浅心形，稀近平截，两面光滑，叶脉羽状，侧脉8-10对；茎下部叶2-3，与丛生叶同形，叶柄具翅，基部有卵形，膨大，近对生抱茎的鞘，鞘长4-8厘米，宽达2厘米，鞘口外展，致使叶柄斜伸；茎中上部叶卵状披针形，直立，长达7厘米，宽约1厘米，向上渐小，先端急尖，边缘具小齿，基部半抱茎或否。总状花序长10-12厘米，幼时密集；苞片卵状披针形或狭披针形，长达3厘米，宽0.3-0.6厘米，边缘有细齿，基部狭缩成短柄；花序梗细，长约5毫米；头状花序多数，辐射状；小苞片线状披针形，长8-10毫米；总苞杯状，长5-8毫米，口部宽约10毫米，总苞片宽倒卵形或宽椭圆形，宽3-4毫米，先端褐色，宽三角形，急尖，有外弯的小尖头，背部光滑，内层具宽膜质边缘。舌状花5-10，黄色，舌片长圆形，长13-15毫米，宽2-4毫米，先端钝，管部长约3毫米；管状花多数，长6-7毫米，管部长约2毫米，冠毛白色与花冠等长。瘦果圆柱形，长约4毫米，光滑。花果期8-9月。

产云南西北部。生于海拔2600-3260米的水边及草坡。模式标本采自丽江（玉龙山）。
