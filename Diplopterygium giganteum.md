8.**Diplopterygium giganteum** (Wallich ex Hooker & Bauer) Nakai Bull. Natl. Sci. Mus., Tokyo. 29: 50. 1950.",

## Description
Plants 2-3 m tall. Rhizomes creeping, cylindrical, ca. 4 mm in diam., covered with rufous lanceolate scales. Fronds 2-pinnate; pinnae opposite, oblong, 90-120 × 27-40 cm, base slightly attenuate, apex acuminate; pinnules many, alternate or subopposite, pinnatifid, linear-lanceolate to lanceolate, 12-21 × 1.5-3.5 cm, base slightly attenuate, apex acuminate; lobes alternate, 30-50(-70) on each side, horizontally spreading, narrowly lanceolate to lanceolate, rarely linear, 10-15(-20) × 2.5-3.5 mm, apex obtuse; lamina papery, glaucous or gray-green abaxially, glabrous, costae and veins with sparse stellate hairs; costae plane or depressed adaxially; veins prominent on both surfaces. Sori with 3-5 sporangia.

> Forest margins, mountain slopes, wet places; 800-2800 m. Hainan (Anding), Hubei (Lichuan), Sichuan (Emei Shan), Xizang (Zayü), Yunnan [Bhutan, India, Myanmar, Nepal].

**16.云南里白**

Hicriopteris yunnanensis Ching, sp. nov.

植株高约2米。一回羽片长圆形，长达1米，宽约40厘米。顶渐尖，基部上侧变狭；小羽片35-45对，近水平开展，彼此分离，相距约2.2厘米，具极短柄，长达20厘米，约2-2.4厘米，线状披针形，顶端渐尖，基部稍变狭，羽状深裂；裂片50-60对，稍斜展，长8-12毫米。宽约3.5毫米，基部的一对稍缩短，披针形，顶圆或钝，基部汇合，缺刻狭尖，边缘全缘，干后稍内捲。中脉上面凹陷，下面稍凸起，侧脉纤细，两面明显，下面稍隆起，几自基部分叉，斜展，直达叶缘。叶纸质，上面深绿色，无毛，下面蓝灰色（幼时绿色），沿小羽轴，中脉及侧脉疏被棕色星状毛，沿小羽轴处很密，后变稀疏。叶轴及羽轴细瘦，绿禾稈色，幼时密被棕色星状毛，后变稀疏，羽轴上面稍凹陷，两侧圆而无边。孢子囊羣一列，靠近中脉，着生于每组一上侧小脉基部弯弓处，由3-4个孢子囊组成，略被棕色星状毛，后变光滑。

特产于云南西南部（景东，无量山）。生山谷灌丛下阴湿处，海拔1900米。
