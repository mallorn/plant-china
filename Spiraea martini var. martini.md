毛枝绣线菊(原变种)",
61a.**Spiraea martini var. martini**",

## Description
Leaf blades pubescent on both surfaces. Pedicels 5–9 mm, glabrous. Follicles glabrous or puberulous only on adaxial suture.

> Thickets, mountain valleys, dry slopes, roadsides; 1400--2100 m. Guangxi, Guizhou, Sichuan, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Spiraea fulvescens* Rehder.
