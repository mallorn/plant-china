## Description
Leaf blade 5–8 × 2.5–4 cm; abaxial surface densely compacted tawny agglutinate-tomentose, sometimes splitting into patches. Corolla 2.3–3.5 cm. Capsule straight. Fl. May–Jun, fr. Jul–Sep.

> *Abies* forests, *Rhododendron* thickets, mountain slopes; 3000–4800 m. W Sichuan, SE Xizang, NW Yunnan.
**162b. 凝毛杜鹃（变种）（中国高等植物图鉴、云南植物志）凝毛栎叶杜鹃（西藏植物志）**

Rhododendron phaeochrysum Balf. f. et W. W. Smith var. agglutinatum (Balf. f. et Forrest) Chamb. ex Cullen et Chamb. in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 36: 120. 1978; Chamb. in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 39: 352. 1982; 西藏植物志3: 621. 1986;云南植物志4: 415. 1986;中国四川杜鹃花158-159. 1986.——R. agglutinatum Balf. f. et Forrest in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 12: 88. 1920; Tagg in Stevenson, Spec. Rhodod. 675. 1930;中国高等植物图鉴3: 127, 图4207. 1974.——R. lophophorum Balf. f. et Forrest in ibid. 11: 95. 1919; Tagg in ibid. 679. 1930.——R. sync-ollum Balf. f. et Forrest in ibid. 11: 142. 1919; Tagg in ibid. 686. 1930.——R. dumulosum Balf. f. et Forrest in ibid. 13: 41. 1920; Tagg in ibid. 378. 1930.

本变种与原变种的区别是幼枝无毛；叶长圆状卵形，长5-8厘米，宽2.5-4厘米，下面毛被黄棕色，粘结，有时呈块状分裂；花较小，花冠长2.3-3.5厘米；蒴果圆柱形，直立。花期5-6月，果期7-10月。

产四川西南部、西部和西北部、云南西北部和西藏东南部。生于海拔3 000-4 800米的高山杜鹃灌丛中或冷杉林下。模式标本采自四川木里。
