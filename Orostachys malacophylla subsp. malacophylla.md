钝叶瓦松(原亚种)",
1a.**Orostachys malacophylla subsp. malacophylla**",

## Description
Rosette leaves persistent at anthesis. Flowering stem 10-20 cm, bearing flowers for less than 1/2 its length. Fl. Jul, fr. Aug-Sep.

> Rock crevices; 1200-1800 m. Hebei, Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol [Japan, Korea, Mongolia, Russia].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Cotyledon malacophylla&lt;/I&gt; Pallas, Reise Russ. Reich. 3: 729. 1776; &lt;I&gt;Sedum malacophyllum&lt;/I&gt; (Pallas) Steudel; &lt;I&gt;Umbilicus malacophyllus&lt;/I&gt; (Pallas) de Candolle.
