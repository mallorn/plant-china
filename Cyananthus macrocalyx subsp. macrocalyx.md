大萼蓝钟花(原亚种)",
3a.**Cyananthus macrocalyx subsp. macrocalyx**",

## Description
Leaf blade ovate, broadly elliptic, or rhombic, margin entire or sparsely crenulate. Pedicel typically less than 5 mm. Calyx tube more than 11 mm.
