伏毛金露梅",
1b.**Potentilla fruticosa var. arbuscula** (D. Don) Maximowicz",

## Description
Shrubs erect. Stems robust. Leaflets abaxially densely appressed white villous, adaxially sparsely pilose or glabrous, prominently elevated reticulate veined, margin strongly revolute. Fl. and fr. Jul–Aug.

> Rocks in forests, thickets, grassy mountain slopes; 2600--4600 m. Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, Nepal, Sikkim].
  "Synonym": "*Potentilla arbuscula* D. Don, Prodr. Fl. Nepal. 256. 1925; *P. lespedeza* H. Léveillé; *P. rigida* Wallich ex Lehmann, p.p.
**1b. 伏毛金露梅（变种）**

Potentilla fruticosa L. var. arbuscula (D. Don) Maxim. in Mel. Biol. 9: 158. 1873. ——P. arbuscula D. Don, Prodr. Fl. Nepal. 256. 1925. ——P. rigida Wall. ex Lehm. Revis. Potent. 19. t. l. 1856. p. p. ——P. lespedeza Levl., China Revue Annuelle, 5, 30 & pl. 1916 & in Le monde des Pl. ser. 3, l8: 54. 1917. ——P. arbuscula D. Don var. rigida (Wall.) Hand. -Mazz. Symb. Sin. 7: 508. 1933. p. p. ——P. fruncosa auct. non L. 1753: Wolf in Bibl. Bot. 71: 55. 1908.

本变种与原变种的主要区别在于，小叶片上面密被伏生白色柔毛，下面网脉较为明显突出，被疏柔毛或无毛，边缘常向下反卷。花果期7-8月。

产四川、云南、西藏。生山坡草地、灌丛或林中岩石上，海拔2600-4600米。
