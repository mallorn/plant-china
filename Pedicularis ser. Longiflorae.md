## Description
Herbs often low. Basal leaves often persistent, long petiolate. Stem leaves alternate, numerous, lanceolate-oblong, often pinnatipartite. Inflorescences centrifugal. Calyx lobes 2, 3, or 5, unequal. Corolla tube very long, more than 2 X as long as calyx; galea beaked; lower lip spreading.
