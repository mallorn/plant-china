小叶绣球绣线菊",
39c.**Spiraea blumei var. microphylla** Rehder",

## Description
Leaf blades broadly ovate, 0.6–0.9 cm. Pedicels and hypanthium abaxially glabrous. Carpels and follicles glabrous.

> Dry sunny slopes, limestone mountain valleys; low to medium elevations.  Gansu, Henan, Shaanxi.
**绣球绣线菊小叶变种**

S. blumei G. Don var. microphylla Rehd. in Journ. Arn. Arb. 9: 55. 1928.

叶片小，宽卵形，长6-9毫米，边缘具波状锯齿，三出脉明显。伞形花序小。

产甘肃。生阳坡燥地，多石灰岩沟谷。模式标本采自白龙江。
