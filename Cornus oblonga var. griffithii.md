毛叶梾木",
1b.**Cornus oblonga var. griffithii** C. B. Clarke",

## Description
Leaf blade often narrowly elliptic, abaxially ± scurfy. Inflorescence, abaxial surface of leaves, and petioles densely pubescent with raised long and soft trichomes.

> Evergreen forests, thickets on slopes; 800–3000 m. Guizhou, Hubei, Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, India].
**2b．毛叶梾木（变种）（四川植物志）图版16：1-2**

Swida oblonga (Wall.) Sojak var. griffithii (Clarke) W. K. Hu in Bull. Res. 4 (3): 102. 1984. ——Cornus oblonga Wall. var. griffithii Clarke in Fl. Brit. Ind., 2: 745. 1879;四川植物志1: 326.图版123.图9-10. 1981, ——Cornus oblonga Wall. forma pilosula Li in Journ. Arn. Arb., 25: 311. 1944.

本变种与原变种的区别在于叶片为狭长形，下面密生长柔毛。

产湖北、四川、贵州、云南等省。生于海拔850-2400米的山坡或森林中。不丹、印度也有分布。
