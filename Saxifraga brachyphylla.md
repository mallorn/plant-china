短叶虎耳草",

## Description
Herbs perennial, cespitose, 11-50 cm tall. Stem proximally brown crisped glandular villous, distally dark brown glandular hairy and leafless. Basal leaves caducous; petiole 6-9 mm, crisped glandular villous at margin; leaf blade subobovate to elliptic, 4.6-8 × 2.5-3.5 mm, glabrous, sometimes pilose. Cauline leaves sessile or subsessile; most proximal one caducous; median ones denser, ovate, 4-6 × 3-4 mm, glabrous except sometimes glandular hairy at basal margin, base truncate, apex acute; distal leaves sparser, lanceolate to oblong, ca. 6 × 2 mm, glandular hairy, apex acute. Cyme 1.7-9 cm, 2-14-flowered; pedicels 3-14 mm, glandular hairy. Sepals reflexed, ovate, 2.1-2.5 × 1.2-1.5 mm, dark brown glandular hairy abaxially and sparsely so at distal margin, veins 3, not confluent at apex, apex acute. Petals yellow, oblong, 4-5 × 2-2.1 mm, 4-callose, 3-veined, base contracted into a claw 0.4-0.6 mm, apex subobtuse. Stamens 3-3.4 mm. Ovary subsuperior, broadly ovoid, 1.9-2 mm; styles 0.8-1 mm. Fl. Aug-Oct.

Saxifraga brachyphylla Franch. Pl. Delav. 237. 1890; Engl. et Irmsch. in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 5(24): 130. 1912 et in Engl. Pflanzenr. 67 (IV. 117) : 92. 1916; Anth. in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 18(86) : 25. 1933; 中国高等植物图鉴补编2: 27. 1983; 云南种子植物名录上册: 234. 1984. ——S. swertiiflora Levl. in Fedde Repert. Sp. Nov. 13: 341. 1914, #$#severtiaeflora#$#.

多年生草本，高10.8-17厘米，丛生。茎中下部被褐色卷曲长腺毛，上部被黑褐色短腺毛。基生叶早枯，具柄，叶片近倒卵形至椭圆形，长4.6-8毫米，宽2.5-3.5毫米，通常无毛，稀具极少柔毛，叶柄长6-9毫米，基部扩大，边缘具卷曲长柔毛；茎生叶近无柄，中部者较密，卵形，长约4.5毫米，宽约2.5毫米，先端急尖，通常两面和边缘均无毛，仅基部边缘具腺毛，上部者较疏，披针形，长约6毫米，宽约2毫米，先端急尖，两面和边缘均具腺毛，最下部者早枯。聚伞花序长1.7-3厘米，具2-4花；花梗长3-11毫米，被腺毛；萼片在花期反曲，卵形，长2.1-2.5毫米，宽1.2-1.5毫米，先端急尖，腹面无毛，背面和边缘上部具黑褐色腺毛，3脉于先端不汇合；花瓣黄色，长圆形，长4-5毫米，宽2-2.1毫米，先端稍钝，基部狭缩成长0.4-0.6毫米之爪，3脉，具4痂体；雄蕊长3-3.4毫米，花丝钻形；子房近上位，阔卵球形，长1.9-2毫米，花柱2，长0.8-1毫米。花期8-10月。

产云南（东川、丽江、洱源）。生于海拔2 500-3 000米的沼泽化草甸或山坡沟旁。合模式标本采自云南洱源（黑山门、百草罗）。
