平脉腺虎耳草",
83.**Saxifraga nakaoides** J. T. Pan",

## Description
Herbs perennial, 6-6.5 cm tall. Stem purple, brown crisped glandular villous at leaf axils, elsewhere subglabrous. Basal leaves with petiole ca. 6.5 mm, margin brown crisped glandular villous; leaf blade elliptic, 5-6.7 × 2.1-3.5 mm, glabrous, apex subobtuse. Cauline leaves oblong, 4.7-8 × 1.5-2.5 mm, margin brown crisped glandular villous at base. Cyme ca. 2 cm, 2- or 3-flowered; pedicels 5-10 mm, dark brown glandular hairy. Sepals erect, ovate, 2.4-2.7 × 1.8-2.1 mm, abaxially brown glandular hairy proximally, veins 3, not confluent at apex, apex subobtuse. Petals yellow, obovate, 8-9.7 × 3.9-4.5 mm, 6-callose near base, 5-veined, base gradually contracted into a claw 1.5-1.7 mm, apex retuse. Stamens 2-3.5 mm. Ovary subsuperior, ovoid, 1.7-3.4 mm; styles 0.5-1.5 mm. Fl. Jul-Aug.

> * Alpine meadows; ca. 4200 m. S Xizang (Cona Xian).
**49. 平脉腺虎耳草（西藏植物志）图版24：24-30**

Saxifraga nakaoides J. T. Pan in C. Y. Wu, Fl. Xizang. 2: 485. f. 160: 1-7. 1985.

多年生草本，高6-6.5厘米。茎紫红色，于叶腋部具褐色卷曲腺柔毛，其他部分则近无毛。基生叶具柄，叶片椭圆形，长5-6.7毫米，宽2.1-3.5毫米，先端稍钝，无毛，叶柄长约6.5毫米，仅边缘具褐色卷曲腺柔毛；茎生叶长圆形，长4.7-8毫米，宽1.5-2.5毫米，仅基部边缘具褐色卷曲腺柔毛。聚伞花序长约2厘米，具2-3花；花梗长0.5-1厘米，被黑褐色腺毛；萼片在花期直立，卵形，长2.4-2.7毫米，宽1.8-2.1毫米，先端稍钝，仅背面中部以下被褐色腺毛，3脉于先端不汇合；花瓣黄色，倒卵形，长8-9.7毫米，宽3.9-4.5毫米，先端微凹，基部渐狭成长1.5-1.7毫米之爪，5脉，基部具6痂体；雄蕊长2-3.5毫米，花丝钻形；子房近上位，卵球形，长1.7-3.4毫米，花柱2，长0.5-1.5毫米。花期7-8月。

产我国西藏错那。生于海拔4 200米左右的高山草甸。模式标本采自西藏错那。
