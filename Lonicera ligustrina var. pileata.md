## Description
Leaves with midvein raised adaxially, glabrous or sparsely hairy. Berry purple or white.
  "Statistics": "● Sandy slopes along watersides, moist places in sparse forests, scrub on mountain slopes; (300-)600-1700(-2200) m. Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, W Hubei, Hunan, S Shaanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Lonicera pileata* Oliver, Hooker’s Icon. Pl. 16: t. 1585. 1887; *Caprifolium pileatum* (Oliver) Kuntze; *L. pileata* var. *linearis* Rehder; *L. tricalysioides* C. Y. Wu ex P. S. Hsu &amp; H. J. Wang.
**41a. 条叶蕊帽忍冬（变种）（新拟）**

Lonicera pileata Oliv. var. linearis Rehd. in Sarg. Pl. Wils. 1: 143. 1911; Roerner in Mittel. Deutsch. Dendr. Ges. 45: 92. 1933.

叶纸质，较狭，条形或条状矩圆形，长1-2.5厘米，宽2-4毫米。花冠外面近无毛。

特产云南西双版纳。生于海拔1000-1600米的山地。模式标本采自思茅。
