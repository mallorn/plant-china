## Description
Shrubs or small trees; young shoots hairy or glabrous. Leaf blade leathery, broadly elliptic to elliptic; abaxial surface with felt or granular indumentum; adaxial surface glabrous when mature. Inflorescence terminal, lax, 5–12-flowered, rachis 1–2.5 cm. Calyx minute, 5-lobed; corolla broadly campanulate, 5-lobed, whitish, pale rose or lilac-purple; stamens 10; ovary glabrous. Capsule cylindric, slightly curved.
