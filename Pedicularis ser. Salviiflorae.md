丹参花系
21.**Pedicularis ser. Salviiflorae** Prain

> One species.


## Description
Herbs tall. Roots glabrous. Stems many branched; branches and leaves opposite. Leaves &amp;#177; ovate, pinnatipartite to pinnatisect. Inflorescences racemose, lax. Corolla 3--3.5 cm; galea falcate or slightly falcate, beakless, toothless; lower lip ca. as long as galea.
