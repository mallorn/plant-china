## Description
Branchlets densely bristly and glandular bristly.

> Mixed forests, stream sides, seashores.  Fujian, Taiwan, Zhejiang.
**79b.密刺硕苞蔷薇（变种）**

R. bracteata Wendl. var. scabriacaulis Lindl. ex Koidz. in Journ. Coll. Sci. Univ. Tokyo 24(2): 227. 1913; Li, Woody Fl. Taiwan 297. 1963; Liu et Su in Fl. Taiwan 3: 99. 1977.

本变种主要特点是小枝密被针刺和腺毛。

产浙江、福建、台湾。多生溪边或杂木林中。
