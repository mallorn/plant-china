## Description
Fronds small. Rachis terete to narrowly winged; lamina pinnate to pinnatisect, 17-30 × less than 12 cm, thinly leathery, yellow-green, thick, margin entire or sometimes indistinctly slightly undulate; pinnae 2-3(-5) pairs, deltoid, largest lobe 5-8 × 1.4-2.2 cm; veinlets sometimes obscure.

> ● On wet rocks in forests. Hainan.

var. longipes (Ching) L. Shi et X. C. Zhang in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 37(1): 76. 1999. ——Colysis longipes Ching, Bull. Fan Mem. Inst. Biol. 4: 332. 1933; Fil. Sin. Fas 2: 92. Pl. 92. 1934: C. Chr., Ind. 本变种特产于海南（保亭、东方、尖峰岭、通什）。生密林下阴湿岩石上。模式标本采自海南。

本变种与其他变种的区别在于，羽片通常2-3对，叶质厚，小脉全部不见，叶片呈三角形。
