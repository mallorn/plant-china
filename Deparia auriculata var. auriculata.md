大耳对囊蕨(原变种)",
15a.**Deparia auriculata var. auriculata**",

## Description
Fronds with basal acroscopic segments of basal pair of pinnae prominently elongated, ca. 2 × as long as other segments, narrowly deltoid-ovate, pinnatipartite or pinnatilobate, acute to shortly acuminate at apex.

> ● Under alpine shrubs, slightly shaded wet places; 2900-3600 m. W Sichuan, NW Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "Basionym: *Lunathyrium auriculatum* W. M. Chu &amp; Z. R. Wang, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 32: 85. 1994.
