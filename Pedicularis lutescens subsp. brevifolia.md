短叶浅黄马先蒿
247c.**Pedicularis lutescens subsp. brevifolia** (Bonati) P. C. Tsoong

## Description
Plants 10--20(--30) cm. Stems few branched basally or unbranched; branches rigid, stout. Leaves &amp;#177; sessile or petiole to 1--2 mm; leaf blade ca. 2 cm. Galea with 4--6 marginal teeth on each side.


> * NW Yunnan.

  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Pedicularis lutescens&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;brevifolia&lt;/I&gt; Bonati, Notes Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinburgh 13: 135. 1921.

**浅黄马先蒿短叶亚种**

Pedicularis lutescens Franch. subsp. brevifolia (Bonati) Tsoong, stat. nov. ——P. lutescens Franch. var. brevifolia Bonati, in Notes Bot. Gard. Edinb. XIII (1921) 135; Limpr. in Repert. Sp. Nov. XX (1924) 227; Hand.-Mazz. Symb. Sin. VII (1936) 855: Li, in Proc. Acad. Nat. Sci. Philad. C (1948) 361.

与第一亚种的区分为植株较低矮，叶较小，近于无柄，长约1-2厘米，茎基部不分枝或少分枝。

产云南西北部。
