欧夏至草属",
13.**Marrubium** Linnaeus",

> About 40 species: temperate N Africa, Asia, and Europe, especially in the Mediterranean region; one species in China.

## Description
Herbs erect perennials or very rarely annuals, &amp;#177; villous to lanate. Leaves circular to ovate, corrugate, margin dentate. Verticillasters axillary, many flowered; bracts subulate, rarely absent. Flowers small. Calyx tubular, 5-10-veined; teeth 5-10, rigid, needlelike, equal or main teeth larger than accessory teeth, straight or spreading-reflexed. Corolla white or purple, rarely yellow, 2-lipped; tube included, glabrous or pilose annulate inside; upper lip straight, &amp;#177; flat, apex emarginate or 2-lobed; lower lip spreading, 3-lobed, middle lobe apex emarginate. Stamens 4, small, included, anterior 2 longer; anther cells 2, divergent. Style included, apex 2-cleft. Nutlets triquetrous, ovoid, smooth, apex rounded.
**16．欧夏至草属——Marrubium Linn.**

Linn Sp. Pl. 582. 1753.

多年生，极稀一年生草本，直立，全体多少被有柔毛或绵状毛。叶圆形，或卵圆形，具皱，有齿。轮伞花序腋生，多花密集；苞片钻形，稀不存在。花小，白色，或紫色，稀黄色。花萼管形，具5-10脉，齿5-10，坚硬，针刺状，等大，或主齿较大，直伸或开张向外反曲。花冠筒不伸出萼筒，内面无毛，但常有毛环，冠檐二唇形，上唇直伸，狭窄，近扁平，先端凹缺或2裂，下唇开张，平展，3裂，中裂片最宽大，先端常微缺。雄蕊4，细小，内藏，前对较长；花药2室，叉开。花盘平顶，或前方稍呈指状膨大。花柱内藏，先端二浅裂。小坚果卵圆状三棱形，光滑，先端浑圆。

约40种，主要分布于欧亚大陆温带及非洲北部，以地中海地区为多；我国仅有下述1种。
