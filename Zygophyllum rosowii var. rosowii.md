石生霸王(原变种)
10a.**Zygophyllum rosowii var. rosowii** (C. B. Clarke) Ridley

## Description
Plants 10-15 cm tall. Leaflet blades ovate, 0.8-1.8 cm, apex acuminate to obtuse. Capsule 1.8-2.5 × ca. 0.5 cm.


> Gravel hills, alluvial gravel slopes, rocky steep slopes. Gansu, Xinjiang [Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Mongolia, Tajikistan].
