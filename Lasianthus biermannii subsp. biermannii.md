梗花粗叶木(原亚种)
4a.**Lasianthus biermannii subsp. biermannii**

## Description
Branches sparsely pubescent or subglabrous. Leaf blade with nerves abaxially sparsely pubescent; nervules reticulate, slender and inconspicuous. Peduncles slender, longer or shorter than petioles. Calyx lobes relatively narrow and short.

  "Statistics": "Montane forests, shaded and wet places; 1200-2500 m. Yunnan [Bhutan, NE India, Myanmar].
