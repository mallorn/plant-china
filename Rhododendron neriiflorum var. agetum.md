## Description
Petioles, pedicels and ovary eglandular; leaf blade 4–9 cm; abaxial surface with marked reticulations that form alveoli, with some papillae horizontal. Pedicel and calyx eglandular.

> *Pinus* forests, bamboo forests; 2700–2800 m. W Yunnan.
