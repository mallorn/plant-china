日本鳞始蕨",
2.**Osmolindsaea japonica** (Baker) Lehtonen & Christenhusz Bot. J. Linn. Soc. 163: 336. 2010.",

## Description
Rhizomes shortly creeping, sparsely scaly; scales reddish brown, ca. 0.5 mm, 1-3 cells wide at base. Stipe stramineous, dark purple throughout, 0.5-5 cm, abaxially rounded, adaxially grooved; lamina lanceolate, 2-7(-10) × 1-2.5 cm, herbaceous, 1-pinnate; rachis abaxially rounded, adaxially grooved, stramineous or dark at base; pinnae 3-10 pairs, alternate, shortly stalked, asymmetrically triangular-dimidiate, less than their width apart, larger ones 7-12 × 3-8 mm, twice as wide as long, unequal, attenuate at base, obtuse to subacute at apex; margins of fertile pinnules entire, margins of sterile ones slightly crenate; basal pinnules often sterile and slightly reduced, upper pinnules slightly reduced, terminal segment free, flabellate; veins immersed, simple to twice forked, ca. 1 mm apart. Sori marginal, continuous; indusia adnate laterally, 0.5-0.7 mm wide, entire, reaching margin; annulus with ca. 15 hardened cells. Spores monolete, smooth. 2*n* = 150.

> Terrestrial or on wet rocks beside streams, forest ravines; 200-500 m. Guangdong, Guizhou (Chishui), Hainan, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Taiwan [Japan, Korea].
  "Synonym": "*Lindsaea cultrata* (Willdenow) Swartz var. *japonica* Baker in Hooker &amp; Baker, Syn. Fil. 105. 1867; *L. japonica* (Baker) Diels; *L. odorata* Roxburgh var. *japonica* (Baker) K. U. Kramer.
**1.日本陵齿蕨**

Lindsaea japonica (Bak.) Diels in Engl. u. Prantl, Nat. Pflanzenfam. I, iv (1899) 221; Makino et Nemoto, Fl. Jap. (1925) 1633; Tagawa in Acta Phytotax. et Geobot. VI (1937) 27, f. l D-E; H. Ito, Fil, Jap. Illustr. (1944). t. 17——Lindsaea cultrata var. japonica Bak, in Hook. et Bak. Syn. Fil. (1867) 105; Franch. et Sav. Enum. Pl. Jap. II (1876) 210; Makino in Bot. Mag. Tokyo IX (1895) 278; XII (1898) 14; Matsum. Ind. Pl. Jap. I (1904) 311——Lindsaea cultrata var. minor Hook.; Miq. Prol. Fl. Jap. (1867) 345; Matsum. l. c.

植株矮小，高6-11厘米。根状茎横走，须根密生，具棕红色钻状小鳞片。叶远生或近生，直立；叶柄长2-2.5厘米，禾稈色，基部带红棕色，光滑，下边圆形，上边有沟；叶片披针形，长4-9厘米，宽1-1.5厘米，向顶部渐变狭，基部几不变狭，一回羽状，顶生小形羽片的先端近截形，基部楔形；羽片10-16对，上部的互生，开展，有柄（长1毫米），彼此接近，斜对开式，三角形，长5-7毫米，宽4毫米，基部楔形，先端钝，下缘直，内缘直而与中轴并行，阔4毫米，上缘通直，着生孢子囊群，常有1-2浅缺刻或全缘，长4-6毫米。叶脉二又分枝，下面不显，上面略显。叶草质，干后灰绿色，两面无毛。中轴禾稈色，光滑，下面圆形，上面有沟。孢子囊群沿羽片全部上缘着生，常为缺刻所中断成2-3个汇生囊群，或不中断；囊群盖横线形，全缘，灰绿色。

产于台湾。日本也有。

本种极似陵齿蕨L. cultrata（Willd.）Sw.，惟形体特瘦小。台湾及日本甚为常见，在中国大陆尚未发现。
