## Description
Rootstock flagellate. Leaflets not 2-pinnatifid, narrowly lanceolate, ca. 5 × as long as wide, apex long caudate-acuminate.

> Forests; 1600-3600 m. Guizhou, Sichuan, Yunnan [Bhutan, NE India, Nepal, NE Thailand].
  "Synonym": "*Aralia quinquefolia* var. *angustifolia* Burkill, Bull. Misc. Inform. Kew 1902: 7. 1902; *P. pseudoginseng* var. *angustifolius* (Burkill) H. L. Li.
