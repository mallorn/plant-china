## Description
Climbing, prostrate or erect herbs. Leaves pinnately 3-foliolate or sometimes 1-foliolate; stipules basifixed; stipels present. Flowers usually in axillary clusters or pseudoracemes. Calyx campanulate, 4- or 5-lobed; upper 2 lobes ± connate. Corolla yellow to light yellowish green, rarely becoming reddish, exserted, glabrous; standard orbicular or elliptic, usually with auricles and 2 long, linear, flat appendages; wings narrow; keel not twisted. Stamens diadelphous; anthers uniform. Ovary 3-13-ovuled; style slender, not thickened upward, bearded around stigma; stigma terminal, capitate. Legumes straight or recurved, linear or linear-oblong, com-pressed, not septate. Seeds compressed; hilum short, central.

**70. 硬皮豆属 Macrotyloma (Wight et Arn.) Verdc.**

Verdc. in Kew Bull. 24:322. 1970, et in Taxon 27:219. 1978; Gillett et al. Fl. Trop. E. Afr. Legum. Pap. (2):581. 1971;台湾植物志3: 330. 1977.——Dolichos Linn. (sect.) a Macrotyloma Wight et Arn. Prodr. 248. 1834.——Dolichos Linn. Subgen. Macrotyloma (Wight et Arn.) Baker in Hook. f. Fl. Brit. Ind. 2:210. 1876.

攀援、匍匐或直立草本。叶具羽状3小叶或有时为单小叶；托叶基部不下延，具小托叶。花通常组成腋生之花簇或假总状花序；花萼钟状，4-5裂，上方2枚裂片合生；花冠黄色或微白至淡黄绿色，稀变红色，伸出萼外，无毛；旗瓣圆形或椭圆形，通常具耳及2枚长线形、片状的附属体，翼瓣狭，龙骨瓣不旋卷；雄蕊10，二体（9+1）；花药一式；子房有胚珠3-13颗；花柱细长，不增粗，柱头顶生，头状，其下通常有一圈毛。荚果颈直或弯曲，线形或线状长圆形，扁，无隔膜；种子扁，种脐短，居中。

25种，分布于亚洲及非洲。我国台湾有1种。

属模式种：硬皮豆 Macrotyloma uniflorum (Lam.) Verdc. (Dolichos uniflora Lam.)

本属和Dolichos属不同之处在于前者的旗瓣具长附属体，花柱细长，不增粗，花粉粒具疣凸，而后者花柱上部粗大，沿内弯一侧有须毛，旗瓣基部的附属体短，花通常为红色或蓝色，花粉粒无刺。
