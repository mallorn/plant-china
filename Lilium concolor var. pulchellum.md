## Description
Leaves (2--)3--4.5(--7) cm × 2--3(--6) mm. Tepals spotted, (2.5--)2.8--3.5(--4) cm × (5--)6--9(--10) mm. 2&lt;I&gt; n&lt;/I&gt; = 24.

> Moist places in forests, sunny grasslands; 600--2200 m. Hebei, Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Shandong, Shanxi [Japan, Korea, Mongolia, Russia (Far East, E Siberia)].
