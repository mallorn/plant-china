## Description
Stem erect, 35-50(-75) cm tall, 3-4 mm in diam. at base, proximally glabrous, distally shortly yellowish brown pilose. Basal leaves petiolate; petiole 8-15 cm, base sheathed, abaxially glabrous, adaxially shortly pilose; leaf blade triangular-sagittate or cordate, 3-10.5 × 4.5-9 cm, both surfaces shortly white pilose, later adaxial hairs deciduous, glabrescent, palmately veined, margin regularly denticulate, apex acute; sinus ca. 1/3 as long as leaf blade, broad; basal lobes short, slightly divergent, outer margin coarsely dentate. Middle stem leaf solitary, subsessile; sheath enlarged, 3-5 cm, margin entire; leaf blade ovate or ovate-cordate, 3-4 × 3.5-5 cm. Distalmost stem leaves sessile, bracteal, ovate-lanceolate. Synflorescence racemose, 7-13 cm; leaflike bracts linear, 1-1.5 cm, submembranous, shortly pilose, margin entire; peduncles 1.5-2.5 cm, shortly pilose. Capitula numerous; supplementary bracts linear. Involucre campanulate, 9-11 × 7-9 mm, outside shortly white pilose; phyllaries 7 or 8, in 2 rows, oblong or lanceolate, 2-3 mm wide, apex obtuse or acuminate; inner phyllaries brown membranous at margin. Ray florets 5 or 6, yellow; lamina linear, to 20 × 1.5 mm; tube 5-6 mm. Tubular florets 8-9 mm; tube as long as limb. Pappus yellowish brown, as long as tube of tubular corolla. Fl. Jul-Sep.

**77．节毛橐吾**

Ligularia ruficoma (Franch.) Hand.-Mazz. in Bot. Jahrb. 69: 134. 1938; S. Y. Hu in Quart. Journ. Taiwan Mus. 20: 69. 1967. ——Senecio ruficomus Franc. in Bull. Soc. Bot. France 39: 298. 1892.

多年生草本。根肉质，多数。茎直立，高35-50（75）厘米，上部被黄褐色有节短柔毛，下部近光滑，基部直径3-4毫米，被枯叶柄纤维包围。丛生叶与茎基部叶具柄，柄长8-15厘米，上半部被有节短柔毛，下部光滑，基部鞘状，叶片三角状箭形或心形，长3-10.5厘米，宽4.5-9厘米，先端急尖，边缘具整齐的细齿，基部弯缺宽，长为叶片的1/3，两侧裂片短，略叉开，边缘有大齿，两面被白色有节短柔毛，后上面脱毛，近光滑，叶脉掌状；茎中部具1叶，无柄或有短柄，鞘膨大，长3-5厘米，全缘，叶片卵形或卵状心形，长3-4厘米，宽3.5-5厘米；最上部叶无柄，卵状披针形，苞叶状。总状花序长7-13厘米；苞片线形，长10-15毫米，全缘，略近膜质，被有节短柔毛；花序梗长1.5-2.5厘米，被有节短柔毛；头状花序多数，辐射状；小苞片线形；总苞钟形，长9-11毫米，宽7-9毫米，总苞片7-8，2层，长圆形或披针形，宽2-3毫米，先端钝或渐尖，背部被白色有节短柔毛，内层具褐色膜质边缘。舌状花5-6，黄色，舌片线形，长达20毫米，宽1.5毫米，管部长5-6毫米；管状花多数，长8-9毫米，管部与檐部等长，冠毛黄褐色与管部等长。瘦果（未熟）光滑。花期7-8月。

产云南西部及西北部。生于海拔3500-3600米的山坡。模式标本采自鹤庆。

本种原描述称舌状花短，有时比总苞短，但从我们的标本中可以看到舌状花的长短有变化，幼时短，后伸长。
