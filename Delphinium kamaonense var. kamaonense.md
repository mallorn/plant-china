光序翠雀花(原变种)",
160a.**Delphinium kamaonense var. kamaonense**",

## Description
Sepals deep blue; spur slightly shorter than sepals, 1.2--1.6 cm, usually slightly upcurved. Staminode limb distally emarginate.

> Grassy slopes; 2800--4100 m. S Xizang [NW India, Nepal].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Delphinium grandiflorum&lt;/I&gt; Linnaeus var. &lt;I&gt;kamaonense &lt;/I&gt; (Huth) Brühl.
