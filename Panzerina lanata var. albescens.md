2c.**Panzerina lanata var. albescens** (Kuprianova) H. W. Li",

## Description
Stems densely tomentose. Calyx teeth short subulate-spinescent, anterior teeth 5-6 mm. Corolla 3-3.5 cm.

> Desert steppes, sandy areas. Gansu, Nei Mongol, Xinjiang [Mongolia, Russia].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Panzeria albescens&lt;/I&gt; Kuprianova, Bot. Mater. Gerb. Bot. Inst. Komarova Akad. Nauk SSSR 15: 363. 1953.
