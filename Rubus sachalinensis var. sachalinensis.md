库页悬钩子(原变种)
18a.**Rubus sachalinensis var. sachalinensis**

## Description
Pedicel and abaxial surface of calyx with glandular hairs. Flowers and fruit ca. 1 cm in diam. Aggregate fruit densely tomentose.


> Forests, forest margins, thickets, grasslands, ravines, valleys, rock crevices; 1000--2500 m. Gansu, Hebei, Heilongjiang, Jilin, Nei Mongol, Qinghai, Xinjiang [Japan, Korea, Mongolia, Russia; Europe].

Léveillé &amp; Vaniot; *R. melanolasius* Focke var. *discolor* Komarov.
