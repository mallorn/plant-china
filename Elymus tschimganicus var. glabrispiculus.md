## Description
Leaf sheath glabrous throughout. Lemma callus smooth, glabrous. Fl. and fr. summer.

> * Mountain slopes; ca. 3500 m. Xinjiang.
  "Synonym": "*Roegneria glabrispicula* (D. F. Cui) L. B. Cai; *R. tschim-ganica* var. *glabrispicula* (D. F. Cui) L. B. Cai.
