宽苞黑水翠雀花",
92c.**Delphinium potaninii var. latibracteolatum** W. T. Wang",

## Description
Raceme ca. 11 cm, ca. 9-flowered. Bracteoles lanceolate-linear or linear, 10--12 × 1--1.2 mm. Spur U-shaped or spirally recurved. Ovary glabrous.

> * Grassy slopes; ca. 2300 m. NC Sichuan (Maowen Qiang Zu Zizhixian).
**61b．宽苞黑水翠雀花（变种）**

Delphinium potaninii var. latibracteolatum W. T. Wang, 植物学报10: 147. 1962.

与黑水翠雀花的区别：小苞片线形或披针状线形，长10-12毫米，宽1-1.2毫米。茎高约120厘米。萼距下部向下成直角弧状弯曲。

产四川茂县。生海拔2300米山地草坡。
