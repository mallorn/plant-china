光叶珙桐",
1b.**Davidia involucrata var. vilmoriniana** (Dode) Wangerin",

## Description
Leaves abaxially glabrous or scarcely pubescent when young, sometimes abaxially glaucous.

> ● Forests; 1500-2000 m. Guizhou, W Hubei, Sichuan.
  "Synonym": "*Davidia vilmoriniana* Dode, Rev. Hort. (Paris) 80: 406. 1908; *D. involucrata* subsp. *vilmoriniana* (Dode) J. Holub.
**1b.光叶珙桐（变种）（中国树木分类学）图版46：5**

Davidia involucrata Baill. var. vilmoriniana (Dode) Wanger. in Engl., Pflanzenr. 41 (IV. 220a): 17. 1910. ——D. vilmoriniana Dode in Rev. Hort. II. 8. 406. 1908 .——D. laeta Dode op. cit. 407. 1908.

与原变种的区别在于本变种叶下面常无毛或幼时叶脉上被很稀疏的短柔毛及粗毛，有时下面被白霜。

产湖北西部、四川、贵州等省；常与珙桐混生。
