## Description
Plants 8-10 cm tall. Rhizome brown, ca. 0.2 mm in diam., with sparse brown short hairs, with sparse hairy rootlets. Stipes remote, 1-1.5 cm apart, brown, filiform, 2-5 cm, subglabrous, narrowly winged at apex, wings decurrent. Rachis and costae winged throughout, wings sharply crisped. Lamina bipinnate to tripinnatifid, triangular-ovate, 4-6 × 2-2.5 cm, membranous, glabrous, base subcordate, apex obtuse; pinnae 8-10 pairs, closely spaced, alternate, subsessile, linear to lanceolate, 1.5-5 cm × 5-8 mm, base unequally cuneate, apex acuminate; pinnules 4-14 pairs, closely spaced, alternate, sessile, oblong to ovate, 4-6 × 3-4 mm, base decurrent, apex obtuse; ultimate segments simple or forked, narrowly linear, 0.5-0.8 mm wide, margin entire, sharply crisped, apex obtuse. Veins dichotomous, slightly raised on each surface, brown, glabrous, with a single veinlet. Sori on upper parts of frond, terminal on ultimate segments; involucres subcircular, oblong, or ovate, deeply cleft, entire or somewhat wavy at apex.

> ● On rocks or on tree trunks; 1800-2600 m. Hubei, Sichuan, Xizang.

**7. 皱叶蕗蕨**

Mecodium corrugatum (Christ) Cop. in Philip. Journ. Sci. LXVII (1938) 21 Hymenophyllum corrugatum Christ in Bull. Boiss. ser. 2, III (1903) 508; in Bull. Acad. Geogr. Bot. Mans (1906) 101; C. Chr. Ind. Fil. (1905) 359; in Acta Hort. Gothob. I (1924) 50; Cop. l. c. LXIV (1937) 124.

植株高8-10厘米。根状茎纤细，丝状，粗约0.2毫米，长而横走，褐色，疏被褐色的短毛，下面疏生纤维状的根。叶远生，相距1-1.5厘米；叶柄长2-5厘米，纤细如丝，褐色，几光滑，顶端稍有下延的狭翅，其余均无翅；叶片三角状卵形，长4-6厘米，宽2-2.5厘米，先端钝圆，基部近心脏形，三回羽裂；羽片8-10对，互生，几无柄，斜向上，线形至披针形，伸长，长度很不一致，长1.5-5厘米，宽5-8毫米，先端渐尖，基部斜楔形，密接；小羽片4-14对，互生，无柄，斜向上，长圆形至斜卵形，长4-6毫米，宽3-4毫米，先端钝，基部下侧下延，密接；裂片2-6个，互生，极斜向上，狭线形，长2-4毫米，宽0.5-0.8毫米，钝头，先端常有浅缺刻，全缘，边缘有极强度的波纹状褶皱，单一或分叉，密接。叶脉叉状分枝，两面稍隆起，褐色，无毛，末回裂片有小脉l条。叶为薄膜质，干后褐色至绿褐色，无毛。叶轴及各回羽轴均全部有翅，翅的边缘有极强度的褶皱。孢子囊羣多数，位于叶片上半部，着生于各个正常裂片顶端；囊苞卵形，长约1毫米，高大于宽，唇瓣深裂达到基部，全缘或先端有不明显的波纹。

本种为四川西南部（天全、峨眉山）特产。生于冷杉林内，常附生于冷杉或杜鹃树于上，与苔藓混生，有时也生在沟中岩石上，海拔1800-2600米。
