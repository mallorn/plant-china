## Description
Bud scales usually deciduous. Corolla deep blackish crimson; ovary eglandular.

> *Rhododendron* thickets, rocky slopes; 3100–4300 m. SE Xizang, NW Yunnan.

产云南西北部及西藏东南部。生于海拔3 100-4 260米的岩坡上或杜鹃灌丛中。模式标本采自云南德钦。
