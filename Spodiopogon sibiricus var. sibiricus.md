大油芒(原变种)",
8a.**Spodiopogon sibiricus var. sibiricus**",

## Description
Leaf blades 20–40 cm; rachis internodes 2.5–5 mm; spikelets 4.5–6 mm.

> Mountain slopes, roadsides, forest margins. Anhui, Gansu, Guangdong, Guizhou, Hainan, Hebei, Heilongjiang, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi, Sichuan, Zhejiang [Japan, Korea, Mongolia, Russia (Siberia)].
  "Synonym": "*Andropogon sibiricus* (Trinius) Steudel; *Saccharum sibiri-cum* (Trinius) Roberty; *Spodiopogon depauperatus* Hackel var. *purpurascens* Honda; *S. sibiricus* var. *purpurascens* (Honda) Honda; *S. sibiricus* var. *tenuis* (Kitagawa) Kitagawa; *S. sibiri-cus* var. *tomentosus* Koidzumi; *S. tenuis* Kitagawa.
