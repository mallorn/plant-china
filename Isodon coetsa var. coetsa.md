## Description
Stems retrorse pubescent or subglabrous. Leaves densely hispidulous along midvein and veins otherwise sparsely strigose or subglabrous.

> Grassy slopes, streamsides, riverbanks, forest margins, thickets, pine forests, evergreen broad-leaved forests; 600-2800 m. Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hunan, Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan [Bangladesh, India, Laos, Myanmar, Nepal, Sri Lanka, Thailand, Vietnam].
