## Description
Stem many branched, 7-12 cm tall, proximally eglandular hairy, distally glandular hairy. Leaf buds present in axils of proximal, median, or distal cauline leaves and bracts. Proximal cauline leaves scalelike, 1-2 mm. Median cauline leaves sessile, oblong or narrowly so to narrowly subobovate, 0.5-1.5 cm × 1-1.5 mm, both surfaces glabrous, margin recurved, eglandular or glandular ciliate, apex rigidly aristate. Flower solitary or cyme 2-4-flowered; pedicels 8-12 mm, glandular hairy. Sepals erect, ovate, 3-3.5 × 1-1.5 mm, abaxially glandular hairy, veins 5-7, confluent into a verruca at apex, margin ± glabrous, apex obtuse or acute. Petals yellow, ovate or elliptic, 4.5-5 × 2-2.7 mm, 2-4-callose, 4-7-veined, base contracted into a claw ca. 1 mm, apex subobtuse or acute. Stamens 3.5-5 mm. Ovary subsuperior, ovoid, 1.5-2.5 mm; styles 2-3 mm. Fl. Aug-Sep.

> * Rocky slopes and crevices; 2600-3200 m. N Yunnan (Eryuan Xian, Lijiang Naxi Zu Zizhixian, Qiaojia Xian).

**100a. 刚毛虎耳草（原变种）图版38：14-19**

Saxifraga oreophila var. oreophila

多年生草本，高8.5-11厘米，丛生。茎多分枝，密被腺毛，叶腋和苞腋具芽。叶片长圆形、狭长圆形至近倒狭卵形，长5-15毫米，宽1.5-4.3毫米，先端具软骨质硬芒，两面和边缘均具刚毛状腺毛。花单生于茎顶，或聚伞花序具2-4花；花梗密被黑褐色腺毛；萼片在花期直立，卵形，长3-3.3毫米，宽1.4-1.8毫米，先端急尖，腹面和边缘无毛，背面被黑褐色腺毛，5-7脉于先端汇合成1疣点；花瓣黄色，卵形或椭圆形，长4.5-5毫米，宽2.2-2.7毫米，先端急尖或稍钝，基部狭缩成长约1毫米之爪，4-7脉，具2-4痂体；雄蕊长3.5-4毫米，花丝钻形；子房近上位，卵球形，长1.5-2.5毫米，花柱2，长1.8-2.9毫米。花期8-9月。

产云南（巧家、丽江、洱源）。生于海拔2 850-3 200米的石隙。模式标本采自洱源（黑山门）。
**100b. 大炮山虎耳草（植物分类学报）**

Saxifraga oreophila var. dapaoshanensis J. T. Pan in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 28(5): 391. 1990.

此变种与原变种之区别是其叶片两面无毛，仅边缘具刚毛状腺睫毛，先端具芒，较矮，高4-6厘米。花期7-8月。

产四川康定。生于海拔4700米左右的山坡。模式标本采自康定（大炮山）。
