## Description
Needles 2 or 3(-5) per bundle, with inconspicuous stomatal bands abaxially. Seed cones 3-4 ×  1.5-2 cm

> Summits of limestone mountains; ca. 500 m. SW Guangxi (Longlin Gezu Zizhixian, Tiandeng Xian) [Vietnam].
