## Description
Shrubs, (0.4-)0.5(-2) m tall. Leaves oblong, 18-35 × 7-14 cm, with 5-7 pairs of leaflets; leaflets ca. 1.5 cm or less wide. Pedicel 1.5-2 mm.
  "Statistics": "● Forests, forest margins, streamsides; 200-1200 m. Guizhou, Hubei, Sichuan.
  "Synonym": "*Berberis ganpinensis* H. Léveillé, Bull. Soc. Agric. Sarthe 59: 317. 1904; *B. confusa* var. *bournei* (Ahrendt) Laferrière; *Mahonia confusa* var. *bournei* Ahrendt; *M. ganpinensis* (H. Léveillé) Fedde.

Mahonia eurybracteata Fedde subsp. ganpinensis (Levl.) Ying et Boufford stat. nov. ——M. ganpinensis (Levl) Fedde in Repent Sp. Nov. 6: 372, 1909. ——Mahonea confuses Sprague van. bournei Ahrendt in Journ. Linn. Soc. Bot. 57: 3 16, 1961, syn. nov.——Berberis ganpinensis Levl. in Bull. Soc. Agric. Sathe 59: 3 17, 1904.

本亚种与原亚种的主要区别在于：小叶较狭，宽1.5厘米以下；花梗较短，长1.5-2毫米。花期7-10月，果期11月至翌年5月。

产于贵州、四川、湖北。生于林下、林缘或溪边。海拔230-1150米。浙江等地有栽培。模式标本采自贵州平坝。
