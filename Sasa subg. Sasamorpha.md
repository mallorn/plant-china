华箬竹亚属",
2.**Sasa subg. Sasamorpha** (Nakai) C. H. Hu",

## Description
Culms and axis of inflorescence thickly white powdery; nodes flat or weakly elevated; culm sheath longer than internode, auricles and oral setae absent; branch ca. 10° angle with culm; leaf blade glossy.
  "Statistics": "Between five and twenty-two species: China, Japan, Korea, E Russia; three species (all endemic) in China.
  "Synonym": "*Sasamorpha* Nakai, J. Fac. Sci. Hokkaido Univ., Ser. 5, Bot. 26: 180. 1931.
