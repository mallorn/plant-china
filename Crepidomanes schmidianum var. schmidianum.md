西藏瓶蕨(原变种)",
10a.**Crepidomanes schmidianum var. schmidianum**",

## Description
Fronds up to 8 cm; segments more compactly placed.

> On rocks in forests; ca. 2300 m. Guangxi, Xizang [Bhutan, N India, Japan, Nepal].
  "Synonym": "*Trichomanes schmidianum* Zenker ex Taschner, Duab. Trichom. Sp. 34. 1843; *Vandenboschia schmidiana* (Zenker ex Taschner) Copeland; *V. titibuensis* H. Itô.
