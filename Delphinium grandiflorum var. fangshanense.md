## Description
Stem and corymb appressed puberulent, without glandular hairs. Inflorescence corymbose. Staminode limb emarginate. Carpels densely puberulent. Fl. Jul--Sep.

> * Grassy places in valleys; 400--600 m. Beijing (Fangshan Xian).
