钝叶鸡蛋花",
2.**Plumeria obtusa** Linnaeus",

## Description
Trees to 5 m tall. Branchlets pale green, thick, fleshy. Petiole puberulent; leaf blade obovate to narrowly so, dark green and shiny adaxially, tertiary venation strongly prominent abaxially, apex rounded. Corolla white, ca. 4 cm in diam., throat yellow; lobes spreading, slightly recurved. Follicles to 15 X 1.5 cm. 2&lt;I&gt;n &lt;/I&gt;= 36.

> Guangdong, Guangxi, Hainan, S Yunnan [native of the Caribbean Islands]
