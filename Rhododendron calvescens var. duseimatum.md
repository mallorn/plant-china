277b.**Rhododendron calvescens var. duseimatum** (I. B. Balfour & Forrest) D. F. Chamberlain",

## Description
Leaf blade 7–13 × 1.7–3 cm; abaxial surface sparsely hairy; adaxial surface rugulose. Pedicel slender, 1.5–2.5 cm, without eglandular hairs; ovary downy. Fl. Jul.

> *Abies* forests; ca. 3600 m. SE Xizang.
**71b. 长梗变光杜鹃（变种）（西藏植物志）**

Rhododendron calvescens Balf. f. et Forrest var. duseimatum (Balf. f. et Forrest) Chamb. ex Cullen et Chamb. in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 36: 119. 1978; Chamb. in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 39: 275. 1982; 西藏植物志3: 596. 1986. ——R. duseimatum Balf. f. et Forrest in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 13: 41. 1920.——R. selense Franch. subsp. duseimatum (Balf. f. et Forrest) Tagg in Stevenson, Spec. Rhodod. 722. 1930.——R. selense Franch. var. duseimatum (Balf. f. Forrest) Cowan & Davidian in Rhodod. Year-book 6: 152. 1952.

与原变种的区别在于本变种叶披针形，长7-13厘米，宽1.7-3厘米，上面有细皱纹，下面有散生的薄毛被；花梗细长，长1.5-2.5厘米，有稀疏的腺体，但无毛；子房上有短绒毛，花柱无毛等特征。花期7月。

产我国西藏东南部。生于海拔3 600米的冷杉林下。模式标本采自西藏察隅。
