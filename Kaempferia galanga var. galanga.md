山柰 (原变种)",
5a.**Kaempferia galanga var. galanga**",

> Cultivated for medicine and spice.

## Description
Leaves 7--13 × 4--9 cm, glabrous on both surfaces or sparsely villous abaxially. Labellum deeply 2-cleft at apex. 2&lt;I&gt; n&lt;/I&gt; = 22.

> Open areas. Guangdong, Guangxi, Taiwan, Yunnan [native to India; widely cultivated in SE Asia].
