掌叶复盆子(原变种)",
88a.**Rubus chingii var. chingii**",

> Further study is required to determine the identity of &lt;I&gt;Rubus septemlobus&lt;/I&gt; H. L. Li (J. Arnold Arbor. 26: 62. 1945), described from Guangxi, which might be synonymous with &lt;I&gt;R. chingii&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;chingii&lt;/I&gt;.

## Description
Leaves palmately 5-parted, rarely 3- or 7-parted. Flowers 2.5–4 cm in diam. Petals 1–1.5(–2) cm.

> Slopes, shaded thickets, roadsides; Low to medium elevations. Anhui, Fujian, Guangxi, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Zhejiang [Japan].
  "Synonym": "*Rubus officinalis* Koidzumi; *R. palmatus* Hemsley (1887), not Thunberg (1784).
