锡金马蓝(原变种)",
69a.**Strobilanthes inflata var. inflata**",

## Description
Stems, petioles, and inflorescences white pubescent. Leaf blade elliptic to ovate, ca. 5.5 × 3 cm. Fl. Jun-Nov.
  "Statistics": "Forests; ca. 1700 m. Xizang (Mêdog, Zayü), Yunnan [Bhutan, India, Myanmar, Nepal].
  "Synonym": "*Pteracanthus inflatus* (T. Anderson) Bremekamp; *Strobilanthes wardii* W. W. Smith.
