## Description
Plants low, 10--20(--30) cm. Stem simple or branched; branches slender. Leaf petiole to 2--2.5 cm; leaf blade ca. 2 cm. Galea with 4--6 marginal teeth on each side.

> * W Sichuan.

**浅黄马先蒿长柄亚种**

Pedicularis lutescens Franch. subsp. longipetiolata (Li) Tsoong, stat. nov. ——P. lutescens Franch. var. longipetiolata Li, in Proc. Acad. Nat. Sci. Philad. CI(1948) 361.

与第一亚种的区别为植株矮小，高约10厘米，枝条纤细较柔弱；叶柄较长，茎下部的长达2-2.5厘米。

产四川西部。
