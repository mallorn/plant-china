海南五针松(原变种)",
32a.**Pinus fenzeliana var. fenzeliana**",

## Description
Needles 10-18 cm. Seed cones narrowly ovoid or ovoid-ellipsoid, 6-9 cm. Seeds chestnut brown; wing 2-4(-7) mm.

> Mountains, usually scattered on ridges and rocks; 1000-1600 m. Guangxi, C and N Guizhou, Hainan, SE Sichuan (Wulong Xian) [Vietnam]
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Pinus parviflora&lt;/I&gt; Siebold &amp; Zuccarini var. &lt;I&gt;fenzeliana&lt;/I&gt; (Handel-Mazzetti) C. L. Wu.
