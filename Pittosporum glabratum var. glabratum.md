光叶海桐(原变种)",
9a.**Pittosporum glabratum var. glabratum**",

> The roots can be used medicinally as an analgesic.

## Description
Leaf blade oblong or oblanceolate, 5–15 × 2–5.5 cm. Fl. Mar–Aug, fr. Sep–Dec.

> Forests, thickets, sunny slopes, river sides; 200--2000 m. Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hainan, Hunan [Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Pittosporum fortunei* Turczaninow.
