玫瑰系",
91.**Pedicularis ser. Roseae** Maximowicz",

> Three species: China, Europe; one species in China.

## Description
Herbs low, &amp;#177; scapose. Leaves alternate. Inflorescences short and capitate, or flowers few, not distinctly centrifugal. Calyx slightly cleft anteriorly, 5-lobed. Corolla tube bent apically, toothless; galea beakless; lower lip spreading. Capsule oblique.
