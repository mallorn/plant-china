毛竹叶花椒",
31b.**Zanthoxylum armatum var. ferrugineum** (Rehder & E. H. Wilson) C. C. Huang",

## Description
Young branchlet apices, inflorescence rachises, and sometimes leaf rachises rust-colored pubescent.

> ●  Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hunan, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Zanthoxylum alatum* f. *ferrugineum* Rehder &amp; E. H. Wilson in Sargent, Pl. Wilson. 2: 215. 1914.
**31b. 毛竹叶花椒（变种）**

var. ferrugineum (Rehd. et Wils.) Huang in Guihaia 7: 1. 1987. ——Zanthoxylum alatum var. planispinum f. ferrugineum Rehd. et Wils. in Sarg. Pl. Wils. 2: 215. 1914. ——Z. planispinum f. ferrugineum (Rehd. et Wils.) Huang in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 6: 32. 1957.

嫩枝梢及花序轴、有时叶轴均有褐锈色短柔毛。花、果期与竹叶花椒相同。

产湖南、广东、广西、贵州、四川、云南。生境与竹叶花椒相同。
