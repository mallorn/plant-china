东洋对囊蕨(原变种)",
49a.**Deparia japonica var. japonica**",

## Description
Pinnae not variegated; indusial margin lacerate, ciliate, perispore surface with shortly aculeate projections. *n* = 120 (6´).

> Wet areas, beside valley streamlets; sea level to 2000 m. Anhui, Chongqing, Fujian, Gansu, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Shandong, Shanghai, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan, Zhejiang [Japan, India, Korea, N Myanmar, Nepal].
  "Synonym": "*Asplenium japonicum* Thunberg in Murray, Syst. Veg., ed. 14, 934. 1784; *Athyriopsis japonica* (Thunberg) Ching; *A. petiolata* Ching; *Athyrium japonicum* (Thunberg) Copeland; *Diplazium japonicum* (Thunberg) Beddome; *D. thunbergii* Nakai ex Momose; *Lunathyrium japonicum* (Thunberg) Sa. Kurata.
