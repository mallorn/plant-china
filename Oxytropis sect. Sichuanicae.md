## Description
Leaves imparipinnate; leaflets not verticillate. Racemes 7-9-flowered or more; peduncle longer than leaves. Lamina of corolla standard oblong, widened in basal half, apex emarginate. Legume evidently stipitate; body narrowly ellipsoid.
