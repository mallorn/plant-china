长管萱草",
5c.**Hemerocallis fulva var. angustifolia** Baker",

## Description
Plants deciduous in winter. Perianth single, orange to reddish orange or rose; tube to 4 cm, rather slender; segments 5--11 cm, outer ones 0.5--2.0 cm wide, inner ones slightly longer than outer, 1--2.5 cm wide. 2&lt;I&gt; n&lt;/I&gt; = 22.

> Long cultivated. Not known in the wild in China [Japan, Korea].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt; Hemerocallis disticha&lt;/I&gt; Donn ex Sweet; &lt;I&gt; H. fulva&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt; longituba&lt;/I&gt; (Miquel) Maximowicz; &lt;I&gt; H. longituba&lt;/I&gt; Miquel.
