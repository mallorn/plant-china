泡花树(原变种)",
2a.**Meliosma cuneifolia var. cuneifolia** (C. B. Clarke) Ridley",

## Description
Petiole slender, 10-20 mm; leaf blade adaxially pubescent, base not decurrent. Panicle 15-20 cm.

> ●  Forests; 500-3300 m. E Gansu, Guizhou, W Henan, W Hubei, S Shaanxi, Sichuan, S Xizang, C and N Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Meliosma dilleniifolia* (Wallich ex Wight &amp; Arnott) Walpers subsp. *cuneifolia* (Franchet) Beusekom; *M. platypoda* Rehder &amp; E. H. Wilson.
