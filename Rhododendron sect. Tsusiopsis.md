## Description
Shrubs, evergreen; branches with flat coarse appressed pubescence. Flowers and vegetative branches arising from the same terminal flower bud. Leaves in whorls of 2–3 at tips of branches.
  "Statistics": "One species: China (Taiwan) and Japan (including Ryukyu Islands).
