## Description
Culms densely retrorsely pubescent in upper part (exserted from leaf sheath) and at nodes. Leaf sheath retrorsely pubescent, especially proximally. Spikelets with 4 or 5 florets. Fl. and fr. Jun–Jul. 2*n* = 28*.
