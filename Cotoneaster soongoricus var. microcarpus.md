小果准噶尔栒子",
11b.**Cotoneaster soongoricus var. microcarpus** (Rehder & E. H. Wilson) G. Klotz",

## Description
Leaf blade oblong to narrowly elliptic, rarely obovate, 1–1.5 cm. Fruit 5–6 mm.

> Mountain valleys; 2300--2600 m. Gansu, Hebei, Sichuan.
  "Synonym": "*Cotoneaster racemiflorus* (Desfontaines) K. Koch var. *microcarpus* Rehder &amp; E. H. Wilson in Sargent, Pl. Wilson. 1: 169. 1912; *C. microcarpus* (Rehder &amp; E. H. Wilson) Flinck &amp; Hylmö; *C. potaninii* Pojarkova.
**准噶尔栒子小果变种 小果总花栒子（经济植物手册）**

C. soongoricus (Regel & Herd.) Popov var. microcarpus (Rehd. & Wils.) Klotz in Wiss. Zeits. Univ. Halle 6: 973. 1957. ——C. racemiflora (Desf.) K. Koch var. microcarpa Rehd. & Wils. in Sarg. Pl. Wils. 1: 169. 1912.——C. potaninii Pojark in Not. Syst. Herb. Inst. Bot. URSS 21: 202. 1961.

叶片较小，长圆形至长椭圆形，稀倒卵形，长1-1.5厘米。果实较小，椭圆形，直径4-5毫米，长5-6毫米，红色。

产河北、甘肃、四川。模式标本采自四川松潘。
