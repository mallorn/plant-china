## Description
Shrublets, often forming pulvinate tufts, non-glandular, with appressed to spreading trichomes. Leaves imparipinnate; petioles and rachis hardened after falling of leaflets and becoming slender spines. Racemes axillary, (1 or)2-5-flowered; peduncle shorter to longer than leaves. Calyx not inflating in fruit, ruptured by legume. Legume ovoid to subspherical, membranous.
