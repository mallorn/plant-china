爱玉子",
93b.**Ficus pumila var. awkeotsang** (Makino) Corner",

> This variety is cultivated for its edible fruit.

## Description
Leaf blade oblong-ovate, 7-12 × 3-5 cm, abaxially densely covered with rust-colored pubescence. Figs cylindric, 6-8 × 3-4 cm; peduncle ca. 1 cm, densely covered with thick hairs; apical pore acuminate.

> * Fujian, Taiwan, SE Zhejiang.
  "Synonym": "*Ficus* *awkeotsang* Makino, Bot. Mag. (Tokyo) 18: 151. 1904; *F*. *nagayamae* Yamamoto.

叶长椭圆状卵形，长7-12厘米，宽3-5厘米，背面密被锈色柔毛；榕果长圆形，长6-8厘米，直径3-4厘米，表面被毛，顶部渐尖，脐部凸起；总梗短，长约1厘米，密被粗毛。

产台湾（模式产地，但多栽培）、福建、浙江（乐清、北雁荡山）。果可食。
