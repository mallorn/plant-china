## Description
Leaflets densely shortly tomentose abaxially.

> Thickets on slopes, montane valleys, ravines; 800--3100 m. Anhui, Gansu, Guizhou, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan.
