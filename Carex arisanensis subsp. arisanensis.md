## Description
Leaves 4-8 mm wide. Involucral bracts sheathing 2-4 cm. Spikes 3 or 4, lowest with long peduncle.
  "Statistics": "Forests; 900-1100 m. Guangxi, Hunan, Taiwan [Japan].
