## Description
Proximal pair of pinnae of similar shape and size as distal ones; lateral veins simple, occasionally forked, pinnae abaxially, except for grayish white short acicular hairs along rachises and costae, glabrous.

> ● Broad-leaved forests on slopes; ca. 1700 m. SE Xizang (Mêdog).
  "Synonym": "*Metathelypteris tibetica* Ching &amp; S. K. Wu, Fl. Xizang. 1: 166. 1983.
**5b．西藏凸轴蕨（变种）**

var. tibetica Shing, comb. nov.——Metathelypteris tibetica Ching et S. K. Wu in Fl. Xizang. 1: 166. f. 40: 5-8. 1983; Johns, Ind. Fil. Suppl. 6: 226. 1996.

与原变种的区别在于基部一对羽片和其上的同形同大，侧脉通常单一，偶为二叉，羽片下面除沿叶轴、羽轴背灰白色的短针毛外，其余光滑。

产西藏东南部（墨脱）。生山坡阔叶林下，海拔1700米。
