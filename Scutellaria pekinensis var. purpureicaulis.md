紫茎
38d.**Scutellaria pekinensis var. purpureicaulis** (Migo) C. Y. Wu & H. W. Li

## Description
Stems purplish throughout, densely pubescent. Leaves sparsely villous, densely pubescent along veins abaxially. Calyx glandular pubescent. Corolla 1.7-2.2 cm.


> * Hills; 200-2200 m. Anhui, Fujian, Hubei, Jiangsu, Shandong, Zhejiang.

  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Scutellaria japonica&lt;/I&gt; Morren &amp; Decaisne var. &lt;I&gt;purpureicaulis&lt;/I&gt; Migo, J. Shanghai Sci. Inst. 3: 97. 1935.

**38d. 紫茎变种**

var. purpureicaulis (Migo) C. Y. Wu et H. W. Li, comb. nov. Scutellaria japonica Franch. et Sav. var. purpureicaulis Migo in Journ. Shanghai Sci. Inst. Sect., 3: 90. 1935.

这一变种与原变种不同在于，茎及叶柄密被短柔毛，常带紫色；叶两面疏被具节柔毛，下面沿脉上密被短柔毛。

产山东，江苏，浙江，江西，福建等地；生于海拔130-2200米的丘陵地。模式标本采自江苏苏州。
