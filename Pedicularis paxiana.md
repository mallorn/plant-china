## Description
Herbs perennial, low, mostly stemless. Roots fleshy, fascicled. Leaves basal, barely in a rosette, glabrous; petiole flat, winged; leaf blade ca. 10 X 1 cm, pinnatipartite; segments semirounded, crenate-dentate. Calyx campanulate, white long woolly, 2-lobed. Corolla white; tube 8--9(--10) X as long as calyx; galea twisted, circular; lobes of lower lip with rounded lateral lobes, very large. Filaments pubescent. Stigma long exserted.

> * ca. 4300 m. W Sichuan.
**323.派氏马先蒿**

Pedicularis paxiana Limpr. in Repert. Sp. Nov. Beih. XII (1922) 483, et in Repert. Sp. Nov. XX (1924) 249; Li, in Proc. Acad. Nat. Sci. Philad. CI (1949) 194.

几无茎。根茎粗，顶端有数条肉质须根束生。叶均基生，几成连座状，无毛，叶柄扁平，有翅，叶片长约10厘米，宽1厘米，有开裂状圆齿，齿半圆形，有小圆齿，端反卷。萼钟形，具二齿，生有白色长绵毛。花单个，白色，管极长，较萼长8-9倍；盔环状，为宽大的下唇所包裹，下唇裂片圆形，侧方者很大；花丝有毛，着生于管端；花柱与头状的柱头伸出很长。

为我国特有种，产四川西部（道孚），生于海拔4，300米的地方（仅采到一次）。

本种未见标本，记载由林泊利许氏原描述转译。
