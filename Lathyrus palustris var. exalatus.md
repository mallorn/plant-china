## Description
Plants glabrous to pubescent. Stem wingless. Stipules large, 12-25(-30) × 2-8 mm; leaflets usually broadly lanceo-late, 30-60 × 5-10(-15) cm, veins subparallel. Raceme lax, (2 or)3-8(-10)-flowered.
