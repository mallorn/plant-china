齿叶柔毛紫茎",
6b.**Stewartia villosa var. serrata** (Hu) T. L. Ming",

## Description
Current year branchlets, midvein of leaf blade abaxial surface, and petioles spreading villous. Leaf blade oblong, margin serrate to base. Fl. Jun, fr. Oct.

> ●  Forests; 200-400 m. S Guangxi.
