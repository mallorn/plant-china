## Description
Terminal leaflet blade ovate-elliptic, broadly ovate, or rhombic, 2-7 × 1.5-5 cm, abaxially densely spreading white tomentose and sericeous. Pedicel 4-10 mm. Lower calyx lobes longer than tube. Legume densely hairy as leaflet abaxially, cili-ate.
  "Statistics": "● Mountain slopes, rocky places, forests; 1700-3100 m. Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Desmodium handelii* Schindler, Anz. Akad. Wiss. Wien, Math.-Naturwiss. Kl. 62: 234. 1925.
**22c. 盐源山蚂蝗**

var. handelii (Schindl.) Ohashi in Hara, Fl. E. Himal 2: 297. 1971 et in Ginkgoana 1: 111. 1973. ——Desmodium handelii Schindl. in Anz. Akad. Wiss. Wien. Math. -Nat. 62:234. 1925; Schindl. in Fedde, Repert. Sp. Nov. 22:267. 1926, et in Handr. -Mazz. Symb. Sin. 7:570. 1933.

本变种与原变种不同点在于小叶下面密被白色开展的绒毛和丝状毛，花萼裂片明显较萼筒长，荚果密被白色开展绒毛和丝状毛，边缘具睫毛。

产四川、云南。生于山坡石砾地或林内，海拔1700-3100米。模式标本采自四川盐源雅砻江流域。
