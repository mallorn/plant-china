## Description
Young branches and leaf abaxially sparsely hairy. Leaf blade papery, base rounded or slightly cordate; lateral veins 7 or 8 pairs; nervules loosely anastomosing, subreticulate; stipules triangular, ca. 3 mm. Bracts slender, filiform.
