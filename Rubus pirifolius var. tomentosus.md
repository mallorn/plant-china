绒毛梨叶悬钩子",
139c.**Rubus pirifolius var. tomentosus** Kuntze ex Franchet",

## Description
Leaf base rounded; blade adaxially and sepals abaxially tomentose. Inflorescences densely hairy.

> C Sichuan (Baoxing Xian).
**89c.绒毛梨叶悬钩子（变种）**

R. pirifolius Smith var. tomentosus Ktze. in Herb. Mus. Paris: Franch. Pl, David. 2:37. 1888.

本变种枝，叶片下面和萼片外面被绒毛。

产四川（穆坪）。未见标本，附此待考订。
