大花肋柱花",
7.**Lomatogonium macranthum** (Diels & Gilg) Fernald",

## Description
Annuals 7-35 cm tall. Stems ascending, angular, few branched from base, glabrous. Basal leaves sessile, lanceolate, ovate-lanceolate, or ovate-triangular, 0.7-2.7 cm &amp;#215; 2-12 mm, base obtuse, apex rounded, midvein distinct. Stem leaves sessile, smaller toward apex, lanceolate, elliptic, or ovate-elliptic, 0.4-2 cm &amp;#215; 3-7 mm, base obtuse, apex acute to obtuse, veins indistinct or only midvein abaxially distinct. &lt;BR&gt; Cymes lax, terminal and axillary. Flowers 5merous. Pedicel spreading to arcuate, to 9 cm, angular, glabrous. Calyx tube 1-1.5 mm; lobes linear to linear-lanceolate, 7-11 mm, base obtuse, margin scabrous, apex acuminate, veins 1-3. Corolla pale blue-purple, with dark blue lines, 2-2.5 cm in diam., tube 1-1.5 mm; lobes oblong to ovate-elliptic, 1.3-2 cm, apex acuminate. Nectary connate at base into a tube pointed outward, apex lamellate, margin lobed. Filaments 8-11 mm; anthers blue, narrowly ellipsoid, 3-3.5 mm. Ovary ensiform to ovoid-ellipsoid, apex triangular acute. Stigma lobes triangular, decurrent to apical part of ovary. Capsules narrowly ovoid-ellipsoid to ellipsoid, as long as persistent corolla. Seeds dark brown, ellipsoid, 0.7-0.9 mm in diam.; seed coat scabrous. Fl. and fr. Aug-Oct.

> Beside streams, hillsides, scrub, forests, alpine meadows; 2500-4800 m. Gansu, Qinghai, W Sichuan, E Xizang [Bhutan, Nepal, Sikkim].

一年生草本，高7-35厘米。茎常带紫红色，分枝少而稀疏，斜升，近四棱形，节间长于叶。叶无柄，卵状三角形、卵状披针形或披针形，长7-27毫米，宽2-12毫米，茎上部及小枝的叶较小，先端急尖或钝，基部钝，叶脉不明显或仅中脉在下面明显。花5数，生于分枝顶端，常不等大，直径一般2-2.5厘米；花梗细瘦，弯垂或斜升，近四棱形，常带紫色，不等长，长至9厘米；花萼长为花冠的1/2-2/3，裂片狭披针形至线形，稍不整齐，长7-11毫米，先端急尖，边缘微粗糙，背面中脉明显；花冠蓝紫色，具深色纵脉纹，裂片矩圆形或矩圆状倒卵形，长13-20毫米，先端急尖或钝，具小尖头，基部两侧各具1个腺窝，腺窝管形，基部稍合生，边缘具长约3毫米的裂片状流苏；花丝线形，长8-11毫米，仅下部稍增宽，花药蓝色，狭矩圆形，长3-3.2毫米；子房无柄，长至16毫米，柱头小，下延至子房下部。蒴果无柄，狭矩圆形或狭矩圆状披针形，长17-21毫米；种子深褐色，矩圆形，长0.7-0.9毫米，表面微粗糙，稍有光泽。花果期8-10月。

产西藏、四川、青海、甘肃。生于河滩草地、山坡草地、灌丛草甸、林下、高山草甸，海拔2500-4800米。模式标本采自青海共和。

本种叶型虽有一些变化，但以卵状披针形为主，前人所定的Swertia deltoidea Burk.与本种无明显区别，应予归并。
