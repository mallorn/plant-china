## Description
Trees small. Branchlets ridged, glabrous. Petiole 8-15 mm; leaf blade oblong-elliptic, 4-9.5 × 2.5-4 cm, leathery, apex acute or obtuse. Fruit ellipsoidal, 6-8 mm in diam.; pyrenes 4 or 5.

> ● Evergreen forests; ca. 100-300 m. S Taiwan (Hengchun peninsula).
  "Synonym": "*Ilex matsudae* Yamamoto, Icon. Pl. Formos., Suppl. 1: 37. 1925 [*\"matsudai\"*].
