## Description
Plants glandular pubescent. Leaf blade 4--12 X 6--20 mm, margin sharply dentate. Bracts 2.5--5(--6) mm, margin entire. Fl. May-Jul.

> Dry grassy slopes; 3300--3500 m. W Xizang [India].
