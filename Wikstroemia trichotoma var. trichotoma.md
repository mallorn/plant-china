白花荛花(原变种)",
48a.**Wikstroemia trichotoma var. trichotoma**",

## Description
Leaf blade with 6-8 pairs of lateral veins. Inflorescence rachis 0.2-1.5 cm, up to 10-flowered. Calyx tube 6.5-7.5 mm. Disk scale linear, lobed. Ovary ca. 3 mm, apex sparsely puberulous.

> Open forests, shaded places, roadsides; ca. 600 m. Anhui, Guangdong, Hunan, Jiangxi, Zhejiang [Japan, S Korea].
