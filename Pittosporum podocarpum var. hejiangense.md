合江海桐",
10b.**Pittosporum podocarpum var. hejiangense** (H. Y. Su) Z. Y. Zhang & Turland",

## Description
Young branchlets sparsely puberulent. Leaf blade abaxially pubescent. Ovary stipe 1–2 mm. Capsule ellipsoid to long ellipsoid, densely brown puberulent. Fr. Aug.

> Forests on slopes; ca. 800 m. SE Sichuan (Hejiang Xian).
  "Synonym": "*Pittosporum hejiangense* H. Y. Su, Bull. Bot. Res., Harbin 4(4): 201. 1984.
