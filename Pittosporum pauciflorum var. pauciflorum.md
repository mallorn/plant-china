少花海桐(原变种)
6a.**Pittosporum pauciflorum var. pauciflorum**

## Description
Capsule ellipsoid or ovoid, ca.1.2 × 1 cm. Fl. Apr–May, fr. May–Oct.


> Thickets, valleys, stream sides, roadsides; 700--1000 m. Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, S Hunan, Jiangxi [Vietnam].

  "Synonym": "*Pittosporum ovoideum* Gowda (1951), not H. T. Chang &amp; S. Z. Yan (1974).
