## Description
Rachis and costae with thin, short, multicellular hairs sparse on abaxial side or subglabrate; pinnae deltoid-lanceolate; pinna segments thinner and approximate, 2-3 mm wide, and truncate at apex.

> ● Coniferous forests on mountain slopes; ca. 3500 m. Yunnan (Gongshan).
  "Synonym": "Basionym: *Lunathyrium sichuanense* var. *gongshanense* Z. R. Wang, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 32: 88. 1994.
**19b. 贡山蛾眉蕨（变种）（横断山区维管植物）**

var. gongshanense Z. R. Wang in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 32 (1): 88-89. 1994 et in W. T. Wang, Vasc. Pl. Hengduan Mount. 1: 73. 1993, nom. nud.

本变种与原变种不同在于叶轴和羽轴下面的节状毛细短而稀疏，近无毛，羽片三角状披针形，裂片细小而密接，宽2-3毫米，平截头。

特产于云南，贡山。生于山坡针叶林下，海拔3500米。
