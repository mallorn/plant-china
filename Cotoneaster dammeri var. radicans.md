## Description
Petiole 6–10 mm; leaf blade usually obovate. Pedicels 1–1.5 cm.

> Mountain areas, rocks; 2000--4100 m. Gansu, Hubei, Sichuan, Xizang.
  "Synonym": "*Cotoneaster radicans* Dammer ex C. K. Schneider, Ill. Handb. Laubholzk. 1: 761. 1906.
