## Description
Twigs densely puberulous, glabrescent. Leaf blade elliptic or ovate-lanceolate, 4–13 × 2–7 cm, glabrous, base rounded to broadly cuneate. Pedicel densely puberulous. Bracts and bracteoles denticulate at margin, puberulous.

> Forests, thickets, open slopes; 2000–2500(–3200) m. W Yunnan.
