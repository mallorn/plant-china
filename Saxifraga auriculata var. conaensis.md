错那虎耳草",
52b.**Saxifraga auriculata var. conaensis** J. T. Pan",

## Description
Sepals 3-veined, margin membranous, glabrous or sparsely glandular hairy, apex erose. Petals obovate to elliptic, distinctly 6-callose. Fl. and fr. Jul-Sep.

> * Shaded and wet places in forests; 3200-3600 m. S Xizang (Cona Xian).
**45b.错那虎耳草（西藏植物志）图版23：16-20**

Saxifraga auriculata var. conaensis J. T. Pan in C. Y. Wu, Fl. Xizang. 2: 471. f. 156: 14-18. 1985.

此变种与原变种之区别在于其萼片先端啮蚀状，边缘膜质且无毛或疏生腺毛，通常3脉，花瓣倒卵形至椭圆形，具明显之6痂体。花果期7-9月。

产我国西藏错那。生于海拔3 200-3 600米的林下阴湿处。模式标本采自西藏错那。
