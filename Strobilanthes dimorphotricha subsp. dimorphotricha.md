球花马蓝(原亚种)",
108a.**Strobilanthes dimorphotricha subsp. dimorphotricha**",

## Description
Leaf blade ovate, elliptic, or elliptic-lanceolate, pairs at each node quite unequal in size, apex acuminate to caudate. Fl. Aug-Nov(-Apr).
  "Statistics": "Thickets on limestone hills, streamsides; 200-2200 m. Chongqing, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hainan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan, Zhejiang [India, Vietnam].
