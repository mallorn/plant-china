马甲菝葜 (原变种)",
52a.**Smilax lanceifolia var. lanceifolia**",

## Description
Leaf blade usually ovate to ovate-lanceolate, usually 1--3 × as long as wide, papery, adaxially not or slightly shiny, main veins raised adaxially (sometimes midvein slightly concave). 2&lt;I&gt; n&lt;/I&gt; = 32.

> Forests, thickets, shaded places on slopes; 600--2800 m. Guangxi, Guizhou, Hubei, Sichuan, Yunnan [Bhutan, NE India, Laos, Myanmar, Sikkim, Thailand, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt; Smilax micropoda&lt;/I&gt; A. de Candolle.
