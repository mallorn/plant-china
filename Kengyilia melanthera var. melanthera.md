黑药以礼草(原变种)",
5a.**Kengyilia melanthera var. melanthera**",

## Description
Culms 15–25 cm tall. Glumes glabrous or puberulent. Lemma apex with awn 2–4 mm. Fl. and fr. late summer.

> * Mountain slopes, sandy grassy slopes, lake banks. Qinghai.
  "Synonym": "*Agropyron melantherum* Keng, Sunyatsenia 6: 62. 1941; *Elymus melantherus* (Keng) ┴. Löve; *Kengyilia thoroldiana* (Oliver) J. L. Yang et al. var. *melanthera* (Keng) L. B. Cai; *Roegneria melanthera* (Keng) Keng.
