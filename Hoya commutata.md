广西球兰",
11.**Hoya commutata** M. G. Gilbert & P. T. Li",

## Description
Habit not known, probably a hanging epiphyte. Stems pubescent. Leaves not known. Pseudumbels globose, pubescent, ca. 30-flowered; peduncle ca. 2 cm. Pedicel to 4 cm, slender, flexuous when dried. Sepals triangular, ca. 2.5 &amp;#215; 0.9-1.3 mm, densely pubescent. Corolla rotate with revolute margin, purple, ca. 1.5 cm in diam., densely papillate-pubescent, limb ca. 0.8 cm wide. Corona lobes acute, stellate spreading, extending beyond sinus between corolla lobes; inner margin acute, &amp;#177; meeting in center.

> Forests. Guangxi [Myanmar].
