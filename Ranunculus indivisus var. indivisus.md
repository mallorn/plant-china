## Description
Upper stem leaves 3-sect, segments narrowly lanceolate or linear. Fl. Jun--Jul, fr. Aug.

> * Scrub, slopes, under rocks; 3400--3900 m. C and NE Qinghai, Shanxi (Jiaocheng Xian).
