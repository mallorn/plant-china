## Description
Low shrubs, deciduous, rarely evergreen, branchlets subverticillate, glabrous or villous. Leaf blade villous or silky-pubescent, glabrescent. Inflorescence 1- to many-flowered; corolla rotate-campanulate or rotate-funnelform, pale rose to purple or red, rarely white, often with flecks within and glabrous on outer surface; stamens 5–10, unequal; ovary villous and/or glandular. Capsules oblong-ovoid to cylindric, rarely conical-ovoid.
