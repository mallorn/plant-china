莎草兰(原变种)",
26a.**Cymbidium elegans var. elegans**",

## Description
Plants epiphytic. Leaves 6-13. Rachis with 20-35 centrifugal flowers. Lamellae on lip without any appendages. Fl. Oct-Dec. 2*n* = 40.
  "Statistics": "Trees in forests, cliffs; 1700-2800 m. SW Sichuan, SE Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, India, Myanmar, Nepal].
  "Synonym": "*Cymbidium longifolium* D. Don, nom. utique rej.; *Cyperorchis elegans* (Lindley) Blume.
