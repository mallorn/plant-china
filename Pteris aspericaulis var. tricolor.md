> *Pteris aspericaulis* var. *tricolor* is a beautiful ornamental plant.

## Description
Plants 0.3-0.5 m tall. Pinnae white or rosy, with pink costa with wide whitish or rose-colored bands along both sides, margins green.

> SE Yunnan (Yingjiang) [Bhutan, India (Sikkim)].
  "Synonym": "*Pteris tricolor* Linden, Gard. Chron. 1860: 123, 217. 1860.
**41d.三色凤尾蕨（中国高等植物图鉴）（变种）**

Pteris aspericaulis var. tricolor Moore apud Lowe, New Ferns 19, t. 9. 1860-61. ——Pteris tricolor Linden, Gard. Chron. 217. 1860; Hieron. in Hedwigia 55:352. 1914.

羽片沿羽轴两侧有白色或玫瑰色的宽带。

产云南西南部（盈江）。锡金及不丹也产。非常美丽，可供观赏。
