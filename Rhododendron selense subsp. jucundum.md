> The type of *Rhododendron hedythamnum* has calyx lobes quite well-developed and the leaves shorter than is usual in *R*. *selense* subsp. *jucundum*.

## Description
Young shoots, petioles, and pedicels with short glandular hairs or sessile glands. Leaf blade broadly elliptic, 5–7 × 3–4 cm; abaxial surface with obvious frosting. Pedicel with glandular hairs; calyx 2–5 mm. Fl. May, fr. Oct.

> *Abies* forests, *Rhododendron* thickets; 3200–3600 m. W Yunnan.

与原亚种的区别在于本亚种叶片宽椭圆形，长5-7厘米，宽3-4厘米，下面有明显的白粉；花梗上有腺毛；萼片较大等。花期5月，果期10月。

产云南西部。生于海拔3 200-3 600米冷杉林下及杜鹃灌丛中。模式标本采自大理（苍山）。
