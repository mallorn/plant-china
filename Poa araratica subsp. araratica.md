阿洼早熟禾(原亚种)",
78a.**Poa araratica subsp. araratica**",

## Description
Ligule 1.5–2.5(–3) mm. Lemma keel shortly villous for 1/2 of length, marginal veins for 1/3, area between veins glabrous; callus glabrous or minutely webbed.

> Open grassy slopes, subalpine forest margins; 3300–4200 m. Xinjiang, Xizang [India, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Mongolia, Nepal, Pakistan, Russia, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan; SW Asia].
