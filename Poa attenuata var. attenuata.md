渐尖早熟禾(原变种)",
79a.**Poa attenuata var. attenuata**",

## Description
Lemma callus webbed. 2*n* = 28, 42.

> Dry grasslands, rocky and stepped slopes; 3300–5500 m. Gansu, Hebei, Nei Mongol, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Xinjiang, Xizang [Bhutan, India, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Mongolia, Nepal, Pakistan, Russia (Siberia), Tajikistan, Uzbekistan].
  "Synonym": "*Poa tetrantha* Keng ex L. Liu.
