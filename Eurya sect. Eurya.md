真柃组",
2.**Eurya sect. Eurya**",

## Description
Current year branchlets terete or 2- or 4-ribbed. Anthers not locellate. Ovary glabrous, with 5-10 or rarely more ovules per locule; style 0.5-4 mm.
  "Statistics": "Fifty-two species: Bhutan, Cambodia, China, India, Indonesia, Japan, Korea, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, Nepal, Philippines, Sri Lanka, Thailand, Vietnam; 52 species (41 endemic) in China.
  "Synonym": "*Pseudoeurya* Yamamoto.
