灰岩香茶菜",
22a.**Isodon calcicolus var. calcicolus**",

## Description
Leaf blade densely pilose adaxially, abaxially rust colored tomentulose on elevated midvein and veins, otherwise gray tomentulose and glandular. Corolla white or purplish.

> * Grassy slopes, grasslands near forest margins in limestone regions; 1600-2600 m. Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Plectranthus calciolus&lt;/I&gt; Handel-Mazzetti, Symb. Sin. 7: 944. 1936.
