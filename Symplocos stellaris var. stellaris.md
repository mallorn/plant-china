33a.**Symplocos stellaris var. stellaris**",

## Description
Twigs and bud-scales tomentellous. Leaf blade margin entire and strongly revolute, apical acumen 0--10 mm, reticulate veins obscure. Fl. Apr-May, fr. Jun.

> Slopes in mixed forests; ca. 1100 m. Anhui, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Jiangsu, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan, Zhejiang [Japan (Okinawa)].
