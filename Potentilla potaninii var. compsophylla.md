裂叶华西委陵菜",
46b.**Potentilla potaninii var. compsophylla** (Handel-Mazzetti) T. T. Yü & C. L. Li",

## Description
Leaflet margin pectinately parted, segments fasciated ligulate. Fl. and fr. Jun–Sep.

> Meadows on mountain slopes; 3300--4700 m. Sichuan, Xizang.
  "Synonym": "*Potentilla compsophylla* Handel-Mazzetti, Acta Horti Gothob. 13: 306. 1939.
**43b.裂叶华西委陵菜（变种）**

P. potaninii Wolf var. compsophylla (Hand. -Mazz) Yu et Li, stat. nov.——P. compsophylla Hand.-Mazz. in Acta Hort. Gothob. 13: 306. 1939.

小叶边缘锯齿分裂较深，裂成篦齿状裂片，裂片呈舌状带形。花果期6-9月。

产四川、西藏（阿里）。生山坡草地，海拔3300-4700米。
