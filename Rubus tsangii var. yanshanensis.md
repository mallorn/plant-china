铅山悬钩子",
62b.**Rubus tsangii var. yanshanensis** (Z. X. Yu & W. T. Ji) L. T. Lu",

## Description
Leaves 5–7(–11)-foliolate, abaxially with gland-tipped hairs along veins. Ovary and aggregate fruit often with glandular hairs.

> Forests, forest margins, foothills; 1100--1800 m. N Jiangxi (Nanchang Xian, Yanshan Xian).
  "Synonym": "*Rubus yanshanensis* Z. X. Yu &amp; W. T. Ji, Acta Bot. Yunnan. 13: 255. 1991.
