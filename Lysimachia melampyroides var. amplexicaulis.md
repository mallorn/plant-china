抱茎山萝过路黄",
56b.**Lysimachia melampyroides var. amplexicaulis** F. H. Chen & C. M. Hu",

## Description
Plants to 48 cm tall. Leaves sessile, clasping at base, 4--7 X 1--2.5 cm. Corolla 7--9 mm; tube ca. 2 mm; lobes rhomboid-ovate, ca. 6 mm wide, apex acute. Fl. Jun.

> * Streamsides, in shrubs; ca. 1000 m. NE Guangxi, W Hunan.
**51b. 抱茎山萝过路黄（变种）图版15：5**

var. amplexicaulis Chen et C. M. Hu in Act. phytotax. Sin. 17: 33. 1979.

叶无柄，基部耳状抱茎。

产于广西东北部（全县）和湖南西南部（东安）。生于山谷溪边和灌丛中。海拔1000米。模式标本采自广西全县。
