## Description
Shrubs evergreen, climbing, with curved prickles in pairs below leaves. Leaflets 5–9; stipules with base adnate to petiole, pectinate. Flower solitary, with large bracts. Hypanthium tomentose. Sepals reflexed after anthesis, margin entire. Styles free, slightly exserted.
  "Statistics": "Two species: E and S Asia; one species in China.
