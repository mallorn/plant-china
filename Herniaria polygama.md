1.**Herniaria polygama** J. Gay",

## Description
Plants annual, pubescent. Stems spreading, 3--15 cm. Petiole short; leaf blade oblong-elliptic, 3--10 × 1--3 mm, base attenuate, apex obtuse. Glomerules leaf-opposed, (6--)10--12- or more flowered. Flowers 4-merous, 1.4--1.5(--1.7) mm. Sepals lanceolate, ca. 1.5 × 0.5 mm, abaxially hairy, apex apiculate. Stamens 4, shorter than sepals. Style (0.3--)0.5 mm, often longer than sepals at maturity, divided for ca. 1/3 length. Achene ovoid, subequaling calyx. Fl. Jul--Aug, fr. Aug--Sep.

> Steppes, riverside grasslands; ca. 500 m. Xinjiang [Russia; E Europe].
**2. 杂性治疝草（新拟） 杂性脱肠草（拉汉种子植物名称）图版11: 6-9**

Herniaria polygama J. Gay in Duch. Rev. Bot. 2: 371. 1847; Muravjeva in Kom. Fl. USSR 6: 571. tab. 34. fig. 4. 1936.

多年生草本，高3-15厘米，全株被短柔毛。茎铺散，稍直立。叶片长圆状椭圆形，长3-10毫米，宽1-3毫米，顶端钝，基部渐狭，两面被短柔毛；叶柄极短；托叶宽卵圆形，白色，膜质，具缘毛。聚伞花序腋生；萼片4，披针形，长1.5毫米，宽0.5毫米，顶端急尖，外面被毛；雄蕊4，短于萼片，花药长圆形；花柱外露，柱头2裂，长约0.5毫米。瘦果卵形，通常与宿存萼近等长；种子未见。花期7-8月，果期8-9月。

产新疆。生于海拔550米草原地带。罗马尼亚、乌克兰、俄罗斯也有。
