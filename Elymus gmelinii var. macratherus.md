## Description
Culms 75–100 cm tall, ca. 3 mm in diam. at base. Glumes subequal, 10–15 mm. Lemma awn 3–4.5 cm. Fl. and fr. Jul–Aug.

> * Mountain slopes, roadsides; 600–2600 m. Nei Mongol, Xinjiang.
  "Synonym": "*Agropyron turczaninowii* var. *macratherum* Ohwi, Acta Phytotax. Geobot. 10: 98. 1941; *Roegneria gmelinii* var. *macra-thera* (Ohwi) Kitagawa; *R. macrathera* (Ohwi) L. B. Cai; *R. **turczaninowii* var. *macrathera* (Ohwi) H. L. Yang &amp; C. P. Wang.
