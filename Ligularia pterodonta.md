## Description
Stem erect, ca. 62 cm tall, ca. 6 mm in diam. at base, shortly pale yellow pilose. Basal leaves petiolate; petiole 22-26 cm, distalmost winged; wings 4.5-5 cm wide, base enlarged sheathed, margin regularly triangularly dentate; leaf blade ovate-cordate, 13-15 × 14-16 cm, glabrous, pinnately veined, base cordate, margin densely dentate, apex acute. Middle to distal stem leaves similar but smaller. Synflorescence racemose, ca. 13 cm; leaflike bracts lanceolate, 2-2.5 cm × 2-3.2 mm; peduncles ca. 1 cm. Capitula numerous; supplementary bracts short. Involucre broadly campanulate, ca. 1 cm, outside shortly pilose; phyllaries ca. 8, in 2 rows, apex acute; outer phyllaries linear, 2-2.5 mm wide; inner phyllaries 3-4 mm wide, margin broadly membranous. Ray florets ca. 8, yellow; lamina ca. 28 × 5 mm, apex obtuse; tube ca. 5 mm. Tubular florets numerous, ca. 10.5 mm; tube ca. 4.5 mm; limb campanulate, ca. 2.5 mm wide. Achenes oblong, ca. 4.5 mm. Pappus reddish brown, ca. 4 mm, as long as tube of tubular corolla. Fl. and fr. Sep.

**90.宽翅橐吾**

Ligularla pterodonta Chang in Bull. Fan Mem. Inst. Biol. Bot. 6: 65. 1935; Hand.-Mazz. in Bot. Jahru. 69: 134. 1938; S. Y. Hu in Quart. Jaurn. Taiwan Mus. 20: 68. 1967.

多年生草本。茎直立，高约62厘米，被淡黄色有节短柔毛，基部直径约6毫米。丛生叶与茎下部叶具柄，柄长22-26厘米，顶部有翅，翅宽4.5-5厘米，边缘有整齐的三角状齿，基部膨大鞘状，叶片卵状心形，长13-15厘米，宽14-16厘米，先端急尖，边缘有密齿，齿端具软骨质小尖头，基部心形，两面光滑，叶脉羽状；茎中上部叶与下部叶相似而．小。总状花序长达13厘米；苞片披针形，长2-2.5厘米，宽2-3.2毫米；花序梗长约1厘米；头状花序多数，辐射状；小苞片短；总苞宽钟形，长约1厘米，总苞片8，2层，先端急尖，背部被有节短柔毛，外层线形，宽2-2.5毫米，内层宽3-4毫米，具宽的膜质边缘。舌状花约8个，黄色，舌片长约28毫米，宽约5毫米，先端钝，管部长约5毫米；管状花多数，长约10.5毫米，管部长4.5毫米，檐部钟形，宽约2.5毫米，冠毛红褐色，长约4毫米，与管部等长。瘦果长圆形，长约4.5毫米，光滑。花果期9月。（未见标本，描述是摘译的）

产我国西藏察隅（独龙江一怒江分水岭，28°24′N，98 °24′E，模式标本产地）。生于海拔4000米的灌丛边缘。
