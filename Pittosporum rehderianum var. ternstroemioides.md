厚皮香海桐
2b.**Pittosporum rehderianum var. ternstroemioides** (C. Y. Wu) Z. Y. Zhang & Turland

## Description
Seeds ca. 8 per capsule.


> Thickets, bases of slopes; ca. 2400 m. W Yunnan (Longling Xian).

  "Synonym": "*Pittosporum ternstroemioides* C. Y. Wu in W. C. Yin, Fl. Yunnan. 3: 320. 1983.
