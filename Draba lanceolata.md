> Schulz confused the limits of &lt;I&gt;Draba ladyginii&lt;/I&gt;, &lt;I&gt;D. lanceolata&lt;/I&gt;, &lt;I&gt;D. lasiophylla&lt;/I&gt;, and &lt;I&gt;D. mongolica&lt;/I&gt;, as evidenced from the varieties he described originally under certain species and in his monograph transferred to others. The first two species are easily separated from the latter two by having usually more than 30 instead of less than 24 ovules/seeds per fruit. Other characters that distinguish the four species are given in the key above.&lt;P&gt;The records of &lt;I&gt;Draba incana&lt;/I&gt; Linnaeus and &lt;I&gt;D. stylaris&lt;/I&gt; J. Gay ex E. A. Thomas from China (e.g., FRPS 168-171; Fl. Qinghai. 1: 449. 1995; Fl. Sichuan. 14: 74. 1999; Fl. Xinjiang. 2(2): 123-126. 1995; Fl. Xizang. 2: 354-355. 1985) are based on misidentified plants of &lt;I&gt;D. lanceolata&lt;/I&gt;. True &lt;I&gt;D. incana&lt;/I&gt; (including &lt;I&gt;D. stylaris&lt;/I&gt;) occurs only in Europe and North America (Canada and N United States). It differs from &lt;I&gt;D. lanceolata&lt;/I&gt; in being biennial (instead of perennial with a distinct caudex) and in having fruits elliptic to oblong and glabrous (instead of almost always lanceolate to lanceolate-linear and tomentose).&lt;P&gt;

## Description
Herbs perennial, 10-30(-40) cm tall, cespitose. Caudex slender, few branched, ultimate branches terminated in rosettes, covered with petiolar remains of previous years. Stems erect, simple, tomentose with subsessile stellate trichomes. Basal leaves rosulate, persistent; petiole to 1.5 cm, often undifferentiated; leaf blade elliptic, oblong, or lanceolate, (0.4-)1-2(-3) cm × (1-)2-6 mm, tomentose with sessile or short-stalked, 4-rayed stellate trichomes 1 or 2 rays of which often branched, base attenuate to cuneate, margin entire or denticulate, apex acute. Cauline leaves 6-12(-16), sessile; leaf blade ovate, oblong, elliptic, or lanceolate, 0.7-2.6(-3.5) cm × 2-7(-10) mm, pubescent as basal leaves, often adaxially pilose with predominantly simple trichomes, base cuneate to obtuse, margin 2-5(-9)-toothed on each side, rarely entire, apex acute. Racemes (7-)14-32(-47)-flowered, ebracteate or lowermost flowers bracteate, elongated in fruit. Fruiting pedicels 2-4.5(-7) mm, erect or ascending, often appressed to rachis, straight, tomentose all around with subsessile, stellate trichomes sometimes mixed with simple ones. Sepals oblong or ovate, 1-2 × 0.5-0.8 mm, erect, abaxially pilose, base of lateral pair not saccate, margin membranous. Petals white, spatulate, 2.5-3.5 × 1-2 mm, apex subemarginate or rounded; claw absent or to 1 mm. Filaments 1.5-2 mm; anthers ovate, 0.2-0.3 mm. Ovules (26-)30-48(-56) per ovary. Fruit lanceolate to lanceolate-linear, (6-)7-11(-12) × 1.5-2 mm, erect and often appressed to rachis, latiseptate, rarely slightly twisted; valves tomentose or very rarely glabrous, obscurely veined, apex acute; style 0.1-0.6 mm. Seeds brown, ovate, 0.8-1.1 × 0.5-0.6 mm. Fl. and fr. Jun-Aug. 2n = 32.

**40. 锥果葶苈（中国高等植物图鉴）**

Draba lanceolata Royle, Illustr. Bot. Himal. Mts. 1: 72. 1839; O. E. Schulz in Engl. Pflanzenr. 89 (4. 105): 296. 1827; A. Tolmaschev in Kom. Fl. URSS 8: 433. 1939; 中国高等植物图鉴2: 50. 图1829. 1972. ——D. stylaris auct. non J. Gay. N. Busch, Fl. Sib. et Or. Bxtr. 3: 375. 1919.
**40a. 锥果葶苈（原变种）**

Draba lanceolata Royle var. lanceolata

多年生，有时二年生丛生草本，高6-35厘米。茎单一或分枝，直立，密生小分枝毛。基生叶莲座状丛生，狭披针形或倒披针形，长1-2厘米，宽3-5毫米，顶端渐尖，基部缩狭，边缘有锯齿或全缘；茎生叶疏生，长卵形或宽披针形，顶端渐尖，边缘有1-4锯齿或近于全缘，无柄；叶都密生小星状毛及分枝毛，边缘有时有单毛、分枝毛。总状花序有花10-40朵，密集，结实时呈辫状伸长；小花梗短，密生分枝毛及星状毛；萼片长椭圆形，背面密生单毛、分枝毛；花瓣白色，倒卵状楔形，长3-4毫米。短角果长圆披针形，长6-12毫米，宽1.5毫米，向上开展，贴近花序轴，排列呈辫状，紧密；果瓣密生分枝毛和星状毛；果梗较短。种子棕色。花期6-7月。

产甘肃、青海、新疆、西藏。生于山坡岩石及路旁，海拔1100-4850米。克什米尔地区、印度西北部及苏联西伯利亚、贝加尔湖地区、阿尔泰苏联部分也有分布。模式标本采自苏联。
**40d. 短锥果葶苈（变种）**

Draba lanceolata Royle var. brachycarpa O. E. Schulz, l. c. 298. 1927.

植株矮小，高9-15厘米。茎生叶长5-8毫米，宽3-4毫米。花小，密集；萼片长约1毫米：花瓣长约2毫米。短角果长5-7毫米，宽1-1.5毫米，有毛；果梗短，长1-2毫米。

产新疆。生冲积平原干旱土上及山坡岩石旁。苏联土耳其斯坦也有分布。模式标本采自苏联。
**40c. 紫茎锥果葶苈（变种）**

Draba lanceolata Royle var. chingii O. E. Schulz, l. c. 298. 1927.

茎高20-30厘米，呈紫色、被灰白色毛。基生叶长1-2厘米；茎生叶较密，长5-12毫米，宽4-5毫米；叶都密被分枝毛、星状毛和单毛。短角果长7-2毫米，无毛；果梗长4-8毫米，与果序轴成近于直角开展。花期7月。

产甘肃、青海、新疆、四川；生于山坡灌木林下阴湿处或沼泽地，海拔3310-4400米。模式标本采自甘肃。
**40b. 光锥果葶苈（变种）**

Draba lanceolata Royle var. leiocarpa O. E. Schulz in Engl. Pflanzenr. 89 (4. 105): 297. 1927.

短角果无毛，直或扭捩。茎上部及小花梗上的毛逐渐疏生或无毛。

产青海、新疆。生于林下或灌木丛中，海拔2750-3900米。
