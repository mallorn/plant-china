丽江百合
32.**Lilium lijiangense** L. J. Peng

## Description
Bulb subglobose, 2.5--4 cm in diam.; scales white, tinged purple, lanceolate, 2.5--5 × 1--2 cm. Stem 55--60 cm, with purple spots or streaks. Leaves scattered, elliptic, suboblong, ovate-lanceolate, or lanceolate, 5--11 × 1.5--3 cm, 7--9-veined, axil with a cluster of white hairs. Flowers solitary or 2--5 in a raceme, nodding, fragrant. Tepals yellow, with purple or brown spots, oblong to lanceolate, 4--4.5 × 0.8--1.5 cm, apex slightly papillose, revolute; nectaries blackish or red, not papillose. Stamens diverging; filaments 2.5--3 cm; anthers ca. 7 mm. Ovary 7--10 × ca. 2 mm. Style 3--3.5 cm. Fl. Jul--Aug.


> * 3300--3400 m. W Sichuan, NW Yunnan.

  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt; Lilium ningnanense&lt;/I&gt; J. M. Xu.
