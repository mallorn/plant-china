> Two species: Bhutan, China, Nepal, Sikkim; one species in China.

## Description
Herbs tall, not drying black. Stems branched. Leaves alternate, &amp;#177; ovate, large, usually more than 3.5 cm wide, 3-pinnatifid to pinnatisect. Inflorescences long racemose. Calyx deeply cleft anteriorly. Corolla small; galea with a widened anther case, margin long bearded; beak semicircular, sinistrorse, slender.
