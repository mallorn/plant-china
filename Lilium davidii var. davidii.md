38a.**Lilium davidii var. davidii**

## Description
Leaves usually 1-veined, axil with a cluster of white woolly hairs. 2&lt;I&gt; n&lt;/I&gt; = 24*.


> * Moist places in forests, forest margins, grassy slopes; 1600--3200 m. Guizhou, Sichuan, NW Yunnan.
