## Description
Culms 12–25(–35) cm tall. Leaf sheath densely pubescent. Spike rachis densely villous. Anthers purple, 2.5–2.7 mm. Fl. and fr. late summer. 2*n* = 42*.

> * Alpine steppes; ca. 4200 m. Xinjiang.
  "Synonym": "*Kengyilia nana* J. L. Yang et al., Canad. J. Bot. 71: 341. 1993.
