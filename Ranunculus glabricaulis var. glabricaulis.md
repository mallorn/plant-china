甘藏毛茛(原变种)",
28a.**Ranunculus glabricaulis var. glabricaulis**",

## Description
Stems and pedicels glabrous. Sepals dark purple, glabrous or abaxially sparsely puberulent.

> * Meadows, gravelly slopes; ca. 5000 m. C Gansu (Tianzhu Xian), E Xizang.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Ranunculus hirtellus&lt;/I&gt; Royle var. &lt;I&gt;glabricaulis &lt;/I&gt; Handel-Mazzetti, Acta Horti Gothob. 13: 151. 1939.
