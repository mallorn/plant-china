宽瓣组",
2.**Cyananthus sect. Cyananthus**",

> Nine species: SW China, Himalaya; six species (two endemic) in China.

## Description
Plants perennial. Caudex densely clothed in persistent and straw-colored scales. Calyx densely covered only with brown-black setae, or mixed with such hairs. Corolla lobes suborbicular, broadly ovate or oblong, as long as or slightly longer than broad.
