绒毛钟花神血宁",
97b.**Polygonum campanulatum var. fulvidum** J. D. Hooker",

## Description
Leaves abaxially densely brown tomentose.

> Slopes, valleys; 1400-4100 m. Guizhou, W Hubei, Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan [Nepal, Sikkim].
