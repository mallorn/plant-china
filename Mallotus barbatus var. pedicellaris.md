长梗毛桐",
17b.**Mallotus barbatus var. pedicellaris** Croizat",

## Description
Small trees 3-6 m tall. Branchlets, petiole, and inflorescences densely brownish floccose-tomentose. Female inflorescences unbranched, 15-32 cm; infructescence erect. Fruiting pedicel often 15-30 mm, sometimes with 1 or 2 bracteoles, subulate, ca. 1.5 mm, deciduous; capsule ca. 20 mm in diam. Fl. May-Jul, fr. Aug-Nov.

> Mountain valleys, forests; 200-700 m. N Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, W Hubei, Hunan, Sichuan, Yunnan [N Thailand].
  "Synonym": "*Mallotus barbatus* var. *hubeiensis* S. M. Hwang.
**18c.长梗野桐（变种）（新拟）**

Mallotus barbatus (Wall.) Muell. Arg. var. pedicellaris Croiz. in Journ. Arn. Arb. 191 135. 1938.

本变种的叶卵形或椭圆状卵形，长10-20厘米，宽7-12厘米，顶端渐尖，基部近圆形，上部边缘稍2裂或全缘；蒴果密生长约6毫米的软刺和星状毛，形成连续的厚毛层；果梗纤细、长1.5-5厘米。果期10月

产于四川、贵州和广西。生于密林中。模式标本采自四川忠县。
