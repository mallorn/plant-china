镰芒针茅(原亚种)
11a.**Stipa caucasica subsp. caucasica**

> This subspecies provides fodder in early spring in desert steppe.


## Description
Plant coarse, at least 20 cm tall; leaf blades 0.5–1 mm in diam., outer surface usually smooth; glumes 3.5–4 cm; awn 7–14 cm.


> Stony slopes, landslips; 1400–4500 m. Xinjiang, Xizang [Afghanistan, Kashmir, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan; SW Asia (Caucasus, N Iran)].

  "Synonym": "*Stipa orientalis* Trinius var. *grandiflora* Ruprecht.
