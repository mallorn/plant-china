短轴山梅花",
9c.**Philadelphus incanus var. baileyi** Rehder",

## Description
Leaf blade abaxially sparsely hairy. Racemes aggregates, 5-7-flowered; rachis 0.2-0.3 cm; pedicels 1-2 mm. Disc and style glabrous.

> * Thickets; ca. 1600 m. Henan, Shaanxi.
**9c. 短轴山梅花（变种）**

var. baileyi Rehd. in Gent. Herb. 1: 27. 1920 et in Journ. Arn. Arb. 5: 153. 1924; Chung in Mem. Sci. Soc. China 1: 64: 1924; S. Y. Hu in Journ. Arn. Arb. 36: 340. 1955.

本变种与原变种不同点在于总状花序密聚，有花5-7朵，花序轴长2-3毫米。

产陕西和河南。生于海拔约1600米灌丛中。模式标本采自河南鸡公山。
