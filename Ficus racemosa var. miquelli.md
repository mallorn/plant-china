柔毛聚果榕",
28b.**Ficus racemosa var. miquelli** (King) Corner",

## Description
Branchlets, young leaf blades, and figs densely covered with white pubescence.

> 100-1700 m. Yunnan [India, N Myanmar, N Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Ficus* *glomerata* Roxburgh var. *miquelli* King, Ann. Roy. Bot. Gard. (Calcutta) 1: 174. 1888; *F*. *chittagonga* Miquel; *F*. *glomerata* var. *chittagonga* (Miquel) King.
**27b.柔毛聚果榕（变种）**

Ficus racemosa Linn. var. miquelli(King) Corner in Gard. Bull. Sing. 21: 34. 1965; 云南种子植物名录, 上册, 707, 1984; Nguyen T. Hiep in Tap chi SIKH HOC 16(4): 61. ——F. chittagonga Miq. in Ann. Mus. Lugd. -Bat. 3: 288, 294. 1847, p, p. foliorum——F. glomerata Roxb. var. miquelli King in Ann. Bot. Gard. Calcutta 1: 173. 1887——F. glomerata Roxb. var. chittagonga(Miq). King 1. c. t. 219 Gagnep. in Lecomte, Fl. Gen. Indo-Chine 5: 807. 1928.

本变种与原变种主要区别在于幼枝、嫩叶、榕果密被白色柔毛。

产云南（禄劝、开远、峨山、新平、南盘江、蒙自、西双版纳、元阳、河口等地）。生于海拔（130-）380-1700米地区。印度、缅甸北部、越南北部也有。
