大理翠雀花(原变种)
117a.**Delphinium taliense var. taliense**

## Description
Leaf blade less divided; ultimate lobules triangular or narrowly so. Spur subulate, 1.3--2.4 cm, base 2--3 mm in diam.; other sepals 1--1.6 cm, appressed puberulent abaxially. Staminode limb 2-lobed nearly to middle or emarginate. Fl. Aug--Nov.


> * Forest margins, meadows; 2800--3500 m. SW Sichuan, NC and NW Yunnan.
