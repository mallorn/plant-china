## Description
Culms 13–20 cm tall. Spikelets often with 3 glumes. Glumes 5-veined. First lemma 6–9 mm; awn 3–9 mm. Fl. Jul–Aug.

> * Mountain grasslands; 2800–3400 m. Xinjiang (Tian Shan).
