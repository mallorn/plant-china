## Description
Plants robust, usually 4-11 cm tall. Cauline leaves more than 1 mm wide. Pedicel brown crisped glandular villous. Fl. and fr. Jul-Sep.

> * Alpine meadows, rock crevices; 3000-4600 m. SW Sichuan (Muli Zang Zu Zizhixian), N Yunnan.
