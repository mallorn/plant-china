## Description
Plants with long dense glandular hairs and bristles to 5 mm.

> Mountain summits, foothills, montane valleys; below 1500 m. Guangdong, Guizhou, Hunan, Jiangxi.
  "Synonym": "*Rubus farinaceus* Cardot.
**120c.长腺灰白毛莓 过江龙，猪尿泡（贵州）**

Rubus tephrodes Hance var. setosissimus Hand. -Mazz. Symb. Sin. 7: 492. 1933. ——R. farinaceus Card. in Lecomte, Not. Syst. 3: 303. 1914. syn. nov.

本变种植株全体均有长可达5毫米的紫褐色腺毛和刺毛。

产江西、湖南、广东、贵州。生山顶、山脚或山谷，海拔达1500米。
