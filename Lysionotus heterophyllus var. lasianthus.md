龙胜吊石苣苔",
6c.**Lysionotus heterophyllus var. lasianthus** W. T. Wang",

## Description
Stems apically puberulent. Petiole puberulent adaxially, glabrous abaxially; leaf blade papery, glabrous, margin denticulate to serrulate. Peduncle glabrous. Pedicel glabrous to sparsely puberulent near apex. Corolla outside sparsely puberulent. Fl. Jun-Jul.

> * Terrestrial or on trees in forests in stream valleys; 1100-1700 m. N Guangxi (Longsheng Xian, Xing'an Xian).
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Lysionotus pauciflorus &lt;/I&gt;Maximowicz var. &lt;I&gt;lasianthus &lt;/I&gt;W. T. Wang.
**6c. 龙胜吊石苣苔（变种）（广西植物）**

L. heterophyllus var. lasianthus W. T. Wang in Guihaia 3(4) : 267. 1983.

与异叶吊石苣苔的区别：花冠外面被短柔毛；退化雄蕊3枚。

产广西龙胜。生于山谷水边，海拔1070-1650米。
