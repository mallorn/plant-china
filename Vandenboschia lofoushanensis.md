## Description
Plants 15-18 cm tall. Rhizome dark brown, 1.5-2 mm in diam., densely covered with dark brown multicellular hairs, with sparse hairy rootlets. Stipes remote, 0.5-2 cm apart, light green, 5-10 cm × ca. 1 mm, broadly winged nearly to base, wings slightly crisped. Rachis and costae light green-brown, wings slightly crisped, glabrous. Lamina tripinnate, oblong-ovate, 8-10 × 4-5 cm, membranous, glabrous; pinnae 8-10 pairs, alternate or basal ones opposite, oblong-ovate to ovate-triangular, 2-3 × 1.5-2 cm, base subcordate to unequally cuneate, apex obtuse; pinnules 3-5 pairs, alternate, sessile, ovate to oblong, 8-10 × 4-6 mm, base cuneate, margin parted, apex obtuse; ultimate segments closely spaced, simple or forked, narrowly linear, 2-3 × ca. 0.8 mm, margin entire, apex rounded. Veins dichotomous, obviously raised on each surface, glabrous, with 1(or 2) veinlets. Sori on upper parts of frond, apical on short acroscopic segments; involucres narrowly tubular, ca. 1 mm, narrowly winged, truncate and not dilated at mouth; receptacles long projecting, ca. 3 mm, slightly bent.

> ● On rocks in forests. Guangdong.
  "Synonym": "*Crepidomanes lofoushanense* (Ching) K. Iwatsuki.
**7.罗浮山瓶蕨**

Vandenboschia lofoushanensis Ching, sp. nov.

植株高15-18厘米。根状茎长，横走，粗1.5-2毫米，黑褐色，密被黑褐色有光泽的多细胞的节状毛，下面疏生密被节状毛的纤维状的根。叶远生，相距0.5-2厘米；叶柄长5-10厘米，粗约1毫米，淡绿色，两侧有阔翅几达基一部，翅多少褶皱，连叶柄宽约2毫米；叶片长圆卵形，长8-10厘米，宽4-5厘米，三回羽状深裂；羽片8-10对，互生或基部的对生，有柄，稍斜向上，长圆卵形至卵状三角形，长2-3厘米，宽1.5-2厘米，相距1-1.5厘米，先端钝，基部近心脏形至阔斜楔形；小羽片3-5对，互生，无柄，几开展至斜向上，卵形至长圆形，长8-10毫米，宽4-6毫米，钝头，基部楔形，相距3-4毫米，深羽裂几达有翅的小羽轴；末回裂片狭线形，长2-3毫米，宽约0.8毫米，单一或分叉，斜向上，密接，圆头，全缘。叶脉叉状分枝，淡绿褐色。两面明显隆起，无毛，末回裂片有小脉1-（2）条，不达裂片先端。叶薄膜质，干后绿色或暗绿色，无毛；叶轴及羽轴淡绿褐色，均有多少褶皱的阔翅，光滑无毛。孢子囊群生在叶片上部向轴的短裂片顶端；囊苞狭管状，长约1毫米，两侧有狭翅，口部截形而不膨大；囊群托长，突出，纤细，长达3毫米，稍弯弓。

为广东（博罗县，罗浮山）特产。生于密林下岩石上。
