川鄂滇池海棠",
23b.**Malus yunnanensis var. veitchii** (Osborn) Rehder",

## Description
Leaf blade ovate, abaxially subglabrous, base usually cordate, lobes acute at apex.

> Guizhou, Hubei, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Xizang.
  "Synonym": "*Pyrus yunnanensis* var. *veitchii* Osborn, Gard. Chron., ser. 3, 78: 227. 1925.
