西宁披碱草",
4d.**Elymus dahuricus var. xiningensis** (L. B. Cai) S. L. Chen",

## Description
Culms more robust, 80–110 cm tall. Leaf sheath glabrous. Glumes scabrous along veins. First lemma ca. 8 mm; awn 2–5 mm. Fl. and fr. late summer.

> * Mountain slopes; ca. 2600 m. Qinghai.
  "Synonym": "*Elymus xiningensis* L. B. Cai, Acta Bot. Boreal.-Occid. Sin. 13: 71. 1993.
