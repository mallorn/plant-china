伏毛银露梅",
2c.**Potentilla glabra var. veitchii** (E. H. Wilson) Handel-Mazzetti",

## Description
Leaflets abaxially sparsely white sericeous or glabrescent, adaxially appressed white sericeous. Pedicels thick, densely white sericeous. Fl. and fr. Jul–Aug.

> Forest margins, alpine meadows, open places, rocky places; 2600--4100 m. Sichuan, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Potentilla veitchii* E. H. Wilson, Gard. Chron., ser. 3, 50: 102. 1911; *Dasiphora fruticosa* (Linnaeus) Rydberg var. *veitchii* (E. H. Wilson) Nakai; *Potentilla davurica* Nestler var. *veitchii* (E. H. Wilson) Jesson; *P. fruticosa* var. *veitchii* (E. H. Wilson) Bean.
**2c. 伏毛银露梅（变种）**

P. glabra Lodd. var. veitchii (Wils.) Hand.-Mazz. in Acta Hort. Gothob. 13: 298. 1939. ——P. veitchii Wils. in Gard. Chron. ser. 3. 50: 102. 1911. ——P. fruticosa L. var. veitchii Bean, Tress & Shrubs Brit. Isl. 2: 223. 1914. ——P. davurica Nestl. var. veitchiii Jesson in Curtis’s Bot. Mag. 141: t. 8637. 1915. ——Dasiphora fruticosa (L.) Rydb. var. veitchii (wils.) Nakai in Journ. Jap. Bot. 15: 601. 1939.

本变种与原变种区别在于，小叶上面伏生白色绢毛，下面疏被白色绢毛或脱落几无毛，花梗较粗，密被白色绢状柔毛。花果期7-8月。

产四川、云南。生高山草地、开旷地、岩石边及林缘，海拔2600-4100米。
