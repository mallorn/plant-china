## Description
Stems erect or branches diffuse basally, many branched or unbranched. Leaves alternate, lanceolate-oblong, pinnatipartite to pinnatisect. Inflorescences racemose or subcapitate. Pedicel short. Calyx deeply cleft anteriorly; lobes (3 or)5, equal, serrate. Corolla tube ca. 2 X as long as calyx; galea curved; beak short or obscure.
