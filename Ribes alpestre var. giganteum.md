大刺茶(藨)子",
8c.**Ribes alpestre var. giganteum** Janczewski",

## Description
Spines stout, to 3 cm. Calyx glabrous or subglabrous. Ovary and fruit glabrous, not stalked glandular.

> * Broad-leaved or coniferous forests and forest margins on shady slopes; 2500-3700 m. E Gansu, SE Ningxia, E Qinghai, E Shanxi, W Sichuan.
