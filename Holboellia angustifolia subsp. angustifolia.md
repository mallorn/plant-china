五月瓜藤(原亚种)",
5a.**Holboellia angustifolia subsp. angustifolia**",

## Description
Leaflets 5--7, ovate-elliptic, 4.5--13 cm, more than 2 × as long as wide, base cuneate, margin entire, apex acute. Fl. Apr--May, fr. Sep--Oct.

> Edge of forests, hillsides, by streams. Anhui, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hubei, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan [Bhutan, N India, Myanmar, Nepal, Sikkim].
