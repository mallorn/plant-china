柔叶隐棒花",
1d.**Cryptocoryne crispatula var. flaccidifolia** N. Jacobsen Aqua Pl. 16: 26. 1991.",

## Description
Leaves usually submerged, green to reddish brown, flaccid, 20-50 × 0.5-1.2 cm, somewhat undulate to almost smooth. Emergent leaves shorter and rather smooth compared to submerged ones. Spathe with a long tube (15-)20-30 cm; limb with a rather long, open spiral, usually grayish, mostly with shorter or longer purplish markings of varying intensities. Fl. Dec-Apr. 2*n* = 36.
