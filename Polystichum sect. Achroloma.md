## Description
Plants medium-sized, evergreen. Lamina thickly papery, 1-pinnate or bipinnatifid. Rachis without proliferous bulbils. Pinnae and/or pinnules without open hard spinules at apex or on margin, with cartilaginous margins. Sori in 1 row on each side of midrib, abaxial on veinlets.
