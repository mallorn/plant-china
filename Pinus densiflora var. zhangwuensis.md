彰武赤松",
7c.**Pinus densiflora var. zhangwuensis** S. J. Zhang et al.",

## Description
First-year branchlets not white powdery. Needles not or only slightly twisted, 13-15 cm, resin canals 3-9, margin usually inflexed. Seed cones pendulous when immature. Seeds 5-7 mm; wing ca. 1.5 cm.

> *  About 230 m. N Liaoning (N of Zhangwu)
