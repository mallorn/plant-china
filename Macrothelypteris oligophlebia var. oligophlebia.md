针毛蕨(原变种)",
4a.**Macrothelypteris oligophlebia var. oligophlebia**",

## Description
Pinnae glabrous on both surfaces.

> Streamsides in valleys, wet soil at forest margins; 400-800 m. S Anhui, N Guangxi, S Henan, Hubei, Hunan, S Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Zhejiang [Japan].
