## Description
Shrubs erect. Branches often with straight prickles and bristles. Leaflets 7–9, rarely 15–17, often small; stipules narrow, adnate to petiole, auriculate. Flower solitary, without bracts. Sepals persistent, erect, margin entire.
  "Statistics": "About 25 species: Asia, Europe; 18 species (12 endemic) in China.
