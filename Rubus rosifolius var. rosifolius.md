空心泡(原变种)",
64a.**Rubus rosifolius var. rosifolius**",

## Description
Branchlets, petioles, and pedicel ± prickly. Flowers single.

> Mixed forests, grassy slopes, roadsides, landslides. Low to medium elevations. Anhui, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan, Zhejiang [Cambodia, NE India, Indonesia, Japan, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, Philippines, Thailand, Vietnam; Africa, Australia].
  "Synonym": "*Rubus glandulosopunctatus* Hayata; *R. hirsutus* Thunberg var. *glabellus* (Focke) Wuzhi; *R. hopingensis* Y. C. Liu &amp; F. Y. Lu; *R. minusculus* H. Léveillé &amp; Vaniot; *R. parvirosifolius* Hayata; *R. tagallus* Chamisso &amp; Schlechtendal; *R. taiwanianus* Matsumura; *R. thunbergii* Siebold &amp; Zuccarini var. *glabellus* Focke.
