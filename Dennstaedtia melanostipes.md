乌柄碗蕨",
8.**Dennstaedtia melanostipes** Ching Fl. Reipubl. Popularis Sin. 2: 357. 1959.",

## Description
Plants more than 1 m tall. Stipes ebony or chestnut-brown, slightly shiny, 45-60 cm, 3-5 mm in diam., with brown articulate hairs, abscising when old, leaving scabrous marks; rachis abaxially and stalks concolorous, gradually discolored upward, sparsely hairy, scabrous; rachillae chestnut-brown, glabrous. Lamina dark green when dried, 3-pinnate, ovate-oblong in outline, 60-70 × 24-28 cm, papery, both surfaces glabrous, apex attenuate; pinnae 15-20 pairs, almost opposite, lower 1-3 pairs distant, 7-10 cm apart, closer upward, 4-5 cm apart, spreading obliquely upward, with stalk 5-10 mm and brown hairy, middle pinnae broadly lanceolate or oblong in outline, usually 16-20 × 6-8 cm, base symmetrical, apex acuminate; pinnules 14-18 pairs, alternate, 1-1.2 cm apart, shortly stalked, anadromous, flat, narrowly lanceolate, 3-4.5 × ca. 1 cm wide at base, gradually shorter upward, base asymmetrical, basiscopic base obliquely cuneate, acroscopic base auriculate, pinnatisect almost to costa, notched at wide interval, apex long and acuminate; ultimate pinnae elliptic or subovate, 1-3-lobed; lobules entire, sharp or obtuse at apex. Costa obvious, lateral veins pinnate and furcate, impressed adaxially, slightly prominent abaxially, each lobule with one veinlet, not reaching lobule margin, with fusiform hydathode at apex. Sori orbicular, terminal on lobule apex; indusium green, small bowl-shaped, thick, glabrous, entire.

> ● Yunnan.
**5.乌柄碗蕨**

Dennstaedtia melanostipes Ching, sp. nov.

植株高达1米以上。叶柄长45-60厘米，粗3-5毫米，乌木色或栗褐色，略有光泽，被棕色节状毛；老则脱落，粗糙有痕。叶片长60-70厘米，宽24-28厘米，卵状长圆形，先端渐狭，三回羽状；羽片15-20对，几为对生，柄长5-10毫米，被棕色毛，斜向上，一般长16-20厘米，宽6-8厘米，阔披针形或长圆形，先端渐尖，基部对称，下部1－3对相距甚远，约7-10厘米，向上为4-5厘米，二回羽状；一回小羽片14-18对，互生，有短柄，上先出，平展，狭披针形，长渐尖头，基部的长3-4.5厘米，宽1厘米左右，向上渐短，相距1.2厘米，基部不对称，下侧斜楔形，上侧耳形，羽状深裂几达小羽轴，有较竟缺刻分开；末回羽片椭圆形或近倒卵形，有1-3个浅裂；小裂片先端尖或钝，全缘。中脉明显，侧脉羽状分叉，上面凹陷，下面略隆起，每小裂片有小脉一条，不达裂片边缘，先端有纺锤形水囊。叶纸盾，干后暗缘色，两面光滑无毛；叶轴下部和叶柄同色，向上渐变淡，粗糙，疏被毛，羽轴光滑无毛，栗色。抱子囊群圆形，位于小裂片顶端；囊群盖小碗形，质厚，绿色，口边全缘，无毛。

特产于云南东南屏边县（大围山）。

本种近于碗蕨D．scabra（Wall.）Moore，但有许多不同之点，为一突出的种。
