长毛韩信草",
33d.**Scutellaria indica var. elliptica** Sun ex C. H. Hu",

## Description
Stems 8-16 cm tall, densely white pilose. Leaves  elliptic  to elliptic-ovate, 1-2.5 &amp;#215; 0.8-1.8 cm, base rounded  to   truncate, apex acute, densely white pilose. Racemes   4-8(-12)  cm.  Corolla  1.4-1.8  cm.

> * Slopes, roadsides, grasslands; 0-900 m. Anhui, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Zhejiang.
**33d. 长毛变种**

var. elliptica Sun ex C. H. Hu in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 11(1): 40. 1966.

这一变种与原变种不同在于茎、叶柄、叶两面密被白色平展具节疏柔毛。

产浙江，安徽，江西，福建，湖北，湖南，广东，广西，贵州及四川；生于海拔900米以下地方的山坡、路旁及草地上。模式标本采自贵州桐梓。
