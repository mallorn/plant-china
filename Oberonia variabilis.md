## Description
Stem very short or inconspicuous. Leaves subbasal, 3-5, distichous-equitant, laterally compressed, linear, straight or slightly falcate, 2.5-7 × 0.4-1 cm, veins obscure, base not articulate, margin often crisped when dried, apex acuminate or acute. Peduncle subterete, 6-10 cm, wingless, lower part often with many whorled sterile bracts; sterile bracts lanceolate, 1.5-2 mm, aristate; rachis 4-7 cm, 2-2.5 mm in diam., many flowered; floral bracts narrowly lanceolate, 1.5-2 mm, apex long acuminate. Flowers ± whorled, green; pedicel and ovary ca. 1 mm, conspicuously shorter than floral bracts. Sepals ovate, ca. 0.8 × 0.5 mm, apex obtuse. Petals suboblong, ca. 1 × 0.4 mm; lip broadly oblong-ovate in outline, ca. 0.8 mm, 3-lobed; lateral lobes subovate-oblong, ca. 0.2 mm, conspicuously smaller than mid-lobe, margin inconspicuously erose or nearly entire; mid-lobe subquadrate or broadly oblong, apex irregularly incised. Column erect, short, stout. Capsule obovoid-ellipsoid, 1.5-2.5 × 1.2-1.8 mm; fruiting pedicel ca. 1 mm. Fl. and fr. Jan-Apr.

**2.密苞鸢尾兰（植物分类学报）**

Oberonia variabilis Kerr in Kew Bull. 214. 1927; Seidenf. in Dansk Bot. Ark. 25(3): 114. fig. 81. 1968, et 33 (1): 39. fig. 32. 1978; F. Maekawa, Wild Orch. Jap. Col. 291. t. 108. 1971; S. C. Chen in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 20 (2): 190. 1982; Averyanov, Iden. Guide Vietnam. Orch. 156. 1994.

茎很短，不明显。叶近基生，3-5枚，二列套叠，两侧压扁，线形，伸直或稍镰曲，长2.5-7厘米，宽4-9.5毫米，先端渐尖或急尖，干后边缘常呈皱波状，下部内侧有干膜质边，脉略可见，基部无关节。花葶从叶簇中央抽出，近圆柱形，长6-10厘米，无翅，下部常有数十枚不育苞片；不育苞片常排成多轮，披针形，长1.5-2毫米，先端多少芒状；总状花序长4-7厘米，粗2-2.5毫米，具数十朵或更多的花；花苞片狭披针形，长1.5-2毫米，先端长渐尖；花梗和子房长约1毫米，明显短于花苞片；花多少排列成轮生状，绿色；萼片卵形，长约0.8毫米，宽约0.5毫米，先端钝；花瓣近长圆形，与萼片近等长，宽约0.4毫米；唇瓣轮廓为宽长圆状卵形，长约0.8毫米，3裂；侧裂片位于唇瓣基部两侧，近卵状长圆形，长约0.2毫米，明显小于中裂片，边缘略呈不明显的啮蚀状或近全缘；中裂片近方形或宽长圆形，先端有不规则的裂缺；蕊柱粗短，直立。蒴果倒卵状椭圆形，长1.5-2.5毫米，宽1.2-1.8毫米；果梗长约1毫米。花果期1-4月。

产海南（陵水）。生于树上。越南和泰国也有分布。模式标本采自泰国。
