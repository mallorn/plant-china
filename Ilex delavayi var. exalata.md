## Description
Branchlets glabrous, not verruculose, shallowly plicate-sulcate. Leaf blade ovate-oblong or oblong-elliptic, 3-8 × 1.5-2.5 cm, midvein, lateral veins, and reticulate veins deeply impressed adaxially. Female flower: pedicel 4-6 mm, glabrous. Fl. Jun, fr. Sep-Nov.

> Mixed woods, azalea forests, shrub forests; 2500-3600 m. W Sichuan, W Yunnan [N Myanmar].
