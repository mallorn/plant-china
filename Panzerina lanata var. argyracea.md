银白变种",
2d.**Panzerina lanata var. argyracea** (Kuprianova) H. W. Li",

## Description
Leaves palmatipartite for 1/2-2/3 their length; lobes broadly cuneate, 4-10 mm wide. Calyx teeth short subulate-spinescent, anterior teeth 3.5-4 mm. Corolla 3-3.5 cm.

> Nei Mongol [Mongolia, Russia].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Panzeria argyracea&lt;/I&gt; Kuprianova, Bot. Mater. Gerb. Bot. Inst. Komarova Akad. Nauk SSSR 15: 364. 1953.
