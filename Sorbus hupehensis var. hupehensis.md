湖北花楸(原变种)
11a.**Sorbus hupehensis var. hupehensis**

## Description
Leaflets 4–8 pairs, blades oblong-lanceolate or ovate-lanceolate, 3–5 × 1–1.8 cm.


> Shaded slopes, dense forests in gullies, thickets; 300--3800 m. Anhui, Gansu, Guizhou, Hubei, Jiangxi, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shandong, Sichuan, Yunnan.

  "Synonym": "*Pyrus hupehensis* (C. K. Schneider) Bean (1933), not Pampinini (1910); *P. mesogea* Cardot; *Sorbus hupehensis* var. *laxiflora* (Koehne) C. K. Schneider; *S. hupehensis* var. *obtusa* C. K. Schneider; *S. hupehensis* var. *syncarpa* Koehne; *S. laxiflora* Koehne.
