女贞叶忍冬(原变种)",
12a.**Lonicera ligustrina var. ligustrina**",

## Description
Leaves (0.5-)1-4(-8) cm, adaxially densely stiffly hairy on midvein, midvein slightly impressed or flat adaxially, apex usually acuminate or mucronate. Corolla 7.5-12 mm. Berry purple or red.
  "Statistics": "Scrub, evergreen broad-leaved forests; (600-)1000-2000(-3000) m. NW Guangxi, Guizhou, SW Hubei, NW Hunan, Sichuan, Yunnan [Bhutan, E India, Nepal].
  "Synonym": "*Caprifolium ligustrinum* (Wallich) Kuntze; *Lonicera buxifolia* H. Léveillé; *L. missionis* H. Léveillé; *L. virgultorum* W. W. Smith; *Xylosteon ligustrinum* (Wallich) D. Don.
