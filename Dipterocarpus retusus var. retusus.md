东京龙脑香(原变种)",
1a.**Dipterocarpus retusus var. retusus**",

## Description
Parts glabrescent.

> Humid valley rain forests, dense forests on limestone; below 1000 m. SE Xizang, SE and W Yunnan [India, Indonesia, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam].

Chevalier) Y. K. Yang &amp; J. K. Wu; *D. retusus* var. *yingjiangensis* Y. K. Yang &amp; J. K. Wu; *D. spanoghei* Blume; *D. tonkinensis* A. Chevalier; *D. trinervis* Blume.
