香润楠(原变种)",
28a.**Machilus zuihoensis var. zuihoensis**",

## Description
Medium-sized trees. Petiole ca. 1.5 cm; leaf blade yellowish green to green when alive, 7-14 × 2.5-3.5 cm, base almost cuneate. Fruit 7-8 mm in diam.
  "Statistics": "● Mixed forests, common; 100-1400 m. Taiwan.
  "Synonym": "*Machilus longipaniculata* Hayata; *M. longisepala* Hayata; *M. zuihoensis* f. *longipaniculata* (Hayata) T. S. Liu &amp; J. C. Liao; *Persea zuihoensis* (Hayata) H. L. Li.
