硬杆地杨梅",
8b.**Luzula multiflora subsp. frigida** (Buchenau) V. I. Kreczetowicz",

## Description
Inner perianth segments ovate to elliptic, margin denticulate, apex mucronulate. Capsule dark brown to blackish brown, broadly ellipsoid to ovoid. 2&lt;I&gt; n&lt;/I&gt; = 36.

> Mountain valleys, grasslands in high mountains; 1900--3000 m. Gansu, Shaanxi, Xinjiang [Mongolia; Europe].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt; Luzula campestris&lt;/I&gt; (Linnaeus) de Candolle var. &lt;I&gt; frigida&lt;/I&gt; Buchenau, Oesterr. Bot. Z. 48: 284. 1898; &lt;I&gt; L. frigida&lt;/I&gt; (Buchenau) Samuelsson ex Lindman.
**8b.硬秆地杨梅（秦岭植物志）（亚种）图版59：10-11**

Luzula multiflora subsp. frigida (Buchen.) V. Krecz., Bot. Zur. 12: 490. 1928; S. Snogerup in Tutin, Fl. Europ. 5: 112. 1980.——L. campestris (Linn. ) DC. var. frigida Buchen. in Osterr. Bot. Zeitschr. 48: 284. 1898 et in Engl. Pflanzenr. 25 (IV. 36) : 93. fig. 55. 1906; 秦岭植物志1 (1) : 300. 图271. 1976. ——L. frigida (Buchen.) G. Sam. ex Lindm. Sv. Fanerogarnfl. 161. 1918.

与原亚种的区别在于：蒴果爿卵形至椭圆形，暗褐色至黑褐色；内轮花被片顶端中央具小尖头，两侧常有小裂齿；茎较粗壮；花序梗粗短。染色体：2n=36。

产陕西、甘肃、新疆。生于海拔1 900-3 000米的高山草地、山谷坡地上。欧洲、俄罗斯西伯利亚也有分布。
