## Description
Evergreen shrubs. Branchlets, petioles, fruiting pedicels, and peduncles densely gray-yellow pubescent. Leaf blade sparsely pubescent and along veins villous abaxially, along veins gray-yellow pubescent adaxially, apex caudate-acuminate. Fruiting peduncle ca. 2 mm.
