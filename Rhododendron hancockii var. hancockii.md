滇南杜鹃(原变种)",
482a.**Rhododendron hancockii var. hancockii**",

## Description
Petiole glabrous; leaf blade 8–13 × 2.5–5 cm. Pedicel yellowish brown pubescent; calyx lobes triangular-ovate to linear-lanceolate, to 9 mm. Fl. Apr–Jun, fr. Jul–Dec.

> Mixed forests, thickets on mountain slopes; 1100–2000 m. C, N, and W Guangxi, C and SE Yunnan.
