宽瓣绣球绣线菊",
39b.**Spiraea blumei var. latipetala** Hemsley",

## Description
Leaf blades rhombic-ovate, to 3.5 mm. Pedicels and hypanthium abaxially puberulous. Petals 4–5 mm. Carpels and follicles glabrous.

> Stream sides, roadsides; low elevations.  Anhui, Guangdong, Zhejiang.
**绣球绣线菊宽瓣变种**

S. blumei G. Don var. latipetala Hemsl in Journ. Linn. Soc. Bot. 23: 224. 1887.

本变种花梗及萼筒具细短柔毛，花瓣宽大。

产浙江、广东。模式标本采自浙江宁波。
