北乌头(原变种)",
136a.**Aconitum kusnezoffii var. kusnezoffii**",

## Description
Leaf segments narrow, subpinnately divided.

> Grassy slopes, forests; 2000--2400 m. Hebei, Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Shanxi [Korea, Russia (Far East, Siberia)].
