单序草(原变种)",
1a.**Polytrias indica var. indica**",

> This grass has been widely known as *Polytrias amaura*, based on *Andropogon amaurus*, which is a superfluous name for *A. diversiflorus*.

## Description
Spikelets 3 per node; lower glume long villous, hairs extending beyond glume apex; upper lemma cordate.

> Grassy spaces. Hong Kong [Indonesia, Malaysia, Myanmar, New Guinea, Philippines, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Phleum indicum* Houttuyn, Nat. Hist.13: 198. 1782; *Andro-pogon amaurus* Buse, nom. illeg. superfl.; *A. diversiflorus* Steudel; *A. firmandus* Steudel; *Eulalia praemorsa* (Nees ex Steudel) Stapf ex Ridley; *Pollinia praemorsa* Nees ex Steudel; *Polytrias amaura* Kuntze, nom. illeg. superfl.; *P. diversiflora* (Steudel) Nash; *P. praemorsa* (Nees ex Steudel) Hackel.
