## Description
Leaf blade 3-partite near base, sometimes 3-lobed, 6--13 × 5--9 mm. Flowers 1.3--1.6 cm in diam. Petals 6--9 × 3.6--6.5 mm, nectary pit without a scale.

> * Gravelly slopes; 4900--5400 m. SW Xizang (Burang Xian).
