## Description
Stems, leaves, petiole, and calyx with scattered hairs. Petiole 1-4.5 cm; leaf blade 6-13 &amp;#215; 4-7.5 cm; glabrescent abaxially. Flowers white.

> * Forests, streamsides; 600-2500 m. Guangdong, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Lycianthes lysimachioides&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;formosana&lt;/I&gt; Bitter.
**9b.中华红丝线（变种）**

var. sinensis Bitter in Abh. Naturw. Ver. Bremen 25: 493. 1919. ——Lycianthes debilissima(Merr.)Chun, comb. nov. in sched.

叶较单花红丝线稍大，茎、叶、花柄及萼外面具有较单花红丝线明显而分散的毛，叶背面几近于光滑；叶柄较长，约1-3厘米；花白色。

产四川、湖北、湖南、江西、广东。生于林下，溪边潮湿地区，海拔635-2000米。
