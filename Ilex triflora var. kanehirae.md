## Description
Shrubs. Leaf blade oblong, obovate, or oblong-elliptic, apex rounded, obtuse, or rarely acute, never acuminate.

> ● Forests, forest margins, shrub forests in valleys; 200-1100 m. Fujian, Guangdong, Hunan, Jiangxi, Taiwan, Zhejiang.

Yamazaki; *I. mutchagara* Makino var. *kanehirae* (Yamamoto) Masamune.
