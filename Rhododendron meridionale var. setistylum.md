> Judging from the types of both names, var. *setistylum* may not be distinct from var. *meridionale*.

## Description
Blade of spring leaves 5–7.5 × to 1.3 cm, margin not conspicuously revolute, lateral veins prominent abaxially. Style coarsely brown setose-strigose at base. Fl. Mar–Apr, fr. Jul–Aug.

> Open forests, ravines; 500–700 m. S Guangxi.
**302b. 糙柱杜鹃（变种）（广西植物）**

Rhododendron meridionale Tam var. setistylum Tam. Survey Rhodod. South China 50 et 103. 1983. ——R. meridionale Tam. var. setistylum Tam in Guihaia 2(3): 72. 1982.

本变种与原变种不同在于花柱基部或下面1/4处被棕褐色刚毛状糙伏毛。花期3-4月，果期7-8月。

产广西南部。生于海拔500-630米溪边疏林下。模式标本采自广西防城（十万大山伕勤乡龙桂）。
