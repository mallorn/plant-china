## Description
Fronds less than 80 cm, with hairs on rachis and costae abaxially thick and long. Pinnae linear-lanceolate; pinna segments oblong and serrulate at margin.

> ● Coniferous forests on mountain slopes; 3300-3800 m. Sichuan, Xizang.
  "Synonym": "Basionym: *Lunathyrium wilsonii* var. *muliense* Z. R. Wang, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 32: 92. 1994.
**8e. 木里蛾眉蕨（变种）（横断山区维管植物）**

var. muliense Z. R. Wang in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 32 (1): 92. 1994 et in W. T. Wang, Vasc. Pl. Hengduan Mount. 1: 75. 1993, nom. seminud.

本变种与原变种不同在于叶轴及羽轴下面毛较粗长，裂片边缘有锯齿。

分布于四川（木里、盐源）和西藏（察隅）。生山坡针叶林下，海拔3 300-3 800米。模式标本采自四川（木里）。
