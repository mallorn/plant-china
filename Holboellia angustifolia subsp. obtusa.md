钝叶五风藤",
5d.**Holboellia angustifolia subsp. obtusa** (Gagnepain) H. N. Qin",

## Description
Leaflets oblong, (3--)5--7, thinly leathery, base cuneate, margin entire, apex obtuse. Fl. Apr--May, fr. Sep--Oct.

> * W and SW Sichuan, E Xizang, N Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Holboellia latifolia&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;obtusa &lt;/I&gt; Gagnepain, Bull. Mus. Hist. Nat. 14: 68. 1908.
