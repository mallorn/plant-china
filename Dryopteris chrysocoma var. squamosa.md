## Description
Stipe and rachis densely clothed with brown or dark brown scales, former with scales oblong-lanceolate.

> ● Evergreen forests; 2600-3000 m. W Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Dryopteris cochleata* (Buchanan-Hamilton ex D. Don) C. Christensen var. *squamosa* C. Christensen, Acta Horti Gothob. 1: 59. 1924.
**31b.密鳞金冠鳞毛蕨（变种）**

Dryopteris chrysocoma (Christ) C. Chr. var. squuantosa (C. Chr.) Ching in Bull. Fan Mem. Inst. Biol. Bot. 8: 438. 1938; 横断山区维管植物上册128. 1993. ——D. cochleata (Don) C. Chr. var. squamosa C. Chr. in Acta Hort. Gothob. 1: 59. 1924

本变种外形与原变种相似，但叶柄和叶轴密被鳞片，叶轴鳞片棕色，披针形，叶柄鳞片深棕色至褐色，长圆披针形，可以区别。

产云南（鹤庆、宾川、大理）。生常绿林下，海拔2600-3000米。
