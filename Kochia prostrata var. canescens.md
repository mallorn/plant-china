灰毛木地肤",
1b.**Kochia prostrata var. canescens** Moquin-Tandon",

## Description
Annual branches densely gray-white pilose. Leaves densely appressed sericeous.

> * Slopes, sandy places. W Gansu, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Xinjiang.
**灰毛木地肤（变种）图版21：12-13**

Kochia prostrata (L.) Schrad. var. canescens Moq. Chenop. Monogr. Enum. 93. 1840 et in DC. Prodr. 13 (2): 132. 1849.

当年生枝有密的灰白色柔毛；叶密生贴伏的绢毛。

产内蒙古、宁夏、甘肃西部、新疆。生于山坡及沙地。
