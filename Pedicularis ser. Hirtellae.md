> One species.

## Description
Herbs reddish or reddish brown hirtellous, drying black. Stems single, unbranched. Basal leaves persistent; stem leaves alternate, short petiolate, less than 12 cm, pinnatifid. Inflorescences spicate. Corolla tube straight, erect; galea pubescent abaxially, 2-toothed at apex; lower lip not spreading.
