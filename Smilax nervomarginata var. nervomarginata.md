缘脉菝葜 (原变种)",
49a.**Smilax nervomarginata var. nervomarginata**",

## Description
Branches minutely verruculose.

> Forests, forest margins, thickets; near sea level to 1000 m. Anhui, Guizhou, Hunan, Jiangxi, Zhejiang [Japan (Ryukyu Islands)].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt; Smilax sempervirens&lt;/I&gt; F. T. Wang.
