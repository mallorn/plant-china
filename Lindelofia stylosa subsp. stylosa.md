长柱琉璃草 (原变种)",
1a.**Lindelofia stylosa subsp. stylosa**",

## Description
Nutlet margin with small glochids.

> Meadows, forests, canyons; 1200-2800 m. C and W Gansu, Xinjiang, NW Xizang [Afghanistan, N India, Kashmir, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Mongolia, Pakistan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Cynoglossum stylosum&lt;/I&gt; Karelin &amp; Kirilov, Bull. Imp. Soc. Naturalistes Moscou 15: 409. 1842; &lt;I&gt;Lindelofia bentha-mii&lt;/I&gt; J. D. Hooker; &lt;I&gt;Solenanthus nigricans&lt;/I&gt; Schrenk ex Fischer &amp; C. A. Meyer.
