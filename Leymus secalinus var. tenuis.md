## Description
Culms 40–100 cm tall, 3–5-noded. Leaf blade adaxial surface scabrous or smooth. Spikelets 1 or 2 per node, with 2 or 3 florets. Glumes nearly subulate. Palea equaling lemma. Anthers 2–3 mm.

> * Lake banks; ca. 4200 m. Xizang (Gyirong, Rutog, Zanda, Zhongba).
