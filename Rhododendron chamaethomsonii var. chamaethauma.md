## Description
Calyx less than 1 mm; corolla whitish to deep pink; ovary densely brownish hairy.

> *Rhododendron* thickets, damp stony alpine slopes; 4200–4400 m. SE Xizang, NW Yunnan.
**228c. 短萼云雾杜鹃（变种）（西藏植物志）图版105：5-8**

Rhododedron chamaethomsonii (Tagg et Forrest) Cowan et Davidian var. chamaethauma (Tagg) Cowan et Davidian in Rhodod. Year-book 6: 71. 1951; Cullen et Chamb. in Not. Bot. Garb. Edinb. 37: 332. 1979; Chamb. in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 39:407. 1982;西藏植物志3: 585. 1986. ——R. repens Balf. f. et Forrest var. chamaethauma Tagg in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 16: 206. 1931.

本变种与原变种的区别在于花冠苍白色至深淡红色；花萼小，长1毫米或更短；子房密被淡黄色毛。

产云南西北部和西藏东南部。生于海拔4 200-4 350米的石坡杜鹃灌丛和高山沼泽地中。模式标本采自西藏米林。
