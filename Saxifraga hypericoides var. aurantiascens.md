橙瓣虎耳草",
56c.**Saxifraga hypericoides var. aurantiascens** (Engler & Irmscher) J. T. Pan & Gornall",

## Description
Flower solitary. Sepals erose or entire at apex. Petals orange, base cordate, contracted into a claw. Styles 1.5-2 mm. Fl. and fr. Aug-Sep.

> * Alpine meadows; 3200-4600 m. Sichuan, Yunnan.
