齿叶扁核木",
4b.**Prinsepia uniflora var. serrata** Rehder",

## Description
Leaf blade ovate-lanceolate to ovate-oblong on sterile shoots, oblong to narrowly elliptic on flowering shoots, margin conspicuously serrate. Pedicel 5–15 mm.

> Slopes, ravines in valleys; 800--2200 m. Gansu, Ningxia, E Qinghai, S Shaanxi, S Shanxi, W Sichuan.
**4b. 齿叶扁核木（变种） 图版1: 3**

Prinsepia uniflora Batal. var. serrata Rehd. in Journ. Arn. Arb. 22: 575. 1941——Prinsepia uniflora Farrer in Journ. Roy Hort. Soc. 42: 103. 1916. non Batal. 1892.

本变种叶片边缘有明显锯齿，不育枝上叶片卵状披针形或卵状长圆形，先端急尖或短渐尖；花枝上叶片长圆形或窄椭圆形；花梗长5-15毫米。

产山西、陕西、甘肃、青海、四川。生于山坡、山谷以及沟边黄土丘陵地，海拔800-2000米。
