对叶兰(原变种)",
14a.**Neottia puberula var. puberula**",

## Description
Leaves uniformly green. Apical lobes of lip straight.
  "Statistics": "Damp places in dense forests; 1400-2600 m. C Gansu, Guizhou, NW Hebei, Heilongjiang, S Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Qinghai, N Shanxi, NW Sichuan [Japan, Korea, Russia (Far East)].
  "Synonym": "*Listera puberula* Maximowicz, Bull. Acad. Imp. Sci. Saint-Pétersbourg 29: 204. 1884; *L. bungeana* Yabe; *L. major* Nakai; *L. savatieri* Maximowicz ex Komarov; *L. yatabei* Makino.
