15a.**Lonicera fragrantissima var. fragrantissima**",

## Description
Shrubs, deciduous. Leaves ovate to lanceolate, 3-8.5 × 1-4.5 cm, acute to acuminate.
  "Statistics": "● Scrub on mountain slopes; 100-2700 m. Anhui, Gansu, Guizhou, Hebei, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi, Sichuan, Zhejiang.
  "Synonym": "*Caprifolium fragrantissimum* (Lindley &amp; Paxton) Kuntze; *Lonicera fragrantissima* subsp. *standishii* (Carrière) P. S. Hsu &amp; H. J. Wang; *L. mamillaris* Rehder; *L. phyllocarpa* Maximowicz; *L. proterantha* Rehder; *L. pseudoproterantha* Pampanini; *L. pseudoproterantha* var. *intermedia* Pampanini; *L. pseudoproterantha* var. *longifolia* Pampanini; *L. standishii* Carrière; *L. standishii* var. *monbeigii* W. W. Smith.
