## Description
Leaves glabrescent adaxially, pilose abaxially. Inflorescence compact, erect. Corolla tube white villous in upper part inside; lobes pubescent outside. Fl. Apr.
  "Statistics": "● Thickets on mountains. Guangxi (Shangsi).
**19c. 毛粗叶水锦树（变种）（植物分类学报）**

var. pilifera How ex W. C. Chen in Acta Phytotax. Sinica 21 (4): 395, fig. 6. 1983.

本变种与原变种不同的是叶下面被疏柔毛，花冠管内面上部被白色长柔毛。花冠裂片外面有柔毛。花期4月。

产于广西上思；生于山地灌丛中。模式标本采自广西上思县十万大山。
