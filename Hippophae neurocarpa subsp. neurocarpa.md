肋果沙棘(原亚种)
6a.**Hippophae neurocarpa subsp. neurocarpa**

## Description
New shoots nearly white, with scales only. Leaf blade margin revolute, abaxially with scales only. Fruit brown, 6-8(-9) mm.


> ● Valley bottoms, flood plains; 3400-4000 m. Qinghai, Sichuan, Xizang.

  "Synonym": "*Hippophae rhamnoides* Linnaeus subsp. *neurocarpa* (S. W. Liu &amp; T. N. He) Hyvönen.
