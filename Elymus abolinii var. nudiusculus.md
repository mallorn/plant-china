## Description
Spikelets 15–20 mm, with 4 or 5 florets. Glumes linear-lanceolate, 6–9-veined. Lemma awn 3–8 mm. Palea apex truncate.

> * River banks; 1500–2000 m. Xinjiang.
  "Synonym": "*Roegneria nudiuscula* L. B. Cai, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 35: 171. 1997.
