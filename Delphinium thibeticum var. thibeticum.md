澜沧翠雀花(原变种)",
143a.**Delphinium thibeticum var. thibeticum**",

## Description
Leaf blade central lobe segments distally 1 or 2 × dissected; ultimate lobules few, narrowly ovate to lanceolate-linear. Raceme rachis retrorsely puberulent.

> * Open woods, grassy slopes; 2800--3800 m. SW Sichuan, NW Yunnan, E Xizang.
