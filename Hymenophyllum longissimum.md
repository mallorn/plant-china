## Description
Plants 10-30 cm tall. Rhizome dark brown, with sparse short multicellular hairs, with sparse rootlets. Stipes remote, 1-2 cm apart, brown, filiform, 2-10 cm, with brown multicellular hairs at base, glabrous upward, wingless, or only with decurrent wings at apex. Rachis and costae brown, glabrous, slightly zigzag, broadly winged throughout, wings flat, ca. 1 mm on each side. Lamina bipinnate to tripinnatifid, narrowly linear, 6-15 × 1-1.5 cm, membranous and semitransparent, glabrous; pinnae 10-16 pairs, closely spaced or slightly imbricate to neighboring ones, alternate, sessile, oblong-ovate, 6-10 × 4-5 mm, base decurrent, apex obtuse; ultimate segments simple or usually forked, closely spaced or imbricate to neighboring ones, flat, oblong, 1-3 × 1-1.2 mm, with a single veinlet, margin entire, apex obtuse or sometimes subtruncate. Veins dichotomous, brown, glabrous. Sori on upper parts of frond, 1 per pinna, apical on short acroscopic segments; involucres oval, ca. 1.5 mm, entire, deeply cleft to base and obtuse at apex.

> ● On wet rocks or on tree trunks in forests; 1800-3700 m. Hubei, Sichuan, Xizang, NW Yunnan.

**8．长叶蕗蕨**

Mecodium longissimum Ching et Chiu, sp. nov.

植株下垂，长30厘米以上。根状茎纤细，丝状，长而横走，褐色，疏被短毛或几光滑，下面疏生纤维状的根。叶远生，相距2-3厘米；叶柄长达10厘米，纤细如丝，褐色，光滑，无翅；叶片线形至线状披针形，长达30厘米，宽约4厘米，两端均稍狭，三回羽裂；羽片很多，常达30对以上，互生，有短柄，极斜向上，狭线形，长3-10厘米，宽6-8毫米，密接而重叠；小羽片6-20对，互生，无柄，斜向上，长圆形，长4-6毫米，宽2-3毫米，先端钝，基部下侧下延，密接；末回裂片2-6个，互生，极斜向上，长圆形，长2-3毫米，宽约1毫米，圆头，全缘或先端有浅缺刻，单一或分叉，通常互相重叠。叶脉叉状分枝，两面明显隆起，褐色，光滑无毛，末回裂片有小脉1条。叶为薄膜质，干后褐色，无毛，叶片平直或稍呈褶皱。叶轴深褐色，无毛，下部有极狭的易脱落的翅，上部有稍呈褶皱的阔翅；羽轴全部有稍呈褶皱的阔翅。孢子囊羣极多数，位于叶片上部，通常着生于裂片腋间的短裂片顶端；囊苞卵形至长圆形，高大于宽，长约1.5毫米，圆头，全缘，唇瓣深裂达到基部。

本种为云南西北部（德钦、维西、大理）特产。生长在山地密林下阴湿的岩石上，海拔3000-3700米。
