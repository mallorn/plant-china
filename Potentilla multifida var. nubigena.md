矮生多裂委陵菜",
33c.**Potentilla multifida var. nubigena** Th. Wolf",

## Description
Flowering stems spreading near ground, 3–8 cm tall. Radical leaves 2.5–4 cm including petiole; leaf blade with (2 or)3 pairs of leaflets; segments ligulate fasciated, abaxially densely tomentose and sericeous-villous, adaxially densely appressed villous. Inflorescence few flowered. Fl. and fr. May–Jul.

> Mountain meadows, alpine valleys; 1300--5000 m. Gansu, Hebei, Nei Mongol, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Xinjiang, Xizang [C and SW Asia].
  "Synonym": "*Potentilla multifida* var. *minor* Ledebour.
**30c.矮生多裂委陵菜（变种）**

P. multifiola L. var. nubigena Wolf in Bibl. Bot. 71: 155. 1908. ——P. multifida L. a. minor Ldb. Fl. Ross. 2: 43. 1844.

植株极为矮小，花茎接近地面铺散，长3-8厘米，花朵较少，基生叶有小叶（2-）3对，连叶柄长2.5-4厘米，小叶裂片呈舌状带形，上面密被伏生柔毛，下面密被绒毛及长绢毛。花果期5-7月。

产内蒙古、河北、陕西、甘肃、青海、新疆、西藏。生高山河谷阶地、山坡草地，海拔1300-5000米。伊朗、苏联中亚地区及阿尔泰高山地区也有分布。
