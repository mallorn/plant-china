灰褐亮鳞杜鹃",
71c.**Rhododendron heliolepis var. fumidum** (I. B. Balfour & W. W. Smith) R. C. Fang",

## Description
Abaxial leaf surface brown when dry; adaxial surface deep brown. Corolla purplish red, with reddish brown spots inside; style glabrous.

> *Rhododendron* thickets, thickets on slopes, wet valleys; 3200–3500 m. NE Yunnan.
**68c. 灰褐亮鳞杜鹃（变种）（云南植物研究）**

Rhododendron heliolepis Franch. var. fumidum (Balf. f. et W. W. Smith) R. C. Fang in Act. Bot. Yunn. 4: 253. 1982. ——R. fumidum Balf. f. et W. W. Smith in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 10: 112. 1917: Hutch. in Stevenson. Spec. Rhodod. 323. 1930.

本变种的特征是花柱无毛，花冠紫红色，内有红褐色斑点，叶片干后上面暗褐色，下面褐色。

产云南东北（禄劝、巧家等地）。生于海拔3 200-3 500米的山坡灌丛、高山杜鹃林或山谷湿润地。模式标本采于巧家野马川（Ye-ma-tchouen，在荞麦地与药山附近）。
