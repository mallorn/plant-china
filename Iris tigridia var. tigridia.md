粗根鸢尾 (原变种)",
54a.**Iris tigridia var. tigridia**",

## Description
Leaves 5--13 cm × 1.5--2 mm at anthesis, ca. 30 cm × 3 mm in fruit. Flowering stems not or barely emerging above ground. Flowers 3.5--3.8 cm in diam. 2&lt;I&gt; n&lt;/I&gt; = 38.

> Dry hillsides, sandy grasslands, dunes. Gansu, Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Qinghai, Shanxi, Sichuan [Kazakhstan, Mongolia, Russia].
