## Description
Plants medium-sized to large, evergreen. Lamina green, broadly lanceolate, papery or thickly papery. Stipe greenish when dry. Rachis scales linear or lanceolate to ovate-lanceolate. Pinnules 10-30 pairs, stalked, pinnules of upper pinnae exauriculate or nearly so. Microscales long-type or short-type. Vein with or without hydathodes at end.Four species: Sino-Himalayan region; four species (two endemic) in China.
