多沟楼梯草",
98.**Elatostema multicanaliculatum** B. L. Shih & Yuen P. Yang",

## Description
Herbs perennial, dioecious, up to 80 cm. Stems ascending or repent, branched, glabrous. Leaves alternate; nanophyll absent; stipules lanceolate, 5-7 × 2 mm, glabrous; petiole 0-1 mm; leaf blade obliquely elliptic to lanceolate-oblong, 5-14.5 × 2.5-5.5 cm, membranous, major basal lateral veins both arising above base of leaf blade, rarely absent and venation pinnate, nearly glabrous, cystoliths conspicuous, dense; base with broader half cordate, margin serrulate to coarsely serrate, apex acuminate. Female inflorescences solitary, ± 4-sided, ca. 12 × 8 mm, sessile or subsessile; receptacle ca. 10 × 2.5-6 mm; bracts connate into a narrowly oblong or lanceolate involucre; bracteoles narrowly lanceolate. Achenes ovoid, 5-6-ribbed. Fl. Sep-Nov.

> * Moist ravines; at medium elevations. Taiwan (Taoyuan).
