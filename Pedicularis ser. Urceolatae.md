坛萼系
75.**Pedicularis ser. Urceolatae** P. C. Tsoong ex H. B. Yang

> Two species: Bhutan, China; one species in China.


## Description
Herbs low, &amp;#177; woody. Stems short, erect. Leaves opposite, pinnatifid to pinnatipartite; segments few. Calyx urceolate-ovate. Corolla rose-red, with yellow at tube apex and galea base; tube 3--4 X as long as calyx; beak slightly recurved, slender; middle lobe of lower lip slightly hoodlike at apex.
