## Description
Branchlets, petiole, pedicel, and abaxial surface of calyx villous.

> Slopes, montane valleys, roadsides, water courses, sparse forests; 300--400 m. N Guangdong (Lianshan Zhuangzu Yaozu Zizhixian).
**68b.柔毛小柱悬钩子（变种）**

Rubus calumellaris Tutcher var. villosus Yu et Lu, 植物分类学报

本变种小枝、叶柄、叶片两面、花梗和花萼均被较密长柔毛。

产广东 (连山) 。生海拔270-400米的山坡、山谷、水边、路旁或疏林中。
