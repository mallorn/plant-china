## Description
Roots robust. Stems robust, ca. 7 mm in diam. at base, stellate strigose, glabrous toward base. Petiole ca. 1/2 as long as blade, densely tufted pubescent; mid stem leaf blades ovate, to 16 &amp;#215; 7 cm, adaxially sparsely minute hispid, densely so on veins, abaxially densely tufted pubescent, base truncate to shallowly oblique cordate, margin mucronate-crenate, apex acute to rounded. Verticillasters compact, 5-8-flowered, widely spaced; floral leaves short petiolate, upper ones circular, ca. 1 cm in diam; bracts lanceolate, 1-2 mm, appressed stellate pilose, spinelike. Flowers sessile or very short pedicellate. Calyx narrowly funnelform, 8-9 mm, stellate tomentose, 11-veined; teeth broadly circular, apical spine 0.5-1 mm. Corolla yellowish, reddish on upper lip, 1.5-1.8 cm, silky-pilose outside; upper lip 6-7 mm, bearded inside, apex 2-lobed; lower lip lobes entire, lateral lobes slightly narrower and shorter than middle lobe.

> Thickets; ca. 1900 m. Yunnan.
**37. 沧江糙苏 图版90:2**

Phlomis ambigua Hand. -Mazz. Symb. Sin. 7: 920. 1936.

茎自粗厚根上生出，极高大，粗壮，具分枝，具四槽，基部粗7毫米，被星状粗糙毛茸，下部无毛。茎中部叶卵形，长至16厘米，为宽的2倍以上，先端急尖或近圆形，基部截形或常为微不等的心形，边缘为具小突尖的圆齿状，草质，上面脉上密被其余部分疏被短硬毛，下面较淡，密被丛生短柔毛，侧脉2-4对，弧状网结，隆起，网脉稀疏，叶柄长为叶片之1/2，密被丛生短硬毛，苞叶向上渐变小，最上面的圆形，直径约1厘米，具短柄。轮伞花序紧密，彼此分离，具5-8花；苞片披针形，长1-2毫米，紧贴，被星状疏柔毛，刺状；花梗无或极短。花萼狭漏斗形，长8-9毫米，被星状绒毛，脉11，隆起，齿合生，阔圆形，端具长0.5-1毫米的小刺尖。花冠淡黄色，下唇带红色，长15-18毫米，外面被长绢毛状疏柔毛，上唇长6-7毫米，内面被髯毛，先端2裂，下唇具全缘裂片，侧裂片几不比中裂片狭，但较短。（标本未见，摘自原描写）

产云南西北部；生于灌丛中，海拔约1900米。模式标本采自云南维西沧江边。
