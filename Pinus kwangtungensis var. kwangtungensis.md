37a.**Pinus kwangtungensis var. kwangtungensis**",

## Description
Needles usually 5 per bundle, with white stomatal bands abaxially. Seed cones 5-9(-17) ×  3-7 cm.

> Hills, slopes, mountain ridges; 700-1600 m. N Guangdong, S Guizhou, Hainan, S Hunan [Vietnam]
