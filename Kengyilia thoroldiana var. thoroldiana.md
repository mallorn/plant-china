梭罗以礼草(原变种)",
20a.**Kengyilia thoroldiana var. thoroldiana**",

## Description
Spike 3–4 cm excluding awns. Glumes villous. Lemma awn 1–2.5 mm. Fl. and fr. late summer.

> Mountain slopes, valleys, river banks; 4700–5100 m. Gansu, Qinghai, Xizang [India (Sikkim)].
  "Synonym": "*Agropyron thoroldianum* Oliver in Hooker's Icon. Pl. 23: t. 2262. 1893; *Elymus thoroldianus* (Oliver) G. Singh; *Roeg-neria thoroldiana* (Oliver) Keng.
