## Description
Perennials 8-25 cm tall. Rhizomes horizontal, sheathed by a few blackish remains of old petiole. Stems erect, angular, slender, fastigiate branched. Basal leaves petioles flattened; leaf blade spatulate to oblong, 1.5-3.5 cm &amp;#215; 5-8 mm, base cuneate, apex obtuse, midvein distinct. Stem leaves subsessile to short petiolate; leaf blade lanceolate to broadly lanceolate, 1.5-3.5 cm &amp;#215; 5-8 mm, base subamplexicaul, apex acuminate to obtuse, midvein distinct. Inflorescences racemelike, 5-7-flowered. Pedicel spreading to erect, rigid, to 6 cm. Calyx tube less than 1.5 mm; lobes linear-spatulate, 7-9 &amp;#215; 1-1.2 mm, base obtuse, apex acute, midvein distinct. Corolla pale blue, with conspicuous dark lines, 2-2.7 cm in diam., tube less than 1.5 mm; lobes elliptic-lanceolate to elliptic, 1.1-1.5 cm &amp;#215; 5-6 mm, apex obtuse. Nectary connate at base into a tube pointed outward, apex lamellate, margin lobed. Filaments 8-10 mm; anthers blue, narrowly ellipsoid, ca. 3.5 mm. Ovary cylindric, 1-1.1 cm, apex subacuminate to obtuse. Stigma lobes decurrent to middle of ovary. Capsules ovoid-ellipsoid, to 1.4 cm. Seeds light brown, subglobose, ca. 0.2 mm in diam. Fl. and fr. Aug-Nov.

> * Beside streams, grassy slopes, alpine scrub and meadows; 3400-4200 m. S Sichuan, E Xizang, NW Yunnan.
**3．长叶肋柱花**

Lomatogonium longifolium H. Smith in Hand. -Mazz. Symb. Sin. 7: 982. 1936; S. Nilsson in Grana Palyn. 5 (3): 304. 1964 et 7 (1): 109. 1967.

多年生草本，高8-25厘米。根茎短。茎从基部起帚状分枝，近直立，四棱形，在基部常具黑褐色枯老残存叶柄。基生叶及下部茎生叶匙形，长1.5-3.5厘米，宽0.5-0.8厘米，先端钝圆，基部渐狭成柄，两面仅中脉明显，叶柄扁平，具狭翅，长至2厘米；茎中上部叶无柄，披针形至线状披针形，长至3.5厘米，宽至0.8厘米，枝上叶较小，仅中脉在两面明显。聚伞花序生分枝顶端；花梗坚硬，直立，长至6厘米；花5数，直径2-2.7厘米；花萼长为花冠的3/5，萼筒长1.5-2毫米，裂片线形，长7-9毫米，宽1-1.2毫米，先端急尖，背面中脉明显；花冠蓝色，裂片椭圆形至椭圆状披针形，长11-15毫米：宽5-6毫米，先端急尖，基部两侧各具1个腺窝，腺窝大而管形，上部边缘具裂片状短流苏；花丝线形，长8-10毫米，花药蓝色，线状矩圆形，长约3.5毫米；子房无柄，长10-11毫米，先端急尖，柱头小，下延至子房下部，蒴果无柄，披针状圆柱形， 短于花冠，长至14毫米；种子淡褐色，近圆球形，直径约0.2毫米。花果期8-11月。

产西藏东部、云南西北部、四川南部。生于河边、草坡、高山灌丛及高山草甸，海拔3400-4200米。模式标本采自云南澜沧江-怒江分水岭。
