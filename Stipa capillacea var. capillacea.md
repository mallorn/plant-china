丝颖针茅(原变种)",
3a.**Stipa capillacea var. capillacea**",

## Description
Glumes 2.5–3 cm. Lemma ca. 10 mm. Anthers ca. 4 mm. Fl. and fr. Jul–Sep.

> Alpine scrub, meadows, hills, montane plains, river banks; 2900–5000 m. Gansu, Qinghai, Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, N India, Kashmir, Nepal].
  "Synonym": "*Stipa koelzii* R. R. Stewart.
