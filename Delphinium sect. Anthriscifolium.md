## Description
Herbs annual or biennial, with taproots. Leaves mostly near middle of stem; leaf blade 2- or 3-pinnate. Petals enlarged distally. Staminodes of same color as sepals, not black-brown, glabrous, distinctly differentiated into a claw and an expanded limb. Carpels 3, widely spreading in fruit. Seeds transversely lamellate.
  "Statistics": "One species: C and S China, N Vietnam.
