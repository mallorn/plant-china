云南崖豆",
17e.**Millettia pulchra var. yunnanensis** (Pampanini) Dunn J. Linn. Soc., Bot. 41: 152. 1912.",

## Description
Leaflet blades oblong, 3.5-10 cm, adaxially pubescent, secondary veins 6 or 7 on each side of midvein, adaxially sunken, and densely yellow villous, base conspicuously asym-metric, apex acute.
  "Statistics": "Thickets, sparse woodlands; 500-1200 m. Yunnan [Myanmar].
  "Synonym": "*Millettia yunnanensis* Pampanini, Nuovo Giorn. Bot. Ital., n.s., 17: 26. 1910.
**15e.云南崖豆（变种）图版46：7**

var. yunnanensis (Pamp.) Dunn in Journ. Linn. Soc. Bot. 41: 152. 1912; 中国主要植物图说·豆科288. 1955.——M. yunnanense Pamp. in Nuov. Giorn. Bot. Ital. 17: 26. 1910, excl. var.

本变种与疏叶崖豆、印度崖豆近似，不同之处为：小叶7-9对，长圆形，先端锐尖，基部两侧不等大，侧脉6-7对，上面凹陷，下面隆起，脉上毛更密，萼齿渐尖。

产云南。缅甸北部也有分布。
