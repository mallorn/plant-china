箭头唐松草(原变种)",
57a.**Thalictrum simplex var. simplex**",

## Description
Plants glabrous. Proximal cauline leaflet blade orbicular-rhombic, broadly rhombic-ovate, or obovate, base rounded; lobes crenate, apex rounded or obtuse. Pedicel ca. 7 mm. Fl. Jul.

> Slopes, damp rocky ledges; 1400--2400 m. Nei Mongol, Xinjiang [C and SW Asia, Europe].
