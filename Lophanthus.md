## Description
Herbs perennial. Stem leaves short petiolate to sessile, dentate or incised-toothed. Cymes axillary; bracts small, linear-lanceolate to linear, rarely lanceolate. Calyx tubular to tubular-campanulate, straight or &amp;#177; incurved, apex regular or oblique; teeth 5, subequal, &amp;#177; 2-lipped [or not], (12-)15-veined, pilose annulate inside. Corolla straight or incurved; tube exserted, gradually dilated upward, twisted; limb 2-lipped, twisted at 90-180&amp;#176; so that position of lips is &amp;#177; reversed; upper lip (true lower) 3-lobed, middle lobe largest; lower lip (true upper) 2-lobed. Stamens 4, exserted or included; anther cells parallel or almost not divergent. Style exserted, rarely included, apex equally or subequally 2-cleft. Nutlets brown, oblong-ovoid, slightly flattened, smooth.

多年生草本。叶边缘具齿或齿裂；苞叶大多数较小。聚伞花序腋生；苞片小，线状披针形或线形，稀有披针形。花萼管状或管状钟形，直立或近弯曲，筒的顶部整齐或斜形，具5齿，齿近相等或呈二唇形，具15脉，稀12-13脉，内面在中部或中部以上具毛环。花冠直立或弯曲，冠筒外伸，向上增大，扭转，冠檐二唇形，倒扭90度至180度，上唇（即下唇）3裂，中裂片较大，下唇（即上唇）2裂。雄蕊4，均外伸或仅2枚外伸，或内藏，药室近平行或几不叉开。花盘前面隆起。花柱外伸，稀不外伸，先端2裂，裂片相等或近相等。小坚果长圆状卵圆形，稍压扁，光滑，褐色。

约18种，从伊朗至苏联及蒙古；我国产4种。
