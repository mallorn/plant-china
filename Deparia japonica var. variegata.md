## Description
Pinnae yellow-variegated on adaxial surface; indusial margin denticulate, perispore ornamentation long clavate and aculeate.

> ● Broad-leaved forests, beside streamlets; below 2000 m. Yunnan (Kunming).
  "Synonym": "Basionym: *Athyriopsis japonica* var. *variegata* W. M. Chu &amp; Z. R. He, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 36: 375. 1998.
**7b. 花叶假蹄盖蕨（变种）（植物分类学报）**

var. variegata W. M. Chu et Z. R. He in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 36 (4): 375. 1998.

与原变种的区别在于羽片上面沿中肋有淡乳黄色的花斑，囊群盖边缘不为撕裂状，仅有细牙状小齿，孢子周壁表面具较长的棒状及刺状纹饰。

特产于云南（昆明西山）。生于海拔2000米的阔叶林下溪沟边。
