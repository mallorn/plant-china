毛盘草(原变种)",
77a.**Elymus barbicallus var. barbicallus**",

## Description
Culms 70–100 cm tall, glabrous; nodes glabrous. Leaf sheath glabrous; leaf blade 15–20 × 0.6–0.8 cm, both surfaces glabrous or scabrous. Proximal glume 7–8 mm; distal glume 8–9 mm. Lemma 8–10 mm. Palea equaling lemma. Fl. and fr. Jun–Jul.

> * Forest margins, mountain slopes, river banks. Hebei, Nei Mongol.
  "Synonym": "*Agropyron barbicallum* Ohwi, Acta Phytotax. Geobot. 11: 257. 1942; *Roegneria barbicalla* (Ohwi) Keng &amp; S. L. Chen; *R. barbicalla* var. *breviseta* Keng.
