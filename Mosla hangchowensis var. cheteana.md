## Description
Verticillasters widely spaced; bracts not overlapping. Calyx teeth long subulate, subequal.

var. cheteana (Sun ex C. H. Hu) C. Y. Wu et H. W. Li in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 12(2): 212. 1974——Orthodon hangchowensis(Matsuda)C. Y. Wu var. cheteana Sun ex C. H. Hu in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 11(1): 46. 1966.

这一变种与原变种不同在于轮伞花序疏离；苞片非覆瓦状，萼齿长钻形，近等长。
