刺蚬壳花椒",
16c.**Zanthoxylum dissitum var. hispidum** (Reeder & S. Y. Cheo) C. C. Huang",

## Description
Branchlets with congested straight spines. Inflorescence rachises with prickles. Fl. Apr-May, fr. Sep-Nov.

> ● Upland open forests; 1500-1800 m. C and SW Sichuan, NE Yunnan (Suijiang).
  "Synonym": "*Fagara dissita* var. *hispida* Reeder &amp; S. Y. Cheo, J. Arnold Arbor. 32: 69. 1951.
小枝的一段密生长短不等的直刺，刺密集如擦子状，花序轴也散生纤细而挺直的刺。花期4-5月，果期9-11月。
