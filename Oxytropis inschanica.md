## Description
Herbs, (3.5-)5-8 cm tall, acaulescent, from a multiheaded caudex, clothed with marcescent leaf bases and stipules. Stip-ules lanceolate, with dense long trichomes, basally adnate to petiole. Leaves (2-)5-8 cm, 5-9-foliolate; leaflet blades linear, (0.6-)1-1.3(-1.4) cm × 0.5-1.5 mm, both surfaces glabrous, base cuneate, margin ciliate, apex attenuate. Racemes 2-5-flow-ered; peduncle shorter than to as long as leaves, with long tri-chomes; bracts ovate-elliptic, ca. 10 × 5-7 mm, both surfaces glabrous, margin ciliate, apex acute. Calyx cylindric, 1-1.5 cm, with dense white and black long trichomes; lobes linear-lan-ceolate, 2.5-4 mm. Corolla purple to bluish purple; standard 2-3 cm, lamina subelliptic, apex rounded or subtruncate; wings 1.8-2 cm, lamina obliquely obovate, apex rounded; keel ca. 1.8 cm, subequal to wings, beak 1-1.8 mm. Legume sessile, ovoid, 1-1.5 × ca. 0.8 cm, inflated, membranous, with spreading dense long trichomes. Fl. May-Jul, fr. Jul-Aug.

Oxytropis inschanica H. C. Fu et Cheng f. 内蒙古植物志 3: 223, 289 (Addenda). 图版113. 图1-7. 1977.

多年生草本。根粗壮，直伸。茎缩短。羽状复叶长5-8厘米；托叶披针形，于基部与叶柄贴生，密被长柔毛；小叶5-9，线形，长1-3厘米，宽0.5-1.5毫米，先端渐尖，基部楔形，边缘疏生缘毛，两面无毛。2-5花组成短总状花序；总花梗较叶短，或与之等长，被长柔毛；苞片卵状椭圆形，长约10毫米，宽5-7毫米，先端尖，两面无毛，疏生缘毛；花长20-30毫米；花萼筒状，长10-15毫米，密被白色和黑色长柔毛，萼齿线状披针形，长约4毫米；花冠紫红色或蓝紫色，旗瓣近椭圆形，长25毫米，瓣片先端圆形或近截形，翼瓣较旗瓣短，长约20毫米，瓣柄细长，耳短，龙骨瓣比翼瓣短或与之等长，喙长1.5毫米；子房有毛，具短柄。荚果膜质，卵形，膨胀，长10-15毫米，宽约8毫米，密被长柔毛。花期5-7月，果期7-8月。

产内蒙古大青山。生于海拔1700-2000米的山顶岩石缝间及向阳的干山坡。模式标本采自内蒙古大青山毕克齐归窝铺。
