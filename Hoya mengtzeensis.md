薄叶球兰",
28.**Hoya mengtzeensis** Tsiang & P. T. Li",

## Description
Subshrubs epilithic or epiphytic, glabrous except for corolla. Stems to 1.5 m, climbing by adventitious roots. Petiole 1-2 cm, adaxial glands 1-3; leaf blade narrowly lanceolate, 6-11 &amp;#215; 1.5-2 cm, thin papery, base attenuate and decurrent to petiole, apex acuminate; lateral veins obscure. Pseudumbels extra-axillary, flat topped, many flowered; peduncle 2-8 cm, stout. Pedicel 1.5-2.5 cm. Sepals ca. 1.5 &amp;#215; 0.8 mm, acuminate. Corolla white, 1-1.5 cm in diam., stellate spreading, glabrous outside, villous inside; tube ca. as long as lobes; lobes triangular, recurved at margin. Corona lobes yellow, ca. as high as gynostegium, outer angle rounded, inner teeth short, incumbent on gynostegium. Follicles linear-lanceolate, ca. 17 cm &amp;#215; 5 mm, smooth. Fl. Jul.

> * Mixed woods, covering rock on cliff. Guangxi, S Yunnan.
**21．薄叶球兰（植物分类学报）**

Hoya mengtzeensis Tsiang et P. T. Li in Acta Phytotax. Sinica 12: 120. Pl. 27. 1974.

半灌木附生在石上生根，除花冠内面被长柔毛外，其余无毛。叶在本属中比较薄，披针形，长6-11厘米，宽1.5-2厘米，顶端渐尖，基部渐狭，向叶柄下延；中脉下陷，侧脉及网脉在干时发皱；叶柄长1-2厘米，其顶端丛生2-3个小腺体。花序腋生，伞房状聚伞花序，着花20朵或更多；总花梗长6-8厘米，无毛，脱落的花梗痕迹甚多；花梗长2厘米；花蕾卵圆形；花萼无毛，5深裂；花冠直径1-1.5厘米，外面无毛，内面具长柔毛；副花冠黄亮，裂片外角圆形；花粉块及雌蕊与属的特征相同。蓇葖单生，线状披针形，长约17厘米，直径5毫米，外果皮无毛。花期7月。

产于云南的蒙自、澂江和广西等地。生长于杂木林中及攀援于大石上。模式标本采自云南蒙自。
