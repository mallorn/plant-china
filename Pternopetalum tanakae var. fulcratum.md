假苞囊瓣芹",
18b.**Pternopetalum tanakae var. fulcratum** Y. H. Zhang",

## Description
Leaves 1–2 under the base of umbel, bract-like, 1–2-ternate; ultimate segments elongate-linear, 10–25 × 1–2 mm. Bracts usually absent or occasionally 1, minute, linear-lanceolate. Fruit vittae 1 in each furrow.

> Among mosses in forests; ca. 1500 m. Anhui, Fujian, Jiangxi, Zhejiang.
