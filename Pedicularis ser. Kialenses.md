## Description
Herbs medium sized, less than 30 cm tall. Stems often many together, erect, unbranched. Leaves alternate, mostly basal, long petiolate, linear to lanceolate or lanceolate-ovate, pinnatipartite to pinnatisect. Inflorescences racemose, lax. Calyx cylindric; lobes 3 or 5, unequal. Corolla tube slightly longer than calyx; galea strongly twisted; beak S-shaped, longer than lower lip, slender; galea margin and beak long villous or glabrous; lobes of lower lip short and wide, &amp;#177; square.
