47.**Elymus mutabilis var. praecaespitosus** (Nevski) S. L. Chen",

## Description
Plants without rhizome. Culms tufted. Spikelets usually glaucous or purplish glaucous. Fl. and fr. Jun–Sep.

> Forest margins, mountain slopes; 1200–2400 m. Xinjiang [Mongolia, Russia].
  "Synonym": "*Agropyron praecaespitosum* Nevski, Izv. Glavn. Bot. Sada SSSR 29: 541. 1930; *Elymus praecaespitosus* (Nevski) Tzvelev; *E. mutabilis* subsp. *praecaespitosus* (Nevski) Tzvelev; *Goulardia praecaespitosa* (Nevski) Ikonnikov; *Roegneria pra-ecaespitosa* (Nevski) Nevski.
