1b.**Rhus chinensis var. roxburghii** (Candolle) Rehder",

## Description
Leaf rachis not winged. Fl. Aug-Sep, fr. Oct-Dec.

> ● Lowland, hill, and mountain forests, thickets; 200-2800 m. Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hainan, Hunan, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Rhus semialata* var. *roxburghii* Candolle, Prodr. 2: 67. 1825; *R. javanica* Linnaeus var. *roxburghii* (Candolle) Rehder &amp; E. H. Wilson; *R. roxburghii* Decaisne ex Steudel.
