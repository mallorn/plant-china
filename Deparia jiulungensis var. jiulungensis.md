九龙对囊蕨(原变种)",
32a.**Deparia jiulungensis var. jiulungensis**",

## Description
Stipe stramineous; multicellular hairs on stipe and rachis straight; indusia shortly ciliate at margin.

> ● Rock crevices, mountain slopes, forests, wet places; 900-2700 m. Anhui, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Zhejiang.
  "Synonym": "Basionym: *Lunathyrium jiulungense* Ching, Bull. Bot. Res., Harbin 2(2): 71. 1982; *L. orientale* Z. R. Wang &amp; J. J. Chien var. *huangshanense* Z. R. Wang; *L. orientale* var. *jiulungense* (Ching) Z. R. Wang.
