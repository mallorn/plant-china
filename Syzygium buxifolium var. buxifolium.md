赤楠(原变种)",
37a.**Syzygium buxifolium var. buxifolium**",

## Description
Branchlets 4-angled. Leaves opposite; leaf blade broadly elliptic, elliptic, or sometimes broadly obovate, 1.5-3 × 1-2 cm, secondary adaxially inconspicuous, base broadly cuneate to obtuse, apex rounded to obtuse and sometimes with an obtuse cusp.

> Sparse forests or scrub in mountains; 200-1200 m. Anhui, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hainan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Zhejiang [S Japan, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Eugenia microphylla* Abel; *E. sinensis* Hemsley; *E. somae* Hayata; *Syllisium buxifolium* (Hooker &amp; Arnott) Meyen &amp; Schauer; *Syzygium somae* (Hayata) Mori.
