牛尾菜 (原变种)",
2a.**Smilax riparia var. riparia**",

## Description
Stem, branches, leaves, and inflorescence glabrous. Female flowers: staminodes absent.

> Forests, thickets, grassy slopes, hillsides along valleys; near sea level to 1600 m. Anhui, Fujian, Gansu, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hebei, Heilongjiang, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Jilin, Liaoning, ?Nei Mongol, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan, Zhejiang [Japan, Korea, Philippines].
