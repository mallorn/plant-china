小叶细弱栒子",
28b.**Cotoneaster gracilis var. difficilis** (G. Klotz) L. T. Lu",

## Description
Leaf blade ovate-elliptic to broadly elliptic, 7–14 × 5.5–8 mm, abaxially tenuously tomentose. Inflorescences 1–3(–6)-flowered.

> Mountain regions; 1800--3000 m. SE Gansu, W Sichuan.
  "Synonym": "*Cotoneaster difficilis* G. Klotz, Wiss. Z. Friedrich-Schiller-Univ. Jena, Math.-Naturwiss. Reihe (Beitr. Phytotax. 3) 21: 1017. 1972.
