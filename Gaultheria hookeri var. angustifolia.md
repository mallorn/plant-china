狭叶红粉白珠",
10b.**Gaultheria hookeri var. angustifolia** C. B. Clarke",

## Description
Leaves lanceolate or linear-lanceolate, 1–1.5 cm wide. Bracts 1–2 mm wide. Calyx lobes acuminate.

> Forests, forest margins, thickets on mountain slopes; 2000–2500(–3000) m. NW Yunnan (Gongshan) [Sikkim].
**8b．狭叶红粉白珠（云南植物研究）（变种）**

var. angustifolia C. B. Clarke in Hook. f. Fl. Brit. Ind. 3. 458. 1882; T. Z. Hsu in Act. Bot. Yunn. 3: 424. 1981; 云南植物志4: 591. 1986.

与原变种不同在于叶披针形，长5.5-6（-10）厘米，宽约2厘米，两端渐尖。

产云南西北部。生于海拔2 000-3 700米的灌丛中。锡金亦有。
