## Description
Stems and peduncles of male inflorescences puberulent. Fl. May-Jul.

> * Shaded wet areas in valleys, sparse forests; 600-700 m. W Guangdong, N Guangxi, W Jiangxi, SE Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Elatostema* *stipulosum* var. *puberulum* W. T. Wang, Bull. Bot. Lab. N. E. Forest. Inst., Harbin 7: 48. 1980.
**46b.短毛楼梯草（变种）**

Elatostema nasutum Hook.f. var. puberulum (W. T. Wang) W. T. Wang in Bull. Bot. Res. (Harbin) 12 (3): 207. 1992. ——E. stipulosum Hand. -Mazz. var. puberulum W. T. Wang in Bull. Bot. Lab. N. -E. For. Inst. 7: 48. 1980.

与托叶楼梯草的区别：茎上部和雄花序梗均密被开展的短柔毛。

产云南东南部（屏边）、广西北部（融水）、广东西部（信宜）、江西西部（井冈山）。生于山谷阴湿处或疏林中，海拔约650米。模式标本采自井冈山。
