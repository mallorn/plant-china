贡山球兰",
25.**Hoya lii** C. M. Burton",

## Description
Shrubs glabrous except for branchlets and petiole. Stems climbing, to 2 m. Petiole 2-3 mm, setose; leaf blade oblong or oblong-elliptic, 6.5-10 &amp;#215; 2-3 cm, papery, base obtuse, apex acuminate to caudate-acuminate; midvein elevated; lateral veins 5-7 pairs, conspicuous adaxially, obscure abaxially. Pseudumbels terminal, ca. 5 cm; peduncle ca. 1 cm. Pedicel ca. 3 cm. Sepals ca. 2 &amp;#215; 3 mm, hidden by reflexed corolla. Corolla white, 1.5-1.8 cm in diam., strongly reflexed; tube shorter than lobes; lobes lanceolate, ca. 1.2 cm &amp;#215; 3.5 mm. Corona lobes ovoid-lanceolate, 4-5 mm, fleshy, erect, base rounded, margin revolute, apex long caudate and exceeding gynostegium, tails 2-2.5 mm. Fl. Aug.

> * Broad-leaved evergreen forests; ca. 1400 m. Yunnan (Gongshan).
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Centrostemma yunnanense&lt;/I&gt; P. T. Li (1990), not &lt;I&gt;Hoya yuennanensis &lt;/I&gt;Handel-Mazzetti (1936); &lt;I&gt;Hoya gongshanica&lt;/I&gt; P. T. Li.
