> One species.

## Description
Plants herbaceous. Stems often ascending to decumbent. Basal leaves long petiolate, lanceolate to elliptic, pinnatisect; segments ovate to oblong; stem leaves often pseudo-opposite. Flowers axillary, pedicellate. Calyx lobes 5. Corolla tube more than 4 X as long as calyx; beak bent upward, slender.
