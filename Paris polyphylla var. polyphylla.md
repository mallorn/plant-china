七叶一枝花 (原变种)",
6a.**Paris polyphylla var. polyphylla**",

## Description
Plants 30--100 cm tall. Leaves 6--10, 2.5--5 cm wide. Inner tepals narrowly linear, 1--2 mm wide, usually slightly longer than outer ones. Filaments 4--7 mm; anthers 5--8 mm; free portion of connective 0.5--1 mm. 2&lt;I&gt; n&lt;/I&gt; = 10*.

> Forests, thickets, grassy slopes; 100--2400 m. Gansu, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hubei, Hunan, Sichuan, Taiwan, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, India, Nepal, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt; Daiswa polyphylla&lt;/I&gt; (Smith) Rafinesque; &lt;I&gt; Paris biondii&lt;/I&gt; Pampanini; &lt;I&gt; P. debeauxii&lt;/I&gt; H. Léveillé; &lt;I&gt; P. taitungensis&lt;/I&gt; S. S. Ying.
