## Description
Petiole glabrous; leaf blade oblong-elliptic, 9-15 × 4-5.5 cm, base cuneate. Fruit 2-2.6 cm in diam.

> ● Forests in hilly areas; ca. 1100 m. Guangdong (Lianshan)
