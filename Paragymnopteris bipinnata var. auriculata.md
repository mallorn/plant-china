> *Paragymnopteris bipinnata* var. *auriculata* is similar to *P. vestita*. It differs in its long pinna stalks (to 1 cm), strongly cordate pinna base, and free veins.

## Description
Lamina 1-pinnate. Pinnae ovate or narrowly ovate, stalked (stalks to 1 cm), base strongly cordate, margins entire or often enlarged forming auricles at both sides near base, sometimes proximal pinnae with 1 or 2 free segments, apex obtuse.

> ● On rocks beneath shrubs or woods; 800-3600 m. Gansu, Hebei, Henan, W Hubei, Nei Mongol, Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan.

一回羽状复叶，羽片卵形或长卵形，基部深心脏形，两侧常扩大成耳形或有1-2片分离小羽片。

产北京（百花山）、河北（围场、阜平、蓟县、内丘、赞皇、涞水）、内蒙古、河南（西峡、卢氏、集川、嵩县）、湖北西部、陕西（略阳、洋县、平利、宁陕、鄂县、赤永）、甘肃（徽县）、四川西部、云南（昆明、嵩明、景东、宾川、鹤庆）、西藏（波密、察隅）。生灌丛或林下石上，海拔800-3500米。常见。模式标本采自北京附近。

本变种形体极似金毛裸蕨Gymnopteris vestita（Presl）Und erw.但羽片基部深心脏形，柄长可达1厘米。
