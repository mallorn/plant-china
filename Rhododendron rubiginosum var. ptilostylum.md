> Based on the type and matching specimens (from Haba and Yongning) the cited differences between var. *rubiginosum* and var. *ptilostylum* are not convincing.

## Description
Calyx densely scaly; filaments pubescent below; style pubescent along lower part.

> *Rhododendron* forests, deciduous broad-leaved forests, *Abies* forest margins, thickets on slopes; 3200–3300 m. Yunnan.
**69b. 毛柱红棕杜鹃（变种）（云南植物研究）**

Rhododendron rubiginosum Franch. var. ptilostylum R. C. Fang in Act. Bot. Yunn. 4: 253. 1982; 云南植物志4: 487. 1986; 横断山区维管植物下册1426. 1994.

本变种的特征是花柱下部有微柔毛。

产云南丽江玉龙山、禄劝拱王山（乌蒙山）。生于杜鹃-落叶阔叶林，冷杉林缘或山坡灌丛，海拔3 200-3 300米。模式标本采自云南丽江。
