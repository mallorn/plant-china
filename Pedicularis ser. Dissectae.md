## Description
Stems branching basally or above. Leaves alternate or proximal ones pseudo-opposite, petiolate, ovate-oblong, pinnatisect. Inflorescence racemose. Flowers often long pedicellate. Calyx deeply cleft anteriorly, 3--5-lobed. Corolla often yellowish white; galea strongly twisted laterally at base; beak bent upward, S-shaped or semicircular, slender.
