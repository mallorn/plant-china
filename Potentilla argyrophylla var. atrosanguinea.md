## Description
Petals basally, stamens, and styles purple.

> Ditch sides; ca. 4000 m. Xizang [Nepal, Pakistan].
  "Synonym": "*Potentilla atrosanguinea* Loddiges, G. Loddiges &amp; W. Loddiges, Bot. Cab. 8: t. 786. 1823.
**53b.紫花银光委陵菜（变种）**

P. argyrophylla Wall. var. atrosanguinea Hook. f, Fl. Brit. Tnd. 2: 357. 1878. ——P. atrosanguinea Lodd. Bot. Cab. 8: t. 786. 1824.

花瓣基部，雄蕊及花柱均紫色。

产我国西藏。生水沟边，海拔4000米。
