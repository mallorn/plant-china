结球马先蒿",
52.**Pedicularis conifera** Maximowicz ex Forbes & Hemsley",

## Description
Herbs annual, to more than 60 cm tall, drying black. Branches whorled, short, slender. Leaves in whorls of 4 or 5; petiole short; leaf blade &amp;#177; oblong, ca. 2.5 X 1 cm, pinnatisect; segments more than 10 pairs, oblong-linear, pinnatilobate, incised-double dentate. Bracts linear. Calyx tubular, ca. 4.5 mm; lobes 5, &amp;#177; equal, serrate, glabrous. Corolla purple, ca. 6 mm, glabrous; tube erect, barely as long as calyx; galea bent at a right angle, prolonged into a beak apically; beak straight, ca. 5.5 mm, slender; lower lip ca. 3.5 X 3 mm, middle lobe smaller than lateral lobes, narrowly ovate. Filaments glabrous.

Pedicularis conifera Maxim. in Bull. Acad. St. Petersb. XXXII (1888) f. 193, et in Mel. Biol. XII (1888) f. 193, et ex Forbes et Hemsl. in Journ. Linn. Soc. Bot. XXVI (1890) 206; Diels, in Bot. Jahrb. XXIX (1900) 572; Limpr. in Repert. Sp. Nov. XX (1922) 243; Li, in Proc. Acad. Nat. Sci. Philad. C (1948) 278.

一年生，高升，干时不变黑色。茎高达60厘米以上，上部分枝，枝轮生，短细，上升。叶4-5轮生，有短柄，长2.5厘米，宽约1厘米，多少长圆形，羽状全裂，裂片紧密，每边10枚以上，长圆状线形，羽状浅裂，小裂片卵形，有具刺尖头的重锯齿。花生于主茎及分枝的顶端，成密而短的总状花序；苞片线形，萼管状，长4.5毫米，有明显的主脉10条，萼齿5枚，长卵形，缘有齿而反卷，视如全缘，约等于管长的一半稍多；花冠很小，长6毫米，无毛，管短而宽，包于萼内，伸直，顶部扩大，短于盔的直立部分，盔以直角转折，前端渐狭为伸直而钝头微凹的喙，喙长5.5毫米，下唇长3.5毫米，宽3毫米，平展，几为圆形，侧片斜倒卵形，中裂完全向前凸出，狭卵形，长0.8毫米，端作兜状；花丝无毛，着生于花管上部1/3处。

为我国特有种，至今为止仅在原产地湖北巴东被采得。
