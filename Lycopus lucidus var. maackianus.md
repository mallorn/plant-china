## Description
Stems slender, 20-50 cm tall. Lower stem leaves elliptic to lanceolate, margin &amp;#177; pinnatipartite; mid leaves serrate; upper leaves linear-lanceolate, margin &amp;#177; entire.

> * Wet grasslands. Heilongjiang.
