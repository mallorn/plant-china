## Description
Young branchlets and petioles glabrous. Leaf blade often narrower, sparsely yellow sericeous abaxially when young, indumentum easily deciduous, glabrate.
  "Statistics": "● Weed-tree forests on mountains; 500-1900 m. N Guangdong, Guangxi, Hunan, Jiangxi, Zhejiang.
  "Synonym": "*Neolitsea paraciculata* Nakai, Fl. Sylv. Kor. 22: 46. 1939.
**10d.云和新木姜子（变种）图版90：5**

var. paraciculata (Nakai) Yang et P. H. Huang in. Act. Phytotax. Sin, 16 (4): 40. 1978.——N. paraciculata Nakei in Fl. Sylv. Kor. 22: 46. 1939, in adnot.

与原变种不同在于幼枝、叶柄均无毛，叶片通常略较窄，下面疏生黄色丝状毛，易脱落，近于无毛，具白粉。

产浙江、江西、湖南、广东北部、广西。生于山地杂木林中，海拔500-1900米。
