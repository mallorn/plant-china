爪哇唐松草(原变种)",
30a.**Thalictrum javanicum var. javanicum**",

> This variety is used medicinally.

## Description
Plants glabrous. Inflorescence pleiochasial, few or many flowered. Carpels 8--15; style 0.6--1 mm. Fl. Apr--Jul.

> Forests, slopes, damp rocky ledges; 1500--3400 m. Gansu, Guangdong, Guizhou, Hubei, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Xizang, Yunnan, Zhejiang [Bhutan, India, Indonesia, Nepal, Sikkim, Sri Lanka].
