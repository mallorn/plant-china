康定毛茛(原变种)",
71a.**Ranunculus dielsianus var. dielsianus**",

## Description
Stems 5--25 cm. Basal leaves adaxially glabrous, abaxially glabrous or sparsely puberulent. Carpels and achenes puberulent.

> * Grassy or gravelly slopes, woods, by streams; 3500--4800 m. W Sichuan, S Xizang, NE Yunnan.
