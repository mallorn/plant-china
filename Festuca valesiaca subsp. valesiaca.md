瑞士羊茅(原亚种)",
52a.**Festuca valesiaca subsp. valesiaca**",

## Description
Leaf blades bluish green. Spikelets 4–6(–7.5) mm; lemmas 2.8–4.2(–4.7) mm.

> Grassy mountain slopes, subalpine meadows, grasslands, roadsides; 1000–3700 m. N Sichuan, Xinjiang, Xizang, Yunnan [Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Mongolia, Russia, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan; SW Asia, Europe].
