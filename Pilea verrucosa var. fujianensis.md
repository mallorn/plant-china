闽北冷水花",
16b.**Pilea verrucosa var. fujianensis** C. J. Chen",

## Description
Petiole 1-2 cm; leaf blade black when dry, lanceolate, 10-14 × 2.3-4.2 cm, membranous, triplinerved, base narrowly cuneate; Achene ca. 0.5 mm, spinulose-verrucose. Fl. May, fr. Jun-Jul.

> * Wet places by streams; 800-1000 m. Fujian (Jianning, Taining).
