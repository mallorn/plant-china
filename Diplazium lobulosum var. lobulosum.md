浅裂双盖蕨(原变种)",
35a.**Diplazium lobulosum var. lobulosum**",

## Description
Pinnae pinnatilobate.

> Evergreen broad-leaved forests, wet calcareous areas; 1500-2500 m. S Xizang (Nyalam), Yunnan [N India, N Myanmar, Nepal].
  "Synonym": "*Asplenium lobulosum* Wallich ex Mettenius, Abh. Senckenberg. Naturf. Ges. 3: 163. 1859; *Allantodia lobulosa* (Wallich ex Mettenius) Ching; *Asplenium longifolium* D. Don (1825), not Schrader (1824); *Athyrium longifolium* (T. Moore) Milde; *Diplazium longifolium* T. Moore.
