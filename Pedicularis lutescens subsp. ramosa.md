## Description
Plants low, 10--20(--30) cm. Stems many branched basally; branches rigid, stout. Leaves &amp;#177; sessile or petiole to ca. 5 mm; leaf blade ca. 2 cm. Galea with 4--6 marginal teeth on each side.

> * SW Sichuan, NW Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Pedicularis lutescens&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;ramosa&lt;/I&gt; Bonati, Notes Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinburgh 13: 135. 1921.
