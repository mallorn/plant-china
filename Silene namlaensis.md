墨脱蝇子草",
58.**Silene namlaensis** (Marquand) Bocquet",

## Description
Plants perennial, 15--40 cm tall, with violet multicellular glandular hairs throughout. Rootstock robust, lignified, brown, without stolons. Stems clustered, erect, simple, basally usually violet. Cauline leaves 2--6 pairs, narrowly orbicular or lanceolate, 3--7 cm × 5--12 mm, glandular villous, midvein prominent, base cuneate, apex acute. Flowers 1--3, slightly nutant, erect in late anthesis. Pedicel 3--5 cm, densely glandular villous; bracts lanceolate. Calyx cylindric-campanulate, slightly inflated-saccate, 2.3--2.7 × 1--1.5 cm, densely violet glandular hairy, open at apex; longitudinal veins dark violet, hairy; calyx teeth broadly triangular, 4--6 mm, margin ciliate, apex acute. Androgynophore ca. 5 mm, villous. Petals exserted 3--6 mm beyond calyx, violet; claws cuneate, ca. 15 × 6 mm, with auricles, subglabrous at base; limbs square, shallowly 4--6-lobed, lobes rounded or laciniate; coronal scales broad, rounded to slightly erose. Stamens slightly exserted beyond corolla throat; filaments glabrous. Styles included. Capsule ovoid. Seeds probably winged. Fl. Aug--Sep.

> * Alpine grasslands; 3600--4500 m. C and SE Xizang.

多年生草本，高30-40厘米，全株密被紫色节腺毛。根较粗壮，木质，褐色。茎丛生或单生，直立，不分枝，下部常显紫色。基生叶花期早枯；茎生叶2-4对，叶片狭椭圆形或披针形，长3-7厘米，宽5-12毫米，基部楔形，顶端急尖，被腺柔毛，中脉明显。花单生或2-3朵，微俯垂，后期直立，花梗长3-5厘米，密被腺柔毛；苞片披针形；花萼圆柱状钟形，微膨大呈囊状，长约25毫米，宽10-15毫米，口张开，基部脐形，密被紫色腺毛，纵脉紫黑色，被细硬毛，萼齿宽三角形，长4-6毫米，顶端急尖，边缘具缘毛；雌雄蕊柄长约5毫米，被疏柔毛；花瓣露出花萼3-6毫米，爪近楔形，长约15毫米，宽约6毫米，具耳，基部近无毛，瓣片微外露，紫色，轮廓近方形，4裂，裂片圆形或缺刻状，副花冠片近楔形，具不规则细圆齿；雄蕊微露花冠喉部，花丝无毛；花柱内藏。蒴果卵形；种子未见。花期8-9月。

产我国西藏（林芝、波密、墨脱、加查、错那、聂拉木）。生于海拔3 600-4 500米高山草地。模式标本采自波密。
