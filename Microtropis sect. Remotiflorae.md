## Description
Dichasium loosely spreading, fastigiate, flabellate or broadly patent. Peduncle generally up to 1 cm; secondary peduncle and pedicels obvious or at least conspicuous. Flowers 5-merous, rarely 4- or 6-merous.
  "Statistics": "Eleven species: S and SE Asia; eleven species (six endemic) in China.
