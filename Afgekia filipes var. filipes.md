猪腰豆(原变种)",
1a.**Afgekia filipes var. filipes**",

## Description
Leaflet blades papery, both surfaces silvery pubescent but glabrescent.
  "Statistics": "Sparse thickets; 200-1300 m. W Guangxi, S Yunnan [Laos, Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam].
