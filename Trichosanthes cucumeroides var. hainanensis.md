## Description
Fruit ovoid or ovoid-oblong. Seeds triangular-ovate, with middle ridge broad; 2 lateral locules narrow, ca. 2.5 mm in diam.
  "Statistics": "● Guangdong, Guangxi, Hainan.

本变种与王瓜（原变种）的主要区别在于种子为三角状卵形，两侧室狭小，径约2.5毫米，中央环带宽而下延。
