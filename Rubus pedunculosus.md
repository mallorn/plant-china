## Description
Shrubs. Branchlets brownish to reddish brown, initially pubescent, gradually glabrescent, with few prickles. Leaves imparipinnate, 3-foliolate, rarely 5-foliolate; petiole 3–5 cm, with rachis pubescent, with sparse, minute prickles; stipules linear or linear-lanceolate, 5–8 mm, soft hairy; blade of leaflets ovate or broadly ovate, rarely ovate-lanceolate, 2.5–6 × 2–5 cm, abaxially densely persistent tomentose, adaxially pubescent, glabrescent, base subrounded to shallowly cordate, margin irregularly incised rough sharp doubly serrate, apex acute to acuminate. Inflorescences corymbose, several flowered. Pedicel 1.5–3.5 cm, soft hairy. Flowers 1.5–2 cm in diam. Calyx to ca. 1.5 cm, abaxially pubescent; tube pelviform; sepals erect after anthesis, narrowly ovate to ovate-lanceolate, 1–1.2 cm × 3–6 mm, margin tomentose, apex long acuminate or caudate. Petals pink, ovate, 7–10 × 4–6 mm, shorter than sepals, base shortly clawed, apex slightly incised or entire. Stamens many. Pistils somewhat shorter than or nearly as long as stamens; ovary sericeous, soft hairy. Aggregate fruit black, ovoid-globose.

> Forested slopes, thickets, logged lands; 3000--3200 m. SE Xizang, W Yunnan [Bhutan, NW India, Kashmir, Nepal, Sikkim].

Don (1832), not Thunberg (1813); *R. pedunculosus* var. *hypargyrus* (Edgeworth) Kitamura.

**38a.纤细悬钩子（原变种）**

Rubus hypargyrus Edgew. var. hypargyrus

灌木；枝圆柱形，幼时被柔毛，老时脱落，有少数小皮刺。小叶3枚，稀5枚，卵形、宽卵形，稀卵状披针形，长2.5-6厘米，宽2-5厘米，顶端急尖至渐尖，顶生小叶基部浅心形，侧生小叶基部近圆形，上面具柔毛，老时渐脱落，下面被薄层绒毛，逐渐脱落至近无毛，边缘具不整齐缺刻状粗锐重锯齿；叶柄长3-5厘米，与叶轴均具柔毛，疏生小皮刺；托叶线形或线状披针形，具柔毛。花常2朵生于叶腋；花梗长1.5-3.5厘米，具柔毛；花较大；花萼长约1.4厘米，内外两面均被柔毛，仅萼片外面边缘具绒毛；萼片卵形至卵状披针形，顶端长渐尖或尾尖，花后直立；花瓣直立，卵形，粉红色，短于萼片；雄蕊多数；雌蕊较多，子房具绢状柔毛。果实卵球形，黄色。

产印度西北部至锡金。我国西藏有1变种。

本种叶片形状及大小、被毛之疏密、花之大小等诸征状均有变异，过去所采用的名称也较混乱，Hara in Journ. Jap, Bot. 53: 137. 1978中已重作处理。由于未见模式标本，难以确定，故暂从Hara的意见处理。
**38b.密毛纤细悬钩子（变种）（西藏植物志）**

Rubus hypargyrus Edgew. var. niveus Hara in Journ. Jap. Bot. 53: 137. 1978. ——R. niveus Wall. (Cat. 22. no. 734. 1829. nom. nud.) ex G. Don in Gen. Syst. 2: 530. 1832; Hook. f. Fl. Brit. Ind. 2: 335. 1878, non Thunb. 1813. ——R. pedunculosus D. Don sensu Wall. Cat. 22. no. 729. 1829; Rehd. Bibl. 288. 1949; Hara＆Ohashi in Fl. E. Himal. 131. 1966. ——R. niveus var. niveus (Wall.) Hook. f. Fl. Brit. Ind. 2: 335. 1878. ——R. gracidis Roxb. sensu Focke, Bibl. Bot. 72(2): 190. 1911, non J.＆C. Presl 1822.

本变种叶片下面绒毛较密，不脱落；花数朵成伞房状花序；果实熟时黑色，微被柔毛。

产西藏东南部（林芝）。生山坡林下、灌丛中或采伐迹地，海拔3000-3200米。据文献记载，云南西部也有，但未见标本。不丹、锡金、尼泊尔至克什米尔地区、印度西北部也有分布。

据原始记载，纤细悬钩子R. hypargyrus Edgew.的花2朵生于叶腋，果实黄色，而Hara归并作为密毛纤细悬钩子异名的R. niveus Wall. ex G. Don和R. gracilis Roxb., 其花常数朵成伞房状花序，果实熟时黑色，此特征与我国西藏所采标本相符合，故修正了上述变种的形态描述。
