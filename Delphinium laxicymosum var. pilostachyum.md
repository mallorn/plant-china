## Description
Stem hispid throughout. Corymb retrorsely puberulent. Upper sepal ca. 1.2 cm; spur ca. 2 cm.

> * Mixed forests in valleys, streamsides; ca. 1300 m. N Sichuan (Qingchuan Xian).
**63b. 毛序聚伞翠雀花**

Delphinium laxicymosum var. pilostachyum W. T. Wang in Addenda.

与聚伞翠雀花的区别：茎全长均被开展的糙毛；花序轴和花梗都有反曲的短柔毛；花稍小，萼片长约1.2厘米，萼距长约2厘米。

产四川北部（青川）。生海拔1300米一带山地谷中沟边或杂木林中。
