平伐含笑(原变种)",
18a.**Michelia cavaleriei var. cavaleriei**",

## Description
Trees, to 10 m tall. Twigs ca. 4 mm in diam. Tepals ca. 12, outer ones 2.5-4 cm. 2*n* = 38*.
  "Statistics": "● Forests; 800-2400 m. Fujian, N Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, W Hubei, SW Hunan, SE Sichuan, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Magnolia cavaleriei* (Finet &amp; Gagnepain) Figlar; *M. maudiae* (Dunn) Figlar var. *hunanensis* (C. L. Peng &amp; L. H. Yan) Sima; *Michelia fallax* Dandy; *M. hunanensis* C. L. Peng &amp; L. H. Yan; *M. xinningia* Y. W. Law &amp; R. Z. Zhou.
