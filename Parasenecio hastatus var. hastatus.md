## Description
Leaves abaxially and phyllaries outside densely glandular puberulent.
  "Statistics": "Forest understories, grasslands along forest margins; 1700-2300 m. Gansu, Hebei, Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Shaanxi, Shanxi [N Japan, Korea, Mongolia, Russia (Far East)].

**6a.山尖子（原变种）**

Parasenecio hastatus (L.) H. Koyama var. hastatus

叶下面和总苞片外面被密腺状短柔毛。

产于东北、华北。生于林下，林缘或草丛中。朝鲜、蒙古和俄罗斯远东地区也有。
