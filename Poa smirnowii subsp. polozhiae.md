朴咯早熟禾",
27c.**Poa smirnowii subsp. polozhiae** (Revjankina) Olonova",

> This taxon was described from Russia (Altai). One gathering is known from China.

## Description
Culms several (ca. 5) per tuft, ca. 20 cm tall. Spikelets viviparous. Fl. and fr. Aug.

> Alpine screes; ca. 3700 m. Xinjiang [Russia (Siberia)].
  "Synonym": "*Poa polozhiae* Revjankina, Fl. Rastitel'n. Altaya 1996: 102. 1996.
