宽苞棘豆(原变种)",
110a.**Oxytropis latibracteata var. latibracteata**",

## Description
Leaflet blades 0.6-1.7 cm. Bracts 8-11 × ca. 2 mm, shorter than calyx. Corolla standard ca. 2.1 cm. Fl. Jul-Aug, fr. Jul-Aug. 2*n* = 16*.
  "Statistics": "● Sunny slopes, *Betula* forests, hillsides, alpine steppes, grass-lands, floodplains; 1700-3800 m. Gansu, Hebei, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Xinjiang, Xizang.
