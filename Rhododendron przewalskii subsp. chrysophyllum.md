## Description
Leaf blade 7–11 × 5–7 cm; abaxial surface densely deep brown to ferruginous floccose short-hirsute, hairs long-rayed stellate, persistent. Inflorescence ca. 10-flowered. Ovary glabrous. Fl. May–Jun.

> Forests, mountain slopes; 2700–2800 m. E Qinghai.
**161b. 金背陇蜀杜鹃（亚种）（高原生物学丛刊）**

Rhododendron przewalskii Maxim. subsp. chrysophyllum Fang et S. X. Wang in Act. Biol. Plateau Sin. 1:227. 1982; 青海木本植物志520. 1987.

本亚种与原亚种的区别在于叶片基部宽楔形或近于圆形，下面密被棕褐色或铁锈色状短硬毛，花冠漏斗形。

产青海东部。生于海拔2 700-2 800米的山坡林中。模式标本采自青海互助县北山林场。
