皱叶柳叶栒子",
1b.**Cotoneaster salicifolius var. rugosus** (E. Pritzel) Rehder & E. H. Wilson",

## Description
Leaf blade elliptic-oblong, adaxially deeply rugose, margin revolute, veins deeply impressed. Fruit subglobose, ca. 6 mm in diam. 2n = 68*.

> Slopes; 400--1900 m. W Hubei, E Sichuan.
  "Synonym": "*Cotoneaster rugosus* E. Pritzel, Bot. Jahrb. Syst. 29: 385. 1900; C. hylmoei Flinck &amp; J. Fryer.
**柳叶栒子皱叶变种 小叶山米麻（中国树木分类学）**

S. salicifolius Franch. var. rugosus (Pritz.) Rehd. & Wils. in Sarg. Pl. Wils. 1: 172. 1912; 陈嵘, 中国树木分类学438. 1937.——C. rugosa Pritz. in Engler, Bot. Jahrb. 29: 385. 1900; Bean in Curtis's Bot. Mag. 143: t. 8694. 1917.——C. rugosa var. typica Schneid. Ill. Handb. Laubh. 1: 758. 1906; Klotz in Wiss. Zeits. Univ. Halle 6: 971. 1957.

叶片较宽大，椭圆长圆形，上面暗褐色，具深皱纹，叶脉深陷，叶边反卷，下面叶脉显著突起，密被绒毛。果实红色，直径约6毫米，具小核2-3。

产湖北西部及四川东部。
