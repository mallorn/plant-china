狭叶五加(原变种)",
13a.**Eleutherococcus wilsonii var. wilsonii**",

## Description
Leaflets glabrous abaxially, margin crenate-serrulate.

> ● Scrub fields, forests; 2500-3600 m. S Gansu, W Hubei, S Shaanxi, Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan.
