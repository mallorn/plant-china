## Description
Fronds obovate to orbicular, 3-10 mm, 1-1.5 × as long as wide, flat, rarely gibbous, sometimes with indistinct papillae on upper surface along veins. Roots (5-)7-21, 1(or 2) perforating scale, 0.5-3 cm. Turions sometimes present, without roots, brownish to olive, orbicular to reniform, 1-2 mm. Ovary with 1 or 2 ovules. Fruit laterally winged toward apex. Seed with 12-20 ribs. Fl. (very rare) Jun-Sep. 2*n* = 30, 32, 38, 40*, 50, 80.
