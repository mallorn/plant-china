## Description
Calyx lobes 2 or 3, posterior one small or absent, lateral pair leaflike, profoundly dentate. Galea prominently crested in front, appendage long filiform, spinescent.

> * Alpine meadows; 3600--4900 m. SW and W Sichuan, NW Yunnan.
