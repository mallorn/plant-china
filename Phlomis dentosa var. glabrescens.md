渐光糙苏",
16b.**Phlomis dentosa var. glabrescens** Danguy",

## Description
Stems sparsely hispidulous to subglabrous. Leaves subglabrous. Bracts and calyx sparsely setose, dusty pube-rulent.

> * Slopes; 0-1200 m. Gansu, Hebei, Nei Mongol, Qinghai.
**16b. 渐光变种**

Phlomis dentosa Franch. var. glabrescens Danguy in Bull. Mus. Hist. Nat. Paris 2: 345. 1911.

这一变种与原变种不同在于茎被极疏具节短刚毛至近无毛；叶近无毛；苞片及花萼疏被具节刚毛及尘状微柔毛。

产河北，内蒙古，甘肃及青海；生于草坡上，海拔至1200米。模式标本采自甘肃。
