## Description
Branchlets and petioles densely gray-yellow appressed pubescent. Leaf blade abaxially densely gray-yellow appressed pubescent, inconspicuously reticulate-veined abaxially.
  "Statistics": "Dense forests on mountain slopes; 600-900 m. W Guangxi, SE Yunnan [Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Litsea variabilis* var. *tonkinensis* Lecomte.
**18b. 毛黄椿木姜子（变种）**

var. oblonga Lec. in Nouv. Arch. Hist. Nat. Paris 5e Ser. 5: 90. 19131 Allen in Ann. Miss. Bot. Gard. 25: 393. 1938.——Litsea variabilis Hemsl. var.tonkinensis Lec. Fl. Gen. Indoch. 5: 134. 1914.

与原变种不同在于小枝、叶下面和叶柄均密被灰黄色贴伏柔毛，叶片椭圆形或长圆形，长7-14厘米，宽2.5-4厘米，叶下面粉绿，网脉不明显。

产广西西部及西南部、云南东南部。生于山坡密林中，海拔600-900米。越南也有分布。
