黑鳞耳蕨系
6.**Polystichum ser. Makinoiana** H. S. Kung & Li Bing Zhang Acta Phytotax. Sin. 34: 194. 1996.

## Description
Plants medium-sized, evergreen. Lamina green, broadly lanceolate or elliptic-lanceolate, papery, not gradually contracted toward base. Stipe greenish when dry. Stipe scales bicolorous, ovate, ovate-lanceolate, or broadly lanceolate. Rachis scales linear, subulate, or lanceolate. Pinnules of upper pinnae auriculate. Microscales short-type.

  "Statistics": "About 17 species: Sino-Himalayan and Sino-Japanese regions; 11 species (five endemic) in China.
