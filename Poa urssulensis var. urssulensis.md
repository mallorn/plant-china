乌苏里早熟禾(原变种)",
74a.**Poa urssulensis var. urssulensis**",

## Description
Ligule 0.5–1.5(–2) mm. Panicle spreading to contracted; rachilla warty, ciliate, or pilose; lemma callus webbed to almost glabrous. 2*n* = 28, 42.

> Open grassy and rocky slopes, thickets; (300–)1000–3200(–4200) m. Gansu, Heilongjiang, Nei Mongol, Xinjiang, Xizang [Kazakhstan, Mongolia, Russia; Europe].
