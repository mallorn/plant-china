## Description
Herbs low, &amp;#177; stemless. Leaves long petiolate, shallowly crenate lobed to pinnatipartite. Flowers axillary. Calyx deeply cleft anteriorly, 2- or 3-lobed. Corolla tube longer than calyx, mostly more than 2 X as long, expanded apically; beak long; lower lip large.
