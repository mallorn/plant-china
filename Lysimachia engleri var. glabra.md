## Description
Stems erect, not arcuately elongate, 15--30 cm. Leaf blade 2--4 X 1.2--2 cm. Corolla ca. 7 mm, ca. as long as to slightly longer than calyx. Fl. May.

> * Clearings, margins of mountain woodlands; ca. 2400 m. N and W Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Lysimachia deltoidea&lt;/I&gt; Wight var. &lt;I&gt;glabra&lt;/I&gt; Bonati, Bull. Soc. Bot. France 56: 466. 1909; &lt;I&gt;L. plicata&lt;/I&gt; Franchet ex R. Knuth.
