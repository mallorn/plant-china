1a.**Pseudolysimachion linariifolium subsp. linariifolium**",

## Description
Leaves alternate or sometimes lower ones opposite; leaf blade linear to linear-oblong, 2-6 X 0.2-1 cm, margin basally entire and apically dentate, occasionally entire. Fl. Jun-Sep.

> Meadows, thickets, sparse forests; 200-2100 m. Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol [Japan, Korea, Mongolia, Russia (Far East, E Siberia)].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Veronica linariifolia&lt;/I&gt; Pallas ex Link, Jahrb. Gewachsk. 1(3): 35. 1820; &lt;I&gt;V&lt;/I&gt;. &lt;I&gt;angustifolia&lt;/I&gt;Fischer ex Link.
