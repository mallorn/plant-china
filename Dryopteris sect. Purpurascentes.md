## Description
Lamina 3- or 4-pinnate; pinnules far apart from each other. Base of stipe clothed with linear-lanceolate, wrinkled, golden scales.
  "Statistics": "About 13 species: mainly in S Asia, Australia, Pacific islands (including Hawaiian Islands), and Africa, one species in South America; one species in China.
