## Description
Stem solitary, erect, to 25 cm tall, proximally glabrous, distally purplish brown pilose. Basal leaves long petiolate, reniform, 1.3-2.5 cm in diam., palmately veined, palmatipartite; lobes 6-12, oblong, glabrous or abaxially sparsely purplish red pilose, very rarely purplish brown pilose on both surfaces, apex mucronate. Middle to distal stem leaves petiolate or sessile, reniform to linear. Capitulum solitary, nodding. Involucre hemispheric, 3.8-6.3 cm in diam., outside glabrous, base blackish purple pilose; phyllaries in 2 rows, oblong or lanceolate, ca. 1.5 cm, margin membranous, apex obtuse. Ray florets purplish red; lamina broadly oblanceolate, apex subtruncate, 2-4-lobed; tube shorter. Tubular florets numerous, purplish red. Achenes short. Pappus white, as long as tubular corolla. Fl. and fr. Jun-Sep.
  "Statistics": "Alpine meadows, gravelly areas on mountains; 3000-4000 m. S Xizang [Bhutan, India (Sikkim)].

**8．掌叶垂头菊**

Cremanthodium palmatum Benth. in Hook. Icon. Pl. 1142. 1873; C. B. Clarke. Comp. Ind. 168. 1876; Hook. f. Fl. Brit. lnd. 3: 331. 1881; Good in Journ. Linn. Soc. Bot. 48: 271. 1929, excl. subsp. rhodocephalum (Diels) Good; S. Y. Hu in Quart. Journ. Taiwan Mus. 19: 212. 1966; H. Koyama in Menu. Fac. Sci. Kyoto Univ. Ser. Biol. 2: 54. 1968. ——C. palmatum subsp. benthami Good, l. c. 48: 271. 1929.

多年生草本。根肉质。茎单生，直立，高达25厘米，上部被紫褐色有节柔毛，下部光滑。基生叶具长柄，叶片肾形，直径1.25-2.5厘米，掌状分裂，裂片6-12，长圆形，先端具骨质小尖头，边缘具紫红色柔毛，两面光滑或下面有疏的紫红色柔毛，叶脉掌状；茎中上部叶具柄或否，叶片肾形至线形，向上渐小。头状花序单生，下垂，辐射状，总苞半球形，基部被黑紫色有节柔毛，直径3.75-6.25厘米（包括舌状花），总苞片2层，长圆形或披针形，长约15毫米，先端钝，边缘膜质，背部光滑。舌状花紫红色，舌片宽倒披针形，先端近平截，2-4浅裂，管部较短；管状花紫红色，多数，冠毛白色，与花冠等长。瘦果短。（未见标本）

产我国西藏南部。生于海拔3000-4000米的高山草地、高山流石滩。在锡金（模式标本产地）、不丹也有分布。
