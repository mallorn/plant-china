库叶堇菜(原变种)",
11a.**Viola sacchalinensis var. sacchalinensis**",

> In FRPS (51: 24. 1991), the name *Viola mauritii* Teplouchow was misapplied to this taxon.

## Description
Rhizome usually not covered by dead leaf stipules. Stem usually longer, to 20 cm tall. Stipules ovate-lanceolate or narrowly ovate, margin densely fimbriate-denticulate. Petals bluish violet, oblong-ovate, apex obtuse; lateral petals distinctly bearded; spur 2.5-3 cm. Stigma papillose. Fl. Apr-Jun, fr. Jun-Sep. 2*n* = 20.

> Forests, forest margins; 400-2000 m. Heilongjiang, Jilin, Nei Mongol [Japan, Korea, Mongolia, Russia].
  "Synonym": "*Viola komarovii* W. Becker; *V. mariae* W. Becker; *V.* *miranda* W. Becker.
