雾灵山
85c.**Scutellaria scordifolia var. wulingshanensis** (Nakai & Kitagawa) C. Y. Wu & W. T. Wang

## Description
Stems densely strigose. Leaves triangular-ovate to lanceolate, 4-14 mm wide, adaxially densely strigose.


> * Deciduous broad-leaved forests; 1500-1700 m. Hebei, Shanxi.

  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Scutellaria wulingshanensis&lt;/I&gt; Nakai &amp; Kitagawa, Rep. Exped. Manchoukuo Sect. IV, 1: 53. 1934.

**85c. 雾灵山变种**

var. wulingshanensis (Nakai & Kitagawa) C. Y. Wu et W. T. Wang, comb. nov. ——Scutellaria wulingshanensis Nakai et Kitagawa in Rep. First Sci. Exp. Manch. Sect. 4, 1: 53. cum fig. 1934; Kitagawa, Lineam. Fl. Mansh. 387. 1937; 刘慎谔等, 东北植物检索表 324. 1959.

这一变种与原变种不同在于茎上部及叶上面毛被较密，且系具节长伏毛。

产河北（兴隆、承德）及山西（霍县）；见于落叶林下，海拔1500-1650米。模式标本采自河北雾灵山。
