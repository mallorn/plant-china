硬毛火炭母",
63d.**Polygonum chinense var. hispidum** J. D. Hooker",

## Description
Stems and branches retrorsely hispid. Leaf blade ovate to elliptic, both surfaces hispid. Fl. Jul-Oct, fr. Jul-Nov.

> Mountain slopes, thickets in valleys; 600-2800 m. Guangxi, Guizhou, Hunan, Sichuan, Yunnan [India, Myanmar, Thailand].
  "Synonym": "*Polygonum* *chinense* f. *hispidum* (J. D. Hooker) Samuelsson.
**63d.硬毛火炭母（变种）**

Polygonum chinense L. var. hispidum Hook. f. Fl. Brit. Ind. 5: 44. 1886.——P. chinense L. f. hispidum (Hook. f.) Sam. in Hand.-Mazz. Symb. Sin. 7: 184. 1929.

本变种与原变种的区别是叶两面被糙硬毛；茎、枝具倒生糙硬毛。

产湖南、四川、贵州、广西及云南。生山坡草地、山谷灌丛，海拔600-2800米。印度也有。
