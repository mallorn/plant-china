假铁角蕨叶系",
88.**Pedicularis ser. Pseudoasplenifoliae** Prain",

## Description
Herbs low. Stems unbranched. Leaves mostly basal, in a rosette, long petiolate; leaf blades lanceolate, pinnatipartite or pinnatisect. Stem leaves few or absent. Inflorescences short, few flowered. Calyx lobes 5, unequal, serrate. Corolla tube longer than calyx but less than 2 X as long; galea with 1 auricle on each margin; beak straight, erose-denticulate at apex.
