## Description
Leaflets glabrous adaxially. Fl. and fr. May-Aug.

> * Forests, rock clefts on slopes; 2000-3700 m. SW Sichuan (Yanbian Xian), N Yunnan (Huize Xian, Lijiang Naxi Zu Zizhixian).
**3b.光腹鬼灯檠 图版4:8-10**

Rodgersia sambucifolia Hemsl. var. estrigosa J. T. Pan in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 29(2): 189. 1991.

与原变种之区别是其小叶片腹面无糙伏毛。花果期5-8月。

产四川（盐边）和云南（会泽、丽江）。生于海拔2000-3700米的林下或山坡石隙。模式标本采自四川（盐边）。
