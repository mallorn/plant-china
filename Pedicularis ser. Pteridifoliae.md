蕨叶系",
36.**Pedicularis ser. Pteridifoliae** H. L. Li",

> One species.

## Description
Stems to 28 cm, unbranched. Basal leaves long petiolate, large, pinnatisect; segments 5--9 pairs. Stem leaves few, alternate, small. Inflorescences short spicate. Calyx 5-lobed. Corolla yellow; tube straight, 2--3 X as long as calyx; galea obscurely 2-toothed, beakless; lower lip shorter than galea.
