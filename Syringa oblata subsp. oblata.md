紫丁香 (原亚种)",
13a.**Syringa oblata subsp. oblata**",

> Widely cultivated in several other provinces.&#x0D;\n&lt;p&gt;&#x0D;\nWhite-flowered plants, which are cultivated in provinces north of the Chang Jiang, have been recognized as cultivar alba.

## Description
Shrubs or small trees to 5 m. Leaf blade ovate-orbicular to reniform, 2.5-10(-14) &amp;#215; 2.5-8(-15) cm, base truncate to usually subcordate, apex abruptly acute to acuminate. Panicles congested or lax, 4-16(-20) &amp;#215; 3-7(-10) cm. Calyx ca. 3 mm. Corolla purple or sometimes white; tube 0.6-1.4 cm; lobes ovate-orbicular to obovate-orbicular, 4-6 mm. Anthers inserted 0-4 mm from the corolla mouth. Capsule 1-1.5(-2) cm. Fl. Apr-May, fr. Jun-Oct. 2&lt;I&gt;n&lt;/I&gt; = 46*.

> * Woods, thickets on slopes, valleys, along streams or roadsides; 300-2600 m. Gansu, Hebei, Henan, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi, NW Sichuan
