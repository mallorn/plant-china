峨山对囊蕨(原变种)",
26a.**Deparia wilsonii var. wilsonii**",

## Description
Pinna segments subentire or crenulate at margin. 2*n* = 80*.

> ● Damp areas, forests on mountain slopes, streamsides; 1400-3700 m. S Gansu, Guizhou, W Hubei, Sichuan, SE Xizang, NW Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Athyrium wilsonii* Christ, Bull. Herb. Boissier, sér. 2, 3: 512. 1903; *Lunathyrium wilsonii* (Christ) Ching.
