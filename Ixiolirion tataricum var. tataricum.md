鸢尾蒜 (原变种)",
1a.**Ixiolirion tataricum var. tataricum**",

## Description
Perianth azure to dark violet, segments 1--7 mm wide, not connivent proximally into a pseudotube. Fl. Apr--Jun.

> Valleys, sandy places, waste grasslands. N Xinjiang [Afghanistan, Kazakhstan, Pakistan, Russia, Turkmenistan].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt; Amaryllis tatarica &lt;/I&gt;Pallas, Reise Russ. Reich. 3: 727. 1776.
