## Description
Herbs, rarely shrubs. Leaves 3-veined from base of blade, rarely main lateral veins arising above base. Inflorescences unisexual. Male perianth lobes (2-)4, valvate; female perianth lobes 3, usually unequal.
  "Statistics": "About 370 species: worldwide in tropical, subtropical, and rarely temperate regions; 58 species (25 endemic) in China.
