锐尖凹瓣梅花草",
27b.**Parnassia mysorensis var. aucta** Diels",

## Description
Basal leaf blade ovate-oblong, base subcordate or truncate.

> * Scrub, grassy slopes; 3200-3300 m. NW Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Parnassia affinis&lt;/I&gt; J. D. Hooker &amp; Thomson var. &lt;I&gt;aucta&lt;/I&gt; (Diels) Nekrassova.
**26b. 锐尖凹瓣梅花草（变种）**

var. aucta Diels in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 5: 281. 1912; Ku in Bull. Bot. Res. North-East. For. Unuv. 7 (1): 36. 1987. ——P. affinis Hook. f. et Thoms. var. aucta Diels in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 25. VI. p. 281. 1912; Nekrass. in Bull. Soc. Bot. France 74: 650. 1927.

本变种主要特征：基生叶片卵状长圆形，先端渐尖，基部截形或微心形。

产云南西北部。生于灌丛中或草坡上，海拔3200-3300米。模式标本采自云南大理。
