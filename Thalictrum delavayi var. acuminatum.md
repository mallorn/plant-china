> Wang (Acta Phytotax. Sin. 31: 214. 1993) recognized two forms under var. &lt;I&gt; acuminatum &lt;/I&gt;: f. &lt;I&gt; acuminatum&lt;/I&gt; (Franchet) W. T. Wang, with anther mucro 0.1–0.25(–0.4) mm, and f. &lt;I&gt; appendiculatum&lt;/I&gt; W. T. Wang, with anther mucro 0.5–0.7 mm.

## Description
Sepals narrowly elliptic to narrowly ovate, 9.5--14 × 4--5 mm. Anther mucro 0.1--0.7 mm.

> * Shady places, along streams; ca. 1800 m. SW Sichuan (Dechang Xian), NW Yunnan (Heqing Xian).
