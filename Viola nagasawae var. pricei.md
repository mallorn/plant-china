锐叶台北堇菜",
31b.**Viola nagasawae var. pricei** (W. Becker) J. C. Wang & T. C. Huang",

## Description
Leaf blade triangular-ovate to lanceolate, both surfaces stiffly hairy, apex acute. Sepals sparsely ciliate or glabrous along margin. Lateral petals bearded. 2*n* = 48*.

> ● Grassy slopes; below 500 m. Taiwan.
  "Synonym": "*Viola pricei* W. Becker, Bull. Misc. Inform. Kew 1928: 252. 1928.
