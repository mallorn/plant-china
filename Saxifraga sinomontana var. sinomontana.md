山地虎耳草(原变种)
86a.**Saxifraga sinomontana var. sinomontana**

## Description
Basal leaves glabrous adaxially. Fl. and fr. May-Oct.


> Scrub, alpine meadows, marshy meadows, rock crevices; 2700-5300 m. S Gansu, Qinghai, S Shaanxi (Taibai Shan), W Sichuan, Xinjiang, E and S Xizang, NW Yunnan [Bhutan, N India, Kashmir, Nepal, Sikkim].
