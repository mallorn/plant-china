长叶兰(原变种)",
14a.**Cymbidium erythraeum var. erythraeum**",

## Description
Sepals and petals green, heavily flushed red-brown; lip pale yellow or white with red-brown venation on lateral lobes and red-brown spots on mid-lobe, 30-43 cm.
  "Statistics": "Trees and rocks at forest margins and in forests; 1400-2800 m. C and SW Guizhou, SW Sichuan, SE Xizang, NW and SE Yunnan [Bhutan, India, Myanmar, Nepal].
  "Synonym": "*Cyperorchis longifolia* (D. Don) Schlechter.
