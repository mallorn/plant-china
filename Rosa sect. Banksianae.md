## Description
Shrubs evergreen, climbing, prickly or not. Leaflets 3–7; stipules caducous, free, subulate. Flowers numerous in umbels or in compound corymbs. Sepals deciduous, reflexed, margin entire or lobed. Styles free, slightly exserted.
  "Statistics": "Two species: China, Laos, Vietnam; two species (one endemic) in China.
