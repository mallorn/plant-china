亚欧唐松草(原变种)",
37a.**Thalictrum minus var. minus**",

## Description
Leaflet blade 0.7--1.5 × 0.4--1.3 cm, abaxially greenish; veins raised abaxially. Pedicel 3--8 mm. Fl. Jun--Jul.

> Forests, scrub, slopes, field margins; 1400--2700 m. Gansu, Qinghai, Shanxi, Xinjiang [temperate Asia and Europe].
