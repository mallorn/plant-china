## Description
Plants medium-sized, evergreen. Stipe greenish when dry. Stipe scales concolorous, linear to linear-lanceolate. Lamina green, broadly lanceolate or elliptic-lanceolate, papery. Rachis scales linear and tortuous. Pinnules of upper pinnae auriculate. Microscales short-type.Four species: Sino-Himalayan region; four species (three endemic) in China.
