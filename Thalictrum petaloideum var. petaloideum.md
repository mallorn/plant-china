瓣蕊唐松草(原变种)",
42a.**Thalictrum petaloideum var. petaloideum**",

## Description
Leaflet blade rhombic, obovate, or orbicular. Fl. Jun--Jul.

> Grassy slopes; 700--3000 m. E Anhui, Gansu, Hebei, W Henan, Heilongjiang, Hubei, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, E Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi, NW Sichuan, Zhejiang [Korea, Mongolia, Russia (Siberia)].
