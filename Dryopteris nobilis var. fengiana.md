## Description
Plants 60-70 cm tall. Lamina lanceolate, 40-50 cm, thinly leathery; pinnules not lobed, dentate or shallowly incised only.

> ● Evergreen broad-leaved forests; ca. 1600 m. NW Yunnan (Gongshan).
**67b.冯氏鳞毛蕨（变种）**

Dryopteris nobilis Ching var. fengiana Ching in Bull. Fam Mem. Inst. Biot. Bot. 9: 67. 1941; 横断山区维管植物上册129. 1993.

本变种与原变种的区别在于形体较小，叶片披针形，长40-50厘米，中部宽约10-20厘米，末回小羽片不分裂仅有锯齿或浅缺刻，成熟后叶片薄革质。

产云南贡山。生于山坡常绿阔叶林下，海拔1600米。
