> Ching (Icon. Filic. Sin. 4: t. 156. 1937) misidentified this species as *Lindsaea lobbiana* Hooker (Sp. Fil. 1: 205. 1846), a species described from Java, Indonesia.

## Description
Rhizomes shortly creeping, densely scaly; scales ± spreading, castaneous, 2 or 3(or 4) cells wide at base, acicular at apex. Fronds approximate; stipe stramineous, 5-10 cm, quadrangular; lamina linear, 20-30 × (0.7-)1.5-2 cm, herbaceous, 1-pinnate; pinnae 40-50 pairs, dimidiate, rhomboid or cuneate toward apex, crenate or shallowly lobed on upper margin, incisions reaching just beyond level of receptacle, apex rounded or obtuse-acute, straight on upper margin; veins free, evident. Sori marginal, terminal on 2-4 veins; indusia linear, interrupted. 2*n* = 94*.

> On rocks beside streams in forests; 200-600 m. Guangdong, Hainan, Jiangxi, S Taiwan (Pingdong, Taidong) [Bangladesh, Bhutan, India, Japan, Malaysia, Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam; Pacific islands].

Lindsaea lucida Bl. Enum. Pl. Jav. (1828) 216; Holt. in Gard. Bull. Str. Settl. IX (1934) 131; Tard.-Blot et C. Chr. in Fl. Indo-Chine VII, ii (1939) 122; Holt. Fl. Mal. II, Ferns Mal. (1954) 328, f. 187 ——Lindsaea lobbiana Hook. Sp. Fil. I (1846) 205, t. 62C; C. Chr. Ind. Fil. Suppl. III (1934) 122; Ching, Ic. Fil. Sinic. IV (1937) t. 156——Lindsaea crenulata Fee, Gen. Fil. (1850-52) 105, t. 28, f. 2 ——Lindsaea propria v. A. v. R. in Bull. Jard. But. Buitenz. ser. 2, XVI (1914) 20.

植株高达40厘米。根状茎短而横走，先端被棕色小鳞片。叶近生；叶柄长4-10厘米，禾稈色，有四稜，光滑；叶片线状披针形，长20-30厘米，宽2-2.5厘米，一回羽状；羽片35-40对，下部各羽片远离，中上部的接近，但稀呈复瓦状，互生，开展，有短柄，对开式，近长方形，长1厘米，宽约6毫米，基部楔形，先端钝圆, 下缘平直，稍向上弯，内缘几直切，接近叶轴，上缘稍呈弧形，带有2-4个浅缺刻，每裂片上横生孢子囊羣1枚。叶脉二又分枝，上面不显，下面明显。叶草质，干后为淡绿色，稍有光泽，叶轴禾稈色，有四稜，上面有狭沟，下面平或稍下凹。孢子囊群中断为3-5个，囊群盖狭，其外缘平直，宽不达羽片边缘。

产海南岛（南部及西南部）。生林下或溪边石上，海拔200-300米以下。越南、泰国、骊来亚至印度尼西亚也有分布。
