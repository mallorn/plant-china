30.**Lepisorus megasorus** (C. Christensen) Ching Bull. Fan Mem. Inst. Biol. 4: 79. 1933.",

## Description
Rhizome long creeping, 2-4 mm in diam., usually naked except for scales at apex and near phyllopodia; scales brown with paler margins, ovate-lanceolate, 2-4 × 1-1.5 mm, basifixed, broad base contrasting with long acuminate apex, margin minutely denticulate; lumina subrhomboid to rectangular. Fronds 0.5-2 cm apart; stipe straw-colored to deep brown, 5-25 cm, 1-2 mm in diam.; lamina lanceolate, 15-40 × 1.5-4 cm, widest toward base, leathery or softly leathery, with scales orbicular or lanceolate; lumina brown and hyaline; veinlets obscure. Sori midway between costa and margins, 3-3.5 mm in diam.; paraphyses 0.5-0.8 mm in diam.

> ● On tree trunks in forests; 500-1900 m. Taiwan.
  "Synonym": "*Polypodium megasorum* C. Christensen, Index Filic. 544. 1906, based on *Polypodium macrosorum* Baker, J. Bot. 23: 106. 1885, not Fée (1850); *P. hypochrysum* Hayata.
**48.长柄瓦韦（植物分类学报）**

Lepisorus petiolatus Y. X. Lin in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 31(5): 475. 1993.

植株高8-15厘米。根状茎横走，密被鳞片；鳞片披针形，网眼大而透明，边缘有锯齿。叶远生；叶柄长2-6厘米，禾秆色；叶片披针形，长5-9厘米，中部以下宽8-12厘米，向上渐狭，圆钝头，基部楔形，下延，干后两面均为棕色，纸质。主脉上下均隆起，小脉不显。孢子囊群近圆形，聚生于叶片上半部，幼时被隔丝覆盖。

特产广西（田阳）。
