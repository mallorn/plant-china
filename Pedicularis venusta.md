## Description
Herbs perennial, 10--40 cm tall. Stems usually single, erect, unbranched, often slender, long woolly. Basal leaf petiole ca. 1/2 to as long as leaf blade, pubescent; leaf blade lanceolate, pinnatisect; segments oblong, pinnatipartite, dentate, teeth callose. Stem leaves similar to basal ones but smaller, distal ones shorter petiolate. Inflorescences oblong, dense or elongated spikes, often scabrously long woolly; proximal bracts ca. as long as calyx, leaflike, middle ones pinnately 3--5-lobed. Pedicel &amp;#177; absent. Calyx campanulate, 8--10 mm, &amp;#177; leathery; lobes 5, broadly triangular, wider than long. Corolla yellow, 2--2.5 cm; tube erect, falcate apically, shorter than galea; galea short, beakless; lower lip slightly shorter than galea, not ciliate. 2 filaments pubescent, 2 glabrous. Capsule compressed, obliquely oblong, 1--1.2 cm, apiculate. Fl. Jun--Jul, fr. Jul--Aug. 2&lt;I&gt;n&lt;/I&gt; = 16.

> Meadows, frequently in alkaline places. Heilongjiang, Nei Mongol, Xinjiang [Mongolia, Russia (Siberia, Far East)].
**193.秀丽马先蒿**

Pedicularis venusta Schangan ex Bunge, in Bull. Acad. St. Petersb. VIII (1841) 252, nomen, et in Bull. Phys.-Math. Acad. St. Petersb. I (1843) 380; Maxim. in Bull. Acad. 1St. Petersb. XXIV (1877) 80, et XXXII (1888) 610, excl. var. et f. 148, et in Mel. Biol. X (1877) 121, et XII (1888) 906, excl. var. et f. 148: Limpr. in Repert. Sp. Nov. XX (1924) 218, pro parte; Li, in Proc. Acad. Nat. Sci. Philad. CI (1949) 19, pro parte; Vved. in Fl. URSS XXII (1955) 769.

多年生草本，根短缩。具有等径的长纤维根。茎通常单条，直立，不分枝，常纤细，被有抽长的卷毛，高可达10-40厘米。叶基生者具有被细长毛的叶柄，柄短于叶片长度2倍多至几相等，上面无毛，下面沿脉有长卷毛，叶片披针形，羽状全裂，裂片疏距，长圆形，渐尖，羽状深裂，小裂片具细而胼胝质的细尖，缘有其胼胝的牙齿，茎生叶向上渐小，下部者有短柄，与基生者相似，上部者则极小。花序长圆形而稠密或伸长，常被粗糙的长卷毛；苞片约与萼等长，下部者与上叶相似，中部者羽状3-5浅裂，而在中间的裂片较大，上部者很小，有胼胝质锯齿或全缘；花梗几不存在；萼钟形，长8-10毫米，近于革质，脉有叉分的短细支脉，齿5枚，宽三角形，短于萼管1倍有余；花冠黄色，长20-25毫米，管伸直，略短于盔，稍向前倾斜，上部镰状弓曲，盔短，端具2齿，下唇比盔稍短，3裂，缘无毛；雄蕊花丝1对有毛。蒴果为偏斜的长圆形，长10-12毫米，顶端具凸尖。花期6-7月，果期7-8月。

我国新疆阿尔泰山及东北黑龙江流域有之（？），生于山坡禾草丛中，亦分布于苏联西伯利亚和蒙古人民共和国。

林泊利许和李惠林氏所提的中国北部的分布，是根据Putjata氏 (China borealis,Chihli, inter Tung kia ying tze et Wi lien Kou in declivibus septentr. 12 Junii 1891) 所采的标本的；不过我们所看到的从列宁格勒借来的这一号标本，棣属于P. rubens Steph.,所以中国北部的计录是不可靠的。
