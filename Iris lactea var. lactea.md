白花马蔺 (原变种)",
21a.**Iris lactea var. lactea**",

## Description
Outer perianth segments pale violet, or milky white with purple veins; inner segments pale violet. 2&lt;I&gt; n&lt;/I&gt; = 40*, 50*.

> Hillsides, grasslands, roadsides; 600--3800 m. Anhui, Gansu, Hebei, Heilongjiang, Henan, Hubei, Jiangsu, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi, Sichuan, Xinjiang, Xizang [Afghanistan, N India, Kazakhstan, Korea, Mongolia, Pakistan, Russia].
