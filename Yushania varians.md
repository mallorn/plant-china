庐山玉山竹",
19.**Yushania varians** T. P. Yi",

## Description
Rhizome neck more than 21 cm; internodes solid, less than 8 mm in diam. Culms 1–2 m, 3–8 mm in diam.; internodes terete, 8–15 cm, initially white powdery, densely so below nodes, glabrous, not ribbed; wall 1.5–2 mm thick; supra-nodal ridge level or prominent; sheath scar prominent, yellow-brown setose. Branches 1–7, erect or ascending. Culm sheath persistent, purple-brown, narrowly rounded-triangular, ca. 1/2 as long as internodes to equal in length, leathery, sparsely appressed brown setose, setae especially dense at base, margins glabrous or initially brown setulose, longitudinal ribs prominent; auricles absent; oral setae absent or present; ligule arcuate or truncate, 1–1.5 mm, glabrous; blade erect or rarely revolute, linear-lanceolate, glabrous. Leaves 2–7 per ultimate branch; sheath glabrous; auricles erect, narrowly rounded or small; oral setae 2–4, radiating, purple or yellow, 2–5 mm; ligule truncate, ca. 1 mm, glabrous; blade linear-lanceolate, 6–11.5 × 0.8–1.2 cm, glabrous, secondary veins 3–5-paired, transverse veins distinct, base cuneate, one margin densely serrulate, other sparsely so, apex long acuminate. Inflorescence unknown. New shoots May–Jul.

> * About 1400 m. N Jiangxi.
**15.庐山玉山竹（竹子研究汇刊）图版144：1-5**

Yushania varians Yi in Journ. Bamb. Res. 5(1): 38. pl. 13. 1986.

竿柄长达21厘米以上，粗3-6毫米。竿高1-2米，粗3-8毫米；节间长8-15厘米，圆筒形，无毛，初时被白粉而在节下方一圈尤浓厚，平滑，竿壁厚1.5-2毫米；箨环隆起，通常生有向下的黄褐色刺毛；竿环平坦或在分枝之节隆起而高于箨环；节内长3-4毫米。竿每节(1）3-7枝，枝直立或上举，直径1-3.5毫米，无毛。箨鞘宿存，革质，长椭圆状三角形，长约为节间的1/2以上乃至与节间近等长，背面有或稀或密的黄褐色贴生刺毛（在鞘基部尤密），纵肋稍明显乃至明显，边缘无毛或初时有黄褐色小刺毛；箨耳无，鞘口两肩无繸毛或各有1-2条长1-2.5毫米黄褐色直立繸毛；箨舌微作圆拱形或截形，无毛，高1-1.5毫米；箨片直立或稀外翻，线状披针形，基底较箨鞘顶端为窄，无毛，干后常内卷。小枝具叶(2) 3-5 (7)片；叶鞘长2.5-4.2厘米，边缘通常无纤毛；叶耳长圆形或微小，直立，边缘有2-4条长2-5毫米紫色至黄褐色放射状开展的繸毛，叶舌截平，无毛，高约1毫米；叶柄长1.5-2毫米；叶片线状披针形，无毛，长6-11.5厘米，宽8-12毫米，先端长渐尖，基部楔形，次脉3-5对，小横脉较清晰，叶缘一侧毛状小锯齿较密，另一侧则较稀疏。花枝未见。笋期5-7月。

产江西北部。生于海拔1400米的松林下。模式标本采自庐山白沙河。
