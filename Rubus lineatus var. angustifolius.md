狭叶绢毛悬钩子",
91b.**Rubus lineatus var. angustifolius** J. D. Hooker",

## Description
Pedicel and abaxial surface of calyx with dense, persistent silvery hairs. Blade of leaflets narrowly lanceolate.

> Mixed forests; 1800--2800 m. NW Yunnan.
**86b.狭叶绢毛悬钩子（变种）**

Rubus lineatus Reinw, var. angustifolius Hook. f. Fl. Brit. Ind. 2: 333. 1878 “angustifolia”; Focke, Bibl. Bot. 83: 24. f. 4 (91). 1914.

产云南西北部。生杂木林下，海拔1800-2800米。

本变种与原变种区别在于叶片较小，狭披针形。
