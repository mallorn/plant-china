## Description
Leaves glabrous. Calyx hispid.

> Tropical evergreen forests, thickets; 500-800 m. Yunnan [India, Myanmar, Nepal, Thailand].
**2c．糙叶变种**

Leucas mollissima Wall. var. scaberula Hook. f. Fl. Brit. Ind. 4: 682. 1885; Mukerj. Lab Ind. 185. 1940; Murata in Hara Fl. E. Himal. 278. 1966, ibid. 2nd. Rep. 117. 1971, in Act. Phytotax. Geobot. 24 (3): 83. 1969.

这一变种与原变种不同在于叶较秃净；萼有刚毛。

产云南南部；生于热带常绿林及灌丛中，海拔500-840米。印度，尼泊尔，锡金，缅甸，泰国也有。等模式标本采自印度。
