毛三裂蛇葡萄",
8b.**Ampelopsis delavayana var. setulosa** (Diels & Gilg) C. L. Li",

## Description
Branchlets, petioles, and inflorescence densely ferruginous pubescent. Leaves 3-foliolate. Fl. Jun-Jul, fr. Sep-Nov.

> ●  Forests, hillsides; 500-2200 m. Gansu, Guizhou, Hebei, Henan, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Ampelopsis aconitifolia* Bunge var. *setulosa* Diels &amp; Gilg, Bot. Jahrb. Syst. 29: 465. 1900; *A. delavayana* var. *gentiliana* (H.
