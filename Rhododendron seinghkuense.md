黄花泡泡叶杜鹃",
3.**Rhododendron seinghkuense** Kingdon Ward ex Hutchinson",

## Description
Prostrate or upright shrubs, often epiphytic, 0.3–0.9 m tall. Petiole 4–13 mm, densely woolly; leaf blade ovate, ovate-lanceolate, oblong-lanceolate or oblong-elliptic, 3–8 × 1.5–4 cm; base rounded or obtuse; apex acuminate, acute or obtuse; abaxial surface densely brown-woolly, scales distant, very small; adaxial surface bullate, scaly, sparsely pubescent. Inflorescence 1- or 2-flowered. Pedicel 1–2 cm, densely woolly; calyx pale green, lobes 5–9 mm, rounded, persisting to enclose mature capsule, scales sometimes present on margin and abaxially, densely woolly; corolla shortly campanulate, yellow, 1.5–2.5 cm; outer surface scaly, glabrescent; stamens 10, slightly shorter than corolla; ovary densely woolly, scaly; style short, sharply deflexed, usually without scales or hairs. Capsule ellipsoid, 8–14 mm, scaly, densely brown-woolly. Fl. Apr–May, fr. Aug–Nov.

> Dense forests, epiphytic on trees, cliffs, shady rocks; (1800–) 2000–2500(–3500) m. SE Xizang, NW Yunnan [N Myanmar].
**3．黄花泡叶杜鹃（西藏植物志）图版4：4-5**

Rhododendron seinghkuense K. Ward ex Hutch. in Stevenson, Spec. Rhodod. 234. 1930; K. Ward in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 16: 174. 1931, latine; Cullen in ibid 39: 27. 1980; Davidian, Rhodod. Spec. 1: 128. t. 14. 1982; 西藏植物志3: 632. 1986; 云南植物志4: 464. 图版122, 5-7. 1986; 横断山区维管植物下册1422. 1994.

平卧或直立灌木，常为附生，高0.3-0.9米。小枝密被褐色、下部硬毛状上部绵毛状长毛，覆盖着鳞片或无。叶卵形、卵状披针形、长圆状披针形或长圆状椭圆形，长3-8厘米，宽1.5-4厘米，顶端突尖、短渐尖或钝，基部圆形或钝，上面泡泡状拱起，无毛或有疏毛，有脱落的鳞片，下面密被黄褐色、下部硬毛状上部绵毛状长毛，密被鳞片，鳞片小，有间距，为毛被所覆盖；叶柄长0.4-1.3厘米，密被褐色绵毛。花序顶生，有花1-2朵，花梗长1-2厘米，密被褐色绵毛；花萼淡绿色，长5-9毫米，5深裂，裂片近圆形，外面及边缘密被绵毛，有少数脱落的鳞片；花冠短钟状，无香气，黄色，长1.5-2.5厘米，外面无毛或筒部散生柔毛，被鳞片；雄蕊10枚，不等长，稍短于花冠；子房5-6室，密被棕褐色硬毛状绵毛，被鳞片，花柱短而弓弯，无毛或仅基部被毛，无鳞片。蒴果椭圆形，长0.8-1.4厘米，密被棕褐色硬毛状绵毛，为宿萼所覆被。花期4-5月，果期11月。

产云南西北部、西藏东南部。附生于密林、混交林或灌丛中的岩石或树上，海拔1 800-3 500米。缅甸北部也有。
