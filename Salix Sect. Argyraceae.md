## Description
Shrubs. Leaf blade oblong, oblong-obovate, or obovate, abaxially densely grayish white silky. Male flower: gland solitary, adaxial; stamens 2, distinct, glabrous. Female flower: gland longer then stipe; ovary gray tomentose; style ca. 1 mm.
