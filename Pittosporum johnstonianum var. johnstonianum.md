滇西海桐(原变种)",
40a.**Pittosporum johnstonianum var. johnstonianum**",

## Description
Infructescences laxly compoundly umbellate; peduncle 2.5–3 cm; pedicels 0.5–1.5 cm. Fl. Mar–May, fr. May–Nov.

> Forests, thickets, slopes; 1200--3100 m. SW Sichuan (Muli Zangzu Zizhixian), NW Yunnan (Gongshan Drungzu Nuzu Zizhixian, Zhongdian Xian) [N Myanmar].
