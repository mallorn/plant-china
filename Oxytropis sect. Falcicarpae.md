## Description
Herbs, caulescent or acaulescent, glandular viscid. Leaves imparipinnate; leaflets not or rarely verticillate. Racemes 2-10-flow-ered; peduncle 1.5-10 cm or longer, shorter than to equaling leaves. Corolla standard usually orbicular, not contracted in middle, not widened at basal half, and without auricles.
