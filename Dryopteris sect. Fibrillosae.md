## Description
Lamina 1-pinnate. Pinnae deeply incised to nearly 2-pinnate. Lateral veins mostly forked, few simple. Abaxial surface of rachis, costa, and pinnae clothed with fibrillose scales.
  "Statistics": "About ten species: mainly in the Himalaya; seven species (three endemic) in China.
