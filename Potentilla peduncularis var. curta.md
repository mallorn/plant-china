22d.**Potentilla peduncularis var. curta** (Soják) H. Ikeda & H. Ohba",

## Description
Leaflets narrowly obovate, 0.5–2 cm, margin 4(or 5)-serrate or apex dentate. Fl. and fr. Jun–Oct.

> Alpine meadows, gravelly slopes; 3400--4800 m. Xizang [Sikkim].
  "Synonym": "*Potentilla curta* Soják, Bot. Jarhb. Syst. 116: 215. 1994.
