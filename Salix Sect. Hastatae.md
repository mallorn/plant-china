## Description
Shrubs, rarely small trees. Stipules often present; leaf blade ovate, elliptic, reniform, or suborbicular, usually glabrous, margin serrate. Male flower: gland solitary, adaxial; stamens 2; filaments distinct, glabrous. Female flower: gland as in male flower; ovary glabrous, stipitate; style conspicuous to subsessile.
