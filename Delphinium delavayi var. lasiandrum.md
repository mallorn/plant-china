毛蕊翠雀花",
79c.**Delphinium delavayi var. lasiandrum** W. T. Wang",

## Description
Stem and petioles spreading hispid; hairs 2--3 mm. Leaf blade central lobe broadly rhombic. Raceme strigose and yellow glandular puberulent. Filaments with 1 or 2 hairs on each side.

> * Forests, forest margins; 3500--3800 m. NW Yunnan (Lijiang Naxi Zu Zizhixian).
