流苏梨籐竹",
1b.**Melocalamus compactiflorus var. fimbriatus** (Hsueh & C. M. Hui) D. Z. Li & Z. H. Guo",

## Description
Plant shorter, with thicker culms. Culm sheath ligule much longer, fimbriate; leaves larger.

> * Forested hills; 1000–1700 m. S Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Melocalamus fimbriatus* Hsueh &amp; C. M. Hui, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 30: 167. 1992.
