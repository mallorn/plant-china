## Description
Petiole dilated at apex; leaf blade narrowly triangular-ovate, base truncate to shallowly cordate, margin sparsely serrate. Racemes terminal or axillary, composed of verticillasters.

> * Valley streamsides, moist forests; ca. 500 m. Guizhou.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Dracocephalum kaitcheense&lt;/I&gt; H. Léveillé, Repert. Spec. Nov. Regni Veg. 8: 422. 1910.
**3b. 长叶变种**

Dr. kaitcheense Uvl.——Meehania urticaefolica auct. non (Miq.) Makino: 孙雄才, 科学社生物所论文集 7：234. 1932, p. p. quoad Tsiang 5728, 5747.

这一变种与原变种不同在于叶狭三角状卵圆形，基部平截至浅心形，疏生锯齿，叶柄顶端宽展；花序顶生或腋生，为由聚伞花序组成的长总状花序。

产贵州（都匀、凯里、安龙）；生于山谷溪边或林下湿润处，海拔500米左右。模式本采自贵州凯里。
