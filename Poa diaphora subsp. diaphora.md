阿尔泰旱禾(原亚种)",
12a.**Poa diaphora subsp. diaphora**",

## Description
Lemmas glabrous or scantily hairy near base of veins. Anthers 0.4–0.6 mm.

> Borders of streams and drying ponds, stony slopes; 1300–4000 m. Xinjiang, Xizang [Afghanistan, NW India, Kashmir, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Pakistan, Russia, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan; SW Asia].
