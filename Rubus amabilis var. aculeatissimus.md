## Description
Abaxial surface of calyx with dense needle-like prickles, with sparse, stalked glands. Aggregate fruit narrowly ovoid, 0.9–1.4 cm.

> Forest margins, roadsides; 1900--2600 m. Chongqing (Nanchuan Xian), N Sichuan (Pingwu Xian).
**40c.刺萼秀丽莓 （变种）**

Rubus amabilis Focke var. aculeatissimus Yu et Lu,植物分类学报20(3): 301. 1982.

花萼密被针刺和稀疏腺毛；花梗具稀疏腺毛；果实直径10-15毫米。

产四川 (平武、南川）。生路旁林边，海拔1900-2600米。
