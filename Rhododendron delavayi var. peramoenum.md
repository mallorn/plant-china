狭叶马缨杜鹃",
338b.**Rhododendron delavayi var. peramoenum** (I. B. Balfour & Forrest) T. L. Ming",

## Description
Leaf blade very narrowly lanceolate, 7.5–9.5(–15) × 1–2 cm; apex acute to shortly cuspidate; abaxial surface indumentum gray to fawn, somewhat thin, compact, persistent; adaxial surface much less rugose. Corolla ca. 5 cm; style glabrous.

> Mixed forests, evergreen broad-leaved forests, forest margins, thickets; 1700–3200 m. W Guizhou, SE Xizang, W Yunnan [NE India, Myanmar].

本变种与原变种的区别在于叶狭长披针形，坚硬，长7.5-9.5（-15）厘米，宽1-2厘米，先端急尖至短尾状，下面有薄层淡黄色宿存而略胶结的毛被。

产贵州西部、云南西部和西藏东南部。生于海拔1700-2600米的常绿阔叶林或针阔叶混交林中。缅甸和印度东北部也有分布。模式标本采自云南腾冲。在贵州系本变种分布的新记录。
