腺毛莓叶悬钩子",
199c.**Rubus fragarioides var. adenophorus** Franchet",

## Description
Blade of leaflets and abaxial surface of calyx soft hairy. Flowering branchlets, pedicels, or base of calyx with soft hairs and glandular hairs.

> Grasslands on slopes, forests; 3000--4000 m. W Sichuan, SE Xizang, NW Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Rubus franchetianus* H. Léveillé; *R. yui* E. Walker.
**191c.腺毛莓叶悬钩子（变种）（西藏植物志）图版31：1-3**

Rubus fragarioides Bertol. var. adenophorus Franch. Pl. Delav. 203. 1890 “adeno. phora”——R. franchetianus Levl. in Bull. Acad. Geog. Bot. 20 (Mem. ) : 71. 1909; FockeBibl. Bot. 83: 16. 1914. ——R. yui Walker in Journ. Wash. Acad. Sci. 32 (9) : 261. 1942

本变种叶片两面脉上和花萼有柔毛，花枝和花梗或花萼基部有腺毛和柔毛。

产四川西部、云南西北部和西藏东南部。生于山坡草地或林中，海拔3 000-4000米。
