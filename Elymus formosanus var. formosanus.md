台湾披碱草(原变种)",
19a.**Elymus formosanus var. formosanus**",

## Description
Culms 60–90 cm tall. Leaf sheath glabrous; leaf blade 0.4–0.6 cm. Spike 15–20 cm. Lemma awn 2–3.5 cm. Fl. and fr. Jun–Jul.

> * Mountain slopes. Taiwan.
  "Synonym": "*Agropyron formosanum* Honda, Bot. Mag. (Tokyo) 41: 385. 1927; *Roegneria formosana* (Honda) Ohwi; *R. formosana* var. *longearistata* Keng.
