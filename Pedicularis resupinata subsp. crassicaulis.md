粗茎返顾马先蒿",
98c.**Pedicularis resupinata subsp. crassicaulis** (Vaniot ex Bonati) P. C. Tsoong",

## Description
Plants densely pubescent. Leaves short, 2.5--3.5 cm. Corolla pink to purple; beak short, truncate. Capsule ovoid, ca. 1.2 cm. Fl. May--Jun.

> * Guangxi, Guizhou, Hubei, Sichuan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Pedicularis crassicaulis&lt;/I&gt; Vaniot ex Bonati, Bull. Acad. Int. Geogr. Bot. 13: 241. 1904; &lt;I&gt;P. resupinata&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;crassicaulis&lt;/I&gt; (Vaniot ex Bonati) H. Limpricht.
**返顾马先蒿粗茎亚种**

Pedicularis resupinata Linn. subsp. crassicaulis (Vaniot ex Bonati) Tsoong, comb. nov.——P. crassicaulis Vaniot, ex Bonati, in Bull. Acad. Geogr. Bot. XIII (1904) 241; Limpr. in Repert. Sp. Nov. XX (1924) 239; Hand. -Mazz. Symb. Sin. VII (1936) 859. ——P. resupinata Linn. var. crassicaulis (Vaniot) Bonati ex Limpr. in Repert. Sp. Nov. XXIII (927) 330; Li, in Proc. Acad. Nat. Sc. Philad. CI (1949) 52.

这一亚种与反顾亚种之别，在于其茎一般多粗壮坚挺，全身密被粗涩之毛；叶长2.5-3.5厘米，边缘胼胝不显著，喙短，先端多少截形，下缘尖端伸出，略似双齿型，果短而粗，其两室不如在反顾亚种中这样地不相称。

分布于我国湖北、四川、贵州、广西等省区。
