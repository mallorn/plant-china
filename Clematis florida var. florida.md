铁线莲(原变种)",
99a.**Clematis florida var. florida**",

## Description
Stamens normally developed, differentiated into filaments and anthers. Fl. Apr--Jun.

> * Scrub, along streams in hilly areas. Guangdong, N Guangxi, Hubei, Hunan, W Jiangxi.
