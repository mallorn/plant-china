## Description
Trees to 30 m tall; branchlets pendulous, usually slender, prominently 6-angled. Leaves slightly curved or straight on young trees, 3-4 mm.

> *  Forests on mountain slopes; 2600-3800 m. SW Sichuan, NW Yunnan
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Sabina pingii&lt;/I&gt; (W. C. Cheng ex Ferre) W. C. Cheng &amp; W. T. Wang.
