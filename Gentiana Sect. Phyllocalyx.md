叶萼组",
6.**Gentiana Sect. Phyllocalyx** (Kusnezow) T. N. Ho",

> One species: Bhutan, China, India, Myanmar, Nepal, Sikkim.

## Description
Perennials. Stems branching sympodially, with short stolons. Calyx membranous, completely surrounded by upper pair of broad obovate obtuse leaves. Stigma lobes expanded, connate or contiguous, forming a discoid or funnelform structure but free after anthesis. Seeds with a discoid wing; seed coat alveolate, with simple, shallow, and hexagonal pits.
