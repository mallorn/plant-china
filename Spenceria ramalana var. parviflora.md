小花马蹄黄",
1b.**Spenceria ramalana var. parviflora** (Stapf) Kitamura",

## Description
Flowers ca. 1 cm in diam. Sepals 3–4 × ca. 1 mm; epicalyx segments 1.2–1.8 × 1–1.5 mm. Petals 4–4.5 × 2.5–3 mm.

> Alpine meadows; 3000--5000 m. Xizang [Bhutan].
  "Synonym": "*Spenceria parviflora* Stapf, Bot. Mag. 149: t. 9007. 1923.
