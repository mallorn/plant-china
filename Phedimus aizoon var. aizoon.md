费菜(原变种)",
2a.**Phedimus aizoon var. aizoon**",

> The plants are used medicinally.

## Description
Plants smooth. Leaves leaf blade narrowly lanceolate, elliptic-lanceolate, or ovate-oblanceolate, 1.2-2 cm wide, apex acute. Fl. Jun-Jul, fr. Aug-Sep.

> Scrub, ravine edges in valleys, field banks, rock crevices; 2500-3100 m. Anhui, Gansu, Hebei, Heilongjiang, Henan, Hubei, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi, Sichuan, Zhejiang [Korea, Japan, Mongolia, Russia].
