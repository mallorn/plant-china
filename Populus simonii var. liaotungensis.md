辽东小叶杨",
18c.**Populus simonii var. liaotungensis** (C. Wang & Skvortzov) C. Wang & S. L. Tung",

## Description
Petiole pilose; leaf blade pubescent along veins. Catkin rachis pubescent. Capsule pubescent, shortly stipitate.

> *  Plains, alluvial deposits. Hebei, Liaoning, Nei Mongol
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Populus liaotungensis&lt;/I&gt; C. Wang &amp; Skvortzov in Liou &amp; al., Ill. Man. Woody Pl. N.-E. China 119. 1955; &lt;I&gt;P. simonii&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;breviamenta&lt;/I&gt; T. Y. Sun; &lt;I&gt;P. simonii&lt;/I&gt; f. &lt;I&gt;liaotungensis&lt;/I&gt; (C. Wang &amp; Skvortzov) Kitagawa.
**16c．辽东小叶杨（变种）辽东杨（东北木本植物图志）图版6：8**

var. liaotungensis (C. Wang et Skv.) C. Wang et Tung, comb. nov.——P. liaotungensis C. Wang et Skv. 刘慎谔等, 东北木本植物图志, 119. 图版19: 1-4. 图版22: 40. 1955.——P. simonii Carr. f. liaotungensis (C. Wang et Skv.) Kitag. Neo-Lineam. Fl. Mansh. 203. 1979.

沿叶脉生有短柔毛，叶柄有稀柔毛。果序轴有短柔毛；蒴果具有短柔毛的短柄。

产辽宁、河北、内蒙古等地。常在河滩沙地上生长。模式标本采自辽宁省金县。

用途及繁殖方法同小叶杨。
