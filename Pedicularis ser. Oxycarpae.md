## Description
Stems erect, branched near base or unbranched, rarely branched apically. Leaves basal and on stem, alternate or pseudo-opposite, oblong to lanceolate, often pinnatisect. Inflorescences racemose or spicate, usually elongated. Calyx lobes 3--5. Corolla tube longer than but less than 2 X as long as calyx; galea twisted or not; beak sometimes twisted, long, bent downward.
