## Description
Trees 6--12 m tall. Branchlets blackish brown, glabrous. Petiole 1 cm; leaf blade narrowly elliptic to oblong, 10--15 X 2.5--4 cm, glabrous, base cuneate, margin very narrowly revolute, apex acuminate, lateral veins 12--15 per side, reticulate veinlets inconspicuous. Male flowers in congested 1--3-flowered cymes, fragrant; pedicel very short, pubescent; calyx cup-shaped, 5--7 mm, very sparsely pubescent to subglabrous, lobes 5--7 and reduced to minute glandlike teeth; corolla ca. 1.5 cm, tubular-campanulate, tube 6--8 mm and glabrous; corolla lobes 5 or 6, ovate-lanceolate, 6--8 X 2.5--3.5 mm; stamens ca. 24, inserted at base of tube. Female flowers not seen. Fruit solitary. Fruiting pedicel ca. 2 mm, stout, densely brown pubescent. Fruiting calyx ca. 1.6 cm in diam., glabrous; lobes 4 or 5, erect, reflexed between lobes, ovate, 6--8 X 9--10 mm, undulately plicate, apex acute and hard when dry. Immature berries globose, often with persistent style base, ca. 1.5 cm in diam., 5-locular, glabrous; style short, persistent at apex. Fl. May-Jul, fr. Jul-Aug.

> * Shaded ravines and thickets, mountains or by streams; 1800--2700 m. SW Yunnan.
**21.腾冲柿（云南热带亚热带植物区系研究报告）**

Diospyros forrestii Arth. in Not. Bot. Gard, Edinb. 25: 196. 1934.

乔木，高达6-12米；小枝黑褐色、，无毛。叶革质，长圆披针形，长10-15厘米，宽2.5-4厘米，先端短渐尖，基部楔形；叶柄长1厘米。雄花1-5朵，生于腋生的很短的．聚伞花序上，芳香；花萼杯状，长5-7毫米，5-7齿裂，有柔毛；花冠管状钟形，管长8毫米，6裂，裂片卵形，长6毫米，宽2.5毫米；雄蕊约24枚，着生在花冠管基部，花药线形，长6毫米，花丝长2毫米，无毛；退化子房小；花梗很短，有柔毛。果（未成熟）球形，直径1.5厘米，腋生，单生，无毛，5室，顶端有宿存的短花柱；宿存萼5浅裂，裂片近圆形，宽约1厘米，长约6毫米。花期5-7月，果期7-8月。

产云南西部（腾冲、怒江一带）；生于山谷荫处，山上灌丛中或涧畔灌丛中，海拔1800-2700米处。
