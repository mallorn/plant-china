疏花长柄山蚂蝗(原亚种)",
4a.**Hylodesmum laxum subsp. laxum**",

## Description
Leaves sparse on or clustered at lower part of stem; ter-minal leaflet elliptic or ovate, 5-12 × 5-5.5 cm. Legume stipe ca. 1 cm. 2*n* = 22.
  "Statistics": "Broad-leaved forests on mountain slopes; 700-1400 m. Fujian, Guangdong, Guizhou, Hainan, Hubei, Hunan, ?Jiangxi, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, India, Indonesia, Japan, Laos, Nepal, Philippines, Sri Lanka, Thailand, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Desmodium laxum* Candolle, Ann. Sci. Nat. (Paris) 4: 102. 1825; *D. podocarpum* Candolle var. *laxum* (Candolle) Baker; *Podocarpium laxum* (Candolle) Y. C. Yang &amp; P. H. Huang.
