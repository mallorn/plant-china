发黄白头翁",
5c.**Pulsatilla patens subsp. flavescens** (Linnaeus) Miller",

## Description
Leaf blade round-ovate or 5-sided, 5.5--7 × 8--11 cm; central leaflet with a 0.6--1.4 cm petiolule; leaf blade lobes finely divided with ultimate lobes linear-lanceolate to narrowly linear. Sepals milk-white to yellowish. Persistent styles 3.5--4.8 cm.

> N Xinjiang [Mongolia, E Russia].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Anemone flavescens&lt;/I&gt; Zuccarini, Regensb. Zeit. 1: 371. 1826; &lt;I&gt;P. flavescens&lt;/I&gt; (Zuccarini) Juzepczuk.
