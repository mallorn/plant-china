短促",
38c.**Scutellaria pekinensis var. transitra** (Makino) H. Hara ex H. W. Li",

## Description
Stems green, subglabrous or sparsely pubescent. Leaves sparsely strigose, sometimes finely pubescent on abaxial veins. Calyx glandular pubescent. Corolla 1.7-2.2 cm.

> Shaded areas in forests; 100-1300 m. Anhui, Fujian, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Zhejiang [Japan, Korea].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Scutellaria transitra&lt;/I&gt; Makino, Bot. Mag. (Tokyo) 18: 70. 1904; &lt;I&gt;S. ussuriensis&lt;/I&gt; (Regel) Kudo var. &lt;I&gt;transitra&lt;/I&gt; (Makino) Nakai.
**38c. 短促变种**

var. transitra (Makino) Hara ex Ohwi, Fl. Jap. 1000. 1956——Scutellaria transitra Makino in Bot. Mag. Tokyo 18: 70. 1904——Scutellaria ussuriensis (Korna rov) Kudo var. transitra (Makino) Nakai in Bot Mag. Tokyo 35: 199. 1921; Kudo in Mecn. rac. Sci. Agr. Taihoku Univ. 2: 258. 1929.

这一变种与原变种不同在于茎及叶柄几无毛或具白色上曲细短柔毛；叶两面极疏生糙伏毛，下面有时沿脉上具细短柔毛；花萼与序轴被具腺平展短柔毛。

产安徽，江苏，浙江，江西，福建及湖南；生于林下荫处，海拔130-1300米。朝鲜，日本也有。模式标本采自日本。
