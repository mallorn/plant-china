## Description
Small trees. Young branchlets and petioles glabrous. Leaves clustered at apex of branchlet; petiole 6-10 mm; leaf blade oblong, 3.5-6 × 1.3-2.3 cm, glabrous on both surfaces, glaucous abaxially, triplinerved, lateral veins 2 or 3 pairs, base obtuse to acute, apex obtuse. Pedicels ca. 3 mm. Perianth tube densely hispid; segments 4, oblong, 2.5-3 mm, apex obtuse or acute, ciliate. Male flowers: fertile stamens 6; filaments pilose at base, of 3rd whorls each with 2 glands at base; rudimentary pistil cylindric. Female flowers: staminodes 6, of 1st and 2nd whorls linear-clavate, hispid, of 3rd whorls sagittate, each with 2 glands at base; ovary ellipsoid, glabrous; style hispid. Fruit ovoid, glabrous.

**45.南仁山新木姜子（存疑种）（台湾植物志）**

Neolitsea hiiranensis Liu et Liao in Quart. Journ. Taiwan. Mus. 24: 409, f. l. 1971; C. E. Chang in Fl. Taiwan 2: 453. pl. 370. 1976.

小乔木。小枝无毛。叶簇生于小枝顶端，长圆形，长3.5-6厘米，宽1.3-2.3厘米，先端钝，基部钝至锐尖，革质，两面无毛，下面苍白色，三出脉，叶片上部还有2-3对侧脉；叶柄长0.6-1厘米，无毛。花雌雄异株，组成伞形花序；总苞片脱落。雄花：花梗长3毫米，花被筒外面密被长硬毛，花被裂片4，长圆形，先端钝或锐尖，长2.5-3毫米，具缘毛；能育雄蕊6，第一、二轮花丝长4毫米，基部有疏柔毛；退化子房圆柱形。雌花：退化雄蕊6，第一、二轮线状棒形，有长硬毛，第三轮箭头形，有腺体；花柱有长硬毛；子房无毛，椭圆形。果卵珠形，无毛。

产我国台湾南部（南仁山）。

据《台湾植物志》载，本种与大武山新木姜子N. daibuensis Kamikoti相近，但叶先端钝而不同，因未见标本，附志于此，留待考订。
