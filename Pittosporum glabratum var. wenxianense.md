文县海桐",
9c.**Pittosporum glabratum var. wenxianense** (G. H. Wang & Y. S. Lian) Z. Y. Zhang & Turland",

## Description
Leaf blade obovate to narrowly so, 3.5–7 × 2–3.5 cm, apex truncate to obtuse. Fr. Aug.

> About 1100 m. S Gansu (Wen Xian).
  "Synonym": "*Pittosporum wenxianense* G. H. Wang &amp; Y. S. Lian, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 34: 210. 1996.
