陇东海棠(原变种)",
16a.**Malus kansuensis var. kansuensis**",

## Description
Pedicel and leaves abaxially sparsely puberulous; hypanthium villous outside.

> Mixed forests, scrub; 1500--3000 m. Gansu, Henan, Shaanxi, Sichuan.
  "Synonym": "*Pyrus kansuensis* Batalin, Trudy Imp. S.-Peterburgsk. Bot. Sada 13: 94. 1893; *Eriolobus kansuensis* (Batalin) C. K. Schneider.
