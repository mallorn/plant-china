## Description
Peduncle, pedicel, and ovary with very dense long white hairs. Dorsal sepal 2.2-2.9 cm wide, pale green-yellow, centrally with a deep maroon and darker-veined area. Floral bract much longer than pedicel and ovary. Fl. Sep-Nov.
