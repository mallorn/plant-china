## Description
Winter buds with a pair of adnate scales. Inflorescence a compound umbel-like cyme, pedicellate, with large sterile radiant flowers. Corolla rotate. Fruit turning red, maturing black or red; pyrenes with 1 broad ventral groove; albumen solid.
