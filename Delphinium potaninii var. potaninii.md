黑水翠雀花(原变种)",
92a.**Delphinium potaninii var. potaninii**",

## Description
Raceme 14--30 cm, 6--15-flowered. Bracteoles narrowly linear or filiform, 2--7 × 0.2--0.4 mm. Spur recurved. Ovary glabrous, rarely sparsely puberulent.

> * Slopes in forests; 1800--3300 m. S Gansu, S Shaanxi, E and NW Sichuan.
