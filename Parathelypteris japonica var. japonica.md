光脚金星蕨(原变种)",
19a.**Parathelypteris japonica var. japonica**",

## Description
Stipes and rachises nearly black at bases, distally castaneous-brown, pinnae abaxially and indusia sparsely pubescent, indusia more glaucous, pubescent.

> Shaded places in forests; 200-2000 m. N Fujian, Guizhou, N Jiangsu, Jiangxi, W Sichuan, Taiwan [Japan, S Korea].
