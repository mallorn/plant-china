## Description
Plants 5--20 cm tall. Petiole 2--4 mm; leaf blade lanceolate to linear-lanceolate, 15--25 X 3--6 mm. Corolla 5--6 mm; tube ca. 1 mm; lobes obovate-elliptic, ca. 4 mm wide. Fl. May-Jun.

> * Grassy mountain slopes, cliffs; 400--900 m. SE Gansu, SW Shanxi, N Sichuan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Lysimachia brunnelloides&lt;/I&gt; Pax &amp; K. Hoffmann, Repert. Spec. Nov. Regni Veg. 17: 194. 1921; &lt;I&gt;L. deltoidea&lt;/I&gt; Wight var. &lt;I&gt;brunnelloides&lt;/I&gt; (Pax &amp; K. Hoffmann) Handel-Mazzetti.
