峨眉含笑(原亚种)",
11a.**Michelia wilsonii subsp. wilsonii**",

## Description
Trees, to 20 m tall. Petiole with a 2-4 mm stipular scar. Fl. Mar-May, fr. Aug-Sep. 2*n* = 38*.
  "Statistics": "● Forests; 600-2000 m. Guizhou, SW Hubei, Jiangxi, C and W Sichuan, SE Yunnan.
