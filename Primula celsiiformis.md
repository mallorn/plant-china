显脉报春",
47.**Primula celsiiformis** I. B. Balfour",

## Description
Herbs perennial. Leaves forming a rosette; petiole 4--15 cm, densely short hirsute and short-stalked glandular; leaf blade oblong to oblong-elliptic, 4--10 X 2--5 cm, abaxially copiously fulvous short hirsute, adaxially short hirsute intermixed with long white hairs, base cordate to subcordate, margin repand-crenate with obscure denticulate crenations, apex rounded to obtuse; reticulate veins elevated abaxially. Scapes 30--40 cm, densely short hirsute and short-stalked glandular; racemes solitary, elongate, many flowered; bracts linear to lanceolate or oblanceolate, 4--6 mm, enlarging to 1.2 cm in fruit, glandular pubescent. Pedicel 0.7--1.5 cm, elongating to 2 cm in fruit, densely scurfy short pubescent. Flowers heterostylous. Calyx broadly campanulate, 7--8 mm, enlarging to 2 cm in fruit, densely short hirsute, glandular, parted ± to middle; lobes broadly triangular to ovate, leaflike, with elevated veins. Corolla violet; tube ca. 1.2 cm, short glandular pubescent outside; limb 1.3--2.5 cm wide; lobes obovate, deeply emarginate. Pin flowers: stamens ca. 3 mm above base of corolla tube; style 7--8 mm. Capsule subglobose, 4--5 mm in diam. Fl. May.

> * Mountains, in rock crevices; 600--2500 m. SW Sichuan, NE Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Primula racemosa&lt;/I&gt; H. Léveillé.
