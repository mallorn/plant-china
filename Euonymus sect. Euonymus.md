## Description
Shrubs to small trees, sometimes shrublike or rarely climbing, evergreen or deciduous. Flowers usually 4-merous, occasionally 5-merous. Fruit rugose, without prickles and wings, not globose, opening by 4 or 5 lobes at maturity.
