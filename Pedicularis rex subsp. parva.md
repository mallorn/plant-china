矮小大王马先蒿",
88b.**Pedicularis rex subsp. parva** (Bonati) P. C. Tsoong",

## Description
Plants barely 20 cm tall. Calyx 2- or 3-lobed. Corolla yellow, ca. 3 cm. Fl. May--Jul.

> * NW Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Pedicularis rex&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;parva&lt;/I&gt; Bonati, Notes Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinburgh 15: 151. 1926.
**大王马先蒿矮小亚种**

Pedicularis rex C. B. Clarke subsp. parea (Bonati) Tsoong, comb. nov. ——P. rex C. B. Clarke var. parva Bonati, in Notes Bot. Gard. Edinb. XV (1926) 151; Limpr. in Repert. Sp. Nov. XIII (1927) 334; Li, in Proc. Acad. Nat. Sci. Philad. C (1948) 339.

这一亚种之别，在体较低矮，高仅20厘米，其萼有清晰的萼齿两三枚。

产我国云南西北部。
