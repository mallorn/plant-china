三裂毛茛",
25c.**Ranunculus hirtellus var. orientalis** W. T. Wang",

## Description
Stems 4.5--15(--20) cm. Basal leaves with petiole 1--5 cm; blade 3-sect or 3-partite, sometimes 3-fid nearly to middle, 0.5--1.4 × 0.8--2 cm. Flowers 0.9--1.2 cm in diam. Receptacle glabrous, rarely with a few hairs. Achene glabrous. Fl. Jun--Aug.

> * Meadows, gravelly slopes, by streams, on rocks; 3000--5000 m. E Qinghai, W Sichuan, S Xizang, NW Yunnan.
