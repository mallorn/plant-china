浅黄皱褶马先蒿",
127b.**Pedicularis plicata subsp. luteola** (H. L. Li) P. C. Tsoong",

## Description
Leaves wide. Inflorescences densely villous. Corolla ca. 2 cm; galea narrower, truncate at apex.

> * Moist stony pastures; ca. 3700 m. NW Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Pedicularis luteola&lt;/I&gt; H. L. Li, Proc. Acad. Nat. Sci. Philadelphia 100: 331. 1948.
**皱褶马先蒿浅黄亚种**

Pedicularis plicata Maxim. subsp. luteola (Li) Tsong, comb. Nov--P. luteola Li, in Proc. Acad. Nat. Sci. Philad. C (1948) 331, f. 54.

这一亚种的不同，在于叶稍较宽，花序之毛较多，花一般较小，盔下缘尖锐而方。

产我国云南西北部，生于海拔3660米的潮湿多石草地中。
