紫柄蕨(原变种)",
7a.**Pseudophegopteris pyrrhorhachis var. pyrrhorhachis**",

> *“Polypodium brunneum”* (Wallich, Numer. List, no. 333. 1829) belongs here but is a nomen nudum and was not therefore validly published (*Melbourne Code*, Art. 38.1(a)).

## Description
Laminae abaxially with dense short acicular hairs along costae, costules, and veins, often hairy between veins.

> Forests beside streams; 800-2400 m. Chongqing, Fujian, S Gansu, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan [Bhutan, N India, Myanmar, Nepal, Sri Lanka, Vietnam].
