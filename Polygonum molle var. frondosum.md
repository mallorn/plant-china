光叶神血宁",
79c.**Polygonum molle var. frondosum** (Meisner) A. J. Li",

## Description
Stems and leaves glabrous.

> Thickets in valleys, grassy slopes; 1200-1500 m. Guangxi, Guizhou, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, Indonesia, Nepal, Sikkim].
  "Synonym": "*Polygonum* *frondosum* Meisner in Candolle, Prodr. 14(1): 137. 1856; *Aconogonon* *molle* (D. Don) H. Hara var. *frondosum* (Meisner) H. Hara; *A*. *molle* var. *paniculatum* (Blume) Yonekura &amp; H. Ohashi; *A*. *paniculatum* (Blume) Haraldson; Polygonum *paniculatum* Blume; *P*. *paniculatum* var. *frondosum* (Meisner) Steward.
**93c.光叶蓼（变种）**

本变种与原变种的区别是茎、叶皆无毛。

产广西、贵州、云南、西藏。生山坡灌丛、山坡草地，海拔1200-2500米。印度西北部也有。
