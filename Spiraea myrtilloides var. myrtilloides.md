细枝绣线菊(原变种)",
52a.**Spiraea myrtilloides var. myrtilloides**",

## Description
Follicles glabrous or puberulous only on adaxial suture.

> Mixed forests, valleys, slopes; 1500--3100 m. Gansu, Henan, Hubei, Jiangxi, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Spiraea microphylla* H. Léveillé; *S. virgata* Franchet.
