轮叶赤楠",
37b.**Syzygium buxifolium var. verticillatum** C. Chen",

## Description
Branchlets 4- or 6-angled. Leaves ternate especially apically on branchlets or opposite; leaf blade broadly elliptic, elliptic, orbicular, or sometimes obovate, 1-2.5 × 0.5-1(-2.2) cm, secondary and intramarginal veins adaxially sometimes depressed, base cuneate to broadly cuneate, apex broadly acute, obtuse, or rounded.

> ● Scrub on mountain slopes, hills; 200-1200 m. Anhui, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hunan, Jiangxi.
