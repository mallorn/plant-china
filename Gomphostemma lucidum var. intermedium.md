中间
8b.**Gomphostemma lucidum var. intermedium** (Craib) C. Y. Wu

## Description
Shrubs erect. Petiole 2-2.5 cm; leaf blade obovate-elliptic, adaxially hirsute and somewhat scabrid with fine stellate hairs, abaxially densely gray or tawny stellate tomentose.


> Forested valleys, grassy streamside slopes; 500-1400 m. Yunnan [Laos, Vietnam].

  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Gomphostemma intermedium&lt;/I&gt; Craib, Bull. Misc. Inform. 23. 1910.

**8b．中间变种 图版22:7-17**

Gomphostemma lucidum Wall. var. intermedium (Craib) C. Y. Wu in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 10 (2): 145. 1965——Gomphostemma intermedaum Craib in Kew Bull. 23. 1910.

这一变种与原变种不同在于直立灌木；叶倒卵状椭圆形，上面被细星状毛及混生的单硬毛，略粗糙，下面密被灰色或污黄色星状绒毛，叶柄长2-2.5厘米。

产云南东南部；生于沟谷林内及溪边草坡上，海拔500-1400米。越南，泰国也有。模式标本采自泰国。
