大花秦艽",
21b.**Gentiana macrophylla var. fetissowii** (Regel & Winkler) Ma & K. C. Hsia",

## Description
Calyx ca. 1/2 as long as corolla. Corolla tubular, 2-2.5 (-2.8) cm, lobes 3.5-4.5 mm. Fl. and fr. Jul-Oct.

> River banks, roadsides, grassland slopes; 600-3700 m. Hebei, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi [Kazakhstan].
