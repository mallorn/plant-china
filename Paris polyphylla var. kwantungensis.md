6j.**Paris polyphylla var. kwantungensis** (R. H. Miao) S. C. Chen & S. Yun Liang",

## Description
Leaves 7. Inner tepals ca. 6 cm, equaling outer ones. Filaments to 10 mm; anthers ca. 15 mm; free portion of connective ca. 1 mm. Stigma lobes usually 7.

> * SW Guangdong (Xinyi Xian).
