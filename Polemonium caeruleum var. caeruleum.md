花荵(原变种)",
1a.**Polemonium caeruleum var. caeruleum**",

## Description
Calyx lobes oblong to narrowly ovate, apex obtuse. Corolla lobes rounded, usually glabrous. Fl. Jun-Jul, fr. Jul-Sep. 2&lt;I&gt;n&lt;/I&gt; = 18.

> Streambanks, open slopes, open sites in forests; (1000-)1700-3700 m. Xinjiang, NW Yunnan [India, Japan, Mongolia, Pakistan, Russia; Europe].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Polemonium laxiflorum&lt;/I&gt; (Regel) Kitamura.
