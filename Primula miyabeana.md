## Description
Herbs perennial. Leaves forming a rosette; petiole 1/3--1/2 as long as leaf blade, broadly winged; leaf blade obovate-elliptic to oblanceolate, 10--20 X 3--5 cm, tapering to base, margin irregularly denticulate, apex rounded. Scapes 20--45 cm, efarinose; umbels 2--4, superimposed, 6--10-flowered; bracts linear, 1--2 cm. Pedicel 1.5--3(--4) cm. Flowers homostylous. Calyx campanulate, 5--7 mm, yellow farinose inside, parted 1/3 to near middle; lobes triangular, apex acuminate. Corolla purple; tube 9--10 mm; limb ca. 1.5 cm wide; lobes oblong, ca. 5 mm, margin crenate-serrate, apex emarginate. Stamens ca. 7 mm above base of corolla tube; style reaching base of anthers. Capsule globose, nearly as long as calyx. Fl. Jun-Jul. 2&lt;I&gt;n&lt;/I&gt; = 22@.

> * Damp areas in forests; 2500--3500 m. Taiwan
**114.玉山灯台报春（新拟）玉山樱草（台湾植物志）**

Primula miyabeana Ito et Kawakami in Miyabe Festschrift 1, t. 29. 1911; W. W. Smith et Fletcher in Trans. dot. Soc. Fdinb. 33: 161. 1941: Kao et DeVol in Taiwania 15: 69. 1970: DeVol in Fl. Taiwan 4: 86. pl. 925. 1978.

多年生草本。根茎短，向下发出成丛之根。叶基生，叶片倒卵状椭圆形至倒披针形，长l0-20厘米，宽3-5厘米，先端钝圆，基部渐狭窄，下延至叶柄，边缘具稍不整齐的小牙齿；叶柄长达叶片的l/3-1/2，具阔翅。花葶高20-45厘米，具伞形花序2-4轮，每轮具6-10花；苞片线形，长10-20毫米；花梗长1.5-3（4）厘米；花萼钟状，长5-7毫米，分裂近达中部，裂片三角形，先端渐尖；花紫红色，冠筒长9-10毫米，喉部具环状附属物，冠檐直径约1.5厘米，裂片长圆形，长约5毫米，先端具凹缺和浅圆齿，雄蕊着生处距冠筒基部约7毫米，柱头与花药达同一高度。蒴果球形，约与花萼等长。

产于我国台湾省（宜兰、新竹、台中、南投、嘉义、高雄等地）。生长于林下湿地，海拔2500-3500米。模式标本采自嘉义新高山。
