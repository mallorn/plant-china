短毛叶赖草
15c.**Leymus secalinus var. pubescens** (O. Fedtschenko) Tzvelev

## Description
Culms 40–100 cm tall, 3–5-noded. Leaf blade abaxial surface densely pubescent. Spike erect, grayish green, (6–)10–15 (–24) × 0.1–1.7 cm. Glumes 3-veined. Palea equaling lemma.


> Stony and aleurite slopes, alkaline swales and pebbles. Xinjiang, Xizang [Russia].

  "Synonym": "*Elymus dasystachys* var. *pubescens* O. Fedtschenko, Trudy Imp. S.-Peterburgsk. Bot. Sada 21: 435. 1903; *Leymus secali-nus* subsp. *pubescens* (O. Fedtschenko) Tzvelev.
