雪峰山梭罗",
14d.**Reevesia pubescens var. xuefengensis** C. J. Qi",

## Description
Leaf blade ovate-oblong, 12-19 × 6-8 cm, abaxially sparsely pilose or glabrous, base shallowly cordate or truncate. Flowers small. Petals ca. 5 mm. Androgynophore 1.2-1.5 cm.
