> One species: Bhutan, China, Nepal, Sikkim.

## Description
Herbs low. Roots fleshy. Stems erect. Basal leaves in a rosette. Stem leaves few, opposite, narrowly lanceolate to linear-lanceolate. Flowers few, opposite. Calyx 1/3 cleft anteriorly, 5-lobed. Corolla tube more than 2 X as long as calyx; galea longer than lower lip; beak S-shaped, slender.
