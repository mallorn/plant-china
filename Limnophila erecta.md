## Description
Annuals, 15-25 cm tall. Stems erect or ascending, simple or much branched, glabrous or hispidulous. Leaves opposite, sessile or base narrowed and appearing stalked; leaf blade linear-elliptic, 0.5-3 cm X 3-8 mm, glabrous, margin crenate and toothed, veins pinnate. Flowers solitary and axillary or in axillary or terminal racemes. Pedicel 2-4 mm, glabrous or hispidulous, recurved in fruit. Bracteoles linear, 0.5-0.8 mm. Calyx 4-5 mm, glabrous, with raised veins in fruit. Corolla white or pink, 6-7 mm, outside glabrous. Capsule ovoid, ca. 3.5 mm. Fl. and fr. Jul-Oct.

> Wet grassland, near water; below 1500 m. Guangdong, Yunnan [Indonesia, Malaysia, Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam].
**9.直立石龙尾 图版9: 1-2**

Limnophila erecta Benth. in DC. Prodr．10：388．1846；Hook．f．Fl． Brit. Ind. 4: 267. 1884；广州植物志596. 1956．

一年生草本，高15-25厘米；茎直立或上升，简单或多分枝，无毛或疏被短硬毛。 叶对生，长5-30毫米，宽3-8毫米，条状椭圆形，具圆齿状牙齿至牙齿，无柄或基部常狭成柄状，无毛，具羽状脉。花单生叶腋或成腋生或顶生的总状花序；花梗长2-4毫米，无毛至被短硬毛，在果实成熟时下弯；小苞片长0.5-0.8毫米，条形；萼长4-5 毫米，无毛，在果实成熟时，具凸起的条纹；花冠长6-7毫米，白色或粉红色，外面无毛。蒴果卵珠形，长约3.5毫米，浅褐色。花果期7-10月。

分布于广东、云南等省。生于水旁或湿草地。缅甸，马来西亚，印度尼西亚亦有。
