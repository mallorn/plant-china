毛芽椴",
10b.**Tilia tuan var. chinensis** (Szyszy&#322;owicz) Rehder & E. H. Wilson",

## Description
Branchlets and terminal buds tomentose. Leaf blade broadly ovate, 10-12 × 7-10 cm, abaxially gray stellate tomentose, base usually oblique, margin prominently dentate. Cymes 16-22-flowered. Bracts 8-12 cm, sessile.

> ●  Guizhou, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Sichuan, Zhejiang.
  "Synonym": "*Tilia miqueliana* Maximowicz var. *chinensis* Szyszy&amp;#322;owicz, Hooker’s Icon. Pl. 20: sub t. 1927. 1890; *T. oblongifolia* var. *sangzhiensis* B. R. Liao &amp; W. X. Wang.
**19b. 毛芽椴（变种）（中国高等值物图鉴）**

var. chinensis Rehd. et Wils. in Sarg. Pl. Wils. 2: 369. 1916.——T. miqueliana Maxim. var. chinensis Szyszyl. in Hook. Ic. Pl. 20: t. 1927. 1890.——T. chinensis Schneid., l. c. 384. fig. 257. l-m, non Maxim.; V. Engl. Monogr. Tilia 130. 1909.

嫩枝及顶芽有茸毛；叶阔卵形，长10-12厘米，宽7-10厘米，下面有灰色星状茸毛，边缘有明显锯齿；花序有花16-22朵；苞片长8-12厘米，无柄。果实球形。

产于江苏、浙江、湖北、四川及贵州。模式标本采自湖北长阳。
