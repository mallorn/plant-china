## Description
Annuals 2.5-6 cm tall. Stems ascending to erect, few branched from base, densely papillate. Basal leaves withered at anthesis; petiole 0.4-0.6 mm; leaf blade obovate to ovate-orbicular, 6-8 &amp;#215; 4-5 mm, densely papillate but glabrescent, margin cartilaginous, apex rounded and mucronate, midvein prominent. Stem leaves 3 or 4 pairs, folded; petiole tube 1-1.5 mm, densely papillate; leaf blade lanceolate, 5-7 &amp;#215; 2-4.5 mm, densely papillate but glabrescent, margin broadly membranous, apex acuminate and caudate, midvein abaxially prominent. Flowers few. Pedicel purple, 0.8-1.2 cm, surrounded by leaves, densely papillate. Calyx narrowly obconic, 1.3-1.5 cm, outside densely papillate but glabrescent; lobes subulate to narrowly triangular, 3.5-5.5 mm, margin broadly membranous, apex acuminate and caudate, midvein prominent. Corolla blue, with blackish stripes, narrowly obconic, 2-2.5 cm; lobes ovate, 3-4 mm, margin entire, apex acute; plicae ovate, 2-3 mm, margin entire or crenulate, apex obtuse. Stamens inserted at middle of corolla tube, unequal; filaments 3-4 mm; anthers ellipsoid, 1.2-1.5 mm. Ovary narrowly ellipsoid, 3-3.5 mm. Style 1.5-2 mm; stigma lobes oblong. Fl. Apr-Jun.

> * Hillsides; 4100-4500 m. SW Sichuan.
**164. 辐射龙胆**

Gentiana radiata Marq. in Kew Bull. 1931: 87, 1931 et 1937: 174. 1937.

一年生草本，高2-6厘米，茎黄绿色或紫红色，被细乳突或光滑，在基部有少数分枝，斜上升，基生叶大，在花期枯萎，宿存，卵形或倒卵圆形，长6-8毫米，宽4-5毫米，先端急尖或钝圆，具短小尖头，边缘软骨质及叶两面均具细乳突，中脉细，在下面突起，叶柄宽，长0.4-0.6毫米；茎生叶3-4对，稍开展，对折，疏离，短于或长于节间，披针形，长5-7毫米，宽2-4.5毫米，先端急尖或钝圆至渐尖，具小尖头，边缘膜质，宽，密生细乳突，两面被细乳突，叶柄密被细乳突，连合成长1-1.5毫米的筒。花数朵，单生于小枝顶端；花梗黄绿色或紫红色，被细乳突或光滑，长8-12毫米，裸露或藏于最上部一对叶中；花萼筒状钟形，长13-15毫米，外面密被细乳突，裂片狭三角形，长3.5-5.5毫米，先端急尖，有小尖头，边缘膜质，光滑，中脉在背面突起或否，弯缺楔形；花冠蓝色，外面具灰绿色宽条纹，漏斗形，长2-2.5厘米，裂片宽卵形，长3.5-4毫米，先端钝圆，褶卵圆形，长2-3毫米，边缘有不明显细齿或近全缘；雄蕊着生于冠筒中部，整齐，花丝丝状钻形，长3-4毫米，花药直立，椭圆形，长-1.2-1.5毫米；子房狭椭圆形，长3-3.5毫米，两端渐狭，柄长约2毫米，花柱线形，连柱头长约2毫米。花期4-6月。

产四川西南部。生于山坡，海拔4150-4460米。模式标本采自四川木里。
