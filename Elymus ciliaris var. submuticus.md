14b.**Elymus ciliaris var. submuticus** (Honda) S. L. Chen",

## Description
Leaf blade usually glabrous. Lemma hispid abaxially, margin long ciliate, apex pointed or with awn 1–3(–7) mm. Palea ciliolate along keels distally. Fl. and fr. May–Jul. 2*n* = 28.

> Mountain slopes, moist meadows, roadsides. Anhui, Hebei, Shaanxi, Shandong, Zhejiang [Japan].
  "Synonym": "*Agropyron ciliare* var. *submuticum* Honda, J. Fac. Sci. Univ. Tokyo, Sect. 3, Bot. 3(1): 27. 1930; *A. ciliare* f. *submu-ticum* (Honda) Ohwi; *Roegneria ciliaris* var. *submutica* (Honda) Keng.
**32b.短芒纤毛草（变种）**

Roegneria ciliaris (Trip.) Nevski var. submutica (Honda) Keng,耿以礼及陈守良, 南京大学学报 (生物学) 1: 62.1963; 秦岭植物志1(1) : 109. 1976; 江苏植物志 (上) : 186. 1977. ——Agropyron ciliare var. submuticum Honda in Journ. Fac. Univ. Tokyo sect. III. Bot. 3: 27. 1930.

与原变种的主要区别在于，外稃先端具1-3(7)毫米的小尖头。

产河北、山东、陕西、安徽、浙江等省。生于山坡草地。模式标本采自日本。
