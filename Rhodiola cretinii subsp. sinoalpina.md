## Description
Flowering stems 2-5 cm. Leaves elliptic-oblong to spatulate, base decurrent, margin entire, apex rounded. Flowers often 4-merous. Petals linear-oblanceolate to linear-spatulate.

> * 4300-4400 m. NW Yunnan.
