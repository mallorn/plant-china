13b.**Pyrus calleryana var. lanceata** Rehder",

## Description
Leaf blade ovate-lanceolate or oblong-lanceolate, margin shallowly serrate or nearly entire.

> Anhui, Fujian, Zhejiang.
**豆梨柳叶变种**

P. calleryatta Dcne. var. lanceolata Rehd. in Journ. Arn. Arb. 6: 28. 1926; Wils. in Journ. Arn. Arb. 8: 122. 1927.

本变种的特点，在其叶片卵状披针形或长圆披针形，具浅钝锯齿或全缘。

产安徽、浙江。模式标本采自安徽青阳至太平间。
