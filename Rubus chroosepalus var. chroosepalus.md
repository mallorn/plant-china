毛萼莓(原变种)",
107a.**Rubus chroosepalus var. chroosepalus**",

## Description
Branchlets soft hairy when young, glabrescent. Petioles glabrous; leaves gray or yellowish white tomentose abaxially. Inflorescences sericeous-villous.

> Thickets on slopes, forest margins; 300--2000 m. Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan [Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Rubus chroosepalus* var. *omeiensis* Matsuda; *R. mouyousensis* H. Léveillé; *R. petaloideus* H. Léveillé.
