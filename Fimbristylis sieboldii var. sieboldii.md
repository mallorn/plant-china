锈鳞飘拂草(原变种)",
29a.**Fimbristylis sieboldii var. sieboldii**",

## Description
Rhizomes short. Culms 20-65 cm tall, thin, stiff. Leaves with a blade. Fl. and fr. Jun-Aug.
  "Statistics": "Seashores, salty marshes; near sea level. Anhui, Fujian, Guangdong, Hainan, Jiangsu, Shandong, Taiwan, Xisha Qundao, Zhejiang [Japan, Korea].
  "Synonym": "*Fimbristylis aginkotensis* Hayata; *F. ferruginea* (Linnaeus) Vahl var. *sieboldii* (Miquel ex Franchet &amp; Savatier) Ohwi.
