二棱大麦(原变种)",
7a.**Hordeum distichon var. distichon**",

## Description
Central spikelet: caryopsis adherent to lemma and palea. Fl. and fr. Jul–Aug. 2*n* = 14.

> Cultivated as a fodder plant, or a weed in fields of *Hordeum vul-gare*. Hebei, Qinghai, Xizang [widely cultivated in temperate regions].
  "Synonym": "*Hordeum vulgare* Linnaeus convar. *distichon* (Linnaeus) Alefeld; *H. vulgare* subsp. *distichon* (Linnaeus) Koernicke; *H. vulgare* var. *distichon* (Linnaeus) J. D. Hooker.
