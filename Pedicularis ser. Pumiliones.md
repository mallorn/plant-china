## Description
Herbs low, some stemless, pubescent. Leaves in a rosette, narrowly oblong, &amp;#177; entire or mostly pinnatifid. Calyx tube very narrow, long, 2-, 3-, or 5-lobed. Corolla tube very long; galea beaked; lower lip large.
