小青杨(原变种)",
21a.**Populus pseudosimonii var. pseudosimonii**",

## Description
Branchlets slightly ascending. Catkin rachis glabrous. Capsule subsessile.

> *  Mountain slopes, valleys, along rivers; circa 2300 m. Gansu, Hebei, Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shanxi, Sichuan
