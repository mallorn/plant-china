长叶多穗神血宁",
81b.**Polygonum polystachyum var. longifolium** J. D. Hooker",

> One of us (Hong; see Pl. Syst. Evol. 186: 95–122. 1993) considers this to be a synonym of *Persicaria* *wallichii* (here treated as a synonym of *Polygonum* *polystachyum* var. *polystachyum*).

## Description
Leaf blade narrowly lanceolate or linear oblong, 8-15 × 1.5-3 cm.

> Forests, valleys; 2200-3800 m. Xizang, W Yunnan [India, Sikkim].
