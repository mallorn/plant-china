毛叶吊石苣苔",
6b.**Lysionotus heterophyllus var. mollis** W. T. Wang",

## Description
Stems densely puberulent. Petiole puberulent; leaf blade leathery, adaxially sparsely puberulent, glabrescent, abaxially puberulent, margin serrulate, sometimes ciliate. Peduncle and pedicel densely puberulent. Corolla outside sparsely puberulent. Fl. Jul-Sep.

> * Rocks in montane regions; 1600-1700 m. W Sichuan (Emei Xian, Hongya Xian).
**6b. 毛叶吊石苣苔（变种）（植物分类学报）**

L. heterophyllus var. mollis W. T. Wang in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 13(2): 69. 1975.

与异叶吊石苣苔的区别：小枝和叶片下面均被较密的短柔毛。

产四川峨眉山、洪雅。生于山地石上，海拔1700米。模式标本采自峨眉山。
