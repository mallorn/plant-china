9.**Pedicularis ser. Imbricatae** P. C. Tsoong ex H. B. Yang",

> Four species: China, Bhutan, India, Nepal, and Sikkim; one species in China.

## Description
Herbs usually 30 cm to 1 m tall, drying black. Stems erect, rarely branched. Leaves alternate, mostly on stem, sessile, linear to linear-oblong, pinnatifid. Inflorescences racemose. Corolla galea crescent-shaped, less than 3 mm wide, sparsely pubescent; beak distinct.
