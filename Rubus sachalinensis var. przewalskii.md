甘肃悬钩子",
18b.**Rubus sachalinensis var. przewalskii** (Prochanov) L. T. Lu",

## Description
Pedicel and abaxial surface of calyx with glandular hairs. Flowers and fruit 1–1.5 cm in diam. Aggregate fruit sparsely hairy or subglabrous.

> Forests; 2100--3100 m. Gansu.
  "Synonym": "*Rubus przewalskii* Prochanov, Bot. Mater. Gerb. Glavn. Bot. Sada RSFSR5: 56. 1924.
