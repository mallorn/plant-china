## Description
Herbs, acaulescent. Leaves imparipinnate; leaflets not verticillate. Racemes 3- or 4-flowered; peduncle 6-8 cm, shorter than leaves. Lamina of corolla standard oblong, widened in basal half, apex emarginate. Legume unknown for Chinese species.
