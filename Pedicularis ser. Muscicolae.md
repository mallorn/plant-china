## Description
Stems many, slender, procumbent and repent, usually branched. Leaves elliptic to lanceolate, pinnatifid to pinnatisect. Flowers axillary. Corolla long tubular; tube at least 2 X as long as calyx; galea twisted or not; beak straight or S-shaped, not bent upward, slender.
