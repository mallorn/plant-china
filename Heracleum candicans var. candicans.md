白亮独活(原变种)",
28a.**Heracleum candicans var. candicans**",

> This variety has reputed medicinal value.

## Description
Pinnae ovate-obovate, apex mucronate or obtuse. Vittae 2 on commissure.

> Sparse forests, coniferous forest margins, scrub on arid slopes and in abandoned fields, streamsides; 1800–4500 m. W Sichuan, E and S Xizang, C and N Yunnan [N India, Kashmir, Nepal, Pakistan].
