四川头花马先蒿",
213b.**Pedicularis cephalantha var. szetchuanica** Bonati",

## Description
Calyx lobes 2, equal, lanceolate, dentate or pinnatifid, apex acute. Fl. Jun--Jul.

> * Alpine meadows; 2800--4500 m. S Sichuan, Yunnan.
**头花马先蒿四川变种 图版LVI, 1-2**

Pedicularis cephalantha Franch. var. szetchuanica Bonati, in Notes Bot. Gard. Edinb. XI1I (1921) 118; Lxmpr. in Repert. Sp. Nov. XX (1924) 240; Hand.-Mazz. Symb. Sill. VII (1936) 862; Li, in Proc. Acad. Nat. Sci. Philad. CI (1949) 122.

这一变种与前一变种的区别，在于其萼齿仅两枚，披针形锐头，有齿或羽状开裂。
