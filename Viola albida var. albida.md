朝鲜堇菜(原变种)",
46a.**Viola albida var. albida**",

## Description
Leaf blade ovate or broadly ovate, 2-11 × 1.5-7 cm, both surfaces glabrous, base deeply or shallowly cordate, margin regularly obtusely serrate, teeth curved toward inside, apex acuminate or shortly acuminate; stipules ca. 1/3 adnate to petioles; flowers white, sometimes reddish inside. Fl. Apr-May, fr. Jun-Oct. 2*n* = 24.

> Mixed forests, thickets; 300-800 m. SE Liaoning [Japan, Korea].
  "Synonym": "*Viola prionantha* Bunge var. *incisa* Kitagawa, Rep. Inst. Sci. Res. Manchoukuo 6(4): 123. 1942; *V. dissecta* Ledebour subvar. *albida* (Palibin) Makino; *V. dissecta* var. *albida* (Palibin) Nakai.
