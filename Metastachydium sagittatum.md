箭叶水苏",
1.**Metastachydium sagittatum** (Regel) C. Y. Wu & H. W. Li",

## Description
Stems 40-70 cm, brown-purple or brown, with small, branched hairs denser below nodes. Petiole of basal leaves 6-8 cm, base dilated, persistent, petiole of stem leaves 2-6 cm, clasping; basal leaf blade 6-8 &amp;#215; 5.5-7.5 cm, adaxially sparsely hispid, abaxially gray, with appressed stellate hairs with unequal arms; stem leaves in 1 or 2 pairs, upper pair shorter than verticillasters. Spikes with 4 remote and 2 or 3 close verticillasters; floral leaves sessile or subsessile; bracteoles subulate, 6-9 mm, partly adnate to flowers, stellate, hispid. Calyx greenish purple, with branched and sparse glandular hairs. Corolla 1.5-2 cm, glabrous, throat and limb with branched hairs (specimens not seen).

> Hillside meadows. Xinjiang [Kyrgyzstan].

**1.箭叶水苏**

Metastachydium sagittatum (Regel) C. Y. Wu et H. W. Li in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 13 (1) : 73. 1975 ——Phlomis sagittata Regel in Act. Hort. Petrop. 6: 373 1879——Ballota sagittata (Regel) Regel in 1. c. 9 (2) : 607. 1886——Metastachys sa. gittata (Regel) Knorr. in Fl. URSS 21: 193. 1954.

多年生草本，具有斜生根茎，其上有长须根。茎高40-70厘米，钝四稜形、褐紫色或褐棕色，被有小而分枝的毛，在节下有较密集的毛被，基部有多数枯叶叶鞘。基出叶箭形，基部深心形，长6-8厘米，宽5.5-7.5厘米，边缘有大的圆锯齿或圆齿，茎生叶1-2对，最上部的变小，比轮伞花序短，叶片均上面绿色，有疏生粗刚毛，下面灰色，密被射线不等长的贴生星状毛；基出叶叶柄长6-8厘米，基部增大，茎生叶叶柄长2-6厘米，抱茎，上部的叶柄短或无。花序穗状，由4个远离及2-3个紧密接近的轮伞花序组成；苞片钻形，部分远离，部分贴近花，在基部合生达2/3，长6-9毫米，被具1-3节的粗毛及长放射线的星状毛。花萼浅绿紫色，被有分枝的毛及稀疏的腺毛。花冠紫堇色，长15-20毫米，冠筒外面目基部几达喉部无毛，喉部及冠檐被分枝的毛。（标本未见，摘自原描写）

产新疆南北；生于中山地带的草甸上。苏联也有。模式标本采自新疆伊犁谷地伊宁附近。
