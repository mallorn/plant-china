> One species: China, Sikkim.

## Description
Herbs perennial, low. Leaves few, alternate, pinnatipartite or pinnatisect. Flowers few, axillary. Calyx 5-lobed. Corolla tube ca. 2 X as long as calyx; galea falcate, with marginal teeth, beaked.
