## Description
Perennials 7-20(-25) cm tall. Stolons 3-5 cm, simple to 3branched. Stems erect, simple, glabrous. Basal leaves withered at anthesis. Stem leaves with petiole 0.8-1.2 cm; leaf blade ovate-elliptic to ovate, 2-4 &amp;#215; 0.7-1.7 cm, base obtuse, margin thick cartilaginous, apex obtuse; vein 1, abaxially distinct. Inflorescences (1 or) 2-4-flowered. Calyx obconic; tube 1.7-2.3 cm, membranous; lobes oblong to elliptic, 6-10 mm, subequal, margin cartilaginous, apex acute to obtuse and apiculate, midvein outside prominent. Corolla blue-purple, with pale yellow-green base and dark blue stripes, tubular-campanulate, 4.5-6 cm; lobes broadly ovate, 4-6 mm, margin entire, apex rounded; plicae ovate, 1.5-2.5 mm, margin denticulate or rarely entire, apex obtuse. Stamens inserted at basal part of corolla tube; filaments 1.2-1.4 cm; anthers narrowly ellipsoid, 2.5-3.5 mm. Style 4-5 mm; stigma lobes linear. Fl. and fr. Aug-Oct.

> Bog meadows, seeps on slopes; 3900-4800 m. SE Xizang [India, Nepal].
**80. 硕花龙胆**

Gentiana amplicrater Burk. in Journ. Asiat. Soc. Bengal n. ser. 2: 312. 1906; H. Smith in Kew Bull. 15 (1): 51. 1961; S. Nilsson in Grana Palyn. 7(1): 101. 1967; Hara, Chater et Williams, Enum. Fl. Pl. Nepal 3: 91. 1982. ——Gentianodes amplicrater (Burk.) A. et D. Love in Bot. Not. 125: 256. 1972.

多年生草本，高7-15厘米。根茎短缩，直立，有少数淡黄色须根。枝直立，单生或2-3个丛生，黄绿色，光滑，基部被黑褐色枯老残叶。叶密集，覆瓦状排列，稀疏离，卵形或卵状椭圆形，长2-4厘米，宽0.7-1.7厘米，两端钝，边缘软骨质，平滑；基生叶在花期枯萎，宿存。花1-4朵，顶生，基部包围于上部叶丛中；无花梗；花萼筒白色膜质，倒锥形，长1.7-2.3厘米，裂片略不整齐，椭圆形，长6-10毫米，宽3-8毫米，先端急尖，具短小尖头，边缘软骨质，平滑，中脉软骨质，在背面突起，平滑，弯缺截形；花冠上部蓝紫色，下部黄绿色，具深蓝色条纹，筒状钟形，长4.5-6厘米，裂片卵圆形，长4.5-5毫米，先端钝圆，全缘，褶整齐，卵形，长1.5-2.5毫米，先端钝，边缘具不整齐细齿，稀全缘；雄蕊着生于冠筒下部，整齐，花丝线状钻形，长13-14毫米，花药狭矩圆形，长2.5-3毫米；子房线状披针形，长1.7-2厘米，先端渐尖，基部钝，柄粗壮，长1.2-1.4厘米，花柱线形，长4-5毫米，柱头2裂，裂片外反，线形。花期8-10月。

产我国西藏东南部及南部。生于沼泽化草甸、山坡流水线处，海拔3900-4800米。模式标本采自西藏拉萨。尼泊尔、锡金也有分布。
