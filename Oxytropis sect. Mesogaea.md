## Description
Herbs, caulescent (but acaulescent in some *Oxytropis maqinensis*), with (1 or)2-6 conspicuous internodes, non-glandular (except *O. tudanensis*). Leaves with leaflets not verticillate. Racemes 6- to many flowered; peduncle 1.5-25 cm or longer, shorter to longer than leaves. Corolla standard broadly ovate, not contracted in middle, not widened at basal half, and without auricles. Legume shortly stipitate or stipitate; body cylindric, narrowly ellipsoid, ovoid, or obovoid.
