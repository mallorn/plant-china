81c.**Elymus abolinii var. divaricans** (Nevski) Tzvelev",

## Description
Spikelets 15–25 mm, with 5–7 florets. Lemma awn 15–35 mm. Fl. and fr. Jun–Sep.

> Grassy mountain slopes; 1300–1900 m. Xinjiang [Mongolia, Russia].
  "Synonym": "*Roegneria abolinii* var. *divaricans* Nevski, Trudy Sredne-Aziatsk. Gosud. Univ., Ser. 8b, Bot. 17: 68. 1934.
