水蔓菁",
1b.**Pseudolysimachion linariifolium subsp. dilatatum** (Nakai & Kitagawa) D. Y. Hong",

> Edible, used as a vegetable.

## Description
Leaves opposite at least on lower nodes; leaf blade broadly linear to ovate-orbicular, 0.5-2 cm wide, margin always dentate. Fl. Jul-Oct.

> * Meadows, thickets; 200-2100 m. Anhui, Fujian, Gansu, Guangdong, Guangxi, Hebei, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan, Zhejiang.
