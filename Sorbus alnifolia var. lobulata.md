裂叶水榆花楸",
48b.**Sorbus alnifolia var. lobulata** Rehder",

## Description
Leaf blade orbicular-ovate or suborbicular, margin usually lobed, doubly serrate. Fruit ovoid-oblong, rarely subglobose.

> Slopes, mixed forests; 700--900 m. Liaoning, Shandong [Korea].
**水榆花楸裂叶变种**

S. alnifolia (Sieb. ＆ Zucc.) K. Koch var. lobuiata Rehd. in Sarg. Pl. Wils. 2: 275, 1915.

叶片边缘有浅裂片和重锯齿。

产辽宁、山东。朝鲜也有分布。
