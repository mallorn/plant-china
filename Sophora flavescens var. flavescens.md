## Description
Branchlets and leaflets pilose or glabrescent. Corolla white or pale yellow. Legume sparsely pubescent or glabrescent.
  "Statistics": "Scrub, hill slopes, farm fields; below 1500 m. All provinces [In-dia, Japan, Korea, Russia].
  "Synonym": "*Sophora angustifolia* Siebold &amp; Zuccarini; *S. flavescens* f. *angustifolia* (Siebold &amp; Zuccarini) Yakovlev; *S. flavescens* var. *angustifolia* (Siebold &amp; Zuccarini) Kitagawa; *S. flavescens *var. *stenophylla *Hayata.
