## Description
Stem erect, 50-70 cm tall, 3-4 mm in diam. at base, proximally glabrous, distally and synflorescence white arachnoid-puberulent. Basal leaves petiolate; petiole to 25 cm, base sheathed, proximally glabrous, distally narrowly winged, white arachnoid-puberulent; leaf blade ovate-cordate, triangular-cordate, or suborbicular, 2.5-12 × 4-15 cm, thin, glabrous, pinnately veined, base shallowly cordate or cordate, margin regularly denticulate, apex rounded or acute; basal lobes triangular. Middle stem leaves shortly petiolate, base enlarged sheathed; leaf blade cordate or ovate-cordate, 2-7 × 4-7 cm. Distalmost stem leaves sessile, lanceolate. Synflorescence racemose, 12-26 cm, lax; leaflike bracts ovate, to 25 × ca. 8 mm, smaller distally, membranous, with prominent reticulate veins on both surfaces, margin denticulate, apex acuminate; peduncles 5-8 mm, slender, white arachnoid-puberulent. Capitula numerous; supplementary bracts narrowly ovate or lanceolate, as long as involucre, membranous, margin denticulate or entire. Involucre campanulate, 6-8 × 5-6 mm, outside white arachnoid-puberulent; phyllaries 7-9, in 2 rows, oblong, 2-3 mm wide, brown ciliate, apex triangular; inner phyllaries broadly membranous at margin. Ray florets 5 or 6, yellow; lamina oblong, 2-3 × ca. 1 mm; tube ca. 4 mm, slender. Tubular florets numerous, 7-8 mm; tube 2-3 mm. Pappus white, as long as tubular corolla. Fl. Jul-Sep. 2*n* = 58.

**93．总状橐吾**

Ligularia botrycdes (C. Winkl.) Hand. -Mazz. in Bot. Jahrb. 69: 126. 1938 et in Acta Hort. Gothob. 12: 303. 1938; S. Y. Hu in Quart. Journ. Taiwan Mus. 20: 55. 1967. ——Senecio botryodes C. Winkl. in Acta Hort. Petrop. 14: 154. 1895. ——Senecillis borryodes (C. Winkl.) Kitam. in Acta phytotax. Geobot. 8: 87. 1939.

多年生草本。根肉质，多数，簇生。茎直立，高50-70厘米，上部以及花序被白色蛛丝状柔毛，下部光滑，基部直径3-4毫米。丛生叶与茎下部叶具柄，柄长达25厘米，上部具窄翅，被白色蛛丝状柔毛，基部鞘状，叶片卵状心形、三角状心形或近圆心形，长2.5-12厘米，宽4-15厘米，先端圆形或急尖，边缘具整齐的小齿，基部浅心形或心形，两侧裂片宽三角状，两面光滑，叶质薄，叶脉羽状；茎中部叶具短柄，有膨大的鞘，叶片心形或卵状心形，长2-7厘米，宽4-7厘米；最上部叶无柄，披针形。总状花序长12-26厘米，疏散；苞片膜质，卵形，长达2.5厘米，宽约0.8厘米，向上渐小，先端渐尖，边缘具细齿，网脉明显；花序梗细，长5-8毫米，被白色蛛丝状柔毛；头状花序多数，辐射状；小苞片狭卵形或披针形，膜质，与总苞近等长；总苞钟形，长6-8毫米，宽5-6毫米，总苞片7-9，2层，长，圆形，宽2-3毫米，先端三角形，被褐色睫毛，背部被白色蛛丝状柔毛，内层具宽膜质边缘。舌状花5-6，黄色，舌片长圆形，长2-3毫米，宽约1毫米，管部纤细，长约4毫米；管状花多数，长7-8毫米，管部长2-3毫米，冠毛白色与花冠等长。瘦果（未熟）光滑。花期7-8月。

产四川西北部和北部、甘肃西南部。生于海拔3120-4000米的草坡和林下。模式标本采自四川康定（打箭炉）。
