少齿龙头黄芩
25b.**Scutellaria meehanioides var. paucidentata** C. Y. Wu & H. W. Li

## Description
Leaves triangular to triangular-lanceolate, margin coarsely 4- or 5-incised-dentate. Corolla ca. 2.3 cm.


> * Slopes; ca. 1500 m. Gansu (Hui Xian).

**25b．少齿变种**

Scutellaria meehanioides C. Y. Wu var. paucidentata C. Y. Wu et H. W. Li in Addenda 580. 1977.

这一变种与原变种不同在于叶三角形或三角状披针形，每侧有4-5对粗大缺刻状牙齿；花较小，长约2.3厘米。

产甘肃南部（徽县）；生于海拔1500米。模式标本采自甘肃徽县。
