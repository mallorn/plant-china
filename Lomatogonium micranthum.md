## Description
Annuals 6-20 cm tall. Stems erect, angular, branched apically; branches slender, glabrous. Leaves sessile; lower to middle stem leaves ovate-lanceolate, 5-10 &amp;#215; 2-3.5 mm, base obtuse, apex acute, midvein distinct abaxially; upper leaves linear-lanceolate, 3-5 &amp;#215; ca. 1 mm, apex acuminate. Inflorescences racemelike, usually in many-flowered panicles. Flowers (4- or) 5merous. Pedicel spreading to erect, 0.6-1.5 cm, slender, angular, glabrous. Calyx tube 1.5-2 mm; lobes linear-lanceolate to narrowly ovate, 3.5-4.5 mm, base obtuse, apex acuminate, midvein distinct. Corolla white, 4-5.5 mm in diam.; tube 1-1.5 mm; lobes lanceolate to narrowly ovate, 5-6 &amp;#215; ca. 2 mm, apex acuminate. Nectaries yellow, naked. Filaments ca. 3 mm; anthers yellow, ellipsoid, ca. 1.2 mm. Ovary ovoid-ellipsoid, apex acuminate. Stigma lobes decurrent to apical part of ovary. Capsules ovoid-ellipsoid, ca. 7.5 mm. Seeds brown, subglobose, ca. 0.2 mm in diam. Fl. and fr. Aug-Sep.

> Grassland slopes; 3000 m. SE Xizang [Nepal].
**13．小花肋柱花**

Lomatogonium micranthum H. Smith in Journ. Jap. Bot. 56 (9): 277. 1981; Hara, Chater et Williams, Enum. Fl. Pl. Nepal 3: 95. 1982.

一年生草本，高6-20厘米。茎从基部起多分枝，四棱形。基生叶在花期枯萎；茎生叶无柄，卵状披针形，长5-10毫米，宽2.5-3.5毫米，先端急尖，基部略狭，下面中脉明显。圆锥状复聚伞花序多花，开展；花梗四棱形，长6-15毫米；花5数；花萼长为花冠之半，裂片线状披针形至线形，长约4.5毫米，宽1-1.2毫米，先端渐尖，中脉在背面明显；花冠白色，裂片披针形，长5-6毫米，宽约2毫米，先端渐尖，基部两侧各具1个腺窝；花丝线形，长约3毫米，花药长约1.2毫米；子房卵状圆柱形，柱头下延。蒴果长约7.5毫米；种子圆球形，直径约0.2毫米。花果期8-9月。

产我国西藏东南部（工布江达）。生于山坡，海拔3000米。模式标本采自尼泊尔。
