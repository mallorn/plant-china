## Description
Leaf blade both surfaces densely pilose. Glumes elliptic-lanceolate, ciliate along veins and margin, apex pointed. Lemma hispid abaxially, margin long ciliate; awn 10–25 mm. Fl. and fr. May–Jul.

> * Mountain slopes, moist meadows, roadsides; 1500–1600 m. Gansu, Hebei, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi.
