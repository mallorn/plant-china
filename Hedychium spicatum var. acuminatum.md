疏花草果药",
11b.**Hedychium spicatum var. acuminatum** (Roscoe) Wallich",

## Description
Spikes laxly few flowered. Flowers yellowish but apex of corolla tube, base of corolla lobes, lateral staminodes, and labellum purplish red. Fl. Aug--Oct.

> Forests; 2000--3200 m. Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, NE India, Nepal, Sikkim].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt; Hedychium acuminatum&lt;/I&gt; Roscoe, Monandr. Pl. Scitam. t. 47. 1824.
**15a. 疏花草果药（新拟）**

var. acuminatum Wall. in Kew Journ. Bot. 5: 328. 1853; Bak. in Hook. f., Fl. Brit. Ind. 6: 227. 1892; K. Schum. in Engl. Pflanzenr. 20 (IV. 46): 50. 1904. ——H. acuminatum Rosc. , Mon. Pl. t. 47. 1828; Hook. f. in Bot. Mag. 57. t. 2969. 1830; Hand. -Mazz. , Symb. Sin. 7: 1320. 1936.

本种和草果药不同之处在于花序上的花较少，排列稀疏，花冠管的顶部、花冠裂片、侧生退化雄蕊及唇瓣的基部紫红色。花期：8-10月。

产云南、西藏；生于林中，海拔2000-3200米。尼泊尔亦有分布。
