10b.**Zygophyllum rosowii var. latifolium** (Schrenk) Popov",

## Description
Plants 15-20 cm tall. Leaflet blades rotund to oblong, 1.5-2.5 cm, apex obtuse. Capsule 3-5 × 0.5-0.7 cm.

> Rocky areas with gravel and sand. Gansu, Nei Mongol, Xinjiang [Kazakhstan, Mongolia].
  "Synonym": "*Zygophyllum latifolium* Schrenk, Bull. Cl. Phys.-Math. Acad. Imp. Sci. Saint-Pétersbourg 2: 198. 1844.
