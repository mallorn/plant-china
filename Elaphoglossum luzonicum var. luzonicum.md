吕宋舌蕨(原变种)",
3a.**Elaphoglossum luzonicum var. luzonicum**",

## Description
Rhizome scales with margin entire or slightly ciliate. Sterile fronds: stipe 4-8 cm, base densely covered with scales, distally with sparse, small, stellate scales; lamina 15-20 × 2-3 cm, base cuneate or broadly cuneate, margin entire; midrib prominent on both surfaces.

> On rocks or tree trunks in forests; 500-1100 m. Taiwan (?Nantou, Pingdong, Taidong) [Malaysia, New Guinea, Philippines].
  "Synonym": "*Elaphoglossum lepidopodum* C. Christensen ex Ogata.
