多毛悬钩子(原变种)",
122a.**Rubus lasiotrichos var. lasiotrichos**",

## Description
Sepals lanceolate to ovate-lanceolate, apex acute to shortly acuminate.

> Dry slopes, rocky mountains, sparse forests; 1800--2700 m. Sichuan, Yunnan [Thailand, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Rubus rufus* Focke var. *hederifolius* Cardot; *R. hederifolius* (Cardot) Thuan.
