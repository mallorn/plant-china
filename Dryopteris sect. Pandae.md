## Description
Lamina 1-pinnate, shallowly pinnately lobed or deeply so (at least basal pairs). Lateral veins mostly forked. Indusia large, membranous or conchiform.
  "Statistics": "About 12 species: widely distributed in the N temperate zone; seven species in China.
