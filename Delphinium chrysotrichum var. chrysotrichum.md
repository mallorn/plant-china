黄毛翠雀花(原变种)",
16a.**Delphinium chrysotrichum var. chrysotrichum**",

## Description
Stem and petioles spreading puberulent. Leaf blade to 3.2 × 5.2 cm. Pedicels spreading puberulent and yellow glandular puberulent.

> * Gravelly slopes; 4200--5000 m. W Sichuan, E Xizang.
