## Description
Leaves simple, sometimes 3-foliolate; blades or leaflets orbicular to suborbicular.

> * Hillsides, grassy places. Hebei, Henan, Hubei, Shanxi.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Salvia charbonnelii&lt;/I&gt; H. Léveillé, Repert. Spec. Nov. Regni Veg. 9: 220. 1911.
**48b.单叶变种**

Salvia miltiorrhiza Bunge var. charbonnelii (Levl.) C. Y. Wu, comb. nov. ——lvia charbonnelii Levl. in Fedde, Repert. Sp. Nov. 9: 220, 1911, e descr.

这一变种与原变种不同在于叶为单叶，间有具3小叶的复叶，叶片或小叶片圆形或近圆形。

产河北，山西，河南；生于草丛、山坡或路旁。模式标本采自河北阜平。
