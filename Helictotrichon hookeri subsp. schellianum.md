## Description
Plant 30–80 cm tall. Panicle 5–15 cm, contracted, branches bearing up to 4 spikelets. Spikelets silvery green, sometimes tinged brownish or violet.

> Hill slopes in steppe, rocky slopes, forest margins. Gansu, Hebei, Heilongjiang, Henan, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shanxi, Xinjiang [Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Mongolia, Russia].
  "Synonym": "*Avena schelliana* Hackel, Trudy Imp. S.-Peterburgsk. Bot. Sada 12: 419. 1892; *Avenula hookeri* (Scribner) Holub subsp. *schelliana* (Hackel) M. N. Lomonosova; *A. schelliana* (Hackel) W. Sauer &amp; H. Chmelistschek; *Helictotrichon schellianum* (Hackel) Kitagawa.
