野苏子系",
2.**Pedicularis ser. Grandiflorae** Prain",

> One species: China and Russia (Far East).

## Description
Herbs tall, drying black. Stems often many branched. Leaves alternate, large, 2- or 3-pinnatisect. Inflorescences long racemose, centripetal. Corolla large; galea toothless, apex acute. Anthers apiculate.
