## Description
Leaves tufted toward stem base or inserted on procumbent stem. Verticillasters congested. Calyx 6-7 mm, campanulate, membranous, yellowish when dry.

> * Streamsides in forests; 600-700 m. Guangdong, Hunan, Jiangxi.
**66b. 钟萼变种**

var. carphocalyx Stib. in Act. Hort. Gothob. 10: 63. 1935.

这一变种与原变种不同在于叶在茎的下部簇生或茎平卧因而叶在整个茎上着生，轮伞花序密集，果萼长6-7毫米，钟形，膜质，干时带黄色。

产广东，江西，湖南；生于林下溪边，海拔600-700米。等模式标本采自广东。
