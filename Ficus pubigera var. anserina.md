## Description
Shrubs, large. Leaf blade narrowly elliptic, 30-35 × 10-15 cm, abaxially densely brown tomentose. Figs pear-shaped, 4-6 × 4-5 cm, ringed on surface, densely covered with rust-colored scale-like hairs; peduncle 3-15 mm. Gall flowers: calyx lobes slightly recurved. Fl. Apr-May, fr. Jun-Aug.

> Rain forests; 700-800 m. S Yunnan [Laos].
**90b.鳞果褐叶榕（变种）（新拟）图版51:6**

Ficus pubigera (Wall. ex Miq.) Miq. var. anserina Corner in Gard. Bull. Sing. 18:5.1960, in ibid. 21:50.1965.

藤状大灌木，叶长椭圆形，长30-35厘米，宽10-15厘米，背面密被褐色绵毛；叶柄长2-3厘米，密被褐色柔毛。榕果体大、梨形，长4-6厘米，直径4-5厘米，表面环形，密被锈褐色鳞毛。总梗长3-15毫米；瘿花花被片微反折。花期4-5月，果期6-8月。中国新记录。

产云南南部（易武、金平）。通常生于沟谷雨林中，约750米处。老挝也有。
