## Description
Leaf  blade  13-20 X   10 cm, base  cuneate,  adaxially blue-black when dry.

> Near farm houses; 500-800 m. S Yunnan [Cambodia, India, Indonesia, Myanmar, Philippines, Vietnam].

产云南西双版纳。生长于海拔600米的村旁。印度、越南、老挝、柬埔寨也有分布。
