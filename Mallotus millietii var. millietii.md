贵州野桐(原变种)",
12a.**Mallotus millietii var. millietii**",

## Description
Branchlets, petiole, and inflorescences yellow stellate- tomentose and stellate-villous. Leaf blade chartaceous or leathery, abaxially dull yellow tomentose. Infructescences 4-5 cm. Fruiting pedicel 1.5-2 mm; capsule densely orange-yellow tomentose. Fl. May-Jun, fr. Jul-Oct.

> ● Limestone regions, forests, thickets; 500-1400 m. Guangxi, Guizhou, SE Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Mallotus kweichowensis* Lauener &amp; W. T. Wang; *Phytolacca esquirolii* H. Léveillé.
