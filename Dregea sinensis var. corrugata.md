## Description
Ovaries pubescent. Follicles transversely plicate-corrugate, sometimes almost transversely winged. Fl. Mar-Aug.

> * Bushland. Gansu, Guizhou, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Dregea corrugata&lt;/I&gt; C. K. Schneider in Sargent, Pl. Wilson. 3: 353. 1916; &lt;I&gt;Wattakaka corrugata&lt;/I&gt; (C. K. Schneider) Stapf; &lt;I&gt;W. sinensis&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;corrugata&lt;/I&gt; (C. K. Schneider) Tsiang.

与原变种的不同在于：蓇葖外果皮具横凸起的皱褶片状；子房被柔毛。花期3-5月，果期7-12月。

产于云南、贵州、四川、甘肃、陕西等省区。生长于山地灌木丛中。模式标本采自四川小金。
