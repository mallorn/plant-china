> One species: E Asia, Pacific Islands.

## Description
Herbs, small, creeping, much branched, cushionlike. Leaves opposite, small. Flowers axillary. Calyx tubular-campanulate, 5-angled, 5-toothed. Corolla subcampanulate, 4-lobed; lower lip 3-lobed, patent; upper lip entire, short, erect. Stamens 2, anterior. Capsule slightly flattened, 2-grooved, loculicidal. Seeds few, ovoid-fusiform, smooth.
**23.小果草属——Microcarpaea R. Br.**

R. Br. Prod. , 435, 1810: Benth. et Hook. f. Gen. Pl. 2: 957. 1876.

匍匐草本。叶对生。花小，单生叶腋。花萼管状钟形，5棱，具5齿；花冠近于钟状，檐部4裂，上唇短而直立，下唇3裂，开展；雄蕊2枚，位于前方。蒴果卵形，略扁，有两条沟槽，室背开裂。种子少数，纺锤状卵形，近于平滑。

单种属，分布于亚洲东南部和南部及大洋洲。
