## Description
Bulb ovoid, 1.5--2.2 cm in diam.; scales lanceolate, 2--2.5 cm × 5--10 mm. Stem 45--60 cm, smooth. Leaves scattered, linear, 5.5--8 cm × 2--4 mm, glabrous, margin recurved. Flower solitary. Tepals green, with dense, purple spots, lanceolate, 3--3.5 cm × 5--6 mm; nectaries neither papillose nor with fimbriate projections. Filaments 6--10 mm, glabrous; anthers narrowly oblong, 9--10 mm. Ovary cylindric, ca. 6 × 1 mm. Style nearly as thick as ovary, ca. 3.5 mm. Fl. Jun.

> * Open and rocky places. NW Yunnan (Zhongdian Xian).
