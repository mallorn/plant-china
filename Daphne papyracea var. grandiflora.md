大花白瑞香",
44d.**Daphne papyracea var. grandiflora** (Meisner ex Diels) C. Y. Chang",

## Description
Branches purplish red or purplish brown, usually glabrous. Leaf blade ovate or broadly elliptic, thickly papery. Calyx tube 14-15 mm; lobes ovate, 5-6 mm.

> ● Forests; ca. 2500 m. W Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Daphne papyracea* f. *grandiflora* Meisner ex Diels, Notes Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinburgh 7: 290. 1912.
**35d.大花白瑞香（变种）（新拟）**

var. grandiflora ( Meisn. ex Diels) C. Y. Chang, scat. nov. ——D. papyracea Wall. ex Steud. f. grandiflora Meisn. ex Diels in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 7: 290. 1912.

本变种小枝紫红色；叶片卵形或广椭圆形，中脉和侧脉在上面凹下，上面具皱纹；花萼筒长圆筒形，淡黄色，芳香，长达15毫米，裂片长5-6毫米，子房无花柱。

产云南西部澜沧江流域海拔2500米左右的地区。
