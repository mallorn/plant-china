通泉草多枝变种
17c.**Mazus pumilus var. delavayi** (Bonati) T. L. Chin ex D. Y. Hong

## Description
Plants more than 10 cm. Stems numerous, much branched. Petiole of basal leaves shorter than blade. Racemes elongated, 10-20-flowered. Pedicel short, less than 5(-7) mm. Calyx not enlarged in fruit.


> Trailsides, wet grassland; 1200-3800 m. Guangxi, W Sichuan, Yunnan [Bhutan, NW India, Kashmir, Nepal, Sikkim].

  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Mazus delavayi&lt;/I&gt; Bonati, Bull. Herb. Boissier 2: 530. 1908; &lt;I&gt;Mazus japonicus&lt;/I&gt; (Thunberg) Kuntze var. &lt;I&gt;delavayi&lt;/I&gt; (Bonati) P. C. Tsoong.

var. delavayi (Bonati) Tsoong, comb. nov ——M. delavayi Bonati, Bull. Herb. Boiss. 2(8): 530. 1908.

本变种与第一变种的区别为茎多数，常4支以上，并极多分枝；花序伸长，花多数，有花10-20朵，花梗较短。

产云南及四川西部。生海拔1200-2800米的路边及湿润草地。在印度半岛东部，西喜马拉雅地区也有分布。
