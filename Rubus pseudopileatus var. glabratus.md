## Description
Pedicel unarmed or nearly so, usually without stalked glands. Calyx abaxially glabrous margin.

> Shaded montane valleys, sparsely forested slopes; 2100--2900 m. W Sichuan.
**22b.光梗假帽莓（变种）**

Rubus pseudopilearus Card. var. glabratus Yu et Lu,植物分类学报20(3): 298. 1982.

本变种花梗光滑无毛；花萼外面无毛，仅内萼片边缘有绒毛。

产四川西部。生山谷阴处、山坡疏林或针、阔叶混交林下，海拔2100-2900米。
