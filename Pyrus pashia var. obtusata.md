钝叶川梨",
14b.**Pyrus pashia var. obtusata** Cardot",

## Description
Branchlets and inflorescences tomentose. Sepals obtuse, rarely acute or acuminate at apex.

> Sichuan, Yunnan.
**川梨钝叶变种**

P. pashia D. Don var. obtusata Card. in Lecomte, Not. Syst. 3: 346. 1918; Hand. -Mazz. Symb. Sin. 7: 465. 1933.

叶片先端圆钝，稀急尖或渐尖，嫩枝和花序常被绒毛。

产云南、四川。
