四川清风藤(原变种)",
2a.**Sabia schumanniana subsp. schumanniana** (Batalin) H. Hara",

> Water (Blumea 26: 25-27. 1980) treated this taxon as a synonym of *Sabia campanulata*.

## Description
Leaf blade light green abaxially. Cyme 1-3-flowered. Sepals, petals, filaments, and disk without red glandular dots.

> ●  Broad-leaved forests, mountain slopes, valleys, streamsides; 1100-2600 m. Chongqing, N and W Guizhou, Henan, Shaanxi, S Sichuan.
  "Synonym": "*Sabia schumanniana* subsp. *longipes* (Rehder &amp; E. H. Wilson) C. Y. Chang; *S. schumanniana* var. *longipes* Rehder &amp; E. H. Wilson.
