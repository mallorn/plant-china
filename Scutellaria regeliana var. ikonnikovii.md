## Description
Stems with appressed, very short pubescence only on angles. Leaves glabrous or adaxially sparsely fine strigose, abaxially with appressed, very short pubescence on veins and margin, otherwise sparsely fine granular glandular.

> Riverbanks, marshy sedge-grasslands; ca. 500 m. Heilongjiang, Jilin, Nei Mongol [Mongolia, Russia].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Scutellaria ikonnikovii&lt;/I&gt; Juzepczuk, Bot. Mater Gerb. Bot. Inst. Komarova Akad. Nauk SSSR 14: 358. 1951;&lt;I&gt; S. angustifolia&lt;/I&gt; (Regel) V. Komarov, p.p.
**80b. 塔头变种 香水水草（内蒙古多伦）**

Scutellaria regeliana Nakai var. ikonnikovii (Juz.) C. Y. Wu et H. W. Li, comb. nov.——Scutellaria ikonnikovii Juz. in Not. Syst. Herb Hort. Bot. URSS 14: 358. 1951, et in Fl. URSS 20: 96, t. 5, f. 1. 1954——Scutellaria angustifolia (Regel) Komarov in Act. Hart. Petrop. 25: 345. 1907. p. p.

这一变种与原变种不同在于茎仅沿棱上被贴生很短的小柔毛；叶上面无毛或散布微糙毛，下面仅在脉上及叶缘被贴生很短的小柔毛，余部有分散细粒状腺体。

产黑龙江，吉林及内蒙古；散生于河岸或莎草沼泽草甸（塔头甸子）上，海拔450-480米。苏联东西伯利亚，蒙古也有。模式标本采自苏联。
