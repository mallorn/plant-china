## Description
Leaf petiole less than 1 cm; blade cuneate at base. Fruit subglobose, 1–1.5 cm in diam.

> Rocky forests on slopes, stream sides; 1300--2700 m. Guizhou, Sichuan.
  "Synonym": "*Aria megalocarpa* var. *cuneata* (Rehder) H. Ohashi &amp; H. Iketani.
**大果花楸圆果变种**

S. megalocarpa Rehd. var. cuneata Rehd. in Sarg. Pl. Wils. 2: 267. 1915.

叶片基部楔形，叶柄稍短；果实近球形，直径1-1.5厘米。

产四川、贵州。生于岩石坡地林内或溪旁，海拔1300-2000米。模式标本采自四川宝兴。
