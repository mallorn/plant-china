蓝匙叶银莲花(原变种)",
46a.**Anemone coelestina var. coelestina**",

## Description
Leaf blade nearly 3-lobed, ovate-oblong. Sepals usually bluish white, vein anastomose solitary. Staminodes absent.

> Scrub, alpine meadows; 3500--4800 m. Sichuan, Yunnan [Sikkim].
