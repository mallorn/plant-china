> Zhou &amp; Wu (Acta Bot. Yunnan. 23: 29–36. 2001) treated this variety as a synonym of var. *buxifolius*.

## Description
Shrubs evergreen, to 3 m tall. Leaf blade elliptic, rarely obovate-elliptic, 7–16 mm, abaxially tomentose, apex obtuse and mucronulate. Inflorescences 2–5(–8)-flowered. Pedicels, hypanthium, and sepals abaxially densely and persistently strigose-tomentose.

> Mountain regions; 2500--3300 m. SE Xizang [Bhutan, India, Nepal].
