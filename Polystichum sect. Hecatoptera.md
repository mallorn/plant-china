## Description
Plants medium-sized, evergreen. Stipe scales brown, lanceolate. Lamina papery, 1-pinnate. Rachis without proliferous bulbils. Pinnae asymmetrical at base, densely regularly long spinulose on margin. Microscales broad-type.
