## Description
Corolla purple; upper petal 13-15 mm, margin conspicuously dentate; crest of outer petals ca. 1 mm, shortly decurrent on spur, not or slightly overtopping apex. Margin of bracts scarious-denticulate. Stigma often with 6 simple papillae rather forwardly disposed, and basal lobes hardly developed.

  "Synonym": "Basionym: *Corydalis curviflora* var. *pseudosmithii* Fedde, Repert. Spec. Nov. Regni Veg. 22: 27. 1926; *C. curviflora* subsp. *pseudosmithii* (Fedde) C. Y. Wu.
**85d. 流苏曲花紫堇（云南植物研究）（亚种）**

ssp. pseudosmithii (Fedde) C. Y. Wu, stat. nov.——Corydalis curviflora Maxim. var. pseudosmithii Fedde in Fedde Repert. 22: 27. 1926; Rehd. et Kobuski in Joum. Am. Arb. 14: 15. 1933; C. Y. Wu et H. Chuang in Act. Bot. Yunn.6 (3): 247. 1984.

与原亚种的区别是苞片长圆状披针形或狭菱状披针形，上部边缘具流苏状细齿；上花瓣长1.5-1.7厘米，下花瓣边缘反折，外花瓣先端有尖突，边缘具不规则的小齿，鸡冠状突起高约2毫米，延伸至距末。

产甘肃西南部（卓尼）、四川西北部（松潘、马尔康、理县），生于海拔（2700-)3000-4100米的林下、灌丛下或草坡。模式标本采自松潘。 此亚种虽与c.和e.两亚种分布区部分重叠，但形态特征足以区别。
