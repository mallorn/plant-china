石生孩儿参",
2.**Pseudostellaria rupestris** (Turczaninow) Pax",

## Description
Root tubers globose or broadly ellipsoid, ca. 1 cm. Stems erect, 5--10 cm tall, slender, unbranched, or sparsely branched apically, glabrous or with 1 or 2 lines of short hairs. Leaves lanceolate or ovate-lanceolate, 1--3 cm × 3--8 mm, base attenuate into a short petiole, glabrous or ciliate, apex acute. Chasmogamic flowers terminal or axillary; pedicel 1.5--2.5 cm, slender, glabrous or pubescent; sepals 5, oblong-lanceolate, veins pilose, margin membranous; petals 5, oblong, ca. 1.5 × as long as sepals, margin entire, rarely emarginate; stamens 10, subequaling petals; styles (2 or)3. Cleistogamic flowers axillary, small; pedicel ca. 1.2 cm; sepals 4; petals absent; stamens 2; styles 2 or 3. Capsule ellipsoid, ca. 4 × 3 mm. Seeds brown, ovoid, ca. 1.5 mm, with anchor-shaped barbs. Fl. May--Jun, fr. Jul--Sep.

> Picea forests, rocky mountain slopes, 2700--3400 m. S Jilin (Changbai Shan), SW Nei Mongol (Helan Shan), Qinghai [Mongolia, Russia].

Baic. -Dahur. 1: 238. 1842; Ledeb. , Fl. Ross. 1: 373. 1842; Regel in Bull. Soc. Nat. Moscou 35 (1): 260. tab. 2. fig. 7-11. 1862; Muravaeva in Kom. Fl. USSR 6: 426. tab. 22. fig. 6. 1936; Грубов, Консп. Фπ. Монг. Hap. 127. 1955; Попов, Φπ. Сред. Сиб. 1: 413. 1957; 东北植物检索表54. 1959; Байтенов и Повπов в Павπов, Φπ. Каэахст. 3: 339. 1960.

多年生草本，高约8厘米。块根球状或宽椭圆状，长约1厘米。茎直立，纤细，不分枝或上部稀疏分枝，无毛或被1-2列短毛。叶片披针形、倒披针形或狭长圆形，长1-3厘米，宽3-8毫米，顶端急尖，基部渐狭成短柄，无毛或边缘具缘毛。开花受精花顶生或腋生；花梗无毛或被短柔毛；萼片长圆状披针形，边缘白色，膜质，沿脉被疏毛；花瓣白色，长圆形，比萼片约长1/3，全缘，稀顶端微凹；雄蕊10，与花瓣近等长；花柱3，稀为2。闭花受精花小形，生于茎下部叶腋；花梗长约12毫米。蒴果卵圆形；种子小，褐色，表面具锚状刺凸。花期5-6月，果期7-9月。

产吉林、内蒙古、青海。生于海拔3 000米处云杉林下或多石质山坡。俄罗斯和蒙古也有。模式标本采自外贝加尔。
