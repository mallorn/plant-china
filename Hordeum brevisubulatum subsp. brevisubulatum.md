短芒大麦(原亚种)",
2a.**Hordeum brevisubulatum subsp. brevisubulatum**",

## Description
Culms glabrous throughout. Leaf blade abaxial epidermis with straight long cells, without silica cells. Lemma of central spikelet glabrous or subglabrous; awn less than 3 mm. Fl. and fr. summer. 2*n* = 14*, 28*.

> Steppe valleys at timberline, saline meadows, stream banks; 1400–3000 m. Gansu, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Xinjiang, Xizang [Mongolia, Pakistan, Russia (SE Siberia); C Asia].
  "Synonym": "*Hordeum secalinum* Schreber var. *brevisubulatum* Trinius, Sp. Gram. 1: pl. 4. 1828; *Critesion brevisubulatum* (Trinius) ┴. Löve; *H. macilentum* Steudel; *H. secalinum* subsp. *brevisub-ulatum* (Trinius) Krylov.
