黄背野桐",
22b.**Mallotus tenuifolius var. subjaponicus** Croizat",

## Description
Branchlets and inflorescences grayish-yellowish stellate-tomentose. Leaf blade ovate or broadly ovate, papery, yellowish green when dry, abaxially yellowish, scattered stellate-pilose and orange glandular-scaly. Male inflorescences 5-10 cm. Male flowers 2- or 3-fascicled; calyx lobes 5, unequal, ca. 4 mm. Female inflorescences or infructescences 8-12 cm. Female flowers: styles 3, ca. 6 mm, base connate for ca. 1 mm. Capsule stellate-pilose, spines 5-7 mm. Seeds ovoid, 4-5 mm, black. Fl. May-Jun, fr. Jun-Aug.

> ● Mountain valleys, forests, thickets; 500-1500 m. Anhui, Fujian, N Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Zhejiang.
  "Synonym": "*Mallotus nepalensis* Müller Argoviensis var. *kwangtungensis* Croizat; *M. subjaponicus* (Croizat) Croizat.
