## Description
Leaflet blade abaxially greenish; veins inconspicuously raised abaxially. Pedicel 15--30 mm. Fl. Jun--Jul.

> Mountains; ca. 2500 m. Xinjiang [N Asia, N Europe].

**61b．长梗亚欧唐松草（变种）**

Thalictrum minus var. stipellatum (C. A. Mey.) Tamura in Acta Phytotax. Geobot. 15: 87. 1953.——T. kemense Fries, Fl. Halland. 95. 1817; Hara in Journ. Fac. Sci. Univ. Tokyo 3 (6) : 56. 1952.——T. kemense var. stipellatum C. A. Mey. ex Maxim. Prim. Fl. Amur. 16. 1859.——T. minus var. kemense (Fries) Trel. in Proc. Bort. Soc. Nat. Hist. 23: 300. 1888.

与亚欧唐松草的区别：花梗较长，长15-30毫米。小叶背面淡绿色，脉不明显 隆起，脉网不明显。

在我国产新疆。生海拔2500米一带山地。在亚洲北部、欧洲北部也有分布。
