59.**Pedicularis ser. Denudatae** Prain",

> Two species: Bhutan, China, Sikkim; one species in China.

## Description
Herbs low. Stems flexuous or diffuse, slender. Stem leaves opposite or whorled, ovate-lanceolate to ovate-oblong, pinnatilobate to pinnatifid. Inflorescences lax. Calyx lobes 5, unequal. Corolla small, tube slightly longer than calyx, decurved apically; galea lacking marginal teeth, forming a very short obtuse beak apically.
