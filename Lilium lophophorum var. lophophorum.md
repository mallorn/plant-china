## Description
Leaves narrowly lanceolate, lanceolate, or oblong-lanceolate. Tepals yellow to pale yellowish green, with extremely sparse, purple-red spots or unspotted.

> * Forests, bushy slopes, alpine grasslands; 2500--4500 m. Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan.
