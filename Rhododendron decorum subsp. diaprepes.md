高尚大白杜鹃",
212b.**Rhododendron decorum subsp. diaprepes** (I. B. Balfour & W. W. Smith) T. L. Ming",

## Description
Petiole 24–40 mm; leaf blade oblong to oblong-obovate, 12–19(–30) × 4.4–11 cm; base rounded. Corolla (6.5–)8–10 × ca. 9 cm, lobes 6–8, emarginate; stigma 3–5(–6.5) mm in diam.

> Evergreen forests, mixed forests; 1700–3300 m. SW Sichuan, W Yunnan [Myanmar].
