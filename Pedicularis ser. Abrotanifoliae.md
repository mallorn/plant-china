## Description
Stems branched or not. Leaves whorled, proximal ones sometimes opposite, oblong to lanceolate, pinnatipartite to pinnatisect. Flowers laxly or compactly arranged in spikes. Calyx lobes 5. Corolla tube bent forward apically; galea &amp;#177; truncate at apex, lacking marginal teeth, beakless to short beaked.
