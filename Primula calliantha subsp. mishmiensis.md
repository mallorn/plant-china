## Description
Leaf blade narrowly ovate to oblanceolate. Calyx ca. 1 cm. Corolla yellow; tube 1.2--1.5 cm; limb ca. 2.5 cm wide Pin flowers: stamens below middle of corolla tube; style slightly shorter than tube. Thrum flowers: stamens toward apex of tube; style ca. 3 mm.

> On rocks of steep humus-clad slopes, among &lt;I&gt;Rhododendron&lt;/I&gt;; 3700--4000 m. SE Xizang [NE India (Assam)].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Primula mishmiensis&lt;/I&gt; Kingdon Ward, Ann. Bot. (Oxford) 44: 122, 125. 1930.
**168c.黄美花报春（新拟）亮叶雪山报春（西藏植物志）**

subsp. mishmiensis (Ward) C. M. Hu, star. nov. ——Priimula mishmiensis Ward in Ann. Bot. 44: 122, 122. 1930; W. W. Smith et Fletcher in Traps. Roy. Soc. Edinb. 60(2): 615. 1942; 西藏植物志3: 797. 1986.

花冠黄色。

产于我国西藏东南米什米山地。生长于岩石上和杜鹃丛下的腐殖土中，海拔3700-4000 米。模式标本采自米什米山地德勒河谷。
