白毛火把花",

## Description
Shrubs erect, 1.5-3 m tall. Old branches subglabrous, with longitudinally exfoliating bark, young branches densely gray lanate-tomentose. Petiole 1.2-1.5 cm; leaf blade ovate to elliptic-ovate, 5.5-12 &amp;#215; 2-6 cm, adaxially dark green, finely bullate, with simple and stipitate stellate hairs, abaxially gray, densely lanate-tomentose, base rounded to shallowly cordate, margin crenulate, apex acute. Verticillasters 6-10-flowered, in &amp;#177; racemose spikes, densely lanate-tomentose except for corollas. Pedicel ca. 1 mm. Calyx &amp;#177; tubular, 1.2-1.5 cm, lanate-tomentose; teeth triangular, 1.5-2 mm. Corolla orange-red, with sparse stellate and simple hairs, ca. 2.5 cm; tube ca. 2 cm, curved outward, throat dilated, narrowed toward base; upper lip ovate; lobes of lower lip ovate, middle lobe emarginate. Filaments bearded. Ovary glandular. Fl. Jul.

> * Thickets; ca. 2000 m. Yunnan.

Colquhounia vestita Wall. Tent. Fl. Nepal. 1: 14. 1824, p. p. quoad specim. Kamaon. ; Pl. Asiat. Rar. 3: 43, pl. 269. 1832, quoad fig.; Benth. in DC. Prodr. 12: 457. 1848, excl. pl. Assam. ; Hook. f. Fl. Brit. Ind. 4: 674. 1885, quoad pl. Kamaon. ;C. Y. Wu in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 8 (1) : 41. 1959 ----Colquhounia coccinea Wall. var.β. vestita Prain in Journ. Asiat. Soc. Bengal 62: 36. 1893.

灌木，直立，高1.5-3米。老枝近圆柱形，近无毛，皮层纵裂脱落，幼枝密被灰白色绵毡绒毛。叶卵圆形或椭圆状卵圆形，长5.5-12厘米，宽2-6厘米，先端锐尖，基部圆形或浅心形，边缘具小圆锯齿，草质，上面深绿色，具细皱，混生有单毛及具柄的星状毛，下面灰白色，密被毡状绒毛，侧脉6-8对，在上面平展或略凹陷，下面隆起；叶柄长1.2-1.5厘米。轮伞花序6-10花，在茎枝上多数聚集，由于向顶生长而组成近总状的穗状花序，除花冠外各部均密被毡状绒毛，下承以苞片；苞片短小，线形；花梗短小，长约1毫米。花萼近管形，长1.2-1.5厘米，外被毡状绒毛，内面无毛，脉不显著，齿5，三角形，长1.5-2毫米。花冠橙红色，外疏被星状毛及单毛，长约2.5厘米，冠筒长约2厘米，向外方弯曲，喉部增大，下部狭长，冠檐二唇形，上唇卵圆形，下唇3浅裂，裂片卵圆形，中裂片先端微缺。雄蕊4，前对较长，花丝丝状，具髯毛，花药卵圆形，2室，室略叉开。花柱丝状，先端不相等2浅裂。花盘平顶。子房具腺点。花期7月。

产云南西部；生于灌丛中，海拔约2000米。模式标本采自印度北部。
