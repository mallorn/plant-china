白瑞香(原变种)",
44a.**Daphne papyracea var. papyracea**",

> *\"Daphne papyrifera* Buchanan-Hamilton,\" an unpublished name listed by D. Don (Prodr. Fl. Nepal. 68. 1825) in the synonymy of *D. odora*, belongs here (see Meisner in A. Candolle, Prodr. 14: 537. 1857).

## Description
Branches grayish brown or grayish black, tomentose. Petiole 4-15 mm; leaf blade elliptic to oblong-lanceolate or oblanceolate, membranous or papery. Calyx tube 10-12(-18) × 2.5-5 mm at throat.

> Forests, shrubby slopes; 700-2000 m. Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hubei, Hunan, Sichuan, Yunnan [India, Nepal].
  "Synonym": "*Daphne cannabina* Wallich, Asiat. Res. 13: 385. 1820, not Loureiro (1790); *D. cavaleriei* H. Léveillé; *D. mairei* H. Léveillé.
