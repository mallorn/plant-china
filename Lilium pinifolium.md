## Description
Bulb subglobose, 3--3.5 cm in diam.; scales white, ovate, 2.5--3.5 × 2--2.5 cm. Stem 70--80 cm. Leaves scattered, narrowly linear or subfiliform, 3--4 cm × 1--2(--3) mm, abaxially sometimes scabrous, margin minutely papillose. Flowers usually 2, nodding, campanulate. Tepals white, green at base, with red spots; outer ones linear, ca. 4 × 1.2 cm; inner ones narrowly oblong, ca. 1.5 cm wide; nectaries blackish green, not papillose. Stamens ca. 2 cm; filaments greenish white; anthers 5--7 mm. Ovary green, ca. 1.5 cm × 3 mm. Style 1.7--2 cm; stigma small. Fl. Jun.

> * Forests; 3300--3400 m. NW Yunnan.
