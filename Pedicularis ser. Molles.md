> One species: Bhutan, China, Nepal, Sikkim.

## Description
Stems several together, erect or ascending, often branched apically. Leaves in whorls of 3--5, pinnatisect. Stem leaves numerous. Inflorescences long spicate, interrupted. Calyx lobes 5. Corolla small; tube slightly curved at middle; galea toothless or beaked.
