阴山长花马先蒿马先蒿",
336c.**Pedicularis longiflora var. yingshanensis** Z. Y. Chu & Y. Z. Zhao",

## Description
Leaves pseudo-opposite. Calyx lobes 3. Corolla small; lower lip with a narrow maroon stripe on each antero-lateral ridge of palate. Fl. Aug.

> * Alpine marshy meadows; ca. 2100 m. Nei Mongol (Yingshan).
