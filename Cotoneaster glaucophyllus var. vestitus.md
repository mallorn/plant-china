毛萼粉叶栒子",
8c.**Cotoneaster glaucophyllus var. vestitus** W. W. Smith",

## Description
Leaf blade 2–2.5 × 1–2 cm, abaxially initially densely yellow tomentose, glabrescent. Inflorescences relatively large, to 40-flowered, initially densely white tomentose, glabrescent.

> Mixed forests, thickets, mountain regions; 2000--3000 m. W Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Cotoneaster vestitus* (W. W. Smith) Flinck &amp; Hylmö.
**粉叶栒子毛萼变种**

C. glaucophyllus Franch. var. vestitus W. W. Smith in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 10: 21. 1917.——C. vestitus (W. W. Smith) Flinck & Hylmo in Bot. Not. Lund. 119: 460. 1966.

叶片椭圆形，长2-2.5厘米，宽1-2厘米，两端圆钝，幼时密被黄色绒毛，逐渐脱落。花序有花多至40朵，总花梗、花梗和萼筒密被白色绒毛，以后脱落减少。

产云南西部，模式标本采自腾冲东山。
