## Description
Terminal leaflet blade narrowly ovate or ovate-lanceolate, 3-4 × 1.2-2 cm, abaxially densely or sparsely pubescent or nearly glabrous. Pedicel 1.3-2.2 cm at anthesis, nearly gla-brous. Lower calyx lobes longer than tube. Legume sparsely puberulent, glabrous at maturity.

**22b. 川南山蚂蝗（变种）**

var. wolohoense (Schindl.) Ohashi in Hara, Fl. E. Himal. 2:297. 1971 et in Ginkgoana 1:111. 1973. ——Desmodium wolohoense Schindl. in Fedde, Repert. Sp. Nov. 21:1. 1925.

本变种与原变种不同点在于顶生小叶为狭卵形或卵状披针形，长3-4厘米，宽1.2-2厘米，花梗较长，开花时长1.3-2.2厘米，近无毛。下部花萼裂片较萼筒长，荚果疏生小柔毛，成熟时无毛。

产四川和云南西北部。生于林缘灌丛中，海拔2900-4000米。模式标本采自四川西昌以南。
