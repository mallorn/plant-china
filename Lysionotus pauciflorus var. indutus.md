## Description
Stems puberulent. Leaf blade puberulent. Flowers and fruits unknown.

> * Hilly regions. W Guizhou (Weining Xian).
**22b.灰叶吊石苣苔（变种）（广西植物）**

L. pauciflorus var. indutus Chun ex W. T. Wang in Guihaia 3 (4) : 275. 1983.

与吊石苣苔的区别：枝条被开展的淡褐色短柔毛；叶两面被白色短柔毛。

产贵州威宁。生于开阔山地。
