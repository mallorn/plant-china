## Description
Shrubs, erect, 40-90 cm tall. Leaves simple or rarely compound; blade lanceolate to ovate-lanceolate. Ovary glabrous, without glands. Fl. Aug.

> * Mountain slopes in calcareous soil; 1400 m. Shanxi (Taihang Shan).
