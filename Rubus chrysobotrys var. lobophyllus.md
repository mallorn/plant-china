## Description
Leaves often undulately 5-divided. Bracts to 2.3 cm.

> Mountainous regions; 2000--2400 m. SW Yunnan.
**123b.裂叶黄穗悬钩子（变种）**

R. chrysobotrys Hand.-Mazz. var. lobflphyllus Hand. -Mazz. Symb. Sin. 7: 494. 1933.

叶边深波状分裂；苞片长达2.3厘米；花梗长达1厘米；花瓣长9毫米。

产云南西北部。
