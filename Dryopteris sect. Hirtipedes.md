## Description
Lamina 1-pinnate. Pinnae lanceolate, shallowly incised or pinnately lobed to middle of pinnae. Lateral veins single, basal pair or upper veinlets short, only reaching middle of pinnae. Sori with or without indusia.
  "Statistics": "About 23 species: E and S Asia, with China and Japan as the center of diversity; 22 species (nine endemic) in China.
