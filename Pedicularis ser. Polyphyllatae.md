## Description
Stems erect or somewhat prostrate, usually many branched. Stem leaves alternate or sometimes opposite, many, short petiolate, ovate to oblong, pinnatifid to pinnatisect. Inflorescences short racemose or capitate. Pedicel well developed. Calyx deeply cleft anteriorly, (3- or)5-lobed. Corolla tube ca. as long as or slightly longer than calyx, straight; galea often dentate; beak slender.
