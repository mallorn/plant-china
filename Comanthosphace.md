## Description
Plants rhizomatous perennial, herbs or subshrubs. Stems usually unbranched, erect. Leaves petiolate or subsessile, dentate. Verticillasters 6-10-flowered, in long, terminal, densely white stellate tomentose spikes; bracts leaflike or scalelike, early deciduous; bracteoles indistinct, minute. Calyx tubular-campanulate, inconspicuously 10-veined, stellate tomentose outside, glabrous inside; teeth 5, short triangular, apex acute, anterior 2 longer. Corolla reddish to purple, 2-lipped; tube funnelform, villous annulate near middle; upper lip 2-lobed or occasionally entire; lower lip 3-lobed, middle lobe ovate, spreading, concave, &amp;#177; saccate; lateral lobes erect, smaller than middle lobe. Stamens 4, anterior 2 longer, much exserted; filaments glabrous; anthers ovoid, cells 1, transversely dehiscent. Ovary brown, glabrous, glandular. Nutlets triquetrous, ellipsoid, yellow-brown, golden glandular.

**79.绵穗苏属——Comanthosphace S. Moore**

S. Moore in Journ. Bot. Brit. & For. 15: 293. 1877; Briq. in Engl. u. Prantl. Nat. Pflanzenfam. 4, 3a: 328. 1897; Kudo in Mem. Fac. Sci. Agr. Taihoku Univ. 2: 57. 1929.

多年生草本或半灌木，有于其上密生须根的根茎。茎单一，通常不分枝，直立。叶具柄或近无柄，具齿。轮伞花序少花，6-10花，在茎及侧枝顶上多数组成长穗状花序，花序通常密被白色星状绒毛；苞片叶状或鳞片状，早落；小苞片不显著，微小；花梗短。花萼管状钟形，向上渐扩大，10脉，不明显，外面被星状绒毛，内面无毛，萼齿5，短三角形，锐尖或具胼胝尖头，前2齿稍宽大。花冠淡红至紫色，外面在伸出萼筒部分被绒毛或近于无毛，内面在冠筒近中部具一圈不规则或宽或狭或密或稀的柔毛环，冠檐二唇形，开张，上唇2裂或偶有全缘，下唇3裂，中裂片卵圆形，较大，张开，内凹，多少成浅囊状，侧裂片直伸。雄蕊4，前对略长，伸出花冠很多，几达花冠长的1倍，花丝无毛，花药卵珠形，一室，横向开裂。花盘平顶。子房褐色，无毛，具腺点。小坚果三稜状椭圆形，黄褐色，具金黄色腺点。

约6种，分布于我国至日本。我国有4种，1变种，产四川，贵州，湖南，广东，江西，安徽，浙江，江苏及台湾。
