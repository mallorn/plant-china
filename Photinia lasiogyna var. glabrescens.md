11b.**Photinia lasiogyna var. glabrescens** L. T. Lu & C. L. Li",

## Description
Rachis and pedicels sparsely tomentose-pilose when young, soon glabrescent, glabrous when old; hypanthium glabrous abaxially.

> Mixed forests, foothills, calcareous slopes; 200--1500 m. Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Hunan, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Yunnan, Zhejiang.
