> This and the following variety are known to us only from their protologues.

## Description
Leaf blade 9-14 × ca. 7 cm, leathery. Inflorescence often 1-flowered. Calyx lobes inside densely yellow stellate-tomentose with sparse brown scales. Filaments ca. 2.5 mm.

> ● 1300-1400(-1600) m. Guangxi (Napo), Yunnan (Malipo).
