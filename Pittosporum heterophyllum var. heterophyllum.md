异叶海桐(原变种)",
35a.**Pittosporum heterophyllum var. heterophyllum**",

> The roots and bark are used medicinally in the treatment of broken bones, burns, and cuts.

## Description
Leaves petiolate; leaf blade oblanceolate, narrowly lanceolate, or linear, 4–8 × 1–2 cm, sometimes narrower. Pedicels 7–15 mm, glabrous. Ovary pubescent. Fl. Mar–May, fr. Jun–Nov.

> Forests, valleys, slopes, stream sides; 800--3000(--4000) m. Sichuan, SE Xizang, N Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Pittosporum truncatum* Pritzel var. *tsaii* Gowda.
