13b.**Lilium henrici var. maculatum** (W. E. Evans) Woodcock & Stearn",

## Description
Inner tepals with a large, purple-red blotch at base adaxially and a few purple-red speckles; outer ones each with a purple-red blotch only.

> * NW Yunnan.
