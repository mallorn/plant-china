钟萼粗叶木(原变种)",
31b.**Lasianthus trichophlebus var. trichophlebus**",

## Description
Branches sparsely strigillose. Leaves glabrous except strigillose or hirsute on midrib and nerves abaxially.
  "Statistics": "Forests, shaded and wet places. Guangdong [Indonesia, Malaysia (Peninsular), Philippines, Thailand, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Lasianthus barbellatus* Ridley; *L. cupreus* Pierre ex Pitard.
