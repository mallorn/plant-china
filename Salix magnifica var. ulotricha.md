卷毛大叶柳",
55c.**Salix magnifica var. ulotricha** (C. K. Schneider) N. Chao",

## Description
Female flower: ovary abruptly contracted at apex, white villous, shortly stipitate. Capsule ovoid, ca. 7 mm, subglabrous.

> *  Near water; 2100-2800 m. W Sichuan
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Salix ulotricha&lt;/I&gt; C. K. Schneider in Sargent, Pl. Wilson. 3: 44. 1916.
**52c.卷毛大叶柳（变种）**

var. ulotricha (Schneid.) N. Chao, comb. nov. ——S. ulotricha Schneid. in Sarg. Pl. Wils. 3: 44. 1916; Gorz in Bull. Fan. Mem. Inst. Biol. 6 (1): 5. 1935; Hao in Fedde, Rep. Beih. 93: 62. 1936,

与原变种的区别在于：子房顶端突然收缩，幼时多少有容易脱落的白色卷曲长柔毛，柄较短。蒴果卵状，较大，长7毫米，近无毛。

产地与原变种相同。模式标本采自宝兴。
