2.**Piptatherum hilariae** Pazij",

> This species is close to *Piptatherum gracile*, but has an abruptly narrowed lemma apex, and the short apical hair tufts are distinctive. It is the only species of *Piptatherum* in China (except for the distinctive *P. kuoi*) with glabrous anthers (1 or 2 short hairs may be present, but never a dense tuft). In neighboring countries it occurs in the upper forest belt and in alpine thickets.&lt;p&gt;&lt;p&gt;The name *Piptatherum humile* refers to small specimens with linear panicles.

## Description
Perennial, densely tufted. Culms 20–50 cm tall, 2–3-noded. Leaf sheaths glabrous, usually shorter than internodes; leaf blades gray-green or gray purple, usually rolled, rarely flat, up to 10(–30) cm, 2–4 mm wide, abaxial surface smooth or scaberulous, adaxial surface puberulous; ligule lanceolate, 3–6 mm. Panicle open or contracted, 5–12(–30) cm; branches 2 per node, spreading at anthesis, erect at maturity, longest less than 1/2 panicle length. Spikelets purplish red or gray-green; glumes lanceolate-ovate, 5–8 mm, apex sharply acuminate; callus glabrous; lemma elliptic to narrowly ovate, 3.5–4 mm, back pubescent with 0.2–0.3 mm hairs, midline glabrescent at maturity, apex usually shortly beaked, pale, glabrous, shortly 2-lobed above awn insertion, lobes rounded, each with tuft of hairs; awn caducous, 2–4 mm, often slightly bent at insertion. Anthers 1.5–2.5 mm, glabrous at apex or almost so. Fl. and fr. Jun–Sep.

> River banks, plateaus; 3100–4500 m. Xizang (Lhozhag, Qusum) [E Afghanistan, NW India, Kashmir, Pakistan, Tajikistan (Pamirs)].

**11．矮落芒草（拟）**

Oryzopsis humilis Bor in Kew Bull. 1951: 445. 1952, et Grass. Burma Ceyl. Ind. Pakist. 639. 1960.

多年生矮小草本。须根细长，具短根茎。秆较细弱，直立，光滑无毛，高20-25厘米，具2-3节。叶鞘基部者干膜质，枯褐色，茎生者绿色，紧裹茎，均无毛，短于节间；叶舌膜质，披针形，有时先端较钝（可能尖端折断），长2-3毫米；叶片线形，扁平，灰绿色，长达7厘米，宽约2毫米，粗糙。圆锥花序向上稍开展，长5-10厘米，花序主轴、分枝和小穗柄均粗糙或被短毛，分枝纤细，每节2枚，向上伸直，长1-2厘米；小穗卵状披针形至椭圆形，灰绿色，先端紫红色，长3.5-4毫米；颖膜质，近相等，长3.5-4毫米，宽约2毫米，光滑无毛，第一颖具5脉，侧脉先端弓曲达中脉，第二颖较第一颖稍窄，具3脉；外稃草质，膨胀，栗褐色，长2.5-3毫米，宽约1毫米，光滑无毛，具5脉，果期为黑褐色变硬革质，基盘光滑无毛，芒自稃体顶端伸出，粗糙，长4-6毫米；内稃与外稃近等长，具2脉，背扁平，光滑无毛，两侧边缘被外稃包被；鳞被3，上面1片线形，长约1毫米，下面2片卵状披针形；雄蕊3，花药黄色，长约1.5毫米，顶端具毫毛。花果期6-8月。

产西藏昌都地区。生于海拔2500-3200米的山坡草地。分布于印度东部（联邦省）。模式标本采自印度东部。

该种与Oryzopsis lateralis (Rebel) Stapf外部轮廓极相似，但小穗各部均较小，外稃且光滑无毛，易区别；与Oryzopsis tibetica (Roshev.) P. C. Kuo的小穗大小、形状很相似，但后者植株高大，花序每节分枝多，常3-5枚，故易区别。
