## Description
Herbs drying black. Roots much thickened, fascicled. Stems erect. Leaves mostly on stem, opposite, lanceolate, pinnatisect. Inflorescences spicate. Calyx inflated, strongly 5-angled, with dense reticulate veins; lobes 5, triangular, entire. Corolla tube slightly bent distally; galea shorter than lower lip, 1-toothed on each side, beakless; lower lip spreading.
