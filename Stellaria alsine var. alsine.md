雀舌草(原变种)",
33a.**Stellaria alsine var. alsine**",

## Description
Flowers 3--5, in terminal or axillary cymes. Leaves lanceolate to oblong-lanceolate, 5--20 × 2--4 mm. Pedicel slightly declinate in fruit, 0.5--2 cm, slender, glabrous. Capsule ovoid-orbicular, subequaling or slightly longer than persistent sepals, 6-valved. Seeds numerous, brown, reniform, slightly compressed, rugulose.

> Fields, streamsides, wetlands; 500--4000 m. Anhui, Fujian, Gansu, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Henan, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Nei Mongol, Sichuan, Taiwan, Xizang, Yunnan, Zhejiang [Bhutan, India, Japan, Kashmir, Korea, Nepal, Pakistan, Vietnam; Europe].
