尖片对囊蕨(原变种)
23a.**Deparia acuta var. acuta**

## Description
Pinna segments 13-17 pairs, distant, with serrulate or subentire margin.


> Forests, valleys; 2700-4000 m. Sichuan, Xizang [W Himalaya, N India, N Pakistan].

  "Synonym": "*Lunathyrium acutum* Ching, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 9: 73. 1964; *L. bomiense* Ching &amp; S. K. Wu; *L. latibasis* Ching; *L. tibeticum* Ching.
