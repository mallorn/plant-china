蒙古糖芥(原亚种)",
7a.**Erysimum flavum subsp. flavum**",

## Description
Plants (10-)20-60(-100) cm tall. Sepals (0.8-)0.9-1.2(-1.4) cm. Petals (1.6-)1.7-2.4(-2.6) cm × 5-8 mm. Anthers (2.5-)3-4 mm.

> Forest clearings, dry meadows, mountain slopes; 1500-4500 m. Heilongjiang, Nei Mongol [Mongolia, Russia (Siberia)].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Hesperis flava&lt;/I&gt; Georgi, Bemerk. Reise Russ. Reich. 1: 225. 1775; &lt;I&gt;Erysimum altaicum&lt;/I&gt; C. A. Meyer var. &lt;I&gt;shinganicum&lt;/I&gt; Y. L. Chang; &lt;I&gt;E. flavum&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;shinganicum&lt;/I&gt; (Y. L. Chang) K. C. Kuan.
