洼瓣花 (原变种)",
2a.**Lloydia serotina var. serotina**",

## Description
Plants 10--20 cm tall. Tepals 1--1.5 cm. Capsule subobovoid, 6--7 mm. Seeds deltoid. 2&lt;I&gt; n&lt;/I&gt; = 24, 36, 48.

> Thickets, grassy slopes; 2400--4000 m. Gansu, Hebei, Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shanxi, Sichuan, Xinjiang, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, India, Japan, Kazakhstan, Korea, Mongolia, Nepal, Pakistan, Russia, Sikkim; Europe, North America].

Pl. 1: 294. 1753; &lt;I&gt; Anthericum serotinum&lt;/I&gt; (Linnaeus) Linnaeus; &lt;I&gt; Lloydia alpina&lt;/I&gt; Salisbury; &lt;I&gt; L. himalensis&lt;/I&gt; Royle; &lt;I&gt; L. serotina&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt; unifolia&lt;/I&gt; Franchet.
