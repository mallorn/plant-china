## Description
Stem 18--35 cm, glabrous. Basal leaves lorate, 30--40 × ca. 2 cm; cauline leaves 5--10, linear, 6.5--15 × 0.4--0.8(--1.7) cm. Raceme laxly 2--4(--6)-flowered; bracts narrowly linear, 1.2--1.5 cm, apex curved. Flowers funnelform; pedicel slightly curved, 0.6--1.5 cm. Tepals pale purple-red or purple, oblanceolate-oblong, 2.5--5 × 0.6--1.5 cm, base narrowed, apex obtuse or rounded. Stamens slightly shorter than tepals; filaments 2--3.5 cm; anthers ca. 5 mm. Ovary oblong, 7--8 × ca. 4 mm. Style 1.5--3.2 cm. Fl. Aug. 2&lt;I&gt; n&lt;/I&gt; = 24.

> Rocky places in &lt;I&gt; Quercus&lt;/I&gt; forests, grassy slopes, meadows; 2800--3400 m. Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, Nepal, Sikkim].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt; Fritillaria macrophylla&lt;/I&gt; D. Don, Prodr. Fl. Nepal. 51. 1825; &lt;I&gt; Lilium macrophyllum&lt;/I&gt; (D. Don) Voss.
**3. 大叶假百合**

Notholirion macrophyllum (D. Don) Boiss., Fl. Orient. 5: 190. 1882; Woodc. et Stearn, Lil. world 379, f. 125, 136. 1950. ——Fritillaria macrophylla D. Don, Prodr. Fl. Nepal 51. 1825.——Lilium macrophyllum (D. Don) Voss, Vilmorins Blumeng. 1: l105. 1895.

茎高20-35厘米，无毛。基生叶带形，茎生叶5-10枚，条形，长6-15厘米，宽4-8毫米。总状花序具花2至6朵；苞片叶状，窄条形，长1.2-2.5厘米，先端弯曲；花梗长0.6-2厘米，微弯；花喇叭形，淡紫红色或紫色，花被片倒披针状矩圆形，长2.5-5厘米，宽0.6-1.5厘米，先端钝或为圆形，基部狭窄；花丝丝状，无毛，长2.3-5厘米；花药长椭圆形，长约5毫米；子房矩圆形，长7-8毫米，宽约4毫米；花柱长1.5-3.2厘米，柱头3裂，裂片钻状，稍反卷。花期8月。

产四川、西藏和云南（东北部和西北部）。生草坡和林间草甸，海拔2800-3400米。尼泊尔和锡金也有分布。

全草可作药用。
