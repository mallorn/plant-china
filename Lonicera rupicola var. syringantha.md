红花岩生忍冬
4b.**Lonicera rupicola var. syringantha** (Maximowicz) Zabel in Beissner et al. Handb. Laubholzben. 462. 1903.

## Description
Plants erect or procumbent. Leaves deciduous, abaxially glabrous or sparsely lanate.

  "Reference": "*Lonicera codonantha* appears to be an intermediate form between var. *rupicola* and var. *syringantha*, which has sessile, abaxially villous leaves, and brown villous stems.*Lonicera codonantha* appears to be an intermediate form between var. *rupicola* and var. *syringantha*, which has sessile, abaxially villous leaves, and brown villous stems.

  "Statistics": "Scrub, forest margins, alpine meadows, scree slopes; 2000-4600 m. Gansu, S Ningxia, E Qinghai, Sichuan, Xizang, NW Yunnan [India].

L. syringantha Maxim. var. minor Maxim. in Bull. Acad. Sci. St. Petersb. 24: 49. 1878 (= L. ericoides Pax et K. Hoffm. in Fedde, Repert. Sp. Nov. 12: 494. 1922) 极似本变种，但叶和花冠均较短小，苞片和小苞片均具细睫毛。因未见模式标本，附记于此，留待后考。
