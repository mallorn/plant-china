## Description
Leaves glabrous except aculeolate adaxially along midvein, abaxially glabrous, margin scaberulous.
  "Statistics": "NE China [Japan, Korea].
  "Synonym": "*Rubia mitis* f. *glabrescens* Nakai, J. Jap. Bot. 14: 115. 1938; *R. chinensis* f. *glabrescens* (Nakai) Kitagawa.
**18b. 无毛大砧草（变种）（东北植物检索表）**

Rubia chinensis Regel et Maack var. glabrescens (Nakai) Kitagawa in Lineam. Fl. Mansb. 405. 1939; 东北植物检索表351. 1959; Satake et al. Wildflowers of Japen 13: 51, Pl. 403. 1981. ——Rubia mitis Miq. f. glabrescens Nakai in Journ. Jap. Bot. 14 (2): 115. 1938.

本变种异于原变种在于叶仅上面沿中脉被睫毛状短刺毛，其余部分光滑无毛。

据记载我国见于东北南部。分布于日本和朝鲜。
