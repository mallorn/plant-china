## Description
Stellate hairs of leaf blade abaxial midvein with long central rays. Inflorescences many flowered. Petals 2.5-3.5 mm. Filaments all toothless. Fl. Jun, fr. Jul-Sep.

> * Mixed thickets, valleys; 1100-1800 m. Hebei, Henan, Shaanxi, Shanxi.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Deutzia micrantha&lt;/I&gt; Engler in Diels, Bot. Jahrb. Syst. 36 (Beibl. 82): 51. 1905.

本变种与原变种不同点在于花序多花，花小；花冠直径5-7毫米。叶下被6-9（-12）辐线星状毛，仅沿叶脉具中央长辐线；花丝全钻形。花期6月，果期7-9月。

产河北、山西、陕西南部、河南。生于海拔1100-1800米山谷灌丛中。模式标本采自秦岭。
