## Description
Herbs perennial, densely cespitose, 2-13.5 cm tall. Stem brown crisped glandular villous, hairs denser at leaf axils. Basal leaves caducous; petiole 2-7 mm, brown glandular villous; leaf blade elliptic to oblong, 4-6 × 1.9-2.3 mm, adaxially ± glandular hairy, apex acute. Proximal cauline leaves with petiole 1.2-3.6 mm, glandular villous at margin; leaf blade elliptic to oblong, 5-6.5 × 1.9-2.1 mm, adaxially ± glandular hairy; distal leaves subsessile to sessile, oblong to narrowly ovate, 7-12 × 2-4 mm, adaxially and marginally subglandular hairy, sometimes glabrous on both surfaces. Flower solitary or cyme 2- or 3-flowered; pedicels 2.2-17 mm, glandular hairy. Sepals erect, then spreading to reflexed, subovate to elliptic, 3-4 × 1.5-2.1 mm, ± glandular hairy abaxially and at margin near apex, glandular hairy adaxially distally, veins 3-5, not confluent at apex, apex obtuse, sometimes erose. Petals yellow, elliptic, rarely ovate-elliptic, 4.2-6.1 × 2.4-3.5 mm, 2-callose near base, 3-5(or 6)-veined, base with a claw 0.3-1 mm, apex retuse or obtuse. Stamens 3-4.1 mm. Ovary subsuperior, ovoid, 2.5-3.7 mm; styles 2-2.5 mm. Fl. and fr. Jul-Sep.

多年生草本，密丛生，高2-13.5厘米。茎被褐色卷曲长腺毛，叶腋部毛较多。基生叶具柄，叶片椭圆形至长圆形，长4-6毫米，宽1.9-2.3毫米，先端急尖，两面和边缘多少具腺毛，叶柄长2-7毫米，边缘具褐色长腺毛；茎生叶，下部者具柄，叶片椭圆形至长圆形，长5-6.5毫米，宽1.9-2.1毫米，两面和边缘多少具腺毛，叶柄长1.2-3.6毫米，边缘具长腺毛，中部以上者近无柄至无柄，长圆形至狭卵形，长7-12毫米，宽2-4毫米，腹面和边缘具腺毛，背面近无毛，有时两面均无毛。单花生于茎顶，或聚伞花序具2-3花；花梗长0.22-1.7厘来，被腺毛；萼片在花期由直立变开展至反曲，近卵形至近椭圆形，长3-4毫米，宽1.5-2.1毫米，先端钝（有时啮蚀状），腹面仅上部被腺毛，背面和边缘（仅先端）多少具腺毛，3-5脉于先端不汇合；花瓣黄色，通常椭圆形，稀卵状椭圆形，长4.2-6.1毫米，宽2.4-3.5毫米，先端微凹或钝圆，基部具长0.3-1毫米之爪，3-5（-6）脉，基部侧脉旁具2痂体；雄蕊长3-4.1毫米，花丝钻形；子房近上位，卵球形，长2.5-3.7毫米，花柱2，长2-2.5毫米。花果期7-9月。

产云南西北部。生于海拔3 000-5 300米的高山草甸和高山石隙。模式标本采自云南丽江。
