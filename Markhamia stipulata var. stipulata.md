西南猫尾木 (原变种)",
1a.**Markhamia stipulata var. stipulata**",

## Description
Leaflets pubescent to glabrescent, margin serrulate to subentire. Calyx and capsule floccose. Fl. Sep-Dec, fr. Feb-Mar. 2&lt;I&gt;n&lt;/I&gt; = 40.

> Forests; 300-1700 m. Guangdong, Guangxi, Hainan, S Yunnan [Cambodia, Laos, Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam].
