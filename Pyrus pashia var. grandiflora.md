大花川梨",
14d.**Pyrus pashia var. grandiflora** Cardot",

## Description
Flowers large, ca. 3 cm in diam., leaf blade initially, petiole, pedicel, and sepals adaxially rusty tomentose.

> Guizhou, Yunnan.
**川梨大花变种**

P. pashia D. Don var. grandiflora Card. in Lecomte, Not. Syst. 3: 346. 1918.

花形特大，直径约3厘米；幼叶、叶柄、花梗及萼片内面均被锈色绒毛。

产贵州、云南。
