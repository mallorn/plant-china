四川山梅花
7c.**Philadelphus purpurascens var. szechuanensis** (W. P. Fang) S. M. Hwang

## Description
Branchlets and leaf blade glabrous. Leaf blade on flowering shoots ovate-lanceolate, base cuneate, apex acute. Raceme rachis 4-10 cm. Calyx tube dark brown. Fl. Jun.


> * Mixed forests, mountain slopes; ca. 2800 m. Sichuan, Yunnan.

  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Philadelphus szechuanensis&lt;/I&gt; W. P. Fang, Acta Sci. Nat. Univ. Szechuan. 1: 36. 1955.

**7c. 四川山梅花（变种）**

var. szechuanensis (Fang) S. M. Hwang in Acta Bot. Aus. Sin. 7: 4. 1991.——P. szechuanensis Fang in Acta Sci. Nat. Univ. Szechuan 1: 36. Pl. 2. 1955.

本变种与原变种不同点在于花萼暗褐色；花序轴长4.5-10厘米。花期6月。

产云南和四川。生于海拔2800米左右山坡林缘灌丛中。模式标本采自四川理县。
