## Description
Roots sparsely pubescent or glabrescent. Leaf blade light green, slightly marked with purple abaxially. Petals 0.6-0.7 cm wide; lip narrowly globose, ca. 1 cm in diam., subtruncate at apex. Staminode white, slightly veined with pale purple in apical half, 0.4-0.5 cm wide. Fl. Feb-Mar.
