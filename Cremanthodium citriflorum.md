9.**Cremanthodium citriflorum** R. D. Good J. Linn. Soc., Bot. 48: 277. 1929.",

## Description
Stem solitary, erect, 25-42 cm tall, ca. 4 mm in diam. at base, proximally glabrous, distally white arachnoid-pilose. Basal leaves petiolate; petiole ca. 11.5 cm, glabrous, base enlarged sheathed; sheath to 6 cm; leaf blade green on both surfaces, reniform, ca. 5 × to 6 cm, glabrous, palmately veined, margin minutely crenate and ciliate, apex rounded; sinus broad. Middle to distal stem leaves only enlarged sheathed; sheath 4-7 × to 4 cm, glabrous, margin obtusely dentate. Capitulum solitary, nodding. Involucre black when dry, hemispheric, ca. 2 × to 2.5 cm, outside glabrous, base pilose; phyllaries 12, in 2 rows, purple, apex obtuse or acute; outer phyllaries lanceolate or oblong, 4-6 mm wide; inner phyllaries broadly elliptic, 7-10 mm, margin broadly membranous. Ray florets yellow or lemon-colored; lamina ovate-lanceolate, 4-5 cm, base to 1 cm wide, with brown veins, apex caudate-acuminate, 3-lobed; tube 4-5 mm. Tubular florets numerous, yellowish brown, 8-10 mm; tube ca. 2 mm; limb campanulate-tubular, ca. 3 mm wide. Achenes fusiform, 6-7 mm, narrowed at both ends, ribbed. Pappus brown, shorter than tubular corolla. Fl. and fr. Aug.
  "Statistics": "Alpine meadows; 3600-4000 m. NW Yunnan [Myanmar].

Cremanthodium citriflorum Good in Journ. Linn. Soc. Bot. 48: 277. 1929; S. Y. Hu in Quart. Journ. Taiwan Mus. 19: 207. 1966; 13. Koyama in Mem. Fac. Sci. Kyoto Univ. Ser. Biol. 2: 55. 1968, in add.

多年生草本。根肉质，多数。茎单生，直立，高25-42厘米，上部被白色蛛丝状柔毛，下部光滑，有棱，基部直径约4毫米。丛生叶和茎基部叶具柄，柄长11.5厘米，光滑，下部具膨大的、长达6厘米的鞘，叶片肾形，长约5厘米，宽至6厘米，先端圆形，边缘具小圆齿，齿端有骨质小尖头，齿间具睫毛，基部弯缺宽，两面光滑，叶脉掌状；茎中上部叶仅有膨大的叶鞘，鞘长4-7厘米，宽达4厘米，边缘具钝齿，光滑。头状花序单生，下垂，辐射状，总苞半球形，基部被毛，长约2厘米，宽至2.5厘米，总苞片12，2层，内层宽椭圆形，宽7-10毫米，具宽的膜质边缘，外层披针形或长圆形，宽4-6毫米，全部总苞片先端钝或急尖，背部光滑，干时黑色。舌状花黄色或柠檬色，舌片长卵状披针形，长4-5厘米，基部宽达1厘米，先端尾状渐尖，3浅裂，脉纹褐色，管部长4-5毫米；管状花黄褐色，多数，长8-10毫米，管部长约2毫米，檐部钟状筒形，宽约3毫米，冠毛褐色，短于花冠。瘦果纺锤形，长6-7毫米，两端狭缩，具明显的纵肋。花果期8月。

产云南西部。生于海拔约4000米的高山草地。在缅甸东北部（模式标本产地）也有分布。

尽管本种的原始描述是根据不完整的标本写成的，但总苞片、舌状花的形状等特征却很显著，我们仍然予以承认。它与红花垂头菊 Cremanthodium farreri W. W. Smith 相近，但叶光滑无毛，舌状花黄色，舌片长卵状披针形，先端尾状渐尖，冠毛褐色而有明显的区别。
