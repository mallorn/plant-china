388b.**Rhododendron alutaceum var. russotinctum** (I. B. Balfour & Forrest) D. F. Chamberlain",

## Description
Young shoots densely glandular; bud scales ± persistent. Leaf blade 5–9.5 × 1.5–2.5 cm; abaxial surface rust-red, upper indumentum thick, loose, felted, ± deciduous. Ovary densely tomentose and shortly glandular-hairy. Fl. Jun–Jul, fr. Oct.

> Coniferous forests, thickets, mountains; 3300–4200 m. SE Xizang, NW Yunnan.

Rhododendron alutaceum Balf. f. et W. W. Smith var. russotinctum (Balf. f. et Forrest) Chamb. ex Cullen et Chamb. in Not. Bot. Card. Edinb. 36: 119. 1978；Chamb. in Not. Bot. Gard. 本变种与原变种的区别在于幼枝密被短柄腺体，芽鳞多少宿存，叶较窄而稍短，通常窄长圆形，长5-9.5厘米，宽1.5-2.5厘米，下面毛被锈红色，易脱落，子房密被绒毛和短柄腺体。花期6-7月，果期10月。

产云南西北部和西藏东南部。生于海拔3 300-4 200米的高山针叶林下或杜鹃灌丛中。模式标本采自云南德钦。
