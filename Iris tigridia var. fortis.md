大粗根鸢尾",
54b.**Iris tigridia var. fortis** Y. T. Zhao",

> &lt;I&gt; Iris ivanovae&lt;/I&gt; Doronkin (Fl. Sibir., Araceae-Orchidaceae, 117. 1987) was recorded in its protologue not only from Mongolia and Russia (Siberia) but also from China. No specimens have been seen by the present authors, but it is said to differ from &lt;I&gt; I. tigridia&lt;/I&gt; in having narrower leaves (to 2 mm wide), narrowly lanceolate spathes, smaller flowers (to 3.5 cm in diam.), and outer perianth segments abruptly narrowed into a filiform claw.

## Description
Leaves 10--20 cm × 3--6 mm. Flowering stems 10--20 cm. Flowers 4.5--5 cm in diam.

> * Forest margins, grasslands on sunny hillsides. C Jilin, E Nei Mongol, Shanxi.
**58a．大粗根鸢尾**

var. fortis Y. T. Zhao, 植物分类学报18 (1): 60. 1980.

本变种与原变种的区别点在于：叶较长而宽，长10-20厘米，宽3-6毫米；花茎高10-20厘米，直径1.5-2毫米；苞片长4-5厘米，宽8-10毫米；花蓝紫色，直径4.5-5厘米；花被管长约2.5厘米，外花被裂片倒卵形，长约5厘米，宽约1.5厘米，内花被裂片倒披针形，长约4厘米，宽约8毫米；雄蕊长约2厘米；花柱分枝长约2.5厘米，顶端裂片钝三角形，子房长约1.8厘米。花期5月，果期6-8月。

产吉林、内蒙古、山西。生于向阳山坡及林缘草地。模式标本采自吉林市郊龙潭山。
