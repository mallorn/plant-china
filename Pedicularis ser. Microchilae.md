## Description
Herbs annual or perennial. Leaves in whorls of 3 or 4, elliptic to lanceolate, pinnatisect to pinnatifid. Inflorescence spicate to capitate. Calyx lobes 5. Corolla &amp;#177; S-shaped; tube deflexed in calyx; galea truncate or toothed at apex.
