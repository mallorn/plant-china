## Description
Plants smooth. Leaves leaf blade elliptic, ovate, broadly obovate, or sometimes orbicular, to 3 cm wide, apex obtuse-rounded. Fl. Jul.

> Sandy, sunny, and grassy places on mountains; 1000-1800 m. Hebei, Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaoning, Shandong [Korea, Russia].
