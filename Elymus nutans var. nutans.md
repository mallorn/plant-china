重穗披碱草(原变种)",
8a.**Elymus nutans var. nutans**",

> This variety is a good forage grass.

## Description
Culms 50–70 cm tall. Spikelets with 2 glumes. Glumes 3- or 4-veined. First lemma ca. 10 mm; awn 12–20 mm. Fl. and fr. Jul–Aug. 2*n* = 42*.

> Mountain slopes, grasslands, riverside sands and pebbles. Gansu, Hebei, Henan, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Xinjiang, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, India, Japan, Mongolia, Nepal; C and SW Asia].
  "Synonym": "*Clinelymus nutans* (Grisebach) Nevski.
