嵩明木半夏",
62b.**Elaeagnus angustata var. songmingensis** W. K. Hu & H. F. Chow ex C. Y. Chang",

## Description
Leaf blade adaxially darker, with overlapping scales similar to those on abaxial surface; scales often shallowly bowl-shaped, not so persistent. Fl. Apr-May, fr. Jul-Aug.

> ● Mountain scrub; 2400-3000 m. N Yunnan.
与原变种的区别在于本变种幼枝具白色或黄白色鳞片；叶片上面具白色和散生黄色鳞片；花梗、萼筒和包围子房的萼管被银白色鳞片。
