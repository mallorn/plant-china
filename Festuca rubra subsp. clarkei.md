## Description
Panicle branches scabrid. Spikelets with 3–5 florets; lemmas glabrous or with very short hairs, 6–8 mm; awn 3–5.5 mm. Anthers 1–2 mm.

> Mountain slopes; 2000–3600 m. Yunnan [Bhutan, N India, Kashmir, Pakistan].
  "Synonym": "*Festuca rubra* var. *clarkei* Stapf in J. D. Hooker, Fl. Brit. India 7: 353. 1896 [\"1897\"]; *F. clarkei* (Stapf) B. S. Sun.
