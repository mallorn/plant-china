## Description
Leaves glabrescent adaxially, sparsely strigose abaxially. Inflorescence lax, pendulous. Corolla tube inside glabrous or pubescent near middle; lobes glabrous outside. Fl. Mar-Apr.
  "Statistics": "● Forests or thickets on mountains; 500-1800 m. W Yunnan.
**19b. 悬花水锦树（变种）（植物分类学报）下垂黄皮花树（云南种子植物名录）**

var. dependens Cowan in Not. Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 16: 292. 1932; W. C. Chen in Acta Phytotax. Sinica 21 (4): 395. 1983.

本变种与原变种不同的是花序较疏而悬垂；花冠较开展；叶上面近无毛，下面被疏糙伏毛。花期3-4月。

产于云南西部；生于海拔500-1778米的山地林中或灌丛中。模式标本采自云南西部。
