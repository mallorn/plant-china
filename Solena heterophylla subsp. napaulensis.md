## Description
Plants monoecious. Anther cells conduplicate. Fruit broadly ovoid, 2.5-3 × ca. 2.5 cm, glabrous, apex and base obtuse. Fl. May-Jun, fr. Jun-Aug.
  "Statistics": "Mountain slopes, grasslands, thickets; 2000-2300 m. SE Xizang, Yunnan [N India, Myanmar, Nepal].
  "Synonym": "*Bryonia napaulensis* Seringe in Candolle, Prodr. 3: 307. 1828.
