## Description
Perennials, 15-40 cm tall, grayish, glabrous. Stems ascending, often branched. Leaves opposite or in whorls of 3 or 4, often irregularly whorled or alternate upward, sessile, ovate, obovate, or oblong, 1.5-3 X 0.5-1.5 cm, base obtuse to subcuneate, apex obtuse to subacute; veins obscurely 3-campylodromous. Inflorescences racemose; bracts similar to but much smaller than leaves. Pedicel 3-5 mm. Calyx lobes ovate to lanceolate, 2.5-4 X 1.5-2.5 mm. Corolla bright yellow, 1.2-1.7 cm (excluding spur); spur 3-6 mm, straight; lateral lobes of lower lip 3-5 mm wide, middle lobe narrower; upper lip longer than lower. Capsule globose, ca. 6 mm in diam. Seeds reniform, ca. 2.5 X 1.5 mm, margin thickened. Fl. Aug.

> Sandy beaches; near sea level. Liaoning [Japan, Korea, Russia (Far East)].
**8.海滨柳穿鱼（东北植物检索表）**

Linaria japonica Miq. Ann. Mus. Bot. Lugd.-Bat. 2: 115, 1865-1866; Kom. Fl. Mansh. 3: 412, 1905.

多年生草本，无毛，带灰色。茎上升，常分枝，高15-40厘米。叶对生或3-4枚轮生，上部的常不规则轮生或互生，无柄，卵形、倒卵形或矩圆形，长1.5-3厘米、宽5-15毫米，顶端钝至近于急尖，基部圆钝至多少楔形，有不很清晰的3出弧状脉。总状花序顶生；苞片与叶同形，但小得多；花梗长3-5毫米；花萼裂片卵形至披针形，长2.5-4毫米，宽1.5-2.5毫米；花冠亮黄色，长12-17毫米，上唇长于下唇，下唇侧裂片宽达3-5毫米，中裂片较窄，距长3-6毫米，伸直。蒴果球状，直径6毫米。种子肾形，长2.5毫米，宽1.5毫米，边缘增厚。花期8月。

产辽宁。朝鲜，日本及苏联远东地区也有。生海边砂地。我们未见我国的标本。
