棱喙毛茛(原变种)",
108a.**Ranunculus trigonus var. trigonus**",

## Description
Stems spreading pubescent, sometimes appressed pubescent above. Petiole spreading pubescent. Petals 3.2--6 mm.

> * Grassy slopes, meadows, woods, by streams or lakes; 1300--3300 m. SW Sichuan, SE Xizang (Zayü Xian), Yunnan.
