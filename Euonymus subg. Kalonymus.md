## Description
Evergreen or deciduous shrubs to small trees; winter buds conical, large, 0.7-2 cm. Flowers 4- or 5-merous. Anthers sessile, of 1 theca. Ovaries 4- or 5-loculed. Capsule with wings, 4- or 5-lobed at maturity.
  "Statistics": "Sixteen species: Asia; 15 species (eight endemic) in China.
