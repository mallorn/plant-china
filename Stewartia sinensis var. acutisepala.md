尖萼紫茎",
13b.**Stewartia sinensis var. acutisepala** (P. L. Chiu & G. R. Zhong) T. L. Ming & J. Li",

## Description
Current year branchlets, winter buds, petioles, pedicels, bracteoles, and sepals glabrous or glabrescent. Pedicel (1.5-)2-3 cm; bracteoles and sepals long ovate to ovate-lanceolate, apex acuminate to long acuminate. Fl. May-Jun, fr. Oct-Nov.

> ●  Forests; 1400-1700 m. Zhejiang.
  "Synonym": "*Stewartia acutisepala* P. L. Chiu &amp; G. R. Zhong, Acta Bot. Yunnan. 10: 57. 1988.
