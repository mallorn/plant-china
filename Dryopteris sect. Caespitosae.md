## Description
Lamina 2-pinnate-pinnatifid. Pinnules or lobes sharply serrate. Indusia small, membranous, lacerate or erose.
  "Statistics": "About ten species: mainly in high mountains above 3500 m of E Himalaya, eastward to S China, Russia, and one species in Europe and North America; seven species (one endemic) in China.
