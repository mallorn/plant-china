毛峨眉翠雀花",
84c.**Delphinium omeiense var. pubescens** W. T. Wang",

> This variety is similar to &lt;I&gt; Delphinium&lt;/I&gt; &lt;I&gt; tongolense,&lt;/I&gt; but differs in having a puberulent stem.

## Description
Stem and petioles appressed puberulent. Sepals 1--1.2 cm; spur 1.6--1.8 cm.

> * Forest margins, scrub; 2200--2600 m. SW Sichuan (Muli Zang Zu Zizhixian).
**56b.柔毛峨眉翠雀花（变种）**

Delphinium omeiense var. pubescens W. T. Wang in Addenda.

与峨眉翠雀花的区别：茎和叶柄被反曲并贴伏的白色短柔毛。

产四川西南部（木里至西昌一带）。生海拔2200-2600米山地灌丛中或林边。
