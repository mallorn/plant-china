光果悬钩子(原变种)",
80a.**Rubus glabricarpus var. glabricarpus**",

## Description
Branchlets, petioles, and pedicel soft hairy, with stalked glands. Aggregate fruit ca. 1 cm in diam.

> Slopes, bases of mountains, river banks, mixed forests; Low to medium elevations. Fujian, Jiangsu, Zhejiang.
  "Synonym": "*Rubus corchorifolius* Linnaeus f. var. *neillioides* Focke; *R. neillioides* (Focke) Migo.
