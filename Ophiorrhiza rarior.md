## Description
Herbs, apparently ascending; stems moderately brown villous or -hirsute with multicellular trichomes to glabrescent, densely lenticellate when young. Petiole 4-6 cm, sparsely villous; leaf blade drying membranous-papery, adaxially olive-green, abaxially pale, narrowly elliptic-oblong, 13-21 × 4.5-5 cm, sparsely strigose adaxially, abaxially glabrescent except sparsely hirsute along veins, base acute, margins subentire, ciliate, apex acuminate or acute; secondary veins 14-18 pairs; stipules caducous, not seen. Inflorescences and flowers not seen. Infructescences branched to several orders, villous; peduncle ca. 8.5 cm, 4-angled; axes 4-5.5 cm, helicoid; bracts lanceolate to linear-subulate, 4-8 mm, ciliate, persistent. Capsules mitriform, ca. 4 × 10-11 mm, crisped villous. Fl. Jun.

**30. 毛果蛇根草（植物研究）**

Ophiorrhiza rarior Lo in Bull. Bot. Res. 10 (2): 38. 1990.

草本，除老枝外各部或多或少被褐色多细胞皱卷长毛；小枝圆柱状，较粗壮，嫩枝有密集的皮孔，被长硬毛。叶膜状纸质，狭长圆形，长13-21厘米，宽4.5-5厘米，顶端渐尖或短尖，基部渐狭，边近全缘，上面榄绿色，散生糙毛，下面苍白，脉上疏生硬毛；中脉在下面压扁，侧脉每边14-18条，在下面压扁，斜升；叶柄长4-6厘米，疏被长柔毛。花未见。果序顶生，被长柔毛，总梗长8.5厘米，有4棱角，分枝7-8条，长4-5.5厘米，螺状；苞片线状钻形，长7-8毫米，小苞片披针形，长4-5毫米，被长缘毛；蒴果僧帽状，长约4毫米，宽10-11毫米，被长柔毛。果期6月。

我国特有，产于广西龙州金龙；生林中湿处。模式标本采自龙州金龙板闭。
