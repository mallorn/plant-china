## Description
Herbs perennial. Stems ca. 16 cm, much branched, sparsely strigose. Petiole 5-8 mm, absent in apical leaves; leaf blade cuneate, 1.2-2 cm &amp;#215; 7-12 mm, 3-lobed, papery, base attenuate, forming a winged petiole; lobes of basal leaves with 2 or more triangular lobules, short spinescent, glabrous, dusty glandular. Verticillasters 4-6-flowered; bracteoles spinescent, 4-7 mm. Calyx narrowly campanulate, ca. 13 &amp;#215; 6.5 mm, densely bristly; teeth triangular, subequal, 4-5 mm, straight or slightly oblique. Corolla ca. 2.6 cm; limb sparsely hairy, densely glandular; upper lip straight, lobes ca. 5 mm, apex rounded to truncate; middle lobe of lower lip ca. 2.5 &amp;#215; 6.5 mm, apex 2-lobulate, lobules and lateral lobes triangular. Anterior stamens to 1/2 as long as upper corolla lip. Ovary glabrous.

> Gravelly slopes. Xinjiang [Kazakhstan].

**2．硬毛兔唇花**

Lagochilus hirtus Fisch. et Mey. Enum. Pl. Nov. 33. 1841; Benth. in DC. Prodr. 12: 516. 1848; Ledeb. Fl. Ross. 3: 433. 1849; Knorr. in Fl. URSS 21: 165 Lagochilopsis hirtus (Fisch. et Mey. ) Knorr. in Nov. Syst. Pl. Vasc. 197. 1966, syn. nov.

多年生植物，高15-20厘米。茎自基部木质化，多数，铺散，下部无毛或被稀疏的毛茸，具花部分有长毛茸。叶楔状，无毛，不深的3-5裂，裂片卵圆形，钝的，仅上部的叶片为渐尖的；苞片长8-10毫米，钻状刺尖，密被长的糙伏毛及具2-3节的粗毛茸和头状腺体。轮伞花序具6-8花。花萼管状钟形，密被毛茸，毛茸为具1-4节粗的及具关节紧缩的，萼齿狭三角形，长3-4毫米，等长，直立的，长为萼筒1/3至1/2。花冠粉红色，为萼长之2倍，上唇深裂至1/3处，分成2长圆形的裂片，下唇3裂，中裂片短而微缺，具2宽卵圆形裂片，侧裂片卵圆形。雄蕊花丝比花冠短。小坚果无腺点。花期5-8月。（标本未见，摘自前人描写）

产新疆南、北部；生于山麓及前山带。苏联也有。模式标本采自苏联。
