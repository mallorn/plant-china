弯缺阴山荠(原亚种)",
8a.**Yinshania sinuata subsp. sinuata**",

## Description
Lowermost leaves always simple. Fruit 5-10 mm. Seeds 0.8-0.9 mm. Fl. Mar, fr. May. 2n = 42*.

> * Dense forests, mountain slopes, rock crevices, shady moist sand; near sea level to 200 m. Anhui, Guangdong, Hunan, Jiangxi.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Cochlearia sinuata&lt;/I&gt; K. C. Kuan, Bull. Bot. Lab. N. E. Forest. Inst., Harbin 1980(8): 39. 1980; &lt;I&gt;Hilliella sinuata&lt;/I&gt; (K. C. Kuan) Y. H. Zhang &amp; H. W. Li.
