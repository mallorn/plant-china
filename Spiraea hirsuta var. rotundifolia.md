30b.**Spiraea hirsuta var. rotundifolia** (Hemsley) Rehder",

## Description
Leaf blades ovate, base broadly cuneate to rounded.

> Forests, thickets, stream sides; 600--2000 m. Henan, Hubei, Shaanxi, Sichuan.
  "Synonym": "*Spiraea blumei* var. *rotundifolia* Hemsley, J. Linn. Soc., Bot. 23: 224. 1887; *S. blumei* var. *maximowicziana* (C. K. Schneider) Dunn; *S. maximowicziana* C. K. Schneider.
