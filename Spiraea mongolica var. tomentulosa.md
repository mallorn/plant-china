毛枝蒙古绣线菊",
57b.**Spiraea mongolica var. tomentulosa** T. T. Yu",

## Description
Branchlets, buds, and petioles densely shortly tomentose. Follicles wholly pubescent.

> Forests, thickets, sunny rocky slopes, stream sides; 1500--4700 m. Gansu, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Xinjiang, Xizang.
  "Synonym": "*Spiraea mongolica* var. *pubescens* Y. Z. Zhao &amp; T. J. Wang; *S. tomentulosa* (T. T. Yü) Y. Z. Zhao (1987), not Rydberg (1908).
**蒙古绣线菊毛枝变种**

S. mongolica Maxim. var. tomentulosa Yu, 植物分类学报8: 216. 1963.

本变种与原变种的异点在其小枝、叶柄及冬芽多少密被短绒毛。

产内蒙古、甘肃、宁夏、新疆。生于阳坡岩石上，海拔1500-1700米。模式标本采自宁夏阿拉善左旗。
