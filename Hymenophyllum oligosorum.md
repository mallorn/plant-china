长毛蕗蕨",
9.**Hymenophyllum oligosorum** Makino Bot. Mag. (Tokyo). 13: 44. 1899.",

## Description
Rhizome long creeping, glabrescent. Stipe 5-10 mm, wingless except at base of lamina. Rachis winged to base, wings flat, entire. Lamina bipinnatifid to tripinnatifid, ovate to lanceolate, 1-5 cm. Fronds abaxially with persistent long hairs along stipe, rachis, and veins. Involucre ovate or rounded, apex slightly toothed. 2*n* = 42.

> Usually on tree trunks in forests; 600-1600 m. Jiangxi, Taiwan [Japan, Korea].
  "Synonym": "*Mecodium oligosorum* (Makino) H. Itô.
