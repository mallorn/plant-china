## Description
Plants less than 1 m tall. Sori not close to costa.

> ● Damp banks of streams and crevices in stone walls; 1700-2000 m. Guizhou (Fanjing Shan).
  "Synonym": "*Dryopteris kweichowicola* Ching ex P. S. Wang, J. Arnold Arbor. 71: 119. 1990.
**50b.贵州鳞毛蕨（变种）**

Dryopteris wallichiana (Spreng.) Hylander var. kweichowicola (Ching et P. S. Wang) S. K. Wu, comb. nov. ——Dryopteris kweichowicola Ching et P. S. Wang in Journ. Arn. Arb. 71: 120, fig. 1. 1990.

本变种与原变种区别在于形体较小，孢子囊群位于羽片中部，不紧靠中肋。

特产贵州三江口。
