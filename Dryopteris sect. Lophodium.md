## Description
Plants similar to *Dryopteris* sect. *Marginatae*, but scales at base of stipe thicker, normally bicolorous, and margin of pinnules with sharply acute teeth.
  "Statistics": "About ten species: N temperate zone and Africa; two species in China.
  "Synonym": "*Lophodium* Newman, Phytologist 4: 371. 1851.
  "keylist": "
1 Stipe as long as lamina, scales concolorous, lamina bipinnate-pinnatifid, pinnae shortly acuminate; sori near apex of veinlet.[*D. carthusiana* 刺叶鳞毛蕨](Dryopteris carthusiana.md)
1 Stipe shorter than lamina, scales bicolorous, lamina bipinnate-pinnatisect, pinnae acuminate; sori on middle of veinlet.[*D. expansa* 广布鳞毛蕨",](Dryopteris expansa.md)
