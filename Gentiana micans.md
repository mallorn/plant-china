> &lt;I&gt;Gentiana micans&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;latifolia&lt;/I&gt; T. N. Ho is endemic to Bhutan.

## Description
Annuals 3-6 cm tall. Stems branched from base, with many short branches crowded into a dense tuft, glabrous. Basal leaves withered at anthesis; petiole 0.5-1.5 mm; leaf blade ovate to ovate-orbicular, 4-7 &amp;#215; 2-3 mm, margin cartilaginous, apex obtuse and mucronate; veins 1-3, prominent. Stem leaves crowded, folded; petiole tube 1-2 mm; leaf blade subulate, 0.8-1.2 cm &amp;#215; 1.5-2 mm, subleathery, rigid, glossy, margin basal half broadly membranous and ciliolate, apical half cartilaginous and smooth, apex acute, midvein abaxially prominent. Flowers few, subsessile. Calyx narrowly obconic, (0.8-)1.1-1.5 cm, tube membranous; lobes subulate, 6-11 mm, as long as or longer than tube, subleathery, rigid, glossy, margin basal half broadly membranous and ciliolate, apical half cartilaginous and smooth, apex acuminate and apiculate, midvein prominent. Corolla pale blue, tubular, (1.1-)1.5-2 cm; lobes ovate, (3-)4-6 mm, margin entire, apex obtuse and mucronate; plicae oblong, 1.5-2 mm, margin entire or 2cleft with both segments denticulate, apex obtuse. Stamens inserted at middle of corolla tube, equal; filaments (2.5-)4-8 mm; anthers linear, 1-1.2 mm. Style 1-1.5 mm; stigma lobes broadly oblong. Capsules obovoid-ellipsoid, 6-7 mm; gynophore to 2.5 cm, slender. Seeds dark brown, ellipsoid, 0.5-0.7 mm. Fl. and fr. May-Sep.

> Alpine meadows; 4300-4800 m. SE Xizang [Bhutan, Nepal, Sikkim]
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Gentiana sororcula&lt;/I&gt; Burkill.
**154. 亮叶龙胆 图版31: 5-7**

Gentiana micans C. B. Clarke in Hook, f. Fl. Brit. Ind. 4: 112. 1883: W. W. Smith et G. H. Cave in Rec. Bot. Surv. Ind. 4: 223. 1911; Hara, Chater et Williams, Enum. Fl. Pl. Nepal 3: 93. 1982.

一年生草本，高1.5-2厘米。茎黄绿色，光滑，自基部多分枝，形成垫状。叶坚硬，革质，发亮；基生叶小，在花期枯萎，宿存，卵圆形，长4-5毫米，宽3-4毫米，先端钝，具短小尖头，边缘软骨质，光滑，两面光滑，叶脉1-3条，软骨质，在下面突起，叶柄膜质，光滑，连合成长0.5毫米的筒；茎生叶大，对折，密集，覆瓦状排列，三角状钻形，长4-6毫米，宽1.5-2毫米，先端急尖或渐尖，边缘有细乳突，上缘软骨质，下缘膜质，且愈向基部膜质边愈宽，两面光滑，中脉软骨质，在下面突起，叶柄膜质，光滑，连合成长1-2毫米的筒。花多数，单生于小枝顶端，下部连花梗均藏于茎生叶中；近无花梗；花萼倒锥状筒形，长8-11毫米，萼筒膜质，裂片坚硬，革质，发亮，绿色，三角状钻形，长4-5毫米，先端渐尖，具小尖头，边缘具细乳突，中部以下膜质，其余软骨质，两面光滑，中脉软骨质，在背面呈脊状突起，并向萼筒下延，弯缺窄，截形；花冠淡蓝紫色，喉部具一圈蓝灰色环纹，宽筒形，长11-13毫米，裂片卵形，长2-3毫米，先端钝，具细尖，褶卵状矩圆形，长0.8-1.5毫米，先端钝，浅2裂，稀不裂，边缘有不整齐细齿；雄蕊着生于冠筒中部，整齐，花丝丝状钻形，长2-2.5毫米，花药直立，线形，长1-1.2毫米；子房狭椭圆形，长5-5.5毫米，两端渐狭，柄长1-2毫米，花柱圆柱形，连柱头长1毫米，柱头2裂，裂片宽矩圆形。蒴果外露或内藏，狭矩圆形，长6-7毫米，两端钝，边缘具由上至下渐狭的翅，柄直立，长至12毫米；种子深褐色，椭圆形，长0.5-0.7毫米，表面具细网纹。花果期5-9月。

产我国西藏南部。生于山坡草甸，海拔4300-4800米。尼泊尔、锡金（模式标本产地）也有分布。
