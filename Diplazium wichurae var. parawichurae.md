## Description
Pinnae short, with uniserrate margin, apex acute or subrounded.

> ● Forests in wet valleys. Jiangsu (Yixing).
  "Synonym": "Basionym: *Allantodia parawichurae* Ching, Fl. Jiangsu. 1: 465. 1977; *A. wichurae* var. *parawichurae* (Ching) W. M. Chu &amp; Z. R. He.
**19b. 龙池短肠蕨（江苏植物志）（新等级）**

var. parawichurae (Ching) W. M. Chu et Z. R. He, st. nov. ——Allantodia parawichurae Ching, 江苏植物志上册: 42, 465, 图55. 1977.

与原变种的区别在于侧生羽片较短，先端急尖或近钝头，边缘的锯齿为尖头单锯齿而不为重锯齿。

特产于江苏（宜兴龙池山）。生于山地林下阴湿沟谷中。
