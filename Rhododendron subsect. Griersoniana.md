## Description
Small shrubs; young shoots densely glandular-setulose and woolly-tomentose; bud scales long, linear-cuspidate. Leaf blade leathery, elliptic; abaxial surface densely white- or brown-tomentose. Inflorescence lax, 5–12-flowered; calyx minute, ca. 1 mm; corolla tubular or funnel-campanulate, deep rose to scarlet, outer surface of tube densely tomentose; stamens 10; filaments pubescent at base; ovary densely glandular and tomentose; style glabrous.
  "Statistics": "One species: China, Myanmar.
