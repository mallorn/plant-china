马肠子树(原变种)
11a.**Pentapanax tomentellus var. tomentellus** (C. B. Clarke) Ridley

## Description
Leaflets abaxially subglabrous. Inflorescence ca. 30 cm. Styles united to middle.


> ● Shaded thickets, along streams, rocky areas, often on limestone; 1200-2600 m. SW Sichuan, Yunnan.

  "Synonym": "*Aralia tomentella* Franchet, J. Bot. (Morot) 10: 304. 1896; *Pentapanax henryi* Harms var. *larium* (Handel-Mazzetti) Handel-Mazzetti; *P. larium* Handel-Mazzetti.
