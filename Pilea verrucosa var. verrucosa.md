疣果冷水花(原变种)",
16a.**Pilea verrucosa var. verrucosa**",

> After publication, it was found that the correct name of this taxon is *Pilea gracilis* Handel-Mazzetti (see Chen &amp; Monro, Novon 17: 26. 2007).&#x0D;\n&lt;p&gt;&#x0D;\nThe plants are used medicinally to stimulate the spleen and relieve edema.

## Description
Petiole 1-7 cm; leaf blade elliptic or oblong lanceolate, 3-18 × 1.8-5 cm, papery, 3-veined, base rounded or broadly cuneate; Achene ca. 0.7 mm, verrucose. Fl. Apr-May, fr. May-Jul.

> Shaded wet places in forests, near streams; 400-1600 m. Chongqing, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hubei, Hunan, Sichuan, Yunnan [N Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Pilea* *gracillis* Handel-Mazzetti; *P*. *nanchuanensis* C. J. Chen; *P*. *purpurella* C. J. Chen; *P*. *symmeria* Weddell var. *subcoriacea* Handel-Mazzetti.
