中国金丝桃",
57b.**Hypericum perforatum subsp. chinense** N. Robson",

## Description
Leaves all petiolate. Inflorescence and/or partial inflorescences congested; branches relatively long, curved-ascending. Petal laminar glands all black or absent.

> ● 400-2200 m. Gansu, Guizhou, Hebei, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan [Japan (introduced)].
