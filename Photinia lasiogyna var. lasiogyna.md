倒卵叶石楠(原变种)",
11a.**Photinia lasiogyna var. lasiogyna**",

## Description
Rachis, pedicels, and hypanthium abaxially densely tomentose.

> Thickets, forests, grassy slopes, fields; 1900--2600 m. Sichuan, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Eriobotrya lasiogyna* Franchet, Pl. Delavay. 225. 1890; *Photinia mairei* H. Léveillé; *Stranvaesia glaucescens* Lindley var. *yunnanensis* Franchet.
