多茎景天(原亚种)",
85a.**Sedum multicaule subsp. multicaule**",

## Description
Stems smooth when dry. Sepals linear to linear-lanceolate, unequal, apex acuminate and apiculate. Nectar scales spatulate-quadrangular. Fl. Jul-Aug, fr. Aug-Sep.

> Rocks on forested slopes, rock crevices, rock walls; 1300-3500 m. Gansu, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, India, Myanmar, Nepal, Pakistan, Sikkim].
