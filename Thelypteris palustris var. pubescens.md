毛叶沼泽蕨
1b.**Thelypteris palustris var. pubescens** (G. Lawson) Fernald Rhodora. 31: 34. 1929.

## Description
Rachises, costae, and veinlets covered with multicellular acicular long hairs abaxially. 2*n* = 70.


> Wet meadows and marshes; below 800 m. Heilongjiang, N Jiangsu, Jilin, Shandong [temperate regions of E Asia and North America].

  "Synonym": "*Lastrea thelypteris* var. *pubescens* G. Lawson, Edinburgh New Philos. J. 19: 277. 1864.
