薄瓣悬钩子(原变种)",
59a.**Rubus croceacanthus var. croceacanthus**",

## Description
Branchlets, petioles, inflorescences, and abaxial surface of calyx with soft hairs. 2n = 14*.

> Forest clearings, forest margins, roadsides, landslides, grasslands, river banks; sea level to medium elevations. Taiwan [Cambodia, India, Japan (Ryukyu Islands), Laos, Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Rubus cardotii* Koidzumi; *R. euphlebophyllus* Hayata; *R. piptopetalus* Hayata ex Koidzumi; *R. rosifolius* Smith var. *formosanus* Cardot; *R. sphaerocephalus* Hayata.
