娃娃山柳",
101b.**Salix opsimantha var. wawashanica** (P. Y. Mao & P. X. He) G. Zhu",

## Description
Branchlets and young leaves yellowish downy. Bracts of male catkin glabrous; bracts of female catkin pilose on both surfaces.

> *  Mountain slopes; 3200-4700 m. Yunnan (Jingdong Xian, Yangbi Xian)
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Salix oreinoma&lt;/I&gt; C. K. Schneider var. &lt;I&gt;wawashanica&lt;/I&gt; P. Y. Mao &amp; P. X. He, Acta Bot. Yunnan. 9: 47. 1987.
