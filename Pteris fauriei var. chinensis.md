## Description
Lateral pinnae broadly lanceolate, 16-22 × 4-6 cm; median segments of pinnae 20-35 × (5-)6-8 mm.

> ● Forests along valleys; 300-700 m. Fujian (Chongan), Guangdong (Gaoyao, Qujiang, Yunfu), Guangxi, S Guizhou (Dushan), Hainan (Chengmai, Danxian, Lingao).
  "Synonym": "*Pteris bifurcata* Ching.

产台湾、福建（崇安）、广东（高要、云浮、曲江）、海南（临高、澄迈、儋县）、广西（苍梧、藤县、恭城、平南、龙津）、贵州南部（独山）。生山谷林下，海拔300-700米。模式标本采自广东。
