## Description
Branchlets and terminal buds puberulent. Leaf blade abaxially gray stellate puberulent, adaxially glabrous. Fruit unknown.

> ●  Forests; 2000-2400 m. Gansu, Sichuan, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Tilia paucicostata* var. *ningshanensis* P. H. Yang.
**26c.少脉毛椴（变种）（新拟）**

var. yunnanensis Diels in Not. Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 5:285. 1912.

和原变种的区别在于嫩枝及顶芽被茸毛，叶下面被灰色星状茸毛。

产于云南及四川。生长于山地杂木林里。模式标本采自云南西部。
