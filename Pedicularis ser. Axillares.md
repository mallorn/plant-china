## Description
Herbs low. Stems very slender, often repent. Leaves &amp;#177; opposite or alternate, pinnatisect. Flowers several, axillary, scattered, usually long pedicellate. Corolla tube from shorter than calyx to more than 2 X as long; beak straight.
