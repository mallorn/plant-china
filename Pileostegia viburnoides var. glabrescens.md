柔毛冠盖藤
1b.**Pileostegia viburnoides var. glabrescens** (C. C. Yang) S. M. Hwang

## Description
Branchlets, leaves, and inflorescences sparsely stellate pubescent. Fl. May.


> * 800-1000 m. Hainan.

  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Pileostegia tomentella&lt;/I&gt; Handel-Mazzetti var. &lt;I&gt;glabrescens&lt;/I&gt; C. C. Yang, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 20: 477. 1982.

**1b. 柔毛冠盖藤（变种）**

var. glabrescens (C. C. Yan) S. M. Hwang in Acta Bot. Aus. Sin. 7: 33. 1991. ——P. tomentella Hand. -Mazz. var. glabrescens C. C. Yan in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 20 (4): 477. 1982.

本变种与原变种不同点在于小枝、叶背、苞片和花序各部均疏被柔毛。花期5月。
