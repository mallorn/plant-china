山峰西番莲",
9.**Passiflora jugorum** W. W. Smith",

## Description
Woody vines, ca. 8 m long. Stem terete, sparsely white pubescent, striate. Petiole 3-5 cm, pubescent, with 2 peg-shaped glands in lower 1/3 part; leaf blade 3-11 × 3-10 cm, membranous, pubescent, often with 2 or 3 pairs of glands near apex of midvein on abaxial surface, apex truncate, (2 or)3-lobed; lobes 5-10 mm, base broadly rounded to cordate, apex acute to acuminate. Inflorescences (sub)sessile, 5-15-flowered, pubescent. Pedicel 2.5-3 cm, pubescent. Flowers whitish, 3-4 cm in diam.; hypanthium 9-12 mm wide. Sepals light purple, 1-2 cm × ca. 5 mm, glabrous. Petals 1-1.5 cm × ca. 5 mm. Coronal filaments in a single series, 6-8 mm; operculum plicate, 2.5-3 mm tall, membranous; disk ca. 0.5 mm high; androgynophore 7-10 mm tall. Stamens 5; filaments 6-10 mm, basally fused for 1.5-3(-4) mm; anthers 3.5-5 × 2-3 mm. Ovary sessile on androgynophore, ellipsoid, glabrous; styles 3, free, 4-5 mm. Fruit blue at maturity, subglobose to ellipsoid, ca. 2 cm in diam., glabrous. Fl. Mar-Apr, fr. May-Jul.

> Forests on limestone hills; 1000-1800 m. S Yunnan [Myanmar].
  "Synonym": "*Passiflora burmanica* Chakravarty.
**8.山峰西番莲 石山南星、燕子尾（云南）**

Passiflora jugorum W. W. Smith in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 9: 115. 1916; C. Y. Wu et W. T. Wang in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 6: 235. 1957; G. Cusset in Fl. Cambodge Laos Vietnam 5: 109. 1967, in clavi; Wilde in Blumea 20: 233. 1972; 云南植物志1: 47. 1977; S. Y. Bao in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 22 (1): 59. 1984. ——P. burmanica Charkravarty in Bull. Bot. Soc. Beng. 3, 1: 49. 1949. (1951).

木质藤本，长达8米；茎圆柱形，被稀疏白色柔毛，有条纹。叶坚纸质，长3-11厘米，宽3-10厘米，先端截形，略成三尖头状或2浅裂，基部宽圆形至心形，下面被淡棕色柔毛，近顶部具2-4枚小腺体，裂片三角形，长0.5-1厘米，急尖至渐尖；叶柄密被淡棕色柔毛，长3-5厘米，从基部往上约1/3处具2枚腺体。花序为聚伞状伞房花序，成对生于粗壮卷须的两侧，有5-15朵花，被灰白色或棕色柔毛；花白色，直径3-4厘米；花梗长2.5-3厘米，被疏柔毛；萼片5枚，长1-2厘米，外面淡紫色，无毛，外面顶端不具角状附属器；外副花冠裂片2轮，丝状，外轮长6-8毫米，内轮长2-3毫米，内副花冠褶状，高2.5-3毫米；具花盘，高约0.5毫米；雌雄蕊柄长7-10毫米；雄蕊5枚，花丝长6-10毫米，基部合生；子房椭圆球形，无柄，无毛；花柱3枚，分离，长约4-5毫米。浆果近球形至椭圆球形，长3.5-4.5厘米，被蜡粉，无毛；种子多数，长圆形，长约6毫米，扁平，淡黄色。花期3-4月，果期5-7月。

产云南西南部和东南部。生于海拔1000-1800米的湿润疏林或杂木灌丛中。缅甸（克钦邦、八莫）亦有分布。模式标本采自云南腾冲。

根可入药，有健胃消食、止痛消炎之效。
