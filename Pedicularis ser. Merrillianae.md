> Two species: Bhutan, China, SW Asia; one species in China.

## Description
Herbs low, &amp;#177; stemless. Leaves alternate, mostly basal, in a rosette, old withered leaf bases persistent. Inflorescences short spicate or capitate, few flowered. Calyx 5-lobed. Corolla large; tube erect; galea with 1 marginal tooth on each side, with a beaklike apex.
