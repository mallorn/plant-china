茳芏(原亚种)",
42a.**Cyperus malaccensis subsp. malaccensis**",

## Description
Apicalmost leaf with an elongated blade. Basal involucral bracts longer than inflorescence, spreading. Glumes slightly incurved. Fl. and fr. Jun-Oct.
  "Statistics": "River margins, ditch margins (usually cultivated); below 100 m. Hainan, Taiwan [India, Indonesia, Japan, Malaysia, Myanmar, Nepal, Pakistan, Papua New Guinea, Philippines, Sri Lanka, Thailand, Vietnam; SW Asia, N Australia].
