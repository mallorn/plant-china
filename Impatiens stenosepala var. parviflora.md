## Description
Stem with remote leaves. Inflorescences short, usually 3- or 4-flowered. Flowers smaller (ca. 1.5 cm deep).

> ●  Forest margins, shaded moist places; 1000-1100 m. Chongqing (Nanchuan).
**154b. 窄萼凤仙花小花（变种）**

Impatiens stenosepala Pritz. ex Diels var. parviflora Pritz. ex Diels in Bot. Jahrb. 29: 453. 1900.

此变种以花较小（长约1.5厘米）；总花梗短，通常具3-4花；茎上部具较疏生的叶与原变种相区别。

产重庆（南川）。生于林缘阴湿处，海拔1050米。
