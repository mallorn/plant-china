> Michio Tamura (pers. comm.) believes that this variety would be better treated as a distinct species.

## Description
Stems toward base and petioles strigose. Leaf blade 3-partite, rarely 3-sect. Upper stem leaves not silvery strigose. Fl. May--Sep.

> Gansu, SW Guizhou, Hebei, Heilongjiang, W Henan, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, E Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi, Sichuan, N Xinjiang, Yunnan [Mongolia, Russia (Siberia)].
