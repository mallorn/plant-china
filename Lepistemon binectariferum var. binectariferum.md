鳞蕊藤 (原变种)",
1a.**Lepistemon binectariferum var. binectariferum**",

## Description
Sepals hirsute abaxially with &amp;#177; erect, yellowish hairs. Ovary glabrous. Seeds glabrous.

> Forests. Hainan [Cambodia, India, Indonesia, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam].
