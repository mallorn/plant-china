短穗杜英(原变种)",
38a.**Elaeocarpus brachystachyus var. brachystachyus**",

## Description
Branchlets not conspicuously striate. Leaves with glands present in axils of lateral veins and veinlets, veinlets not prominently raised adaxially.

> ●  Evergreen forests along riverbanks; 1300-2300 m. Yunnan (Gongshan).
