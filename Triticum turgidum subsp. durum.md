## Description
Spike simple, never branched at base, dense. Glumes 10–12 mm, slightly shorter than or equaling lemma, leathery, winged. Fl. and fr. Jun–Jul. 2*n* = 28.

> Frequently cultivated for food in China [cultivated in E Africa, C and SW Asia, S Europe, and the Mediterranean region].
