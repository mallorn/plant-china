> ● Two species: China.

## Description
Radical leaf 1, stem leaves 1, rarely 2 or few, bracteal-leaflike. Capitula 3 or 4 to numerous, erect or pendulous, narrowly paniculate. Involucres campanulate or narrowly campanulate; phyllaries uniseriate or nearly biseriate, 8-10. Florets 10-28. Pappus white.
