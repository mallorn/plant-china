立氏大王马先蒿",
88c.**Pedicularis rex subsp. lipskyana** (Bonati) P. C. Tsoong",

## Description
Plants 10--90 cm tall. Calyx 2-lobed. Corolla purple-red, ca. 3 cm. Filaments sparsely pubescent. Fl. May--Jul.

> * W Hubei, W Sichuan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Pedicularis lipskyana&lt;/I&gt; Bonati, Bull. Soc. Bot. France 57(Sess. Extraord.): 58. 1911; &lt;I&gt;P. lamarum&lt;/I&gt; H. Limpricht; &lt;I&gt;P. rex&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;purpurea&lt;/I&gt; Bonati.

Pedicularis rex C. B. Clarke subsp. lipskyana Tsoong, comb. nov. ——P. lipskyana Bonati, in Bull. Soc. Bot. France LVII (1911) , Sess. Extraord. lxiii; Limpr. in Repert. Sp. Nov. XVIII (1922) 243. ——P. cyathophylla auct. non Franch. : Limpr. in Repert. Sp. Nov. Beih. XII (1922) 485, pro parte.

这一亚种的区别在于其花色为紫红而不为黄色，其雄蕊花丝上的毛也不如洛氏变种那样密，但只是程度上的区别。

分布在我国湖北西部与四川西部，而不再向南至四川西南部与云南西北部。
