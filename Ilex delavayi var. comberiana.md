丽江陷脉冬青",
96c.**Ilex delavayi var. comberiana** S. Y. Hu",

## Description
Branchlets pubescent, verruculose. Leaf blade oblong or elliptic, 2-5(-7) × 1-2 cm. Fruit globose, ca. 7 mm in diam.; fruiting pedicel 2-3.5 mm; persistent stigma thickly discoid or subcapitate. Fl. May-Jul, fr. Sep.

> ● Forests, hill slopes; (2600-)2800-3600 m. NW Yunnan.
