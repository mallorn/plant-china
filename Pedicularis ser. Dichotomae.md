> One species.

## Description
Stems dichotomously branched or unbranched. Leaves opposite, ovate to lanceolate, pinnatisect. Stem leaves long petiolate. Inflorescences spicate. Flowers opposite. Calyx large, strongly 5-angled, with dense reticulate veins; lobes 5, entire. Corolla tube slightly exceeding calyx; galea 1-toothed on each side; beak often straight, slender.
