> Two species: China, Kazakhstan, Mongolia, Russia (W Siberia); one species in China.

## Description
Herbs tall, not drying black. Stems erect, unbranched. Leaves alternate, &amp;#177; lanceolate, pectinate-pinnatisect; segments linear-lanceolate. Inflorescences long. Calyx lobes short, entire. Corolla tube less than 2 X as long as calyx; galea &amp;#177; navicular, margin bearded or not; beak short.
