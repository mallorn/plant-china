秦氏金星蕨(原变种)",
12a.**Parathelypteris chingii var. chingii**",

## Description
Plants 35-60 cm tall. Pinnae sessile, abaxially along costae and veins with grayish white acicular hairs, adaxially along costal grooves with appressed short setae and multicellular acicular hairs.

> ● Wet places in dense valley forests; 300-500 m. N Fujian, S Jiangxi.
