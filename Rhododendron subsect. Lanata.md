## Description
Shrubs or small trees; young shoots densely woolly-tomentose. Leaf blade leathery, elliptic to obovate; abaxial surface with dense 1-layered woolly indumentum, hairs straight or crispate dendroid-branched. Inflorescence terminal, lax or dense, 3–15-flowered, rachis 3–10 mm. Calyx minute, 5-lobed; corolla tubular-campanulate to open-campanulate, rarely saucer-shaped, 5-lobed, yellow, white to pink; stamens 10; ovary densely tomentose, rarely glandular (in R. circinnatum).
