## Description
Plants 5--15 cm tall. Leaf segments 7--14 pairs. Lower corolla lip ca. 7 mm X 1 cm. Fl. Jul--Aug.

> * Alpine or swampy meadows, damp soil by stream and gully margins, &lt;I&gt;Picea&lt;/I&gt; woodlands. NE Qinghai.
