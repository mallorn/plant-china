大乌泡(原变种)",
123a.**Rubus pluribracteatus var. pluribracteatus**",

## Description
Sepals to 1.5 cm, palmately to pinnately lobed; lobes usually undivided.

> Slopes, river valleys, roadsides, forests; 300--2700 m. Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Yunnan [Cambodia, Laos, Thailand, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Rubus andropogon* H. Léveillé; *R. clinocephalus* Focke; *R. major* Focke; *R. mallodes* Focke.
