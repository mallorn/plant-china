紫喉百合",
24a.**Lilium primulinum var. burmanicum** (W. W. Smith) Stearn",

## Description
Bulb 5--6 cm in diam. Leaves lanceolate, 5.5--12 × 0.8--1.4 cm. Tepals oblong, 6.5--9 × 1.3--1.7 cm. Fl. Jul--Oct, fr. Sep--Dec. 2&lt;I&gt; n&lt;/I&gt; = 24.

> Forests, forest margins, thickets, grassy slopes; hillsides along ravines; 1200--2700 m. Yunnan [Myanmar, Thailand].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt; Lilium nepalense&lt;/I&gt; D. Don var. &lt;I&gt; burmanicum&lt;/I&gt; W. W. Smith, Trans. Bot. Soc. Edinburgh 28: 135. 1922; &lt;I&gt; L. ochraceum&lt;/I&gt; Franchet var. &lt;I&gt; burmanicum&lt;/I&gt; (W. W. Smith) Cotton.
**窄叶百合（变种）图版38: 1-4**

var. burmanicum W. W. Sm. in Trans. Bot. Soc. Edinb. 28: 135. 1922.

本变种与紫斑百合不同在于叶长而窄，长9-16厘米，宽0.8-1.4厘米。

产云南。生山坡草丛中或林缘，海拔1200-2200米。缅甸和泰国也有分布。
