## Description
Cymes dichotomous, axillary, short pedunculate; peduncles 5-10 mm. Calyx teeth lanceolate, posterior tooth longer than other teeth.

> * Hillsides. Hunan.
**16b.短梗变种**

var. brevipedunculata C. Y. Wu et Hsuan in Act. Phytotax, Sin, 10(1) : 54. 1965 ——Microtoena urucifolia auct, non Hemsl, : C, Y, Wu in Act, Phytotax. Sin. 8(1) : 45, 1959, p. p. min.

这一变种与原变种不同在于花序为腋生二歧聚伞花序；总梗短，长5-10毫米；萼齿披针形，后面的略长。

产湖南西部；生于山坡上。模式标本采自湖南黔阳太婆山。
