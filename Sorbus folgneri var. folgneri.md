石灰花楸(原变种)",
60a.**Sorbus folgneri var. folgneri**",

## Description
Leaf margin serrate, with obtuse or slightly ± acute teeth.

> Valleys, slopes, stream sides in mixed forests; 800--2000 m. Anhui, Fujian, Gansu, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Micromeles folgneri* C. K. Schneider, Bull. Herb. Boissier, sér. 2, 6: 318. 1906; *Aria folgneri* (C. K. Schneider) H. Ohashi &amp; H. Iketani; *Pyrus folgneri* (C. K. Schneider) C. K. Schneider ex Bean; *Sorbus nubium* Handel-Mazzetti.
