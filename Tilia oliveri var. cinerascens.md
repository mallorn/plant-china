灰背椴",
5b.**Tilia oliveri var. cinerascens** Rehder & E. H. Wilson",

## Description
Petiole 3-5 cm; leaf blade to 14 cm, abaxially loosely yellow-brown or gray stellate tomentose to pubescent, marginal teeth short and broad.

> ●  Forests; 1600-2300 m. Hubei.
  "Synonym": "*Tilia populifolia* Hung T. Chang (1982), not Lesquereux [fossil].
**1b.灰背椴（变种）**

var. cinerascens Rehd. et Wils. in Sarg. Pl. Wils. 2: 367. 1916.

和原变种的区别在于叶下面被灰色的星状茸毛，而不是白色或灰白色的毛被。

产于湖北西部，从房县到恩施一带。模式标本采自湖北房县。
