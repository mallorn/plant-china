## Description
Petiole to 3 cm; lateral veins 8–10 pairs.

> N Guangdong (Lechang Xian).
  "Synonym": "*Aria caloneura* var. *kwangtungensis* (T. T. Yü) H. Ohashi &amp; H. Iketani.

S. caloneura (Stapf) Rehd. var. kwangtungensis Yu, 植物分类学报8: 223. 1963.

本变种叶柄较长，长达3厘米，叶脉对数较少，8-10对。
