## Description
Annuals. Roots fibrous. Culms densely tufted, 2-20 cm tall, slender, compressed triquetrous, smooth, base not swollen. Leaves equaling or slightly shorter than culm; sheath brownish purple, basal 1 or 2 bladeless; leaf blade 2-3 mm wide, flaccid. Involucral bracts 3 or 4, leaflike, much longer than inflorescence, spreading to downward reflexed, base usually broadening. Spike 1(-3), subglobose, 6-9 mm in diam., with many spikelets. Spikelets densely congested, broadly ovoid, 2.5-3 × 1.5-2 mm, compressed, 1-flowered. Glumes pale to straw-colored and sometimes rusty brown maculate, boat-shaped, 2.5-3 mm, keel green, winged, with 6-10 slightly broad teeth shaped like a cockscomb on apical ca. 2/3 of margin, and 1-3 spinelike trichomes at apex of teeth, apex with a strict mucro. Stamens 1 or 2; anthers oblong. Style of medium length; stigmas 2. Nutlet at first yellow but dark brown when mature, ellipsoid, ca. 2/3 as long as subtending glume, compressed, with dense punctate processes. Fl. and fr. Aug-Oct. 2*n* = 14.

一年生草本，具许多须根。稈密丛生，柔弱，直立或外倾，高2-20厘米，扁锐三稜形，平滑，基部不膨大。叶等长或稍短于稈，宽2-3毫米，萎软状；叶鞘棕紫色，下面的1-2个叶鞘顶端无叶片。苞片3-4枚，叶状，较长，基部常增宽，平展或向下反折；穗状花序通常1个，很少2-3个，近于球形，直径6-9毫米，具多数小穗；小穗密聚，宽卵形，压扁，长2.5-3毫米，宽1.5-2毫米，具1朵花；鳞片膜质，舟状，长同于小穗，顶端具直的短尖，苍白色或麦稈黄色，有时有锈色斑点，背面龙骨状突起绿色，具翅，翅从基部开始，其全长2/3部分的边缘具6-10个粗齿而呈鸡冠状，齿顶端具1-3条刺状硬毛；雄蕊2个或1个，花药长圆形；花柱中等长，柱头2。小坚果椭圆形，扁，长约为鳞片的2/3，初时黄色，后期呈暗栗色，具密的稍突起细点。花果期8-10月。

产于云南；生长于山谷潮湿的草地上或林下。也分布于尼泊尔、印度、越南以及非洲、喜马拉雅山区和美洲热带地区。
