大花香水月季",
57b.**Rosa odorata var. gigantea** (Crépin) Rehder & E. H. Wilson",

## Description
Flowers single, 8–10 cm in diam. Petals white. 2n = 21*.

> Mixed forests, thickets, scrub on hillsides, pastures, grassy slopes, roadsides; 1400--2700 m. Yunnan [Myanmar, N Thailand, N Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Rosa gigantea* Collett ex Crépin, Bull. Soc. Roy. Bot. Belgique 27: 148. 1888; *R. duclouxii* H. Léveillé; *R. macrocarpa* Watt ex Crépin; *R. xanthocarpa* Watt ex E. Willmott.
