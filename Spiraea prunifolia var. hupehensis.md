无毛李叶绣线菊",
59d.**Spiraea prunifolia var. hupehensis** (Rehder) Rehder",

## Description
Leaf blades glabrous abaxially, margin 1–4-serrate on each side near apex. Flowers single, less than 1 cm in diam. Hypanthium glabrous abaxially. Follicles glabrous.

> Steep dry cliffs; near sea level to 300 m. Hubei, Shaanxi.
  "Synonym": "*Spiraea hypericifolia* Linnaeus var. *hupehensis* Rehder in Sargent, Pl. Wilson. 1: 439. 1913.
