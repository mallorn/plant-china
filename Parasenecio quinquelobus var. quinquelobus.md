五裂蟹甲草(原变种)",
29a.**Parasenecio quinquelobus var. quinquelobus**",

## Description
Leaf blade margin lobed in distal 1/3. Capitula arranged in narrow panicles.
  "Statistics": "Understories of *Quercus* or *Abies* forests, alpine meadows; 2800-4100 m. SW Sichuan, Xizang, NW Yunnan [Bhutan, N India, Myanmar, Nepal].
  "Synonym": "*Prenanthes quinqueloba* Wallich ex Candolle, Prodr. 7: 195. 1838; *Cacalia pentaloba* Handel-Mazzetti; *C. quinqueloba* (Wallich ex Candolle) Kitamura; *Koyamacalia quinqueloba* (Wallich ex Candolle) H. Robinson &amp; Brettell; *Senecio quinquelobus* (Wallich ex Candolle) J. D. Hooker &amp; Thomson ex C. B. Clarke (1876), not (Thunberg) Candolle (1838).
