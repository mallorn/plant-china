粗距蓝翠雀花",
162d.**Delphinium caeruleum var. crassicalcaratum** W. T. Wang & M. J. Warnock",

## Description
Stem 5--9 cm, spreading pilose, appressed puberulent, and with some spreading, glandular hairs. Proximal leaves present at anthesis; cauline leaf blade 0.4--2.2 cm wide, adaxially densely puberulent; ultimate lobules 1--2 mm wide, apically acute. Spur ca. 2.8 cm, base 3--4 mm in diam. Ovaries villous. Fl. Jun.

> * Gravelly slopes; ca. 4600 m. NW Yunnan (Dêqên Xian).
