## Description
Leaf blade lanceolate, 3–5 × 0.5–0.8 cm. Sepals 1–2 mm.

> Limestone areas, bases of slopes; ca. 800 m. SE Yunnan (Funing Xian).
  "Synonym": "*Pittosporum podocarpifolium* C. Y. Wu in W. C. Yin, Fl. Yunnan. 3: 318. 1983.
