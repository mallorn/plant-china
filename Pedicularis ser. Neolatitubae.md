## Description
Herbs less than 10 cm tall. Stems scarcely present, weak, curved. Leaves mostly basal. Pedicel to 4 cm. Flowers axillary. Calyx deeply cleft anteriorly, 3-lobed. Corolla &amp;#177; red-purple; tube 5--6 X as long as calyx; galea circinate-incurved basally; beak semicircular; lower lip large.
