屏东木姜子(原变种)",
65a.**Litsea akoensis var. akoensis**",

## Description
Petiole ca. 1 cm, pubescent; leaf blade oblong-obovate or long elliptic, 6-10 × 2-4 cm, along veins pubescent abaxially, glabrous adaxially, except pubescent on base of midrib. Peduncles of inflorescence 6-10 mm, pubescent. Male flowers: filaments pubescent; rudimentary pistil small.
  "Statistics": "● Evergreen broad-leaved forests; below 500 m. Taiwan.
  "Synonym": "*Actinodaphne akoensis* (Hayata) T. S. Liu &amp; J. C. Liao; *Cylicodaphne akoensis* (Hayata) Nakai; *Tetradenia akoensis* (Hayata) Nemoto.
