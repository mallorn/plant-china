林缘披碱草
47b.**Elymus mutabilis var. nemoralis** S. L. Chen ex D. F. Cui

## Description
Plants with rhizome. Spikelets usually green. Glumes broadly lanceolate. Lemma pubescent throughout; awn 1–2 mm.


> * Forest glades, mountain slopes; 1800–1900 m. Xinjiang.

  "Synonym": "*Roegneria mutabilis* var. *nemoralis* (D. F. Cui) L. B. Cai.
