羽叶钉柱委陵菜
51c.**Potentilla saundersiana var. subpinnata** Handel-Mazzetti

## Description
Radical leaves subpinnately (3–)5–7(or 8)-foliolate. Inflorescence a many-flowered cyme. Epicalyx segments acute or 1- or 2-fid at apex.


> Alpine meadows, gravel fields; 3100--3600 m. Sichuan, Yunnan.

  "Synonym": "*Potentilla forrestii* var. *subpinnata* (Handel-Mazzetti) Handel-Mazzetti.

**48c. 羽叶钉柱委陵菜（变种）**

P. saundersiana Royle var. subpinnata Hand. -Mazz. Symb. Sin. 7: 513. 1933. ——P. forrestii W. W. Smith var. subpinnata Hand.-Mazz. in Acta Hort. Gothob. 13: 312. 1939.

本变种的特点：基生叶小叶(3-)5-7(-8)近羽状排列，上面密被伏生绢状柔毛；副萼片顶端急尖或有1-2裂齿。

产四川、云南。生高山草地、多石砾地，海拔3100-3600米。
