短距翠雀花(原变种)",
1a.**Delphinium forrestii var. forrestii**",

## Description
Stem densely retrorsely hispid. Sepals bluish gray or tinged greenish, abaxial hairs 1.2--2 mm.

> * Stony places near Rhododendron thicket margins, gravelly slopes, cliffs; 3800--4900 m. SW Sichuan, N Yunnan.
