毛叶川滇蔷薇",
85b.**Rosa soulieana var. yunnanensis** C. K. Schneider",

## Description
Rachis and leaflet abaxially pubescent. Flowers numerous, in corymbs, rarely solitary; pedicel pubescent and glandular.

> Chongqing (Nanchuan Xian), C Sichuan (Kangding Xian), NW Yunnan (Lijiang Naxizu Zizhixian, Zhongdian Xian).
**72b. 毛叶川滇蔷薇**

R. soulieana Crep. var. yunnanensis Schneid. in Bat. Gaz. 66: 77. 1917. ——R. moschata var. yunnanensis Focke in Not. Roy. Bot. Card. Edinb. 5: 69. 1911. nom.nud.

小叶下面和叶轴被柔毛，花梗被柔毛和腺毛。

产四川（康定、南川）、云南（中甸、丽江）。
