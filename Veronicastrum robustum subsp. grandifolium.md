大叶腹水草",
8b.**Veronicastrum robustum subsp. grandifolium** T. L. Chin & D. Y. Hong",

## Description
Leaves glabrous; leaf blade ovate-orbicular, rarely lanceolate, 15-27 X 7-11 cm, green or brown, not lucid, base cordate, truncate, or rounded, margin mostly dentate.

> * Sparse forests, thickets. NW Guangxi, SW Hunan.
**大叶腹水草**

subsp. grandifolium Chin et Hong, subsp. nov. , in Addenda 402.

叶各处无毛，叶片上面浅绿色或褐色，无光泽，常为卵圆形而基部心形，少为披针形而基部平截至圆钝，长15-27厘米，宽7-11厘米，叶缘多为三角状粗锯齿。

产湖南西南部和广西（龙胜）。生疏林及灌丛中。
