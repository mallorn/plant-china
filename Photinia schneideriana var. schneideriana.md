绒毛石楠(原变种)",
36a.**Photinia schneideriana var. schneideriana**",

## Description
Rachis, pedicels, and hypanthium abaxially sparsely villous, glabrescent; petals glabrous.

> Forests, river valleys, near water courses; 400--1600 m. Anhui, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Zhejiang.
  "Synonym": "*Photinia beauverdiana* C. K. Schneider var. *lohfauensis* F. P. Metcalf; *P. dabeishanensis* M. P. Deng &amp; K. Yao; *P. fauriei* Cardot; *P. zhijiangensis* T. C. Ku.
