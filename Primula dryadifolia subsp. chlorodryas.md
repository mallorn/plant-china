## Description
Corolla pale yellow; tube pubescent inside. Fl. Jun-Jul.

> * On limestone boulders; ca. 4500 m. NW Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Primula chlorodryas&lt;/I&gt; W. W. Smith, Notes Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinburgh 16: 223. 1931.
**261c.黄花岩报春（亚种）（新拟）**

Primula dryadifolia Franch. subsp. chlrodryas (W. W. Smith) Chen et C. M. Hu, stat. nov.——Primula calorodryas W. W. Smith in Not. Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 16: 223. 1931; W. W. Smith. et Fletcher in Trans. Bot. Soc. Edinb. 34: 138. 1944.

花冠黄色。

产于云南西北部。生于石灰岩石缝中，海拔4450米。模式标本采自维西县西南部澜沧江与怒江分水岭。
