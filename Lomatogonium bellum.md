## Description
Annuals 7-40 cm tall. Stems erect, angular, few to much branched from base; branches crowded, glabrous. Leaves sessile; lower to middle stem leaves ovate to subcordate, 1.6-2.8 cm &amp;#215; 5-11 mm; upper leaves ovate-lanceolate; base of all leaves rounded to subcordate and subamplexicaul, apex acute, midvein abaxially distinct. Inflorescences racemelike, usually in many-flowered panicles. Flowers 5merous. Pedicel spreading to erect, to 13 cm, angular, glabrous. Calyx tube 1-1.5 mm; lobes ovate-lanceolate, 0.7-1.7 cm &amp;#215; 2-4.5 mm, base obtuse, apex acute, midvein distinct. Corolla pale blue, with dark blue lines, 1.5-2.5 cm in diam., tube 1-1.5 mm; lobes obovate-oblong, elliptic, or narrowly oblong-ovate, 1.3-2 cm, apex acuminate to obtuse. Nectary connate at base into a tube pointed outward, apex lamellate, margin lobed. Filaments 8-11 mm; anthers blue, narrowly ellipsoid, ca. 4 mm. Ovary ensiform to ovoid-ellipsoid, 1-1.5 cm, apex triangular acute. Stigma lobes triangular, decurrent to apical part of ovary. Capsules narrowly obovoid-ellipsoid, 1.8-2.2 cm. Seeds brown, ellipsoid, 0.6-0.8 mm in diam.; seed coat scabrous. Fl. and fr. Aug-Oct.

> * Grassland slopes, wet and shady places, forests; 1300-3200 m. W Hubei, S Shaanxi, E Sichuan, Yunnan.

Lomatogonium bellum (Hemsl.) H. Smith apud S. Nilsson in Grana Palyn. 7(1): 109, 145. 1967.——Swertia bella Hemsl. in Journ. Linn. Soc. Bot. 26: 138. 1890; Batal. in Acta Hort. Sichuan; Diels in Bot. Jahrb. 29: 537. 1900.

一年生草本，高7-40厘米，全株光滑。茎直立，基部单一，上部有分枝或从基部起分枝，斜升，几四棱形，有时带紫色。叶无柄，茎中下部叶卵形或近心状卵形，长16-28毫米，宽5-11毫米；上部叶卵状披针形，长13-20毫米，宽2.5-4.5毫米，全部叶先端急尖或钝，基部圆形或钝，稀近心形，半抱茎，仅中脉在两面明显。圆锥状复聚伞花序；花梗直立或斜升，不等长，近四棱形，长至13厘米；花5数，直径1.5-2.5厘米，花萼长为花冠的2/3或等长，裂片与茎上部叶相似，卵状披针形或披针形，长7-17毫米，宽2-4.5毫米，先端急尖或钝，背面中脉明显；花冠蓝色，具深蓝色条纹，裂片狭卵状矩圆形、狭椭圆形至倒卵状矩圆形，长13-20毫米，先端钝，具小尖头，基部两侧各具1个腺窝，腺窝基部管形，上部具不整齐的裂片状流苏；花丝线形，长8-11毫米，花药蓝色，线状矩圆形，长约4毫米；子房无柄，长10-15毫米，柱头小，三角形，2裂，沿心皮的缝线下延至子房下部。蒴果无柄，狭倒卵状矩圆形，长18-22毫米；种子褐色，矩圆形，长0.6-0.8毫米，表面微粗糙。花果期8-10月。

产四川、贵州、湖北西部、陕西（太白山）。生于山坡草地、阴湿地、林下，海拔1300-3200米。模式标本采自湖北房县。
