> Henry Noltie (pers. comm.) considers &lt;I&gt; Lilium euxanthum&lt;/I&gt; (from SE Xizang and Yunnan) to be distinct from &lt;I&gt; L. nanum&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt; flavidum&lt;/I&gt; (from S Xizang (Chumbi valley), N Myanmar, and Sikkim). The former has oblong leaves, which do not overtop the flower, and golden yellow tepals; the latter has linear, finely tapering leaves, which overtop the flower, and pale lemon-colored tepals.

## Description
Tepals yellow, unspotted.

> Forest margins, alpine grasslands; 3800--4300 m. SE Xizang, Yunnan [N Myanmar, Sikkim].
