冰雪虎耳草",
149.**Saxifraga glacialis** H. Smith",

## Description
Herbs perennial, cespitose, 2.3-7 cm tall. Stem glabrous, with leaf rosettes. Rosette leaves spatulate to spatulate-lanceolate, 4.5-10 × 1.2-2.3 mm, carnose, both surfaces glabrous, margin glabrous or sparsely setose-ciliate, apex rounded. Cauline leaves remote, ensiform, 4-6.6 × 1-1.5 mm, carnose, glabrous. Cyme 2-6-flowered or flower solitary; pedicels slender, 4-15 mm, glabrous. Sepals spreading, ovate, 1.5-3 × 1-2 mm, carnose, glabrous, veins 3, confluent or not at apex, apex obtuse. Petals yellow or abaxially purple and adaxially yellow, elliptic or ovate to narrowly so, 3-5 × 1.5-2.5 mm, 2-callose, 3-veined, base tapered into a claw 0.5-1 mm, apex obtuse or acute. Stamens 3-4 mm. Ovary subsuperior, dark purple, broadly ovoid, ca. 2.5 mm; styles ca. 2 mm. Fl. and fr. Jul-Sep.

> * Alpine meadows, rock crevices; 4100-5000 m. SE Qinghai (Jigzhi Xian), N and W Sichuan, NW Yunnan (Weixi Xian).

Saxifraga glacialis H. Smith in Acta Hort. Gothob. 1:14. f. 4a-f. t. 9A-B. 1924; J. T. Pan in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 16(2):15. 1978;中国高等植物图鉴补编 2: 33. 1983. ——S. glacialis H. Smith var. rubra Anth. in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 18 (86):31. 1933: 云南种子植物名录上册: 238. 1984.

多年生草本，高2.3-7厘米，丛生。茎无毛，具莲座叶丛。莲座叶肉质肥厚，匙形至匙状倒披针形，长4.5-9毫米，宽1.2-2.3毫米，先端稍钝，两面无毛，边缘疏生刚毛状睫毛；莲座叶丛以上之茎生叶较疏，剑形，长6.6毫米，宽约1毫米，无毛，肉质。聚伞花序具2-6花；花梗长4-7毫米，纤细，无毛；萼片在花期开展，肉质肥厚，卵形，长1.5-2.9毫米，宽1-2毫米，无毛，3脉于先端不汇合至汇合；花瓣黄色，或腹面黄色，背面紫红色，椭圆形、卵形至狭卵形，长3-3.9毫米，宽1.5-2毫米，先端钝或急尖，基部狭缩成长0.5-1毫米之爪，3脉，侧脉旁具2痂体；雄蕊长3-3.5毫米，花丝钻形，子房黑紫色，近上位，阔卵球形，长约2.5毫米，花柱长约2毫米。花果期7-9月。

产青海（久治）、四川（松潘、马尔康、康定、稻城）和云南（维西）。生于海拔4 100- 5 000米的高山草甸和高山碎石隙。合模式标本采自四川松潘和马尔康（马塘）。
