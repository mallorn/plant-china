山荆子(原变种)",
1a.**Malus baccata var. baccata**",

## Description
Trees, to 10–14 m tall. Branchlets slender, slightly curved. Leaf blade 3–8 cm, margin acutely serrate. Flowers 3–3.5 in diam. Styles 5 or 4.

> Mixed forests on slopes, among shrubs in valleys; sea level to 1500 m. Gansu, Hebei, Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi [Korea, Mongolia, Russia (Siberia)].
