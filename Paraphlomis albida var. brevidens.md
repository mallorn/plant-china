## Description
Calyx teeth broadly ovate-triangular, apex acute.

> * Evergreen and pine forests, thickets; 100-900 m. Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Hunan, Jiangxi, Taiwan.
**19b. 短齿变种**

Paraphlomis albida Hand. -Mazz. var. brevidens Hand. -Mazz Symb. Sin. 7: 922. 1936; H. W. Li in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 10 (1): 71. 1965——Paraphlomis albiflora auct. non (Hemsl.) Hand.- Mazz.: Y. Z. Sun in Act. Phytotax. Sin.(1): 49. 1955, p. p. quoad specimen Tsiang 10266——Paraphlomis albida auct. non Hand. -Mazz.: C. Y. Wu in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 8 (1): 35. 1959.

这一变种与原变种不同在于萼齿宽卵圆状三角形，先端短尖。

产广西，湖南，广东，江西，福建，台湾；生于常绿林、松林或灌丛中，海拔80-900米。模式标本采自江西萍乡。
