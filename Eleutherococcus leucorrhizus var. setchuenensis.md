## Description
Leaflets usually 3, rarely 4 or 5, both surfaces glabrous, abaxially glaucous or glaucescent, margin entire or sparsely and irregularly serrate.

> ● Scrub fields; 1000-3200 m. Gansu, Guizhou, Henan, Hubei, Shaanxi, Sichuan.
  "Synonym": "Basionym: *Acanthopanax setchuenensis* Harms, Bot. Jahrb. Syst. 29: 488. 1900; *A. setchuenensis* var. *latifoliatus* G. Hoo; *Eleutherococcus setchuenensis* (Harms) Nakai; *E.* *setchuenensis* var. *latifoliatus* (G. Hoo) H. Ohashi.
**阔叶蜀五加（变种）**

Acanthopanax setchuenensis Harms var. latifoliatus Hoo,植物分类学报增刊1: 161. 1965.

本变种和原变种的区别在于小叶片圆卵形，先端有尾尖，边缘有不整齐裂齿。

产于甘肃南部。生于海拔 2500米处。模式标本采自甘肃武都。
