西川红景天(原变种)",
26a.**Rhodiola alsia subsp. alsia**",

## Description
Flowering stems 2-6, to 20 cm. Flowers bisexual or female (plants gynodioecious), 7-10 mm wide. Sepals 2.2-2.6 mm, or 2.6-3.5 mm in female flowers.

> * &lt;I&gt;Rhododendron&lt;/I&gt; forests, rocky slopes; 3400-4800 m. NW Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Sedum alsium&lt;/I&gt; Fröderström, Acta Horti Gothob. 15: 8. 1942; &lt;I&gt;Rhodiola staminea&lt;/I&gt; (Paulsen) S. H. Fu; &lt;I&gt;Sedum doratocarpum&lt;/I&gt; Fröderström; &lt;I&gt;S. stamineum&lt;/I&gt; Paulsen.
