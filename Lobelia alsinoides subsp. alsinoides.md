短柄半边莲(原亚种)",
6a.**Lobelia alsinoides subsp. alsinoides**",

## Description
Leaves suborbicular, ± as long as broad, margin serrate.
  "Statistics": "Wet places; lower elevations. Hainan [Bangladesh, India, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, Nepal, New Guinea, Sri Lanka, Thailand, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Lobelia stipularis* Roth ex Schultes; *L. trigona* Roxburgh.
