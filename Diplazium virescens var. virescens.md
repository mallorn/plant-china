淡绿双盖蕨(原变种)",
25a.**Diplazium virescens var. virescens**",

## Description
Pinnules moderately asymmetrical at base, veinlets simple or sometimes forked; sori medial or supramedial.

> Evergreen broad-leaved forests; below 100-1500 m. Anhui, Chongqing, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hubei (Suizhou), Hunan, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan, Zhejiang [Japan, Korea, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Allantodia virescens* (Kunze) Ching; *Asplenium virescens* (Kunze) Mettenius; *Athyrium virescens* (Kunze) Ohwi; *Diplazium lutchuense* Koidzumi.
