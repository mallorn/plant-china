## Description
Plants ?perennial. Stems branched from the base upward or only\nat apex, 23-28 cm tall, spreading white pubescent. Petiole of\nupper stem leaves 3-14 mm, longer in basal leaves; leaf blade\nbroadly ovate to broadly elliptic, 1-1.8 &amp;#215; 0.9-1.2 cm, appressed\npubescent, apex obtuse or rounded, apiculate; lateral veins indistinct.\nInflorescences 1.5-11 cm, few to many flowered, axis with spreading\nhairs at base, minutely strigose at apex. Pedicel 1.5-4.5 mm,\nto 7 mm in fruit, minutely strigose. Calyx lobes oblong-ovate,\nca. 3 mm, strigose, with long white hairs at margin, slightly\nconnected at base. Corolla blue, ca. 4.5 mm wide; tube ca. 1.1\nmm; appendages low trapeziform, ca. 0.4 mm; lobes 5, suborbicular.\nAnthers oblong, ca. 0.5 mm. Nutlets subulate, trigonous-tetrahedral,\nsmooth, with very short hairs; abaxial surfaces deltate-ovate,\nca. 1.2 &amp;#215; 1.2 mm, inner surfaces subequal in size, joined\nat sharp angles; stipe indistinct. Fl. and fr. Jul.

> * Mountain slopes; above 3000 m. Sichuan.
