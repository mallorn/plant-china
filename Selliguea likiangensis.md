## Description
Rhizome ca. 4 mm in diam., densely scaly; scales brown, lanceolate, margin and both surfaces with fibrous hairs, apex acuminate. Fronds monomorphic. Stipe light brown, ca. 15 cm, glabrous; lamina pinnate near base, upper part pinnatisect, 20-25 × 15-20 cm, base cordate, margin entire. Lateral pinnae/lobes usually 3 or 4 pairs, ovate-lanceolate, 10-12 × 2-3 cm, base contracted, widest at middle, apex acuminate. Costa and lateral veins distinct, veinlets obscure. Lamina papery, both surfaces glabrous. Sori orbicular, in 1 row or 2 irregular rows on either side of costa, slightly closer to costa.

> ● Epiphytic on rocks in forests; ca. 2400 m. Yunnan (Lijiang).

**21.丽江假瘤蕨（横断山区维管植物）**

Phymatopteris likiangensis (Ching) Pic. Serm. in Webbia 28 (2): 463. 1973; Jarrettet al., Ind. Fil, Suppl. 5: 133. 1985. ——Phymatopsis likiangensis Ching in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 9 (2): 190. 1964; W. T. Wang, Vasc. Pl. Hengduan Mts. 1: 176. 1993.

附生植物。根状茎横走，粗约4毫米，密被鳞片；鳞片披针形，棕色，顶端渐尖，边缘和两面疏被纤维状毛。叶近生；叶柄长约15厘米，淡棕色，光滑无毛；叶片羽状全裂或中上部为羽状深裂，长约20-25厘米，宽约15-20厘米，基部楔形；羽片或裂片约3-4对，长10-12厘米，宽约2-3厘米，顶端渐尖，中部最宽，基部略收缩，边缘全缘并具加厚的软骨质边。中脉和侧脉明显，小脉隐约可见。叶纸质，两面无毛。孢子囊群在裂片中脉两侧各一行或不规则二行，略靠近中脉着生。

产云南西北部 (丽江) 。附生林下石上，海拔2400米。模式标本采自丽江。
