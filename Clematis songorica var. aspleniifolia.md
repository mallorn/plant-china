## Description
Lower leaves undivided, upper ones pinnatisect. Sepals adaxially glabrous. Anthers 3--4 mm.

> Grassy areas, gravelly banks, along streams; 500--2500 m. W Xinjiang [Afghanistan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan].
