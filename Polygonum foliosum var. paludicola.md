宽基多叶蓼",
36b.**Polygonum foliosum var. paludicola** (Makino) Kitamura",

## Description
Leaves basally broadly cuneate or rounded; cilia of ocrea 2-3 mm.

> Water sides, along ditches; sea level to 300 m. Anhui, Heilongjiang, Jiangsu, Jilin, Liaoning [Japan, Russia (Far East)].
  "Synonym": "*Polygonum* *paludicola* Makino, Bot. Mag. (Tokyo) 28: 113. 1914; *Persicaria* *paludicola* (Makino) Nakai.
**36b.宽基多叶蓼（变种）**

Polygonum foliosum H. Lindb. var. paiudicola (Mak.) Kitam. in Acta Phytotax. Geobot. 20: 207. 1962.

本变种与原变种的区别是：叶基部宽楔形或近圆形；托叶鞘缘毛长2-3毫米。

产东北、江苏（苏州）、安徽（泾县）。生沟边、水池旁，海拔50-250米。分布于日本。
