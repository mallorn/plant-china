**34b. 光叶野丁香（变种）（新拟）**

Leptodermis pilosa Diels var. glabrescens H. Winkl. in Fedde, Repert. Sp. Nov, 18: 160. 1922; Rehd. in Journ. Arn. Arb. 16: 327. 1935; Hand. -Mazz. symb. Sin. 7 (4): 1025. 1936. ——L. mairei Levl. in Fedde, Repert. Sp. Nov. 13: 179. 1914 et Cal. Pl. Yunnan 246. 1917.
