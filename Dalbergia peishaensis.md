## Description
Woody climbers. Stems glabrous; young shoots sparsely puberulent. Leaves 10-14 cm; petiole puberulent; leaflets 21-31; petiolules ca. 1 mm, puberulent; blades oblong, small, 8-15 × 3-6 mm, membranous, both surfaces appressed silky pu-bescent, base rounded or cuneate, somewhat slightly oblique, apex obtuse to rounded, cuspidate. Panicles axillary, 3-5 × 3-4 cm, rusty pubescent; peduncles 1-2 cm. Flowers minute, 2.5-3 mm, aggregated at apices of few branches of inflorescence; bracts ovate, minute, ca. 0.8 mm, apex acute; bracteoles 2, ob-long, larger than bracts, persistent. Pedicel ca. 1 mm. Calyx shallowly campanulate, ca. 1 mm, 5-toothed; teeth unequal, lowest broadly ovate, upper pair half-orbicular, larger than lat-eral ones, lateral 2 ovate. Corolla white; petals subequal in length, shortly clawed; standard transversely elliptic, base sub-hastate, apex emarginate; wings obovate-oblong; keel broadly elliptic, base of blade auriculate. Stamens 10, monadelphous. Ovary stipitate, oblanceolate, glabrous except on lower suture, 3-ovuled; style short. Legume oblong, finely reticulate through-out. Fl. Apr, fr. Jun.

**3.白沙黄檀（新拟）白沙檀（植物分类学报）**

Dalbergia peishaensis Chun et T. Chen in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 7: 24. 1958; 海南植物志2: 288. 1965.

藤本；茎无毛。幼枝略被短柔毛，有椭圆形或近圆形皮孔。羽状复叶长10-14厘米；叶柄被褐色短柔毛；小叶10-15对，膜质，长圆形，长8-15毫米，宽3-6毫米，先端钝至圆，有锐尖头，基部圆或楔形，有时稍偏斜，两面被褐色丝质柔毛；小叶柄长约1毫米，被毛。圆锥花序腋生，长3-5厘米，径3-4厘米，被褐色长柔毛，分枝少；总花梗长1-2厘米；基生小苞片极小，长不及1毫米，卵形，急尖；副萼状小苞片2枚，基部贴合，长圆形，较基生小苞片大；花梗长约1毫米；花微小，聚集于花序分枝的顶端，长2.5-3毫米；花萼浅钟状，长约1毫米，被褐色柔毛，萼齿5，下方1枚最长，阔卵状兜形，上方2枚半圆形，侧方2枚较小，卵形；花瓣近等长，具短柄，旗瓣横椭圆形，先端凹缺，基部近戟形，翼瓣倒卵状长圆形，龙骨瓣阔，椭圆形，基部内侧有耳；雄蕊10,单体；子房具柄，倒披针形，除腹缝有毛外其余无毛，有胚珠3粒，花柱短。荚果长圆形，长约4.5厘米，宽约9毫米，顶端急尖，基部渐狭为果颈，果瓣有细网纹。花期4月。

产海南。生于密林中。模式标本采自白沙。
