渥丹 (原变种)",
11a.**Lilium concolor var. concolor**",

> The bulbs are medicinal and edible.

## Description
Leaves 3.5--7 cm × 3--6 mm. Tepals unspotted, 2.2--4 cm × 4--7 mm. 2&lt;I&gt; n&lt;/I&gt; = 24*.

> * Thickets, grassy slopes, hillsides; 300--2000 m. Hebei, Henan, Hubei, Jilin, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi, Yunnan.
