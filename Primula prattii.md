雅砻黄报春
226.**Primula prattii** Hemsley

## Description
Herbs perennial, with fibrous remains of old leaves at base.\nLeaves forming a rosette; petiole inconspicuous to 1/2 as long\nas leaf blade, narrowly winged; leaf blade spatulate to narrowly\nlanceolate or linear-oblanceolate, 1.5--8 X 0.2--1 cm, abaxially\ndensely yellow farinose, base attenuate, margin crenate and often\nnarrowly revolute, apex subobtuse. Scapes 6--25 cm; umbels 4--12-flowered;\nbracts lanceolate to linear-lanceolate, 4--10 mm, base slightly\ngibbous. Pedicel 0.5--2.5 cm, initially farinose, becoming efarinose.\nFlowers heterostylous. Calyx campanulate to narrowly campanulate,\n5--8.5 mm, densely farinose inside, parted to middle or slightly\nbelow; lobes narrowly triangular to sublanceolate, apex acute\nto subacuminate. Corolla bright yellow; tube 1--1.2 cm; limb 1.5--2\ncm wide; lobes elliptic, deeply emarginate. Pin flowers: stamens\nca. 2 mm above base of corolla tube; style slightly shorter than\ntube. Thrum flowers with positions reciprocal. Capsule oblong,\nslightly longer to 2 X as long as calyx.


> * Damp meadows; 3000--4300 m. W Sichuan.

**218. 雅砻黄报春（拉汉种子植物名称）**

Primula prattii Hemsl. in Journ. Linn. Soc. Lond. Bot. 29: 314. 1893; Pax in Engl. Pflanzenr. 22 (IV-237): 102. 1905; Cowan in Bot. Mag. t. 8918. 1938; W. in. Smith et Fletcher in Trans. Roy. Soc. Edinb. 61 (1): 40. 1943;中国高等植物图鉴3: 244, fig. 4441. 1974. ——Prmula sulphurea Pax et Hoffm. in Fedde Resert. Sp. Nov. 17: 96. 1921, non Cralb (1913).

多年生草本，具粗短的根状茎和成丛的粗根。叶丛基部具分解成纤维状的渴色枯叶柄。叶片匙形至狭披针形或线状倒披针形，长1.5-8厘米，宽2-10毫米，先端稍钝，基部渐狭窄，边缘具钝圆浅齿，常极窄外卷，上面秃净，下面密被黄粉，中肋及5-8对侧脉清晰可见；叶柄甚短或长达叶片的1/2，具狭翅。花葶稍纤细，高6-25厘米；伞形花序4-12花；苞片披针形至线状披针形，长4-10毫米，基部微膨大；花梗长5-25毫米，初时被粉，渐为无粉；花萼钟状或狭钟状，长5-8.5毫米，内面密被粉，分裂达中部或略超过中部，裂片狭三角形至近披针形，先端锐尖或稍渐尖；花冠鲜黄色，冠筒长10-12毫米，冠檐直径1.5-2厘米，裂片椭圆形，先端2裂；长花柱花：冠筒稍宽，花柱稍短于冠筒，雄蕊着生处距冠筒基部约2毫米；短花柱花：冠筒管状，花柱长约2毫米，雄蕊顶端接近冠筒口。蒴果长圆形，与花萼等长或稍短。花期5-6月，果期8-9月。

产于四川西部（新龙、乾宁、康定、木里）。生长于高山草地，海拔3000-4300米。模式标本采自康定。

本种与丽花报春P. pulchella Franch.极相似，但花冠黄色，分布区的纬度亦较高。
