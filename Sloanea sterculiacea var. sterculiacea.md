贡山猴欢喜(原变种)",
8a.**Sloanea sterculiacea var. sterculiacea**",

## Description
Leaf blade abaxially pubescent at least on veins.

> Forests; 1400-2500 m. SE Xizang, NW Yunnan [Bhutan, India, Myanmar, Nepal].
  "Synonym": "*Echinocarpus sterculiaceus* Bentham, J. Proc. Linn. Soc., Bot. 5(Suppl. 2): 72. 1861; *Sloanea forrestii* W. W. Smith; *S. rotundifolia* Hung T. Chang.
