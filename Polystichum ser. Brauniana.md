## Description
Plants medium-sized to large, evergreen. Lamina papery, green, broadly lanceolate, gradually contracted toward base or not contracted. Stipe greenish when dry. Stipe scales ovate, ovate-lanceolate, or broadly lanceolate, concolorous or bicolorous. Rachis scales broadly ovate to ovate-lanceolate. Pinnules 10-30 pairs, stalked, pinnules of upper pinnae auriculate. Microscales long-type.
  "Statistics": "About ten species: mainly in the Sino-Japanese region, one species with circumboreal distribution; seven species (three endemic) in China.
