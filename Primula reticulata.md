## Description
Herbs perennial. Leaves forming a rosette; petiole 1--3 X as long as leaf blade; leaf blade ovate-oblong to oblong, 2--10 X 2--4.5 cm, glabrous or sparsely minutely glandular along veins abaxially, base shallowly cordate to rounded, margin dentate to crenate-dentate, apex rounded. Scapes 18--45 cm, scarcely farinose toward apex; umbels 4--12-flowered; bracts linear-lanceolate to lanceolate, 0.7--2 cm, occasionally dentate. Pedicel 1--5 cm, farinose. Flowers heterostylous. Calyx tubular-campanulate, 5--11 mm, sparsely pale yellow farinose outside, more densely so inside, parted to 1/3, prominently 5-veined; lobes slightly recurved, lanceolate, apex acute. Corolla white to cream-white or cream-yellow; tube 1--1.4 cm; limb 1--2 cm wide; lobes obovate to obovate-oblong, margin entire to obscurely emarginate. Pin flowers: stamens at middle of corolla tube; style ca. as long as tube or slightly exserted. Thrum flowers: stamens toward apex of corolla tube; style ca. 2 mm. Capsule cylindric, ca. as long as to slightly longer than calyx. Fl. Jun. 2&lt;I&gt;n&lt;/I&gt; = 22.

> In thickets on mountain slopes; ca. 3000 m. S Xizang (Nyalam Xian) [Bhutan, NE India, Nepal, Sikkim].
**143.网叶钟报春（西藏植物志）**

Primula reticulata Wall. in Roxb., Fl. Ind. 2: 21. 1824; Duby in DC., Prodr. 8: 41. 1844: Hook. f., Fl. Brit. Ind. 3: 483. 1882: Pax in Engl. Pflanzenr. 22 (IV-237): 120. 1905; W. W. Smith et Fletcher in Trans. Bot. Soc. Edinb. 33: 446. 1943; Hara Fl. East. Hima1. 249. 1966; S. Gou1d in Hara ＆ Williams Enum. Fl.Pl. Nepal 3: 73. 1982; 西藏植物志: 3: 833. 1986.

多年生草本，具粗短的根状茎和纤维状长根。叶丛高10-30(40）厘米；叶片卵状矩圆形至矩圆形，长2-10厘米，宽2-4.5厘米，先端圆形，基部浅心形或圆形，边缘具牙齿或圆齿状牙齿，两面秃净或下面沿叶脉被极稀疏的小腺体，中肋稍宽，侧脉12-14对，在下面显著；叶柄与叶片近等长至长于叶片2-3倍。花葶高18-45厘米，近顶端微被粉；伞形花序4-12花；苞片线状披针形至披针形，先端边缘有时具小齿，腹面多少被粉；花梗纤细，长1-5厘米，近顶端被粉；花萼狭钟状，长5-11毫米，明显具5脉，外面被小腺体和稀薄黄粉，内面密被黄粉，分裂深达全长的1/3，裂片披针形，先端锐尖，微向外反；花冠白色、乳白色或乳黄色，冠筒长10-14毫米，喉部无环状附属物，筒口周围被黄粉，冠檐直径1-2厘米，裂片倒卵形或倒卵状矩圆形，先端全缘或微具凹缺；长花柱花：雄蕊着生于冠筒中部，花柱与冠筒等长或微露出筒口；短花柱花：雄蕊着生于冠筒中上部，花药顶端靠近筒口，花柱长约2毫米。蒴果筒状，等长于或稍长于花萼。花期6月。

产于我国西藏南部（聂拉木）。生长于山坡灌丛中，海拔3000米。分布于尼泊尔、锡金、不丹。模式标本采自尼泊尔。
