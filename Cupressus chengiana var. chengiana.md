岷江柏木 (原变种)",
7a.**Cupressus chengiana var. chengiana**",

## Description
Branchlets terete. Seed cones globose to subglobose; cone scales 8-10.

> *  Mountain slopes, valleys; 900-2900 m. S Gansu, N and W Sichuan
