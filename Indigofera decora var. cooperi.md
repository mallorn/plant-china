## Description
Leaves 13-17(-23)-foliolate; leaflet blades alternate or opposite, both surfaces with sparse appressed medifixed 2-branched trichomes.
  "Statistics": "● Near streams, scrub, trailsides; 400-1500 m. Fujian, Jiangxi, Zhejiang.
  "Synonym": "*Indigofera cooperi* Craib, Notes Roy. Bot. Gard. Edin-burgh 8: 50. 1913.
