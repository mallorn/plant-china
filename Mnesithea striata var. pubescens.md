## Description
Culms and peduncles pubescent above. Leaf sheaths with tubercle-based hairs; leaf blades densely pubescent.

> Open forests, grassy hill slopes; 600–1200 m. SW Yunnan [NE India].
  "Synonym": "*Rottboellia striata* var. *pubescens* Hackel in A. Candolle &amp; C. Candolle, Monogr. Phan. 6: 302. 1889; *Coelorachis stri-ata* var. *pubescens* (Hackel) Bor.
**1b. 毛空轴茅（拟）毛罗氏草（禾本科图说）图版66：6-7**

Coelorachis striata var. pubescens (Hack.) Bor, Grass. Burma Ceyl. Trid. Pakist. 121. 1960. ——Rottboellia striata Nees ssp. genuina var. pubescens Hack. in DC. Monogr. Phan. 6: 302. 1889; 中国主要植物图说. 禾本科802. 1959.

与原变种的区别：叶片较宽，两面密被柔毛，叶鞘及花序柄亦被柔毛。花果期秋季。

产于云南南部至西南部；生于路旁草丛中。印度、缅甸也有分布。模式标本采自印度。
