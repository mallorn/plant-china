无毛光果悬钩子",
80b.**Rubus glabricarpus var. glabratus** C. Z. Zheng & Y. Y. Fang",

## Description
Branchlets, petioles, and pedicel glabrous, with sparse, stalked glands. Aggregate fruit 1.5–2 cm in diam. or slightly larger.

> Forests on slopes; ca. 1100 m. Zhejiang (Suichang Xian).
  "Synonym": "*Rubus jiangxiensis* Z. X. Yu et al.
