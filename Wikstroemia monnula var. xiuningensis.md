> The varietal epithet was misspelled *\"xiningensis\"* on p. 646 of the protologue. It was correctly spelled *\"xiuningensis*,\" corresponding with the transliteration of the type locality, Xiuning, on p. 453 (key), p. 457 (Chinese description), and p. 693 (index). The same name was published two months later by the same authors with the same type (Acta Phytotax. Sin. 28: 162. Apr 1990) and spelled *\"xiuningensis\"* throughout.
