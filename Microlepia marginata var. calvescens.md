光叶鳞盖蕨",
5b.**Microlepia marginata var. calvescens** (Wallich ex Hooker) C. Christensen Index Filic. 208. 1905.",

## Description
Fronds 1-pinnate or bipinnatifid, thickly papery, both surfaces glabrous or almost glabrous; pinnae long stalked. Sori mostly near sinus; indusium glabrous or almost glabrous.

> Forests, shrublands; below 100-1300 m. Chongqing, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hainan, Hunan, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan, Zhejiang [India, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Davallia calvescens* Wallich ex Hooker, Sp. Fil. 1: 172. 1845; *D. urophylla* Wallich ex Hooker; *Microlepia calvescens* (Wallich ex Hooker) C. Presl; *M. marginalis* var. *calvescens* (Wallich ex Hooker) Beddome; *M. urophylla* T. Moore.

Microlepia calvescens (Wall.) Presl, Epim. Bot. (1849) 95——Davallia calvescens Wall. List. (1828) n. 2983; Hook. Sp. Fil. I (1846) 172, t. 4813——Microlepia marginata var. calvescens C. Chr. Ind. Fil. (1905) 208; in Contr. U. S. Nat. Herb. XXVI (1931) 205; Wu, Wong et Pong in Bull. Dept. Biol. Sun Yatsen Univ. No. 3 (1932) 114, t. 48; Tard.-Blot et C. Chr. in Fl. Indo-Chine. VII, u (1939) 94; Ching in Sunyatsensia VI (1941) 11——Davallia urophylla Wall. List (1828) n. 2683——Microlepia urophylla Moore, Ind. Fil. (1861) 290; Bedd. Ferns Brit. Ind. (1866) t. 103.

植株强大，高约1.2米。根状茎粗，直径达5毫米，密被褐红色钻状毛。叶远生，相距2厘米；叶柄长70厘米，粗壮，禾桿色，圆，上面有狭纵沟，除基部被毛外，通体光滑；叶片长70厘米，宽24-26厘米，长圆形，先端长尾状，几为二回羽状；羽片22-25对，基部近对生，远离，相距5.5-8厘米，上部的互生，斜展，有柄（柄长7毫米），长披针形，镰刀状，长18-20厘米，宽2.3-2.7厘米，先端长渐尖，基部不等，下侧略短，楔形，羽状深裂；裂片斜长圆形，宽7毫米，密接，边缘有锯齿。叶脉在裂片上呈羽状，粗而隆起，4-5对，单一或二又，斜出。叶厚纸质，干后棕绿色，两面光滑或几光滑，叶轴及羽轴下面密被棕黄色短柔毛。抱子囊群圆形，每裂片上5-7个，近边缘着生；囊群盖杯形，较宽，上边缘截形，距叶缘较远，光滑或几为光滑。

产浙江、福建、广东、海南、广西、贵州、四川及云南。越南及印度也有。
