## Description
Stem erect or slightly prostrate. Leaves ovate-lanceolate, 7--12 × 2.5--4 cm, glabrous or subglabrous, base sheathed, margin ciliate, apex acuminate. Cymes terminal, laxly several flowered. Flowers trumpet-shaped. Tepals 1.5--2.5 cm, adaxially pubescent. Stamens included; filaments slender. Ovary densely pubescent. Style subequaling stigmatic lobes. Capsule 2.5--3 cm, densely pubescent. Fl. and fr. Jul--Sep.

> * Near sea level to 1600 m. Taiwan.
