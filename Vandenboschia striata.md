> *Trichomanes striatum* is now confirmed as an earlier name for the plant formerly called *Vandenboschia birmanica* (see Ebihara et al., Acta Phytotax. Geobot. 60: 33. 2009).The names *Vandenboschia radicans* (Swartz) Copeland (Philipp. J. Sci. 67: 54. 1938; *Trichomanes radicans* Swartz, J. Bot. (Schrader) 1800(2): 97. 1801) and *V. orientalis* (C. Christensen) Ching (Fl. Reipubl. Popularis Sin. 2: 187. 1959; *T. orientale* C. Christensen, Index Filic. 646. 1906) have been misapplied to Chinese plants.

## Description
Plants 15-40 cm tall. Rhizome deep smoke-colored, 1-1.5 mm in diam., densely covered with dark brown multicellular hairs, with sparse hairy rootlets. Stipes remote, 2-4 cm apart, light brown, 4-10 cm × 0.8-2.5 mm, glabrous, broadly winged nearly to base. Rachis and costae broadly winged throughout, glabrous. Lamina tripinnate to quadripinnatifid, broadly lanceolate, 8-14 × 2.5-5 cm, membranous, glabrous; pinnae 10-12 pairs, alternate, sessile, spreading, oblong-ovate, 1-2.5 × 0.5-2 cm, base unequally cuneate, apex obtuse; pinnules 3-5 pairs, alternate, sessile, cuneate to obovate, 3-7 × 2.5-6 mm, base decurrent, apex obtuse; ultimate segments simple or forked, narrowly linear, 2-3 × 0.6-0.8 mm, with 1(or 2) veinlets, margin entire, apex rounded. Veins dichotomous, dark green-brown, obviously raised on each surface, glabrous. Sori on upper parts of frond, apical on short acroscopic segments; involucres tubular, ca. 1.5 mm, truncate and not dilated at mouth, narrowly winged, lobes slightly constricted below base of involucre; receptacles projecting, brown, filiform, ca. 3 mm, straight. 2*n* = 72.

**11.华东瓶蕨**

Vandenboschia orientalis (C. Chr. ) Ching, comb. nov.——Trichomanes orientale C. Chr. Ind. Fil. (1905) 646; Dunn & Tutch. Fl. Kwangt. & Hongk. in Kew Bull. Add. Ser. X (1912) 336; Ogata, Ic. Fil. Jap . I (1928) t. 47; DeVol, Ferns East. Centr. China in Notes Bot. Chin. Mus. Heude No. 7(1945) 48——T. japonicum Franch. et Sav. Enum. Pl. Jap. II (1879) 207, 618, non Thunb. 1784, nec Poir. 1808.

植株高10-15厘米。根状茎长，横走，粗1-1.5毫米，暗褐色，密被黑褐色多细胞的节状毛。叶远生，相距0.5-2厘米；叶柄长3-5厘米，粗不及1毫米，淡褐色，上面有浅沟，两侧有阔翅几达基部，翅连叶柄宽2-2.5毫米，基部被节状毛，余几光滑；叶片卵状长圆形，长5-10厘米，宽3-5厘米，3-4回羽裂；羽片8-12对，互生，几无柄或具有翅的短柄，下部的开展，上部的斜向上，最下部的三对最大，三角状长圆形至斜卵形，长1.5-2厘米，宽1-1.5厘米，间隙宽1-1.5厘米，先端渐尖，基部斜楔形；小羽片3-5对，互生，无柄，斜向上，卵形至斜卵形，长5-8毫米，宽4-6毫米，钝头，基部楔形，相距2-3毫米，基部上侧的小羽片较大且常复盖叶轴；末回裂片线状长圆形，长1-3毫米，宽约1毫米，单一或分叉，斜向上，圆头，全缘。叶脉叉状分枝，暗绿褐色，两面明显隆起，无毛。叶薄膜质，干后暗绿褐色，光滑无毛。叶轴淡褐色，全部有平坦的翅，无毛，上面有浅沟。孢子囊群通常生于小羽片下半部向轴的短裂片顶端；囊苞管状，长约1毫米，宽约0.8毫米，两侧有狭翅，口部稍膨大；囊群托长，突出，纤细如丝，长达8毫米，黑褐色，稍弯弓。

产于浙江（杭州）、福建、台湾、江西（庐山、武功山）、湖南（雪峰山）、广西（瑶山）、贵州（都匀）、云南（蒙自）。生于林下溪边潮湿的岩石上，海拔660-700米。分布于我国西南、华南及华东，北达日本及朝鲜。
