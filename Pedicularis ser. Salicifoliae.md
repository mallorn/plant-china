柳叶系",
50.**Pedicularis ser. Salicifoliae** Bonati",

> One species.

## Description
Stems erect, branched or not. Leaves opposite, lanceolate to linear, margin entire or obscurely crenate. Inflorescences spicate. Calyx lobes 5, &amp;#177; equal or sometimes unequal, entire. Corolla tube slightly longer than calyx; galea short, toothless, beakless.
