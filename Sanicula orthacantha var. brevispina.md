> This variety has reputed medicinal value.

## Description
Roots fibrous, without rhizome. Inflorescence elongate, loose and spreading; 6 or 7 flowers per umbellule. Fruit erose-spinose on the ribs, tuberculate in the furrows.

> Forests, roadsides on mountain slopes; 1700–2400 m. Chongqing (Jinfo Shan), SC Sichuan (Emei Shan).
**10a. 短刺变豆菜（拟）鸭脚七（四川）图版22**

Sanicula orthacantha S. Moore var. brevispina de Boiss. in Bull. Soc. Fr. 53: 421. 1906; Wolff in Engl. Pflanzenr. 61 (IV. 228): 55. 1913; Shan in Sinensia 7 (4): 485. 1936.

本变种与正种的区别，在于伞形花序较长而疏展，顶端伞梗1-2，小伞形花序有雄花6-7朵；花柄也较硬，果棱槽中的皮刺呈细瘤状。

产四川（峨眉山、南川金佛山）；生长在山坡路旁或林下；海拔1780-2400米。模式标本采自四川峨眉山。

全草可作药用。
