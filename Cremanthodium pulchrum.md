10.**Cremanthodium pulchrum** R. D. Good J. Linn. Soc., Bot. 48: 274. 1929.

## Description
Stem solitary, erect, 15-40 cm tall, slender, ca. 2 mm in diam. at base, proximally glabrous, distally white and brown pilose. Basal leaves petiolate; petiole 8-12 cm, slender, glabrous, base enlarged sheathed; leaf blade abaxially pale green, adaxially green, reniform or ovate-cordate, 1.2-2 × 2.7-3 cm, glabrous, palmately veined, with conspicuous reticulate veins, margin coarsely triangular- or orbicular-dentate, apex rounded or subrounded. Middle to distal stem leaf solitary, bracteal, ovate-lanceolate, ca. 1.5 cm. Capitulum solitary, nodding. Involucre hemispheric, ca. 1 × to 1.5 cm, outside initially white and brown pilose, glabrescent; phyllaries 10-12, in 2 rows, blackish purple, oblong or elliptic, 3-5 mm wide, apex obtuse or acute; inner phyllaries broadly membranous at margin. Ray florets yellow; lamina ovate-lanceolate, to 2.5 cm, ca. 7 mm wide at base, apex acuminate, 3-lobed. Tubular florets numerous, 4-5 mm; tube ca. 1 mm; limb funnelform, ca. 2.5 mm wide. Achenes (immature) obovoid, ca. 2 mm. Pappus initially white, later yellowish brown, as long as tubular corolla. Fl. Jul-Sep, fr. Aug-Sep.

  "Statistics": "Grassy slopes, stream banks; ca. 4000 m. NW Yunnan [Myanmar].

**16.美丽垂头菊**

Cremanthodium pulchrum Good in Journ. Linn. Soc. Bot. 48: 274. 1929; S. Y. Hu in Quart. Journ. Taiwan Mus. 19: 216. 1966; H. Koyama in Mem. Fac. SCi. Kyoto Univ. Ser. Biol. 2: 56. 1968, in add.

多年生草本。根肉质，粗而少。茎直立，纤细，高15-40厘米，上部被白色和褐色有节柔毛，下部光滑，基部直径约2毫米。丛生叶与茎基部叶具柄，柄细长，光滑，长8-12厘米，基部有膨大的鞘，叶片肾形或卵状心形，长1.2-2厘米，宽2.7-3厘米，先端圆形或近圆形，边缘具三角状或圆形大齿，齿端有小尖头，两面光滑，上面深绿色，下面淡绿色，叶脉掌状，网脉明显；茎中上部具1叶，卵状披针形，长1.5厘米，苞叶状。头状花序单生，下垂，辐射状，总苞半球形，长约1厘米，宽至1.5厘米，总苞片10-12，黑紫色，长圆形或椭圆形，宽3-5毫米，先端钝或急尖，背部幼时被与茎上部一样的毛，后脱毛光滑，内层具膜质边缘。舌状花黄色，舌片卵状披针形，长达2.5厘米，基部宽约7毫米，先端渐尖，3浅裂；管状花多数，长4-5毫米，管部长约1毫米，檐部漏斗形，先端宽约2.5毫米，冠毛初时白色，后成黄褐色，与花冠等长。瘦果（未熟）倒卵形，长约2毫米，光滑。花期7月。

产云南西北部。生于海拔4000米的山坡、水边。缅甸东北部（模式标本产地）也有分布。
