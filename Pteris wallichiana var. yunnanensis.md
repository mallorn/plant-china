73b.**Pteris wallichiana var. yunnanensis** (Christ) Ching & S. H. Wu Fl. Reipubl. Popularis Sin. 3(1): 83. 1990.",

## Description
Plants up to 2 m tall. Stipes and rachises with dense purple-brown multicellular hispid hairs; costules abaxially slightly purple-brown and with multicellular hispid hairs.

> Mountain forests, along valleys; 500-2300 m. S and W Yunnan [N India, Nepal].
  "Synonym": "*Pteris yunnanensis* Christ, Bull. Herb. Boissier 6: 957. 1898; *P. tomentella* Handel-Mazzetti.

Pteris wallichiana Agardh var. yunnanensis (Christ) Ohing et S. H. Wu, stat. nov. ——Pteris yunana-nensis Christ, in Bull. Herb. Boiss. 6: 957. 1898; Diels in Engl. u. 植株高达2米。叶柄及叶轴密被紫褐色节状刚毛；小羽轴下面也疏被紫褐色节状毛。

特产云南南部及西部（蒙自、景东、大理、贡山）。生山地林下沟边，海拔500-2 600米。
