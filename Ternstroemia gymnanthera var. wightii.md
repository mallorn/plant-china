## Description
Leaf blade 10-12 × 3.5-5.5 cm, margin apically sparsely serrate. Flowers 1.5-1.8 cm in diam. Fruit ca. 1.5 cm in diam. Fl. May-Jun, fr. Sep-Nov.

> Forests, thickets; 1400-2800 m. Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hubei, Hunan, Sichuan, Yunnan [India].
  "Synonym": "*Cleyera wightii* Choisy, Mém. Soc. Phys. Genève 14: 115. 1855; *Ternstroemia japonica* (Thunberg) Thunberg var. *wightii* (Choisy) Dyer.
