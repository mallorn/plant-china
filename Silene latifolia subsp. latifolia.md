叉枝蝇子草(原亚种)",
46a.**Silene latifolia subsp. latifolia**",

## Description
Fruiting calyx strongly inflated; capsule teeth spreading or recurved. Seeds with flat surfaces.

> Grasslands, scrub grasslands; 1100--1500 m. Xinjiang [SW Asia, S Europe].
