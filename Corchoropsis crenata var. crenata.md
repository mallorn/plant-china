田麻(原变种)",
1a.**Corchoropsis crenata var. crenata**",

> *Corchoropsis tomentosa* var*. micropetala* was described as having much smaller flowers, with petals only ca. 3 mm, but otherwise fits here.

## Description
Ovary densely tomentose. Capsule very densely to sparsely hairy.

> Anhui, Fujian, Gansu, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hebei, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Shaanxi, Shanxi, Sichuan, Zhejiang [Japan, Korea].
  "Synonym": "*Corchoropsis tomentosa* Makino var*. micropetala* Y. T. Zhang; *C. tomentosa* var. *tomentosicarpa* P. L. Chiu &amp; G. R. Zhong.
