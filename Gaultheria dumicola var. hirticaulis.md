糙茎丛林白珠
2e.**Gaultheria dumicola var. hirticaulis** R. C. Fang

## Description
Young and mature twigs densely brown hirsute. Leaf blade ovate, 3–8 × 2–4 cm, abaxially shortly brown hispid, adaxially glabrous, base rounded to broadly cuneate.


> Forest margins, dry places; ca. 2000 m. SE Yunnan (Malipo).
