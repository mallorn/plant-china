金丝马尾连(原变种)",
24a.**Thalictrum glandulosissimum var. glandulosissimum**",

> This variety is used medicinally.

## Description
Petiolule hairs ca. 0.2 mm; leaflet blade 0.7--1.6 × 0.7--1.6 cm. Anther apex mucronate. Stigma triangular winged. Fl. Jun--Aug.

> * Slopes, meadows; ca. 2500 m. W Yunnan.
