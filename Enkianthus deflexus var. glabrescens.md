## Description
Leaf petiole glabrous; leaf blade glabrescent on both surfaces or abaxially sparsely hispidulous on midvein. Inflorescence rachis and pedicels pubescent, also with glandular hairs.

> Mixed forests on mountain slopes; ca. 1600 m. S Gansu (Wenxian).
