## Description
Petiole 1–2 mm; leaf blade usually elliptic, 6–12 × 4–7 mm, both sides glabrous or abaxially subglabrous, glabrescent. Hypanthium and sepals abaxially initially sparsely pubescent, glabrescent. Petals pinkish.

> High mountain areas, rocks; 3900--4200 m. SE Xizang, NW Yunnan [Bhutan, India, Kashmir, Myanmar, Nepal, Sikkim].
  "Synonym": "*Cotoneaster congestus* Baker; *C. glacialis* (J. D. Hooker ex Wenzig) G. Panigraphi &amp; A. Kumar; *C. microphyllus* var. *nivalis* G. Klotz.
