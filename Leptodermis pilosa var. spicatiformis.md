## Description
Leaves to 1.7 cm. Cymes often in spikelike compound inflorescences.
  "Statistics": "● Thickets on roadsides on mountain slopes; ca. 800 m. S Gansu (Huixian, Liangdang, Tianshui), SW Shaanxi (Baoji, Fengxian, Lüeyang).
**34c. 穗花野丁香（变种）**

var. spicatiformis Lo in Addenda 338. 16. 1999.

本变种与原变种的区别是叶较大，长达1.7厘米；聚伞花序常复合成穗状花序状。

产陕西略阳、宝鸡和凤县，甘肃两当、徽县和天水。生于海拔720米处山坡路旁的灌丛中。
