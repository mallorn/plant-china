## Description
Leaves densely pubescent. Pedicel and calyx with brown multicellular glandular hairs. Fl. Jun-Aug.

> Alpine meadows; ca. 4000 m. Yunnan (Bijiang Xian) [NE Myanmar].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Veronica himalensis&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;yunnanensis&lt;/I&gt; P. C. Tsoong, Fl. Reipubl. Popularis Sin. 67(2): 402. 1979.
**大花婆婆纳多腺变种（新变种）**

var. yunnanensis Tsoong, var. nov. in Addenda 403.

花梗和花萼密被栗色多细胞腺毛，叶片被毛也较密。

产云南（碧江）。生海拔4000米的高山草甸
