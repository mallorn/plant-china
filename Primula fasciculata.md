239.**Primula fasciculata** I. B. Balfour & Kingdon-Ward",

## Description
Herbs perennial, efarinose. Leaves forming a rosette; petiole narrowly winged, 2--4 X as long as leaf blade; leaf blade oblong to elliptic or suborbicular, 4--15 X 2.5--7 mm, base rounded to broadly cuneate, margin entire, apex rounded. Scapes to 2.5 cm; umbels 1--6-flowered; bracts linear, 8--10 mm. Pedicel 1.5--3 cm, sometimes scape obsolete with a solitary flower arising from leaf rosette and pedicel to 10 cm, without bracts. Flowers heterostylous. Calyx tubular, 4--6.5 mm, parted to middle or below, distinctly 5-veined; lobes narrowly oblong to triangular, apex subobtuse. Corolla pink to rose; tube 4.5--8 mm, slightly longer than calyx; limb 1--1.5 cm wide; lobes broadly obovate, deeply emarginate. Pin flowers: stamens at middle of corolla tube; style reaching mouth. Thrum flowers with positions reciprocal. Capsule cylindric, 5--10 mm. Fl. May-Jun. 2&lt;I&gt;n&lt;/I&gt; = 18@.

> * Wet meadows, peatlands; 2900--4800 m. Gansu, Qinghai, W Sichuan, NW Yunnan

Primula fasciculata Balf. f. et Ward in Not. Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 9: 26. 1915; Chen in Bull. Fan Mem. Inst. Biol. Bot. 9: 287. 1939: W. W. Smith et Fletcher in Trans. Roy. Soc. Edinb. 61(1): 50. 1943;中国高等植物图鉴3: 797. 1974;西藏植物志3: 784. 1986. ——Primula reginella Balf. f. in Not. Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 9: 197. 1916

多年生小草本，常多数聚生成丛。根状茎粗短，具多数须根。叶丛基部外围有褐色膜质枯叶柄；叶片矩圆形、椭圆形或近圆形，长4-15毫米，宽2.5-7毫米，先端圆形，基部圆形或阔楔形，全缘，鲜时稍带肉质，两面秃净，无粉，中肋稍宽，侧脉隐蔽或不显著；叶柄纤细，具狭翅，比叶片长1-4倍。花葶高可达2.5厘米，花1-6朵生于花葶端；苞片线形，长5-10毫米，基部不膨大；花梗长1.5-3厘米；有时花葶不发育，花1至数朵自叶丛中抽出，无苞片，花梗长可达10厘米，花萼筒状，长4-6.5毫米，明显具5棱，分裂深达全长的1/3-1/2，裂片狭长圆形或三角形，先端稍钝；花冠淡红色或鲜红色，冠筒口周围黄色，冠筒长4.5-8毫米，仅稍长于花萼，冠檐开展，直径1-1.5厘米，裂片阔倒卵形，先端深2裂；长花柱花：雄蕊着生于冠筒中部，花柱长达冠筒口；短花柱花：雄蕊着生于冠筒上部，花药顶端微露出筒口，花柱长约2毫米。蒴果筒状，长5-10毫米。花期6月，果期7-8月。

产于甘肃、青海、四川西部、云南西北部和西藏东部。生长于沼泽草甸和水边、池边草地，海拔2900-4800米。模式标本采自云南中甸。
