星萼龙胆",
157.**Gentiana asterocalyx** Diels",

## Description
Annuals 7-15 cm tall. Stems erect, branched from base, with spreading branches, densely papillate. Leaves crowded; leaf blade subulate to lanceolate, 0.8-1.2 cm &amp;#215; 1.5-2.5 mm, longer than internodes, longer toward stem apex, subleathery, rigid, glossy, margin basal half broadly membranous and ciliolate, apical half cartilaginous and smooth, apex acuminate; veins 1-3, abaxially prominent. Basal leaves withered at anthesis; petiole 0.5-1.5 mm. Stem leaves spreading; petiole tube 1-2 mm. Flowers few, subsessile. Calyx tube tubular, 7-9 mm, membranous, with 15 prominent longitudinal veins; lobes spreading rigidly, needlelike, as long as tube, subleathery, glossy, base narrowed, margin basal half broadly membranous and ciliolate, apical half cartilaginous and smooth, apex acuminate and apiculate, veins 1-3 and prominent. Corolla blue-purple, with pale yellow-white base, tubular-funnelform, 1.8-2 cm; lobes ovate-elliptic, 4-5 mm, margin entire, apex obtuse; plicae oblong, 2.5-3 mm, margin denticulate, subtruncate. Stamens inserted at middle of corolla tube, equal; filaments 3-4 mm; anthers narrowly ellipsoid, 1-1.5 mm. Style 3-4 mm; stigma lobes linear. Capsules narrowly obovoid, 8-9 mm; gynophore to 2.5 cm, stout. Seeds light brown, ellipsoid, 1.1-1.3 mm. Fl. and fr. Jul-Aug.

> * Grasslands on limestone; 3000-3200 m. NW Yunnan.

Gentiana asterocalyx Diels in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 5: 220. 1912: Levl. Cat. Pl. Yunnan 112. 1916; H. Smith in Hand. -Mazz. Symb. Sin. 7: 958. 1936; Marq. in Kew Bull. 1937: 171. 1937: S. Nilsson in Grana Palyn. 7 (1): 103. 1967.

一年生草本，高7-15厘米。茎紫红色，密被乳突，从基部起多分枝，枝坚硬，直立。叶革质，坚硬，有光泽，披针形至钻形，长8-12毫米，宽1.5-2.5毫米，愈向茎上部愈长，先端急尖，边缘密生短睫毛，上缘软骨质，下缘膜质，两面光滑，叶脉1-3条，中脉软骨质，在下面呈脊状突起，光滑，叶柄膜质，背面密生乳突，连合成长1.5-2毫米的筒；基生叶在花期枯萎，宿存；茎生叶对折，开展，密集，长于节间。花数朵，单生于小枝顶端；花梗紫红色，密被乳突，长仅1毫米，连花的下部均藏于叶中，花萼筒膜质，筒状，长7-9毫米，有15条突起的纵纹，裂片革质，坚硬，有光泽，星状开展，钻形，与萼筒等长，先端急尖，基部微收缩，边缘软骨质，具乳突，两面光滑，脉3条，软骨质，在背面呈脊状突起，并向萼筒下延，弯缺宽，截形，花冠上部蓝紫色，下部黄绿色，外面具黄绿色宽条纹，筒状漏斗形，长18-20毫米，两面密生细乳突，裂片椭圆状卵形，长4-5毫米；先端钝，褶矩圆形，长2.5-3毫米，先端截形，边缘有不整齐细齿；雄蕊着生于冠筒中部，整齐，花丝丝状钻形，长3-3.5毫米，花药直立，短圆形，长1.3-1.5毫米；子房椭圆形，长3-3.5毫米，两端渐狭，柄粗壮，长仅1毫米，花柱线形，连柱头长3-4毫米，柱头2裂，裂片外卷，线形。蒴果内藏，矩圆形，长8-9毫米，先端钝圆，具宽翅，两侧边缘具狭翅，翅有不整齐细齿，柄粗，长仅2毫米；种子红褐色，有光泽，椭圆形，长1.1-1.3毫米，表面具致密的细网纹。花果期7-8月。

产云南（丽江，模式标本产地）。生于山坡白垩土上，海拔3000-3140米。
