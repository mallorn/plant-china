广西少齿悬钩子",
73b.**Rubus paucidentatus var. guangxiensis** T. T. Yu & L. T. Lu",

## Description
Blade of leaflets oblong-elliptic to elliptic, terminal leaflet slightly longer than lateral leaflets, margin nearly entire.

> Montane valleys, dense forests along stream sides; ca. 800 m. S Guangxi (Shiwan Dashan).
**69b. 广西少齿悬钧子（变种）**

R. paucidentatus Yu et Lu var. guangxiensis Yu et Lu, 植物分类学报 20(3): 304. 1982.

与原变种区别在于小叶片长圆状椭圆形或椭圆形，长5-7厘米，边缘近全缘，顶生小叶仅稍长于侧生小叶。

产广西（思乐十万大山）。生于山谷或溪旁密林中，海拔800米。
