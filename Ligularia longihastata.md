## Description
Stem erect, to 56 cm tall, ca. 4 mm in diam. at base, proximally glabrous, distally white arachnoid-puberulent and shortly yellowish brown pilose. Basal leaves petiolate; petiole to 30 cm, glabrous, base sheathed, apically narrowly winged; leaf blade narrowly triangular-hastate or ovate-cordate, 4-8.5 × 5-9 cm, abaxially shortly yellowish brown pilose only along veins, adaxially shortly yellowish brown pilose, palmately veined, margin denticulate, rarely crenate, apex acuminate or acute, rarely retuse; sinus 1/4-1/3 as long as leaf blade, broad; basal lobes divergent. Middle stem leaves smaller, cordate-reniform, shortly petiolate; sheath slightly enlarged, to 6 cm. Distalmost stem leaves sessile, broadly triangular; sheath enlarged. Synflorescence racemose, to 7 cm; leaflike bracts linear-lanceolate; peduncles 7-15 mm, shortly yellowish brown pilose. Capitula numerous. Involucre campanulate, 1-1.2 cm × 7-9 mm, outside glabrous; phyllaries 8 or 9, in 2 rows, lanceolate, 2-4 mm wide, margin membranous, apex acuminate. Ray florets yellow; lamina linear, 1.5-2(-3) cm × 1.5-2 mm, apex 3-lobed; tube ca. 5 mm. Tubular florets ca. 9 mm; tube ca. 4 mm; limb ca. 2 mm wide. Pappus pale yellow, as long as tube of tubular corolla. Fl. Jul-Aug.

Ligularia longihastata Hand.-Mazz. in. Sitzgsanz. Akad. Wiss. Wien, Math. -Nat. 62: 11. 1925, Symb. Sin. 7: 1137. t. 3l. f. 5. 1936 et in Bot. Jahrb. 69: 134. 1938; S. Y. Hu in Quart. Journ. Taiwan Mus. 20: 65. 1967.

多年生草本。根肉质。茎直立，高达56厘米，上部被白色蛛丝状柔毛和黄褐色有节短柔毛，下部光滑，基部直径约4毫米，被枯叶柄纤维包围。丛生叶与茎下部叶具柄，柄长达30厘米，光滑，最上部有窄翅，基部鞘状，叶片长三角状戟形或卵状心形，长4-8.5厘米，宽5-9厘米，先端渐尖或钝，稀凹缺，边缘具细齿，稀为圆形齿，基部弯缺宽，长为叶片的1/3至1/4，两侧裂片叉开，上面被黄褐色有节短柔毛，下面脉上被同样的毛，叶脉掌状；茎中部叶心状肾形，较小，具短柄，鞘略膨大，长达6厘米；最上部叶无柄，宽三角形，鞘膨大。总状花序长约7厘米；苞片线状披针形；花序梗长7-15毫米，被黄褐色有节短毛；头状花序多数，辐射状；总苞钟状，长10-12毫米，宽7-9毫米，总苞片8-9，披针形，宽2-3毫米，先端渐尖，背部光滑，边缘膜质。舌状花黄色，舌片线形，长15-20（30）毫米，宽1.5-2毫米，先端3浅裂，管部长约5毫米；管状花多数，长约9毫米，管部长约4毫米，檐部宽约2毫米，冠毛淡黄色与花冠管部等长。瘦果（未熟）光滑。花期7-8月。

产云南西北部。生于海拔3400-3800米的草坡。模式标本采自澜沧江一怒江分水岭。
