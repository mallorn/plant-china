狭瓣纺锤毛茛
72b.**Ranunculus limprichtii var. flavus** Handel-Mazzetti

## Description
Stems and leaves glabrous. Flowers 0.6--0.7 cm in diam. Sepals abaxially glabrous. Petals long elliptic, 3--3.7 × 1--1.4 mm, nectary pit without a scale, apex obtuse.


> * Rocky areas, scrub; 4000--4200 m. NW Sichuan (Songpan Xian).
