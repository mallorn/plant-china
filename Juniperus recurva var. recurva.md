7a.**Juniperus recurva var. recurva**",

## Description
Trees small, rarely shrubs. Branchlets short, pendulous. Leaves 3-8 mm long, adaxial surface of leaves greenish white, slightly glaucous, with an obscure midvein. Seed cones 7-12 ×  6-9 mm. Seeds ovoid, 6-8 ×  5-6 mm.

> Forests or thickets; 2700-3900 m. SE Xizang, NW Yunnan [Afghanistan, Bhutan, N India, Kashmir, Nepal, Pakistan, Sikkim]
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Sabina recurva&lt;/I&gt; (Buchanan-Hamilton ex D. Don) Antoine.
