## Description
Leaves present in winter; petiole distinctly winged; leaf blade triangular-lanceolate, 3-7 × 3.5-5 cm, usually glabrous, rarely pubescent, base truncate or slightly cordate, apex acuminate. Fl. Nov-Apr, fr. May-Oct. 2*n* = 24*.
