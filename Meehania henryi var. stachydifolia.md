## Description
Petiole slightly dilated at apex, villous; leaf blade narrowly elliptic-oblong, base rounded, margin sparsely serrate. Terminal raceme shorter or ternate on stem apex.

> * Streamsides in montane forests; ca. 700 m. Guizhou.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Dracocephalum stachydifolium&lt;/I&gt; H. Léveillé, Repert. Spec. Nov. Regni Veg. 8: 422. 1910.
**3c. 圆基叶变种**

Meehania henryi (Hemsl.) Sun var. stachydifolia (Levl.) C. Y. Wu in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 8 (1)：16. 1959——Dracocephalum stachydifolium Levl. in Fedde, Repert. Sp. Nov. 8：422. 1910; Dunn in Journ. Linn. Soc. Bot. 39：443. 1911——Dracocephalum urticifolium Miq. var. angustifolia f. racemosa Dunn in Notes Bot. Gard. Edinburgh 6：171. 1915, p. p. quoad syn. Dr. stachydifolium Levl. et specim. Cavalerie 1034——Meehania urticaefolia auct. non (Miq.) Makino: 孙雄才, 科学社生物所论文集 7: 234. 1932, p. p., quoad Tsiang 6316.

这一变种与原变种不同在于叶长椭圆状卵圆形，基部圆形，疏生尖锯齿，叶柄顶端稍宽，被具节长柔毛；花序顶生总状，较短，基部常呈三分枝。

产贵州（三合、平坝）；生于山间溪边林下，海拔约700米。模式标本采自贵州平坝。
