## Description
Lianas. Stem striate, glabrous. Leaves 3-foliolate; petiole ca. 9 cm, glabrous except villous at joint connected with petiolules; leaflets: petiolules 1-2 cm, sparsely villous; blade orbicular, 10-11 × ca. 9 cm, thickly papery or subleathery, abaxially puberulent on veins, adaxially glabrous, lateral veins 6- or 7-paired, conspicuous on both surfaces, veinlets lax, conspicuous abaxially, base subcordate, apex mucronate. Cymose panicles axillary, lax, large. Flowers unknown. Fruit 7-9 cm in diam., 2-winged, wings 3-4 cm wide. Fr. Mar.

**1.圆叶青藤 图版128: 1**

Illegera orbiculata C. Y. Wu in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 17 (2): 75, f. 1: 1. 1979.

藤本。茎具条纹，无毛。指状复叶有3小叶，叶柄长约9厘米，除与小叶柄相连接处有柔毛外，其余无毛。小叶柄长1-2厘米，疏被柔毛。小叶圆形，长10-11厘米，宽约9厘米，厚纸质或近革质，先端突渐尖，基部近心形，全缘，上面无毛，下面沿脉疏被微柔毛；侧脉6-7对，两面明显，网脉仅在下面较显著，稀疏。聚伞状圆锥花序腋生，较大而疏松。花未见。果具2翅，直径7-9厘米，具条纹。果期3月。

产云南南部。生于水边林中，海拔约600米。

本种与三叶青藤I. trifoliata (Griff.) Dunn近似；但果实较大，直径7-9厘米，小叶圆形而不同。
