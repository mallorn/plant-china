小基隆毛茛",
25b.**Ranunculus hirtellus var. humilis** W. T. Wang",

## Description
Stems 7--14 cm. Basal leaves with petiole 1.5--3 cm; blade 3-sect or 3-partite, sometimes 3-fid nearly to middle, 0.5--1.5 × 0.7--2 cm. Flowers 0.9--1.1 cm in diam. Receptacle puberulent. Achene puberulent or glabrous. Fl. Jul--Sep.

> * Alpine meadows, on rocks; 4000--4800 m. S Qinghai, W Sichuan, S and E Xizang.
