贡山狭叶委陵菜",
18b.**Potentilla stenophylla var. cristata** (H. R. Fletcher) H. Ikeda & H. Ohba",

## Description
Leaflets 0.8–1.5 cm, with 11–13 teeth, glabrous except for tufts of hairs on apices of teeth. Fl. Jul–Aug.

> Alpine meadows; 3500--3700 m. NW Yunnan (Gaoligong Shan) [Myanmar].
  "Synonym": "*Potentilla cristata* H. R. Fletcher, Notes Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinburgh 20: 218. 1950.
