## Description
Shrubs evergreen, climbing, with scattered, curved prickles. Leaflets 3; stipules free or with base adnate to petiole, caducous, serrate. Flower solitary, large; pedicel and hypanthium densely glandular bristly; bracts absent. Sepals persistent, erect, margin entire. Styles free, not exserted.
  "Statistics": "One species: China, Vietnam, cultivated elsewhere.
