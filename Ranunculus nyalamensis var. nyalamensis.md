聂拉木毛茛(原变种)",
78a.**Ranunculus nyalamensis var. nyalamensis**",

## Description
Stems 6--11.5 cm, puberulent from base to apex. Leaf blade adaxially glabrous, abaxially puberulent. Flower 1--1.2 cm in diam. Petals oblate, 5.5--6 × 3.5--5 mm, nectary pit without a scale. Fl. Jun.

> * Grassy places by streams; ca. 4200 m. S Xizang (Nyalam Xian).
