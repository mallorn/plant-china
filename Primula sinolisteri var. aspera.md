## Description
Scapes and petiole pilose, with multicellular hairs. Flowers homostylous. Calyx to 1 cm. Corolla rose-lavender or dark purplish rose; stamens and style located in upper portion of tube.

> * On rocks and mossy banks of shaded slopes bordering valleys; 3000--3300 m. SW Yunnan (Tengchong Xian).
**16b.糙叶铁梗报春（变种）（新拟）**

var. aspera W. W. Smith et. Fletcher in Trans. Roy. Soc. Edinb. 61 (2): 428. 1946.

叶柄、花葶被多细胞柔毛；花同型，花萼长达1厘米，花冠紫红色。

产于云南西部。生长于山谷湿润岩石上，海拔3000-3300米。模式标本采自腾冲。
