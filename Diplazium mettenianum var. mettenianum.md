江南双盖蕨(原变种)",
36a.**Diplazium mettenianum var. mettenianum**",

## Description
Fronds medium-sized. Lamina 25-40 × 15-25 cm; pinnae up to 15(-18) × 4 cm, pinnatilobate to pinnatipartite; veins pinnate, veinlets 5-7 pairs. Sori 2-5(-7) pairs.

> Valley forests; 400-1400 m. Anhui, Chongqing, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hainan, Hunan, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan, Zhejiang [Japan, N Thailand, N Vietnam].

Christensen ex Wu; *D. mettenianum* var. *isobasis* (Christ) Tagawa; *D. textorii* (Miquel) Makino.
