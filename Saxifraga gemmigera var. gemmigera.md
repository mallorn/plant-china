芽虎耳草(原变种)",
142a.**Saxifraga gemmigera var. gemmigera**",

## Description
Cauline leaves narrowly ovate to oblong-linear, 5.1-6.5 mm, margin setose-ciliate or glandular ciliate. Petals narrowly ovate, ca. 4 × 1.4-1.5 mm, not callose, 3-veined, base contracted into a claw 0.9-1 mm.

> * Alpine meadows; 3100-3700 m. S Shaanxi (Miaowang Shan, Taibai Shan).
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Hirculus gemmigerus&lt;/I&gt; (Engler) Losinskaja.
