## Description
Leaf blade lanceolate, 5-9 × 0.8-2 cm, apex long acuminate to caudate-acuminate. Fr. Jun-Sep.

> Along rivers and streams in bushlands; ca. 1700 m. Gansu, Sichuan, Yunnan [India].
  "Synonym": "*Excoecaria himalayensis* var. *cuspidata* Müller Argoviensis, Linnaea 32: 122. 1863; *E. acerifolia* var. *lanceolata* Pax &amp; K. Hoffmann; *E. cuspidata* (Müller Argoviensis) Chakrabarty &amp; M. Gangopadhyay.
**6b.狭叶土沉香（新拟）**

var. cuspidata (Muell, Arg.) Muell. Arg. in DC. Prodr. 15: 1222. 1866; Pax et Hoffm. in Engl. Pflanenr. 52 (IV. 147. V.): 168. 1912. ——Excoecaria himalayensis Muell. Arg. var. cuspidata Muell. Arg. in Linnaea 32: 122. 1863. ——Excoecaria acerifolia Didr. var. lanceolata Pax et Hoffm. in Engl. Pflazaenr. 52 (IV. 147. V.): 168. 1912. syn. nov.

本变种的叶很狭，披针形，长5-9厘米，宽0.8-2厘米，顶端长渐尖至尾状渐尖而与原变种显然有别。果期6-9月。

分布于云南（思茅）、四川（金川、丹巴、康定、泸定）和甘肃南部（文县）。生于河边或灌丛中，海拔约1 700米。印度也有。
