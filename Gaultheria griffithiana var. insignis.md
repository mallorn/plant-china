## Description
Leaf base subcordate. Rachis and pedicels lanuginose or tomentose; bracts orbicular-ovate, 5–7 mm, abaxially densely sericeous at lower part, adaxially densely sericeous. Calyx densely sericeous adaxially.

> *Tsuga* forest margins; ca. 2800 m. SE Xizang (Zayü).
