巴塔以礼草(原变种)",
21a.**Kengyilia batalinii var. batalinii**",

## Description
Culms 20–40 cm tall. Leaf sheath glabrous, rarely pubescent or margin ciliate. Spike rachis glabrous or puberulent. Anthers yellow, ca. 4 mm. Fl. and fr. Jun–Sep. 2*n* = 42.

> Mountain slope grasslands; 2100–3500 m. Xinjiang (Kunlun Shan, Tian Shan), Xizang [Mongolia, Russia].
  "Synonym": "*Triticum batalinii* Krasnov, Bot. Zap. 2: 21. 1887–1888 [*\"batalini\"*]; *Agropyron argenteum* *(Nevski) Pavlov*; *A. bata-linii* (Krasnov) Roshevitz; *Elymus batalinii* (Krasnov) ┴. Löve; *Elytrigia argentea* Nevski; *E. batalinii* (Krasnov) Nevski.
