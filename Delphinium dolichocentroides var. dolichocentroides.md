拟长距翠雀花(原变种)",
131a.**Delphinium dolichocentroides var. dolichocentroides**",

## Description
Leaf blade 3-parted or nearly 3-sect. Pedicels bracteolate near middle or distally. Staminode limb 2-lobed. Ovaries densely puberulent.

> * Grassy slopes, meadows; 3000--3600 m. SW Sichuan (Muli Zang Zu Zizhixian).
