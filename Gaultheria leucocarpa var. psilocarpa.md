秃果白珠",
21c.**Gaultheria leucocarpa var. psilocarpa** (Copeland) R. C. Fang",

> Specimens from the Philippines have scattered, marginal hairs on the filaments, but as Copeland (in the protologue) noted, this is a poor character.

## Description
Twigs glabrous or puberulous, intermixed glandular pilose, or only sparsely glandular setulose. Leaf blade glandular setulose or setulose-apiculate at margin. Inflorescence rachis and pedicels glabrous. Filaments glabrous. Ovary and capsule glabrous.

> Slopes; (800–)1000–2600 m. Taiwan [Philippines].
