金花忍冬(原变种)",
39a.**Lonicera chrysantha var. chrysantha**",

## Description
Leaves abaxially sparsely strigose. Ovary glandular hairy.
  "Statistics": "Forests, scrub at forest margins; 200-2000(-3000) m. S Gansu, Hebei, S Heilongjiang, W Henan, Hubei, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, E Jilin, S Liaoning, S Nei Mongol, Ningxia, E Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi, Sichuan [Japan, N Korea, Russia].

Tung; *L. chrysantha* var. *crassipes* Nakai; *L. chrysantha* var. *linearifolia* S. W. Liu &amp; T. N. Ho; *L. chrysantha* var. *longipes* Maximowicz; *L. chrysantha* f. *villosa* Rehder; *Xylosteon chrysanthum* (Turczaninow ex Ledebour) Ruprecht.
