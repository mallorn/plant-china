## Description
Herbs, acaulescent or subacaulescent with a very short stem. Leaves imparipinnate; leaflets not whorled. Racemes many flowered (rarely only 5-10-flowered); peduncle typically much longer than leaves (shorter in *Oxytropis avisoides*). Legume sessile, subsessile, or with a 1.5-5 mm stipe; body cylindric, ellipsoid, ovoid, or subspherical, scarious or membranous.
