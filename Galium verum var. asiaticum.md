## Description
Plants stout, 50-120 cm tall. Leaf blade to 5-7 cm, glabrous and smooth adaxially. Corolla yellow. Ovary and mericarps glabrous. Fl. and fr. Jun-Aug.
  "Reference": "According to Yamazaki (Fl. Japan 3a: 240. 1993) *Galium verum* subsp. *asiaticum* includes all Japanese forms of *G. verum* and differs from the typical subspecies by longer leaves and hirsute (not minutely pubescent) stems.According to Yamazaki (Fl. Japan 3a: 240. 1993) *Galium verum* subsp. *asiaticum* includes all Japanese forms of *G. verum* and differs from the typical subspecies by longer leaves and hirsute (not minutely pubescent) stems.
  "Statistics": "Mountain grasslands, open fields, river beaches; below 100-1700 m. Anhui, Gansu, Hebei, Heilongjiang, ?Henan, Hubei, Jiangsu, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Shandong, Shanxi, Sichuan, Zhejiang [Japan, Korea, Russia].
