孔岩草(原变种)",
1a.**Kungia aliciae var. aliciae**",

## Description
Basal leaves spiral; middle and distal stem leaves alternate. Inflorescence racemiform, simple or few branched.

> * 2000-2500 m. S Gansu, N Sichuan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Crassula aliciae&lt;/I&gt; Raymond-Hamet, Bull. Soc. Bot. France 55: 710. 1908; &lt;I&gt;Orostachys aliciae&lt;/I&gt; (Raymond-Hamet) H. Ohba; &lt;I&gt;Sedum aliciae&lt;/I&gt; (Raymond-Hamet) Raymond-Hamet; &lt;I&gt;Sinocrassula aliciae&lt;/I&gt; (Raymond-Hamet) A. Berger.
