卵叶獐牙菜",
54.**Swertia tetrapetala** Pallas",

## Description
Annuals 20-30 cm tall. Roots yellow, stout. Stems erect, 1-2 mm in diam., subquadrangular, branched. Basal leaves withered at anthesis. Stem leaves sessile, ovate-triangular, 1-2.7 &amp;#215; 0.4-1.5 cm, base cordate and subamplexicaul, apex acute, veins 3. Inflorescences panicles of cymes, many flowered, narrow. Flowers 4merous. Pedicel erect, to 2 cm, slender. Calyx tube 1-1.5 mm; lobes linear-lanceolate, 5-7 mm, apex acute, midvein distinct. Corolla purple, 1.2-1.5 cm in diam., tube 1-1.5 mm; lobes elliptic, 6-8 mm, apex acute. Nectaries 2 per corolla lobe, radially elongated, margin raised with many compound fimbriae. Filaments 5-7 mm; anthers ellipsoid, ca. 1 mm. Style indistinct; stigma lobes capitate. Capsules ovoid-ellipsoid, 6-9 mm. Seeds subglobose; seed coat warty. Fl. Aug-Sep.

> Meadows; ca. 700 m. Jilin (Changbai Shan) [Japan, Korea, E Russia].

一年生草本，高20-30厘米。根黄褐色，主根明显。茎直立，四棱形，棱上有窄翅，下部直径1-2毫米，仅花序有分枝。基生叶在花期枯存；茎生叶无柄，三角状卵形，长10-27毫米，宽4-15毫米，先端急尖，基部心形，半抱茎，叶脉3-5条，于下面明显突起。圆锥状复聚伞花序狭窄；花梗细瘦，直立，有条棱，长达2厘米；花4数，直径1.2-1.5厘米，开展；花萼绿色，稍短于花冠，裂片线状披针形，长5-7毫米，先端急尖，背面中脉明显；花冠紫色，裂片椭圆形，长6-8毫米，先端急尖，中部具2个腺窝，腺窝沟状，边缘具篦齿状短流苏；花丝线形，长约5毫米，花药矩圆形，长约1毫米；子房具短柄，披针形，先端渐狭，花柱明显，柱头2裂。花期8-9月。

产吉林（长白山）。生于草甸，海拔664米。苏联（模式标本产地）、朝鲜、日本也有分布。
