巨齿西南花楸",
24c.**Sorbus rehderiana var. grosseserrata** Koehne",

## Description
Leaflet margin deeply coarsely serrate. Buds, leaflet blades abaxially along midvein, and inflorescences sparsely rust-brown pubescent, glabrescent. Fruit pinkish white.

> Forests, forest margins; 2600--3000 m. NW Sichuan.
**西南花楸巨齿变种**

S. rehderiana Koehne var. grosseserrata Koehne in Sarg. Pl Wils. 1: 465. 1913.

本变种小叶片边缘锯齿深而粗大，数目也较多，果实带白色，可以区别。

产四川西北部，海拔2600-3000米。模式标本采自汶川。
