## Description
Plants at least partly glabrous; Leaves 1.2--1.8 cm wide, less deeply pinnatifid; segments 3--4 mm wide. Leaves and calyx lobes &amp;#177; white, serrulate-denticulate, teeth callose. Calyx lobes usually 3. Corolla small. Capsule narrow.

> NE Shanxi [Europe].

**轮叶马先蒿宽裂亚种**

Pedicularis verticillata Linn. subsp. latisecta (Hulten) Tsoong, stat. nov.——P. verticillata Linn. var. latisecta Hulten, in Svensk. Vet. Akad. Handl. III, viii (1930) 125; Li, in Proc. Acad. Nat. Sci. Philad. C (1948) 323.

与前一亚种之别，在其叶较宽，宽约1.2-1.8厘米，开裂较浅，裂片宽达3-4毫米。

曾在蕯哈林岛被描述，我国山西五台山有之。
