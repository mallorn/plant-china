密叶翠雀花(原变种)",
68a.**Delphinium kingianum var. kingianum**",

## Description
Stem densely retrorsely puberulent and yellow glandular puberulent. Leaf segments apically acute. Raceme densely yellow glandular puberulent, mixed with a few retrorse, white, short hairs. Ovary densely puberulent.

> * Slopes; ca. 4600 m. S Xizang.
