多花偃卧繁缕",
56d.**Stellaria decumbens var. polyantha** Edgeworth & J. D. Hooker",

## Description
Leaves subulate-lanceolate or subulate-ovate, margin ciliate or glabrous. Flowers in dense headlike cymes.

> Stony areas; 4600--5000 m. Xizang [India, Kashmir, Nepal, Sikkim].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Stellaria cherleriae&lt;/I&gt; (Fischer ex Seringe) F. N. Williams var. &lt;I&gt;polyantha &lt;/I&gt; (Edgeworth &amp; J. D. Hooker) R. R. Stewart.
