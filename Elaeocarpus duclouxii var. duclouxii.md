冬桃(原变种)",
27a.**Elaeocarpus duclouxii var. duclouxii**",

## Description
Leaf blade oblong-oblanceolate or oblong, 3-6 cm wide, papery.

> ●  Evergreen forests; 700-1000 m. Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Elaeocarpus fengjieensis* P. C. Tuan.
