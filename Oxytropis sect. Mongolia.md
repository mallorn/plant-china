## Description
Herbs, 10-15 cm tall, acaulescent. Leaves imparipinnate; leaflets not verticillate. Racemes 3-10-flowered; peduncle typically longer than leaves. Calyx inflated at late anthesis; lobes shorter than tube. Corolla standard 1.8-2 cm.
