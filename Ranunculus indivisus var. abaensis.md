## Description
Upper stem leaves undivided, narrowly lanceolate or linear-lanceolate, margin entire or 1-toothed, rarely 3-lobed. Fl. Jun--Jul, fr. Jun--Aug.

> * Scrub, wet places, alpine meadows; 2100--4300 m. SW Gansu, C and NE Qinghai, NW Sichuan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Ranunculus abaensis&lt;/I&gt; W. T. Wang, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 25: 36. 1987.
