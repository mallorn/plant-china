绿盔密穗马先蒿",
175c.**Pedicularis densispica subsp. viridescens** P. C. Tsoong",

## Description
Plants to 60 cm tall, usually glabrous. Bracts 2--3 X as long as flowers. Corolla ca. 1.6 cm; galea yellowish green; lower lip slightly longer than galea. Fl. Jun--Jul.

> * SE Xizang.
**密穗马先蒿绿盔亚种**

Pedicularis densispica Franch. subsp. viridescens Tsoong in Acta Phytotax. Sin. III (1954) , 297, et in Bull. Brit. Mus. (Nat. Hist.) Bot. II, i. (1955) 21.

与前二亚种之别为全体大部光滑无毛；花冠较大，长16毫米，盔干时变黄绿色。

产我国西藏昌都专区西部。
