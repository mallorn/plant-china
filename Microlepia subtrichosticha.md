## Description
Plants terrestrial, ca. 2.6 m tall. Rhizome creeping, ca. 15 mm in diam., densely brownish shortly hairy. Fronds distant; stipe brown, 1.2-1.5 cm in diam., smooth abaxially; rachillae densely brownish villous; lamina yellow-brown when dried, 4-pinnatisect, oblong, 100-200 × 70-150 cm, thinly herbaceous, abaxially gray pubescent throughout, with brown short hairs adaxially and along veins; pinnae many, spreading obliquely upward, with stalk ca. 1.5 cm, ovate-oblong, middle pinnae tripinnate, ca. 90 × 26 cm, apex caudate and acuminate; pinnules more than 20 pairs, alternate, ca. 5 cm apart, with stalk ca. 3 mm, anadromous, almost spreading, lower pinnules 2-pinnate, broadly lanceolate, ca. 13 × 4-4.5 cm, base equal in width, apex acuminate; ultimate pinnules 16-18 pairs, adjacent, ca. 1 cm apart, spreading, sessile, 1-pinnate, broadly lanceolate, 2.5-3 × 7-9 mm, base asymmetrical, basiscopically broadly cuneate, decurrent, acroscopically truncate, parallel to rachillae, apex acuminate; distal lobes 5 or 6 pairs, oblong to obovoid, margins shallowly crenate, apex rounded-obtuse, suprabasal ones slightly longer, pinnatifid, entire upward. Veins obvious abaxially, pinnately branching. Sori on margin and along ultimate lobules; indusium brownish, shallowly bowl-shaped, slightly hairy.

> ● Fertile sandy beaches in forests; 500-800 m. Hainan.
**51.尖山鳞盖蕨**

Microlepia subtrichosticha Ching, sp. nov.

植株高达2.6米，根状茎横走，粗1.5厘米，密被淡棕色短毛。叶远生，柄粗1.5厘米，褐棕色，上面有狭纵沟，下面平滑，四回羽状深裂；羽片多数，中部的长90厘米，宽约26厘米，卵状长圆形，先端尾状渐尖，柄长约1.5厘米。斜向上，三回羽状；一回小羽片20对以上，互生，相距5厘米；下部的长13厘米左右，宽4-4.5厘米，阔披针形，渐尖头，基部等宽，柄长约3毫米，上先出，几开展，二回羽状；二回小羽片16-18对，接近，长2.5-3厘米，宽7-9毫米，阔披针形，渐尖头，基部不对称，上侧平截，与小羽轴并行，下侧广楔形，下延，无柄，相距的1厘米，开展，一回羽状；末回裂片5-6对，长圆形或倒卵形，圆钝头，略有圆齿，基部上侧一片略长，浅羽裂，向上的全缘。叶脉下面较显，羽状分枝。叶薄草质，干后黄褐色，上面沿叶脉有褐色短毛；下面遍被灰色短柔毛；小羽轴有淡棕色长柔毛密生。孢子囊群沿末回裂片的边缘着生，通常上侧两个，下侧一个；基部上侧一片较大，有2-3对；向顶端每片一个，生上侧近缺刻处；囊群盖浅碗形，谈棕色，略有毛。

产海南岛陵水县（尖山，蕃万村及其附近）。生海滨肥沃的沙质土上。

本种为本属中最大的一种，叶片为四回羽状深裂，易与相近的种区别。
