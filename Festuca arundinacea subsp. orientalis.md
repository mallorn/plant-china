东方羊茅",
26b.**Festuca arundinacea subsp. orientalis** (Hackel) Tzvelev",

## Description
Leaf sheaths usually smooth throughout. Lemmas awned; awn 0.7–2.5(–5) mm.

> Forest margins, wetlands. Xinjiang [Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Russia, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan; SW Asia, Europe].
  "Synonym": "*Festuca elatior* Linnaeus subvar. *orientalis* Hackel, Monogr. Fest. Eur. 154. 1882; *F. arundinacea* var. *aristata* Regel; *F. orientalis* (Hackel) V. I. Kreczetowicz &amp; Bobrov; *F. regeli-ana* Pavlov.

与原亚种的主要区别是，外具短芒，芒长0.7-2.5 (5)毫米。染色体2n＝42（Malik C. P．，Thomas 1966）。

产新疆（温宿、新源、尼勒克、伊宁、塔城）；生于林缘和潮湿的河谷草甸；海拔500-2400米。分布于欧亚大陆温带。
