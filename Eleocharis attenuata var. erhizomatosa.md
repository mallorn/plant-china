## Description
Rhizomes absent. Culms usually ± short. Spikelet ellipsoid-ovoid, ca. 6 mm. Perianth bristles laxly retrorsely spinulose. Persistent style base ca. 1/3 to nearly 1/2 as long as nutlet, nearly as wide as nutlet. Fl. and fr. Jun-Sep.
