## Description
Plants 7--15 cm tall. Leaves 7--14. Inner tepals equaling or slightly longer than outer ones. Filaments 1--2 mm; anthers ca. 6 mm; free portion of connective ca. 4 mm. 2&lt;I&gt; n&lt;/I&gt; = 10*.

> * Forests; 1500--2500 m. Sichuan.
