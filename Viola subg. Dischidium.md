## Description
Plant with aerial stem. Stipules free, small. Flowers yellow. Anterior petal spurred. Style not beaked at apex; stigmas 2-lobed.
  "Statistics": "About 16 species: Asia, Europe, North America; 11 species (eight endemic) in China.
