美穗草 (原亚种)",
1a.**Veronicastrum brunonianum subsp. brunonianum**",

## Description
Stems terete, wingless. Filaments hairy. Fl. Jul-Sep.

> Ravines, shaded slopes with grasses, under forests; 1500-3000 m. Guizhou (Fangjing Shan, Panxian Tequ), W Hubei, Sichuan (E of Erlang Shan), Xizang (Mainling Xian), Yunnan [Bhutan, Nepal, Sikkim].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Calorhabdos brunoniana&lt;/I&gt; Bentham, Bot. Reg. 21: ad t. 1770. no. 72. 1835.
