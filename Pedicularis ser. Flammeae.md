## Description
Herbs low. Roots fusiform, fleshy. Leaves long, &amp;#177; narrow, pinnatipartite to pinnatisect. Inflorescences spicate, centrifugal, usually very long, with dense flowers. Pedicel long. Calyx 5-lobed. Corolla tube decurved distally; galea toothless; beakless; lower lip spreading. Capsule oblique.
