尖瓣木蓝
77.**Indigofera acutipetala** Y. Y. Fang & C. Z. Zheng Acta Phytotax. Sin. 21: 325. 1983.

## Description
Shrubs, to 1.3 m tall. Stems dark brown when dry, terete, 4-ribbed, with small white lenticels, with appressed sparse white medifixed symmetrically 2-branched trichomes. Stipules linear, ca. 2 mm. Leaves ca. 3 cm, 5-11-foliolate; petiole 2-6 mm; stipels not visible; petiolules short; leaflet blades opposite, obovate to obovate-oblong, 3-7 × 2-4 mm, both surfaces with dense appressed white and brown medifixed trichomes but abaxially denser, secondary veins not visible, base cuneate to broadly cuneate, apex rounded and mucronate. Racemes 5-9 cm, densely flowered; peduncle 2-3 cm, longer than petioles, with some glands. Pedicel ca. 1 mm. Calyx with appressed white medifixed trichomes; tube ca. 1 mm; teeth lanceolate, ca. 1.5 mm. Corolla dark purple; standard 7-7.5 × ca. 4 mm, outside hairy, apical mucro ca. 1 mm; wings ca. 6 × 2 mm; keel ca. 6.5 mm. Stamens 5-6.5 mm; anthers ovoid, apex with a ca. 0.3 mm mucro. Ovary glabrous. Fl. Jul, fr. unknown.

Indigofera acutipetala Y. Y. Fang et C. Z. Zheng in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 21: 325. 1983, et 27: 175. 1989.

灌木，高约1.3米。茎圆柱形，干时暗褐色，具白色小皮孔，分枝四棱形，被少数白色平贴丁字毛。羽状复叶长约3厘米；叶柄长2-6毫米；托叶线形，长约2毫米；小叶2-5对，较小，倒卵形或倒卵状长圆形，长3-7毫米，宽2-4毫米，先端圆钝，具小尖头，基部楔形或阔楔形，两面密被白色杂有褐色较粗的平贴丁字毛，下面尤密，侧脉不明显；小叶柄短；小托叶不明显。总状花序长5-9厘米，花密集；总花梗通常比叶柄明显为长，长2-3厘米，与叶轴均有少数腺体；萼筒长约1毫米，萼齿披针状钻形，长约1.5毫米，被白色较粗的平贴丁字毛；花冠暗紫色，旗瓣长7-7.5毫米，宽约4毫米，顶端有长约1毫米的喙，外面密被灰色短绢毛，翼瓣长约6毫米，宽约2毫米，龙骨瓣长约6.5毫米，在中部具短距；花药卵形，药隔顶端具长约0.3毫米硬尖；子房无毛。花期7月。

产四川。生于潮湿山坡，海拔2400米。模式标本采自九龙摩天岭。
