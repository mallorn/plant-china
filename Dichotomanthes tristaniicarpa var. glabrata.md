## Description
Leaf blade initially densely yellowish white tomentose abaxially, gradually glabrescent, subglabrous or slightly pubescent only along midvein when old.

> Slopes; 1300--1500 m. S Yunnan (Simao Xian).
  "Synonym": "*Dichotomanthes tristaniicarpa* f. *glabrata* (Rehder) C. Y. Wu &amp; Lihua Zhou.
