## Description
Shrubs scandent. Branchlets brown or dark purplish brown, terete, yellowish villous, with sparse, stipitate glandular hairs and sparse, curved prickles. Leaves simple; petiole 6–8 mm, yellowish villous, sparsely stipitate glandular; stipules caducous, free, deeply palmately divided nearly to base, lobes linear, villous, stipitate glandular; blade lanceolate or oblong-lanceolate, 7–12 × 2–4.5 cm, veins 6–8 pairs, slightly raised abaxially, papery, abaxially gray tomentose and with yellowish long hairs along veins, sometimes glabrescent, adaxially pubescent only along veins, base rounded to shallowly cordate, margin irregularly serrate, apex acuminate. Inflorescences terminal or axillary, cymose panicles, large, lax, many flowered; rachis and pedicels yellowish villous, stipitate glandular; bracts smaller than stipules, sparsely villous, with stalked glands. Pedicels unequal in length, longer ones to 2 cm, shorter ones 6–7 mm. Flowers not more than 1 cm in diam. Calyx purplish red, yellowish villous, with stalked glands; sepals erect in fruit, ovate-lanceolate, 5–7 × 3–5 mm, margin entire, apex caudate. Petals absent. Stamens many; filaments linear. Pistils few, longer than stamens; style and ovary glabrous. Aggregate fruit black, small, glabrous, with few drupelets, enclosed in calyx; pyrenes rugose. Fl. Apr–May, fr. Jul–Aug.

> Stream sides, roadsides, rockslides, montane valleys, forest margins; 600--700 m. S Fujian, E Guangdong.

**97. 饶平悬钩子**

Rubus raopingensis Yu et Lu, 植物分类学报 20(3): 309. pl. 2. f. 3. 1982.
**97a.饶平悬钩子（原变种）图版17：1-4**

R. raopingensis Yu et Lu var. raopingensis

攀援灌木；枝圆柱形，褐色或暗紫褐色，具浅黄色长柔毛和稀疏腺毛，疏生钩状皮刺。单叶纸质，披针形或长圆状披针形，长7-12厘米，宽2-4.5厘米，顶端渐尖，基部圆形至浅心形，上面仅沿叶脉具柔毛，下面被灰色绒毛，沿叶脉具浅黄色长柔毛，边缘密被不整齐的锐锯齿，叶脉6-8对，在下面稍突起；叶柄长6-8毫米，被浅黄色长柔毛和疏腺毛；托叶离生，深裂几达基部，裂片线形，具长柔毛和腺毛，脱落。花成大型圆锥花序，有多数花，顶生或腋生；总花梗、花梗和花萼均被浅黄色长柔毛和腺毛；花梗长短不等，短者仅6-7毫米，长者可达2厘米；苞片较托叶小，疏生长柔毛和腺毛；花小，直径不到1厘米；花萼紫红色；萼片卵圆披针形，顶端尾尖，全缘，在果期常直立；无花瓣；雄蕊多数，花丝线形；雌蕊少数，花柱长于雄蕊，子房无毛。果实小，由少数小核果组成，包藏在萼内，黑色，无毛；核具皱网纹。花期4-5月，果期7-8月。

产广东。生低海拔的山溪边、路旁、山谷、石边或林缘。

此种和长叶悬钩子R. dolichophyllus Hand.-Mazz.相近，区别在于后者托叶仅上半部分裂，叶片近革质；枝、叶柄和花序均无毛，也无腺毛或仅于花梗和花萼具稀疏腺毛；萼片披针形，外被灰白色绒毛，在果期常反折。
