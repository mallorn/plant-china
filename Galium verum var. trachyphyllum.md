粗糙蓬子菜",
62g.**Galium verum var. trachyphyllum** Wallroth Sched. Crit. 56. 1822.",

## Description
Leaf blade pubescent and scabrous adaxially. Corolla yellow. Ovary and mericarps glabrous. Fl. May-Aug, fr. Aug-Sep.
  "Statistics": "Forests or grasslands on mountain slopes or in valleys, open fields, river beaches; 300-4100 m. Anhui, Gansu, Hebei, Heilongjiang, Henan, Jiangsu, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi, Sichuan, Xinjiang [Korea; Europe].
**36c. 粗糙蓬子菜（变种）（秦岭植物志）　毛拉拉蔓（陕西蓝田），糙叶蓬子菜（黄土高原植物志）**

var. trachyphyllum Wallr. Sched. Crit. Pl. Fl. Halensis 56. 1822; DC. Prodr. 4: 603. 1830; Nakai in Journ. Jap. Bot. 15: 345. 1939; Cuf. in Oesterr. Bot. Zeitschr. 89: 217. 1940; 秦岭植物志 1(5): 19. 1985; 黄土高原植物志 5: 18. 1989.

本变种与蓬子菜不同的是：叶上面被毛，粗糙。花期5-8月，果期8-9月。
