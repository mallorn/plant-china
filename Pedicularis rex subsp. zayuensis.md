察隅大王马先蒿",
88d.**Pedicularis rex subsp. zayuensis** H. P. Yang",

## Description
Plants 50--60 cm tall. Calyx 2-lobed. Corolla white, ca. 3 cm; galea purplish. 2 filaments densely pubescent, 2 glabrous. Fl. Jun--Jul.

> * Alpine &lt;I&gt;Pinus&lt;/I&gt; forests; ca. 3200 m. SE Xizang (Zayü Xian).
