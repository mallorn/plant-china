狭颖披碱草(原变种)",
47a.**Elymus mutabilis var. mutabilis**",

## Description
Plants ± with rhizome. Spikelets usually purple. Glumes lanceolate. Lemma *±* pilose; awn 2–5 mm. Fl. and fr. Jul–Aug.

> Sparse forests, forest glades, among shrubs, mountain slopes, meadows, pebbles; 1300–2400 m. Xinjiang [Mongolia, Russia; SW Asia, Europe].
  "Synonym": "*Agropyron mutabile* Drobow, Trudy Bot. Muz. Imp. Akad. Nauk 16:88. 1916; *A. angustiglume* Nevski; *Goulardia mutabilis* (Drobow) Ikonnikov; *Roegneria angustiglumis* (Nevski) Nevski; *R. mutabilis* (Drobow) Hylander.
