山里红
1b.**Crataegus pinnatifida var. major** N. E. Brown

> This taxon has been long cultivated as an important fruit tree.


## Description
Pedicel and peduncle pubescent, glabrate. Fruit relatively large, ca. 2.5 cm in diam.


> Cultivated in N, NE, and NW China [of cultivated origin].

  "Synonym": "*Crataegus pinnatifida* var. *korolkowii* (Ascherson &amp; Graebner) Yabe; *Mespilus korolkowii* Ascherson &amp; Graebner.

**山里红 红果、棠棣（河北土名），大山楂（江苏土名）**

本变种果形较大，直径可达2.5厘米，深亮红色；叶片大，分裂较浅；植株生长茂盛。

在河北山区为重要果树，果实供鲜吃、加工或作糖胡芦用。一般用山楂为砧木嫁接繁殖。
