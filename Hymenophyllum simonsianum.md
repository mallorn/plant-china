宽片膜蕨",
3.**Hymenophyllum simonsianum** Hooker Sec. Cent. Ferns. t. 13. 1860.",

## Description
Plants 4-10 cm tall. Rhizome dark brown, ca. 0.3 mm in diam., subglabrous, with filiform rootlets. Stipes remote, 1-3 cm apart, dark brown, filiform, columnar, 1-3 cm, subglabrous, narrowly winged at apex. Rachis dark brown, broadly winged throughout, slightly zigzag, glabrous or nearly so. Lamina pinnate to bipinnatifid, linear-oblong to linear-lanceolate, (3-)5-8 × 1.5-2.5 cm, membranous and semitransparent, glabrous; pinnae (3-)5-8 pairs, 2-3 mm apart, alternate, sessile, obdeltoid to obovate, 1-1.5 cm × 6-8 mm; ultimate segments oblong, short, 1.5-2 × 2-4 mm, with 1 or 2 veinlets, margin irregularly serrate, apex obtuse or truncate. Veins dichotomous, dark brown, filiform, obviously raised on each surface. Sori terminal on ultimate segments; involucres oblong to oval, deeply cleft to base, irregularly serrate and obtuse at apex; receptacles clavate, usually included.

> Wet places in forests; 1600-3000 m. Taiwan, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, NE India, Nepal].
  "Synonym": "*Hymenophyllum alishanense* De Vol.

Hymenophyllum simonsianum Hook. 2nd. Cent. Ferns (1860) t. 13; Hook. et Bak. Syn. Fil. (1864) 68; Clarke in Trans. Linn. Soc. II, Bot. I (1880) 438; Bedd. Ferns Brit. Ind. (1868) t. 281: Handb. Ferns Brit. Ind. (1883) 34; Tngl. u. Prantl. Nat. Pflanzenfam. I, iv (1849) 110; C. Chr. Ind. Fil. (1905) 368; Cop. in Philip. Journ. Sci. LXVII (1938) 38; Ogata, Ic. Fil. Jap. VI (1935) t. 276; H. Ito, Fil. Jap. Illustr. (1944) t. 466.

植株高4-6-10厘米。根状茎细长，丝状，横走，粗约0.3毫米，暗褐色，仅在与叶柄相连处疏被柔毛，其余几光滑。叶远生，相距2-3厘米；叶柄长1-1.5厘米，丝状，圆柱形，暗褐色，仅顶端有狭翅，疏被柔毛或几光滑；叶片线状长圆形至线状披针形，长3-5-8厘米，宽1.5-2厘米，一回羽裂几达有阔翅的叶轴；羽片倒三角形至倒卵形，约3-5-8对，长约1厘米，宽约6毫米，相距2-3毫米，互生，无柄，斜升，基部下侧下延，上部指状浅裂为2-3个裂片；末回裂片短，长圆形，长1.5-2毫米，宽2-2.5毫米，钝头或截头，边缘有不整齐的尖锯齿。叶脉2-3回叉状分枝，纤细，暗褐色，两面明显隆起，末回裂片有小脉1-（2）条，不达到裂片先端。叶为膜质，半透明，干后呈淡绿褐色，光滑无毛。叶轴暗褐色，全部有阔翅，光滑无毛或几无毛。孢子囊羣大，直径1.5-2毫米，着生于裂片顶端；囊苞卵形或卵圆形，两唇瓣分裂达基部，先端钝并有不整齐的尖齿。

产于云南（贡山县，菖蒲涌，四季涌；金平县及俅江流域）、台湾。生长在林下沟边岩石及树干上，海拔200-3000米。在印度北部、锡金很常见。
