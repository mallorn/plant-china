## Description
Leaf blade narrowly oblong, rarely lanceolate or elliptic; abaxial surface indumentum whitish.

> Rocky slopes, cliffs; 3900–4200. NW Yunnan.
**220b. 维西纯红杜鹃（变种）（云南植物研究）**

Rhododendron sperabile Balf. f. et Farrer var. weihsiense Tagg et Forrest in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 15: 219. 1927; Tagg in Stevenson, Spec. Rhodod. 534. 1930; Cullen et Chamb. in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 37: 334. 1979; Chamb. in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 39:412. 1982; 云南植物志4: 433. 1986.

本变种与原变种的区别在于叶为狭长形，稀披针形或长椭圆形，下面毛被灰白色，上表面不呈水泡状。

产云南西北部。生于海拔3 960-4260米的悬岩或岩坡上。模式标本采自云南维西。
