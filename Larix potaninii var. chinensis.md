> A vulnerable plant. The name &lt;I&gt;Larix potaninii&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;chinensis&lt;/I&gt; was published as a new combination based on &lt;I&gt;L. chinensis&lt;/I&gt; Beissner (Mitt. Deutsch. Dendrol. Ges. 5: 215. 1896). However, the latter is illegitimate, being a later homonym of &lt;I&gt;L. chinensis&lt;/I&gt; Miller (1768), so &lt;I&gt;L. potaninii&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;chinensis&lt;/I&gt; is effectively a new name based on Beissner’s type.

## Description
First-year long branchlets grayish yellow, light yellow, or brownish yellow. Short branchlets 3-4 mm in diam., densely yellow pubescent. Seed cones 2.5-5 ×  1.5-2.8 cm. Seed scales 40-55, ±  strigose abaxially.

> *  Mountains; 2600-3500 m. S Shaanxi
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Larix chinensis&lt;/I&gt; Beissner (1896), not Miller (1768).
