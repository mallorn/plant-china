红毛花楸(原变种)",
33a.**Sorbus rufopilosa var. rufopilosa**",

## Description
Branchlets, leaflet blades, and inflorescences with rust-red hairs. Leaflet blade elliptic or narrowly elliptic, 1–2 cm × 6–10 mm.

> Coniferous or mixed forests, thickets in valleys; 2700--4000 m. Guizhou, Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, India, N Myanmar, Nepal, Sikkim].
