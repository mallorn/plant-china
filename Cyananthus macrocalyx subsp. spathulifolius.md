## Description
Leaf blade obovate, spatulate, elliptic, or narrowly elliptic, margin slightly crenulate or subentire. Pedicel 5-10 mm. Calyx tube usually less than 12 mm, rarely up to 16 mm.
  "Statistics": "Alpine meadows; 3000-5300 m. SE Xizang [Bhutan, NE India (Assam, Sikkim), Myanmar, E Nepal].
  "Synonym": "*Cyananthus spathulifolius* Nannfeldt, Acta Horti Gothob. 5: 30. 1930.
