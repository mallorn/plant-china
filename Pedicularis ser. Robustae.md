## Description
Herbs low, sometimes &amp;#177; stemless. Leaves alternate, pinnatifid. Inflorescences racemose. Calyx 1/3 cleft anteriorly, 5-lobed. Corolla tube not or slightly longer than calyx; beak straight or &amp;#177; coiled, usually downward uncinate, 2-cleft apically; lower lip large, often wider than long.
