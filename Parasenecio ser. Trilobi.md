## Description
Petiole basally expanded into an amplexicaul auricle or not; leaf blade ovate or broadly ovate-cordate, 3-lobed or coarsely toothed. Involucres cylindric, 2-4 mm; phyllaries 4 or 5. Florets 2-5.
  "Synonym": "*Cacalia* ser. *Trilobae* H. Koyama, Mem. Fac. Sci. Kyoto Univ., Ser. Biol. 2(2): 170. 1969.
