长叶润楠(原变种)",
8a.**Machilus japonica var. japonica**",

## Description
Branchlets yellowish when dry. Petiole 0.8-1.8 cm; leaf blade thinly leathery, oblong-oblanceolate to oblanceolate, 10-13 × 2.5-4 cm, abaxially puberulent at first, becoming glaucescent after, lateral veins 9-12 pairs, flattened on both surfaces, veinlets slightly conspicuously reticulate abaxially, inconspicuous adaxially. Involucral bracts greenish yellow. Fr. Aug-Oct.
  "Statistics": "Broad-leaved forests; 700-2300 m. Taiwan [S Japan, Korea].
  "Synonym": "*Machilus pseudolongifolia* Hayata; *M. thunbergii* var. *japonica* (Siebold &amp; Zuccarini) Yatabe; *Persea japonica* (Siebold &amp; Zuccarini) Kostermans; *P. pseudolongifolia* (Hayata) Kostermans.
