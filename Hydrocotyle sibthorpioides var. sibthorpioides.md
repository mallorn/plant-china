天胡荽(原变种)",
3a.**Hydrocotyle sibthorpioides var. sibthorpioides**",

> This variety is the important medicinal herb “tian hu sui” of traditional Chinese medicine.

## Description
Leaf blade shallowly 5–7-lobed or nearly entire, crenate.

> Forests, wet grassy places, stream banks; 400–3000 m. Anhui, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hainan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan, Zhejiang [Bhutan, India, Indonesia, Japan, Korea, Nepal, Thailand, Vietnam; tropical Africa].
