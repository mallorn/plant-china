大炮山景天(原亚种)",
65a.**Sedum erici-magnusii subsp. erici-magnusii**",

## Description
Basal spur of leaf blade ± 2-cleft. Seeds minutely papillate. Fl. Aug, fr. Aug-Sep.

> * Pastures on slopes, granite in glacial valleys, gravelly places, sandy beaches, rock crevices on slopes; 3800-4900 m. W Sichuan, E Xizang.
