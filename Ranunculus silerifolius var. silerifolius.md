钩柱毛茛(原变种)",
115a.**Ranunculus silerifolius var. silerifolius**",

## Description
Roots fibrous. Basal leaf blades ternate to biternate. Petals 4--5 × ca. 2 mm. Fl. Apr--Oct, fr. Apr--Nov.

> By streams, forests, grassy slopes; 100--2500 m. Fujian, N Guangdong, N and E Guangxi, Guizhou, SW Hubei, Hunan, S Jiangxi, W and SE Sichuan, Taiwan, S and E Yunnan [Bhutan, NE India, Indonesia, Japan, Korea].
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Ranunculus ternatus&lt;/I&gt; Thunberg var. &lt;I&gt;hirsutus &lt;/I&gt; H. Boissieu.
