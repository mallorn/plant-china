红萼蝇子草",
64.**Silene rubricalyx** (Marquand) Bocquet",

## Description
Herbs perennial, 5--15(--20) cm, with procumbent stems, rooting at nodes. Stems sparsely caespitose, erect, simple, densely violet glandular hairy. Basal leaves ovate or narrowly oblanceolate, 2.5--5 cm × 7--13 mm, subglabrous to sparsely villous with long hairs, especially toward margin, apex acute or nearly so; cauline leaves 1 or 2 pairs, elliptic, smaller, 1--3 cm × 4--6 mm. Flowers 1--3, slightly nutant, erect after anthesis. Pedicel 2.5--3 cm, glandular villous; bracts lanceolate, glandular hairy. Calyx campanulate or cylindric-campanulate, membranous, saccate, 1.3--1.7 cm × 8--10 mm, densely glandular villous, umbilicate at base, open at apex, inflated in fruit, longitudinal veins brown or dark violet; calyx teeth broadly triangular, ca. 4 mm, weakly hairy, margin ciliate. Androgynophore ca. 3 mm, woolly-hairy. Petals exserted ca. 7 mm beyond calyx; claws pale red, exserted 2--3 mm beyond calyx, 1.3--1.4 cm × 5--6 mm, cuneate, with triangular-ovate auricles above, villous at base; limbs spreading, violet or dark violet, broadly ovate, ca. 5 mm, 4-lobed or deeply so, medial lobes with minute laciniae or round teeth; lateral lobes dentate or linear, smaller; coronal scales broadly cuneate, 1--1.5 mm, laciniate or dentate. Stamens slightly exserted beyond corolla throat; filaments villous at base. Styles not exserted. Capsule globose or ellipsoid, 7--10 × 6--8 mm, shorter than calyx, 5-toothed. Seeds brown, slightly flattened, ca. 1.5 mm, lustrous, abaxially faintly tuberculate. Fl. Aug--Sep, fr. Aug--Sep.

> * Alpine meadows; 3400--3600 m. SW Sichuan, E Xizang.

**38. 红萼蝇子草（新拟） 红萼女娄菜（西藏植物志）**

Silene rubricalyx (Marq.) Bocquet in Candollea 22 (1): 15. 1967 et Phaner. Monogr. 1: 83. tab. 13. fig. a-c. et tab, 36. fig. e. 1969. ——Lychnis rubricalyx Marq. in Journ. Linn. Soc. Bot. 48: 164. 1929. ——Melandrium rubricalyx (Marq.) Pay et Hoffm. in Engler u. Prantl, Nat. Pflanzenfam. 2. Aufl. 16c: 342. 1934; 西藏植物志1: 724. 1983.

多年生草本，高5-15 (-20) 厘米。根粗壮，木质，常具匍匐茎。茎疏丛生，直立，不分枝，密被紫色腺柔毛。基生叶叶片匙形或狭倒披针形，长2.5-5厘米，宽7-13毫米，顶端急尖或近急尖，下面中脉明显，被柔毛：茎生叶1-2对，较小，叶片椭圆形，长1-2厘米。花1-3朵，微俯垂，花后直立，花梗长2.5-3厘米，被腺柔毛；苞片披针形，被腺毛；花萼钟形或筒状钟形，膜质，呈囊状，长13-17毫米，宽8-10毫米，基部脐形，口张开，果时膨大，密被腺柔毛，纵脉褐色或紫黑色，萼齿宽三角形，长约4毫米，被细柔毛，边缘具缘毛；雌雄蕊柄约长3毫米，被绵毛；花瓣露出花萼约7毫米，爪外露2-3毫米，楔形，长13-14毫米，宽5-6毫米，上部具三角状卵形耳，淡红色，基部具柔毛，瓣片开展，轮廓宽卵形，长约5毫米，紫色或深紫色，4裂或4深裂，中裂片具不明显的缺刻或圆齿，侧裂片较小，齿状或线形；副花冠片宽楔形，呈穹窿状，长1-1.5毫米，具缺刻或牙齿；雄蕊微露花冠喉部，花丝基部被柔毛；花柱不外露。蒴果球形或椭圆形，长7-10毫米，宽6-8毫米，比宿存萼短，5齿裂；种子近球形，微压扁，长约1.5毫米，褐色，光亮，脊具不明显小瘤。花期8-9月，果期9-10月。

产西藏东部（察隅、墨脱）、四川（西南）。生于海拔3 400-3 600米高山草地。模式标本采自西藏米林（昌都至波密间）。
