## Description
Stems erect, unbranched. Leaves in whorls of 3 or 4, pinnatisect. Bases of petioles and bracts greatly dilated, connate. Calyx 2--5-lobed. Corolla tube ca. 3 X as long as calyx; galea strongly deflexed; beak curved, slender.
