光穗披碱草(原变种)
29a.**Elymus glaberrimus var. glaberrimus**

## Description
Plants without rhizome. Lemma awn 10–20 mm. Fl. and fr. Aug–Sep.


> * Rocky slopes; 1400–2300 m. Qinghai, Xinjiang.

  "Synonym": "*Roegneria glaberrima* Keng &amp; S. L. Chen, Acta Univ. Nankin. Sci. Nat. 1963(1): 72. 1963.
