毛角萼翠雀花",
128c.**Delphinium ceratophorum var. hirsutum** W. T. Wang",

## Description
Leaf blade to 6.5 × 10 cm, less divided; central lobe 3-lobulate; ultimate lobules ovate or triangular. Bracteoles contiguous to flower or nearly so. Sepals abaxially hirsute; hairs to 3.5 mm; cornicle 1--1.5 mm. Staminode limb emarginate.

> * Slopes; ca. 3600 m. NW Yunnan (Weixi Lisu Zu Zizhixian).
**81c．毛角萼翠雀花（变种）**

Delphinium ceratophorum var. hirsutum W. T. Wang, 植物学报10: 158. 1962.

与角萼翠雀花的区别：萼片外面被长糙毛，毛长约2毫米。萼片的角长1-1.5毫米。退化雄蕊的瓣片顶端微凹。

产云南西北部维西一带。生海拔3600米山坡。
