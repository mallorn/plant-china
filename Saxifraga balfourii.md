## Description
Stem branched or simple, 6-18 cm tall, glandular or eglandular pilose. Leaf buds prominent in axils at proximal nodes. Proximal cauline leaves often scalelike, less than 1/2 size of median ones, ca. 3 mm. Median cauline leaves elliptic, 0.7-2.9 cm × 3-13 mm, both surfaces densely strigose, margin entire, eglandular or glandular hairy, apex acute. Flower solitary or cyme 2-5-flowered; pedicels glandular hairy. Sepals erect, then spreading, ovate to subelliptic, 3-3.6 × 1.8-2.1 mm, abaxially glandular hairy, veins 4-7, confluent at apex, margin ± glabrous, apex acute. Petals yellow, elliptic to obovate, 6-7.7 × 2.5-4.2 mm, 4-6(-8)-callose, sometimes obscurely so, 3-7-veined, base contracted into a claw 1-1.2 mm, apex obtuse. Stamens 3.5-4 mm. Ovary subsuperior, 1.8-2.2 mm; styles ca. 1.6 mm. Fl. and fr. Jul-Sep. 2n = 32*, 48*.

> * Mixed forests, alpine meadows, rock crevices; 2300-4600 m. NW Yunnan (Heqing Xian, Lijiang Naxi Zu Zizhixian).

Saxifraga balfourii Engl. et Irmsch. in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 5(24): 141. 1912 et in Bot. Jahrb. 48:593. f.9. 1912 et in Engl. pflanzenr. 67(IV. 117) : 138. f. 31. 1916: Hand. -Mazz. Symb. Sin. 7(2) : 422. 1931; J. T. Pan in Acta Biol. Plat. Sin. 1:28. 1982; 中国高等植物图鉴补编 2:31. 1983; 云南种子植物名录上册: 234. 1984.

多年生草本，高6-18厘米，丛生。茎分枝或不分枝，被腺柔毛。茎生叶通常以中部者较大，向下则变小，长圆形、椭圆形至倒卵形，长0.7-2.9厘米，宽0.3-1.3厘米，先端急尖，两面密被糙伏毛，边缘具腺睫毛。花单生于茎顶，或聚伞花序具2-5花；花梗被褐色腺毛；萼片在花期由直立变开展，卵形至近椭圆形，长3-3.6毫米，宽1.8-2.1毫米，先端急尖，腹面和边缘无毛，背面被褐色腺毛，4-7脉于先端汇合；花瓣黄色，椭圆形，长6-7.7毫米，宽2.5-4.2毫米，先端钝圆，基部狭缩成长1-1.2毫米之爪，3-7脉，具4-6（-8）痂体，有时痂体不明显；雄蕊长3.5-4毫米，花丝钻形；子房近上位，长1.8-2.2毫米，花柱2，长约1.6毫米。花果期7-9月。

产云南（丽江、鹤庆）。生于海拔3 300-4600米的杂木林下、高山草甸和岩壁石隙。合模式标本采自丽江与鹤庆。
