斑花紫堇(原亚种)",
68a.**Corydalis cheilosticta subsp. cheilosticta**",

## Description
Outer petals cream with distinct red margins to lower lip; nectary ca. 1/2 as long as spur.
  "Statistics": "● Mossy calcareous ledges under *Juniperus*, steep rocky sides of river gorges, rocky banks by streams; 3300-3600 m. Qinghai.
