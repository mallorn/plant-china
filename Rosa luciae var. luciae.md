光叶蔷薇(原变种)",
70a.**Rosa luciae var. luciae**",

## Description
Flowers 2–3 cm in diam. Petals white.

> Thickets, sea cliffs; sea level to 500 m. Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Taiwan, Zhejiang [Japan (Ryukyu Islands), Korea, Philippines].
  "Synonym": "*Rosa acicularis* Lindley var. *taquetii* (H. Léveillé) Nakai; *R. luciae* var. *wichurana* (Crépin) Koidzumi; *R. taquetii* H. Léveillé; *R. wichurana* Crépin (as “*wichuraiana*”).
