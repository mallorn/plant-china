## Description
Leaf blade with both surfaces densely grayish white or brownish pubescent, base rounded, broadly cuneate, or shallowly cordate.

**13b. 钟氏柳（变种）**

var. tsoongii (Cheng) C. Y. Yu et S. D. Zhao, comb. nov. ——S. tsaongii Cheng in Contr. Biol. Lab. Sci. Soc. China, Bot. 10 (1): 68. f. 7. A-C. 1935; Hao in Fedde, Rep. Beih. 93: 41. 1936, pro syn. S. wilsonii Seemen.

与原变种的区别点为叶两面密被灰白色或稍带褐色的短绒毛，基部圆形、宽楔形或浅心形。

产浙江东部。生河边。模式标本采自奉化。
