卵叶玉盘柯",
38b.**Lithocarpus uvariifolius var. ellipticus** (F. P. Metcalf) C. C. Huang & Y. T. Chang",

## Description
Shrubs or small trees, 1-4 m tall. Leaf blade ovate, 4-10 ×  2-4.5 cm, abaxially with short hairs, margin entire, apex acuminate. Cupule rarely over 3.5 cm in diam.

> *  Dry, hilly areas; 400-1000 m. C to S Fujian, NE Guangdong
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Lithocarpus ellipticus&lt;/I&gt; F. P. Metcalf, Lingnan Sci. J. 20: 218. 1942; &lt;I&gt;L. kwangtungensis&lt;/I&gt; H. T. Chang.
**37b. 卵叶玉盘柯（变种）图版35: 6-9**

var. ellipticus (Metc.) Huang et Y. T. Chang in Guihaia 8(1): 16. 1988. ——Lithocarpus elliptica Metc. Fl. Fukien 64. 1942, pro parte. ——L. kwangtungensis H. T. Chang in Journ. Sun Yatsen Univ. 1960 (1): 29. 1960.

叶卵形，长4-10厘米，宽2-4.5厘米，顶端渐尖，全缘，叶背被较短的柔毛及星状毛；叶柄较细长；壳斗通常比紫玉盘柯小，宽很少超过35毫米。

产福建中部及南部、广东东部及东北部。生于丘陵坡地，较干燥地方。模式标本采自福建南平。
