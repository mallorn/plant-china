三脉钝叶黄芩",
9b.**Scutellaria obtusifolia var. trinervata** (Vaniot) C. Y. Wu & H. W. Li",

## Description
Leaves elliptic to rhombic-elliptic, 3-8 cm, base cuneate-attenuate, margin remotely undulate-dentate, apex acute. Corolla 2-2.5(-3) cm.

> * Streamsides; 600-2500 m. Guangxi, Guizhou.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Scutellaria trinervata&lt;/I&gt; Vaniot, Bull. Acad. Int. Geogr. Bot. 14: 189. 1904; &lt;I&gt;S. vaniotiana&lt;/I&gt; H. Léveillé ex Dunn.
**9b．三脉变种**

Scutellaria obtusifolia Hemsl. var. trinervata (Vaniot) C. Y. Wu et H. W. Li, comb. nov. ——Scutellaria trinervata Vaniot in Bull. Acad. Geogr. Bot. 1: 189. 1904; Dunn in Journ. Linn. Soc. Bot. 39: 492. 1911; et in Notes Bot. Gard. Edinburgh 6:175. 1915; Hand. -Mazz. in Act. Gothob. 13: 340. 1939,descr. emend. ——Scutellaria vaniotiana Levi. ex Dunn in Notes Bot. Gard. Edinburgh: 173. 1915 ——Scutellaria sp. Dunn in Notes Bot. Gard. Edinburgb 6: 174. 1915,quoad Esquirol 291——Scutellaria formosana auct. non N. E. Brown: Kudo in Mem. Fac. Sci. Agr Taiboku Univ. 2: 280. 1929,p. p. min.,quoad syn. Sc. trinervata Vaniot.

这一变种与原变种不同在于叶椭圆形或菱状卵圆形，但绝不为倒卵圆形至近圆形，较大，长3-8厘米，宽1.3-3厘米，先端急尖或近急尖，基部楔状渐狭，边缘不明显疏生波状犬齿（每侧4-8），但绝不为全缘；花较小，一般长2-2.5厘米（罕有达3厘米）。

产贵州南部，广西西部；生于海拔600-1000 （-2500）米林下湿地或溪边。模式标本采自贵州独山。
