## Description
Stipules persistent. Leaf blade appressed pubescent on both surfaces, becoming subglabrous. Male flower: filaments partly connate, glabrous. Female flower: ovary pubescent.

> Mountain slopes; circa 4400 m. W Xizang [Afghanistan, ?Bhutan, India, Pakistan]
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Salix oxycarpa&lt;/I&gt; Andersson, J. Linn. Soc., Bot. 4: 45. 1860.
**241b．尖果密穗柳（变种）图版105：5-8**

var. oxycarpa (Anderss.) Y. L. Chou et C. F. Fang, comb. nov.——S. oxycarpa Anderss. in Journ. Linn. Soc. 4: 45. 1860; Hook. f. Fl. Brit. Ind. 5: 636. 1888; Schneid. in Sarg. Pl. Wils. 3: 171. 1916; Hao in Fedde, Rep. Beih. 93: 112. 1936.

与原变种的区别：子房多毛，花丝部分合生，无毛; 叶两面散生伏柔毛，后近无毛；托叶线形，有锯齿。花期6月。

产我国西藏（日土县）。生于海拔4360米的山坡。分布同原变种。
