## Description
Lemma almost entirely glabrous, sometimes with only minute or single hairs on keel and marginal veins. 2*n* = 28, 42.

> Open grassy slopes, subalpine forest margins; 3300–4200 m. Gansu, Qinghai, Sichuan, Xinjiang, Xizang [Tajikistan].
