## Description
Trees. Branchlets and young leaves glabrous. Petiole slender, 5-15 mm; leaf blade glaucous abaxially, obovate or oblanceolate to oblong, thinly leathery, lateral veins 10-12 pairs, inconspicuous, base cuneate, apex acute to acuminate. Inflorescences densely filiform hairy. Perianth linear-oblong, filiform hairy on both surfaces. Filaments subglabrous. Fruit ca. 1.3 cm in diam.
  "Statistics": "Mountain slopes, evergreen broad-leaved forests; 1500-2000 m. Yunnan (Gongshan) [N Myanmar].
  "Synonym": "*Persea kurzii* (King ex J. D. Hooker) Kostermans.
