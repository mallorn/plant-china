## Description
Leaves and inflorescences scattered on branches rather than near apex of branchlets. Leaflet blades oblong-elliptic, 3.5-10 × 1.5-4 cm, abaxially pale green and finely appressed pilose, adaxially pubescent, secondary veins 6 or 7 on each side of midvein, base symmetric or only slightly asymmetric, apex acute.
  "Statistics": "Thickets on riverbanks; 200-1100 m. Fujian, Guangdong, Guang-xi, Guizhou, Hainan, Hunan, Jiangxi, Yunnan [India].
  "Synonym": "*Millettia pulchra* f. *laxior* Dunn, J. Linn. Soc., Bot. 41: 151. 1912.

var. laxior(Dunn) Z. Wei in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 23: 280. 1985.——M. pulchra Kurz. var. typica f. laxior Dunn in Journ. Linn. Soc. Bot. 41: 151. 1912; 中国主要植物图说·豆科287. 1955.

本变种与原变种不同为：叶和花序均散布在枝上，非集生于枝梢；小叶较大，长3.5-10厘米，宽1.5-4厘米。

产江西、福建、湖南、广东、海南、广西、贵州、云南。印度也有分布。
