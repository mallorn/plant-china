牯岭山梅花",
20b.**Philadelphus sericanthus var. kulingensis** (Koehne) Handel-Mazzetti",

## Description
Leaf blade ovate-elliptic, adaxially glabrous or subglabrous, margin sharply and prominently 9-12-serrate. Racemes 5-10-flowered; rachis 3-10 cm. Corolla 2-2.5 cm. Fl. Jun.

> * Mixed forests; ca. 1200 m. Jiangxi, ?Zhejiang.
