糙叶毛梗翠雀花
93b.**Delphinium eriostylum var. hispidum** (W. T. Wang) W. T. Wang

## Description
Proximal leaves 3; leaf blade hispid. Ovary glabrous. Fl. Apr.


> * Grassy places in valleys; ca. 1200 m. S Guizhou (Wangmo Bouyei-Miao Zu Zizhixian).

  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Delphinium&lt;/I&gt; &lt;I&gt;bonvalotii&lt;/I&gt; var.&lt;I&gt;hispidum &lt;/I&gt; W. T. Wang, Fl. Reipubl. Popularis Sin. 27: 412. 1979.

**62c. 毛柄川黔翠雀花（变种）**

Delphinium bonvalotii var. hispidum W. T. Wang in Addenda.

与川黔翠雀花的区别：叶柄疏被白色糙毛；花梗有黄色腺毛和少数白色反曲的柔毛；心皮无毛。萼距向下弧状弯曲，与萼片近等长。

产贵州南部（望谟）。生海拔1200米山谷中草地。
