多齿总梗委陵菜",
22b.**Potentilla peduncularis var. shweliensis** (H. R. Fletcher) H. Ikeda & H. Ohba",

## Description
Leaflets  broadly ovate, both surfaces densely hairy, margin more than 10-serrate. Fl. and fr. Jun–Oct.

> Forests, alpine meadows, gravelly slopes; ca. 3300 m. Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Potentilla shweliensis* H. R. Fletcher, Notes Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinburgh 20: 215. 1950.
