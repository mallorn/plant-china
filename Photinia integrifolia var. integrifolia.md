全缘石楠(原变种)",
1a.**Photinia integrifolia var. integrifolia**",

## Description
Flowers 4–5 mm in diam. Petals white.

> Evergreen forests; 1500--2500 m. W Guangxi, Guizhou, SE Xizang, W Yunnan [Bhutan, India, Laos, Myanmar, Nepal, Sikkim, Thailand, Vietnam].
  "Synonym": "*Eriobotrya integrifolia* (Lindley) Kurz; *Photinia integrifolia* var. *notoniana* (Wight &amp; Arnott) Vidal; *P. integrifolia* var. *yunnanensis* T. T. Yü; *P. notoniana* Wight &amp; Arnott; *P. sambuciflora* W. W. Smith; *P. scandens* Stapf; *Stranvaesia scandens* (Stapf) Handel-Mazzetti.
