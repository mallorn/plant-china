裂叶堇菜(原变种)",
44a.**Viola dissecta var. dissecta**",

## Description
Leaf blade cordate, glabrous or sparsely pubescent, deeply divided. Fl. Apr-Sep, fr. May-Oct. 2*n* = 24*.

> Grasslands on mountain slopes, mixed forest margins, thickets, field margins, roadsides; below 3000 m. Gansu, Hebei, Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi, N Sichuan [Korea, Mongolia, Russia].
  "Synonym": "*Viola dissecta* f. *pubescens* (Regel) Kitagawa; *V. dissecta* var. *pubescens* (Regel) Kitagawa; *V. ×lii* Kitagawa; *V. pinnata* Linnaeus var. *dissecta* (Ledebour) Regel; *V.** pinnata* subsp. *multifida* W. Becker.
