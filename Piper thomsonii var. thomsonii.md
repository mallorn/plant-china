球穗胡椒(原变种)",
26a.**Piper thomsonii var. thomsonii**",

> This taxon was included within &lt;I&gt;Piper sylvaticum&lt;/I&gt; by Long (Fl. Bhutan 1(2): 342-351. 1984).

## Description
Petiole 1-2 cm; leaf blade 6.5-14 ×  4-8 cm, base usually oblique, obtuse, or shallowly cordate; veins 7. Peduncle shorter than petioles.

> Valley forests, on trees; 1300-1700 m. SC to SE Yunnan [Bhutan, E India, Vietnam]
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Piper bavinum&lt;/I&gt; C. de Candolle; &lt;I&gt;Piper punctulivenum&lt;/I&gt; C. de Candolle; &lt;I&gt;P. punctulivenum&lt;/I&gt; var. &lt;I&gt;parvifolium&lt;/I&gt; C. de Candolle.
