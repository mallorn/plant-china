37b.**Rubus mesogaeus var. oxycomus** Focke",

## Description
Leaflets persistently tomentose abaxially. Branchlets, petioles, and pedicels ± with stalked glands.

> Mixed forested slopes, montane valleys, roadsides, stream sides; below 2800 m. Gansu, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan.
**34b. 腺毛喜阴悬钩子（变种）**

R. mesogaeus Focke var. oxycomus Focke in Engler, Bot. Jahrb. 29: 399. 1900 et in l. c. 36 (Beibl. 82): 56. 1905; id. Bibl. Bat. 72(2): 205. 1911: Rehd. in Journ. Arn. Arb. 5: 198. 1924.

本变种枝、叶柄和花梗均近无刺或有稀疏针刺和疏密不等的腺毛。

产陕西、甘肃、四川。生山坡路旁杂木林中或山谷、溪边，海拔达2800米。
