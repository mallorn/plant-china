## Description
Herbs moderately tall. Roots fusiform. Leaves almost all basal; leaf blade pinnatisect. Inflorescences centrifugal. Calyx lobes 5. Corolla large; galea projected into a broad beaklike apex, with several marginal teeth on each side.
