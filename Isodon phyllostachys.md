叶穗香茶菜",
5.**Isodon phyllostachys** (Diels) Kudô",

## Description
Shrubs or subshrubs 0.9-3 m. Branches erect, densely spreading pilose, glabrescent. Petiole 1-6 mm, pilose; leaf blade ovate, 1.5-5 × 0.8-4.5 cm, corrugate, adaxially puberulent or pilose, abaxially gray tomentose, base cordate, margin crenate, apex obtuse, sometimes acute. Panicles spikelike, 4-25 × 1-2 cm.; cymes 4-15-flowered, pedunculate; upper floral leaves bractlike. Calyx campanulate, ca. 2 × 2 mm, densely pilose outside; teeth ovate-triangular, ca. 1/2 as long as calyx; fruiting calyx dilated, ovoid, teeth ca. 1/3 as long as calyx. Corolla yellowish or white with purple spots, ca. 6 mm, pilose outside, tube ca. 3 mm. Nutlets brown, ovoid. Fl. Aug-Oct, fr. Oct.

> * Thickets, grassy hills; 1000-3000 m. Sichuan, Yunnan.

**12b.薄叶变种**

var. leptophylla C. Y. Wu et H. W. Li云南植物志1: 765. 1976.

这一变种与原变种不同在于叶薄，卵状长圆形，具不十分明显的皱纹，除上部渐变小的苞叶外，均具长2-5毫米的柄。

产云南西北部；生于石灰岩山上，海拔2200-2400米。模式标本采自云南中甸白地。
