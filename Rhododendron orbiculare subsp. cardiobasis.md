## Description
Leaf blade ovate-orbicular, 8–12.5 × 5.5–9 cm; base subcordate or ± rounded; abaxial surface glabrous. Corolla lobes 7; stamens 14.

> Forests; 1500–2200 m. NE Guangxi.
**20b. 心基杜鹃（亚种）（中国高等植物图鉴）图版10: 5**

Rhododendron orbiculare Decne. subsp. cardiobasis (Sleumer) Chamb. (subsp.cardiobasis (Hand. -Mazz.) Chamb. ex Cullen et Chamb. in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 37:331. 1979 sphalm.) Chamb. in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 39: 238. 1982.——R. cardiobasis Sleumer in Notzbl. Bot. Gart. Berlin-Dahlem 12:434. 1935; 中国高等植物图鉴3: 105. 图4164. 1974; 华南杜鹃花志23. 1983.

本亚种与原亚种的区别在于叶片基部浅心形，花冠粉红色，花丝基部略有白色的微柔毛。

产广西东部及东北部。生于海拔1 550-2 140米的山坡疏林中或山顶草地上。模式标本采自广西瑶山。
