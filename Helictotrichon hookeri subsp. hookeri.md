异燕麦(原亚种)",
3a.**Helictotrichon hookeri subsp. hookeri**",

## Description
Plant 20–50 cm tall. Panicle 4–8 cm, dense, branches usually bearing a single spikelet. Spikelets golden brown.

> Mountain meadows and rocky mountain slopes; below 3500 m. Qinghai, Sichuan, Xinjiang, Yunnan [Mongolia, Russia; North America].
  "Synonym": "*Avena hookeri* Scribner, True Grasses 123. 1890; *A. ten-toensis* Honda; *Avenastrum asiaticum* Roshevitz; *A. tentoense* (Honda) Kitagawa; *Helictotrichon asiaticum* (Roshevitz) Grossheim.
