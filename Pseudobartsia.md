> One species: endemic to China.&#x0D;\n&lt;p&gt;&#x0D;\n&lt;I&gt;Pseudobartsia&lt;/I&gt; is one of the most primitive members of the tribe &lt;I&gt;Rhinantheae&lt;/I&gt;. The genus is related to &lt;I&gt;Odontites&lt;/I&gt; Ludwig and &lt;I&gt;Bartsia&lt;/I&gt; Linnaeus, and it is also similar to\n&lt;I&gt;Phtheirospermum&lt;/I&gt; Bunge. Tao (Acta Bot. Yunnan. 15: 232. 1993) treated &lt;I&gt;Pseudobartsia yunnanensis&lt;/I&gt; as conspecific with the Himalayan &lt;I&gt;Phtheirospermum glandulosum&lt;/I&gt; Bentham.\nHowever, more comparative material is needed before the generic placement of &lt;I&gt;P. glandulosum&lt;/I&gt; and the possible synonymy of &lt;I&gt;P. yunnanensis&lt;/I&gt; can be confirmed.

## Description
Herbs, annual. Leaves opposite; leaf blade palmatisect, 3-parted. Racemes terminal. Calyx 10-veined, 5-lobed, parted to ca. 1/2 length, upper lobe slightly shallower. Corolla 2-lipped; lower lip exserted in bud, 3-lobed at anthesis, lobes patent, base 2-plicate; upper lip obscurely galeate, parted slightly beyond middle. Stamens 4, didynamous, enclosed by galea; anther locules equal, apically confluent, obovoid, pointed at base. Stigma capitate. Capsule loculicidal. Seeds numerous, sculptured, slightly curved; seed coat reticulate.
**50.五齿萼属（新属)——Pseudobartsia Hong, gen. nov,, in Addenda 406.**

一年生草本。叶全部对生，叶片3深裂。花数朵，疏离，组成总状花序，无小苞片。花萼钟状，具10条脉，5裂至中部，后面（近轴面）一枚略小。花冠2唇形，下唇裂片在花蕾中据外方，上唇稍呈盔状，深裂略过半，裂片伸直而不卷，下唇裂片根部有两个横皱褶，裂片3枚，开展。雄蕊4枚，2强，伸至盔下，两药室相等，连着，倒卵形，基部有尾尖。子房和花柱被毛，柱头头状。蒴果矩圆状，侧扁，顶端微凹，室背2裂。种子多数，椭圆状，稍弯曲，具网纹。
<p style='text-indent:28px'>这个属与Odontites, Bartsia最亲近，但以花萼具5裂片；花冠上唇仅仅很不明显地呈盔状，深裂；种子无翅而具网纹，明显有别。而且这3个特征也表明这个属在这个族中是一个原始的类型。

仅一种，产我国云南省。
