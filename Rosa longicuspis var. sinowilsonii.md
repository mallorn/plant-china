多花长尖叶蔷薇",
78b.**Rosa longicuspis var. sinowilsonii** (Hemsley) T. T. Yü & T. C. Ku",

## Description
Leaflets 5(or 7), abaxially glabrous or slightly pubescent, adaxially slightly rugose. Compound corymb 30-flowered.

> Guizhou, Sichuan, Yunnan.
  "Synonym": "*Rosa sinowilsonii* Hemsley, Bull. Misc. Inform. Kew 1906: 158. 1906.
**69b.多花长尖叶蔷薇（变种）**

Rosa longicuspis Bertoll. var. sinowilsonii (Hemsl.) Yu et Ku in Bull. Bot. Res. 1 (4): 15. 1981. ——R. sinoiuilsoni Hemsl. in Kew Bull. 1906: 158. 1906; Willmott, Gen. Ros. 1: 73. 1911.

本变种与原变种的异点在其叶形较大，花朵较多，小叶片常5片，稀7，边缘为单锯齿或偶有重锯齿，上面微皱，下面无毛或微有短柔毛；复伞房花序，有花多至30余朵，易于区别。

产四川、贵州、云南。
