## Description
Herbs annual, robust. Stems rigid, erect, usually branched apically. Leaves whorled, pinnatisect. Inflorescences racemose. Calyx lobes 5, &amp;#177; leaflike, serrate. Corolla tube slightly curved; galea with a very short beak at apex and 1 tooth on each side of margin.
