台湾唐松草(原变种)",
70a.**Thalictrum urbainii var. urbainii**",

## Description
Plants 10--40 cm tall. Flowers ca. 1 cm in diam. Sepals 4, 3--3.5 × 1.5--2 mm. Fl. May.

> * Taiwan.
  "Synonym": "&lt;I&gt;Thalictrum fauriei&lt;/I&gt; Hayata, J. Coll. Sci. Imp. Univ. Tokyo 22: 7. Sep 1906, not H. Léveillé &amp; Vaniot (Jul 1906), nor H. Léveillé (1909); &lt;I&gt;T. hayatanum&lt;/I&gt; Koidzumi.
