武夷唐松草",
74.**Thalictrum wuyishanicum** W. T. Wang & S. H. Wang",

> This species is similar to &lt;I&gt; Thalictrum reticulatum,&lt;/I&gt; but differs in having inflorescence monochasial, cymose, and 2-flowered or helicoid and 4-flowered.

## Description
Plants 17--30 cm tall, glabrous. Stems simple or distally branched. Basal leaves 1--3; petiole 8--12 cm; leaf blade 3-ternate, 4.5--9 cm; petiolule 1.5--2 cm; leaflet blade broadly ovate or broadly rhombic, 1.8--2.5 × 1.5--1.8 cm, papery or thinly leathery, base obtuse, apex obtuse, 3-lobed; lobes slightly toothed; veins raised on both surfaces or flat adaxially. Inflorescence terminal or axillary, monochasial, 2--4-flowered. Pedicel 1.2--3 mm. Sepals 4, early deciduous, pale pinkish red, elliptic or narrowly so, 3--4 × 1.5--2 mm, apex obtuse. Stamens 12--16; filament base filiform, to 2 × as broad as anther; anther oblong, ca. 0.8 mm, apex obtuse or mucronate. Carpels (4--)6 or 7; stigma sessile. Achene body fusiform, 3--4 mm; veins ca. 6. Fl. Apr.

> * About 2000 m. N Fujian (Chong’an Xian: Wuyi Shan), EC Jiangxi (Nanfeng Xian).

Thalictrum wuyishanicum W. T. Wang et S. H. Wang in Addenda.

植株全体无毛。茎高17-29厘米，细，中部以上有少数分枝或不分枝。基生叶1-3，有长柄，为二回三出复叶；叶片长4.5-9厘米；小叶圆菱形、倒卵状菱形，有时宽卵形，长1.8-2.5厘米，宽1.5-2.5厘米，两端钝或圆形，三浅裂，边缘有少数牙齿，两面脉隆起，脉网明显，小叶柄长1.5-2厘米；叶柄长8-12厘米。茎生叶为一回三出复叶。复杂或简单的单歧聚伞花序顶生或腋生，有少数花；花梗长1.2-3厘米；萼片淡粉红色，椭圆形或狭椭圆形，长3-4毫米，宽1.5-2毫米，钝，早落；雄蕊12-16，长约5毫米，花药椭圆形，长约0.8毫米，顶端钝，花丝比花药稍宽或宽为其2倍，上部倒披针形，下部丝形；心皮（4-）6-7，柱头无柄，小。瘦果纺锤形，长3-4毫米，有6条纵肋，无柄，宿存柱头小，近头形。4月开花。

分布于江西东部（南丰）、福建西北部（崇安）。生海拔2000米一带山地岩石上。模式标本采自福建崇安。

本种近网脉唐松草T. reticulatum, 但花序为有2花的简单单歧聚伞花序或有约4朵花的蝎尾状聚伞花序，而网脉唐松草的花序分枝较多，呈伞房状，有较多花。
