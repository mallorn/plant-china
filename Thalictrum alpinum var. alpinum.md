高山唐松草(原变种)",
2a.**Thalictrum alpinum var. alpinum**",

> This variety may be found near sea-level in North America.

## Description
Plants 5--20 cm tall. Leaflets 3--5 mm wide. Peduncle simple. Flowering pedicels recurved. Achenes sessile. Fl. Jun--Aug.

> Damp valleys, wet meadows, bogs; 4400--5300 m. Xinjiang, Xizang [Afghanistan, Bhutan, India, Kazakhstan, Mongolia, Nepal, Pakistan, Russia (Siberia), Sikkim, Vietnam; Europe, North America].
