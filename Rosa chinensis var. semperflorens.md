紫月季花",
56c.**Rosa chinensis var. semperflorens** (Curtis) Koehne",

## Description
Branchlets slender, with short prickles. Flowers double or semi-double, often solitary, or 2 or 3 and fasciculate. Leaflets 5–7, thinner, often tinged with purple-red. Petals deep red or deep purple. Sepals often with a few lobes. 2n = 14*.

> Cultivated in China [widely cultivated elsewhere; of cultivated origin].
  "Synonym": "*Rosa semperflorens* Curtis, Bot. Mag. 8: t. 284. 1794.
**55c. 紫月季花（变种）**

R. chinensis Jacq. var. semperflorens (Curbs) Koehne, Deuts. Dendr. 281. 1893; Byhouwer in Journ. Arn. Arb. 10: 97. 1929; 秦岭植物志 1(2): 572. 1974. ——R. semperflorens Curtis in Bot. Mag. 8: t. 284. 1794

本变种枝条纤细，有短皮刺；小叶5-7，较薄，常带紫红色；花大部单生或2-3朵，深红色或深紫色，重瓣，有细长花梗。

产福建，具体地点不详，钟心煊采6425 是否应属本组，有待研究确定。

Metcalf 发表福建蔷薇 Rosa fukienensis Metc. in Journ. Arn. Arb. 21: 274. 1940. 直立灌木，具有小叶5片，无毛；花多朵呈伞房状圆锥花序，萼片披针形有羽状裂片，花柱外伸，离生，被柔毛，果实近球形，直径约7毫米，无毛。
