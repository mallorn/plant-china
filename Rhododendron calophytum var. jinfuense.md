金佛山美容杜鹃",
207d.**Rhododendron calophytum var. jinfuense** Fang & W. K. Hu",

> Fang (Acta Phytotax. Sin. 26: 68. 1988) published this taxon independently, based on the same type and with the same validating diagnosis, but at the rank of subspecies.

## Description
Leaf blade 10–14 × 3–4 cm. Pedicel 2–2.5 cm; corolla purple, small, lobes 5.

> Deciduous broad-leaved forests; 2200–2300 m. Chongqing (Nanchuan).
