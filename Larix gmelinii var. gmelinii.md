落叶松 (原变种)
8a.**Larix gmelinii var. gmelinii**

## Description
First-year long branchlets slender, ca. 1 mm in diam. Short branchlets 2-3 mm in diam. Seed cones 1.2-3 ×  1-2 cm. Seed scales 14-30, dehiscent at maturity.


> Hills, mountains, peatlands, swamps, lowland subarctic plains, river basins, valleys; 300-1200 m. Heilongjiang, Jilin, Nei Mongol [Korea, Mongolia, E Russia]
