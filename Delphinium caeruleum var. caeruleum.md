蓝翠雀花(原变种)",
162a.**Delphinium caeruleum var. caeruleum**",

## Description
Stem 8--60 cm, appressed puberulent, without glandular hairs. Proximal leaves present at anthesis; cauline leaf blade to 8 cm wide; ultimate lobules 2--3 mm wide, apically acute. Spur 1.8--2.8 cm, base 2--3 mm in diam. Ovaries puberulent. Fl. Jul--Sep.

> Grassy or gravelly slopes; 2100--4000 m. Gansu, Qinghai, W Sichuan, NW Yunnan, Xizang [Bhutan, Nepal].
